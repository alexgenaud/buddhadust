Death

Death is the fall away,
the fall out, passing,
the ending, passing away,
the vanishing, disappearance,
the death in the dying,
the finishing of the lifespan,
the breaking up of the stockpiles,
the laying down of the body,
of such and such a being
in this or that world where beings live
