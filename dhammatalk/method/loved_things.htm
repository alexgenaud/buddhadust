The Things of This World That Are Loved

The Worlds of the visible,
hearable,
smellable,
tasteable,
touchable,
and knowable

sound,
scent,
taste,
touch,
and ideas.

The Stimulation of sight,
hearing,
smell,
taste,
touch,
and mind.

The Experience of Sensations arising from the stimulation of sight,
hearing,
smell,
taste,
touch,
and mind.

The Perception of sights,
sounds,
scents,
tastes,
touches
and ideas.

The Intentions connected with sights,

scents,
tastes,

and ideas.

The Hunger/thirst for sights,

and ideas.

and ideas.
