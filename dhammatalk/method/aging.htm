Aging

Whatsoever for this or that being
of this or that group of beings
is aging,
agedness,
the deterioration,
the falling apart,
the breaking,
the graying,
the wrinkling,
the rotting away,
the diminishment of the remainder of the lifespan,
the weakening of the powers,
the getting old of the six-fold sense spheres
this, beggars is said to be 'aging.'
