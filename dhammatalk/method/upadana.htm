Bindups

Upadana, Graspings

Pray what is grasping?"

"There are these four graspings, friend:
the grasping after sensuality,
the grasping after opinion,
the grasping after rule and ritual,
the grasping after theory of self.

—SN 4.38.12; DN 33.03
