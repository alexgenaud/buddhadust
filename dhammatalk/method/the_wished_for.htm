The Wished For

What is Wished for is in reference to the case when such and such a being wishes:

"O,O,O If Only
I were not subject to Birth,
Aging and Death,
Grief and Lamentation,
Pain and Misery,
and Despair!"

Such is not to be had by Wishing.
