Saṅsāra

Saṅsāra. Saṅ = on, own, with, one's own; sarati: to go, flow, run, move along. On-flow, own-flow. Another case where the 'saṅ' would be well-served by being translated 'own'. The identification with an aspect (a living being) in the on going flow of existence.
