X. Gandhabbakāya Saɱyutta, III.249

Covering suttas 1-112. On Gandharvas. Follows the scheme of the previous samyuttas. Gandhabbas are from the description similar to our 'little people', fairies, etc. They are considered to be the first level of deva above the human realm. They live in fragrences and are said to be the beings that are reborn (or at least are present at) the conjunction of a male and female human in order for conception to take place. Pheramons?

PTS: The Kindred Sayings on Gandharvas, III.197
WP: Connected Discourses on Gandhabbas, I.1025
