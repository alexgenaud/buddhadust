XI. Valaha Saṃyutta, III.254

Covering suttas 1-57. On Cloud-Spirits. Follows the pattern of the previous samyuttas. Another series similar to the previous but describing various factors leading to rebirth as a Valāha-Deva. These are the beings known in the west as 'fairies', or 'Spirits' and which are described here as in habiting clouds of various sorts.
