II. Anāthapiṇḍika Vagga, I.51

Covering Suttas 11-20. Exchanges with 'Sons of the Gods', Gods whose names are known, hence the suttas take on depth. Of special interest is Sn.1.2.10: Anāthapiṇḍika which shows that a Non-returner is not incapable of returning to this world for a visit.
