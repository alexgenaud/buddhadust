II. Anāthapiṇḍika Vagga, I.51

Covering Suttas 11-20. Exchanges with 'Sons of the Gods', Gods whose names are known, hence the suttas take on depth. Of special interest is Sn.1.2.10: Anāthapiṇḍika which shows that a Non-returner is not incapable of returning to this world for a visit.

17. Subrahmā Suttaṃ, I.53

A Deva asks the Buddha if there is any place of safety. The Buddha responds that apart from serious pursuit of the Seven Dimensions of Awakening, guarding of the senses and letting it all go, he sees no safety for living beings.

PTS: Subrahmā, I.76
ATI: Subrahmā the Deva's Son, Bhk. Thanissaro, trans.
WP: Subrahmā, 149
