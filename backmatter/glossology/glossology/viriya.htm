[EDITED ENTRY]

 

Viriya: [fr. vīra; cp. Vedic vīrya & vīria] lit. "state of a strong man," i. e. vigour, energy, effort, exertion.... --viriyaŋ āra(m)bhati to put forth energy, to make an effort... -- As adj. (--*) in alīna* alert, energetic...; āraddha* full of energy, putting forth energy, strenuous...; ossaṭṭha* one who has given up effort...; hīna* lacking in energy... -- v. is one of the indriyas, the balas & the sambojjhangas. --indriya the faculty of energy... --bala the power of energy...
