[EDITED ENTRY]

Jarā: old age, etc. ...decay, decrepitude, old age Vin I.10, 34; A I.51, 138 (as Death's messenger); V.144 ...Sn 311 (cp. D III.75); J I.59;...Defined as "yā tesaŋ sattānaṃ tamhi tamhi sattanikāye jarā jīraṇatā khaṇḍiccaṃ pāliccaṃ valittacatā āyuno saŋhāni indriyānaṃ paripāko" D II.305=M I.49= S II.2... Frequently combd with maraṇa (maccu, etc.) "decay and death"...: -maraṇa, D II.31 sq.; M I.49; Sn 575;... ajarāmara not subject to decay and death...
