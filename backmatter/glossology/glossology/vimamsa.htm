Vīmaṃsā: (f.) [fr. vīmaṃsati] consideration, examination, test, investigation, the fourth of the Iddhipādas, q. v.; D III.77 (-samādhi), 222; S V.280; A I.39, 297; III.37, 346...

Vīmaṃsati: [Vedic mīmāṃsate, Desid. of man. The P. form arose through dissimilation m>v, cp. Geiger, P.Gr. 46, 4] "to try to think," to consider, examine, find out, investigate, test, trace, think over...

 

From VI MANSA SATI: Re-Member-Memory. To piece together memories. The aspect of investigation of the etiology of a thing that involves tracing (remembering) memories back to their point of origin.
