Sacca: [cp. Sk. satya] real, true D I.182; M II.169; III.207;...; nt. saccaṅ truly, verily, certainly...; saccaṅ kira is it really true? D I.113; Vin I.45, 60; J I.107; saccato truly S III.112. -- (nt. as noun) saccaṅ the truth A II.25, 115 (paramaḥ);...; also: a solemn asseveration...Sacce patiṭṭhaya keeping to fact, M I.376. -- pl. (cattari) saccani the (four) truths M II.199; A II.41, 176;...-- The 4 ariya-saccani are the truth about dukkha, dukkhasamudaya, dukkha-nirodha, and dukkha-nirodha-gāminipaṭipadā. Thus e. g. at Vin I.230; D II.304 sq.; III.277; A I.175 sq.; Vism 494 sq.;... A shortened statement as dukkha, samudaya, nirodha, magga is freq. found, e. g. Vin I.16;...
-ānupaṭṭi realization of truth M II.173 sq.
-ānubodha awakening to truth M II.171 sq.
-ānurakkhaṇa warding of truth, M II.176.
-ñāṇa knowledge of the truth...
-paṭivedha penetration of the truth Ps II.57.
-vādin truthful, speaking the truth D I.4; III.170; A II.209; IV.249, 389; S I.66;...
