Tendency[49]

[1][bodh][olds] THUS have I heard:

Once the Exalted One was staying near Sāvatthī at Jeta Grove, in Anāthapiṇḍika's Park.

... It conduces to the utter destruction of tendency.

 

 

[49]Anusayo. Cf. text, p. 60. There are seven of these 'inclinations.'
