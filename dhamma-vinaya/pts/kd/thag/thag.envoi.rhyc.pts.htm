PSALMS OF THE BRETHREN

Envoi

Translated from the Pali by Mrs. C.A.F. Rhys Davids.

Public Domain

[412]

Singing the pæan of their 'lion's' roar,
These children of the Buddha, sane, immune,
Winning the safe sure haven of their quest,
Dwelt in blest cool like flame of fire extinct.
