On giving (b)

[236] [161]

[1][olds][bodh]

Faith, modesty[1] and faultless giving -
These are the things wise men pursue.
This is the way[2] divine, they say,
The high-way to the deva-world.[3]

 

[1] Hiriyaɱ, a rare form of hiri.

[2] Magga.

[3] Quoted at Kvu 341; trsl. 199; Expositor 217; Vv.A. 194.
