Sutta 177

Vaṇijjā Suttaɱ

Business (Wrong Livelihood)

Translated from the Pali by Thanissaro Bhikkhu.

Proofed against and modified in accordance with the revised edition at dhammatalks.org
For free distribution only.

 

 

[1][pts][olds] "Monks, a lay follower should not engage in
five types of business.

Which five?

Business in weapons,
business in living beings,
business in meat,
business in intoxicants,
and business in poison.

"These are the five types of business
that a lay follower should not engage in."
