Aṇguttara Nikāya
Catukka Nipāta
15. Ābhā Vagga

Sutta 144

Obhāsa Suttaṃ

Brightness

Translated from the Pali by Thanissaro Bhikkhu.

Proofed against and modified in accordance with the revised edition at dhammatalks.org
For free distribution only.

 

 

[1][pts] "There are these four types of brightness.

Which four?

The brightness of the sun,
the brightness of the moon,
the brightness of fire,
and the brightness of discernment.

These are the four types of brightness.

And of these four types of brightness,
the foremost is the brightness of discernment."
