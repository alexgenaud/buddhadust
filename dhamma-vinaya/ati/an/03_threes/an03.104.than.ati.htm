III. Tika Nipāta
XI. Sambodhi Vagga

Sutta 104 [DTO 109]

Atitti Suttaɱ

No Satiation

Translated from the Pali by Thanissaro Bhikkhu.
Sourced from the edition at dhammatalks.org
Provenance, terms and conditons

 

 

[104][pts][olds] "There is no satiation through indulging in three things.

Which three?

There is no satiation through indulging in sleep.

There is no satiation through indulging in distilled and fermented drinks.

There is no satiation through indulging in the climax of sexual intercourse.

There is no satiation through indulging in these three things."
