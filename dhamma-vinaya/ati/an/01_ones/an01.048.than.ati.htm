Aŋguttara-Nikāya
I. Ekanipāta

Sutta 48

Lahu-parivatta Sutta

Quick to Reverse Itself

Translated from the Pali by Thanissaro Bhikkhu.
Provenance, terms and conditons

 

 

[48][pts][olds] "I don't envision a single thing
that is as quick to reverse itself
as the mind
— so much so
that there is no feasible simile
for how quick to reverse itself it is."
