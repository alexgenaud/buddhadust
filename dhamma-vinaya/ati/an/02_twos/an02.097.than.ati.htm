Aṇguttara-Nikāya
II. Duka Nipāta
X. Bāla Vagga

Sutta 97

Bāla Suttaṃ

Fools

Translated from the Pali by Thanissaro Bhikkhu.
Proofed against and modified in accordance with the revised edition at dhammatalks.org
Provenance, terms and conditons

 

 

[97.1][pts][olds] "Monks, these two are fools.

Which two?

The one who takes up a burden that hasn't fallen to him,
and the one who doesn't take up a burden that has.

These two are fools."
