Sutta 120

Dullabha Suttaɱ

Rarely Having Enough

Translated from the Pali by Thanissaro Bhikkhu.
Sourced from the edition at dhammatalks.org
Provenance, terms and conditons

 

 

[118.1][pts][olds] "Monks, these two persons rarely have enough.

Which two?

One who piles [hoards] gain and gain, and one who spends [squanders] gain and gain.

These two persons rarely have enough."
