Aṇguttara-Nikāya
II. Duka Nipāta
III. Bāla Vagga

Sutta 23

Abhāsita Suttaṃ

What Was Not Said

Translated from the Pali by Thanissaro Bhikkhu.
Proofed against and modified in accordance with the revised edition at dhammatalks.org
Provenance, terms and conditons

 

 

[23][pts][olds] "Monks, these two slander the Tathāgata.

Which two?

He who explains what was not said or spoken by the Tathāgata
as said or spoken by the Tathāgata.
And he who explains what was said or spoken by the Tathāgata
as not said or spoken by the Tathāgata.

These are two who slander the Tathāgata."
