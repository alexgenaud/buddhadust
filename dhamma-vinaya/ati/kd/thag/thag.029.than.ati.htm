Theragatha
Chapter I — Single Verses

29

Harita

Translated from the Pali by Thanissaro Bhikkhu.

For free distribution only.

 

§

 

    Harita,
raise yourself up-
    right
and, straightening your mind
-- like a fletcher, an arrow --
    shatter ignorance
        to bits.
