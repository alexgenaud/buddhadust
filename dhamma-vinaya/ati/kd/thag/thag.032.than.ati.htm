Theragatha
Chapter I — Single Verses

32

Suppiya

Translated from the Pali by Thanissaro Bhikkhu.

For free distribution only.

 

§

 

I'll make a trade:
aging for the Ageless,
burning for the Unbound:
    the highest peace,
    the unexcelled rest
        from the yoke.
