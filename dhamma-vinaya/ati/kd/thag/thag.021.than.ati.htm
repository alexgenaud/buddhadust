Theragatha
Chapter I — Single Verses

21

Nigrodha

Translated from the Pali by Thanissaro Bhikkhu.

For free distribution only.

 

§

 

I'm not afraid     of danger,
            of fear.
Our Teacher's adept
in the Deathless.
Where danger, where fear
do not remain:
    that's the path
    by which the monks go.
