Theragatha
Chapter I — Single Verses

84

Nita

Translated from the Pali by Thanissaro Bhikkhu.

For free distribution only.

 

§

 

Asleep the whole night,
delighting in company by day:
    when, when
    will the fool
    bring suffering and stress
        to an end?
