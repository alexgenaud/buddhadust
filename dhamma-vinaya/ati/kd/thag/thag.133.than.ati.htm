Theragatha
Chapter II — Pairs of Verses

133

Heraññakani

Translated from the Pali by Thanissaro Bhikkhu.

For free distribution only.

 

 

Days and nights
      fly past.
Life
   comes to an end.
The span of mortals
      runs out,
like the water of a piddling stream.
But the fool doing evil deeds
doesn't realize that later
it's bitter for him:
evil for him
      the result.
