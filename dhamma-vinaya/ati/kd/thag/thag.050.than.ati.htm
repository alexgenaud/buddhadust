Theragatha
Chapter I — Single Verses

50

Vimala

Translated from the Pali by Thanissaro Bhikkhu.

For free distribution only.

 

§

 

The earth's sprinkled
with rain, wind
is blowing, lightning
wanders the sky,
but my thoughts are stilled,
    well-centered
    my mind.
