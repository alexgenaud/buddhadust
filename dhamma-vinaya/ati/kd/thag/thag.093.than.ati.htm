Theragatha
Chapter I — Single Verses

93

Eraka

Translated from the Pali by Thanissaro Bhikkhu.

For free distribution only.

 

§

 

Sensual pleasures are stressful,
            Eraka.
Sensual pleasures aren't ease.
Whoever loves sensual pleasures
    loves stress,     Eraka.
Whoever doesn't,
    doesn't love stress.
