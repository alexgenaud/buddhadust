Theragatha
Chapter I — Single Verses

1

Subhuti

Translated from the Pali by Thanissaro Bhikkhu.

For free distribution only.

 

 

My hut is roofed, comfortable,
    free of drafts;
my mind, well-centered,
    set free.
I remain ardent.
    So, rain-deva.
    Go ahead and rain.
