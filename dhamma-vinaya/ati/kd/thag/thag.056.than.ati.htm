Theragatha
Chapter I — Single Verses

56

Kutiviharin (1)

Translated from the Pali by Thanissaro Bhikkhu.

For free distribution only.

 

§

 

Who's in the hut?
A monk's in the hut --
    free from passion,
    with well-centered mind.
Know this, my friend:
    The hut you built
    wasn't wasted.
