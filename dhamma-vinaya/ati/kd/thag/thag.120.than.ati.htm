Theragatha
Chapter I — Single Verses

120

Isidatta

Translated from the Pali by Thanissaro Bhikkhu.

For free distribution only.

 

§

 

The five aggregates,
having been comprehended,
stand with their root
    cut through.
For me
    the ending of stress
        is reached;
    the ending of effluents,
        attained.
