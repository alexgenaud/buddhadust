Theragatha
Chapter I — Single Verses

2

Mahā Koṭṭhika

Translated from the Pali by Thanissaro Bhikkhu.

For free distribution only.

[idx][pali][rhyc]

 

§

 

Calmed, restrained,
giving counsel unruffled,
he lifts off evil states of mind --
    as the breeze,
    a leaf from a tree.
