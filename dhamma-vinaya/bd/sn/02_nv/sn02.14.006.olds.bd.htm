Sutta 6

Dhātu [Bāhira-Dhātu-Nānatta] Suttaɱ

Data

Translated from the Pali
by
Michael M. Olds

 

 

[1][pts][bodh] I HEAR TELL:

Once upon a time, The Lucky Man, Sāvatthi Town revisiting, Anāthapiṇḍika Park, Jeta Grove.

2. "I will point out to you the diversity of data, beggars,
give ear, pay attention, I will speak!"

"Say on, elder!" said the beggars to The Lucky Man in response.

3. The Lucky Man said this:

"And what, beggars, are the diverse data?

4. Form data,
sound data,
scent data,
taste data,
impact data,
things data.

These beggars, are what are called the diverse data."
