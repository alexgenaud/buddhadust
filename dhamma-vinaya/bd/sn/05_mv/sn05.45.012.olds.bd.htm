V. Mahā-Vagga
45. Magga Saɱyuttaa

Sutta 12

Dutiya Vihāra Suttaɱ

Residence (2)

Translated from the Pali
by
Michael M. Olds

 

 

[1][pts][bodh] I hear tell this was cast in Sāvatthī

There then The Lucky Man said this to the beggars:

"Beggars!"

And the beggars responding, "Bhante!"
the Lucky Man said:

"I wish, beggars, for three months, to dwell in retreat.

No other should approach me save the one who brings my mess bowl."

"Even so, bhante!" said those beggars in response,
and no one approached The Lucky Man save the one who brought him his mess bowl.

Then, at the conclusion of this three months retreat,
The Lucky Man addressed the bhikkhus:

I have, beggars, during this retreat,
approached the manner of living
I had when first self-awakened
when I thus understood:

The result of contrary view is just experience,
the result of tranquilized contrary view is just experience,
the result of consummate view is just experience,
the result of tranquilized consummate view is just experience,
the result of contrary principles is just experience,
the result of tranquilized principles view is just experience,
the result of consummate view is just experience,
the result of tranquilized contrary view is just experience,
the result of contrary speech is just experience,
the result of tranquilized contrary speech view is just experience,
the result of consummate speech is just experience,
the result of tranquilized consummate speech is just experience,
the result of contrary works is just experience,
the result of tranquilized contrary works is just experience,
the result of consummate works is just experience,
the result of tranquilized consummate works is just experience,
the result of contrary lifestyle is just experience,
the result of tranquilized contrary lifestyle is just experience,
the result of consummate lifestyle is just experience,
the result of tranquilized consummate lifestyle is just experience,
the result of contrary self-control is just experience,
the result of tranquilized contrary self-control is just experience,
the result of consummate self-control is just experience,
the result of tranquilized contrary view is just experience,
the result of contrary minding is just experience,
the result of tranquilized consummate self-control is just experience,
the result of consummate minding is just experience,
the result of tranquilized contrary minding is just experience,
the result of contrary serenity is just experience,
the result of tranquilized contrary serenity is just experience,
the result of consummate serenity is just experience
the result of tranquilized consummate serenity is just experience,
the result of wanting is just experience,
the result of tranquilized wanting is just experience,
the result of thinking is just experience,
the result of tranquilized thinking is just experience,
the result of perceiving is just experience,
the result of tranquilized perceiving is just experience.

And having turbulant desires,
and having turbulant thoughts,
and having turbulant perceptions,
of this too the result is just experience.

And having tranquil desires,
and having tranquil thoughts,
and having tranquil perceptions,
of this too the result is just experience.

For one of small attainment
there is striving
this set up, there is further attainment
of this too the result is just experience.
