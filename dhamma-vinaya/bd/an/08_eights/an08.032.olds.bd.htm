Atthaka Nipāta
4. Dāna Vagga

Sutta 32

Dutiya Dāna Suttaɱ

Giving 2

Translated from the Pali
by
Michael M. Olds

 

 

[1][pts][bodh]

Faith, shame, skill and giving
these things the good man's habits
this the highway devine they say
to the getting of godly living.
