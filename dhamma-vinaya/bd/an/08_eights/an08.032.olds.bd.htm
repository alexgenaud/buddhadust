Aṅguttara Nikāya
Aṭṭhaka Nipāta
4. Dāna Vagga

Sutta 32

Dutiya Dāna Suttaṃ

Giving 2

Translated from the Pāḷi
by
Michael M. Olds

 

 

[1][pts][bodh]

Faith, shame, skill and giving
these things the good man's way;
this the highway divine they say
to the getting of godly living.
