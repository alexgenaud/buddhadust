Aṅguttara Nikāya
Pañcaka-Nipāta
IV. Sumanā Vagga

The Book of Fives

Sutta 37

Bhojana Suttaṃ

The Food Giver

Translated from the Pāḷi
by
Michael M. Olds

 

 

[1][pts][than][olds][bodh] I Hear Tell:

One time the Lucky man addressed the beggars, saying:

"Beggars!"

And "Bhagava!" the beggars responded.

Then the Lucky Man said:

"There are these five gifts given by the food giver.

What five?

Life,
beauty,
ease,
energy
and strength of wits.

But, Beggars, giving such as such as this,
he shares in five benefits.

What five?

Life,
beauty,
ease,
energy
and strength of wits.

Both here and in the hereafter.
