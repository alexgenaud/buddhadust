Adapted from the 1995 edition of the digital version of the Sri Lanka Buddha Jayanti Tripitaka Series.

Namo tassa bhagavato arahato Sammā-Sam-Buddhassa

Ekaka Nipāto

1

[pts][than]

Itthaɱ sudaɱ aññatarā therī apaññātā bhikkhunī gāthaɱ abhāsitthāti

[1] Sukhaɱ supāhi therike katvā coḷena pārutā,||
Upasanto hi te rāgo sukkhaḍākaɱ va kumbhiyaɱ.|| ||

 

2
Muttā-Therī Gāthā

[pts]

Itthaɱ sudaɱ bhagavā muttaɱ sikkhamānaɱ imāya gāthāya abhiṇhaɱ ovada

[2] Mutte muccassu yogehi cando Rāhuggaho iva,||
Vippamuttena cittena anaṇā bhuñjāhi piṇḍakaɱ.|| ||

 

3
Puṇṇā-Therī Gāthā

[pts][than]

Itthaɱ sudaɱ puṇṇā therī gāthaɱ abhāsitthāti

[3] Puṇṇe pūrassu dhammehi cando paṇṇarase-r-iva,
Paripuṇṇāya paññāya tamok-khandhaɱ padālaya.|| ||

 

4
Tisisā-Therī Gāthā

[pts]

Itthaɱ sudaɱ tissā therī gāthaɱ abhāsitthāti

[4] Tisse sikkhassu sikkhāya mā taɱ yogā upaccaguɱ,||
Sabba-yoga-visaɱyuttā cara loke anāsavā' ti.|| ||

 

5
Aññatarā Tissā-Therī Gāthā

[pts]

Itthaɱ sudaɱ tissā therī gāthaɱ abhāsitthāti

[5] Tisse yuñjassu dhammehi khaṇo taɱ mā upaccagā,||
Khaṇātītā hi socanti nirayamhi samappitā' ti.|| ||

 

6
Dhīrā-Therī Gāthā

[pts]

Itthaɱ sudaɱ dhīrā therī gāthaɱ abhāsitthāti

[6] [124] Dhīre nirodhaɱ phussehi saññā-vūpasamaɱ sukhaɱ,||
ārādhayāhi Nibbānaɱ yoga-k-khemaɱ anuttaraɱ.|| ||

Aññatarā Dhīrā or Vīrā-Therī Gāthā

[pts]

[7] Dhīrā dhīrehi dhammehi bhikkhunī bhāvit'indriyā,||
Dhārehi antimaɱ dehaɱ chchetvā Māraɱ savāhanaɱ.|| ||

Bhadrā-Therī Gāthā

10
Upasamā-Therī Gāthā

[pts]

Itthaɱ sudaɱ upasamā therī gāthaɱ abhāsitthāti

11
Muttā-Therī Gāthā

[pts][than]

Itthaɱ sudaɱ muttā therī gāthaɱ abhāsitthāti

[11] Sumuttā sādhu muttā'mhi tīhi khujjehi muttiyā,||
Udukkhalena musalena patinā khujjakena ca,||
Muttā'mhi jāti-maraṇā bhavanetti samūhatā.|| ||

 

12
Dhammadinnā-Therī Gāthā

[pts]

Itthaɱ sudaɱ dhammadinnā therī gāthaɱ abhāsitthāti

[12] Chanda-jātā avasāye manasā ca phuṭā siyā,||
Kāmesu appaṭi-baddha-cittā uddhaɱ sotā ti vuccatī.|| ||

 

13
Visākhā-Therī Gāthā

[pts]

Itthaɱ sudaɱ visākhā therī gāthaɱ abhāsitthāti

[13] Karotha Buddha-sāsanaɱ yaɱ katvā nānutappati,||
Khippaɱ pādāni dhovitvā ekam-ante nisīdathā.|| ||

 

14
Sumanā-Therī Gāthā

[pts]

Itthaɱ sudaɱ sumanā therī gāthaɱ abhāsitthāti

[14] Dhātuyo dukkhato disvā mā jāti punar āgami,||
Bhave chandaɱ virāchchetvā upasantā carissasī.|| ||

 

15
Uttarā-Therī Gāthā

[pts]

Itthaɱ sudaɱ uttarā therī gāthaɱ abhāsitthāti

[15] [125] Kāyena saɱvutā āsiɱ vācāya uda cetasā,||
Samūlaɱ taṇhaɱ abbuyha sītibhūta'mhi nibbutā.|| ||

 

16
Sumanā Vuḍḍha-Pabbajitā-Therī Gāthā

[pts]

Itthaɱ sudaɱ sumanā vuḍḍha-pabbajātā therī gāthaɱ abhāsitthāti

[16] Sukhaɱ tvaɱ vuḍḍhake sehi katvā coḷena pārutā,||
Upasanto hi te rāgo sītibhūtā si nibbutā.|| ||

 

17
Dhammā-Therī Gāthā

[pts][than]

Itthaɱ sudaɱ dhammā therī gāthaɱ abhāsitthāti

[17] Piṇḍapātaɱ caritvāna daṇḍam olubbha dubbalā,||
Vedhamānehi gattehi tatth'eva nipatiɱ chamā,||
Disvā ādīnavaɱ kāye atha cittaɱ vimucci meti.|| ||

 

18
Saŋghā-Therī Gāthā

[pts]

Itthaɱ sudaɱ saŋghā therī gāthaɱ abhāsitthāti

[18] Hitvā ghare pabba-jitvā hitvā puttaɱ pasuɱ piyaɱ,||
Hitvā rāgañ ca dosañ ca avijjañ ca virājiya,||
Samūlaɱ taṇhaɱ abbuyha upasanta'mhi nibbutā.|| ||

Abhirūpa-Nandā-Therī Gāthā

20
Jentā-Therī Gāthā

[pts]

Itthaɱ sudaɱ jentā therī gāthāyo abhāsitthāti

Bhāvitā te mayā sabbe yathā Buddhena desitā.|| ||

[22] Diṭṭho hi me so bhagavā antimo'yaɱ samussayo,||
Vikkhīṇo jāti-saɱsāro n'atthi dāni puna-b-bhavo.|| ||

 

21
Sumaŋgala-mātā-Therī Gāthā

[pts][than]

Itthaɱ sudaɱ Sumaŋgala-mātā therī gāthāyo abhāsitthāti

[23] [126] Sumuttike sumuttikā sādhu muttikā'mhi musalassa,||
Ahiriko me chattakaɱ vā pi ukkhalikā me deḷiddabhāvā ti.|| ||

[24] Rāgañ ca ahaɱ dosañ ca viccindantī viharāmi,||
Sā rukkha-mūlaɱ upagamma aho sukhan ti sukhato jhāyāmī.|| ||

 

22
Aḍḍhakāsī-Therī Gāthā

[pts]

Itthaɱ sudaɱ aḍḍhakāsītherī gāthāyo abhāsitthāti

[25] Yāva Kāsi-jana-pado suŋko me tattako ahu,||
Taɱ katvā negamo agghaɱ agghaɱ agghe'nagghaɱ ṭhapesi maɱ.|| ||

[26] Atha nibbind'ahaɱ rūpe nibbindañ ca virajj'ahaɱ,||
Mā puna jāti-saɱsāraɱ sandhāveyyaɱ puna-p-punaɱ,||
Tisso vijjā sacchi-katā kataɱ Buddhassa sāsanaɱ.|| ||

 

23
Cittā-Therī Gāthā

[pts]

Itthaɱ sudaɱ cittā therī gāthāyo abhāsitthāti

[27] Kiñ cāpi kho'mhi kisikā gilānā bāḷhadubbalā,||
Daṇaḍam olubbha gacchāmi pabbataɱ abhirūhiya.|| ||

[28] Saŋghāṭiɱ nikkhipitvāna pattakaɱ ca nikujjiya,||
Sele khambhesiɱ attānaɱ tamok-khandhaɱ padāliyā.|| ||

 

24
Mettikā-Therī Gāthā

[pts]

Itthaɱ sudaɱ mettikā therī gāthāyo abhāsitthāti

[29] Kiñ cāpi kho'mhi dukkhitā dubbalā gatayobbanā,||
Daṇaḍam olubbha gacchāmi pabbataɱ abhirūhiya.|| ||

[30] Nikkhipitvāna saɱghāṭiɱ pattakaɱ ca nikujjiya||
Nisinnā c'amhi selamhi atha cittaɱ vimucci me,||
Tisso vijjā anuppattā kataɱ Buddhassa sāsanaɱ.|| ||

 

25
Mettā-Therī Gāthā

[pts]

Itthaɱ sudaɱ mettā therī gāthāyo abhāsitthāti

[31] Cātuddasī pañcadasī yā ca pakkhassa aṭṭhamī
Pārihārikapakkhañ ca aṭṭh'aŋgasu-samāgataɱ,||
[127] Uposathaɱ upāgañchiɱ deva-kāyābhinandinī.|| ||

[32] Sājja ekena bhattena muṇḍā saɱghāṭipārutā||
Devakāyaɱ na patthe'haɱ vineyya hadaye daraɱ.|| ||

 

26
Abhaya-mātā-Therī Gāthā

[pts]

[33] Uddhaɱ pādatalā amma adho ce kesa-matthakā,||
Paccavekkhassu'maɱ kāyaɱ asuciɱ pūtigandhikaɱ.|| ||

[34] Evaɱ vihāramānāya sabbo rāgo samūhato,||
Pariḷāho samucchinno sītibhūtā'mhi nibbutā.|| ||

 

27

Aññatarā Sāmā-Therī Gāthā

[pts]

[445] Atha soḷasame vasse disvāna maɱ patta yobbanaɱ,||

[446] Tassa pi aññā bhariyā sīlavatī guṇavatī yasavatī ca,||

Dāsī va upaṭṭhahantiɱ tassa pi anto kato mayā ti.|| ||
