1. Yamaka Vagga

Index Than

 

1. Mano-pubbaŋ-gamā dhammā mano-seṭṭhā mano-mayā,||
Manasā ce paduṭṭhena bhāsati vā karoti vā||
Tato naɱ dukkham anveti cakkaɱ va vahato padaɱ.|| ||

2. Mano-pubbaŋ-gamā dhammā mano-seṭṭhā mano-mayā,||
Manasā ce pasannena bhāsati vā karoti vā||
Tato naɱ sukham anveti chāyā va anapāyinī.|| ||

3. "Akkocchi maɱ avadhi maɱ ajini maɱ ahāsi me"||
Ye taɱ upanayhanti veraɱ tesaɱ na sammati.|| ||

[2] 4. "Akkocchi maɱ avadhi maɱ ajini maɱ ahāsi me"||
Ye taɱ na upanayhanti veraɱ tes'ūpasammati.|| ||

5. Na hi verena verāni sammant'idha kudācana||
Averena ca sammanti, esa dhammo sanantano.|| ||

6. Pare ca na vijānanti: "mayam ettha yamāmase"||
Ye ca tattha vijānanti tato sammanti medhagā.|| ||

7. Subhānupassiɱ viharantaɱ indriyesu asaɱvutaɱ||
Bhojanamhi cāmattaññuɱ kusītaɱ hīna-vīriyaɱ||
Taɱ ve pasahati Māro vāto rukkhaɱ va dubbalaɱ.|| ||

[3] 8. Asubhānupassiɱ viharantaɱ indriyesu susaɱ-vutaɱ||
Bhojanamhi ca mattaññuɱ saddhaɱ āraddha-vīriyaɱ||
Taɱ ve na-ppasahatī Māro vāto selaɱ va pabbataɱ.|| ||

9. Anikkasāvo kāsāvaɱ yo vatthaɱ paridahessati||
Apeto damasaccena na so kāsāvam arahati.|| ||

10. Yo ca vantakasāv'assa sīlesu susamāhito||
Upeto damasaccena sa ve kāsāvam arahati.|| ||

11. Asāre sāramatino sāre cāsāradassino||
Te sāraɱ nādhi-gacchanti micchā-saɱkappa-gocarā.|| ||

12. Sārañ ca sārato ñatvā asārañ ca asārato||
Te sāraɱ adhigacchanti sammā-saɱkappa-gocarā.|| ||

[4] 13. Yathā agāraɱ ducchannaɱ vuṭṭhi samativijjhati||
Evaɱ abhāvitaɱ cittaɱ rāgo samativijjhati.|| ||

14. Yathā agāraɱ succhannaɱ vuṭṭhi na samativijjhati||
Evaɱ subhāvitaɱ cittaɱ rāgo na samativijjhati.|| ||

15. Idha socati pecca socati||
pāpakārī ubhayattha socati||
So socati so vihaññati||
disvā kamma kiliṭṭham attano.|| ||

16. Idha modati pecca modati||
katapuñño ubhayattha modati||
So modati so pamodati||
disvā kamma visuddhim attano.|| ||

[5] 17. Idha tappati pecca tappati||
pāpakārī ubhayattha tappati||
"Pāpaɱ me katan" ti tappati||
bhiyyo tappati duggatiɱ gato.|| ||

18. Idha nandati pecca nandati||
katapuñño ubhayattha nandati,||
"Puññ me katan" ti nandati.||
bhiyyo nandati suggatiɱ gato.|| ||

19. Bahum pi ce sahitaɱ bhāsa-māno||
Na takkaro hoti naro pamatto||
Gopo va gāvo gaṇayaɱ paresaɱ||
Na bhāgavā sāmaññassa hoti.|| ||

[6] 20. Appam pi ce sahitaɱ bhāsa-māno||
Dhammassa hoti anuDhamma-cārī||
Rāgañ ca dosañ ca pahāya mohaɱ||
Sammappajāno su-vimutta-citto||
Anupādiyāno idha vā huraɱ vā||
Sa bhāgavā sāmaññassa hoti.|| ||

2. Appamāda Vagga

Index Than

23. Te jhāyino sātatikā niccaɱ daḷha-parakkamā||

24. Uṭṭhānavato satimato||
sucikammassa nisammakārino||

appamattassa yaso'bhivaḍḍhati.|| ||

[8] 25. Uṭṭhānen'appamādena saññamena damena ca||
Dīpaɱ kayirātha medhāvī yaɱ ogho nābhikīrati.|| ||

26. Pamādam anuyuñjanti||
bālā dummedhino janā||
Appamādañ ca medhāvi||
dhanaɱ seṭṭhaɱ va rakkhati.|| ||

27. Mā pamādam anuyuñjetha mā kāmarati-santhavaɱ||
Appamatto hi jhāyanto pappoti vipulaɱ sukhaɱ.|| ||

28. Pamādaɱ appamādena yadā nudati paṇḍito||
Paññāpāsādam āruyha asoko sokiniɱ pajaɱ||
Pabbataṭṭho va bhummaṭṭhe dhīro bāle avekkhati.|| ||

[9] 29. Appamatto pamattesu suttesu bahujāgaro||
Abalassaɱ va sīghasso hitvā yāti sumedhaso.|| ||

30. Appamādena Maghavā devānaɱ seṭṭhataɱ gato,||
Appamādaɱ pasaɱ-santi pamādo garahito sadā.|| ||

31. Appamādarato bhikkhu pamāde bhaya-dassivā||
Saŋyojanaɱ aṇuɱ thūlaɱ ḍahaɱ aggī va gacchati.|| ||

32. Appamādarato bhikkhu pamāde bhaya-dassivā||
Abhabbo parihāṇāya nibbāṇass'eva santike.|| ||

5. Bāla Vagga
