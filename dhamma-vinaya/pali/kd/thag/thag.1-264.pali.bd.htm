Adapted from the 1995 edition of the digital version of the Sri Lanka Buddha Jayanti Tripitaka Series

 

 

Namo tassa bhagavato arahato Sammā-Sam-Buddhassa

Nidānagāthā

Sīhānaɱ'va nadantānaɱ dāṭhīnaɱ girigabbhare||
Suṇātha bhāvit'attānaɱ gāthā attūpanāyikā|| ||

Yathānāmā yathāgottā yathā-dhamma-vihārino||
Yathādhimuttā sappaññā vihariɱsu atanditā|| ||

Tattha tattha vipassitvā phusitvā accutaɱ padaɱ||
Katantaɱ pacc-a-vekkhantā imamatthamabhāsisuɱ.|| ||
