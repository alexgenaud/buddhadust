Sutta 1

Lobha Suttaɱ

[1.1][than] Vuttaɱ h'etaɱ bhagavatā vuttam-arahatā ti me sutaɱ.|| ||

1. Eka dhammaɱ bhikkhave pajahatha ahaɱ vo pāṭibhogo Anāgāmitāya.|| ||

Sutta 18

[18.1][than] Vuttaɱ h'etaɱ bhagavatā vuttam-arahatā ti me sutaɱ:|| ||

Eka dhammo bhikkhave loke uppajja- [11] māno uppajjati bahu-janā-hitāya bahu-janā-sukhāya bahuno janassa anatthāya ahitāya dukkhāya deva-manussānaɱ.|| ||

Saŋghabhedo.|| ||

Saŋghe kho pana bhikkhave bhinne||
aññam-aññaɱ bhaṇḍanāni c'eva honti,||
aññam-aññaɱ paribhāsā ca honti,||

Tatth'etaɱ iti vuccati:|| ||

18.2. "Āpāyiko nerayiko||
kappaṭṭho Saŋgha-bhedako,||
Vaggārāmo adhammaṭṭho||
yoga-k-khemato dhaɱsati,||
Saŋghaɱ samaggaɱ bhetvāna||
kappaɱ Nirayamhi paccatī' ti.|| ||

Ayam pi attho vutto bhagavatā iti me sutan ti.|| ||
