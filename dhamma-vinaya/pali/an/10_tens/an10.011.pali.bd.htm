Aŋguttara Nikāya
X. Dasaka-Nipāta
II. Nātha Vagga

Sutta 11

Sen'āsana Suttaɱ

Adapted from the 1995 edition of the digital version of the Sri Lanka Buddha Jayanti Tripitaka Series.

 

[15]

[1][pts][bodh] Evaɱ me sutaɱ:|| ||

Ekaɱ samayaɱ Bhagavā Sāvatthiyaɱ viharati.|| ||

Tatra kho Bhagavā bhikkhū āmantesi:|| ||

"Bhikkhavo" ti.|| ||

"Bhadante" ti te bhikkhū Bhagavato paccassosuɱ.|| ||

Bhagavā etad avoca:|| ||

2. "Pañc'aŋga-samannāgato bhikkhave||
bhikkhu pañc'aŋga-samannāgataɱ sen'āsanaɱ seva-māno bhajamāno||
na cirass'eva āsavānaɱ khayā||
anāsavaɱ ceto-vimuttiɱ paññā-vimuttiɱ||
diṭiṭh'eva dhamme sayaɱ abhiññā sacchi-katvā upasampajja vihareyya.|| ||

 

§

 

2. Kathañ ca bhikkhave bhikkhu pañc'aŋga-samannāgato hoti?|| ||

Idha, bhikkhave, bhikkhu saddho hoti,||
sadda-hati Tathāgatassa bodhiɱ:|| ||

'Iti pi so Bhagavā arahaɱ sammā samiBuddho vijjā-caraṇa sampanno Sugato loka-vidū anuttaro purisa-dhamma-sārathi Satthā deva-manussānaɱ Buddho Bhagavā' ti.|| ||

■

Appābādho hoti appātaŋiko samavepākiniyā gahaṇiyā samannāgato nātisītāya nāccuṇhāya majjhamāya padhānakkhamāya.|| ||

■

Asaṭho hoti amāyāvī,||
yathā-bhūtaɱ attānaɱ āvīkattā Satthari vā viññūsu vā sabrahma-cārīsu.|| ||

■

Āraddha-viriyo viharati||
akusalānaɱ dhammānaɱ pahānāya||
kusalānaɱ dhammānaɱ upasamipadāya||
thāmavā daḷha-parakkamo||
anikkhitta-dhuro kusalesu dhammesu.|| ||

■

Paññavā hoti||
uday'attha-gāminiyā paññāya||
samannāgato ariyāya nibbedhi-kāya||
sammā-dukkha-k-khaya-gāminiyā.|| ||

Evaɱ kho bhikkhave bhikkhu pañc'aŋga-samannāgato hoti.|| ||

 

§

 

3. Kathañ ca bhikkhave sen'āsanaɱ pañc'aŋga-samannāgataɱ hoti?|| ||

Idha, bhikkhave, sen'āsanaɱ nātidūraɱ hoti nāccāsannaɱ gaman-ā-gamana-sami-pannaɱ divā appakiṇṇaɱ rattiɱ appa-saddaɱ appa-nigghosaɱ,||
appaḍaɱsa-makasa-vāt'ātapa-siriɱsapa-samphassaɱ.|| ||

Tasmiɱ kho pana sen'āsane viharantassa appakasiran'eva uppajjanti cīvara-piṇḍa-pāta-sen'āsana-gilāna-paccaya-bhesajja-parikkhāraɱ.|| ||

Tasmiɱ kho pana sen'āsane therā bhikkhu viharanti bahu-s-sutā āgatāgamā [16] dhamma-dharā vinaya-dharā mātikā-dharā,||
te kālena kālaɱ upasaŋikamitvā paripucichati paripañhati:|| ||

"Idam bhante kathaɱ,||
imassa ko attho" ti?|| ||

Tassa te āyasmanto avivaṭañ c'eva vivaranti,||
anuttānīkatañ ca uttānīɱ karonti.|| ||

Aneka-vihitesu ca kaŋkhāṭṭhānīyesu dhammesu kaŋikhaɱ paṭivinodenti.|| ||

Evaɱ kho bhikkhave sen'āsanaɱ pañc'aŋga-samannāgataɱ hoti.|| ||

Pañc'aŋga-samannāgato kho bhikkhave bhikkhu pañc'aŋga-samannāgataɱ sen'āsanaɱ seva-māno bhajamāno na cirass'eva āsavānaɱ khayā anāsavaɱ ceto-vimuttaɱ paññā-vimuttiɱ diṭṭhe'va dhamme sayaɱ abhiññā sacchi-katvā upasampajja vihareyyā" ti.|| ||
