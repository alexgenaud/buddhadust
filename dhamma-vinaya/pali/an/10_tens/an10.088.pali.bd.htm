Aŋguttara Nikāya
X. Dasaka-Nipāta
IX: Thera-Vagga

Sutta 88

Akkosaka Suttaɱ

Adapted from the 1995 edition of the digital version of the Sri Lanka Buddha Jayanti Tripitaka Series.

 

[169]

[1][pts] Evaɱ me sutaɱ:|| ||

Ekaɱ samayaɱ Bhagavā Sāvatthiyaɱ viharati.|| ||

Tatra kho Bhagavā bhikkhū āmantesi:|| ||

"Bhikkhavo" ti.|| ||

"Bhadante" ti te bhikkhū Bhagavato paccassosuɱ.|| ||

Bhagavā etad avoca:|| ||

2. "Yo so bhikkhave bhikkhu akkosaka paribhāsako ariy'ūpavādī sabrahma-cārīnaɱ,||
aṭṭhānam etaɱ anavakāso,||
yaɱ so dasannaɱ vyasanānaɱ aññataraɱ vyasanaɱ na niga-c-cheyya.|| ||

Katamesaɱ dasannaɱ?|| ||

3. [1] Anadhigataɱ nādhigacchati,||

■

[2] adhigatā parihāyati,||

■

[3] Sad'Dhammassa na vodāyati||

■

[4] Sad'Dhammesu vā adhimāniko hoti,||

■

[5] anabhirato vā Brahma-cariyaɱ carati,||

■

[6] aññataraɱ vā saŋkiliṭṭhaɱ āpattiɱ āpajjati,||

■

[7] gāḷhaɱ vā rog'ātaŋkaɱ phusati,||

■

[8] ummādaɱ vā pāpuṇāti cittakkhepaɱ,||

■

[9] sammūḷho kālaɱ karoti,||

■

[10] kāyassa bhedā param maraṇā apāyaɱ,||

duggatiɱ vinipātaɱ Nirayaɱ uppajjati.|| ||

Yo so bhikkhave bhikkhu akkosaparibhāsako ariy'ūpavādī sabrahma-cārīnaɱ,||
aṭṭhānam etaɱ avakāso,||
yaɱ so imesaɱ dasannaɱ vyasanānaɱ aññataraɱ vyasanaɱ na nigaccheyyā" ti.
