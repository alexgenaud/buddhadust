Aŋguttara Nikāya
X. Dasaka-Nipāta
V. Akkosa Vagga

Sutta 48

Dasa-Dhamma Suttaɱ

Adapted from the 1995 edition of the digital version of the Sri Lanka Buddha Jayanti Tripitaka Series.

 

[87]

[1][pts][than][piya][olds] Evaɱ me sutaɱ:|| ||

Ekaɱ samayaɱ Bhagavā Sāvatthiyaɱ viharati.|| ||

Tatra kho Bhagavā bhikkhū āmantesi:|| ||

"Bhikkhavo" ti.|| ||

"Bhadante" ti te bhikkhū Bhagavato paccassosuɱ.|| ||

Bhagavā etad avoca:|| ||

2. "Dasa yime bhikkhave dhammā||
pabba-jitena abhiṇhaɱ pacc-a-vekkhītabbā.|| ||

Katame dasa?|| ||

3. 'Vevaṇṇiyamhi ajjhūpagato' ti.|| ||

Pabba-jitena abhiṇhaɱ pacc-a-vekkhitabbaɱ.|| ||

■

4. 'Parapaṭibaddhā me jīvikā' ti.|| ||

Pabba- [88] jitena abhiṇhaɱ pacc-a-vekkhitabbaɱ.|| ||

■

5. 'Añño me ākappo karaṇīyo' ti.|| ||

Pabba-jitena abhiṇhaɱ pacc-a-vekkhitabbaɱ.|| ||

■

6. 'Kacci nu kho me attā sīlato na upavadatī' ti?|| ||

Pabba-jitena abhiṇhaɱ pacc-a-vekkhitabbaɱ.|| ||

■

7. 'Kacci nu kho maɱ anuvicca viññū sabrahma-cārī sīlato na upavadantī' ti?|| ||

Pabba-jitena abhiṇhaɱ pacc-a-vekkhitabbaɱ.|| ||

■

8. 'Sabbehi me piyehi manāpehi nānābhāvo vinābhāvo' ti.|| ||

Pabba-jitena abhiṇhaɱ pacc-a-vekkhitabbaɱ.|| ||

■

9. 'Kamma-s-sakho'mhi||
kamma-dāyādo||
kamma-yoni||
kamma-bandhu||
kamma-paṭisaraṇo,||
yaɱ kammaɱ karissāmi kalyāṇaɱ vā||
pāpakaɱ vā||
tassa dāyādo bhavissāmī' ti.|| ||

Pabba-jitena abhiṇhaɱ pacc-a-vekkhitabbaɱ.|| ||

■

10. 'Kathaɱ bhūtassa me rattin-divā vītipatantī' ti?|| ||

Pabba-jitena abhiṇhaɱ pacc-a-vekkhitabbaɱ.|| ||

■

11. 'Kacci nu kho'haɱ suññāgāre abhiramāmī' ti?|| ||

Pabba-jitena abhiṇhaɱ pacc-a-vekkhitabbaɱ.|| ||

■

12. 'Atthi nu kho me uttarimanussa-dhammā alam-ariya-ñāṇa-dassana viseso adhigato,||
so'haɱ pacchime kāle sabrahma-cārīhi puṭṭho na maŋku bhavissāmī' ti?|| ||

Pabba-jitena abhiṇhaɱ pacc-a-vekkhitabbaɱ.|| ||

Ime kho bhikkhave dasa dhammā||
pabba-jitena abhiṇhaɱ pacc-a-vekkhitabbā" ti.|| ||
