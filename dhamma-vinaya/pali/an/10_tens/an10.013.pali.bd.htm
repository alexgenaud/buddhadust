Aṇguttara Nikāya
X. Dasaka-Nipāta
II. Nātha Vagga

Sutta 13

Saṇyojana Suttaṃ

Adapted from the 1995 edition of the digital version of the Sri Lanka Buddha Jayanti Tripitaka Series.

 

[17]

[1][pts][than][bodh] Evaṃ me sutaṃ:|| ||

Ekaṃ samayaṃ Bhagavā Sāvatthiyaṃ viharati.|| ||

Tatra kho Bhagavā bhikkhū āmantesi:|| ||

"Bhikkhavo" ti.|| ||

"Bhadante" ti te bhikkhū Bhagavato paccassosuṃ.|| ||

Bhagavā etad avoca:|| ||

Dasa yimāni gakkhave saṃyojanāni,||
katamāni dasa?|| ||

Pañc'oramibhāgiyāni saṃyojanāni,||
pañc'uddha-m-bhāgiyāni saṃyojanāni.|| ||

Katamāni pañc'oram-bhāgiyāni saṃyojanāni?|| ||

Sakkāya-diṭiṭhiṃ,||
vicikicichā||
sīla-b-bata-parāmāso,||
kāma-c-chando,||
vyāpādo.|| ||

Imāni pañc'oram-bhāgiya saṃyojanāni.|| ||

 

§

 

Katamāni pañc'uddha-m-bhāgiyāni saṃyojanāni?|| ||

Rūpa-rāgo,||
arūpa-rāgo,||
māno,||
uddhaccaṃ,||
avijjā.|| ||

Imāni kho pañc'uddha-m-bhāgiyāni saṃyojanāni.|| ||

Imāni kho bhikkhave dasa saṃyojanānī" ti.|| ||
