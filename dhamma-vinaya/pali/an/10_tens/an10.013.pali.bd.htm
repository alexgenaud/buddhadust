Aŋguttara Nikāya
X. Dasaka-Nipāta
II. Nātha Vagga

Sutta 13

Saŋyojana Suttaɱ

Adapted from the 1995 edition of the digital version of the Sri Lanka Buddha Jayanti Tripitaka Series.

 

[17]

[1][pts][than][bodh] Evaɱ me sutaɱ:|| ||

Ekaɱ samayaɱ Bhagavā Sāvatthiyaɱ viharati.|| ||

Tatra kho Bhagavā bhikkhū āmantesi:|| ||

"Bhikkhavo" ti.|| ||

"Bhadante" ti te bhikkhū Bhagavato paccassosuɱ.|| ||

Bhagavā etad avoca:|| ||

Dasa yimāni gakkhave saŋyojanāni,||
katamāni dasa?|| ||

Pañc'oramibhāgiyāni saŋyojanāni,||
pañc'uddha-m-bhāgiyāni saŋyojanāni.|| ||

Katamāni pañc'ora-m-bhāgiyāni saŋyojanāni?|| ||

Sakkāya-diṭiṭhiɱ,||
vicikicichā||
sīl'abbata-parāmāso,||
kāma-c-chando,||
vyāpādo.|| ||

Imāni pañc'ora-m-bhāgiya saŋyojanāni.|| ||

 

§

 

Katamāni pañc'uddha-m-bhāgiyāni saŋyojanāni?|| ||

Rūpa-rāgo,||
arūpa-rāgo,||
māno,||
uddhaccaɱ,||
avijjā.|| ||

Imāni kho pañc'uddha-m-bhāgiyāni saŋyojanāni.|| ||

Imāni kho bhikkhave dasa saŋyojanānī" ti.|| ||
