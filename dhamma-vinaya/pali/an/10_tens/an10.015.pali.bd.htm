Aŋguttara Nikāya
X. Dasaka-Nipāta
II. Nātha Vagga

Sutta 15

Appamāda Suttaɱ

Adapted from the 1995 edition of the digital version of the Sri Lanka Buddha Jayanti Tripitaka Series.

 

[21]

[1][pts][than][olds][bodh] Evaɱ me sutaɱ:|| ||

Yāvatā bhikkhave sattā apadā vā||
dipadā vā||
catu-p-padā vā||
bahu-p-padā vā||
rūpino vā arūpino vā||
saññino vā asaññino vā||
n'eva-saññī-nāsaññino vā,||
Tathāgato tesaɱ aggam akkhāyati arahaɱ Sammā-Sam-Buddho.|| ||

Evam eva kho bhikkhave ye keci kusalā dhammā,||
sabbe te appamāda-mūlakā||
appamāda-samo-saraṇā,||
appamādo tesaɱ dhammānaɱ aggam akkhāyati.|| ||

■

2. Seyyathā pi, bhikkhave, yāni kānici jaŋgamānaɱ pāṇānaɱ pada-jātāni,||
sabbāni tāni hatthi-pade samodhānaɱ gacichanti,||
hatthi-padaɱ tesaɱ aggam akkhāyati,||
yad idaɱ mahantattena.|| ||

Evam eva kho, bhikkhave, ye keci kusalā dhammā||
sabbe te appamāda-mūlakā||
appamāda-samo-saraṇā||
appamādo tesaɱ dhammānaɱ aggam akkhāyati.|| ||

■

3. Seyyathā pi, bhikkhave, kūṭā-gārassa yā kāci gopānasiyo sabbā tā kūṭa-ḍigamā kūṭa-ninnā kūṭa-samo-saraṇā,||
kūṭo tāsaɱ aggam akkhāyati.|| ||

Evam eva kho, bhikkhave, ye keci kusalā dhammā||
sabbe te appamāda-mūlakā||
appamāda-samo-saraṇā||
appamādo tesaɱ dhammānaɱ aggam akkhāyati.|| ||

■

[22] 4. Seyyathā pi, bhikkhave, ye keci mūla-gandhā,||
kā'ānusāriyaɱ tesaɱ aggam akkhāyati.|| ||

Evam eva kho, bhikkhave, ye keci kusalā dhammā||
sabbe te appamāda-mūlakā||
appamāda-samo-saraṇā||
appamādo tesaɱ dhammānaɱ aggam akkhāyati.|| ||

■

5. Seyyathā pi, bhikkhave, ye keci sāra-gandhā lohita-candanaɱ tesaɱ aggam akkhāyati.|| ||

Evam eva kho, bhikkhave, ye keci kusalā dhammā||
sabbe te appamāda-mūlakā||
appamāda-samo-saraṇā||
appamādo tesaɱ dhammānaɱ aggam akkhāyati.|| ||

■

6. Seyyathā pi, bhikkhave, ye keci puppha-gandhā,||
vassikaɱ tesaɱ aggam akkhāyati.|| ||

Evam eva kho, bhikkhave, ye keci kusalā dhammā||
sabbe te appamāda-mūlakā||
appamāda-samo-saraṇā||
appamādo tesaɱ dhammānaɱ aggam akkhāyati.|| ||

■

7. Seyyathā pi, bhikkhave, ye keci kuḍḍa-rājāno,||
sabbe te rañño cakka-vattissa anuyantā bhavanti,||
rājā tesaɱ cakka-vatti aggam akkhāyati.|| ||

Evam eva kho, bhikkhave, ye keci kusalā dhammā||
sabbe te appamāda-mūlakā||
appamāda-samo-saraṇā||
appamādo tesaɱ dhammānaɱ aggam akkhāyati.|| ||

■

8. Seyyathā pi, bhikkhave, yā kāci tāraka-rūpānaɱ pabhā,||
sabba tā canda-p-pabhāya kalaɱ nāgghanti soḷasiɱ canda-p-pabhā tāsaɱ aggam akkhāyati.|| ||

Evam eva kho, bhikkhave, ye keci kusalā dhammā||
sabbe te appamāda-mūlakā||
appamāda-samo-saraṇā||
appamādo tesaɱ dhammānaɱ aggam akkhāyati.|| ||

■

9. Seyyathā pi, bhikkhave, sarada-samaye viddho vigata-valāhake deve ādicco nahaɱ abbhussu-k-kamāno sabbaɱ ākāsagataɱ tamagataɱ abhivihacca bhāsate ca tapate ca virocati ca.|| ||

Evam eva kho, bhikkhave, ye keci kusalā dhammā||
sabbe te appamāda-mūlakā||
appamāda-samo-saraṇā||
appamādo tesaɱ dhammānaɱ aggam akkhāyati.|| ||

■

10. Seyyathā pi, bhikkhave, yā kāci mahā-nadiyo, seyyathīdaɱ:||
Gaŋgā,||
Yamunā||
Aciravatī,||
Sarabhū||
Mahī,||
sabbā tā samudd'aŋgamā samudda-ninnā samudda-ponā samudda-pabbhārā,||
mahā-samuddo tesaɱ aggam akkhāyati.|| ||

Evam eva kho, bhikkhave, ye keci kusalā dhammā||
sabbe te appamāda-mūlakā||
appamāda-samo-saraṇā||
appamādo tesaɱ dhammānaɱ aggam akkhāyati" ti.|| ||
