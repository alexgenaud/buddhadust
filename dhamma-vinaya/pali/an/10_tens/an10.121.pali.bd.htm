Aŋguttara Nikāya
X. Dasaka-Nipāta
XII: Paccorohaṇī-Vagga

Sutta 121

Pubbaŋgama Suttaɱ

Adapted from the 1995 edition of the digital version of the Sri Lanka Buddha Jayanti Tripitaka Series.

 

[236]

[1][pts] Evaɱ me sutaɱ:|| ||

Ekaɱ samayaɱ Bhagavā Sāvatthiyaɱ viharati.|| ||

Tatra kho Bhagavā bhikkhū āmantesi:|| ||

"Bhikkhavo" ti.|| ||

"Bhadante" ti te bhikkhū Bhagavato paccassosuɱ.|| ||

Bhagavā etad avoca:|| ||

2. "Suriyassa bhikkhave udayato etaɱ pubbaŋ-gamaɱ etaɱ pubba-nimittaɱ yad idaɱ aruṇaggaɱ.|| ||

Evam eva kho bhikkhave kusalānaɱ dhammānaɱ etaɱ pubbaŋ-gamaɱ,||
etaɱ pubba-nimittaɱ,||
yad idaɱ sammā-diṭṭhi.|| ||

 

§

 

Sammā-diṭṭhi'ssa bhikkhave sammā saŋkappo pahoti.|| ||

■

Sammā-saŋkappassa sammā-vācā pahoti.|| ||

■

Sammā-vāc'assa sammā-kammanto pahoti.|| ||

■

Sammā-kammantassa sammā-ājīvo pahoti.|| ||

■

Sammā-ājīvassa sammā-vāyāmo pahoti.|| ||

■

Sammā-vāyāmassa sammā-sati pahoti.|| ||

■

Sammā-satissa sammā-samādhi [237] pahoti.|| ||

■

Sammā-samādhissa sammā-ñāṇaɱ pahoti.|| ||

■

Sammā-ñāṇissa sammā-vimutti pahotī ti.|| ||
