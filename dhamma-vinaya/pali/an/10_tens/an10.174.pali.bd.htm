Aŋguttara Nikāya
X. Dasaka-Nipāta
XVII: Jāṇussoṇi-Vagga

Sutta 174

Kamma-Nidāna Suttaɱ

Adapted from the 1995 edition of the digital version of the Sri Lanka Buddha Jayanti Tripitaka Series.

 

 

[1][pts][olds] Evaɱ me sutaɱ:|| ||

Ekaɱ samayaɱ Bhagavā bhikkhū āmantesi:|| ||

"Bhikkhavo" ti.|| ||

"Bhadante" ti te bhikkhū Bhagavato paccassosuɱ.|| ||

Bhagavā etad avoca:|| ||

2. "Pāṇ-ā-tipātam p'ahaɱ bhikkhave tividhaɱ vadāmi||
lobha-hetukam pi||
dosa-hetukam pi||
moha-hetukam pi.|| ||

■

Adinn'ādānam p'ahaɱ bhikkhave kividhaɱ vadāmi||
lobha-hetukam pi||
dosa-hetukam pi||
moha-hetukam pi.|| ||

■

Kāmesu micchā-cāram p'ahaɱ bhikkhave tividhaɱ vadāmi||
lobha-hetukam pi||
dosa-hetukam pi||
moha-hetukam pi.|| ||

■

Musā-vādam p'ahaɱ bhikkhave tividhaɱ vadāmi||
lobha-hetukam pi||
dosa-hetukam pi||
moha-hetukam pi.|| ||

■

Pisuṇaɱ-vācam p'ahaɱ bhikkhave tividhaɱ vadāmi||
lobha-hetukam pi||
dosa-hetukam pi||
moha-hetukam pi.|| ||

■

Pharusaɱ-vācam p'ahaɱ bhikkhave tividhaɱ vadāmi||
lobha-hetukam pi||
dosa-hetukam pi||
moha-hetukam pi.|| ||

■

Sampha-p-palāpam p'ahaɱ bhikkhave tividhaɱ vadāmi||
lobha-hetukam pi||
dosa-hetukam pi||
moha-hetukam pi.|| ||

■

Abhijjham p'ahaɱ [262] bhikkhave tividhaɱ vadāmi||
lobha-hetukam pi||
dosa-hetukam pi||
moha-hetukam pi.|| ||

■

Vyāpādam p'ahaɱ bhikkhave tividhaɱ vadāmi||
lobha-hetukam pi||
dosa-hetukam pi||
moha-hetukam pi.|| ||

■

Micchā-diṭṭhim p'ahaɱ bhikkhave tividhaɱ vadāmi||
lobha-hetukam pi||
dosa-hetukam pi||
moha-hetukam pi.|| ||

■

Iti kho bhikkhave||
lobho kamma-nidāna sambhavo,||
doso kamma-nidāna sambhavo,||
moho kamma-nidāna sambhavo.|| ||

Lobha-k-khayā kamma-nidāna-saŋkhayo,||
dosa-k-khayā kamma-nidāna-saŋkhayo,||
moha-k-khayā kamma-nidāna-saŋkhayo" ti.|| ||
