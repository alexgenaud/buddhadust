Aŋguttara Nikāya
X. Dasaka-Nipāta
II. Nātha Vagga

Sutta 19

Paṭhama Āriya-Vāsa Suttaɱ

Adapted from the 1995 edition of the digital version of the Sri Lanka Buddha Jayanti Tripitaka Series.

 

[29]

[1][pts][bodh] Evaɱ me sutaɱ:|| ||

Ekaɱ samayaɱ Bhagavā Sāvatthiyaɱ viharati
Jetavane Anāthapiṇḍikassa ārāme.|| ||

Tatra kho Bhagavā bhikkhū āmantesi:|| ||

"Bhikkhavo" ti.|| ||

"Bhadante" ti te bhikkhū Bhagavato paccassosuɱ.|| ||

Bhagavā etad avoca:|| ||

2. "Dasa ime bhikkhave ariya-vāsā ye ariyā||
āvasiɱsu vā||
āvasanti vā||
āvasissanti vā.|| ||

Katame dasa?|| ||

Idha, bhikkhave, bhikkhu pañc'aŋga-vi-p-pahīno hoti,||
chaḷ-aŋga-samannā-gato,||
ek'ārakkho,||
catur'āpasseno,||
panuṇṇa-pacc'eka-sacco,||
sama-vaya-saṭṭh'esano,||
anāvila-saŋkappo,||
pasasaddha-kāya-sankhāro,||
su-vimutta-citto,||
su-vimutta-pañño.|| ||

Ime kho bhikkhave dasa ariya-vāsā ye ariyā||
āvasiɱsu vā||
āvasanti vā||
āvasissanti vā" ti.|| ||
