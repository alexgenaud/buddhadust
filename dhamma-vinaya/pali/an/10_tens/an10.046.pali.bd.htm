Aŋguttara Nikāya
X. Dasaka-Nipāta
V. Akkosa Vagga

Sutta 46

Sakya Suttaɱ

Adapted from the 1995 edition of the digital version of the Sri Lanka Buddha Jayanti Tripitaka Series.

 

[83]

[1][pts][than] Evaɱ me sutaɱ:|| ||

Ekaɱ samayaɱ Bhagavā Sakkesu viharati Kapilavatthusmiɱ Nigrodhārāme.|| ||

Atha kho sambahulā Sakkā upāsakā tadah'uposathe yena Bhagavā ten'upasankamiɱsu.|| ||

Upasaŋkamitvā Bhagavantaɱ abhivādetvā eka-m-antaɱ nisīdiɱsu.|| ||

Eka-m-antaɱ nisinne kho Sakke upāsake Bhagavā etad avoca:|| ||

2. "Api nu kho tumhe Sakkā aṭṭh'aŋga-samannāgataɱ uposathaɱ upavasathā" ti?|| ||

"App-ekadā mayaɱ bhante aṭṭh'aŋga-samannāgataɱ uposathaɱ upavasāma,||
app-ekadā na upavasāmā" ti.|| ||

"Tesaɱ vo Sakkā alābhā tesaɱ dulladdhaɱ,||
ye tumhe evaɱ soka-sabhaye jīvite maraṇa-sabhaye jīvite app-ekadā aṭṭh'aŋga-samannāgataɱ uposathaɱ upavasatha,||
app-ekadā na upavasatha.|| ||

 

§

 

"Taɱ kiɱ maññatha Sakkā?

Idha puriso yena kenaci kamma-ṭ-ṭhānena anāpajja akusalaɱ divasaɱ aḍḍha-kahāpaṇe nibbiseyya,||
[84] 'Dakkho puriso uṭṭhāna-sampanno' ti alaɱ vacanāyā" ti?|| ||

"Evaɱ bhante."|| ||

■

"Taɱ kiɱ maññatha Sakkā?"|| ||

"Idha puriso yena kenaci kamma-ṭ-ṭhānena anāpajja akusalaɱ divasaɱ kahāpaṇaɱ nibbiseyya,||
'Dakkho puriso uṭṭhāna-sampanno' ti alaɱ vacanāyā" ti?|| ||

"Evaɱ bhante" ti.|| ||

■

"Taɱ kiɱ maññatha Sakkā?

Idha puriso yena kenaci kamma-ṭ-ṭhānena anāpajja akusalaɱ divasaɱ dve kahāpaṇe nibbiseyya,||
'Dakkho puriso uṭṭhāna-sampanno' ti alaɱ vacanāyā" ti?|| ||

"Evaɱ bhante" ti.|| ||

■

"Taɱ kiɱ maññatha Sakkā?

"Evaɱ bhante" ti.|| ||

"Evaɱ bhante" ti.|| ||

■

"Taɱ kim maññatha Sakkā?|| ||

Api nu so puriso divase divase kahāpaṇa-sataɱ||
kahāpaṇa-sahassaɱ||
nibbisamāno laddhaɱ laddhaɱ nikkhipanto||
vassa-tāyuko||
vassa-sata-jīvī||
mahantaɱ bhoga-k-khandhaɱ adhigaccheyyā" ti?|| ||

"Evaɱ bhante" ti.|| ||

 

§

 

"Taɱ kiɱ maññatha Sakkā?|| ||

Api nu so puriso bhoga-hetu bhoga-nidānaɱ bhog-ā-dhikaraṇaɱ||
ekaɱ vā rattiɱ||
ekaɱ vā divasaɱ||
upaḍḍhaɱ vā rattiɱ||
upaḍḍhaɱ vā divasaɱ||
ekanta-sukha-paṭisaŋvedī vihareyyā" ti?|| ||

"No h'etaɱ bhante.|| ||

Taɱ kissa hetu?|| ||

Kāmā hi bhante aniccā tucchā musā mosa-dhammā" ti.|| ||
