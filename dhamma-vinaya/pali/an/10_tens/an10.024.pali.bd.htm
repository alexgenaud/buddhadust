Aŋguttara Nikāya
X. Dasaka-Nipāta
III. Mahā Vagga

Sutta 24

Mahā Cunda Suttaɱ

Adapted from the 1995 edition of the digital version of the Sri Lanka Buddha Jayanti Tripitaka Series.

 

[41]

[1][pts][than][bodh] Evaɱ me sutaɱ:|| ||

Ekaɱ samayaɱ āyasmā Mahā Cundo Cetīsu viharati Sahajātiyaɱ.|| ||

Tatra kho āyasmā Mahā Cundo bhikkhu āmantesi|| ||

"Āvuso bhikkhavo" ti.|| ||

"Āvuso" ti kho te [42] bhikkhu āyasmato Mahā Cundassa paccassosuɱ.|| ||

Āyasmā Mahā Cundo etad avoca.|| ||

2. "Ñāṇavādaɱ āvuso bhikkhu vada-māno||
'Jānām'imaɱ dhammaɱ passām'imaɱ dhamman' ti.|| ||

Tañ ce āvuso bhikkhuɱ lobho Abhibhuyya tiṭṭhati,||
so evam assa veditabbo:|| ||

Na ayam āyasmā tathā pajānāti,||
yathā pajānato lobho na hoti.|| ||

Tathā h'imaɱ āyasmantaɱ lobho Abhibhuyya tiṭṭhati.|| ||

■

Tañ ce āvuso bhikkhuɱ doso Abhibhuyya tiṭṭhati,||
so evam assa veditabbo:|| ||

Na ayam āyasmā tathā pajānāti,||
yathā pajānato doso na hoti.|| ||

Tathā h'imaɱ āyasmantaɱ doso Abhibhuyya tiṭṭhati.|| ||

■

Tañ ce āvuso bhikkhuɱ moho Abhibhuyya tiṭṭhati,||
so evam assa veditabbo:|| ||

Na ayam āyasmā tathā pajānāti,||
yathā pajānato moho na hoti.|| ||

Tathā h'imaɱ āyasmantaɱ moho Abhibhuyya tiṭṭhati.|| ||

■

Tañ ce āvuso bhikkhuɱ kodho Abhibhuyya tiṭṭhati,||
so evam assa veditabbo:|| ||

Na ayam āyasmā tathā pajānāti,||
yathā pajānato kodho na hoti.|| ||

Tathā h'imaɱ āyasmantaɱ kodho Abhibhuyya tiṭṭhati.|| ||

■

Tañ ce āvuso bhikkhuɱ upanāho Abhibhuyya tiṭṭhati,||
so evam assa veditabbo:|| ||

Na ayam āyasmā tathā pajānāti,||
yathā pajānato upanāho na hoti.|| ||

Tathā h'imaɱ āyasmantaɱ upanāho Abhibhuyya tiṭṭhati.|| ||

■

Tañ ce āvuso bhikkhuɱ makkho Abhibhuyya tiṭṭhati,||
so evam assa veditabbo:|| ||

Na ayam āyasmā tathā pajānāti,||
yathā pajānato makkho na hoti.|| ||

Tathā h'imaɱ āyasmantaɱ makkho Abhibhuyya tiṭṭhati.|| ||

■

Tañ ce āvuso bhikkhuɱ paḷāso Abhibhuyya tiṭṭhati,||

Na ayam āyasmā tathā pajānāti,||
yathā pajānato macchariyaɱ na hoti.|| ||

5. Seyyathā pi āvuso puriso daḷiddo'va samāno aḍḍavādaɱ vadeyya,||
adhano'va samāno dhanavādaɱ vadeyya,||
abhogavā'va samāno bhogavādaɱ vadeyya.|| ||

So kismiñci-d-eva dhanakaraṇīye samuppanno na sakakuṇeyya upanihattuɱ dhanaɱ vā dhaññaɱ vā rajataɱ vā jāta-rūpaɱ vā.|| ||

Tam enaɱ evaɱ jāneyya:|| ||

'Daḷiddo'va ayam āyasmā samāno aḍḍavādaɱ vadeti,||
adhano'va ayam āyasmā samāno dhanavādaɱ vadeti,||
abhogavā'va ayam āyasmā samāno bhogavādaɱ vadeti' ti.|| ||

Taɱ kissa hetu?|| ||

Tathā hi ayam āyasmā kismiñci-d-eva dhanakaraṇīye samuppannena na Sakkoti upanihattuɱ dhanaɱ vā dhaññaɱ vā rajataɱ vā jāta-rūpaɱ vā.|| ||
