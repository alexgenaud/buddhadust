Aŋguttara Nikāya
X. Dasaka-Nipāta
IV. Upāli Vagga

Sutta 40

Dutiya Ānanda Saŋgha-Sāmaggi Suttaɱ

Adapted from the 1995 edition of the digital version of the Sri Lanka Buddha Jayanti Tripitaka Series.

 

[76]

[1][pts] Evaɱ me sutaɱ:|| ||

Ekaɱ samayaɱ Bhagavā Sāvatthiyaɱ viharati Jetavane Anāthapiṇḍikassa ārāme.|| ||

Atha kho āyasmā Ānanda yena Bhagavā ten'upasankami.|| ||

Upasaɱkamitvā Bhagavantaɱ abhivādetvā eka-m-antaɱ nisīdi.|| ||

Eka-m-antaɱ nisinno kho āyasmā Ānanda Bhagavantaɱ etad avoca:|| ||

"Bhinnaɱ pana bhante Saŋghaɱ samaggaɱ katvā kiɱ so pasavatī" ti?|| ||

"Brahmaɱ Ānanda puññaɱ pasavatī" ti.|| ||

"Kiɱ pana bhante brahmaɱ puññan" ti?|| ||

"Kappaɱ Ānanda saggamhi modatī" ti.|| ||

[77] Sukhā Saŋghassa sāmaggi samaggānañca anuggaho||
Samaggarato dhammaṭṭho yogakkhemā na dhaɱsati||
Saŋghaɱ samaggaɱ katvāna kappaɱ saggamhi modatī ti.|| ||
