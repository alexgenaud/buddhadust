Aŋguttara Nikāya
X. Dasaka-Nipāta
II. Nātha Vagga

Sutta 12

Pañc'Aŋga Suttaɱ

Adapted from the 1995 edition of the digital version of the Sri Lanka Buddha Jayanti Tripitaka Series.

 

[16]

[1][pts][bodh] Evaɱ me sutaɱ:|| ||

Ekaɱ samayaɱ Bhagavā Sāvatthiyaɱ viharati.|| ||

Tatra kho Bhagavā bhikkhū āmantesi:|| ||

"Bhikkhavo" ti.|| ||

"Bhadante" ti te bhikkhū Bhagavato paccassosuɱ.|| ||

Bhagavā etad avoca:|| ||

"Pañc'aŋga-vi-p-pahīno bhikkhave bhikkhu pañc'aŋga samannāgato imasmiɱ Dhamma-Vinaye:|| ||

'Kevalī vusitavā uttama-puriso' ti vuccati.|| ||

 

§

 

Kathañ ca bhikkhave bhikkhu pañc'aŋga-vi-p-pahīno hoti?|| ||

2. Idha, bhikkhave, bhikkhuno||
kāma-c-chando pahīno hoti,||
vyāpādo pahīno hoti,||
thīna-middhaɱ pahīnaɱ hoti,||
uddhaccha-kukkuccaɱ pahīnaɱ hoti,||
vicikicchā pahīnā hoti.|| ||

Evaɱ kho bhikkhave bhikkhu pañc'aŋga-vi-p-pahīno hoti.|| ||

 

§

 

Kathañ ca bhikkhave bhikkhu pañc'aŋga-samannāgato hoti?|| ||

Idha, bhikkhave, bhikkhu||
asekhena sīla-k-khandhena samannāgato hoti,||
asekhena samādhi-k-khandhena samannāgato hoti,||
asekhena paññā-k-khandhena samannāgato hoti,||
asekhena vimutti-k-khandhena samannāgato hoti,||
asekhena vimutti-ṇaṇa-dassana-k-khandhena samannāgato hoti.|| ||

Evaɱ kho bhikkhave bhikkhu pañc'aŋga-samannāgato hoti.|| ||

 

§

 

Pañc'aŋga-vi-p-pahīno bhikkhave||
bhikkhu pañc'aŋga-samannāgato imasmiɱ Dhamma-Vinaye:|| ||

'Kevalī vusitavā uttama-puriso' ti vuccatī.|| ||

 

 

Kāma-c-chando ca vyāpādo thīna-middhañ ca bhikkhuno,||
Uddhaccaɱ vicikicchā ca sabibaso'va na vijjati.|| ||

[17] Asekhena ca sīlena asekhena samādhinā||
Vimuttiyā ca samipanno ñāṇena ca tathāvidho.|| ||

Sa ve pañc'aŋgasamipanno pañca aŋge vivajjayaɱ||
Imasmiɱ Dhamma-Vinaye kevalī iti vuccatī ti.|| ||
