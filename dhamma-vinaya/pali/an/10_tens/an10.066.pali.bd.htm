Aŋguttara Nikāya
X. Dasaka-Nipāta
VII. Yamaka Vagga

Sutta 66

Dutiya Sukha Suttaɱ

Adapted from the 1995 edition of the digital version of the Sri Lanka Buddha Jayanti Tripitaka Series.

 

[121]

[1][pts][niza] Evaɱ me sutaɱ:|| ||

Ekaɱ samayaɱ Sāriputto Magadhesu, viharati Nālakagāmake.|| ||

Atha kho Sāmaṇḍakāni paribbājako yen'āyasmā Sāriputto ten'upasankami.|| ||

Upasaɱkamitvā āyasmatā Sāriputtena saddhiɱ sammodi.|| ||

Sammodanīyaɱ kathaɱ sārāṇīyaɱ vīti-sāretvā eka-m-antaɱ nisīdi.|| ||

Eka-m-antaɱ nisinno kho Sāmaṇḍakāni paribbājako [122] āyasmantaɱ Sāriputtaɱ etad avoca:|| ||

"Kin nu kho āvuso Sāriputta imasmiɱ dhamma vinaye sukhaɱ kiɱ dukakhan" ti?|| ||

"Anabhirati kho āvuso imasmiɱ Dhamma-Vinaye dukkhā||
akhirati sukhā.|| ||

Anabhiratiyā āvuso sati idaɱ dukkhaɱ pāṭikaŋkhaɱ:|| ||

Gacchanto pi sukhaɱ sātaɱ nādhigacchati.|| ||

Ṭhito pi sukhaɱ sātaɱ nādhigacchati.|| ||

Nisinno pi sukhaɱ sātaɱ nādhigacchati.|| ||

Sayāno pi sukhaɱ sātaɱ nādhigacchati.|| ||

Gāma-gato pi sukhaɱ sātaɱ nādhigacchati.|| ||

Arañña-gato pi sukhaɱ sātaɱ nādhigacchati.|| ||

Rukkha-mūla-gato pi sukhaɱ sātaɱ nādhigacchati.|| ||

Suññ-ā-gāra-gato pi sukhaɱ sātaɱ nādhigacchati.|| ||

Abbhokāsa-gato pi sukhaɱ sātaɱ nādhigacchati.|| ||

Bhikkhu-majjha-gato pi sukhaɱ sātaɱ nādhigacchati.|| ||

Anabhiratiyā āvuso sati idaɱ dukkhaɱ pāṭikaŋkhaɱ.|| ||

 

§

Nisinno pi sukhaɱ sātaɱ adhigacchati.|| ||

idaɱ sukhaɱ pāṭikaŋkhan" ti.|| ||
