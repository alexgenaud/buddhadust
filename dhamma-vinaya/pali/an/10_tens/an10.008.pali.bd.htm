Aŋguttara Nikāya
X. Dasaka-Nipāta
I. Ānisaɱsa Vagga

Sutta 8

Saddha Suttaɱ

Adapted from the 1995 edition of the digital version of the Sri Lanka Buddha Jayanti Tripitaka Series.

 

[10]

[1][pts][bodh] Evaɱ me sutaɱ:|| ||

Ekaɱ samayaɱ Bhagavā Sāvatthiyaɱ viharati.|| ||

Tatra kho Bhagavā bhikkhū āmantesi:|| ||

"Bhikkhavo" ti.|| ||

"Bhadante" ti te bhikkhū Bhagavato paccassosuɱ.|| ||

Bhagavā etad avoca:|| ||

"Saddho ca bhikkhave bhikkhu hoti||
no ca sīlavā.|| ||

Evaɱ so ten'aŋgena aparipūro hoti.|| ||

Tena taɱ aŋgaɱ paripūretabbaɱ:|| ||

'Kintāhaɱ saddho ca assaɱ||
sīlavā cā' ti?|| ||

Yato ca kho bhikkhave bhikkhu saddho ca hoti||
sīlavā ca,||
evaɱ so ten'aŋgena paripūro hoti.|| ||

■

2. Saddho ca bhikkhave bhikkhu hoti,||
sīlavā ca,||
no ca bahu-s-suto.|| ||

Evaɱ so ten'aŋgena aparipūro hoti.|| ||

Tena taɱ aŋgaɱ paripūretabbaɱ:|| ||

'Kintāhaɱ saddho ca assaɱ||
sīlavā cā,||
bahu-s-suto ca' ti?|| ||

Yato ca kho bhikkhave bhikkhu saddho ca hoti||
sīlavā ca,||
bahu-s-suto ca||
evaɱ so ten'aŋgena paripūro hoti.|| ||

■

3. Saddho ca bhikkhave bhikkhu hoti,||
sīlavā ca,||
bahu-s-suto ca||
no ca dhamma-kathiko.|| ||

Evaɱ so ten'aŋgena aparipūro hoti.|| ||

Tena taɱ aŋgaɱ paripūretabbaɱ:|| ||

"Kintāhaɱ saddho ca assaɱ||
sīlavā cā,||
bahu-s-suto ca,||
dhamma-kathiko ca" ti?|| ||

Yato ca kho bhikkhave bhikkhu saddho ca hoti||
sīlavā ca,||
bahu-s-suto ca,||
dhamma-kathiko ca,||
evaɱ so ten'aŋgena paripūro hoti.|| ||

■

4. Saddho ca bhikkhave bhikkhu hoti,||
sīlavā ca,||
bahu-s-suto ca,||
dhamma-kathiko ca,||
no ca parisā-vacaro.|| ||

Evaɱ so ten'aŋgena aparipūro hoti.|| ||

Tena taɱ aŋgaɱ paripūretabbaɱ:|| ||

'Kintāhaɱ saddho ca assaɱ||
sīlavā cā,||
bahu-s-suto ca,||
dhamma-kathiko ca,||
parisā-vacaro ca' ti?|| ||

Yato ca kho bhikkhave bhikkhu saddho ca hoti||
sīlavā ca,||
bahu-s-suto ca,||
dhamma-kathiko ca,||
parisā-vacaro ca,||
evaɱ so ten'aŋgena paripūro hoti.|| ||

■

5. Saddho ca bhikkhave bhikkhu hoti,||
sīlavā ca,||
bahu-s-suto ca,||
dhamma-kathiko ca,||
parisā-vacaro ca,||
no ca visārado parisāya dhammaɱ deseti.|| ||

Evaɱ so ten'aŋgena aparipūro hoti.|| ||

Tena taɱ aŋgaɱ paripūretabbaɱ:|| ||

'Kintāhaɱ saddho ca assaɱ||
sīlavā cā,||
bahu-s-suto ca,||
dhamma-kathiko ca,||
parisā-vacaro ca,||
visārado ca parisāya dhammaɱ deseti' ti?|| ||

Yato ca kho bhikkhave bhikkhu saddho ca hoti||
sīlavā ca,||
bahu-s-suto ca,||
dhamma-kathiko ca,||
parisā-vacaro ca,||
visārado ca parisāya dhammaɱ deseti,||
evaɱ so ten'aŋgena paripūro hoti.|| ||

■

6. Saddho ca bhikkhave bhikkhu hoti,||
sīlavā ca,||
bahu-s-suto ca,||
dhamma-kathiko ca,||
parisā-vacaro ca,||
visārado ca parisāya dhammaɱ deseti,||
no ca vinaya-dharo.|| ||

Evaɱ so ten'aŋgena aparipūro hoti.|| ||

Tena taɱ aŋgaɱ paripūretabbaɱ:|| ||

'Kintāhaɱ saddho ca assaɱ||
sīlavā cā,||
bahu-s-suto ca,||
dhamma-kathiko ca,||
parisā-vacaro ca,||
visārado ca parisāya dhammaɱ deseti,||
vinaya-dharo ca' ti?|| ||

Yato ca kho bhikkhave bhikkhu saddho ca hoti||
sīlavā ca,||
bahu-s-suto ca,||
dhamma-kathiko ca,||
parisā-vacaro ca,||
visārado ca parisāya dhammaɱ deseti,||
vinaya-dharo ca,||
evaɱ so ten'aŋgena paripūro hoti.|| ||

■

7. Saddho ca bhikkhave bhikkhu hoti,||
sīlavā ca,||
bahu-s-suto ca,||
dhamma-kathiko ca,||
parisā-vacaro ca,||
visārado ca parisāya dhammaɱ deseti,||
vinaya-dharo ca,||
no ca āraññako pantasen'āsano.|| ||

Evaɱ so ten'aŋgena aparipūro hoti.|| ||

Tena taɱ aŋgaɱ paripūretabbaɱ:|| ||

'Kintāhaɱ saddho ca assaɱ||
sīlavā cā,||
bahu-s-suto ca,||
dhamma-kathiko ca,||
parisā-vacaro ca,||
visārado ca parisāya dhammaɱ deseti,||
vinaya-dharo ca,||
āraññako ca pantasen'āsano' ti?|| ||

Yato ca kho bhikkhave bhikkhu saddho ca hoti||
sīlavā ca,||
bahu-s-suto ca,||
dhamma-kathiko ca,||
parisā-vacaro ca,||
visārado ca parisāya dhammaɱ deseti,||
vinaya-dharo ca,||
āraññako ca pantasen'āsano||
evaɱ so ten'aŋgena paripūro hoti.|| ||

■

8. Saddho ca bhikkhave bhikkhu hoti,||
sīlavā ca,||
bahu-s-suto ca,||
dhamma-kathiko ca,||
parisā-vacaro ca,||
visārado ca parisāya dhammaɱ deseti,||
vinaya-dharo ca,||
āraññako ca pantasen'āsano,||
no ca cakunnaɱ jhānānaɱ ābhiceta-sikānaɱ diṭṭha-dhamma-sukha-vihārānaɱ nikāma-lābhī hoti akicichalābhī akasira-lābhī.|| ||

Evaɱ so ten'aŋgena aparipūro hoti.|| ||

Tena taɱ aŋgaɱ paripūretabbaɱ:|| ||

'Kintāhaɱ saddho ca assaɱ||
sīlavā cā,||
bahu-s-suto ca,||
dhamma-kathiko ca,||
parisā-vacaro ca,||
visārado ca parisāya dhammaɱ deseti,||
vinaya-dharo ca,||
āraññako ca pantasen'āsano,||
cakunnaɱ ca jhānānaɱ ābhiceta-sikānaɱ diṭṭha-dhamma-sukha-vihārānaɱ nikāma-lābhī hoti akicichalābhī akasira-lābhī' ti?|| ||

Yato ca kho bhikkhave bhikkhu saddho ca hoti||
sīlavā ca,||
bahu-s-suto ca,||
dhamma-kathiko ca,||
parisā-vacaro ca,||
visārado ca parisāya dhammaɱ deseti,||
vinaya-dharo ca,||
āraññako ca pantasen'āsano,||
catunnañ ca jhānānaɱ ābhiceta-sikānaɱ diṭṭha-dhamma-sukha-vihārānaɱ nikāma-lābhīassaɱ akiccha-lābhī akasira-lābhī,||
evaɱ so ten'aŋgena paripūro hoti.|| ||

■

9. Saddho ca bhikkhave bhikkhu hoti,||
sīlavā ca,||
bahu-s-suto ca,||
dhamma-kathiko ca,||
parisā-vacaro ca,||
visārado ca parisāya dhammaɱ deseti,||
vinaya-dharo ca,||
āraññako ca pantasen'āsano,||
catunnañ ca jhānānaɱ ābhiceta-sikānaɱ diṭṭha-dhamma-sukha-vihārānaɱ nikāma-lābhī hoti akiccha-lābhī akasira-lābhī,||
no ca āsavānaɱ ca khayā anāsavaɱ ceto-vimuttiɱ paññā-vimuttiɱ diṭṭhe'va dhamme sayaɱ abhiññā sacchi-katvā upasampajja viharati.|| ||

Evaɱ so ten'aŋgena aparipūro hoti.|| ||

Tena taɱ aŋgaɱ paripūretabbaɱ:|| ||

'Kintāhaɱ saddho ca assaɱ||
sīlavā cā,||
bahu-s-suto ca,||
dhamma-kathiko ca,||
parisā-vacaro ca,||
visārado ca parisāya dhammaɱ deseyyaɱ,||
vinaya-dharo ca,||
āraññako ca pantasen'āsano,||
catunnañ ca jhānānaɱ ābhicetasi- [11] kānaɱ diṭṭha-dhamma-sukha-vihārānaɱ nikāma-lābhīassaɱ akiccha-lābhī akasira-lābhī,||
āsavānaɱ ca khayā anāsavaɱ ceto-vimuttiɱ paññā-vimuttiɱ diṭṭhe'va dhamme sayaɱ abhiññā sacichikatvā upasampajja vihareyyan' ti?|| ||

Yato ca kho bhikkhave bhikkhu saddho ca hoti||
sīlavā ca,||
bahu-s-suto ca,||
dhamma-kathiko ca,||
parisā-vacaro ca,||
visārado ca parisāya dhammaɱ deseti,||
vinaya-dharo ca,||
āraññako ca pantasen'āsano,||
catunnañ ca jhānānaɱ ābhiceta-sikānaɱ diṭṭha-dhamma-sukha-vihārānaɱ nikāma-lābhī hoti akiccha-lābhī akasira-lābhī,||
āsavānaɱ ca khayā anāsavaɱ ceto-vimuttiɱ paññā-vimuttiɱ diṭṭhe'va dhamme sayaɱ abhiññā sacchi-katvā upasampajja viharati,||
evaɱ so ten'aŋgena paripūro hoti.|| ||

■

Imehi kho bhikkhave dasahi dhammehi samannāgato bhikkhu samanta-pāsādiko ca hoti sabbākāra paripūrovā" ti.|| ||
