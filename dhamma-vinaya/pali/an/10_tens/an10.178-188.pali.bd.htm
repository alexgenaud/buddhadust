Aṅguttara Nikāya
X. Dasaka-Nipāta
XVIII: Sādhu-Vagga

Suttas 178-188

Adapted from the 1995 edition of the digital version of the Sri Lanka Buddha Jayanti Tripitaka Series.

 

[273]

Sutta 178

Sādhu Suttaɱ

[178.1][pts] Evaɱ me sutaɱ:|| ||

Ekaɱ samayaɱ Bhagavā Sāvatthiyaɱ viharati.|| ||

Tatra kho Bhagavā bhikkhū āmantesi:|| ||

"Bhikkhavo" ti.|| ||

"Bhadante" ti te bhikkhū Bhagavato paccassosuɱ.|| ||

Bhagavā etad avoca:|| ||

2. "Sādhuñ ca vo bhikkhave desissāmi,||
asādhuñ ca.|| ||

Taɱ suṇātha, sādhukaɱ manasi-karotha, bhāsissāmī" ti.|| ||

"Evaɱ [274] bhante" ti kho te bhikkhu Bhagavato paccassosuɱ.|| ||

Bhagavā etad avoca:|| ||

"Katamañ ca bhikkhave asādhu?|| ||

Pāṇ-ā-tipāto,||
adinn'ādānaɱ,||
kāmesu micchā-cāro,||
musā-vādo,||
pisuṇā-vācā,||
pharusā-vācā,||
sampha-p-palāpo,||
abhijjhā,||
vyāpādo,||
micchā-diṭṭhi.|| ||

Idaɱ vuccati bhikkhave asādhu.|| ||

Katamañ ca bhikkhave sādhu?|| ||

Pāṇ-ā-tipātā veramaṇī,||
adinn'ādānā veramaṇī,||
kāmesu micchā-cārā veramaṇī,||
musā-vādā veramaṇī,||
pisuṇāya vācāya veramaṇī,||
pharusāya vācāya veramaṇī,||
sampha-p-palāpā veramaṇī,||
anabhijjhā,||
avyāpādo,||
sammā-diṭṭhi.|| ||

Idaɱ vuccati bhikkhave sādhū" ti.|| ||
