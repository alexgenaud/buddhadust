Aṇguttara Nikāya
X. Dasaka-Nipāta
XVIII: Sādhu-Vagga

Suttas 178-188

Adapted from the 1995 edition of the digital version of the Sri Lanka Buddha Jayanti Tripitaka Series.

 

[273]

Sutta 178

Sādhu Suttaṃ

[178.1][pts][olds] "Sādhuñ ca vo, bhikkhave||
desissāmi,||
asādhuñ ca.|| ||

Taṃ suṇātha,||
sādhukaṃ manasi-karotha,||
bhāsissāmī" ti.|| ||

"Evaṃ [274] bhante" ti||
kho te bhikkhu Bhagavato paccassosuṃ.|| ||

Bhagavā etad avoca:|| ||

"Katamañ ca, bhikkhave,||
asādhu?|| ||

Pāṇātipāto,||
adinnādānaṃ,||
kāmesu micchā-cāro,||
musā-vādo,||
pisuṇāvācā,||
pharusā-vācā,||
sampha-p-palāpo,||
abhijjhā,||
vyāpādo,||
micchā-diṭṭhi.|| ||

Idaṃ vuccati, bhikkhave,||
asādhu.|| ||

 

■

 

Katamañ ca, bhikkhave,||
sādhu?|| ||

Pāṇātipātā veramaṇī,||
adinnādānā veramaṇī,||
kāmesu micchā-cārā veramaṇī,||
musā-vādā veramaṇī,||
pisuṇāya vācāya veramaṇī,||
pharusāya vācāya veramaṇī,||
sampha-p-palāpā veramaṇī,||
anabhijjhā,||
avyāpādo,||
sammā-diṭṭhi.|| ||

Idaṃ vuccati, bhikkhave,||
sādhū" ti.|| ||
