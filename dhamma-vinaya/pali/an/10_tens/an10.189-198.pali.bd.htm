Aṅguttara Nikāya
X. Dasaka-Nipāta
XIX: Ariya-Magga-Vagga

Suttas 189-198

Adapted from the 1995 edition of the digital version of the Sri Lanka Buddha Jayanti Tripitaka Series.

 

[278]

Sutta 189

Ariya-Magga Suttaɱ

[189.1][pts] Evaɱ me sutaɱ:|| ||

Ekaɱ samayaɱ Bhagavā Sāvatthiyaɱ viharati.|| ||

Tatra kho Bhagavā bhikkhū āmantesi:|| ||

"Bhikkhavo" ti.|| ||

"Bhadante" ti te bhikkhū Bhagavato paccassosuɱ.|| ||

Bhagavā etad avoca:|| ||

2. "Ariya-Maggañ ca vo bhikkhave desissāmi, anAriya-Maggañ ca.|| ||

Taɱ suṇātha, sādhukaɱ manasi-karotha, bhāsissāmī" ti.|| ||

"Evaɱ [274] bhante" ti kho te bhikkhu Bhagavato paccassosuɱ.|| ||

Bhagavā etad avoca:|| ||

"Katamañ ca bhikkhave anariya-maggo?|| ||

Pāṇ-ā-tipāto,||
adinn'ādānaɱ,||
kāmesu micchā-cāro,||
musā-vādo,||
pisuṇā-vācā,||
pharusā-vācā,||
sampha-p-palāpo,||
abhijjhā,||
vyāpādo,||
micchā-diṭṭhi.|| ||

Idaɱ vuccati bhikkhave anariya-maggo.|| ||

Katamañ ca bhikkhave ariyo Maggo?|| ||

Pāṇ-ā-tipātā veramaṇī,||
adinn'ādānā veramaṇī,||
kāmesu micchā-cārā veramaṇī,||
musā-vādā veramaṇī,||
pisuṇāya vācāya veramaṇī,||
pharusāya vācāya veramaṇī,||
sampha-p-palāpā veramaṇī,||
anabhijjhā,||
avyāpādo,||
sammā-diṭṭhi.|| ||

Idaɱ vuccati bhikkhave ariyo Maggo" ti.|| ||
