Adapted from the 1995 edition of the digital version of the Sri Lanka Buddha Jayanti Tripitaka Series.

Sutta 155

Sevitabba Suttaɱ

[155.1][pts] Evaɱ me sutaɱ:|| ||

Ekaɱ samayaɱ Bhagavā Sāvatthiyaɱ viharati.|| ||

Tatra kho Bhagavā bhikkhū āmantesi:|| ||

"Bhikkhavo" ti.|| ||

"Bhadante" ti te bhikkhū Bhagavato paccassosuɱ.|| ||

Bhagavā etad avoca:|| ||

2. "Dasahi bhikkhave dhammehi samannāgato puggalo na sevitabbo.|| ||

Katamehi dasahi?|| ||

[248] Micchā-diṭṭhi hoti,||
micchā-saŋkappo hoti,||
micchā-vācā hoti,||
micchā-kammanto hoti,||
micchā-ājīvo hoti,||
micchā-vāyāmo hoti,||
micchā-sati hoti,||
micchā-samādhi hoti,||
micchā-ñāṇaɱ hoti,||
micchā-vimutti hoti.|| ||

Imehi kho bhikkhave dasahi dhammehi samannāgato puggalo na sevitabbo.|| ||

■

Dasahi bhikkhave dhammehi samannāgato puggalo sevitabbo.|| ||

Katamehi dasahi?|| ||

Sammā-diṭṭhi hoti,||
sammā-saŋkappo hoti,||
sammā-vācā hoti,||
sammā-kammanto hoti,||
sammā-ājīvo hoti,||
sammā-vāyāmo hoti,||
sammā-sati hoti,||
sammā-samādhi hoti,||
sammā-ñāṇaɱ hoti,||
sammā-vimutti hoti.|| ||

Imehi kho bhikkhave dasahi dhammehi samannāgato puggalo sevitabbo" ti.|| ||
