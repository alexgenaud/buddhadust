Aŋguttara Nikāya
X. Dasaka-Nipāta
VIII: Ākaŋkha-Vagga

Sutta 80

Āghāta-Paṭivinaya Suttaɱ

Adapted from the 1995 edition of the digital version of the Sri Lanka Buddha Jayanti Tripitaka Series.

 

[150]

[1][pts][than] Evaɱ me sutaɱ:|| ||

Ekaɱ samayaɱ Bhagavā Sāvatthiyaɱ viharati.|| ||

Tatra kho Bhagavā bhikkhū āmantesi:|| ||

"Bhikkhavo" ti.|| ||

"Bhadante" ti te bhikkhū Bhagavato paccassosuɱ.|| ||

Bhagavā etad avoca:|| ||

2. "Dasa ime bhikkhave āghāta-paṭivinayā.|| ||

Katame dasa?|| ||

[1] 3. 'Anattham me acari,||
taɱ kut'ettha labbhā' ti?||
āghātaɱ [151] paṭivineti.|| ||

■

[2] 4. 'Anattham me carati,||
taɱ kut'ettha labbhā' ti?||
āghātaɱ paṭivineti.|| ||

■

[3] 5. 'Anattham me carissati,||
taɱ kut'ettha labbhā' ti?||
āghātaɱ paṭivineti.|| ||

■

[4] 6. 'Piyassa me manāpassa anatthaɱ acari,||
taɱ kut'ettha labbhā' ti?||
āghātaɱ paṭivineti.|| ||

■

[5] 7. 'Piyassa me manāpassa anattaɱ carati,||
taɱ kut'ettha labbhā' ti?||
āghātaɱ paṭivineti.|| ||

■

[6] 8. 'Piyassa me manāpassa anatthaɱ carissati,||
taɱ kut'ettha labbhā' ti?||
āghātaɱ paṭivineti.|| ||

■

[7] 8. 'Appiyassa me amanāpassa atthaɱ acari||
taɱ kut'ettha labbhā' ti?||
āghātaɱ paṭivineti.|| ||

■

[8] 7. 'Appiyassa me amanāpassa anattaɱ carati,||
taɱ kut'ettha labbhā' ti?||
āghātaɱ paṭivineti.|| ||

■

[9] 8. 'Appiyassa me amanāpassa anatthaɱ carissati,||
taɱ kut'ettha labbhā' ti?||
āghātaɱ paṭivineti.|| ||

■

[10] Aṭṭhāne ca na kuppati.|| ||

Ime kho bhikkhave dasa āghāta-paṭivinayā ti.|| ||
