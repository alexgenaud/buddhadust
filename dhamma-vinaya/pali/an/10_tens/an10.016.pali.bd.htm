Aŋguttara Nikāya
X. Dasaka-Nipāta
II. Nātha Vagga

Sutta 16

Āhuneyya Suttaɱ

Adapted from the 1995 edition of the digital version of the Sri Lanka Buddha Jayanti Tripitaka Series.

 

[23]

[1][pts][bodh] Evaɱ me sutaɱ:|| ||

Ekaɱ samayaɱ Bhagavā bhikkhū āmantesi:|| ||

"Bhikkhavo" ti.|| ||

"Bhadante" ti||
te bhikkhū Bhagavato paccassosuɱ.|| ||

Bhagavā etad avoca:|| ||

"Dasa ime bhikkhave puggalā āhuneyyā pāhuneyyā dakkhiṇeyyā añjali-karaṇīyā anuttaraɱ puñña-k-khettaɱ lokassa.|| ||

Katame dasa?|| ||

Tathāgato arahaɱ Sammā-Sam-Buddho,||
Pacceka-Buddho,||
ubhatobhāga vimutto,||
paññā-vimutto,||
kāyasakkhī,||
diṭṭhippatto,||
saddhā-vimutto,||
dhamm'ānusārī,||
saddh'ānusārī,||
gotrabhū.|| ||

Ime kho bhikkhave dasa puggalā āhuneyyā pāhuneyyā, dakkhiṇeyyā, añjali-karaṇīyā anuttaraɱ puñña-k-khettaɱ lokassā" ti.|| ||
