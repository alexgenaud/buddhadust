Aŋguttara Nikāya
X. Dasaka-Nipāta
VIII: Ākaŋkha-Vagga

Sutta 79

Āghāta-Vatthu Suttaɱ

Adapted from the 1995 edition of the digital version of the Sri Lanka Buddha Jayanti Tripitaka Series.

 

[150]

[1][pts] Evaɱ me sutaɱ:|| ||

Ekaɱ samayaɱ Bhagavā Sāvatthiyaɱ viharati.|| ||

Tatra kho Bhagavā bhikkhū āmantesi:|| ||

"Bhikkhavo" ti.|| ||

"Bhadante" ti te bhikkhū Bhagavato paccassosuɱ.|| ||

Bhagavā etad avoca:|| ||

2. "Dasa yimāni bhikkhave āghāta-vatthūni.|| ||

Katamāni dasa?|| ||

[1] 'Anattham me acarī' ti āghātaɱ bandhati.|| ||

[2] 'Anattam me caratī' ti āghātaɱ bandhati.|| ||

[3] 'Anattham me carissatī' ti āghātaɱ bandhati.|| ||

[4] 'Piyassa me manāpassa anatthaɱ acari' ti āghātaɱ bandhati.|| ||

[5] 'Piyassa me manāpassa anatthaɱ caratī' ti āghātaɱ bandhati.|| ||

[6] 'Piyassa me manāpassa anatthaɱ carissatī' ti āghātaɱ bandhati.|| ||

[7] 'Appiyassa me amanāpassa atthaɱ acari' ti āghātaɱ bandhati.|| ||

[8] 'Appiyassa me amanāpassa atthaɱ caratī' ti āghātaɱ bandhati.|| ||

[9] 'Appiyassa me amanāpassa atthaɱ carissatī' ti āghātaɱ bandhati.|| ||

[10] Aṭṭhāne ca kuppati.|| ||

Imāni kho bhikkhave dasa āghāta-vatthūnī" ti.|| ||
