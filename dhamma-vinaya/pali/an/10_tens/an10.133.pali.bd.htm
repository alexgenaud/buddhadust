Aŋguttara Nikāya
X. Dasaka-Nipāta
XIII: Parisuddha-Vagga

Sutta 133

Ekādasama Suttaɱ

Adapted from the 1995 edition of the digital version of the Sri Lanka Buddha Jayanti Tripitaka Series.

 

[240]

[1][pts] Evaɱ me sutaɱ:|| ||

Ekaɱ samayaɱ Bhagavā Sāvatthiyaɱ viharati.|| ||

Tatra kho Bhagavā bhikkhū āmantesi:|| ||

"Bhikkhavo" ti.|| ||

"Bhadante" ti te bhikkhū Bhagavato paccassosuɱ.|| ||

Bhagavā etad avoca:|| ||

2. "Dasa ime bhikkhave sammattā.|| ||

Katame dasa?|| ||

Sammā-diṭṭhi,||
sammā-saŋkappo,||
sammā-vācā,||
sammā-kammanto,||
sammā-ājīvo,||
sammā-vāyāmo,||
sammā-sati,||
sammā-samādhi,||
sammā-ñāṇaɱ,||
sammā-vimutti.|| ||

Ime kho bhikkhave dasa sammattā" ti|| ||
