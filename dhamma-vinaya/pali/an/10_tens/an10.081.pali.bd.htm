Adapted from the 1995 edition of the digital version of the Sri Lanka Buddha Jayanti Tripitaka Series.

 

[151]

[1][pts][than][olds] Evaɱ me sutaɱ:|| ||

Ekaɱ samayaɱ Bhagavā Campāyaɱ viharati Gaggarāya pokkharaṇiyā tīre.|| ||

Atha kho āyasmā Bāhuno yena Bhagavā ten'upasankami.|| ||

Upasaŋkamitvā Bhagavantaɱ abhivādetvā eka-m-antaɱ nisīdi.|| ||

"Katihi nu kho bhante dhammehi Tathāgato||
nissaṭṭho||
visaŋyutto||
vippamutto||
vimariyādī-katena cetasā viharitī" ti?|| ||
