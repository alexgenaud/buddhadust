Aŋguttara Nikāya
X. Dasaka-Nipāta
III. Mahā Vagga

Sutta 25

Kasiṇa Suttaɱ

Adapted from the 1995 edition of the digital version of the Sri Lanka Buddha Jayanti Tripitaka Series.

 

[46]

[1][pts][olds][bodh] Evaɱ me sutaɱ: [No Nidana]|| ||

2. Dasa imāni bhikkhave kasiṇ-āyatanāni.|| ||

Katamāni dasa?|| ||

Paṭhavi-kasiṇam eko sañjānāti uddhaɱ adho tiriyaɱ advayaɱ appamāṇaɱ.|| ||

Āpo-kasiṇam eko sañjānāti uddhaɱ adho tiriyaɱ advayaɱ appamāṇaɱ.|| ||

Tejo-kasiṇam eko sañjānāti uddhaɱ adho tiriyaɱ advayaɱ appamāṇaɱ.|| ||

Vāyo-kasiṇam eko sañjānāti uddhaɱ adho tiriyaɱ advayaɱ appamāṇaɱ.|| ||

Nīla-kasiṇam eko sañajānāti uddhaɱ adho tiriyaɱ advayaɱ appamāṇaɱ.|| ||

Pīta-kasiṇam eko sañjānāti uddhaɱ adho tiriyaɱ advayaɱ appamāṇaɱ.|| ||

Lohita-kasiṇam eko sañjānāti uddhaɱ adho tiriyaɱ advayaɱ appamāṇaɱ.|| ||

Odāta-kasiṇam eko sañjānāti uddhaɱ adho tiriyaɱ advayaɱ appamāṇaɱ.|| ||

Ākāsa-kasiṇam eko sañjānāti uddhaɱ adho tiriyaɱ advayaɱ appamāṇaɱ.|| ||

Viññāṇa-kasiṇam eko sañjānāti uddhaɱ adho tiriyaɱ advayaɱ appamāṇaɱ.|| ||

Imāni kho bhikkhave dasa kasiṇ'āyatanānī" ti.|| ||
