Aŋguttara Nikāya
IX. Navaka Nipāta
I. Sambodha Vagga

Sutta 9

Puggala Suttaɱ

Adapted from the 1995 edition of the digital version of the Sri Lanka Buddha Jayanti Tripitaka Series.

 

[372]

[1][pts][upal][olds] Evaɱ me sutaɱ:|| ||

Sāvatthi nidānaɱ.|| ||

"Nava ime bhikkhave, puggalā santo saŋvijj'amānā lokasmiɱ.|| ||

Katame nava?|| ||

Arahā,||
arahattāya paṭi-panno,||
Anāgāmi,||
Anāgāmi-phalaɱ sacchi-kiriyāya paṭi-panno,||
Sakad-āgāmi,||
Sakad-āgāmi-phala sacchi-kiriyāya paṭi-panno,||
Sot'āpanno,||
sot'āpatti-phala sacchi-kiriyāya paṭi-panno,||
puthujjano.|| ||

Ime kho bhikkhave, navapuggalā santo saŋvijj'amānā lokasmin" ti.|| ||
