Aŋguttara Nikāya
VIII. Navaka Nipāta
III. Satt'Āvāsa Vagga

Sutta 22

Khaḷuŋka Suttaɱ

Adapted from the 1995 edition of the digital version of the Sri Lanka Buddha Jayanti Tripitaka Series.

 

[397]

[1][pts] Evaɱ me sutaɱ ekaɱ samayaɱ Bhagavā Sāvātthiyaɱ viharati Jetavane Anāthapiṇḍikassa ārāme.|| ||

"Tayo ca bhikkhave, assa-khaḷuŋke desissāmi||
tayo ca purisa-khaḷuŋke,||
tayo ca assa-sadasse||
tayo ca purisa-sadasse||
tayo ca bhadde ass-ā-jānīye||
tayo ca bhadde puris-ā-jānīye.|| ||

Taɱ suṇātha,||
sādhukaɱ manasi-karotha,||
bhāsissāmī" ti.|| ||

"Evaɱ bhante" ti kho te bhikkhu Bhagavato paccassosuɱ.|| ||

Bhagavā etad avoca:|| ||

2. "Katame ca bhikkhave, tayo assa-khaḷuŋkā?|| ||

Idha, bhikkhave, ekacco assa-khaḷuŋko||
java-sampanno ca hoti,||
na vaṇṇa-sampanno,||
na āroha-pariṇāha-sampanno.|| ||

Idha pana bhikkhave, ekacco assa-khaḷuŋko||
java-sampanno ca hoti||
vaṇṇa-sampanno ca||
na āroha-pariṇāha-sampanno.|| ||

Idha pana bhikkhave, ekacco assa-khaḷuŋko||
java-sampanno ca hoti||
vaṇṇa-sampanno ca||
āroha-pariṇāha-sampanno ca.|| ||

Ime kho bhikkhave tayo assa-khaḷuŋkā.|| ||

 

 

3. Katame ca bhikkhave, tayo purisa-khaḷuŋko?|| ||

Idha bhikkhave, ekacco purisa-khaḷuŋko||
java-sampanno hoti||
na ca vaṇṇa-sampanno hoti,||
na āroha-pariṇāha sampanno.|| ||

Idha pana bhikkhave, ekacco purisa-khaḷuŋko||
java-sampanno ca hoti,||
vaṇṇa-sampanno ca,||
na āroha-pariṇāha sampanno.|| ||

Idha pana bhikkhave, ekacco purisa-khaḷuŋko||
java-sampanno ca hoti,||
vaṇṇa-sampanno ca,||
āroha-pariṇāha sampanno ca.|| ||

 

 

4. Kathañ ca bhikkhave, purisa-khaḷuŋko||
java-sampanno hoti||
na vaṇṇa-sampanno,||
na āroha-pariṇāha sampanno?|| ||

Idha, bhikkhave, bhikkhu||
'idaɱ dukkhan' ti yathā-bhūtaɱ pajānāti,||
'ayaɱ dukkha-samudayo' ti yathā-bhūtaɱ pajānāti,||
'ayaɱ dukkha-nirodho' ti yathā-bhūtaɱ pajānāti||
'ayaɱ dukkha-nirodha-gāminī paṭipadā' ti yathā-bhūtaɱ pajānāti.|| ||

[398] Idam assa javasmiɱ vadāmi.|| ||

Abhidhamme kho pana abhivinaye pañhaɱ puṭṭho saɱsādeti,||
no vissajjeti.|| ||

Idam assa na vaṇṇasmiɱ vadāmi.|| ||

Na kho pana lābhī hoti cīvara-piṇḍa-pāta-sen'āsana-gilāna-paccaya-bhesajja-parikkhārānaɱ.

Idam assa na āroha-pariṇāhasmiɱ vadāmi.|| ||

Evaɱ kho bhikkhave,||
purisa-khaḷuŋko java-sampanno hoti||
na vaṇṇa sampanno,||
na āroha-pariṇāha-sampanno.|| ||

■

5. Kathañ ca bhikkhave, purisa-khaḷuŋko||
java-sampanno ca hoti||
vaṇṇa-sampanno ca,||
na āroha-pariṇāha-sampanno?|| ||

Idha, bhikkhave, bhikkhu||
'idaɱ dukkhan' ti yathā-bhūtaɱ pajānāti,||
'ayaɱ dukkha-samudayo' ti yathā-bhūtaɱ pajānāti,||
'ayaɱ dukkha-nirodho' ti yathā-bhūtaɱ pajānāti||
'ayaɱ dukkha-nirodha-gāminī paṭipadā' ti yathā-bhūtaɱ pajānāti.|| ||

Idam assa javasmiɱ vadāmi.|| ||

Abhidhamme kho pana abhivinaye pañhaɱ puṭṭho vissajjeti||
saɱsādeti.|| ||

Idam assa vaṇṇasmiɱ vadāmi.|| ||

Na kho pana lābhī hoti cīvara-piṇḍa-pāta-sen'āsana-gilāna-paccaya-bhesajja-parikkhārānaɱ.|| ||

Idam assa na āroha-pariṇāhasmiɱ vadāmi.|| ||

Evaɱ kho bhikkhave, purisa-khaḷuŋko||
java-sampanno ca hoti
vaṇṇa-sampanno ca,||
na āroha-pariṇāha-sampanno.|| ||

purisa-khaḷuŋko java-sampanno ca hoti||

[399] Lābhī kho pana hoti cīvara-piṇḍa-pāta-sen'āsana-gilāna-paccaya-bhesajja-parikkhārānaɱ|| ||

Evaɱ kho bhikkhave, purisa-khaḷuŋko||

java-sampanno hoti||
na vaṇṇa-sampanno,||
na āroha-pariṇāha-sampanno ca?|| ||

Idha, bhikkhave, bhikkhu pañcannaɱ ora-m-bhāgiyānaɱ saŋyojanānaɱ parikkhayā opapātiko hoti||
tattha parinibbāyī anāvattīdhāmmo tasmā lokā.|| ||

Idam assa javasmiɱ vadāmi.|| ||

Abhidhamme kho pana abhivinaye pañhaɱ puṭṭho vissajjeti,||
no saɱsādeti.|| ||

12.1 Kathañ ca bhikkhave, bhadro puris-ā-jānīye||
java-sampanno hoti||
na vaṇṇa-sampanno,||
na āroha-pariṇāha-sampanno ca?|| ||

Idha, bhikkhave, bhikkhu āsavānaɱ khayā||
anāsavaɱ ceto-vimuttaɱ||
paññā-vimuttiɱ||
diṭṭhe'va dhamme||
sayaɱ abhiññā sacchi-katvā||
upasampajja viharati.|| ||

Idaɱ massa javasmiɱ vadāmi.|| ||

Abhidhamme kho pana abhivinaye pañhaɱ puṭṭho vissajjeti,||
no saɱsādeti.|| ||

Idam assa na vaṇṇasmiɱ vadāmi.

Na kho pana lābhī kho pana hoti cīvara-piṇḍa-pāta-sen'āsana-gilāna-paccaya-bhesajja-parikkhārānaɱ.|| ||

Idam assa na āroha-pariṇāhasmiɱ vadāmi.

Evaɱ kho bhikkhave, bhadro purisajānīyo java-sampanno ca hoti||
vaṇṇa sampanno ca||
āroha-pariṇāha sampanno ca.|| ||
