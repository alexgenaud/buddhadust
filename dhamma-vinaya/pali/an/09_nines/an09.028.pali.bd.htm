Aŋguttara Nikāya
VIII. Navaka Nipāta
III. Satt'Āvāsa Vagga

Sutta 28

Dutiya Vera-Bhaya Suttaɱ

Adapted from the 1995 edition of the digital version of the Sri Lanka Buddha Jayanti Tripitaka Series.

 

[407]

[1][pts] Evaɱ me sutaɱ:|| ||

Ekaɱ samayaɱ Bhagavā Sāvātthiyaɱ viharati Jetavane Anāthapiṇḍikassa ārāme,||
tatra kho Bhagavā bhikkhu āmantesi.|| ||

2. Yato kho bhikkhave ariya-sāvakassa pañca bhayāni verāni vūpasantāni honti,||
catūhi ca sot'āpattiyaŋgehi samannāgato hoti,||
so ākaŋkha-māno attanā va attānaɱ vyākareyya:|| ||

'Khīṇa-Nirayo'mih,||
khīṇa-tiracchāna-yoni,||
khīṇa-petti-visayo,||
khīṇ-ā-pāya-duggati-vinipāto,||
Sot'āpanno'ham asmi,||
avinipāta-dhammo niyato sambodhi-parāyano.'|| ||

 

§

 

3. Katamāni pañca bhayāni verāni vūpasantāni honti?|| ||

Yaɱ bhikkhave pāṇāti-pātī pāṇāti-pāta-p-paccayā diṭṭha-dhammikam pi bhayaɱ veraɱ pasavati,||
samparāyikam pi bhayaɱ veraɱ pasavati,||
ceta-sikam pi dukkhaɱ domanassaɱ paṭisaɱvedetī.|| ||

Pāṇāti-pātā paṭivirato n'eva diṭṭha-dhammakam pi bhayaɱ veraɱ pasavati,||
na samparāyikam pi bhayaɱ veraɱ pasavati,||
na ceta-sikam pi dukkhaɱ domanassaɱ paṭisaɱvedeti.|| ||

Pāṇāti-pātā paṭiviratassa evaɱ taɱ bhayaɱ veraɱ vūpasantaɱ hoti.|| ||

■

4. Yaɱ bhikkhave adinn'ādāyī adinnāna-p-paccayā diṭṭha-dhammikam pi bhayaɱ veraɱ pasavati,||
samparāyikam pi bhayaɱ veraɱ pasavati,||
ceta-sikam pi dukkhaɱ domanassaɱ paṭisaɱvedetī.|| ||

Adinnādānā paṭivirato n'eva diṭṭha-dhammakam pi bhayaɱ veraɱ pasavati,||
na samparāyikam pi bhayaɱ veraɱ pasavati,||
na ceta-sikam pi dukkhaɱ domanassaɱ paṭisaɱvedeti.|| ||

Adinnādānā paṭiviratassa evaɱ taɱ bhayaɱ veraɱ vūpasantaɱ hoti.|| ||

■

5. Yaɱ bhikkhave kāmesu micchā-cārī kāmesu-micchā-cāra-p-paccayā diṭṭha-dhammikam pi bhayaɱ veraɱ pasavati,||
samparāyikam pi bhayaɱ veraɱ pasavati,||
ceta-sikam pi dukkhaɱ domanassaɱ paṭisaɱvedatī.|| ||

Kāmesu micchā-cārā paṭivirato n'eva diṭṭha-dhammakam pi bhayaɱ veraɱ pasavati,||
na samparāyikam pi bhayaɱ veraɱ pasavati,||
na ceta-sikam pi dukkhaɱ domanassaɱ paṭisaɱvedati.|| ||

Kāmesu micchā-cārā paṭiviratassa evaɱ taɱ bhayaɱ veraɱ vūpasantaɱ hoti.|| ||

■

6. Yaɱ bhikkhave musā-vādī musā-vādāppaccayā diṭṭha-dhammikam pi bhayaɱ veraɱ pasavati,||
samparāyikam pi bhayaɱ veraɱ pasavati,||
ceta-sikam pi dukkhaɱ domanassaɱ paṭisaɱvedatī.|| ||

Musā-vādā paṭivirato n'eva diṭṭha-dhammakam pi bhayaɱ veraɱ pasavati,||
na samparāyikam pi bhayaɱ veraɱ pasavati,||
na ceta-sikam pi dukkhaɱ domanassaɱ paṭisaɱvedati.|| ||

Musā-vādā paṭiviratassa evaɱ taɱ bhayaɱ veraɱ vūpasantaɱ hoti.|| ||

■

7. Yaɱ bhikkhave surā-mera-yamajja-pamā-daṭṭhāyī surā-mera-yamajja-pamā-daṭṭhāna-p-paccayā diṭṭha-dhammikam pi bhayaɱ veraɱ pasavati,||
samparāyikam pi bhayaɱ veraɱ pasavati,||
ceta-sikam pi dukkhaɱ domanassaɱ paṭisaɱvedatī.|| ||

Surā-mera-yamajja-pamā-daṭṭhānā paṭivirato n'eva diṭṭha-dhammakam pi bhayaɱ veraɱ pasavati,||
na samparāyikam pi bhayaɱ veraɱ pasavati,||
na ceta-sikam pi dukkhaɱ domanassaɱ paṭisaɱvedati.|| ||

Surā-mera-yamajja-pamā-daṭṭhānā paṭiviratassa evaɱ taɱ bhayaɱ veraɱ vūpasantāni hoti.|| ||

Imāni pañca bhayāni verāni vūpasanthāni honti.|| ||

 

§

 

8. Katamehi catūhi sot'āpattiyaŋgehi samannāgato hoti?|| ||

Idha bhikkhave ariya-sāvako Buddhe avecca-p-pasādena samannāgato hoti:|| ||

'Iti pi so Bhagavā arahaɱ Sammā-Sam-Buddho vijjā-caraṇa-sampanno Sugato loka-vidū anuttaro purisa-damma-sārathī Satthā deva-manussānaɱ Buddho Bhagavā' ti.|| ||

■

9. Dhamme avecca-p-pasādena samannāgato hoti:|| ||

'Svākkhāto Bhagavatā dhammo sandiṭṭhiko akāliko ehi passiko opanayiko paccattaɱ veditabbo viññūhī' ti.|| ||

■

10. Saŋghe avecca-p-pasādena samannāgato hoti.|| ||

'Su-paṭipanno Bhagavato sāvaka-saŋgho,||
uju-paṭipanno Bhagavato sāvaka-saŋgho ñāya-paṭipanno Bhagavato sāvaka-saŋgho,||
sāmīci-paṭipanno Bhagavato sāvaka-saŋgho,||
yad idaɱ cattāri purisa-yugāni aṭṭha purisa-puggalā,||
esa Bhagavato sāvaka-saŋgho āhuneyyo pāhuneyyo dakkhineyyo añjali-karaṇīyo anuttaraɱ puñña-k-khettaɱ lokassā' ti.|| ||

Ariyakantehi sīlehi samannāgato hoti,||
akhaṇḍehi acachiddehi asabalehi akammāsehi bhujissehi viññūppasatthehi aparām-aṭṭhehi samādhi-saŋvatta-nikehi.|| ||

Imehi catūhi sotāpāttiyaŋgehi samannāgato hoti.|| ||

 

§

 

[408] 11. Yato kho bhikkhave ariya-sāvakassa imāni pañca bhayāni verāni vūpasantāni honti,||
imehi ca catūhi sotāpāttiyaŋgehi samannāgato hoti,||
so ākaŋkha-māno attanā'va attānaɱ vyākareyya:|| ||

'Khīṇa Nirayo'mhi,||
khīṇa-tiracchāna-yoni,||
khīṇa-petti-visayo,||
khīṇaapāyaduggativinipāto,||
Sot'āpanno'ham asmi,||
avinipāta-dhammo niyato sambodhi-parāyano' ti" ti.|| ||
