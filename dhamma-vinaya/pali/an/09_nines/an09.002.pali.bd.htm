Aŋguttara Nikāya
IX. Navaka Nipāta
I. Sambodha Vagga

Sutta 2

Nissaya-Sampanna Suttaɱ

Adapted from the 1995 edition of the digital version of the Sri Lanka Buddha Jayanti Tripitaka Series.

 

[353]

[1][pts][upal] Evaɱ me sutaɱ.|| ||

Ekaɱ samayaɱ Bhagavā Sāvātthiyaɱ viharati||
Jetavane Anāthapiṇḍikassa ārāme.|| ||

Atha kho aññataro bhikkhu yena Bhagavā ten'upasankami.|| ||

Upasaŋkamitvā Bhagavantaɱ abhivādetvā eka-m-antaɱ nisidi.|| ||

Eka-m-antaɱ nisinno kho so bhikkhu Bhagavantaɱ etad avoca:|| ||

"'Nissaya-sampanno nissaya-sampanno' ti bhante vuccati.|| ||

Kittāvatā nu kho bhante bhikkhu nissaya-sampanno hotī" ti?|| ||

2. "Saddhañ ce bhikkhu, bhikkhu nissāya||
akusalaɱ pajahati kusalaɱ bhāveti pahīnam ev'assa taɱ akusalaɱ hoti.|| ||

■

Hiriñ ce bhikkhu, bhikkhu nissāya||
akusalaɱ pajahati kusalaɱ bhāveti pahīnam ev'assa taɱ akusalaɱ hoti.|| ||

■

Ottappañ ce bhikkhu, bhikkhu nissāya||
akusalaɱ pajahati kusalaɱ bhāveti pahīnam ev'assa taɱ akusalaɱ hoti.|| ||

■

Viriyañ ce bhikkhu, bhikkhu nissāya||
akusalaɱ pajahati kusalaɱ bhāveti pahīnam ev'assa taɱ akusalaɱ hoti.|| ||

■

Paññañ ce bhikkhu, bhikkhu nissāya||
akusalaɱ pajahati kusalaɱ bhāveta pahīnam ev'assa taɱ akusalaɱ hoti.|| ||

Taɱ hī'ssa bhikkhuno akusalaɱ pahīnaɱ hoti suppahīnaɱ,||
yaɱsa ariyāya paññāya disvā pahīnaɱ.|| ||

 

§

 

Tena ca pana bhikkhū, bhikkhunā imesu pañcasu dhammesu patiṭṭhāya cattāro dhammā upanissāya vihātabbā.|| ||

Katame cattāro?|| ||

Idha bhikkhu, bhikku||
saŋkhāy'ekaɱ paṭisevati,||
saŋkhāy'ekaɱ adhivāseti,||
saŋkhāy'ekaɱ parivajjeti,||
saŋkhāy'ekaɱ vinodeti.|| ||

Evaɱ kho bhikkhu bhikkhu nissaya-sampanno hotī ti.|| ||
