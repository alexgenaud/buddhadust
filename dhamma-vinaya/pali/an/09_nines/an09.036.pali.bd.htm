Aŋguttara Nikāya
VIII. Navaka Nipāta
IV. Mahā Vagga

Sutta 36

Jhāna-Nisasaya Suttaɱ

Adapted from the 1995 edition of the digital version of the Sri Lanka Buddha Jayanti Tripitaka Series.

 

 

[1][pts][than] Evaɱ me sutaɱ:|| ||

Sāvatthi nidānaɱ.|| ||

Paṭhamam p'ahaɱ Bhikkhave jhānaɱ nissāya āsavānaɱ khayaɱ vadāmi.|| ||

Dutiyam p'ahaɱ Bhikkhave jhānaɱ nissāya āsavānaɱ khayaɱ vadāmi.|| ||

Tatiyam p'ahaɱ bhikkhave jhānaɱ nissāya āsavānaɱ khayaɱ vadāmi.|| ||

Catuttham p'ahaɱ bhikkhave jhānaɱ nissāya āsavānaɱ khayaɱ vadāmi.|| ||

Ākāsanañ-c'āyatanam p'ahaɱ bhikkhave Nissāya āsavānaɱ khayaɱ vadāmi.|| ||

Viññānañ-c'āyatanam p'ahaɱ bhikkhave nissāya āsavānaɱ khayaɱ vadāmi.|| ||

Ākiñcaññ'āyatanaɱ p'ahaɱ bhikkhave nissāya āsavānaɱ khayaɱ vadāmi.|| ||

N'eva-saññā-nā-saññ'āyatanam p'ahaɱ bhikkhave nissāya āsavānaɱ khayaɱ vadāmi.|| ||

"Paṭhamam p'ahaɱ bhikkhave jhānaɱ nissāya āsavānaɱ khayaɱ vadāmī" ti||
iti kho pan'etaɱ vuttaɱ,||
kiñ c'etaɱ paṭicca vuttaɱ?|| ||

Idha, bhikkhave, vivicc'eva kāmehi vivicca akusalehi dhammehi sa-vitakkaɱ sa-vicāraɱ viveka-jaɱ pīti-sukhaɱ paṭhamaɱ-jhānaɱ upasampajja viharati.|| ||

So yad eva tattha hoti rūpa-gataɱ,||
vedanā-gataɱ,||
saññā-gataɱ,||
sankhāra-gataɱ,||
viññāṇa-gataɱ,||
te dhamme aniccato,||
dukkhato,||
rogato,||
gaṇḍato,||
sallato,||
aghato,||
ābādhato,||
parato,||
palokato,||
suññato,||
[423] anattato samanupassati.|| ||

So tehi dhammehi cittaɱ paṭivāpeti.|| ||

So tehi dhammehi cittaɱ paṭivāpetvā
amatāya dhātuyā cittaɱ upasaŋharati.|| ||

"Etaɱ santaɱ etaɱ paṇītaɱ||
yad idaɱ sabba-sankhāra-samatho||
sabb'upadhi-paṭinissaggo||
taṇha-k-khayo||
virāgo||
nirodho||
Nibbānan" ti.|| ||

So tattha ṭhito āsavānaɱ khayaɱ pāpuṇāti.|| ||

No ce āsavānaɱ khayaɱ pāpuṇāti ten'eva dhammarāgena tāya dhammanandiyā pañcannaɱ ora-m-bhāgiyānaɱ saŋyojanānaɱ parikkhayā opapātiko hoti tattha parinibbāyi anāvattidhammo tasmā lokā.|| ||

Seyyathā pi, bhikkhave, issāso vā issāsantevāsī vā tiṇapurisake.|| ||

Vā mattikāpuñje vā yoggaɱ karitvā so aparena samayena durepātī ca hoti akkhaṇa-vedhī ca mahato ca kāyassa padā'etā.|| ||

Evam eva kho bhikkhave bhikkhu vivicc'eva kāmehi vivicca akusalehi dhammehi sa-vitakkaɱ sa-vicāraɱ viveka-jaɱ pīti-sukhaɱ paṭhamaɱ-jhānaɱ upasampajja viharati.|| ||

So yad eva tattha hoti rūpa-gataɱ vedanā-gataɱ saññā-gataɱ sankhāra-gataɱ viññāṇa-gataɱ te dhamme aniccato dukkhato rogato gaṇḍato sallato aghato ābādhato parato palokato suññato anattato samanupassati.|| ||

So tehi dhammehi cittaɱ paṭivāpeti.|| ||

Sotehi dhammehi cittaɱ paṭivāpetvā.|| ||

Amatāya dhātuyā cittaɱ upasaŋharati.|| ||

"Etaɱ santaɱ etaɱ paṇītaɱ yad idaɱ sabba-sankhāra-samatho sabb'upadhi-paṭinissaggo taṇha-k-khayo virāgo nirodho Nibbānan" ti.|| ||

So tattha ṭhito āsavānaɱ khayaɱ pāpuṇāti.|| ||

No ce āsavānaɱ khayaɱ pāpuṇāti ten'eva dhammarāgena tāya dhammanandiyā pañcannaɱ ora-m-bhāgiyānaɱ saŋyojanānaɱ parikkhayā opapātiko hoti tattha parinibbāyi anāvattidhammo tasmā lokā.|| ||

"Paṭhamam p'ahaɱ bhikkhave jhānaɱ nissāyaɱ āsavānaɱ khayaɱ vadāmī" ti iti yaɱ taɱ vuttaɱ.|| ||

Idam etaɱ paṭicca vuttaɱ.|| ||

Dutiyam p'ahaɱ bhikkhave jhānaɱ nissāya āsavānaɱ khayaɱ vadāmī ti.|| ||

Tatiyam p'ahaɱ bhikkhave jhānaɱ nissāya āsavānaɱ khayaɱ vadāmī ti.|| ||

Catuttham p'ahaɱ bhikkhave jhānaɱ nissāya āsavānaɱ khayaɱ vadāmī ti.|| ||

Ākāsanañ-c'āyatanam p'ahaɱ bhikkhave nissāya āsavānaɱ khayaɱ vadāmī" ti.|| ||

Iti kho pan'etaɱ vuttaɱ,||
kiñ c'etaɱ paṭicca vuttaɱ:|| ||

Idha, bhikkhave, bhikkhu sabbaso rūpa-saññānaɱ samati-k-kamā paṭigha-saññānaɱ atthaŋ-gamā.|| ||

Nānattasaññānaɱ amanasi-kārā anatto ākāsoti Ākāsanañ-c'āyatanaɱ upasampajja viharati,||
so yad eva tattha hoti vedanā-gataɱ saññā-gataɱ sankhāra-gataɱ viññāṇa-gataɱ te dhamme aniccato dukkhato rogato gaṇḍato sallato aghato ābādhato parato palokato suññato anattato samanupassati.|| ||

So tehi dhammehi cittaɱ paṭivāpeti.|| ||

Sotehi dhammehi cittaɱ paṭivāpetvā amatāya dhātuyā cittaɱ upasaŋharati.|| ||

"Etaɱ santaɱ etaɱ paṇītaɱ yad idaɱ sabba-sankhāra-samatho sabb'upadhi-paṭinissaggo taṇha-k-khayo virāgo nirodho Nibbānan" ti.|| ||

So tattha ṭhito āsavānaɱ khayaɱ pāpuṇāti.|| ||

No ce āsavānaɱ khayaɱ pāpuṇāti ten'eva dhammarāgena tāya dhammanandiyā pañcannaɱ ora-m-bhāgiyānaɱ saŋyojanānaɱ parikkhayā opapātiko hoti tattha parinibbāyi anāvattidhammo tasmā lokā.|| ||

Seyyathā pi, bhikkhave, issāso vā issāsantevāsi vā tiṇapurisake vā mattikāpuñje vā yoggaɱ karitvā so aparena samayena durepāti ca hoti akkhaṇa-vedhī ca mahato kāyassa padā'etā.|| ||

Evam eva kho bhikkhave bhikkhu sabbaso rūpa-saññānaɱ samati-k-kamā paṭisasaññānaɱ atthaŋ-gamā nānatta-saññānaɱ amanasi-kārā anatto ākāsoti Ākāsanañ-c'āyatanaɱ upasampajja viharati. So yad eva tattha hoti vedanā-gataɱ saññā-gataɱ sankhāra-gataɱ viññāṇa-gataɱ te dhamme aniccato dukkhato rogato gaṇḍato sallato aghato ābādhato parato palokato suññato anattato samanupassati.|| ||

So tehi dhammehi cittaɱ paṭivāpeti sotehi dhammehi cittaɱ paṭivāpetvā amatāya dhātuyā cittaɱ upasaŋharati.|| ||

"Etaɱ santaɱ etaɱ paṇītaɱ yad idaɱ sabba-sankhāra-samatho sabb'upadhi-paṭinissaggo taṇha-k-khayo virāgo nirodho Nibbānan" ti.|| ||

So tattha ṭhito āsavānaɱ khayaɱ pāpuṇāti.|| ||

No ce āsavānaɱ khayaɱ pāpuṇāti ten'eva dhammarāgena tāya dhammanandiyā pañcannaɱ ora-m-bhāgiyānaɱ saŋyojanānaɱ parikkhayā opapātiko hoti tattha parinibbāyi anāvattidhammo tasmā lokā.|| ||

Ākāsanañ-c'āyatanam p'ahaɱ bhikkhave nissāya āsavānaɱ khayaɱ vadāmīti iti yaɱ taɱ vuttaɱ ida me taɱ paṭicca vuttaɱ.|| ||

Viññāṇañ-c'āyatanam p'ahaɱ bhikkhave nissāya āsavānaɱ khayaɱ vadāmīti.|| ||

Ākiñcaññ'āyatanam p'ahaɱ bhikkhave nissāya āsavānaɱ khayaɱ vadāmīti.|| ||

Iti kho pan'etaɱ vuttaɱ,||
kiñ c'etaɱ paṭicca vuttaɱ:||
idha bhikkhave bhikkhu sabbaso Viññāṇañ-c'āyatanaɱ samati-k-kamma n'atthi kiñciti Ākiñcaññ'āyatanaɱ upasampajja viharati.|| ||

So yad eva tattha hoti vedanā-gataɱ saññā-gataɱ sankhāra-gataɱ viññāṇa-gataɱ te dhamme aniccato dukkhato rogato gaṇḍato sallato aghato ābādhato parato palokato suññato anattato samanupassati.|| ||

So tehi dhammehi cittaɱ paṭivāpeti sotehi dhammehi cittaɱ paṭivāpetvā amatāya dhātuyā cittaɱ upasaŋharati.|| ||

"Etaɱ santaɱ etaɱ paṇītaɱ yad idaɱ sabba-sankhāra samatho sabbupadhipaṭi nissaggo taṇha-k-khayo virāgo nirodho Nibbānan" ti.|| ||

So tattha ṭhito āsavānaɱ khayaɱ pāpuṇāti.|| ||

No ce āsavānaɱ khayaɱ pāpuṇāti ten'eva dhammarāgena tāya dhammanandiyā pañcannaɱ ora-m-bhāgiyānaɱ saŋyojanānaɱ parikkhayā opapātiko hoti tattha parinibbāyi anāvattidhammo tasmā lokā.|| ||

"Paṭhamam p'ahaɱ bhikkhave jhānaɱ nissāyaɱ āsavānaɱ khayaɱ vadāmī" ti iti yaɱ taɱ vuttaɱ.|| ||

Idam etaɱ paṭicca vuttaɱ.|| ||

Iti kho bhikkhave yāvatā saññā-samāpatti tāvatā aññāpaṭivedho yāni ca kho imāni bhikkhave āyatanāni N'eva-saññā-nā-saññ'āyatanasamāpatti ca saññāvedadhita nirodho ca jhāyīhete bhikkhave bhikkhuna samāpatti-kusalehi samāpāttivuṭṭhāna-kusalehi samāpajjitvā vuṭṭha-hitvā samakkhātabbānīti vadāmī ti.|| ||
