Aŋguttara Nikāya
VIII. Navaka Nipāta
IV. Mahā Vagga

Sutta 40

Āraññaka-Nāg'Opama Suttaɱ

Adapted from the 1995 edition of the digital version of the Sri Lanka Buddha Jayanti Tripitaka Series.

 

[435]

[1][pts][than] Evaɱ me sutaɱ:|| ||

Sāvatthi nidānaɱ.|| ||

"Yasmiɱ bhikkhave samaye āraññakassa nāgassa gocarapasutassa hatthī pi||
hatthiniyo pi||
hattikalabhā pi||
hatthicchā pi||
purato gantvā tiṇaggāni chindanti,||
tena bhikkhave āraññako nāgo aṭṭhiyati harāyati jigucchati.|| ||

Yasmiɱ bhikkhave, samaye āraññakassa nāgassa gocarapasutassa hatthī pi||
hatthiniyo pi||
hatthikalabhā pi||
hatthicchāpā pi||
ohaggobhaggaɱ sākhābhaŋgaɱ khādanti.|| ||

Tena bhikkhave, āraññako nāgo aṭṭīyati harāyati jigucchati.|| ||

Yasmiɱ bhikkhave,||
samaye āraññakassa nāgassa ogāhaɱ otiṇṇassa hatthī pi||
hatthiniyo pi||
hatthikalabhā pi||
hatthicchāpā pi||
purato purato ganatvā soṇḍāya udakaɱ ālolonti.|| ||

Tena bhikkhave, āraññako nāgo aṭṭīyati harāyati jigucchati.|| ||

Yasmiɱ bhikkhave, samaye āraññakassa nāgassa ogābhaɱ otiṇṇassa hatthīniyo kāyaɱ upanighaɱsantiyo gacchanti,||
tena bhikkhave,||
āraññako nāgo aṭṭhiyati harāyati jigucchati.|| ||

2. Tasmaɱ bhikkhave, samaye āraññakassa nāgāssa evaɱ hoti:||
'ahaɱ kho etarahi ākaṇṇo viharāmi,||
hatthihi hatthinīhi hatthikalabhehi hatthicchāpehi,||
chinnaggāni c'eva tiṇāni khādāmi.|| ||

Obhaggobhaggañ ca me sākhabhaŋgaɱ khādanti.|| ||

Āvilāni ca pānīyāni pivāmi||
ogāhaɱ ca me otiṇṇassa hatthiniyo kāyaɱ upanighaɱsanniyo gaccanti||
yan nūnāhaɱ eko gaṇasmā vūpakaṭṭho vihareyyan' ti.|| ||

So aparena samayena eko gaṇasmā vūpakaṭṭho viharati||
accinnaggāni c'eva tiṇāni khādati.|| ||

Ohaggobhaggañ c'assa [436] sākhābhaŋgaɱ na khādanti anāvilāni ca pāniyāni pivati.|| ||

Ogāhañ c'assa otiṇṇassa na hatthiniyo kāyaɱ upanighaɱsanniyo gaccanti.|| ||

Tasmiɱ bhikkhave, samaye āraññakassa nāgāssa evaɱ hoti:||
'Ahaɱ kho pubbe ākiṇṇo vihāsiɱ hatthihi hatthinīhi hatthīkalabhehi hatthiccāpehi,||
chinnaggāni c'eva tiṇāni khādiɱ,||
ohaggobhaggañ ca me sākhābhaŋghaɱ khādiɱsu.|| ||

Āvilāni ca pānīyāni āpāyiɱ,||
ogāhañ ca me otiṇṇassa hatthiniyo kāyaɱ upanighaɱsanniyo agamaɱsu.|| ||

So'haɱ etarahi eko gaṇasmā vūpakaṭṭho viharāmi,||
acchinnaggāni c'eva tiṇāni khādāmi.|| ||

Obhaggobhaggañ ca me sākhābhaŋgaɱ na khādanti||
anāvilāni ca pānīyāni pivāmi,||
ogāhañ ca otiṇnassa na bhatthiniyo kāyāɱ upanighaɱsantiyo gaccantī' ti.|| ||

'So soṇḍāya sākhābhaŋgaɱ bhañjitvā sākhābhaŋgena kāyaɱ parimadditvā atta-mano kaṇḍuɱ saɱhanti.|| ||

[3] Evam eva kho bhikkhave, yasmiɱ samaye bhikku ākiṇṇo viharati||
bhikkhuhi bhikkhunīhi upāsakehi upāsākāhi||
raññā rāja-mahā-mattehi titthiyehi titthiyasāvakehi,||
tasmiɱ samaye bhikkhussa evaɱ hoti:||
'ahaɱ kho etarahi ākiṇṇo viharāmi,||
bhikkhuhī bhikkhunīhi upāsakehi upāsikāhi||
raññā rāja-mahā-mattehi titthiyehi titthiyasāvakehi.|| ||

Yan nūnāhaɱ eko gaṇasmā vūpakaṭṭho vihareyyan' ti.|| ||

So vicittaɱ sen'āsanaɱ bhajati araññaɱ [437] rukkha-mūlaɱ pabbataɱ kandaraɱ giri-guhaɱ susānaɱ vana-patthaɱ abbhokāsaɱ palālapuñjaɱ.|| ||

So araññagato vā rukkhamūgato vā suññ-ā-gāra-gato vā nisīdati pallaŋkaɱ ābhujitvā ujuɱ kāyaɱ paṇidhāya parimukhaɱ satiɱ upaṭṭha-petvā.|| ||

So abhijjhaɱ loke pahāya vigatābhijejhana cetasā viharati,||
abhijjhāya cittaɱ parisodheti.|| ||

Vyāpāda-padosaɱ pahāya akhyāpannacitto viharati||
sabba-pāṇa-bhutahitānukampī,||
vyāpāda-padosā cittaɱ parisodheti.|| ||

Thīna middhaɱ pahāya vigatathīna-middho viharati||
āloka-saññi sato sampajāno,||
thinamiddhā cittaɱ parisodheti.|| ||

Uddhacca-kukkuccaɱ pahāya anuddhato viharati||
ajjhattaɱ vūpasanta-citto uddhacca-kukkuccā cittaɱ parisodheti.|| ||

Vici-kicchaɱ pahāya tiṇṇa-vici-kiccho viharati||
akathaŋkathi kusalesu dhammesu,||
vicikicchāya cittaɱ parisodheti.|| ||

So ime pañca nīvaraṇe pahāya cetaso upakkilese paññāya dubbalī-karaṇe vivicc'eva kāmehi||
vivicca akusalehi dhammehi||
sa-vitakkaɱ sa-vicāraɱ||
viveka-jaɱ pīti-sukhaɱ||
paṭhamaɱ-jhānaɱ upasampajja viharati.|| ||

So atta-mano kaṇḍuɱ saɱhanti.|| ||

vitakka-vicārānaɱ vūpasamā||
ajjhattaɱ sampasādanaɱ||
cetaso ekodi-bhāvaɱ||
avitakkaɱ avicāraɱ||
samādhi-jaɱ pīti-sukhaɱ||
dutiyaɱ-jhānaɱ upasampajja viharati.|| ||

So atta-mano kaṇḍuɱ saɱhanti.|| ||

Pītiyā ca virāgā upekkhako ca||
viharati sato ca sampajāno,||
sukhañ ca kāyena paṭisaŋvedeti,||
yaɱ taɱ ariyā āvikkhanti upekkhako satimā sukha-vihārī ti,||
tatiyaɱ-jhānaɱ upasampajja viharati.|| ||

So atta-mano kaṇḍuɱ saɱhanti.|| ||

Sukhassa ca pahānā dukkhassa ca pahānā||
pubb'eva somanassa-domanassānaɱ atthaŋ-gamā||
adukkha-ɱ-asukhaɱ upekkhā-sati-pārisuddhiɱ||
catutthaɱ-jhānaɱ upasampajja viharati.|| ||

So atta-mano kaṇḍuɱ saɱhanti.|| ||

Sabbaso rūpa-saññānaɱ samati-k-kamā paṭigha-saññānaɱ atthaŋ-gamā nānatta-saññānaɱ amanasi-kārā 'anatto ākāso' ti,||
Ākāsanañ-c'āyatanaɱ upasampajja viharati.|| ||

So atta-mano kaṇḍuɱ saɱhanti.|| ||

Sabbaso Ākāsanañ-c'āyatanaɱ samati-k-kama 'anantaɱ viññānan' ti,||
Viññānañ-c'āyatanaɱ upasampajja viharati.|| ||

So atta-mano kaṇḍuɱ saɱhanti.|| ||

Sabbaso Viññānañ-c'āyatanaɱ samati-k-kamma 'n'atthi kiñci' ti||
Ākiñcaññ'āyatanaɱ upasampajja viharati.|| ||

So atta-mano kaṇḍuɱ saɱhanti.|| ||

Sabbaso Ākiñcaññ'āyatanaɱ samati-k-kamma N'eva-saññā-nā-saññ'āyatanaɱ upasampajja viharati.|| ||

So atta-mano kaṇḍuɱ saɱhanti.|| ||

Sabbaso N'eva-saññā-nā-saññ'āyatanaɱ [438] samati-k-kamma saññā-vedayita nirodhaɱ upasampajja viharati.|| ||

Paññāya c'assa disvā āsavā parikkhiṇā honti.|| ||

So atta-mano kaṇḍuɱ saɱhantī" ti.|| ||
