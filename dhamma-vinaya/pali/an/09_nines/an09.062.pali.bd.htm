Aṇguttara Nikāya
IX. Navaka Nipāta
VI. Khema Vagga

Sutta 62

Arahattā Bhabb-ā-Bhabba-Dhamma Suttaṃ

Adapted from the 1995 edition of the digital version of the Sri Lanka Buddha Jayanti Tripitaka Series.

 

[456]

[1][pts][than] Evaṃ me sutaṃ:|| ||

Sāvatthi nidānaṃ|| ||

"Nava bhikkhave dhamme a-p-pahāya abhabbo Arahattaṃ sacchi-kātuṃ.|| ||

Katame nava?|| ||

Rāgaṃ,||
dosaṃ,||
mohaṃ,||
kodhaṃ,||
upanāhaṃ,||
makkhaṃ,||
palāsaṃ,||
issaṃ,||
macchariyaṃ.|| ||

Ime kho bhikkhave nava dhamme a-p-pahāya abhabbo Arahattaṃ sacchi-kātuṃ.|| ||

 

§

 

Nava bhikikave dhamme pahāya bhabbo Arahattaṃ sacchi-kātuṃ|| ||

Katame nava?|| ||

Rāgaṃ,||
dosaṃ,||
mohaṃ,||
kodhaṃ,||
upanāhaṃ,||
makkhaṃ,||
palāsaṃ,||
issaṃ,||
macchariyaṃ."|| ||
