Aŋguttara Nikāya
IX. Navaka Nipāta
VI. Khema Vagga

Sutta 62

Arahattā Bhabb-ā-Bhabba-Dhamma Suttaɱ

Adapted from the 1995 edition of the digital version of the Sri Lanka Buddha Jayanti Tripitaka Series.

 

[456]

[1][pts][than] Evaɱ me sutaɱ:|| ||

Sāvatthi nidānaɱ|| ||

"Nava bhikkhave dhamme a-p-pahāya abhabbo Arahattaɱ sacchi-kātuɱ.|| ||

Katame nava?|| ||

Rāgaɱ,||
dosaɱ,||
mohaɱ,||
ko'dhaɱ,||
upan'āhaɱ,||
makkhaɱ,||
palāsaɱ,||
issaɱ,||
macchariyaɱ.|| ||

Ime kho bhikkhave nava dhamme a-p-pahāya abhabbo Arahattaɱ sacchi-kātuɱ.|| ||

 

§

 

Nava bhikikave dhamme pahāya bhabbo Arahattaɱ sacchi-kātuɱ|| ||

Katame nava?|| ||

Rāgaɱ,||
dosaɱ,||
mohaɱ,||
ko'dhaɱ,||
upan'āhaɱ,||
makkhaɱ,||
palāsaɱ,||
issaɱ,||
macchariyaɱ."|| ||
