Aŋguttara-Nikāya
III. Tika Nipāta
IV. Deva-Dūta Vagga

Sutta 39

Sukhumāla Suttaɱ (b)

Adapted from the 1995 edition of the digital version of the Sri Lanka Buddha Jayanti Tripitaka Series.

 

[146]

[1][pts][than] Evaɱ me sutaɱ.|| ||

Ekaɱ samayaɱ Bhagavā bhikkhū āmantesi:|| ||

"Bhikkhavo" ti.|| ||

"Bhadante" ti te bhikkhū Bhagavato paccassosuɱ.|| ||

Bhagavā etad avoca:|| ||

"Tayo'me bhikkhave madā.|| ||

Katame tayo?|| ||

Yobbana-mado,||
ārogya-mado,||
jīvita-mado.|| ||

 

§

 

Yobbana-mada-matto vā bhikkhave a-s-sutavā puthujjano||
kāyena du-c-caritaɱ carati,||
vācāya du-c-caritaɱ carati,||
manasā du-c-caritaɱ carati.|| ||

So kāyena du-c-caritaɱ caritvā||
vācāya du-c-caritaɱ caritvā||
manasā du-c-caritaɱ caritvā||
kāyassa bhedā param maraṇā apāyaɱ duggatiɱ vinipātaɱ Nirayaɱ uppajjati.|| ||

■

Āregya-mada-matto vā bhikkhave a-s-sutavā puthujjano||
kāyena du-c-caritaɱ carati,||
vācāya du-c-caritaɱ carati,||
manasā du-c-caritaɱ carati.|| ||

So kāyena du-c-caritaɱ caritvā||
vācāya du-c-caritaɱ caritvā||
manasā du-c-caritaɱ caritvā||
kāyassa bhedā param maraṇā apāyaɱ duggatiɱ vinipātaɱ Nirayaɱ uppajjati.|| ||

■

Jīvita-mada-matto vā bhikkhave a-s-sutavā puthujjano||
kāyena ducca- [147] ritaɱ carati,||
vācāya du-c-caritaɱ carati,||
manasā du-c-caritaɱ carati.|| ||

So kāyena du-c-caritaɱ caritvā||
vācāya du-c-caritaɱ caritvā||
manasā du-c-caritaɱ caritvā||
kāyassa bhedā param maraṇā apāyaɱ duggatiɱ vinipātaɱ Nirayaɱ uppajjati.|| ||

 

§

 

Yobbana-mada-matto vā bhakkhave bhikkhu sikkhaɱ pacca-k-khāya hīnāyāvattati.|| ||

Ārogya-mada-matto vā bhikkhave bhikkhu sikkhaɱ pacca-k-khāya hīnāyāvattati.|| ||

Jīvita-mada-matto vā bhikkhave bhikkhu sikkhaɱ pacca-k-khāya hīnāy'āvattatī" ti.|| ||

 

 

2. Vyādhi-dhammā jarā-dhammā atho maraṇa-dhammino.||
Yathā dhammā tathā santā jigucchanti puthujjanā.||
Ahaɱ ce'taɱ jiguccheyyaɱ evaɱ dhammesu pāṇisu,||
Na me'taɱ paṭirūpassa mama evaɱ vihārino.||
So'haɱ evaɱ viharanto ñatvā dhammaɱ nirūpadhiɱ,||
ārogye yobbanasmiɱ ca jivitasmiɱ ca yo madā.||
Sabbe made abhibho'smi nekkhammaɱ daṭṭhu khemato,||
Tassa me āhu ussāho nibbāṇaɱ abipassato.||
Nāhaɱ bhabbo etarahi kāmāni patisevituɱ.||
Anivattī bhavissāmi buhma-cariya-parāyano.|| ||
