Aŋguttara-Nikāya
III. Tika Nipāta
V. Cūḷa Vagga

Sutta 46

Sīlavanta Suttaɱ

Adapted from the 1995 edition of the digital version of the Sri Lanka Buddha Jayanti Tripitaka Series.

 

[151]

[1][pts][bodh] Evaɱ me sutaɱ.|| ||

Evaɱ me sutaɱ ekaɱ samayaɱ Bhagavā Sāvatthiyaɱ viharati.|| ||

Tatra kho Bhagavā bhikkhū āmantesi:|| ||

"Bhikkhavo" ti.|| ||

"Bhadante" ti te bhikkhū Bhagavato paccassosuɱ.|| ||

Bhagavā etad avoca:|| ||

"Yaɱ bhikkhave sīlavanto pabba-jitā gāmāɱ vā||
nigamaɱ vā upanissāya viharanti,||
tattha manussā tīhi ṭhānehi bahuɱ puññaɱ pasavanti.|| ||

Katamehi tīhi?|| ||

[152] Kāyena,
vācāya,
manasā.|| ||

Yaɱ bhikkhave sīlavanto pabba-jitā gāmaɱ vā||
nigamaɱ vā upanissāya viharanti,||
tattha manussā imehi tīhi ṭhānehi bahuɱ puññaɱ pasavantī" ti.|| ||
