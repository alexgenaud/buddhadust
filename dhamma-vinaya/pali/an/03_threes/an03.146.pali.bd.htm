Aŋguttara-Nikāya
III. Tika Nipāta
XV. Maŋgala Vagga

Sutta 146

Dutiya Khata Suttaɱ

Adapted from the 1995 edition of the digital version of the Sri Lanka Buddha Jayanti Tripitaka Series.

 

[293]

[1][pts] Evaɱ me sutaɱ|| ||

Ekaɱ samayaɱ Bhagavā Sāvatthiyaɱ viharati.|| ||

Tatra kho Bhagavā bhikkhū āmantesi:|| ||

"Bhikkhavo" ti.|| ||

"Bhadante" ti te bhikkhū Bhagavato paccassosuɱ.|| ||

Bhagavā etad avoca:|| ||

"Tīhi bhikkhave dhammehi samannāgato bālo avyatto asappuriso khataɱ upahataɱ attānaɱ pariharati||
sāvajjo ca hoti||
sānuvajjo vuññūtaɱ||
bahuñ ca apuññaɱ pasavati.|| ||

Katamehi tīhi?|| ||

Sāvajjena kāya-kammena,||
sāvajjena vacī-kammena,||
sāvajjena mano-kammena.|| ||

Imehi ko bhikkhave tīhi dhammehi samannāgato bālo avyatto asappurise khataɱ upahataɱ attānaɱ pariharati,||
sāvajjo ca hoti,||
sānuvajjo viññūtaɱ,||
pahuñ ca apuññaɱ pasavati.|| ||

 

§

 

Tīhi bhikkhave dhammehi samannāgato paṇḍito viyatto sappuriso akkhataɱ anupahataɱ attānaɱ pariharati,||
anavajjo ca hoti,||
ananuvajjo viññūtaɱ,||
pahuñ ca puññaɱ pasavati.|| ||

Katamehi tīhi?|| ||

Anavajjena kāya-kammena,||
anavajjena vacī-kammena,||
anavajjena mano kammena.|| ||

Imehi ko bhikkhave tīhi dhammehi samannāgato bālo avyatto asappuriso khataɱ upahataɱ attānaɱ pariharati,||
sāvajjo ca hoti,||
sānuvajjo viññūnaɱ,||
pahuñ ca apuññaɱ pasavatī" ti.|| ||
