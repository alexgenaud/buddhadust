Aŋguttara-Nikāya
III. Tika Nipāta
IX. Samaṇa Vagga

Sutta 87

Tatiya Sikkha Suttaɱ

Adapted from the 1995 edition of the digital version of the Sri Lanka Buddha Jayanti Tripitaka Series.

 

[234]

[1][pts] Evaɱ me sutaɱ.|| ||

Ekaɱ samayaɱ Bhagavā bhikkhū āmantesi:|| ||

"Bhikkhavo" ti.|| ||

"Bhadante" ti te bhikkhū Bhagavato paccassosuɱ.|| ||

Bhagavā etad avoca:|| ||

"Sādhikam idaɱ bhikkhave diyaḍḍha-sikkhāpadasataɱ anvaddhamāsaɱ uddesaɱ āga-c-chati,||
yattha attha-kāmā kula-puttā sikkhanti.|| ||

Tisso imā bhikkhave sikkhā,||
yatth'etaɱ sabbaɱ samodhānaɱ gacchati.|| ||

Katamā tisso?|| ||

Adhisīla-sikkhā,||
adhicitta-sikkhā,||
adhipaññā-sikkhā.|| ||

Imā ko bhikkhave tisso sikkhā,||
yatth'etaɱ sabbaɱ samodhānaɱ gacchati.|| ||

 

§

 

2. Idha pana bhikkhave bhikkhu sīlesu paripūra-kārī hoti,||
samādhismiɱ paripūra kārī||
paññāya paripūra kārī.|| ||

So yāni tāni khuddānukhuddakāni sikkhā-padāni,||
tāni āpajjati pi||
vuṭṭhāti pi.|| ||

Taɱ kissa hetu?|| ||

Na hi m'ettha bhikkhave abhabbatā vuttā.|| ||

■

Yāni kho tāni sikkhā-padāni ādibrahma-cariyikāni buhmacariyasāruppāni,||
tattha dhuvasīlo ca hoti||
ṭhitasīli ca||
samādāya sikkhati sikkhā-padesu.|| ||

So āsavānaɱ khayā anāsavaɱ||
cetāvimuttiɱ||
paññā-vimuttiɱ||
diṭṭhe'va dhamme sayaɱ abhiññā sacchi-katvā||
upasampajja viharati.|| ||

■

3. Taɱ vā pana anabhisambhavaɱ appaṭivijjhaɱ pañcannaɱ ora-m-bhāgiyānaɱ saŋyojanānaɱ parikkhayā antarā-parinibbāyī hoti.|| ||

■

Taɱ vā pana anabhisambhavaɱ appaṭivijjhaɱ pañcannaɱ ora-m-bhāgiyānaɱ saŋyojanānaɱ parikkhayā upahacca-parinibbāyī hoti.|| ||

■

So pañcannaɱ ora-m-bhāgiyānaɱ saŋyojanānaɱ parikkhayā asa-sankhāra-parinibbāyī hoti.|| ||

■

So pañcannaɱ ora-m-bhāgiyānaɱ saŋyojanānaɱ parikkhayā sa-sankhāra-parinibbāyī hoti.|| ||

■

So pañcannaɱ ora-m-bhāgiyānaɱ saŋyojanānaɱ parikkhayā uddhaɱ-soto hoti.|| ||

■

So pañcannaɱ ora-m-bhāgiyānaɱ saŋyojanānaɱ parikkhayā Akaniṭṭha-gāmi hoti.|| ||

■

Taɱ vā pana anabhisambhavaɱ appaṭivijjhaɱ tiṇaɱṇaɱ saŋyojanānaɱ parikkhayā rāga-dosa-mohānaɱ tanuttā Sakad'āgāmī hoti,||
sakideva imaɱ [235] lokaɱ āgannvā dukkhassantaɱ karoti.|| ||

■

Taɱ vā pana anabhisambhavaɱ appaṭivijjhaɱ tiṇaɱṇaɱ saŋyojanānaɱ parikkhayā eka-bīji hoti,||
ekaɱ yeva mānusakaɱ bhavaɱ nibbattetvā dukkhassantaɱ karoti.|| ||

■

Taɱ vā pana anabhisambhavaɱ appaṭivijjhaɱ tiṇaɱṇaɱ saŋyojanānaɱ parikkhayā kolaŋkolo hoti,||
dve vā tīṇi vā kulāni sandhāvitvā dukkhassantaɱ karoti.|| ||

■

Taɱ vā pana anabhisambhavaɱ appaṭivijjhaɱ tiṇaɱṇaɱ saŋyojanānaɱ parikkhayā satta-k-khatt'uparamo hoti.|| ||

Satta-k-khatt'uparamaɱ deve ca manusse ca sandhāvitvā saɱsaritvā dukkhassanta karoti.|| ||

■

Iti kho bhikkhave paripūraɱ||
paripūra-kārī||
ārādheti padesaɱ||
padesakārī.|| ||

Avañjhāni tvevāhaɱ bhikkhave sikkāpadāni vadimi" ti.|| ||
