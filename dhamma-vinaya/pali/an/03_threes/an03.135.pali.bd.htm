Aŋguttara-Nikāya
III. Tika Nipāta
XIV. Yodh-ā-jīva Vagga

Sutta 135

Kesa-Kambalo Suttaɱ

Adapted from the 1995 edition of the digital version of the Sri Lanka Buddha Jayanti Tripitaka Series.

 

[286]

[1][pts][olds] Evaɱ me sutaɱ:|| ||

Ekaɱ samayaɱ Bhagavā Sāvatthiyaɱ viharati.|| ||

Tatra kho Bhagavā bhikkhū āmantesi:|| ||

"Bhikkhavo" ti.|| ||

"Bhadante" ti te bhikkhū Bhagavato paccassosuɱ.|| ||

Bhagavā etad avoca:|| ||

"Seyyathā pi, bhikkhave, yāni kānici tantāvutānaɱ vatthānaɱ kesakambhalo tesaɱ paṭikiṭṭho akkhāyati.

Kesa-kambalo bhikkhave||
sīte sīto||
uṇhe uṇho,||
dubbaṇṇo||
duggandho||
dukkha-sampasso||
evam eva kho, bhikkhave, yāni kānici puthu-samaṇappavādānaɱ Makkhalivādo tesaɱ paṭikiṭṭho akkhāyati.|| ||

■

Makkhalī bhikkhave mogha-puriso evaɱ-vādi evaɱ-diṭṭhi:|| ||

'N'atthi kammaɱ,||
n'atthi kiriyaɱ,||
n'atthi viriyan' ti.|| ||

 

§

 

[287] [2][pts][olds] Ye pi te bhikkhave ahesuɱ atītam addhānaɱ arahanto Sammā-Sam-Buddhā,||
te pi Bhagavanto kamma-vādā c'eva ahesuɱ kiriya-vādā ca viriyavādā ca.|| ||

Te pi bhikkhave Makkhalī mogha-puriso paṭibāhati:|| ||

'N'atthi kammaɱ,||
n'atthi kiriyaɱ,||
n'atthi viriyan' ti.|| ||

■

[3][pts][olds] Ye pi te bhikkhave bhavissanti anāgataɱ addhānaɱ arahanto Sammā-Sam-Buddhā,||
te pi Bhagavanto kammāvādā c'eva bhavissanti kiriya-vādā ca viriyavādā ca.|| ||

Te pi bhikkhave Makkhalī mogha-puriso paṭibāhati:|| ||

'N'atthi kammaɱ,||
n'atthi kiriyaɱ,||
n'atthi viriyan' ti.|| ||

■

[4][pts][olds] Aham pi bhikkhave etarahi arahaɱ Sammā-Sam-Buddho kammavādo ca kiriyavādo ca viriyavādo.|| ||

Mam pi bhikkhave Makkhalī mogha-puriso paṭibāhati:|| ||

'N'atthi kammaɱ,||
n'atthi kiriyaɱ,||
n'atthi viriyan' ti.|| ||

■

[5][pts][olds] Seyyathā pi, bhikkhave, nadī-mukhe khipaɱ uḍḍeyya bahunnaɱ macchānaɱ ahitāya dukkhāya anayāya vyāsanāya,||
evam eva kho, bhikkhave, Makkhalī mogha-puriso manussakhipaɱ maññe loke uppanno bahunnaɱ sattānaɱ ahitāya dukkhāya anayāya vyasanāyā" ti.|| ||
