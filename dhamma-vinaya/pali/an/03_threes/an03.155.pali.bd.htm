Aŋguttara-Nikāya
III. Tika Nipāta
XVI. Acelaka Vagga

Sutta 155

Nikkhitto Niraye Suttaɱ (c)

Adapted from the 1995 edition of the digital version of the Sri Lanka Buddha Jayanti Tripitaka Series.

 

[297]

[1][pts] Evaɱ me sutaɱ:|| ||

Ekaɱ samayaɱ Bhagavā Sāvatthiyaɱ viharati.|| ||

Tatra kho Bhagavā bhikkhū āmantesi:|| ||

"Bhikkhavo" ti.|| ||

"Bhadante" ti te bhikkhū Bhagavato paccassosuɱ.|| ||

Bhagavā etad avoca:|| ||

"Tīhi bhikkhave dhammehi samannāgato yath'ābhataɱ nikkhitto evaɱ Niraye.|| ||

Katamehi tīhi?|| ||

Attanā ca kāmesu micchā-cārī hoti,||
parañ ca kāmesu micchā-cāre sam-ā-dapeti,||
kāmesu micchā-cāre ca samanuñño hoti.|| ||

Imehi ko bikkhave tīhi dhammehi samannāgato yath'ābhataɱ nikkitto evaɱ Niraye.|| ||

 

§

 

"Tīhi bhikkhave dhammehi samannāgato yath'ābhataɱ nikkhitto evaɱ sagge.|| ||

Katamehi tīhi?|| ||

Attanā ca kāmesu micchā-cārā paṭivirato hoti,||
parañ ca kāmesu micchā-cārā veramaṇiyā sam-ā-dapeti,||
kāmesu micchā-cārā veramaṇiyā ca samanuñño hoti.|| ||

Imehi ko bhikkhave tīhi dhammehi samannāgato yath'ābhataɱ nikkhitto evaɱ Niraye" ti.|| ||
