Aŋguttara-Nikāya
III. Tika Nipāta
XIII. Kusināra Vagga

Sutta 128

Dutiya Anuruddha Suttaɱ

Adapted from the 1995 edition of the digital version of the Sri Lanka Buddha Jayanti Tripitaka Series.

 

[281]

[1][pts][olds]Ekaɱ samayaɱ Bhagavā Bārāṇasiyaɱ viharati Isipatane Migadāye.|| ||

Atha ko āyasmā Anuruddho yen'āyasmā Sāriputto ten'upasankami.|| ||

Upasaŋkamitvā āyasmatā Sāriputtena saddhiɱ sammodi.|| ||

Sammodanīyaɱ kathaɱ sārāṇiyaɱ vītisā- [282] retvā eka-m-antaɱ nisīdi.|| ||

Eka-m-antaɱ nisinno kho āyasmā Anuruddho āyasmantaɱ Sāriputtaɱ etad avoca:|| ||

"Idāhaɱ āvuso Sāriputta,||
dibbena cakkhunā visuddhena atikkanta-mānusa-kena sahassaɱ lokaɱ olokemi.|| ||

Āraddhaɱ kho pana me viriyaɱ asallīnaɱ upaṭṭhitā sati asammuṭṭhā.|| ||

Passaddho kāyo asāraddho samāhitaɱ cittaɱ ek'aggaɱ.|| ||

Atha ca pana me na anupādāya āsavehi cittaɱ vumuccatī" ti.|| ||

[Sāriputto:]|| ||

2. "Yaɱ kho te āvuso Anuruddha evaɱ hoti:|| ||

'Āhaɱ dibbena cakkhunā visuddhena atikkanta-mānusa-kena sahassaɱ lokaɱ olokemī' ti|| ||

Idan te mānasmiɱ.|| ||

Yam pi te āvuso Anuruddha evaɱ hoti:|| ||

'Āraddhaɱ kho pana me viriyaɱ asallīnaɱ upaṭṭhitā sati apammuṭṭhā passaddho kāyo asāraddho samāhitaɱ cittaɱ ek'aggan' ti|| ||

Idan te uddhaccasmiɱ.|| ||

Yam pi te āvuso Anuruddha evaɱ hoti:|| ||

'Atha ca pana me na anupādāya asāvehi cittaɱ vimuccatī' ti|| ||

Idan te kukkuccasmiɱ.|| ||

Sādhu vat'āyasmā Anuruddho ime tayo dhamme pahāya ime tayo dhamme a-mana-sikaritvā amatāya dhātuyā cittaɱ upasaŋharatū" ti.|| ||

■

3. Atha kho āyasmā Anuruddho aparena samayena ime tayo dhamme pahāya ime tayo dhamme a-mana-sikaritvā amatāya dhātuyā cittaɱ upasaŋhasi.|| ||

Atha kho āyasmā Anuruddho eko vūpakaṭṭho appamatto ātāpi pahit'atto viharanto na cirass'eva yass'atthāya kula-puttā samma-d-eva agārasmā anagāriyaɱ pabbajanti tad anuttaraɱ brahmaciraya-pariyosānaɱ diṭṭhe'va dhamme sayaɱ abhiññā sacchi-katvā upasampajja vihāsi.|| ||

"Khīnā jāti,||
vūsitaɱ Brahma-cariyaɱ,||
kataɱ karaṇīyaɱ,||
nāparaɱ itthattāyā" ti abbhaññāsi.|| ||

Aññataro ca pan'āyasmā Anuruddho arahataɱ ahosī.|| ||
