Aŋguttara-Nikāya
III. Tika Nipāta
V. Cūḷa Vagga

Sutta 44

Kathā-Pavatti Suttaɱ

Adapted from the 1995 edition of the digital version of the Sri Lanka Buddha Jayanti Tripitaka Series.

 

[151]

[1][pts][bodh][olds] Evaɱ me sutaɱ.|| ||

Evaɱ me sutaɱ ekaɱ samayaɱ Bhagavā Sāvatthiyaɱ viharati.|| ||

Tatra kho Bhagavā bhikkhū āmantesi:|| ||

"Bhikkhavo" ti.|| ||

"Bhadante" ti te bhikkhū Bhagavato paccassosuɱ.|| ||

Bhagavā etad avoca:|| ||

"Tīhi bhikkhave ṭhānehi kathā pavattanī hoti.|| ||

Katamehi tīhi?|| ||

Yo dhammaɱ deseti,||
so attha-paṭisaŋvedī ca hoti dhamma-paṭisaŋvedī ca.|| ||

Yo dhammaɱ suṇāti,||
so attha-paṭisavedī ca hoti dhamma-paṭisaŋvedī ca.|| ||

Yo c'eva dhammaɱ deseti,||
yo ca dhammaɱ suṇāti,||
ubho attha-paṭisaŋvedino ca honti||
dhamma-paṭisaŋvedino ca.|| ||

Imehi kho bhikkhave tīhi ṭhānehi kathā pavattanī hotī" ti.|| ||
