Aṅguttara-Nikāya
III. Tika Nipāta
XV. Maṅgala Vagga

Sutta 145

Paṭhama Khata Suttaɱ

Adapted from the 1995 edition of the digital version of the Sri Lanka Buddha Jayanti Tripitaka Series.

 

[293]

[1][pts] Evaɱ me sutaɱ:|| ||

Ekaɱ samayaɱ Bhagavā Sāvatthiyaɱ viharati.|| ||

Tatra kho Bhagavā bhikkhū āmantesi:|| ||

"Bhikkhavo" ti.|| ||

"Bhadante" ti te bhikkhū Bhagavato paccassosuɱ.|| ||

Bhagavā etad avoca:|| ||

"Tīhi bhikkhave dhammehi samannāgato bālo avyatto a-sappuriso khataɱ upahataɱ attāṇaɱ pariharati||
sāvajjo ca hoti||
sānuvajjo vuññūtaɱ||
bahuñ ca apuññaɱ pasavati.|| ||

Katamehi tīhi?|| ||

Akusalena kāya-kammena,||
akusalena vacī-kammena,||
akusalena mano-kammena.|| ||

Imehi ko bhikkhave tīhi dhammehi samannāgato bālo avyatto a-sappurise khataɱ upahataɱ attāṇaɱ pariharati,||
sāvajjo ca hoti,||
sānuvajjo viññūtaɱ,||
pahuñ ca apuññaɱ pasavati.|| ||

 

§

 

Tīhi bhikkhave dhammehi samannāgato paṇḍito viyatto sappuriso akkhataɱ anupahataɱ attāṇaɱ pariharati,||
anavajjo ca hoti,||
ananuvajjo viññūtaɱ,||
pahuñ ca puññaɱ pasavati.|| ||

Katamehi tīhi?|| ||

Kusalena kāya-kammena,||
kusalena vacī-kammena,||
kusalena mano kammena.|| ||

Imehi ko bhikkhave tīhi dhammehi samannāgato bālo avyatto a-sappuriso khataɱ upahataɱ attāṇaɱ pariharati,||
sāvajjo ca hoti,||
sānuvajjo viññūnaɱ,||
pahuñ ca apuññaɱ pasavatī" ti.|| ||
