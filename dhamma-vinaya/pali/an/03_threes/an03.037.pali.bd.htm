Aŋguttara-Nikāya
III. Tika Nipāta
IV. Deva-Dūta Vagga

Sutta 37

Dutiya Catu-Mahārāja Suttaɱ

Adapted from the 1995 edition of the digital version of the Sri Lanka Buddha Jayanti Tripitaka Series.

 

[143]

[1][bit][pts][bodh] Evaɱ me sutaɱ.|| ||

Ekaɱ samayaɱ Bhagavā bhikkhū āmantesi:|| ||

"Bhikkhavo" ti.|| ||

"Bhadante" ti te bhikkhū Bhagavato paccassosuɱ.|| ||

Bhagavā etad avoca:|| ||

"Bhūta-pubbaɱ bhikkhave Sakko devānam indo deve Tāvatiɱse anunayamāno tāyaɱ velāyaɱ imaɱ gāthaɱ abhāsi:|| ||

[144] 'Cātuddasiɱ pañcadasi yāva pakkhassa aṭṭhamī||
Pāṭihāriyapakkhañ ca aṭṭh'aŋga-susamā-gataɱ,||
Uposathaɱ upavaseyya yo p'assa mādiso naro' ti.|| ||

Sā kho pan'esā bhikkhave Sakkena devānam indena gāthā duggītā, na sugītā,||
dubbhā-sitā, na subhā-sitā.|| ||

Taɱ kissa hetu?|| ||

Sakko bhikkave devānam indo avīta-rāgo avīta-doso avīta-moho.|| ||

Yo ca kho so bhikkhave bhikkhu arahaɱ khīṇ'āsavo vusitavā kata-karaṇīyo ohita-bhāro anuppatta-sadattho pari-k-khīṇa-bhava-saŋyojano sammadaññā-vimutto.|| ||

Tassa kho etaɱ bhikkhave bhikkhuno kallaɱ vacanāya:|| ||

'Cātuddasiɱ pañcadasi yāva pakkhassa aṭṭhamī||
Pāṭihāriyapakkhaɱ ca aṭṭh'aŋga-susamā-gataɱ,||
Uposathaɱ upavaseyya yo p'assa mādiso naro' ti.|| ||

Taɱ kissa hetu?|| ||

So hi bhikkhave bhikkhu vīta-rāgo||
vīta-doso||
vīta-moho.|| ||

Bhūta-pubbaɱ bhikkhave Sakko devānam indo deve Tāvatiɱse anunayamāno tāyaɱ velāyaɱ imaɱ gāthaɱ abhāsi:-|| ||

'Cātuddasi pañcadasi yāva pakkhassa aṭṭhamī,||
Pāṭihāriyapakkhaɱ ca aṭṭh'aŋga-susamā-gataɱ||
Uposathaɱ upavaseyya yo p'assa mādiso naro' ti.|| ||

Sā kho pan'esā bhikkhave Sakkena devānam indena gāthā duggītā,||
na sugītā,||
dubbhā-sitā,||
na subhā-sitā.|| ||

Taɱ kissa hetu?|| ||

Sakko hi bhikkhave devānam indo aparimutto jātiyā jarāya maraṇena sokehi paridevehi dukkhehi domanassehi upāyāsehi,||
aparimutto dukkhasmā ti vadāmi.|| ||

Yo ca kho so bhikkhave bhikkhu arahaɱ khīṇ'āsavo vusitavā kata-karaṇīyo ohita-bhāro anuppattasadattho pari-k-khīṇa-bhava-saŋyojano sammadaññā-vimutto,||
tassa kho etaɱ bhikkhave bhikkhuno kallaɱ vacānāya.|| ||

'Cātuddasi pañcadasī yāva pakkhassa aṭṭhamī||
Pāṭihāriyapakkhañ ca aṭṭh'aŋga-susamā-gataɱ,||
Uposathaɱ upavaseyya yo p'assa mādiso naro' ti. || ||

[145] Taɱ kissa hetu?|| ||

So bhikkhave bhikkhu parimutto jātiyā jarāyā maraneṇa sokehi paridevehi dukkhehi domanassehi upāyāsehi,||
parimutto dukkhasmā ti vadāmi" ti.|| ||
