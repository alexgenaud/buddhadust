Aŋguttara-Nikāya
III. Tika Nipāta
V. Cūḷa Vagga

Sutta 42

Tiṭhāna Suttaɱ

Adapted from the 1995 edition of the digital version of the Sri Lanka Buddha Jayanti Tripitaka Series.

 

[150]

[1][pts][bodh] Evaɱ me sutaɱ.|| ||

Evaɱ me sutaɱ ekaɱ samayaɱ Bhagavā Sāvatthiyaɱ viharati.|| ||

Tatra kho Bhagavā bhikkhū āmantesi:|| ||

"Bhikkhavo" ti.|| ||

"Bhadante" ti te bhikkhū Bhagavato paccassosuɱ.|| ||

Bhagavā etad avoca:|| ||

"Tīhi bhikkhave ṭhānehi saddho pasanno veditabbo.|| ||

Katamehi tīhi?|| ||

Sīla-vataɱ dassana-kāmo hoti,||
Sad'Dhammaɱ sotukāmo hoti,||
vigata-mala maccherena cetasā agāraɱ ajjhāvasati mutta-cāgo payata-pāṇī vossagga-rato yā cayogo dāna-saɱvibhāga-rato.|| ||

Imehi kho bhikkhave tīhi ṭhānehi saddho pasanno veditabbo" ti.|| ||

 

 

Dassanakāmo sīla-vataɱ Sad'Dhammaɱ sotum icchati,||
Vineyya macchera-malaɱ sa ve saddho ti vuccatī.|| ||
