Aŋguttara-Nikāya
III. Tika Nipāta
XIII. Kusināra Vagga

Sutta 122

Bhaṇḍana Suttaɱ

Adapted from the 1995 edition of the digital version of the Sri Lanka Buddha Jayanti Tripitaka Series.

 

[275]

[1][pts] Evaɱ me sutaɱ.|| ||

Ekaɱ samayaɱ Bhagavā Sāvatthiyaɱ viharati.|| ||

Tatra kho Bhagavā bhikkhū āmantesi:|| ||

"Bhikkhavo" ti.|| ||

"Bhadante" ti te bhikkhū Bhagavato paccassosuɱ.|| ||

Bhagavā etad avoca:|| ||

"Yassaɱ bhikkhave disāyaɱ bhikkhū bhaṇḍana-jātā kalaha-jātā vivādāpannā añña-maññaɱ mukha-sattīhi vitu-dantā viharanti,||
mana-sikātum pi me esā bikkhave disā na phāsu hoti,||
pageva gantuɱ.|| ||

Niṭṭhaɱ ettha gacchāmi:|| ||

'Addhā te āyasmanto tayo dhamme pajahiɱsu,||
tayo dhamme bahulī-m-akaɱsu' ti.|| ||

■

Katame tayo dhamme pajahiɱsu?|| ||

Nekkhamma-vitakkaɱ,||
avyāpāda-vitakkaɱ,||
avihiɱsā-vitakkaɱ.|| ||

Ime tayo dhamme pajahiɱsu.|| ||

■

Katame tayo dhamme bahulī-m-akaɱsu?|| ||

Kāma-vitakkaɱ,||
vyāpāda-vitakkaɱ,||
vihiɱsā-vitakkaɱ.|| ||

Ime tayo dhamme bahulī-m-akaɱsu|| ||

■

Yassaɱ bhikkhave disāyaɱ bhikkhū bhaṇḍana-jātā kalaha-jātā vivādāpannā añña-maññaɱ mukhasattihi vitu-dantā viharanti,||
mana-sikātum pi me esā bhikkhave disā na phāsu hoti,||
pageva gantuɱ.|| ||

Niṭṭham ettha gacchāmi:|| ||

'Addhā te āyasmanto ime tayo dhamme pajahiɱsu' ti.|| ||

 

§

 

Ime tayo dhamme bahulī-m-akaɱsu.|| ||

Yassaɱ bhikakhave disāyaɱ bhikkhū samaggā sammo-damānā aviva-damānā khīrodakī-bhūtā añña-maññaɱ piya-cakkhūhi sampassantā viharanti.|| ||

Gantum pi me esā bikkhave disā phāsu hoti||
pageva mana-sikātuɱ.|| ||

Niṭṭhamettha gacchāmi:|| ||

'Addhā te āyasmanto ime tayo dhamme pajahiɱsu,||
ime tayo dhamme bahulī-m-akaɱsu' ti.|| ||

■

Katame tayo dhamme pajahiɱsu?|| ||

[276] Kāma-vitakkaɱ,||
vyāpāda-vitakkaɱ,||
vihiɱsā-vitakkaɱ.|| ||

Ime tayo dhamme pajahiɱsu.|| ||

■

Katame tayo dhamme bahulī-m-akaɱsu?|| ||

Nekkhamma-vitakkaɱ,||
avyāpāda-vitakkaɱ||
avihiɱsā-vitakkaɱ.|| ||

Ime tayo dhamme bahulī-m-akaɱsu.|| ||

■

Yassa bhikkhave disāyaɱ bhikkhū samaggā sammoda-mānā avivada-mānā khīrodakī-bhūtā añña-maññaɱ piyacakkhūhi sampassantā viharanti,||
gantum pi me esā bhikkhave disā phāsu hoti||
pageva mana-sikātuɱ.|| ||

Niṭṭhamettha gacchāmi:|| ||

'Addhā te āyasmanto ime tayo dhamme pajahiɱsu,||
ime tayo dhamme bahulamakaɱsū'ti" ti.
