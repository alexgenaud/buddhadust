Aŋguttara-Nikāya
III. Tika Nipāta
XIII. Kusināra Vagga

Sutta 123

Gotamaka-Cetiya Suttaɱ

Adapted from the 1995 edition of the digital version of the Sri Lanka Buddha Jayanti Tripitaka Series.

 

[276]

[1][pts][than][olds] Evaɱ me sutaɱ:|| ||

Ekaɱ samayaɱ Bhagavā Vesāliyaɱ viharati Gotamake cetiye.|| ||

Tatra kho Bhagavā bhikkhū āmantesi:|| ||

"Bhikkhavo" ti.|| ||

"Bhadante" ti te bhikkhū Bhagavato paccassosuɱ.|| ||

Bhagavā etad avoca:|| ||

"Abhiññāyāhaɱ bikkave Dhammaɱ desemi||
no anabiññāya.|| ||

Sanidān'āhaɱ bhikkhave Dhammaɱ desemi||
no anidānaɱ.|| ||

Sappāṭihāriyāhaɱ bhikkhave Dhammaɱ desemi||
no appāṭihāriyaɱ.|| ||

Tassa mayhaɱ bikkave abhiññāya Dhammaɱ desayato||
no anabhiññāya,||
sanidānaɱ Dhammaɱ desayato||
no anidānaɱ,||
sappāṭihāriyaɱ Dhammaɱ desayato||
no appāṭihāriyaɱ,||
karaṇiyo ovādo karaṇiyā anusāsanī.|| ||

Alañ ca pana vo bhikkhave tuṭṭhiyā,||
alaɱ atta-manatāya,||
alaɱ somanassāya 'Sammā-Sam-Buddho Bhagavā,||
svākkhāto Bhagavatā Dhammo,||
su-paṭipanno saŋgho'" ti.|| ||

Idam avoca Bhagavā.|| ||

Attamanā te bhikkhū Bhagavato bhāsitaɱ abhinanduɱ.|| ||

Imasmiñ ca pana veyyā-kara-ṇasmiɱ bhaññamāne sahassī loka-dhātu akampitthā.
