Aṅguttara-Nikāya
III. Tika Nipāta
XI. Sambodhi Vagga

Sutta 108

Dutiya Nidāna Suttaɱ

Adapted from the 1995 edition of the digital version of the Sri Lanka Buddha Jayanti Tripitaka Series.

 

[263]

[1][pts] Evaɱ me sutaɱ.|| ||

Ekaɱ samayaɱ Bhagavā Sāvatthiyaɱ viharati.|| ||

Tatra kho Bhagavā bhikkhū āmantesi:|| ||

"Bhikkhavo" ti.|| ||

"Bhadante" ti te bhikkhū Bhagavato paccassosuɱ.|| ||

Bhagavā etad avoca:|| ||

"Tīṇ'imāni bhikkhave nidānāni kammānaɱ samudayāya.|| ||

Katamāni tīṇi?|| ||

Alobho nidānaɱ kammānaɱ samudayāya.|| ||

Adoso nidānaɱ kammānaɱ samudayāya.|| ||

Amoho nidānaɱ kammānaɱ samudāyāya.|| ||

Yaɱ bhikkhave alobha-pakataɱ kammā||
alobha-jaɱ||
alobha-nidānaɱ||
alobha-samudayaɱ.|| ||

Taɱ kammaɱ kusalaɱ,||
taɱ kammaɱ anavajjaɱ,||
taɱ kammaɱ sukkha-vipākaɱ,||
taɱ kammaɱ kammana-nirodhāya saɱvaṭṭati.|| ||

Na taɱ kammā kamma-samudayāya saɱvaṭṭati.|| ||

Yaɱ bhikkhave adosa-pakataɱ kammaɱ||
adosa-jaɱ||
adosa-nidānaɱ||
adosa-samudayaɱ.|| ||

Taɱ kammaɱ kusalaɱ,||
taɱ kammaɱ anavajjaɱ,||
taɱ kammaɱ sukkha-vipākaɱ,||
taɱ kammaɱ kammana-nirodhāya saɱvaṭṭati.|| ||

Na taɱ kammā kamma-samudayāya saɱvaṭṭati.|| ||

Yaɱ bhikkhave amoha-pakataɱ kammaɱ||
amoha-jaɱ||
amoha-nidānaɱ||
amoha-samudayaɱ.|| ||

Taɱ kammaɱ kusalaɱ,||
taɱ kammaɱ anavajjaɱ,||
taɱ kammaɱ sukkha-vipākaɱ,||
taɱ kammaɱ kammana-nirodhāya saɱvaṭṭati.|| ||

Na taɱ kammā kamma-samudayāya saɱvaṭṭati.|| ||

Imāni ko bhikkhave tīni nidānāni kammānaɱ samudayāya" ti.|| ||
