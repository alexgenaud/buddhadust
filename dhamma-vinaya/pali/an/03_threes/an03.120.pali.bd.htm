Aŋguttara-Nikāya
III. Tika Nipāta
XII. Āpāyika Vagga

Sutta 120

Moneyya Suttaɱ

Adapted from the 1995 edition of the digital version of the Sri Lanka Buddha Jayanti Tripitaka Series.

 

[273]

[1][pts][than] Evaɱ me sutaɱ.|| ||

Ekaɱ samayaɱ Bhagavā Sāvatthiyaɱ viharati.|| ||

Tatra kho Bhagavā bhikkhū āmantesi:|| ||

"Bhikkhavo" ti.|| ||

"Bhadante" ti te bhikkhū Bhagavato paccassosuɱ.|| ||

Bhagavā etad avoca:|| ||

"Tīṇ'imāni bhikkhave moneyyāni.|| ||

Katamāni tīṇi?|| ||

Kāya-moneyyaɱ,||
vacī-moneyyaɱ,||
mano-moneyyaɱ.|| ||

 

§

 

Katamañ ca bhikkhave kāya-moneyyaɱ?|| ||

Idha, bhikkhave, bhikkhū pāṇāti-pātā paṭivirato hoti.|| ||

Adinnādānā paṭivirato hoti.|| ||

Abrahma-cariyā paṭivirato hoti.|| ||

Idaɱ vuccati bhikkhave kāya-moneyyaɱ.|| ||

■

Katamañ ca bhikkhave vacī-moneyyaɱ?|| ||

Idha, bhikkhave, bhikkhu musā-vādā paṭivirato hoti.|| ||

Pisuṇā-vācā paṭivirato hoti.|| ||

Pharusā-vācā paṭivirato hoti.|| ||

Samphappalāpā paṭivirato hoti.|| ||

Idaɱ vuccati bhikkhave vacī-moneyyaɱ.|| ||

■

Katamañ ca bhikkhave manomoneyyaɱ?|| ||

Idha, bhikkhave, bhikkhu āsavānaɱ khayā anāsavaɱ ceto-vimuttiɱ paññā-vimuttiɱ duṭṭheva dhamme sayaɱ abhiññā sacchi-katvā upasampajja viharati.|| ||

Idaɱ vuccati bhikkhave mano-moneyyaɱ.|| ||

Imāni ko bhikkhave tīṇi moneyyānī" ti.|| ||

 

 

Kāya-munaɱ vacī-muniɱ ceto-muni-manāsavaɱ,||
Muniɱ moneyya-sampannaɱ āhu sabbappahāyinan.|| ||

Āpāyika Vagga Dutiya
