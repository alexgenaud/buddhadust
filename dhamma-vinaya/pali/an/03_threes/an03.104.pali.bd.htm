Aŋguttara-Nikāya
III. Tika Nipāta
XI. Sambodhi Vagga

Sutta 104

Atitti Suttaɱ

Adapted from the 1995 edition of the digital version of the Sri Lanka Buddha Jayanti Tripitaka Series.

 

[261]

[1][pts][olds] Evaɱ me sutaɱ.|| ||

Ekaɱ samayaɱ Bhagavā Sāvatthiyaɱ viharati.|| ||

Tatra kho Bhagavā bhikkhū āmantesi:|| ||

"Bhikkhavo" ti.|| ||

"Bhadante" ti te bhikkhū Bhagavato paccassosuɱ.|| ||

Bhagavā etad avoca:|| ||

"Tinṇaɱ bhikkhave paṭisevanāya n'atthi titti.|| ||

Katamesaɱ tiṇṇaɱ?|| ||

Soppassa bhikkhave paṭisevanāya n'atthi titti.|| ||

Surā-merayapānassa bhikkhave paṭisevanāya n'atthi titti.|| ||

Methuna-dhamma-samāpattiyā bhikkhave paṭisevanāya n'atthi titti.|| ||

Imesaɱ kho bhikkhave tinṇaɱ paṭisevanāya n'atthi tintī" ti.|| ||
