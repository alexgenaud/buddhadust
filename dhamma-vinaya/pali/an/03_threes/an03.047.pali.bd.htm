Aŋguttara-Nikāya
III. Tika Nipāta
V. Cūḷa Vagga

Sutta 47

Asaŋkhata-Lakkhaṇa Suttaɱ

Adapted from the 1995 edition of the digital version of the Sri Lanka Buddha Jayanti Tripitaka Series.

 

[152]

[1][pts][than][bodh][olds] Evaɱ me sutaɱ.|| ||

Evaɱ me sutaɱ ekaɱ samayaɱ Bhagavā Sāvatthiyaɱ viharati.|| ||

Tatra kho Bhagavā bhikkhū āmantesi:|| ||

"Bhikkhavo" ti.|| ||

"Bhadante" ti te bhikkhū Bhagavato paccassosuɱ.|| ||

Bhagavā etad avoca:|| ||

"Tīṇ'imāni bhikkhave saŋkhatassa saŋkhata-lakkhaṇāni.|| ||

Katamāni tīṇi?|| ||

Uppādo paññāyati,||
vayo paññāyati,||
ṭhitassa aññathattaɱ paññāyati.|| ||

Imāni kho bhikkhave tīṇi saŋkhatassa saŋkhata-lakkhaṇānī.|| ||

■

"Tīṇ'imāni bhikkhave asaŋkhatassa asaŋkhata-lakkhaṇāni.|| ||

Katamāni tīṇi?|| ||

Na uppādo paññāyati,||
na vayo paññāyati,||
na ṭhitassa añññathattaɱ paññāyati.|| ||

Imāni kho bhikkhave tīṇi asaŋkhatassa asaŋkhata-lakkhaṇānī" ti.|| ||
