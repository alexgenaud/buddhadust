Aṅguttara-Nikāya
III. Tika Nipāta
V. Cūḷa Vagga

Sutta 43

Attha-Vasa Suttaɱ

Adapted from the 1995 edition of the digital version of the Sri Lanka Buddha Jayanti Tripitaka Series.

 

[151]

[1][pts][bodh][olds] Evaɱ me sutaɱ.|| ||

Evaɱ me sutaɱ ekaɱ samayaɱ Bhagavā Sāvatthiyaɱ viharati.|| ||

Tatra kho Bhagavā bhikkhū āmantesi:|| ||

"Bhikkhavo" ti.|| ||

"Bhadante" ti te bhikkhū Bhagavato paccassosuɱ.|| ||

Bhagavā etad avoca:|| ||

"Tayo'me bhikkhave attha-vase sampassamānena alam eva paresaɱ dhammaɱ desetuɱ.|| ||

Katame tayo?|| ||

Yo Dhammaɱ deseti,||
so attha-paṭisaɱvedī ca hoti Dhamma-paṭisaɱvedī ca.|| ||

Yo Dhammaɱ suṇāti,||
so attha-paṭisaɱvedī ca hoti Dhamma-paṭisaɱvedī ca.|| ||

Yo c'eva Dhammaɱ deseti,||
yo ca Dhammaɱ suṇāti,||
ubho attha-paṭisaɱvedino ca honti Dhamma-paṭisaɱvedino ca.|| ||

Ime kho bhikkhave tayo attha-vase sampassamānena alam eva paresaɱ dhammaɱ desetun" ti.|| ||
