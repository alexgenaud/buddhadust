Aŋguttara-Nikāya
Pañcaka-Nipāta
26. Upasampadā Vagga

Suttas 318-322

Adapted from the 1995 edition of the digital version of the Sri Lanka Buddha Jayanti Tripitaka Series.

 

[275]

Sutta 318

Appamatta-kavissajjako Suttaɱ

[318.1][pts] Evaɱ me sutaɱ.|| ||

Ekaɱ samayaɱ Bhagavā Sāvatthiyaɱ viharati.|| ||

Tatra kho Bhagavā bhikkhu āmantesi:|| ||

"Bhikkhavo" ti.|| ||

"Bhadante" ti te bhikkhū Bhagavato paccassosuɱ.|| ||

Bhagavā etad avoca:|| ||

"Pañcahi bhikkhave, dhammehi samannāgato appa-mattakavissajjako na sammannitabbo.|| ||

Katamehi pañcahi?|| ||

Chandāgatiɱ gacchati,||
dos-ā-gatiɱ gacchati,||
moh-ā-gatiɱ gacchati,||
bhayāgatiɱ gacchati,||
vissajjitāvissajjitaɱ na jānāti.|| ||

Imehi kho bhikkhave, pañcahi dhammehi samannāgato appa-mattakavissajjako na sammannitabbo.|| ||

■

Pañcahi bhikkhave, dhammehi samannāgato appa-mattakavissajjako sammannitabbo.|| ||

Katamehi pañcahi?|| ||

Na chand-ā-gatiɱ gacchati,||
na dos-ā-gatiɱ gacchati,||
na moh-ā-gatiɱ gacchati,||
na bhayāgatiɱ gacchati,||
vissajjitāvissajjitaɱ jānāti.|| ||

Imehi kho bhikkhave, pañcahi dhammehi samannāgato appa-mattakavissajjako sammannitabbo" ti.|| ||
