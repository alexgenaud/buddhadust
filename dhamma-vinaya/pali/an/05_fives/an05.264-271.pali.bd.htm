Aṅguttara-Nikāya
Pañcaka-Nipāta
26. Upasampadā Vagga

Suttas 264-271

Adapted from the 1995 edition of the digital version of the Sri Lanka Buddha Jayanti Tripitaka Series.

 

[273]

Sutta 264

Apara-Paṭhama-j-Jhāna Suttaɱ

[264.1][pts] Evaɱ me sutaɱ.|| ||

Ekaɱ samayaɱ Bhagavā Sāvatthiyaɱ viharati.|| ||

Tatra kho Bhagavā bhikkhu āmantesi:|| ||

"Bhikkhavo" ti.|| ||

"Bhadante" ti te bhikkhū Bhagavato paccassosuɱ.|| ||

Bhagavā etad avoca:|| ||

"Pañc'ime bhikkhave dhamme appāhāya abhabbo paṭhamaɱ-jhānaɱ upasampajja viharituɱ.|| ||

Katame pañca?|| ||

Āvāsa-macchariyaɱ,||
kula-macchariyaɱ,||
lābha-macchariyaɱ,||
vaṇṇa-macchariyaɱ,||
akata-ñ-ñutam akata-veditam.|| ||

Ime kho bhikkhave pañca dhamme a-p-pahāya abhabbo paṭhamaɱ-jhānaɱ upasampajja viharituɱ.|| ||

■

Pañc'ime bhikkhave, dhamme pahāya bhabbo paṭhamaɱ-jhānaɱ upasampajja viharituɱ.|| ||

Katame pañca?|| ||

Āvāsa-macchariyaɱ,||
kula-macchariyaɱ,||
lābha-macchariyaɱ,||
vaṇṇa-macchariyaɱ,||
akata-ñ-ñutam akata-veditam.|| ||

Ime kho bhikkhave, pañca dhamme pahāya bhabbo paṭhamaɱ-jhānaɱ upasampajja viharitun" ti.|| ||
