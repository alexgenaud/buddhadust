Aŋguttara Nikāya
Pañcaka Nipāta
14. Rāja Vaggo

Sutta 132

Dutiya Cakkānuvattana Suttaɱ

Adapted from the 1995 edition of the digital version of the Sri Lanka Buddha Jayanti Tripitaka Series.

 

[148]

[1][pts] Evaɱ me sutaɱ:|| ||

Ekaɱ samayaɱ Bhagavā Sāvatthiyaɱ viharati.|| ||

Tatra kho Bhagavā bhikkhu āmantesi:|| ||

"Bhikkhavo" ti.|| ||

"Bhadante" ti te bhikkhū Bhagavato paccassosuɱ.|| ||

Bhagavā etad avoca:|| ||

2. "Pañcahi bhikkhave aŋgehi samannāgato rañño cakka-vattissa jeṭṭho putto pitarā pavattitaɱ cakkaɱ dhammen'eva anuppavatteti,||
taɱ hoti cakkaɱ appati-vattiyaɱ kenaci manussa-bhutena pacc'atthi-kena pāṇinā.|| ||

Katamehi pañcahi?|| ||

3. Idha, bhikkhave, rañño cakka-vattissa jeṭṭho putto||
atth'aññū ca hoti,||
Dhamm'aññū ca,||
matt'aññū ca,||
kāl'aññū ca,||
paris'aññū ca.|| ||

Imehi kho bhikkhave pañcahi aŋgehi samannāgato rañño cakka-vattissa jeṭṭho putto pitarā pavattitaɱ cakkaɱ Dhammen'eva anuppavatteti,||
taɱ hoti cakkaɱ appati-vattiyaɱ1 kenaci manussa-bhutena pacc'atthi-kena pāṇinā.|| ||

 

§

 

[149] 4. Evam eva kho bhikkhave pañcahi dhammehi samannāgato Sāriputto Tathāgatena pavattitaɱ anuttaraɱ Dhamma-cakkaɱ samma-d-eva anuppavatteti,||
taɱ hoti cakkaɱ appati-vattiyaɱ samaṇena vā brāhmaṇena vā devena vā mārena vā brahmunā vā kenaci vā lokasmiɱ.|| ||

Katamehi pañcahi?|| ||

Idha, bhikkhave, Sāriputto||
atth'aññū,||
Dhamm'aññū,||
matt'aññū,||
kāl'aññū,||
paris'aññū.|| ||

Imehi kho bhikkhave pañcahi dhammehi samannāgato Sāriputto Tathāgatena anuttaraɱ Dhamma-cakkaɱ pavattitaɱ samma-d-eva anuppavatteti,||
taɱ hoti cakkaɱ appati-vattiyaɱ samaṇena vā brāhmaṇena vā devena vā mārena vā brahmunā vā kenaci vā lokasmin" ti.|| ||
