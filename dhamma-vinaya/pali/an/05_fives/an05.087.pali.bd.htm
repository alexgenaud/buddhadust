Aŋguttara Nikāya
Pañcaka Nipāta
9. Thera Vagga

Sutta 87

Sīla Suttaɱ

Adapted from the 1995 edition of the digital version of the Sri Lanka Buddha Jayanti Tripitaka Series.

 

 

[1][pts] Evaɱ me sutaɱ:|| ||

Ekaɱ samayaɱ Bhagavā Sāvatthiyaɱ viharati.|| ||

Tatra kho Bhagavā bhikkhu āmantesi:|| ||

"Bhikkhavo" ti.|| ||

"Bhadante" ti te bhikkhū Bhagavato paccassosuɱ.|| ||

Bhagavā etad avoca:|| ||

Pañcahi bhikkhave dhammehi||
samannāgato thero bhikkhu sabrahma-cārinaɱ||
piyo ca hoti||
manāpo ca||
garu ca||
bhāvanīyo ca.|| ||

Katamehi pañcahi?|| ||

Sīlavā hoti, Pātimokkha-saŋvara-saɱvuto viharati ācāra-gocara sampanno aṇumattesu vajjesu bhaya-dassāvī,||
samādāya sikkhati sikkhā-padesu.|| ||

■

Bahu-s-suto hoti suta-dharo suta-sanni-cayo,||
ye te dhammā ādi-kalyāṇā||
majjhe kalyāṇā||
[114] pariyosāna-kalyāṇā sātthā savyañ janā kevala-paripuṇṇaɱ parisuddhaɱ brahma-cārīyaɱ abhivadanti,||
tathā-rūpāssa dhammā bahu-s-sutā honti dhatā vacasā paricitā manas-ā-nupekkhitā diṭṭhiyā suppaṭi-viddhā.|| ||

■

Kalyāṇa-vāco hoti kalyāṇa-vākkaraṇo poriyā vācāya samannāgato vissaṭṭhāya anelagalāya atthassa viññāpaniyā.|| ||

■

Catuntaɱ jhanānaɱ ābhiceta-sikānaɱ diṭṭha-dhamme-sukha-vihārānaɱ nikāma-lābhī hoti,||
akiccha-lābhī akasira-lābhī.|| ||

■

Āsavānaɱ khayā anāsavaɱ ceto-vimuttiɱ paññā-vimuttīɱ diṭṭhe'va dhamme sayaɱ abhiññā sacchi-katvā upasampajja viharati.|| ||

Imehi kho bhikkhave pañcahi dhammehi||
samannāgato thero bhikkhu sabrahma-cārīnaɱ||
piyo ca hoti||
manāpo ca||
garu ca||
bhāvaniyo cā' ti.|| ||
