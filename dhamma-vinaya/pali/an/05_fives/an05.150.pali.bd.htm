Aŋguttara Nikāya
Pañcaka Nipāta
15. Tikaṇḍaki Vaggo

Sutta 150

Dutiya Samaya-Vimutta Suttaɱ

Adapted from the 1995 edition of the digital version of the Sri Lanka Buddha Jayanti Tripitaka Series.

 

 

[1][pts][olds] Evaɱ me sutaɱ:|| ||

Ekaɱ samayaɱ Bhagavā Sāvatthiyaɱ viharati.|| ||

Tatra kho Bhagavā bhikkhu āmantesi:|| ||

"Bhikkhavo" ti.|| ||

"Bhadante" ti te bhikkhū Bhagavato paccassosuɱ.|| ||

Bhagavā etad avoca:|| ||

2. "Pañc'ime bhikkhave dhammā samaya-vimuttassa bhikkhuno parihānāya saŋvattanti.|| ||

Katame pañca?|| ||

Kamm'ārāmatā,||
bhass-ā-rāmatā,||
nidd-ā-rāmatā,||
indriyesu agutta-dvāratā,||
bhojane amatt'aññutā|| ||

Ime kho bhikkhave pañca dhammā samaya-vimuttassa bhikkhuno parihānāya saŋvattanti.|| ||

 

§

 

Pañc'ime bhikkhave dhammā samaya-vimuttassa bhikkhuno aparihānāya saŋvattanti.|| ||

Katame pañca?|| ||

Na kamm-ā-rāmatā,||
na bhass-ā-rāmatā,||
na nidd-ā-rāmatā,||
indriyesu gutta-dvāratā,||
bhojane matt'aññutā.|| ||

[174] Ime kho bhikkhave pañca dhammā samaya-vimuttassa bhikkhuno aparihānāya saŋvattantī" ti.|| ||
