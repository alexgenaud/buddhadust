Aŋguttara-Nikāya
Pañcaka-Nipāta
24. Āvāsika Vagga

Sutta 235

Anukampaka Suttaɱ

Adapted from the 1995 edition of the digital version of the Sri Lanka Buddha Jayanti Tripitaka Series.

 

[263]

[1][pts] Evaɱ me sutaɱ.|| ||

Ekaɱ samayaɱ Bhagavā Sāvatthiyaɱ viharati.|| ||

Tatra kho Bhagavā bhikkhu āmantesi:|| ||

"Bhikkhavo" ti.|| ||

"Bhadante" ti te bhikkhū Bhagavato paccassosuɱ.

Bhagavā etad avoca:|| ||

"Pañcahi bhikakhave, dhammehi samannāgato āvāsiko bhikkhu gihīnaɱ anukampati.|| ||

Katame pañcahi?|| ||

2. Adhisīle sam-ā-dapeti.|| ||

■

Dhamma-dassane niveseti.|| ||

■

Gilānake upasankamitvā satiɱ uppādeti:|| ||

'Arahaggataɱ [264] āyasmanto satiɱ upa-ṭ-ṭh-ā-pethā' ti.|| ||

■

Mahā kho pana bhikkhu-saŋgho abhikkanto nānā-verajjakā bhikkhu,||
gihīnaɱ upasankamitvā āroceti:|| ||

"Mahā kho āvuso, bhikkhu-saŋgho abhikkanto,||
nānā-verajjakā bhikkhu,||
tarotha puññāni,||
samayo puññāni kātun" ti.|| ||

■

Yaɱ kho pan'assa bhojanaɱ denti lūkhaɱ vā||
paṇitaɱ vā||
taɱ attanā paribhuñjati,||
saddhādeyyaɱ na vinipāteti.|| ||

Imehi kho bhikkhave, pañcahi dhammehi samannāgato āvāsiko bhikkhu gihīnaɱ anukampatī" ti.|| ||
