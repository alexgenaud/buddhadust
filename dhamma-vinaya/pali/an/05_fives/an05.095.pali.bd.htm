Aṇguttara Nikāya
Pañcaka-Nipāta
Kakudha vaggo

Sutta 95

Akuppa Suttaṃ

Adapted from the 1995 edition of the digital version of the Sri Lanka Buddha Jayanti Tripitaka Series.

 

[119]

[1][pts] EVAṂ ME SUTAṂ.|| ||

Ekaṃ samayaṃ Bhagavā Sāvatthiyaṃ viharati.|| ||

Pañcahi bhikkhave dhammehi samannāgato bhikkhu na cirass'eva akuppaṃ paṭivijjhati.|| ||

Katamehi pañcahi?|| ||

2. Idha, bhikkhave, bhikkhu attha-paṭisambidāpatto hoti,||
dhamma-paṭisambidāpatto hoti,||
nirutti-paṭisambidāpatto hoti,||
paṭibhāna-paṭisambidāpatto hoti,||
yathā vimuttaṃ cittaṃ pacc'avekkhati.|| ||

Imehi kho bhikkhave pañcahi dhammehi samannāgato bhikkhu na cirass'eva akuppaṃ paṭivijjhatī ti.|| ||
