Aŋguttara Nikāya
Pañcaka-Nipāta
Kakudha vaggo

Sutta 95

Akuppa Suttaɱ

Adapted from the 1995 edition of the digital version of the Sri Lanka Buddha Jayanti Tripitaka Series.

 

[119]

[1][pts] EVAṂ ME SUTAṂ.|| ||

Ekaɱ samayaɱ Bhagavā Sāvatthiyaɱ viharati.|| ||

Pañcahi bhikkhave dhammehi samannāgato bhikkhu na cirass'eva akuppaɱ paṭivijjhati.|| ||

Katamehi pañcahi?|| ||

2. Idha, bhikkhave, bhikkhu attha-paṭisambidāpatto hoti,||
dhamma-paṭisambidāpatto hoti,||
nirutti-paṭisambidāpatto hoti,||
paṭibhāna-paṭisambidāpatto hoti,||
yathā vimuttaɱ cittaɱ pacc-a-vekkhati.|| ||

Imehi kho bhikkhave pañcahi dhammehi samannāgato bhikkhu na cirass'eva akuppaɱ paṭivijjhatī ti.|| ||
