Aŋguttara Nikāya
Pañcaka Nipāta
VII: Saññā Vagga

Sutta 69

Nibbidā Suttaɱ

Adapted from the 1995 edition of the digital version of the Sri Lanka Buddha Jayanti Tripitaka Series.

 

[83]

[1][pts][olds] Evaɱ me sutaɱ:|| ||

Ekaɱ samayaɱ Bhagavā Sāvatthiyaɱ viharati.|| ||

Tatra kho Bhagavā bhikkhu āmantesi:|| ||

"Bhikkhavo" ti.|| ||

"Bhadante" ti te bhikkhū Bhagavato paccassosuɱ.|| ||

Bhagavā etad avoca:|| ||

"Pañc'ime bhikkhave dhammā bhāvitā bahulī-katā ekanta-nibbidāya virāgāya nirodhāya upasamāya abhiññāya sambodhāya nibbānāya saŋvattanti.|| ||

Katame pañca?|| ||

Idha, bhikkhave, bhikkhu asubhānupassī kāye viharati,||
āhāre paṭi-k-kūla-saññī,||
sabba-loke anabhirata-saññī,||
sabba-sankhāresu anicc'ānupassī.|| ||

Maraṇa-saññā kho panassa ajjhattaɱ sūpaṭṭhitā hoti.|| ||

Ime kho bhikkhave pañca dhammā bhāvitā bahulī-katā ekanta-nibbidāya virāgāya nirodhāya upasamāya abhiññāya sambodhāya nibbānāya saŋvattantī" ti.|| ||
