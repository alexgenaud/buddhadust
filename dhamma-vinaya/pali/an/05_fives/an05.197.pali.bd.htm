Aŋguttara Nikāya
Pañcaka-Nipāta
XX. Brāhmaṇa Vagga

Sutta 197

Vassantarāya Suttaɱ

Adapted from the 1995 edition of the digital version of the Sri Lanka Buddha Jayanti Tripitaka Series.

 

[243]

[1][pts] Evaɱ me sutaɱ:|| ||

Ekaɱ samayaɱ Bhagavā Sāvatthiyaɱ viharati.|| ||

Tatra kho Bhagavā bhikkhu āmantesi:|| ||

"Bhikkhavo" ti.|| ||

"Bhadante" ti te bhikkhū Bhagavato paccassosuɱ.|| ||

Bhagavā etad avoca:|| ||

"Pañc'ime bhikkhave, vassassa antarāyā,||
yaɱ nemittā na jānanti,||
yattha nemittānaɱ cakkhuɱ na kamati.|| ||

Katame pañca?|| ||

2. Upari bhikkhave, ākāse tejo-dhātu pakuppati,||
tena uppannā meghā paṭivigacchanti.|| ||

Ayaɱ bhikkhave, paṭhamo vassassa antarāyo||
yaɱ nemittā na jānanti,||
yattha nemittānaɱ cakkhuɱ na kamati.|| ||

■

3. Puna ca paraɱ bhikkhave, upari ākāse vāyo-dhātu pakuppati,||
tena uppannā meghā paṭivigacchanti.|| ||

Ayaɱ bhikkhave, dutiyo vassassa antarāyo||
yaɱ nemittā na jānanti,||
yattha nemittānaɱ cakkhuɱ na kamati.|| ||

■

4. Puna ca paraɱ bhikkhave, Rāhu Asurindo pāṇinā udakaɱ paṭicchitvā mahā-samudde chaḍḍeti.|| ||

Ayaɱ bhikkhave, tatiyo vassassa antarāyo||
yaɱ nemittā na jānanti,||
yattha nemittānaɱ cakkhuɱ na kamati.|| ||

■

5. Puna ca paraɱ bhikkhave, vassavalāhakā devā pamattā honti.|| ||

Ayaɱ bhikkhave, catuttho vassassa antarāyo||
yaɱ nemittā na jānanti,||
yattha nemittānaɱ cakkhuɱ na kamati.|| ||

■

6. Puna ca paraɱ bhikkhave, manussā adhammikā honti.|| ||

Ayaɱ bhikkhave, pañcamo vassassa antarāyo||
yaɱ nemittā na jānanti,||
yattha nemittānaɱ cakkhuɱ na kamati.|| ||

Ime kho bhikkhave, pañca vassassa antarāyā,||
yaɱ nemittā na jānanti,||
yattha nemittānaɱ cakkhuɱ na kamatī" ti.|| ||
