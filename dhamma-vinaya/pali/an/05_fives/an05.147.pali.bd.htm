Aŋguttara Nikāya
Pañcaka Nipāta
15. Tikaṇḍaki Vaggo

Sutta 147

Asappurisa-Dāna Suttaɱ

Adapted from the 1995 edition of the digital version of the Sri Lanka Buddha Jayanti Tripitaka Series.

 

[171]

[1][pts][olds] Evaɱ me sutaɱ:|| ||

Ekaɱ samayaɱ Bhagavā Sāvatthiyaɱ viharati.|| ||

Tatra kho Bhagavā bhikkhu āmantesi:|| ||

"Bhikkhavo" ti.|| ||

"Bhadante" ti te bhikkhū Bhagavato paccassosuɱ.|| ||

Bhagavā etad avoca:|| ||

2. Pañc'imāni bhikkhave asappurisa-dānāni.|| ||

Katamāni pañca?|| ||

Asakkaccaɱ deti,||
acittīkatvā deti,||
asahatthā deti,||
apaviddhaɱ deti,||
anāgamana-diṭṭhiko deti.|| ||

Imāni kho bhikkhave pañca asappurisa-dānāni.|| ||
