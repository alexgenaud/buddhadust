Aŋguttara Nikāya
Pañcaka Nipāta
II: Bala Vagga

Sutta 18

Parahita Suttaɱ

Adapted from the 1995 edition of the digital version of the Sri Lanka Buddha Jayanti Tripitaka Series.

 

[13]

[1][pts][bodh] Evaɱ me sutaɱ:|| ||

Ekaɱ samayaɱ Bhagavā Sāvatthiyaɱ viharati.|| ||

Tatra kho Bhagavā bhikkhu āmantesi:|| ||

"Bhikkhavo" ti.|| ||

"Bhadante" ti te bhikkhū Bhagavato paccassosuɱ.|| ||

Bhagavā etad avoca:|| ||

Pañcahi bhikkhave dhammehi samannāgato bhikkhu parahitāya paṭipanto hoti,||
no attahitāya.|| ||

Katamehi pañcahi?|| ||

Idha, bhikkhave, bhikkhu attanā na sīla-sampanno hoti,||
paraɱ sīla-sampadāya sam-ā-dapeti.|| ||

Attanā na samādhi-sampanno hoti,||
paraɱ samādhi-sampadāya sam-ā-dapeti.|| ||

Attanā na paññā-sampanno hoti,||
paraɱ paññā-sampadāya sam-ā-dapeti.|| ||

Attanā na vimutti-sampanno hoti,||
paraɱ vimutti-sampadāya sam-ā-dapeti.|| ||

Attanā na vimutti-ñāṇa-dassana-sampannano hoti,||
paraɱ-vimutti-ñāṇa-dassana-sampadāya sam-ā-dapeti.|| ||

Imehi kho bhikkhave pañcahi dhammehi samannāgato bhikkhu parahitāya paṭipanno hoti,||
no attahitāyā" ti.|| ||
