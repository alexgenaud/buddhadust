Aṇguttara-Nikāya
Pañcaka-Nipāta
26. Upasampadā Vagga

Suttas 303-307

Adapted from the 1995 edition of the digital version of the Sri Lanka Buddha Jayanti Tripitaka Series.

 

[275]

Sutta 303

Yāgu-Bājako Suttaṃ

[303.1][pts] Evaṃ me sutaṃ.|| ||

Ekaṃ samayaṃ Bhagavā Sāvatthiyaṃ viharati.|| ||

Tatra kho Bhagavā bhikkhu āmantesi:|| ||

"Bhikkhavo" ti.|| ||

"Bhadante" ti te bhikkhū Bhagavato paccassosuṃ.|| ||

Bhagavā etad avoca:|| ||

"Pañcahi bhikkhave, dhammehi samannāgato yāgu-bājako na sammannitabbo.|| ||

Katamehi pañcahi?|| ||

Chandāgatiṃ gacchati,||
dos-ā-gatiṃ gacchati,||
moh-ā-gatiṃ gacchati,||
bhayāgatiṃ gacchati,||
bhājakābhājakṃ na jānāti.|| ||

Imehi kho bhikkhave, pañcahi dhammehi samannāgato yāgu-bājako na sammannitabbo.|| ||

■

Pañcahi bhikkhave, dhammehi samannāgato yāgu-bājako sammannitabbo.|| ||

Katamehi pañcahi?|| ||

Na chand-ā-gatiṃ gacchati,||
na dos-ā-gatiṃ gacchati,||
na moh-ā-gatiṃ gacchati,||
na bhayāgatiṃ gacchati,||
bhājakābhājakṃ jānāti.|| ||

Imehi kho bhikkhave, pañcahi dhammehi samannāgato yāgu-bājako sammannitabbo" ti.|| ||

Imehi kho bhikkhave, pañcahi dhammehi samannāgato yāgu-bājako khataṃ upahataṃ attāṇaṃ pariharati.|| ||

■

Pañcahi bhikkhave, dhammehi samannāgato yāgu-bājako akkhata anupahataṃ attāṇaṃ pariharati.|| ||

Katamehi pañcahi?|| ||

Chandāgatiṃ gacchati,||
dos-ā-gatiṃ gacchati,||
moh-ā-gatiṃ gacchati,||

Chandāgatiṃ gacchati,||
