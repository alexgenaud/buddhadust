Aŋguttara-Nikāya
Pañcaka-Nipāta
24. Āvāsika Vagga

Sutta 238

Tatiya Avaṇṇāraha Suttaɱ

Adapted from the 1995 edition of the digital version of the Sri Lanka Buddha Jayanti Tripitaka Series.

 

[265]

[1][pts] Evaɱ me sutaɱ.|| ||

Ekaɱ samayaɱ Bhagavā Sāvatthiyaɱ viharati.|| ||

Tatra kho Bhagavā bhikkhu āmantesi:|| ||

"Bhikkhavo" ti.|| ||

"Bhadante" ti te bhikkhū Bhagavato paccassosuɱ.

Bhagavā etad avoca:|| ||

"Pañcahi bhikkhave, dhammehi samannāgato āvāsiko bhikkhu||
yath'ābhataɱ nikkhitto evaɱ Niraye.|| ||

Katamehi pañcahi?|| ||

Ananuvicca apariyogāhetvā avaṇṇārahassa vaṇṇaɱ bhāsati;|| ||

■

ananuvicca apariyogāhetvā vaṇṇārahassa avaṇṇaɱ bhāsati;|| ||

■

āvāsa-maccharī hoti;|| ||

■

kula-maccharī hoti;|| ||

■

lābha-macchari hoti.|| ||

Imehi kho bhikkhave, pañcahi dhammehi samannāgato āvāsiko bhikkhu||
yath'ābhataɱ nikkhitto evaɱ Niraye.|| ||

 

§

 

Pañcahi bhikkhave dhammehi samannāgato āvāsiko bhikkhu||
yath'ābhataɱ nikkhitto evaɱ sagge.|| ||

Katamehi pañcahi?|| ||

Anuvicca pariyogāhetvā avaṇaṇārahassa avaṇaṇaɱ bhāsati;|| ||

■

anuvicca pariyogāhetvā vaṇaṇārahassa vaṇaṇaɱ bhā- [266] sati;|| ||

■

na āvāsa-macchari hoti;|| ||

■

na kula-macchari hoti;|| ||

■

na lābha-macchari hoti.|| ||

Imehi kho bhikkhave pañcahi dhammehi samannāgato āvāsiko bhikkhu||
yath'ābhataɱ nikkhitto evaɱ sagge" ti.|| ||
