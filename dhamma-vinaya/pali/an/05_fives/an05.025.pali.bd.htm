Aŋguttara Nikāya
Pañcaka Nipāta
III: Pañc'aŋgika Vagga

Sutta 25

Anuggahīta Suttaɱ

Adapted from the 1995 edition of the digital version of the Sri Lanka Buddha Jayanti Tripitaka Series.

 

[20]

[1][pts][than][bodh] Evaɱ me sutaɱ:|| ||

Ekaɱ samayaɱ Bhagavā Sāvatthiyaɱ viharati.|| ||

Tatra kho Bhagavā bhikkhu āmantesi:|| ||

"Bhikkhavo" ti.|| ||

"Bhadante" ti te bhikkhū Bhagavato paccassosuɱ.|| ||

Bhagavā etad avoca:|| ||

"Pañcahi bhikkhave aŋgehi anuggahitā sammā-diṭṭhi||
ceto-vimutti-phalā ca hoti,||
ceto-vimutti-phalā-nisaɱsā ca,||
paññā-vimutti-phalā ca hoti||
paññā-vimutti-phalā-nisaɱsā ca.|| ||

Katamehi pañcahi?|| ||

[21] Idha, bhikkhave, sammā-diṭṭhi sīlā-nuggahitā ca hoti.|| ||

Sutā-nuggahitā ca hoti.|| ||

Sākacchā-nuggahitā ca hoti.|| ||

Samathā-nuggahitā ca hoti.|| ||

Vipassanā-nuggahitā ca hoti.|| ||

Imehi kho bhikkhave pañcahi aŋgehi anuggahitā sammā-diṭṭhi||
ceto-vimutti-phalā ca hoti,||
ceto-vimutti-phalā-nisaɱsā ca,||
paññā-vimutti-phalā ca hoti||
paññā-vimutti-phalā-nisaɱsā ca" ti.|| ||
