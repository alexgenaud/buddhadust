Aŋguttara Nikāya
Pañcaka Nipāta
III: Pañc'aŋgika Vagga

Sutta 29

Cankamā-Nisaɱsa Suttaɱ

Adapted from the 1995 edition of the digital version of the Sri Lanka Buddha Jayanti Tripitaka Series.

 

[29]

[1][pts][olds][agku][bodh] Evaɱ me sutaɱ:|| ||

Ekaɱ samayaɱ Bhagavā Sāvatthiyaɱ viharati.|| ||

Tatra kho Bhagavā bhikkhu āmantesi:|| ||

"Bhikkhavo" ti.|| ||

"Bhadante" ti te bhikkhū Bhagavato paccassosuɱ.|| ||

Bhagavā etad avoca:|| ||

"Pañc'ime bhikkhave caŋkame ānisaɱsā.|| ||

Katame pañca?|| ||

[30] Addhānakkhamo hoti.|| ||

Padhānakkhamo hoti.|| ||

Appābādho hoti.|| ||

Asita-pīta-khāyita-sāyitaɱ-sammā-parināmaɱ gacchati.|| ||

Cankamādhigato samādhi cira-ṭ-ṭhitiko hoti.|| ||

Ime kho bhikkhave pañca caŋkame ānisaɱsā" ti.|| ||
