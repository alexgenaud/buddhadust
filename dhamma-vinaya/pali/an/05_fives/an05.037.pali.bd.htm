Aŋguttara-Nikāya
Pañcaka-Nipāta
IV. Sumanā Vagga

Sutta 37

Bhojana Suttaɱ

Adapted from the 1995 edition of the digital version of the Sri Lanka Buddha Jayanti Tripitaka Series.

 

[42]

[1][pts][than][olds][bodh] Evaɱ me sutaɱ:|| ||

Ekaɱ samayaɱ Bhagavā bhikkhu āmantesi:|| ||

"Bhikkhavo" ti.|| ||

"Bhadante" ti te bhikkhū Bhagavato paccassosuɱ.|| ||

Bhagavā etad avoca:|| ||

"Bhojanaɱ bhikkhave dadamāno dāyako paṭiggāhakānaɱ pañca ṭhānāni deti.

Katamāni pañca?

2. Āyuɱ deti,||
vaṇṇaɱ deti,||
sukhaɱ deti,||
balaɱ deti,||
paṭibhānaɱ deti.|| ||

Āyuɱ kho pana datvā āyussa bhāgī hoti||
dibbassa vā mānusassa vā,||
vaṇaṇaɱ datvā vaṇṇassa bhāgī hoti||
dibbassa vā mānussa vā,||
sukhaɱ datvā sukhassa bhāgī hoti||
dibbassa vā mānusassa vā,||
paṭihānaɱ datvā paṭibhānassa bhāgī hoti||
dibbassa vā mānusassa vā.|| ||

Bhojanaɱ bhikkhave dada-māno dāyako paṭiggāhakānaɱ imāni pañca ṭhānāni deti" ti.|| ||

 

Āyudo balado dhīro vaṇṇado paṭibhāṇado||
sukhassa dātā medhāvī sukhaɱ so adhigacchati.|| ||

Āyuɱ datvā balaɱ vaṇṇaɱ sukhañ ca paṭibhāṇao||
dīghāyu yasavā hoti yattha yatth'upapajjatī ti.|| ||
