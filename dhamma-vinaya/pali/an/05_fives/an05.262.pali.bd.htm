Aŋguttara-Nikāya
Pañcaka-Nipāta
26. Upasampadā Vagga

Sutta 262

Anāgāmi-Phalaɱ Suttaɱ

Adapted from the 1995 edition of the digital version of the Sri Lanka Buddha Jayanti Tripitaka Series.

 

[272]

[1][pts][than] Evaɱ me sutaɱ.|| ||

Ekaɱ samayaɱ Bhagavā Sāvatthiyaɱ viharati.|| ||

Tatra kho Bhagavā bhikkhu āmantesi:|| ||

"Bhikkhavo" ti.|| ||

"Bhadante" ti te bhikkhū Bhagavato paccassosuɱ.|| ||

Bhagavā etad avoca:|| ||

"Pañc'ime bhikkhave dhamme appāhāya abhabbo Anāgāmi-phalaɱ Suttaɱ sacchi-kātuɱ.|| ||

Katame pañca?|| ||

Āvāsa-macchariyaɱ,||
kula-macchariyaɱ,||
lābha-macchariyaɱ,||
vaṇṇa-macchariyaɱ,||
dhamma-macchariyaɱ.|| ||

Ime kho bhikkhave pañca dhamme a-p-pahāya abhabbo Anāgāmi-phalaɱ sacchi-kātuɱ.|| ||

 

§

 

Pañc'ime bhikkhave, dhamme pahāya bhabbo Anāgāmi-phalaɱ sacchi-kātuɱ.|| ||

Katame pañca?|| ||

Āvāsa-macchariyaɱ,||
kula-macchariyaɱ,||
lābha-macchariyaɱ,||
vaṇṇa-macchariyaɱ,||
dhamma-macchariyaɱ.|| ||

Ime kho bhikkhave, pañca dhamme pahāya bhabbo Anāgāmi-phalaɱ sacchi-kātuɱ" ti.|| ||
