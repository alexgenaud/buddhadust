Aŋguttara-Nikāya
Pañcaka-Nipāta
26. Upasampadā Vagga

Suttas 511-560

2. Kodha Peyyālaɱ

Adapted from the 1995 edition of the digital version of the Sri Lanka Buddha Jayanti Tripitaka Series.

 

 

Sutta 511

Kodhassa Abhiññāya Suttaɱ 1

[511.1][pts] Evaɱ me sutaɱ:|| ||

Ekaɱ samayaɱ Bhagavā Sāvatthiyaɱ viharati.|| ||

Tatra kho Bhagavā bhikkhu āmantesi:|| ||

"Bhikkhavo" ti.|| ||

"Bhadante" ti te bhikkhū Bhagavato paccassosuɱ.|| ||

Bhagavā etad avoca:|| ||

2. Kodhassa bhikkhave abhiññāya pañca dhammā bhāvetabbā.|| ||

Katame pañca?|| ||

Asubha-saññā,||
maraṇa-saññā,||
ādīnavasaññā,||
āhāre paṭikkūla-saññā,||
sabba-loke anabhirata-saññā.|| ||

Kodhassa bhikkhave abhiññāya ime pañca dhammā bhāvetabbā' ti.|| ||
