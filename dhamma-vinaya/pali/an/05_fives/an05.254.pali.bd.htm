Aŋguttara-Nikāya
Pañcaka-Nipāta
26. Upasampadā Vagga

Sutta 254

Macchariya Suttaɱ

Adapted from the 1995 edition of the digital version of the Sri Lanka Buddha Jayanti Tripitaka Series.

 

[272]

[1][pts][than] Evaɱ me sutaɱ.|| ||

Ekaɱ samayaɱ Bhagavā Sāvatthiyaɱ viharati.|| ||

Tatra kho Bhagavā bhikkhu āmantesi:|| ||

"Bhikkhavo" ti.|| ||

"Bhadante" ti te bhikkhū Bhagavato paccassosuɱ.|| ||

Bhagavā etad avoca:|| ||

"Pañc'imāni bhikkhave macchariyāni.|| ||

Katamehi pañcahi?|| ||

Āvāsa-macchariyaɱ,||
kula-macchariyaɱ,||
lābha-macchariyaɱ,||
vaṇṇa-macchariyaɱ,||
dhamma-macchariyaɱ.|| ||

Imāni kho bhikkhave pañcahi macchariyāni.|| ||

Imesaɱ kho bhikkhave pañcannaɱ macchariyānaɱ etaɱ patikiṭṭhaɱ yad idaɱ dhamma-macchariyan" ti.|| ||
