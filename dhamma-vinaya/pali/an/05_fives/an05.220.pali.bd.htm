Aŋguttara-Nikāya
Pañcaka-Nipāta
22. Akkosaka Vagga

Sutta 220

Madhurā Suttaɱ

Adapted from the 1995 edition of the digital version of the Sri Lanka Buddha Jayanti Tripitaka Series.

 

[256]

[1][pts] Evaɱ me sutaɱ.|| ||

Ekaɱ samayaɱ Bhagavā Sāvatthiyaɱ viharati Jetavane Anāthapiṇḍikassa Ārāme.||
Tatra kho Bhagavā bhikkhu āmantesi:|| ||

Bhikkhavo ti.|| ||

Bhadante ti te bhikkhū Bhagavato paccassosuɱ.

Bhagavā etad avoca:|| ||

Pañc'ime bhikkhave ādīnavā Madhurāyaɱ.|| ||

Katame pañca?|| ||

Visamā,||
bahu-rajā,||
caṇḍa-sunakhā,||
vāḷayakkhā,||
dullabha-piṇḍā.|| ||

Ime kho bhikkhave pañca ādīnavā Madhurāyan ti.|| ||
