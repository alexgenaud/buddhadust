Aŋguttara Nikāya
Pañcaka Nipāta
XXI. Kimbila-Vagga

Sutta 209

Gītassara Suttaɱ

Adapted from the 1995 edition of the digital version of the Sri Lanka Buddha Jayanti Tripitaka Series.

 

[251]

[1][pts] Evaɱ me sutaɱ.|| ||

Ekaɱ samayaɱ Bhagavā Sāvatthiyaɱ viharati Jetavane Anāthapiṇḍikassa Ārāme.|| ||

Tatra kho Bhagavā bhikkhu āmantesi:|| ||

Bhikkhavo ti.|| ||

Bhadante ti te bhikkhū Bhagavato paccassosuɱ.

Bhagavā etad avoca:|| ||

Pañc'ime bhikkhave, ādinavā āyata-kena gīta-s-sarena dhammaɱ bhaṇantassa.|| ||

Katame pañca?|| ||

2. Attanā pi tasmiɱ sare sāra-j-jati,||
pare pi tasmiɱ sare sārajjanti,||
gahapatikā pi ujjhāyanti:|| ||

'Yatheva mayaɱ gāyāma,||
evam eva ime samaṇā Sakkiya-puttiyā gāyantī' ti,|| ||

sara-kuttim pi nikāmaya-mānassa samādhissa bhaŋgo hoti,||
pacchimā janatā diṭṭh'ānugatiɱ āpajjati.|| ||

Ime kho bhikkhave pañca ādīnavā āyata-kena gīta-s-sarena dhammaɱ bhaṇantassā ti.|| ||
