Aŋguttara Nikāya
Pañcaka Nipāta
14. Rāja Vaggo

Sutta 133

Dhamma-Rāja Suttaɱ

Adapted from the 1995 edition of the digital version of the Sri Lanka Buddha Jayanti Tripitaka Series.

 

[149]

[1][pts] Evaɱ me sutaɱ:|| ||

Ekaɱ samayaɱ Bhagavā Sāvatthiyaɱ viharati.|| ||

Tatra kho Bhagavā bhikkhu āmantesi:|| ||

"Bhikkhavo" ti.|| ||

"Bhadante" ti te bhikkhū Bhagavato paccassosuɱ.|| ||

Bhagavā etad avoca:|| ||

2. "Yo pi kho bhikkhave rājā cakka-vatti Dhammiko Dhamma-rājā,||
so pi na arājakaɱ cakkaɱ pavattetī ti.|| ||

Evaɱ vutte aññataro bhikkhu Bhagavantaɱ etad avoca:|| ||

"Ko pana bhante rañño cakka-vattissa Dhammikassa Dhamma-rañño rājā" ti?|| ||

■

"Dhammo bhikkhū" ti Bhagavā avoca.|| ||

 

§

 

"Idha bhikkhu rājā cakka-vatti Dhammiko Dhamma-rājā||
Dhammaɱ yeva nissāya||
Dhammaɱ sak'karonto||
Dhammaɱ garu-karonto||
Dhammaɱ apacāya-māno||
Dhammaddhajo||
Dhamma-ketu||
Dhammādhipateyyo||
Dhamimikaɱ rakkhāvaraṇa-guttiɱ saɱvidahati antojanasmiɱ.|| ||

■

Puna ca paraɱ bhikkhu rājā cakka-vatti Dhammiko Dhamma-rājā||
Dhammaɱ yeva nissāya||
Dhammaɱ sakkaronto||
Dhammaɱ garukaronato||
Dhammaɱ apacāya-māno||
Dhammadadhajo||
Dhamma-ketu||
Dhammādhipateyyo||
Dhammikaɱ rakkhāvaraṇa-guttiɱ saɱvidahati khattiyesu anuyuttesu,||
balakāyasmiɱ,||
brāhmaṇa-gahapatikesu,||
nega-majāna-padesu,||
samaṇa-brāhmaṇesu,||
miga-pakkhisu.|| ||

■

[150] Sa kho so bhikkhu rājā cakka-vatti Dhammiko Dhamma-rājā||
Dhammaɱ yeva nissāya||
Dhammaɱ sakkarontā||
Dhammaɱ garu-karonto||
Dhammaɱ apacāya-māno||
Dhammaddhajo||
Dhamma-ketu||
Dhammādhipateyyo||
Dhammikaɱ||
rakkhāvaraṇa-guttiɱ||
saɱvida-hitvā||
antojanasmiɱ,||
Dhammikaɱ rakkhācaraṇa-guttiɱ saɱvida-hitvā khattiyesu anuyuttesu,||
balakāyasmiɱ,||
brahmaṇa-gahapatikesu nega-majāna-padesu,||
samaṇa-brāhmaṇesu,||
miga-pakkisu,||
Dhammen'eva cakkaɱ pavatteti,||
taɱ hoti cakkaɱ appati-vattiyaɱ kenaci manussa-bhutena pacc'atthi-kena pāṇinā.|| ||

 

§

 

Evam eva kho bhikkhu Tathāgato arahaɱ Sammā-Sam-Buddho Dhammiko Dhamma-rājā||
Dhammaɱ yeva nissāya||
Dhammaɱ sakkaronto||
Dhammaɱ garu-karonto||
Dhammaɱ apacāya-māno||
Dhammaddhajo||
Dhamma-ketu||
Dhammādhipateyyo||
Dhammikaɱ rakkhāvaraṇa-guttiɱ saɱvidahati bhikkhusu.|| ||

Eva-rūpaɱ kāya-kammaɱ sevitabbaɱ,||
eva-rūpaɱ kāya-kammaɱ na sevitabbaɱ,||
eva-rūpaɱ vacī-kammaɱ sevitabbaɱ,||
eva-rūpaɱ vacī-kammaɱ na sevitabbaɱ,||
eva-rūpaɱ mano-kammaɱ sevitabbaɱ,||
eva-rūpaɱ mano-kammaɱ na sevitabbaɱ,||
eva-rūpo ājīvo sevitabbo,||
eva-rūpo ājivo na sevitabbo,||
eva-rūpo gāmani-gamo sevitabbo,||
eva-rūpo gāmani-gamo na sevitabbo" ti.|| ||

■

Puna ca paraɱ bhikkhu Tathāgato arahaɱ Sammā-Sam-Buddho dhamimiko Dhamma-rājā||
Dhammaɱ yeva nissāya||
Dhammaɱ sakkaronto||
Dhammaɱ garu-karonto||
Dhammaɱ apacāya-māno||
Dhammaddhajo||
Dhamma-ketu||
Dhammādhipateyyo||
Dhammikaɱ rakkhāvaraṇa-guttiɱ saɱvidahati bhikkhunīsu.|| ||

Eva-rūpaɱ kāya-kammaɱ sevitabbaɱ,||
eva-rūpaɱ kāya-kammaɱ na sevitabbaɱ||
eva-rūpaɱ vacikammaɱ sevitabbaɱ||
eva-rūpaɱ vacikammaɱ na sevibbaɱ||
eva-rūpaɱ mano-kammaɱ sevitabbaɱ,||
eva-rūpaɱ mano-kammaɱ na sevitabbaɱ,||
eva-rūpo ājīvo sevitabbo,||
eva-rūpo ājivo na sevitabbo,||
eva-rūpo gāmani-gamo sevitabbo,||
eva-rūpo gāmani-gamo na sevitabboti.|| ||

■

Puna ca paraɱ bhikkhu Tathāgato arahaɱ Sammā-Sam-Buddho dhamimiko Dhamma-rājā||
Dhammaɱ yeva nissāya||
Dhammaɱ sakkaronto||
Dhammaɱ garu-karonto||
Dhammaɱ apacāya-māno||
Dhammaddhajo||
Dhamma-ketu||
Dhammādhipateyyo||
Dhammikaɱ rakkhāvaraṇa-guttiɱ saɱvidahati upāsakesu.|| ||

Eva-rūpaɱ kāya-kammaɱ sevitabbaɱ,||
eva-rūpaɱ kāya-kammaɱ na sevitabbaɱ||
eva-rūpaɱ vacikammaɱ sevitabbaɱ||
eva-rūpaɱ vacī-kammaɱ na sevitabbaɱ,||
eva-rūpaɱ mano-kammaɱ sevitabbaɱ,||
eva-rūpaɱ mano-kammaɱ na sevitabbaɱ,||
eva-rūpo ājivo sevitabbo,||
eva-rūpo ājivo na sevitabbo,||
eva-rūpo gāmani-gamo sevitabbo,||
eva-rūpo gāmani-gamo na sevitabbo ti.|| ||

■

Puna ca paraɱ bhikkhu Tathāgato arahaɱ Sammā-Sam-Buddho dhamimiko Dhamma-rājā||
Dhammaɱ yeva nissāya||
Dhammaɱ sakkaronto||
Dhammaɱ garu-karonto||
Dhammaɱ apacāya-māno||
Dhammaddhajo||
Dhamma-ketu||
Dhammādhipateyyo||
Dhammikaɱ rakkhāvaraṇa-guttiɱ saɱvidahati upāsikāsu.|| ||

Eva-rūpaɱ kāya-kammaɱ sevitabbaɱ||
eva-rūpaɱ kāya-kammaɱ na sevitatabbaɱ,||
eva-rūpaɱ vacī-kammaɱ sevitabbaɱ,||
eva-rūpaɱ vacī-kammaɱ na sevitabbaɱ,||
eva-rūpaɱ mano-kammaɱ sevitabbaɱ,||
eva-rūpaɱ mano-kammaɱ na sevitabbaɱ,||
eva-rūpo ājivo sevitabbo,||
eva-rūpo ājivo na sevitabbo,||
eva-rūpo gāmani-gamo sevitabbo,||
eva-rūpo gāmani-gamo na sevitabbo" ti.|| ||

■

Sa kho so bhikkhu Tathāgato arahaɱ Sammā-Sam-Buddho [151] Dhammiko Dhamma-rājā||
Dhammaɱ yeva nissāya||
Dhammaɱ sakkaronto||
Dhammaɱ garu-karonto||
Dhammaɱ apacāya-māno||
Dhammaddhajo||
Dhamma-ketu||
Dhammādhipateyyo||
Dhammikaɱ rakkhāvaraṇa-guttiɱ saɱvida-hitvā bhikkhusu,||
Dhammikaɱ rakkhāvaraṇa-guttiɱ saɱvida-hitvā bhikkhunīsu,||
Dhammikaɱ rakkhāvaraṇa-guttiɱ saɱvida-hitvā upāsakesu,||
Dhammikaɱ rakkhāvaraṇa-guttiɱ saɱvida-hitvā upāsikāsu,||
Dhammen'eva anuttaraɱ Dhammcakkaɱ pacatteti,||
taɱ hoti cakkaɱ appati-vattiyaɱ samaṇena vā brāhmaṇena vā devena vā mārena vā brahmunā vā kenaci vā lokasmin ti.|| ||
