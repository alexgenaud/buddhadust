Aŋguttara-Nikāya
Pañcaka-Nipāta
23. Dīgha-Cārika Vagga

Sutta 227

Bhoga Suttaɱ

Adapted from the 1995 edition of the digital version of the Sri Lanka Buddha Jayanti Tripitaka Series.

 

[259]

[1][pts] Evaɱ me sutaɱ.|| ||

Ekaɱ samayaɱ Bhagavā Sāvatthiyaɱ viharati.|| ||

Tatra kho Bhagavā bhikkhu āmantesi:|| ||

"Bhikkhavo" ti.|| ||

"Bhadante" ti te bhikkhū Bhagavato paccassosuɱ.

Bhagavā etad avoca:|| ||

"Pañc'ime bhikkhave ādinavā bhogesu.|| ||

Katame pañca?|| ||

Aggi-sādhāraṇā bhogā,||
udaka-sādhāraṇā bhogā,||
rāja-sādhāraṇā bhogā,||
cora-sādhāraṇā bhogā,||
appiyehi dāyādehi sādhāraṇā bhogā.|| ||

Ime kho bhikkhave pañca ādīnavā bhogesu.|| ||

 

§

 

Pañc'ime bhikkhave ānisaɱsā bhogesu.|| ||

Katame pañca?|| ||

Bhoge nissāya attānaɱ sukheti||
pīṇeti sammā sukhaɱ pariharati;|| ||

■

mātā-pitaro sukheti||
pīṇoti sammā sukhaɱ pariharati;|| ||

■

putta-dāra-dāsa-kamma-kara-porise sukheti||
pīṇeti sammā sukhaɱ pariharati;|| ||

■

mitt-ā-macce sukheti||
pīṇeti sammā sukhaɱ pariharati;|| ||

■

samaṇa-brāhmaṇesu uddhaggikaɱ dakkhiṇaɱ patiṭṭhāpeti sovaggikaɱ sukha-vipākaɱ sagga-saŋvatta-nikaɱ.|| ||

Ime kho bhikkhave pañca ānisaɱsā bhogesū" ti.|| ||
