Aŋguttara Nikāya
Pañcaka Nipāta
9. Thera Vagga

Sutta 85

Akkhama Suttaɱ

Adapted from the 1995 edition of the digital version of the Sri Lanka Buddha Jayanti Tripitaka Series.

 

[112]

[1][pts] Evaɱ me sutaɱ:|| ||

Ekaɱ samayaɱ Bhagavā Sāvatthiyaɱ viharati.|| ||

Tatra kho Bhagavā bhikkhu āmantesi:|| ||

"Bhikkhavo" ti.|| ||

"Bhadante" ti te bhikkhū Bhagavato paccassosuɱ.|| ||

Bhagavā etad avoca:|| ||

Pañcahi bhikkhave dhammehi||
samannāgato thero bhikkhu sabrahma-cārīnaɱ||
appiyo ca hoti||
amanāpo ca||
agaru ca||
abhāvanīyo ca.|| ||

Katamehi pañcahi?|| ||

Akkhamo hoti rūpānaɱ,||
akkhamo saddānaɱ,||
akkhamo gandhānaɱ,||
akkhamo rasānaɱ,||
akkhamo phoṭṭhabbānaɱ.|| ||

[113] Imehi kho bhikkhave pañcahi dhammehi||
samannāgato thero bhikkhu sabrahma-cārinaɱ||
appiyo ca hoti||
amanāpo ca||
agaru ca||
abhāvaniyo cā' ti.|| ||

 

§

 

Pañcahi bhikkhave dhammehi||
samannāgato thero bhikkhu sabrahma-cārinaɱ||
piyo ca hoti||
manāpo ca||
garu ca||
bhāvanīyo ca.|| ||

Katamehi pañcahi?|| ||

Khamo hoti rūpānaɱ,||
khamo sadudānaɱ,||
khamo gandhānaɱ,||
khamo rasānaɱ,||
khamo phoṭṭhabbānaɱ.|| ||

Imehi kho bhikkhave pañcahi dhammehi||
samannāgato thero bhikkhu sabrahma-cārīnaɱ||
piyo ca hoti||
manāpo ca||
garu ca||
bhāvaniyo cā' ti.|| ||
