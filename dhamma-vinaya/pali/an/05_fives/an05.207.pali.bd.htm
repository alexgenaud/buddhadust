Aṅguttara Nikāya
Pañcaka Nipāta
XXI. Kimbila-Vagga

Sutta 207

Yāgu Suttaɱ

Adapted from the 1995 edition of the digital version of the Sri Lanka Buddha Jayanti Tripitaka Series.

 

[250]

[1][pts] Evaɱ me sutaɱ:|| ||

Ekaɱ samayaɱ Bhagavā Sāvatthiyaɱ viharati.|| ||

Tatra kho Bhagavā bhikkhu āmantesi:|| ||

"Bhikkhavo" ti.|| ||

"Bhadante" ti te bhikkhū Bhagavato paccassosuɱ.|| ||

Pañc'ime bhikkhave, ānisaɱsā yāguyā.|| ||

Katame pañca?|| ||

Khudaɱ paṭihanti,||
pipāsaɱ paṭivineti,||
vātaɱ anulometi,||
vatthiɱ sodheti,||
āmāvasesaɱ pāceti.|| ||

Ime kho bhikkhave, pañca ānisaɱsā yābhuyāti.|| ||
