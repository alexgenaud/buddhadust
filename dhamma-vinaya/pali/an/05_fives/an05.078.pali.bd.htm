Aŋguttara Nikāya
Pañcaka Nipāta
8. Yodhājīva Vagga

Sutta 78

Dutiya Anāgata-Bhaya Suttaɱ

Adapted from the 1995 edition of the digital version of the Sri Lanka Buddha Jayanti Tripitaka Series.

 

[103]

[1][than][pts] Sāvatthi nidānaɱ|| ||

Pañc'imāni bhikkhave anāgata-bhayāni sampassamānena alam eva bhikkhunā|| ||

Appamattena ātāpinā pahit'attena viharituɱ appattassa pattiyā anadhi-gatassa adhigamāya asacchi-katassa sacchi-kiriyāya.|| ||

Katamāni pañca?|| ||

Idha, bhikkhave, bhikkhu iti paṭisañcikkhati:|| ||

"Ahaɱ kho etarahi daharo yuvā susu kā'akeso bhadrena yobbanena samannāgato paṭhamena vayasā hoti kho pana so samayo yaɱ imaɱ kāyaɱ jarā phusati.|| ||

Jiṇṇena kho pana jarāya abhibhūtena na sukaraɱ Buddhānaɱ sāsanaɱ mana-sikātuɱ.|| ||

Na sukarāni araññe vana-patthāni pantāni sen'āsanāni paṭisevituɱ purā maɱ so dhammo āga-c-chati aniṭṭho akanto amanāpo.|| ||

Handāhaɱ paṭigacce va viriyaɱ ārabhāmi appattassa pattiyā anadhi-gatassa adhigamāya asacchi-katassa sacchi-kiriyāya yen'āhaɱ dhammena samannāgato jiṇṇako pi phāsuɱ viharissāmī" ti.|| ||

Idaɱ, bhikkhave, paṭhamaɱ anāgata-bhayaɱ sampassamānena alam eva bhikkhunā appamattena ātāpinā pahit'attena viharituɱ appattassa pattiyā anadhi-gatassa adhigamāya asacchi-katassa sacchi-kiriyāya.|| ||

■

Puna ca paraɱ bhikkhave bhikkhu iti paṭisañcikkhati:|| ||

'Ahaɱ kho etarahi appābādho appātaŋko samavepākiniyā gahaṇiyā samannāgato nātisītāya nāccuṇhāya majjhimāya padhānakkhamāya.|| ||

Hoti kho pana so samayo yaɱ imaɱ kāyaɱ vyādhi phusati.|| ||

Khyādhitena kho pana byādhābhibhūtena na sukaraɱ Buddhānaɱ sāsanaɱ mana-sikātuɱ.|| ||

Na sukarāni araññe vana-patthāni pantāni sen'āsanāni paṭisevituɱ purā maɱ so dhammo āga-c-chati aniṭṭho akanto amanāpo,||
handāhaɱ paṭigacce va viriyaɱ ārabhāmi appattassa pattiyā anadhi-gatassa adhigamāya asacchi-katassa sacchi-kiriyāya- [104] yen'āhaɱ dhammena samannāgato byādhitopi phāsuɱ viharissāmī' ti.|| ||

Idaɱ, bhikkhave, dutiyaɱ anāgata-bhayaɱ sampassamānena alam eva bhikkhunā appamattena ātāpinā pahit'attena viharituɱ appattassa pattiyā anadhi-gatassa adhigamāya asacchi-katassa sacchi-kiriyāya.|| ||

■

Puna ca paraɱ bhikkhave bhikkhu iti paṭisañcikkhati:||
'etarahi kho subhikkhaɱ susassaɱ sulabhapiṇḍaɱ,||
sukaraɱ uñchena paggahena yāpetuɱ.|| ||

Hoti kho pana so samayo yaɱ dubbhikkhaɱ hoti dussassaɱ dullabhapiṇḍaɱ,||
na sukaraɱ uñchena paggahena yāpetuɱ.|| ||

Dubbhikkhena kho pana manussā yena subhikkhaɱ tena saŋkamanti tattha sangaṇ'ikavihāro hoti ākiṇṇavihāro.|| ||

Saŋgaṇikavihāre kho pana sati ākiṇṇavihāre na sukaraɱ Buddhānaɱ sāsanaɱ mana-sikātuɱ.|| ||

Na sukarāni araññe vana-patthāni pantāni sen'āsanāni paṭisevituɱ.|| ||

Purā maɱ so dhammo āga-c-chati aniṭṭho akanto amanāpo handāhaɱ paṭigacc'eva viriyaɱ ārabhāmi appattassa pattiyā anadhi-gatassa adhigamāya asacchi-katassa sacchi-kiriyāya-yen'āhaɱ dhammena samannāgato dubbhikkhe'pi phāsuɱ viharissāmī' ti|| ||

Idaɱ, bhikkhave, tatiyaɱ anāgata-bhayaɱ sampassamānena alam eva bhikkhunāappamattena ātāpinā pahit'attena viharituɱ appattassa pattiyā anadhi-gatassa adhigamāya asacchi-katassa sacchi-kiriyāya.|| ||

■

Puna ca paraɱ bhikkhave iti paṭisañcikkhati:||
etarahi kho manussā samaggā sammodamānā avivadamānā khīrodakībhutā añña-maññaɱ piyacakkhūhi sampassantā viharanti.|| ||

Hoti kho pana so samayo yaɱ bhayaɱ hoti aṭavisaɱkopo.|| ||

Cakkasamārū'hā jāna-padā pariyāyanti.|| ||

Bhaye kho pana sati manussā yena khemaɱ tena saŋkamanti.|| ||

Tattha sangaṇ'ikavihāro hoti ākiṇṇavihāro [105] saḍgaṇikavihāre kho pana sati ākiṇṇa vihāre na sukaraɱ Buddhānaɱ sāsanaɱ mana-sikātuɱ.|| ||

Na sukarāni araññe vana-patthāni pantāni sen'āsanāni paṭisevituɱ.|| ||

■

Purā maɱ so dhammo āga-c-chati aniṭṭho akanto amanāpo,||
handā'haɱ paṭigacc'eva viriyaɱ ārabhāmi||
appattassa pattiyā,||
anadhi-gatassa ādhigamāya,||
asacchi-katassa sacchi-kiriyāya - yen'āhaɱ dhammena samannāgato bhaye'pi phāsuɱ viharissāmī' ti.|| ||

Idaɱ, bhikkhave, catutthaɱ anāgata-bhayaɱ sampassamānena alam eva bhikkhunā appamattena ātāpinā pahit'attena viharituɱ||
appattassa pattiyā||
'anadhi-gatassa adhigamāya asacchi-katassa sacchi-kiriyāya.|| ||

■

Puna ca paraɱ bhikkhave bhikkhu iti paṭisañcikkhati:||
"etarahi kho saŋgho samaggo sammodamāno avivada-māno ekuddeso phāsuɱ viharati.|| ||

Hoti kho pana so samayo yaɱ saŋgho bhijjati.|| ||

Saŋghe kho pana bhinne na sukaraɱ Buddhānaɱ sāsanaɱ mana-sikātuɱ.|| ||

Na sukarāni araññe vana-patthāni pantāni sen'āsanāni paṭisevituɱ.|| ||

Purā maɱ so dhammo āga-c-chati aniṭṭho akanto amanāpo,||
handā'haɱ paṭigacc'eva viriyaɱ ārabhāmi appattassa pattiyā anadhi-gatassa adhigamāya asacchi-katassa sacchi-kiriyāya,||
yen'āhaɱ dhammena samannāgato bhinne'pi saŋghe phāsuɱ viharissāmī" ti|| ||

Idaɱ, bhikkhave, pañcamaɱ anāgata-bhayaɱ sampassamānena alam eva bhikkhunā appamattena ātāpinā pahit'attena viharituɱ appatassa pattiyā anadhi-gatassa adhigamāya asacchi-katassa sacchi-kiriyāya.|| ||

Imāni kho bhikkhave pañca anāgata-bhayāni sampassamānena alam eva bhikkhunā appamattena ātāpinā pahit'attena viharituɱ appattassa pattiyā anadhi-gatassa adhigamāya asacchi-katassa sacchi-kiriyāyā' ti.|| ||
