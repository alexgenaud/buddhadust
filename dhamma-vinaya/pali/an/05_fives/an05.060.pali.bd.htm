Aŋguttara Nikāya
Pañcaka Nipāta
VI: Nīvaraṇa Vagga

Sutta 60

Dutiya Vuḍḍha-Pabba-Jita Suttaɱ

Adapted from the 1995 edition of the digital version of the Sri Lanka Buddha Jayanti Tripitaka Series.

 

[78]

[1][pts][bodh] Evaɱ me sutaɱ:|| ||

Ekaɱ samayaɱ Bhagavā Sāvatthiyaɱ viharati.|| ||

Tatra kho Bhagavā bhikkhu āmantesi:|| ||

"Bhikkhavo" ti.|| ||

"Bhadante" ti te bhikkhū Bhagavato paccassosuɱ.|| ||

Bhagavā etad avoca:|| ||

"Pañcahi bhikkhave dhammehi samannāgato dullabho vuḍḍha-pabba-jito.|| ||

Katamehi pañcahi?|| ||

Dullabho bhikkhave buḍaŋa-pabba-jito suvaco,||
dullabho [79] suggahītaggāhī,||
dullabho pada-k-khiṇaggāhī,||
dullabho Dhamma-kathiko,||
dullabho vinaya-dharo.|| ||

Imehi kho bhikkhave pañcahi dhammehi samannāgato dullabho vuḍḍha-pabba-jito" ti.|| ||
