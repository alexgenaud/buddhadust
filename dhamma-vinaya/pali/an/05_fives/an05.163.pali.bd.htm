Aŋguttara Nikāya
Pañcaka-Nipāta
XVII. Āghāta Vaggo

Sutta 163

Alaɱ-Sākaccha Suttaɱ

Adapted from the 1995 edition of the digital version of the Sri Lanka Buddha Jayanti Tripitaka Series.

 

[190]

[1][pts] Evaɱ me sutaɱ:|| ||

Ekaɱ samayaɱ āyasmā Sāriputto bhikkhu āmantesi:|| ||

"Āvuso bhikkhavo" ti.|| ||

"Āvuso" ti te bhikkhū Sāriputassa paccassosuɱ.|| ||

Āyasmā Sāriputto etad avoca:|| ||

[191] "Pañcah'avuso dhammehi samannāgato bhikkhu alaɱ-sākaccho sabrahma-cārīnaɱ.|| ||

Katamehi pañcahi?|| ||

Idha, bhikkhave, bhikkhu attanā ca sīla-sampanno hoti,||
sīla-sampadāya-kathāya ca āgataɱ pañhaɱ vyākattā hoti.|| ||

■

Attanā ca samādhi-sampanno hoti,||
samādhi-sampadāya kathāya ca āgataɱ pañhaɱ vyākattā hoti.|| ||

■

Attanā ca paññā-sampanno hoti,||
paññā-sampadāya kathāya ca āgataɱ pañhaɱ vyākattā hoti.|| ||

■

Attanā ca vimutti-sampanno hoti,||
vimutti-sampadāya kathāya ca āgataɱ pañhaɱ vyākattā hoti.|| ||

■

Attanā ca vimutti-ñāṇa-dassana-sampanno hoti,||
vimutti-ñāṇa-dassana-sampadāya kathāya ca āgataɱ pañhaɱ vyākattā hoti.|| ||

Imehi kho bhikkhave pañcahi dhammehi samannāgato bhikkhu alaɱ-sākaccho sabrahma-cārīnanti.|| ||
