Aŋguttara Nikāya
Pañcaka Nipāta
II: Bala Vagga

Sutta 11

Tathāgata Bala Suttaɱ

Adapted from the 1995 edition of the digital version of the Sri Lanka Buddha Jayanti Tripitaka Series.

 

 

[1][pts][bodh] Evaɱ me sutaɱ:|| ||

Ekaɱ samayaɱ Bhagavā Sāvatthiyaɱ viharati.|| ||

Tatra kho Bhagavā bhikkhu āmantesi:|| ||

"Bhikkhavo" ti.|| ||

"Bhadante" ti te bhikkhū Bhagavato paccassosuɱ.|| ||

Bhagavā etad avoca:|| ||

Pubibāhaɱ bhikkhave ananussutesu dhammesu abhiññā-vosāna pāramippatto paṭijānāmi.|| ||

Pañc'imāni bhikkhave Tathāgatassa Tathāgata-balāni yehi balehi samannāgato Tathāgato āsabhaɱ ṭhānaɱ paṭijānāti,||
parisāsu sīha-nādaɱ nadati,||
brahma-cakkaɱ pavatteti.|| ||

[10] Katamāni pañca?|| ||

Saddhā-balaɱ,||
hiri-balaɱ,||
ottappa-balaɱ,||
viriya-balaɱ,||
paññā-balaɱ.|| ||

Imāni kho bhikkhave pañca Tathāgatassa Tathāgata-balāni yehi balehi samannāgato Tathāgato āsabhaɱ ṭhānaɱ paṭijānāti,||
parisāsu sīha-nādaɱ nadati,||
brahma-cakkaɱ pavatenti ti.|| ||
