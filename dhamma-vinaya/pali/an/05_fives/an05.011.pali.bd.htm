Aṇguttara Nikāya
Pañcaka Nipāta
II: Bala Vagga

Sutta 11

Tathāgata Bala Suttaṃ

Adapted from the 1995 edition of the digital version of the Sri Lanka Buddha Jayanti Tripitaka Series.

 

 

[1][pts][bodh] Evaṃ me sutaṃ:|| ||

Ekaṃ samayaṃ Bhagavā Sāvatthiyaṃ viharati.|| ||

Tatra kho Bhagavā bhikkhu āmantesi:|| ||

"Bhikkhavo" ti.|| ||

"Bhadante" ti te bhikkhū Bhagavato paccassosuṃ.|| ||

Bhagavā etad avoca:|| ||

Pubibāhaṃ bhikkhave ananussutesu dhammesu abhiññā-vosāna pāramippatto paṭijānāmi.|| ||

Pañc'imāni bhikkhave Tathāgatassa Tathāgata-balāni yehi balehi samannāgato Tathāgato āsabhaṃ ṭhānaṃ paṭijānāti,||
parisāsu sīha-nādaṃ nadati,||
brahma-cakkaṃ pavatteti.|| ||

[10] Katamāni pañca?|| ||

Saddhā-balaṃ,||
hiri-balaṃ,||
ottappa-balaṃ,||
viriya-balaṃ,||
paññā-balaṃ.|| ||

Imāni kho bhikkhave pañca Tathāgatassa Tathāgata-balāni yehi balehi samannāgato Tathāgato āsabhaṃ ṭhānaṃ paṭijānāti,||
parisāsu sīha-nādaṃ nadati,||
brahma-cakkaṃ pavatenti ti.|| ||
