Aŋguttara Nikāya
Pañcaka Nipāta
10. Kakudha Vagga

Sutta 98

Catuttha Akuppa Suttaɱ aka Ārañña Suttaɱ

Adapted from the 1995 edition of the digital version of the Sri Lanka Buddha Jayanti Tripitaka Series.

 

 

[1][pts][than] Evaɱ me sutaɱ:|| ||

Ekaɱ samayaɱ Bhagavā Sāvatthiyaɱ viharati.|| ||

Tatra kho Bhagavā bhikkhu āmantesi:|| ||

"Bhikkhavo" ti.|| ||

"Bhadante" ti te bhikkhū Bhagavato paccassosuɱ.|| ||

Bhagavā etad avoca:|| ||

"Pañc'ahi bhikkhave dhammehi samannāgato bhikkhu anāpānasatiɱ bahulī-karonto na cirass'eva akuppaɱ paṭivijjhati.|| ||

Katamehi pañcahi?|| ||

Idha, bhikkhave, bhikkhu appaṭṭho hoti||
appakicco subharo susantoso jivita-parikkhāresu,|| ||

■

appāhāro hoti ānodarikattaɱ anuyutto,|| ||

■

appamiddho hoti jāgariyaɱ anuyutto,|| ||

■

āraññako hoti panta-sen'āsano,|| ||

■

yathā-vimuttaɱ cittaɱ pacc-a-vekkhati.|| ||

Imehi kho bhikkhave pañcahi dhammehi samannāgato bhikkhu ānāpāna-satiɱ bahulī-karonto na cirass'eva akuppaɱ paṭivijjhat ti.|| ||
