Aŋguttara Nikāya
Pañcaka Nipāta
III: Pañc'aŋgika Vagga

Sutta 27

Samādhi Suttaɱ

Adapted from the 1995 edition of the digital version of the Sri Lanka Buddha Jayanti Tripitaka Series.

 

[24]

[1][pts][than][bodh][olds] Evaɱ me sutaɱ:|| ||

Ekaɱ samayaɱ Bhagavā Sāvatthiyaɱ viharati.|| ||

Tatra kho Bhagavā bhikkhu āmantesi:|| ||

"Bhikkhavo" ti.|| ||

"Bhadante" ti te bhikkhū Bhagavato paccassosuɱ.|| ||

Bhagavā etad avoca:|| ||

"Samādhiɱ bhikkhave bhāvetha appamāṇaɱ nipakā patissatā.|| ||

Samādhiɱ bhikkhave bhāvayataɱ appamāṇaɱ nipakānaɱ patissatānaɱ pañca ñāṇāni paccattaɱ yeva uppajjanti.|| ||

Katamāni pañca?|| ||

'Ayaɱ samādhi pacc'uppanna-sukho c'eva āyatiɱ ca sukha-vipāko' ti||
paccattaɱ yeva ñāṇaɱ uppajjati.|| ||

'Ayaɱ samādhi ariyo nirāmiso' ti||
paccattaɱ yeva ñāṇaɱ uppajjati.|| ||

'Ayaɱ samādhi akāpurisasevito' ti||
paccattaɱ yeva ñāṇaɱ uppajjati.|| ||

'Ayaɱ samādhi santo paṇīto paṭippassaddha-laddho ekodi-bhāvādhi-gato na ca sa-sankhāra-niggayha-vārita vato' ti||
paccattaɱ yeva ñāṇaɱ uppajjati.|| ||

So kho pan'āhaɱ:|| ||

'Imaɱ samādhiɱ||
sato va samāpajjāmi||
sato va vuṭṭhahāmī' ti||
paccattaɱ yeva ñāṇaɱ uppajjati.|| ||

Samādhiɱ bhikkhave bhāvetha appamāṇaɱ nipakā patissatā.|| ||

Samādhiɱ bhikkhave bhāvayataɱ appamāṇaɱ nipakānaɱ patissatānaɱ imāni pañca ñāṇāni paccattaɱ yeva uppajjantī ti.|| ||
