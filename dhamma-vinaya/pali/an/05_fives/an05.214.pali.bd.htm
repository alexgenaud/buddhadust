Aŋguttara Nikāya
Pañcaka Nipāta
XXII. Akkosaka-Vagga

Sutta 214

Bahu-Bhāṇi Suttaɱ

Adapted from the 1995 edition of the digital version of the Sri Lanka Buddha Jayanti Tripitaka Series.

 

[254]

[1][pts] Evaɱ me sutaɱ:|| ||

Ekaɱ samayaɱ Bhagavā Sāvatthiyaɱ viharati.|| ||

Tatra kho Bhagavā bhikkhu āmantesi:|| ||

"Bhikkhavo" ti.|| ||

"Bhadante" ti te bhikkhū Bhagavato paccassosuɱ.|| ||

Bhagavā etad avoca:|| ||

"Pañc'ime bhikkhave ādīnavā bahu-bhāṇiyasmiɱ puggale.|| ||

Katame pañca?|| ||

Musā bhaṇati,||
pisunaɱ bhaṇati,||
pharusaɱ bhaṇati,||
sampha-p-palāpaɱ bhaṇati,||
kāyassa bhedā parammaṇā apāyaɱ duggatiɱ vinipātaɱ Nirayaɱ uppajjati.|| ||

Ime kho bhikkhave pañca ādīnavā bahu-bhāṇismiɱ puggale.|| ||

 

§

 

Pañc'ime bhikkhave ānisaɱsā manta-bhāṇismiɱ puggale.|| ||

Katame pañca?|| ||

Na musā bhaṇti,||
na pisunaɱ bhaṇti,||
na pharusaɱ bhaṇati,||
na sampha-p-palāpaɱ bhaṇti,||
kāyassa bhedā param maraṇā sugatiɱ saggaɱ lokaɱ uppajjati.|| ||

Ime kho bhikkhave pañca ānisaɱsā manta-bhāṇismīɱ puggale" ti.|| ||
