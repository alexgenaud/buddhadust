Aŋguttara Nikāya
Pañcaka Nipāta
9. Thera Vagga

Sutta 89

Paṭhama Sekha Suttaɱ

Adapted from the 1995 edition of the digital version of the Sri Lanka Buddha Jayanti Tripitaka Series.

 

[116]

[1][pts] Evaɱ me sutaɱ:|| ||

Ekaɱ samayaɱ Bhagavā Sāvatthiyaɱ viharati.|| ||

Tatra kho Bhagavā bhikkhu āmantesi:|| ||

"Bhikkhavo" ti.|| ||

"Bhadante" ti te bhikkhū Bhagavato paccassosuɱ.|| ||

Bhagavā etad avoca:|| ||

Pañc'ime bhikkhave dhammā sekhassa bhikkhuno parihānāya saŋvattanti.|| ||

Katame pañca?|| ||

Kamm'ārāmatā,||
bhass-ā-rāmatā,||
nidd-ā-rāmatā,||
saɱgaṇikārāmatā,||
yathā-vimuttaɱ cittaɱ na pacc-a-vekkhati.|| ||

Ime kho bhikkhave pañca dhammā sekhassa bhikkhuno parihānāya saŋvattanti.|| ||

 

§

 

Pañc'ime bhikkhave dhammā sekhassa bhikkhuno aparihānāya saŋvattanti.|| ||

Katame pañca?|| ||

Na kamm-ā-rāmatā,||
na bhass-ā-rāmatā,||
na ninaddārāmatā,||
na saɱgaṇikārāmatā,||
yathā-vimuttaɱ cittaɱ pacc-a-vekkhati.|| ||

Ime kho bhikkhave pañca dhammā sekhassa bhikkhuno aparihānāya saŋvattanti.|| ||
