Aŋguttara Nikāya
Pañcaka Nipāta
15. Tikaṇḍaki Vaggo

Sutta 141

Avajānāti Suttaɱ

Adapted from the 1995 edition of the digital version of the Sri Lanka Buddha Jayanti Tripitaka Series.

 

[164]

[1][pts] Evaɱ me sutaɱ:|| ||

Ekaɱ samayaɱ Bhagavā Sāvatthiyaɱ viharati.|| ||

Tatra kho Bhagavā bhikkhu āmantesi:|| ||

"Bhikkhavo" ti.|| ||

"Bhadante" ti te bhikkhū Bhagavato paccassosuɱ.|| ||

Bhagavā etad avoca:|| ||

2. Pañcame bhikkhave puggalā santo saŋvijj'amānā lokasmiɱ.|| ||

Katame pañca?|| ||

Datvā avajānāti,||
saŋvāsena avajānāti,||
ādiyyamukho hoti,||
lolo hoti,||
mando momūho hoti.|| ||

 

§

 

3. Kathañ ca bhikkhave puggalo datvā avajānāti?|| ||

Idha, bhikkhave, puggalo puggalassa deti cīvara-piṇḍa-pāta-sen'āsana-gilāna-paccaya-bhesajja-parikkhāraɱ.|| ||

Tassa evaɱ hoti:|| ||

'Ahaɱ demi, ayaɱ patigaṇhātī' ti.|| ||

Tam enaɱ datvā avajānāti.|| ||

Evaɱ kho bhikkhave puggalo datvā avajānāti.|| ||

■

4. Kathañ ca bhikkhave puggalo saŋvāsena avajānāti?|| ||

[165] Idha bhikkhave puggalo puggalena saddhiɱ saɱvasati dve vā tiṇi vā vassāni.|| ||

Tam enaɱ saŋvāsena avajānāti.|| ||

Evaɱ kho bhikkhave puggalo saŋvāsena avajānāti.|| ||

■

5. Kathañ ca bhikkhave puggalo ādiya-mukho hoti?|| ||

Idha, bhikkhave, ekacco puggalo parassa vaṇeṇe vā avaṇeṇa vā bhāsiyamāne taɱ khippaɱ yeva adhimuccitā hoti.|| ||

Evaɱ kho bhikkhave puggalo ādiya-mukho hoti.|| ||

■

6. Kathañ ca bhikkhave puggalo lolo hoti?|| ||

Idha, bhikkhave, ekacco puggalo ittara-saddho hoti,||
ittara-bhatti,||
ittara-pemo,||
ittarappasādo.|| ||

Evaɱ kho bhikkhave puggalo lolo hoti.|| ||

■

7. Kathañ ca bhikkhave puggalo mando momūho hoti?|| ||

Idha bhikkhave ekacco puggalo kusalākusale dhamme na jānāti,||
sāvajjānavajje dhamme na jānāti,||
hīnappaṇite dhamme na jānāni,||
kaṇha-sukka-sappaṭi-bhāge dhamme na jānāti.|| ||

Evaɱ kho bhikkhave puggalo mando momūho hoti.|| ||

Ime kho bhikkhave pañca puggalā santo saŋvijj'amānā lokasmin" ti.|| ||
