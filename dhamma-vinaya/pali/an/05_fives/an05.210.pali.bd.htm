Aŋguttara Nikāya
Pañcaka Nipāta
XXI. Kimbila-Vagga

Sutta 210

Muṭṭha-s-Sati Suttaɱ

Adapted from the 1995 edition of the digital version of the Sri Lanka Buddha Jayanti Tripitaka Series.

 

[251]

[1][pts][olds] Evaɱ me sutaɱ:|| ||

Ekaɱ samayaɱ Bhagavā Sāvatthiyaɱ viharati.|| ||

Tatra kho Bhagavā bhikkhu āmantesi:|| ||

"Bhikkhavo" ti.|| ||

"Bhadante" ti te bhikkhū Bhagavato paccassosuɱ.|| ||

Bhagavā etad avoca:|| ||

"Pañc'ime bhikkhave ādīnavā muṭṭha-s-satissa asampajānassa niddaɱ okkamato.|| ||

Katame pañca?|| ||

Dukkhaɱ supati,||
dukkhaɱ paṭibujjhati,||
pāpakaɱ supinaɱ passati,||
devatā na rakkhanti,||
asuci muccati.|| ||

Ime kho bhikkhave pañca ādīnavā muṭṭha-s-satissa asampajānassa niddaɱ okkamato.|| ||

 

§

 

Pañc'ime bhikkhave ānisaɱsā ānisaɱsā upaṭṭhita-satissa sampajānassa niddaɱ okkamato.|| ||

Katame pañca?|| ||

Sukhaɱ supati,||
sukhaɱ paṭibujjhati,||
na pāpakaɱ supinaɱ passati,||
devatā rakkhanti,||
asuci na muccati.|| ||

Ime kho bhikkhave pañca ānisaɱsā upaṭṭhita-satissa sampajānassa niddaɱ okkamato ti.|| ||
