Aŋguttara Nikāya
Pañcaka Nipāta
II: Bala Vagga

Sutta 15

Bala-Daṭṭhabba Suttaɱ

Adapted from the 1995 edition of the digital version of the Sri Lanka Buddha Jayanti Tripitaka Series.

 

[11]

[1][pts][bodh] Evaɱ me sutaɱ:|| ||

Ekaɱ samayaɱ Bhagavā Sāvatthiyaɱ viharati.|| ||

Tatra kho Bhagavā bhikkhu āmantesi:|| ||

"Bhikkhavo" ti.|| ||

"Bhadante" ti te bhikkhū Bhagavato paccassosuɱ.|| ||

Bhagavā etad avoca:|| ||

Pañc'imāni bhikkhave balāni.|| ||

Katamāni pañca?|| ||

Saddhā-balaɱ,||
viriya-balaɱ,||
sati-balaɱ,||
samādhi-balaɱ,||
[12] paññā-balaɱ.|| ||

■

Kattha ca bhikkhave saddhā-balaɱ daṭṭhabbaɱ?|| ||

Catusu sot'āpattiyaŋgesu.|| ||

Ettha saddhā-balaɱ daṭṭhabbaɱ.|| ||

■

Kattha ca bhikkhave viriya-balaɱ daṭṭhabbaɱ?|| ||

Catusu samma-p-padhānesu.|| ||

Ettha viriya-balaɱ daṭṭhabbaɱ.|| ||

■

Kattha ca bhikkhave sati-balaɱ daṭṭhabbaɱ?|| ||

Catusu sati-paṭṭhānesu.|| ||

Ettha sati-balaɱ daṭṭhabbaɱ.|| ||

■

Kattha ca bhikkhave samādhi-balaɱ daṭṭhabbaɱ?|| ||

Catusu jhānesu.|| ||

Ettha samādhi-balaɱ daṭṭhabbaɱ.|| ||

■

Kattha ca bhikkhave paññā-balaɱ daṭṭhabba?|| ||

Catusu ariya-saccesu.|| ||

Ettha paññā-balaɱ daṭṭhabbaɱ.|| ||

Imāni kho bhikkhave pañca-balānī" ti.|| ||
