Aŋguttara Nikāya
XI. Ekā-Dasaka Nipāta
I. Nissāya

Sutta 1

Kim Atthiya? Suttaɱ

Adapted from the 1995 edition of the digital version of the Sri Lanka Buddha Jayanti Tripitaka Series.

 

[311]

[1][pts][than] Evaɱ me sutaɱ:|| ||

Ekaɱ samayaɱ Bhagavā Sāvatthiyaɱ viharati||
Jetavane Anāthapiṇḍikassa ārāme.|| ||

2. Atha kho āyasmā Ānando yena Bhagavā ten'upasankami.|| ||

Upasaŋkamitvā Bhagavantaɱ abhivādetvā eka-m-antaɱ nisīdi.|| ||

Eka-m-antaɱ nisinno kho āyasmā Ānando Bhagavantaɱ etad avoca:|| ||

"Kim-atthiyāni bhante kusalāni sīlāni kim-ānisaɱsānī" ti?|| ||

"Avi-p-paṭisāra-t-thāni kho Ānanda kusalāni sīlāni avi-p-paṭisār-ā-nisaɱsānī" ti.|| ||

■

"Avi-p-paṭisāro pana bhante kim-atthiyo kim-ānisaɱso" ti?|| ||

"Avi-p-paṭisāro kho Ānanda pāmujjāttho pāmujj-ā-nisaɱso" ti.|| ||

■

"Pāmujjaɱ pana bhante kim-atthiyaɱ kim-ānisaɱsan" ti?|| ||

"Pāmujjaɱ kho Ānanda pītatthaɱ pīt-ā-nisaɱsaɱ" ti.|| ||

■

"Pīti pana bhante kim-atthiyā kim-ānisaɱsā" ti?|| ||

"Pīti kho Ānanda passaddhatthā passaddhā-nisaɱsā" ti.|| ||

■

"Passaddhi pana bhante kim-atthiyā kim-ānisaɱsā" ti?|| ||

"Passaddhi kho Ānanda sukhatthā sukh-ā-nisaɱsā" ti.|| ||

■

"Sukhaɱ pana bhante kim-atthiyaɱ kim-ānisaɱsan" ti?|| ||

"Sukhaɱ kho Ānanda samādhatthaɱ samādhā-nisaɱsaɱ" ti.|| ||

■

"Samādhi pana bhante kim-attho kim-ānisaɱso" ti?|| ||

"Samādhi kho Ānanda yathā-bhūta-ñāṇa-dassanattho yathā-bhūta-ñāṇa-dassan-ā-nisaɱso" ti.|| ||

■

"Yathā-bhūta-ñāṇa-dassanaɱ pana bhante kim-atthiyaɱ kim-ānisaɱsan" ti?|| ||

"Yathā-bhūta-ñāṇa-dassanaɱ kho Ānanda nibbindanatthaɱ nibbidā-nisaɱsaɱ" ti.|| ||

■

"Nibbidā pana bhante kim-attho kim-ānisaɱso" ti?|| ||

"Nibbidā kho Ānanda virāgathā [312] virāgā-nisaɱsā" ti.|| ||

■

"Virāgo pana bhante kim-atthiyo kim-ānisaɱso." ti?|| ||

"Virāgo kho Ānanda vimutti-ñāṇa-dassanattho vimutti-ñāṇa-dassan-ā-nisaɱso.|| ||

3. Iti kho Ānanda kusalāni sīlāni avi-p-paṭisāra-t-thāni avi-p-paṭisār-ā-nisaɱsāni,||
Avi-p-paṭisāro pāmujjattho pāmujj-ā-nisaɱso,||
Pāmujjaɱ pītatthaɱ pīt-ā-nisaɱsaɱ,||
Pīti passaddhatthā passaddhā-nisaɱsā,||
Passaddhi sukhatthā sukh-ā-nisaɱsā,||
Sukhaɱ samādhatthaɱ samādhā-nisaɱsaɱ,||
Samādhi yathā-bhūta-ñāṇa-dassanattho yathā-bhūta-ñāṇa-dassan-ā-nisaɱso,||
Yathā-bhūta-ñāṇa-dassanā nibbindatthaɱ nibbidā-nisaɱsaɱ,||
nibbidā virāgatthā virāgā-nisaɱsā,||
virāgo vimutti-ñāṇa-dassanattho vimutti-ñāṇa-dassan-ā-nisaɱso.|| ||

Iti kho Ānanda kusalāni sīlāni anupubbena aggāya parentī" ti.|| ||
