Aŋguttara Nikāya
Ekā-Dasaka Nipāta

Suttas 483-962

Adapted from the 1995 edition of the digital version of the Sri Lanka Buddha Jayanti Tripitaka Series.

 

 

11. 3. 481- 960

Ekādasahi bhikkhave aŋgehi samannāgato gopālako bhabbo gogaṇaɱ pariharituɱ, phātikatuɱ.|| ||

Katamehi ekādasahi?|| ||

Idha bhikkhave gopālako rūpaññū hoti ... pe ...|| ||

Evam eva kho bhikkhave ekādasahi dhammehi samannāgato bhikkhu bhabbo cakkhusmiɱ anicc'ānupassī viharituɱ ... pe ... paṭinissagg-ā-nupassī viharitunti.|| ||

Sāmaññavaggo tatiyo.|| ||
