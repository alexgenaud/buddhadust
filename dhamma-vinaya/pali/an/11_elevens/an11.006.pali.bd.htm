Aŋguttara Nikāya
XI. Ekā-Dasaka Nipāta
I. Nissāya

Sutta 6

Vyasana Suttaɱ

Adapted from the 1995 edition of the digital version of the Sri Lanka Buddha Jayanti Tripitaka Series.

 

[317]

[1][pts] "Yo so bhikkhave bhikkhu akkosaka-paribhāsako ariy'ūpavādī sabrahma-cārīnaɱ||
aṭṭhānam etaɱ anavakāso||
yaɱ so ekādasannaɱ vyasanānaɱ aññataraɱ vyasanaɱ na niga-c-cheyya.|| ||

 

§

 

Katamesaɱ ekā-dasannaɱ?

2. [1] Anadhigataɱ nādhigacchati;||
[2] adhigatā parihāyati;||
[3] Sad'Dhammassa na vodāyati;||
[4] Sad'Dhammesu vā adhimāniko hoti;||
[5] anabhirato vā Brahma-cariyaɱ carati;||
[6] aññataraɱ vā [318] saŋkiliṭṭhaɱ āpattiɱ āpajjati;||
[7] sikkhaɱ vā pacca-k-khāya hīnāyāvattati;||
[8] gāḷhaɱ vā rog'ātaŋkaɱ phusati;||
[9] ummādaɱ vā pāpuṇāti cittakkhepaɱ;||
[10] sammūḷho kālaɱ karoti;||
[11] kāyassa bhedā param maraṇā apāyaɱ duggatiɱ vinipātaɱ Nirayaɱ uppajjati.|| ||

 

§

 

Yo so bhikkhave bhikkhu akkosaka-paribhāsako ariy'ūpavādī sabrahma-cārīnaɱ||
aṭṭhānam etaɱ anavakāso||
yaɱ so ekādasannaɱ vyasanānaɱ aññataraɱ vyasanaɱ na niga-c-cheyya" ti.|| ||
