Aŋguttara-Nikāya
II. Dukanipāta
VIII. Nimitta Vagga

Adapted from the 1995 edition of the digital version of the Sri Lanka Buddha Jayanti Tripitaka Series.

Suttas 76-85

 

[82]

Sutta 76

Sanimittā

[76.1][pts][olds] "Sanimittā bhikkhave uppajjanti pāpakā akusalā dhammā,||
no animittā.|| ||

Tass'eva nimittassa pahānā evaɱ te pāpakā akusalā dhammā na hontī" ti.|| ||
