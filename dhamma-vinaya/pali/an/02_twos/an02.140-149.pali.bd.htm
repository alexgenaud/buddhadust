Aŋguttara-Nikāya
II. Duka Nipāta
XIII. Dāna Vagga

Adapted from the 1995 edition of the digital version of the Sri Lanka Buddha Jayanti Tripitaka Series.

Suttas 140-149

 

[91]

Sutta 140

[140.1][pts][olds] "Dve'māni bhikkhave dānāni.|| ||

Katamāni dve?|| ||

Āmisa-dānañ ca,||
dhamma-dānañ ca.|| ||

Imāni kho bhikkhave dve dānāni.|| ||

Etad aggaɱ bhikkhave imesaɱ dvinnaɱ dānānaɱ yad idaɱ dhamma-dānan" ti.|| ||

 

§
