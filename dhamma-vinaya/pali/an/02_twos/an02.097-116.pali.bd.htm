Aṇguttara-Nikāya
II. Duka Nipāta
X. Bāla Vagga

Adapted from the 1995 edition of the digital version of the Sri Lanka Buddha Jayanti Tripitaka Series.

Suttas 97-116

 

[84]

Sutta 97

[97.1][pts][than][olds] "Dve'me bhikkhave bālā.|| ||

Katame dve?|| ||

Yo ca anāgataṃ bhāraṃ vahati,||
yo ca āgataṃ bhāraṃ na vahati.|| ||

Ime kho bhikkhave dve bālā" ti.|| ||

Sutta 107

[107.1][pts][olds] "Dvinnaṃ bhikkhave āsavā vaḍḍhanti.|| ||

Katamesaṃ dvinnaṃ?|| ||

Yo ca na kukkuccāyitabbaṃ kukkuccāyati,||
yo ca kukkuccāyitabbaṃ na kukkuccāyati.|| ||

Imesaṃ kho bhikkhave dvinnaṃ āsavā vaḍḍhantī" ti.|| ||
