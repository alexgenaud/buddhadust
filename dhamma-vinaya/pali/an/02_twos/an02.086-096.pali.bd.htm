Aŋguttara-Nikāya
II. Dukanipāta
IX. Dhamma Vagga

Adapted from the 1995 edition of the digital version of the Sri Lanka Buddha Jayanti Tripitaka Series.

Suttas 86-96

 

[83]

Sutta 86

[86.1][pts][olds] "Dve'me bhikkhave dhammā.|| ||

Katame dve?|| ||

Ceto-vimutti ca paññā-vimutti ca.|| ||

Ime kho bhikkhave dve dhammā" ti.|| ||

 

 

Sutta 87

[87.1][pts][olds] "Dve'me bhikkhave dhammā.|| ||

Katame dve?|| ||

Paggāho ca avikkhepo ca.|| ||

Ime kho bhikkhave dve dhammā" ti.|| ||

 

 

Sutta 88

[88.1][pts][olds] "Dve'me bhikkhave dhammā.|| ||

Katame dve?|| ||

Nāmaɱ ca rūpaɱ ca.|| ||

Ime kho bhikkhave dve dhammā" ti.|| ||

 

 

Sutta 89

[89.1][pts][olds] "Dve'me bhikkhave dhammā.|| ||

Katame dve?|| ||

Vijjā ca vimutti ca.|| ||

Ime kho bhikkhave dve dhammā" ti.|| ||

 

 

Sutta 90

[90.1][pts][olds] "Dve'me bhikkhave dhammā.|| ||

Katame dve?|| ||

Bhava-diṭṭhi ca vibhava-diṭṭhi ca.|| ||

Ime kho bhikkhave dve dhammā" ti.|| ||

 

 

Sutta 91

[91.1][pts][olds] "Dve'me bhikkhave dhammā.|| ||

Katame dve?|| ||

Ahirikañ ca anottappañ ca.|| ||

Ime kho bhikkhave dve dhammā" ti.|| ||

 

 

Sutta 92

[92.1][pts][olds] "Dve'me bhikkhave dhammā.|| ||

Katame dve?|| ||

Hiri ca ottappañ ca.|| ||

Ime kho bhikkhave dve dhammā" ti.|| ||

 

 

Sutta 93

[93.1][pts][olds] "Dve'me bhikkhave dhammā.|| ||

Katame dve?|| ||

Dovacassatā ca pāpa-mittā ca.|| ||

Ime kho bhikkhave dve dhammā" ti.|| ||

 

 

Sutta 94

[94.1][pts][olds] "Dve'me bhikkhave dhammā.|| ||

Katame dve?|| ||

Sovacassatā ca kalyāṇa-mittatā ca.|| ||

Ime kho bhikkhave dve dhammā" ti.|| ||

 

 

Sutta 95

[95.1][pts][olds] "Dve'me bhikkhave dhammā.|| ||

Katame dve?|| ||

Dhātukusalatā ca mana-sikārakusalatā ca.|| ||

Ime kho bhikkhave dve dhammā" ti.|| ||

 

 

Sutta 96

[96.1][pts][olds] [84] "Dve'me bhikkhave dhammā.|| ||

Katame dve?|| ||

Āpatti-kusalatā ca āpatti-vuṭṭhāna-kusalatā ca.|| ||

Ime kho bhikkhave dve dhammā" ti.|| ||
