Aŋguttara-Nikāya
II. Duka Nipāta
XV. Samāpatti Vagga

Adapted from the 1995 edition of the digital version of the Sri Lanka Buddha Jayanti Tripitaka Series.

Suttas 162-178

 

[94]

Sutta 162

[162.1][pts][olds] "Dve'me bhikkhave dhammā.|| ||

Katame dve?|| ||

Samāpatti-kusalatā ca||
samāpatti-vuṭṭhāna-kusalatā ca.|| ||

Ime kho bhikkhave dve dhammā" ti.|| ||

[163.1][pts][olds] "Dve'me bhikkhave dhammā.|| ||
