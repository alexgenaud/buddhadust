Aṅguttara Nikāya
Sattaka Nipāta
5. Mahāyaññā Vaggo

Sutta 43

Paṭhama Aggi Suttaɱ

Adapted from the 1995 edition of the digital version of the Sri Lanka Buddha Jayanti Tripitaka Series.

 

[41]

[1][pts][upal] EVAṂ ME SUTAṂ.|| ||

Ekaɱ samayaɱ Bhagavā Sāvatthiyaɱ viharati.|| ||

Satt'ime bhikkhave, aggī.|| ||

Katame satta?|| ||

Rāg'aggi,||
dos'aggi,||
moh'aggi,||
āhuṇeyy'aggi,||
gahapat'aggi,||
dakkhiṇeyy'aggi,||
kaṭṭh'aggi.|| ||

Ime kho bhikkhave, satta aggī' ti.|| ||
