Aŋguttara Nikāya
Sattaka Nipāta
Avyākata Vagga

Sutta 57

Satta-Dhamma Suttaɱ

Adapted from the 1995 edition of the digital version of the Sri Lanka Buddha Jayanti Tripitaka Series.

 

[85]

[1][pts] EVAṂ ME SUTAṂ.|| ||

Ekaɱ samayaɱ Bhagavā Sāvatthiyaɱ viharati.|| ||

Tatra kho Bhagavā bhikkhū āmantesi "Bhikkhavo" ti.|| ||

"Bhadante" ti te bhikkhū Bhagavato paccassosuɱ.|| ||

Bhagavā etad avoca:|| ||

"Sattahi bhikkhave, dhammehi samannāgato bhikkhu na cirass'eva āsavānaɱ khayā||
anāsavaɱ ceto-vimuttiɱ||
paññā-vimuttiɱ||
diṭṭhe'va dhamme sayaɱ abhiññā sacchi-katvā upasampajja vihareyya.|| ||

 

§

 

Katamehi sattahi?|| ||

Idha, bhikkhave, bhikkhū saddho hoti,||
sīlavā hoti,||
bahu-s-suto hoti,||
paṭisallīno hoti,||
āraddha-viriyo hoti,||
satimā hoti,||
paññavā hoti.|| ||

Imehi kho bhikkhave, sattahi dhammehi samannāgato bhikkhu na cirass'eva āsavānaɱ khayā,||
anāsavaɱ ceto-vimuttiɱ,||
paññā-vimuttiɱ,||
diṭṭhe'va dhamme sayaɱ abhiññā sacchi-katvā upasampajja vihareyyā" ti.|| ||
