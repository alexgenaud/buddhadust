Aŋguttara Nikāya
Sattaka Nipāta
III: Vajjī Vaggo

Sutta 25

Pañcama Bhikkhū Aparihānīya Suttaɱ

Adapted from the 1995 edition of the digital version of the Sri Lanka Buddha Jayanti Tripitaka Series.

 

[24]

[1][pts] EVAṂ ME SUTAṂ.|| ||

Ekaɱ samayaɱ Bhagavā Sāvatthiyaɱ viharati.|| ||

Satta vo bhikkhave,||
aparihānīye dhamme desissāmi.|| ||

Taɱ sunātha||
sādhukaɱ||
manasi-karotha||
bhāsissāmi ti.

'Evaɱ bhante' ti kho te bhikkhū Bhagavato paccassosuɱ.|| ||

Bhagavā etad avoca:|| ||

2. Katame ca bhikkhave, satta aparihānīyā dhammā?|| ||

Yāvakīvañ ca bhikkhave, bhikkhū anicca-saññaɱ bhāvessantī,||
vuddhi yeva bikkhave,||
bhikkhūnaɱ pāṭikaŋkhā||
no parihānīti.|| ||

Yāvakīvañ ca bhikkhave, bhikkhū anatta-saññaɱ bhāvessanti,||
vuddhi yeva bikkhave,||
bhikkhūnaɱ pāṭikaŋkhā||
no parihānīti.|| ||

Yāvakīvañ ca bhikkhave, bhikkhu asubha-saññaɱ bhāvessanti,||
vuddhi yeva bikkhave,||
bhikkhūnaɱ pāṭikaŋkhā||
no parihānīti.|| ||

Yāvakīvañ ca bhikkhave, bhikkhū ādīnava-saññaɱ bhāvessanti,||
vuddhi yeva bikkhave,||
bhikkhūnaɱ pāṭikaŋkhā||
no parihānīti.|| ||

Yāvakīvañ ca bhikkhave, bhikkhū pahāna-saññaɱ bhāvessanti,||
vuddhi yeva bikkhave,||
bhikkhūnaɱ pāṭikaŋkhā||
no parihānīti.|| ||

Yāvakīvañ ca bhikkhave bhikkhu virāga-saññaɱ bhāvessanti,||
vuddhi yeva bikkhave,||
bhikkhūnaɱ pāṭikaŋkhā||
no parihānīti.|| ||

Yāvakīvañ ca bhikkhave, bhikkhū nirodha-saññaɱ bhāvessanti, vuddhi yeva bhikkhave, bhikkhūnaɱ pāṭikaŋkhā no parihānī.|| ||

Yāvakīvañ ca bhikkhave, ime satta aparihānīyā dhammā bhikkhusu ṭhassanti.|| ||

Imesu ca sattasu aparihānīyesu dhammesu bhikkhū sandissanti,||
vuddhi yeva bhikkhave,||
bhikkhūnaɱ pāṭikaŋkhā||
no parihānī ti.
