Aŋguttara Nikāya
Sattaka Nipāta
IV. Devatā Vagga

Sutta 32

Hiri-Gārava Suttaɱ

Adapted from the 1995 edition of the digital version of the Sri Lanka Buddha Jayanti Tripitaka Series.

 

[28]

[1][pts][than] EVAṂ ME SUTAṂ.|| ||

Ekaɱ samayaɱ Bhagavā Sāvatthiyaɱ viharati.|| ||

Imaɱ bhikkhave, rattiɱ aññatarā devatā abhikkantāya rattiyā||
abhikkantavaṇnā kevala-kappaɱ Jetavanaɱ obhāsetvā [29] yen'āhaɱ ten'upasankami.|| ||

Upasaŋkamitvā maɱ abhivādetvā eka-m-antaɱ aṭṭhāsi.|| ||

Eka-m-antaɱ ṭhitā ko bikkhave,||
sā devatā maɱ etad avoca:|| ||

Satt'imi bhante, dhammā bhikkhuno aparihānāya saŋvattantī.|| ||

Katame satta?|| ||

Satthu-gāravatā,||
Dhamma-gāravatā,||
Saŋgha-gāravatā,||
sikkhā-gāravatā,||
samādhi-gāravatā,||
hiri-gāravatā,||
ottappa-gāravatā.|| ||

Ime kho bhante, satta dhammā bhikkhuno aparihānāya saŋvattanti, ti.|| ||

Idam avoca bikkhave, sā devatā.|| ||

Idaɱ vatvā maɱ abhivādetvā padakkhiṇaɱ katvā tatth'ev'antara-dhāyī, ti.

Satthu-garu dhamma-garu saŋghe ca tibba-gāravo,||
Samādhi-garu ātāpi sikkhāya tibba-gāravo.||
Hir'ottappa'sampanno sappatisso sagāravo,||
Abhabbo parihānāya Nibbānass'eva santike" ti.|| ||
