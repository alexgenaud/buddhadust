Aŋguttara Nikāya
Sattaka Nipāta
1. Dhana Vaggo

Sutta 8

Satta Saŋyojana Suttaɱ

Adapted from the 1995 edition of the digital version of the Sri Lanka Buddha Jayanti Tripitaka Series.

 

[7]

[1][pts][upal] EVAṂ ME SUTAṂ.|| ||

Ekaɱ samayaɱ Bhagavā Sāvatthiyaɱ viharati.|| ||

Tatra kho Bhagavā bhikkhū āmantesi:|| ||

"Bhikkhavo" ti.|| ||

"Bhadante" ti te bhikkhū Bhagavato paccassosuɱ.|| ||

Bhagavā etad avoca:|| ||

"Satt'imāni bhikkhave, saŋyojanānī.|| ||

Katamāni satta?|| ||

Anunaya-saŋyojanaɱ,||
paṭigha-saŋyojanaɱ,||
diṭṭhi-saŋyojanaɱ,||
vicikicchā-saŋyojanaɱ,||
māna-saŋyojanaɱ,||
bhava-rāga-saŋyojanaɱ,||
avijjā-saŋyojanaɱ.|| ||

Imāni kho bhikkhave satta saŋyojanānī" ti.|| ||
