Aŋguttara Nikāya
Sattaka Nipāta
2. Anusaya Vagga

Sutta 17

Dukkh'ānupassi Suttaɱ — Anatt'ānupassī Suttaɱ — Nibbāne Sukh'ānupassī Suttaɱ[ed1]

Adapted from the 1995 edition of the digital version of the Sri Lanka Buddha Jayanti Tripitaka Series.

 

[14]

Sutta 17 (a)

Dukkh-ā-nupassi Suttaɱ

[1][pts] EVAṂ ME SUTAṂ.|| ||

Ekaɱ samayaɱ Bhagavā Sāvatthiyaɱ viharati.|| ||

Tatra kho Bhagavā bhikkhū āmantesi:|| ||

"Bhikkhavo" ti.|| ||

"Bhadante" ti te bhikkhū Bhagavato paccassosuɱ.|| ||

Bhagavā etad avoca:|| ||

Satt'ime bhikkhave, puggalā āhuneyyā pāhuneyyā dakkhiṇeyyā añjali-karaṇīyā anuttaraɱ puñña-k-khettaɱ lokassa.|| ||

Katame satta?|| ||

Idha, bhikkhave, ekacco puggalo sabba-sankhāresu dukkh-ā-nupassī viharati dukkha-sañña dukkha-paṭisaɱvedī satataɱ samitaɱ abbokiṇṇaɱ cetasā adhimuccamāno paññāya pariyogāhamāno.|| ||

So āsavānaɱ khayā ceto-vimuttiɱ paññā-vimuttiɱ diṭṭhe'va dhamme sayaɱ abhiññā sacchi-katvā upasampajja viharati.|| ||

Ayaɱ bhikkhave, paṭhamo puggalo āhuneyyo pāhuneyyo dakkhiṇeyyo añjali-karaṇīyo anuttaraɱ puñña-k-khettaɱ lokassa.|| ||

■

3. Puna ca paraɱ bhikkhave, idh'ekacco puggalo dukkh-ā-nupassī viharati dukkha-sañña dukkha-paṭisaɱvedī satataɱ samitaɱ abbokiṇṇaɱ cetasā adhimuccamāno paññāya pariyogāhamāno.|| ||

Tassa apubbaɱ acarimaɱ āsava-pariyādānañ ca hoti jivita-pariyādānañ ca.|| ||

Ayaɱ bhikkhave, dutiyo puggalo āhuneyyo pāhuneyyo dakkhi neyyo añjali-karaṇeyyo anuttaraɱ puñña-k-khettaɱ lokassa.|| ||

■

4. Puna ca paraɱ bhikkhave, idh'ekacco puggalo dukkh-ā-nupassī viharati dukkha-sañña dukkha-paṭisaɱvedī satataɱ samitaɱ abbokiṇṇaɱ cetasā adhimuccamāno paññāyā-pariyogāhamāno.|| ||

So pañcannaɱ ora-m-bhāgiyānaɱ saŋyojanānaɱ parikkhayā antarā-parinibbāyī hoti.|| ||

Ayaɱ tatiyo puggalo āhuneyyo pāhuneyyo dakkhiṇeyyo añjali-karaṇīyo anuttaraɱ puñña-k-khettaɱ lokassa.|| ||

■

5. Puna ca paraɱ bhikkhave, idh'ekacco puggalo dukkh-ā-nupassī viharati dukkha-sañña dukkha-paṭisaɱvedī satataɱ samitaɱ abbokiṇṇaɱ cetasā adhimuccamāno paññāyā-pariyogāhamāno.|| ||

So pañcannaɱ ora-m-bhāgiyānaɱ saŋyojanānaɱ parikkhayā upahacca-parinibbāyi hoti.|| ||

Ayaɱ catuttho puggalo āhuneyyo pāhuneyyo dakkhiṇeyyo añjali-karaṇīyo anuttaraɱ puñña-k-khettaɱ lokassa.|| ||

■

6. Puna ca paraɱ bhikkhave, idh'ekacco puggalo dukkh-ā-nupassī viharati dukkha-sañña dukkha-paṭisaɱvedī satataɱ samitaɱ abbokiṇṇaɱ cetasā adhimuccamāno paññāyā-pariyogāhamāno.|| ||

So pañcannaɱ ora-m-bhāgiyānaɱ saŋyojanānaɱ parikkhayā asankhāra-parinibbāyi hoti.|| ||

Ayaɱ pañcamo puggalo āhuneyyo pāhuneyyo dakkhiṇeyyo añjali-karaṇīyo anuttaraɱ puñña-k-khettaɱ lokassa.|| ||

■

7. Puna ca paraɱ bhikkhave, idh'ekacco puggalo dukkh-ā-nupassī viharati dukkha-sañña dukkha-paṭisaɱvedī satataɱ samitaɱ abbokiṇṇaɱ cetasā adhimuccamāno paññāyā-pariyogāhamāno.|| ||

So pañcannaɱ ora-m-bhāgiyānaɱ saŋyojanānaɱ parikkhayā sa-sankhāra-parinibbāyi hoti.|| ||

Ayaɱ chaṭṭhamo puggalo āhuneyyo pāhuneyyo dakkhiṇeyyo añjali-karaṇīyo anuttaraɱ puñña-k-khettaɱ lokassa.|| ||

■

8. Puna ca paraɱ bhikkhave, idh'ekacco puggalo dukkh-ā-nupassī viharati dukkha-sañña dukkha-paṭisaɱvedī satataɱ samitaɱ abbokiṇṇaɱ cetasā adhimuccamāno paññāyā-pariyogāhamāno.|| ||

So pañcannaɱ ora-m-bhāgiyānaɱ saŋyojanānaɱ parikkhayā uddhaɱ-soto hoti Akaniṭṭha-gāmi.|| ||

Ayaɱ bhikkhave, sattamo puggalo āhuneyyo, pāhuneyyo dakkhinayyo añjali-karaṇīyo anuttaraɱ puñña-k-khettaɱ lokassa.|| ||

Ime kho bhikkhave, satta puggalā āhuneyyā pāhuneyyā dakkhiṇeyyā añjali-karaṇīyā anuttaraɱ puñña-k-khettaɱ lokassā ti.|| ||
