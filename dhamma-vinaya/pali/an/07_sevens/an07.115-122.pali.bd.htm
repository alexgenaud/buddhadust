Aŋguttara Nikāya

Adapted from the 1995 edition of the digital version of the Sri Lanka Buddha Jayanti Tripitaka Series.

 

 

Sutta 115

Jhivhā Anicc-ā-nupassī Suttaɱ

[115.1][pts] EVAṂ ME SUTAṂ.|| ||

Ekaɱ samayaɱ Bhagavā Sāvatthiyaɱ viharati.|| ||

Satt'ime bhikkhave, puggalā āhuneyyā pāhuneyyā dakkhiṇeyyā añjali-karaṇīyā anuttaraɱ puñña-k-khettaɱ lokassa.|| ||

Katame satta?|| ||

2. Idha, bhikkhave, ekacco puggalo jivhāya anicc'ānupassī viharati,||
anicca-saññī anicca-paṭisaɱvedī satataɱ samitaɱ abbokiṇṇaɱ cetasā adhimuccamāno paññāya pariyogāhamāno.|| ||

So āsavānaɱ khayā anāsavaɱ ceto-vimuttiɱ paññā-vimuttiɱ diṭṭhe'va dhamme sayaɱ abhiññā sacchi-katvā upasampajja viharati.|| ||

Ayaɱ kho bhikkhave, paṭhamo puggalo āhuneyyā pāhuneyyā dakkhiṇeyyā añjali-karaṇīyā anuttaraɱ puñña-k-khettaɱ lokassa.|| ||

3. Puna ca paraɱ bhikkhave idh'ekacco puggalo jivhāya anuccānupassī viharati,||
aniccasaññi anicca-paṭisaɱvedī satataɱ samitaɱ abbokiṇṇaɱ cetasā adhimuccamāno paññāya pariyogāhamāno.|| ||

Tassa apubbaɱ acarimaɱ āsava-pariyādānañ ca hoti jivitapariyādānañ ca.|| ||

Ayaɱ bhikkhave, dutiyo puggalo āhuneyyo pāhuneyyo dakkhiṇeyyo añjali-karaṇīyyo anuttaraɱ puñña-k-khettaɱ lokassa.|| ||

4. Puna ca paraɱ bhikkhave, idh'ekacco puggalo jivhāya anicc'ānupassī viharati aniccasaññi anicca-paṭisaɱvedī satataɱ samitaɱ abbokkiṇnaɱ cetasā adimuccamāno paññāya pariyogāhamāno.|| ||

So pañcannaɱ ora-m-bhāgiyānaɱ saŋyojanānaɱ parikkhayā antarā-parinibbāyī hoti.|| ||

Ayaɱ bhikkhave, tatiyo puggalo āhuneyyo pāhuneyyo dakkhiṇeyyo añjali-karaṇīyyo anuttaraɱ puñña-k-khettaɱ lokassa.|| ||

5. Puna ca paraɱ bhikkhave, idh'ekacco puggalo jivhāya anicc'ānupassī viharati aniccasaññi anicca-paṭisaɱvedī satataɱ samitaɱ abbokkiṇnaɱ cetasā adimuccamāno paññāya pariyogāhamāno.|| ||

So pañcannaɱ ora-m-bhāgiyānaɱ saŋyojanānaɱ parikkhayā upahacca-parinibbāyī hoti.|| ||

Ayaɱbhikkhave, catuttho puggalo āhuneyyo pāhuneyyo dakkhiṇeyyo añjali-karaṇīyyoanuttaraɱ puñña-k-khettaɱ lokassa.|| ||

6. Puna ca paraɱ bhikkhave, idh'ekacco puggalo jivhāya anicc'ānupassī viharati aniccasaññi anicca-paṭisaɱvedī satataɱ samitaɱ abbokkiṇnaɱ cetasā adimuccamāno paññāya pariyogāhamāno.|| ||

So pañcannaɱ ora-m-bhāgiyānaɱ saŋyojanānaɱ parikkhayā asankhāra-parinibbāyī hoti.|| ||

Ayaɱ bhikkhave, pañcamo puggalo āhuneyyo pāhuneyyo dakkhiṇeyyo aññalikaraṇīyyo anuttaraɱ puñña-k-khettaɱ lokassa.|| ||

7. Puna ca paraɱ bhikkhave, idh'ekacco puggalo jivhāya anicc'ānupassī viharati aniccasaññi anicca-paṭisaɱvedī satataɱ samitaɱ abbokkiṇnaɱ cetasā adimuccamāno paññāya pariyogāhamāno.|| ||

So pañcannaɱ ora-m-bhāgiyānaɱ saŋyojanānaɱ parikkhayā sa-sankhāra parinibbāyī hoti.|| ||

Ayaɱ bhikkhave, chaṭṭho puggalo āhuneyyo pāhuneyyo dakkhiṇeyyo añjali-karaṇiyyo anuttaraɱ puñña-k-khettaɱ lokassa.|| ||

8. Puna ca paraɱ bhikkhave, idh'ekacco puggalo jivhāya anicc'ānupassī viharati aniccasaññi anicca-paṭisaɱvedī satataɱ samitaɱ abbokkiṇnaɱ cetasā adimuccamāno paññāya pariyogāhamāno.|| ||

So pañcannaɱ ora-m-bhāgiyānaɱ saŋyojanānaɱ parikkhayā uddhaɱ-soto hoti Akaniṭṭha-gāmī.|| ||

Ayaɱ bhikkhave, sattamo puggalo āhuneyyo pāhuneyyo dakkhiṇeyyo añjali-karaṇiyyo anuttaraɱ puñña-k-khettaɱ lokassa.|| ||

Ime kho bhikkhave, satta puggalā āhuneyyā pāhuneyyā dakkhiṇeyyā añjali-karaṇīyā anuttaraɱ puñña-k-khettaɱ lokassāti.|| ||
