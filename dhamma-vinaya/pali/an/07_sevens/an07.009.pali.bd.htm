Aṅguttara Nikāya
Sattaka Nipāta

Sutta 9

Saṅyojana-p-Pahāna Suttaɱ

Adapted from the 1995 edition of the digital version of the Sri Lanka Buddha Jayanti Tripitaka Series.

 

[7]

[1][pts][upal] EVAṂ ME SUTAṂ.|| ||

Ekaɱ samayaɱ Bhagavā Sāvatthiyaɱ viharati.|| ||

Tatra kho Bhagavā bhikkhū āmantesi:||
Bhikkhavo ti.||
Bhadante ti te bhikkhū Bhagavato paccassosuɱ.||
Bhagavā etad avoca:|| ||

Sattannaɱ bhikkhave, saɱyojanānaɱ pahānāya samucchedāya Brahma-cariyaɱ vussati.|| ||

 

§

 

Katamesaɱ sattannaɱ?|| ||

[8] Anunaya-saɱyojanassa pahānāya samucchedāya Brahma-cariyaɱ vussati.|| ||

■

Paṭigha-saɱyojanassa pahānāya samucchedāya Brahma-cariyaɱ vussati.|| ||

■

Diṭṭhi-saɱyojanassa pahānāya samucchdoya Brahma-cariyaɱ sati.|| ||

■

Vicikicchā-saɱyojanassa pahānāya samucchedāya Brahma-cariyaɱ vussati.|| ||

■

Māna-saɱyojanassa pahānāya samucchedāya Brahma-cariyaɱ vussati.|| ||

■

Bhava-rāga-saɱyojanassa pahānāya samucchedāya Brahma-cariyaɱ vussati.|| ||

■

Avijjā-saɱyojanassa pahānāya samucchdoya Brahma-cariyaɱ vussati.|| ||

Imesaɱ kho bhikkhave, sattannaɱ saɱyojanānaɱ pahānāya samucchedāya Brahma-cariyaɱ vussati.|| ||

 

§

 

Yato ca kho bhikkhave bhikkhuno anunaya-saɱyojanaɱ pahīnaɱ hoti||
ucchinna-mūlaɱ tāl-ā-vatthu-kataɱ||
anabhāva-kataɱ āyatiɱ anuppāda-dhammaɱ;|| ||

■

yato ca kho bhikkhave bhikkhuno paṭigha-saɱyojanaɱ pahīnaɱ hoti||
ucchinna-mūlaɱ tāl-ā-vatthu-kataɱ||
anabhāva-kataɱ āyatiɱ anuppādhammaɱ;|| ||

■

yato ca kho bhikkhave bhikkhuno diṭṭhi-saɱyojanaɱ pahīnaɱ hoti||
ucchinna-mūlaɱ tāl-ā-vatthu-kataɱ||
anabhāva-kataɱ āyatiɱ anuppāda dhammaɱ;|| ||

■

yato ca kho bhikkhave bhikkhuno vicikicchā-saɱyojanaɱ pahīnaɱ hoti||
ucchinna-mūlaɱ tāl-ā-vatthu-kataɱ||
anabhāva-kataɱ āyatiɱ anuppāda-dhammaɱ;|| ||

■

yato ca kho bhikkhave bhikkhuno māna-saɱyojanaɱ pahīnaɱ hoti
ucchinna-mūlaɱ tāl-ā-vatthu-kataɱ||
anabhāva-kataɱ āyatiɱ anuppāda-dhammaɱ;|| ||

■

yato ca kho bhikkhave bhikkhuno Bhava-rāga-saɱyojanaɱ pahīnaɱ hoti
ucchinna-mūlaɱ tāl-ā-vatthu-kataɱ||
anabhāva-kataɱ āyatiɱ anuppāda-dhammaɱ;|| ||

■

yato ca kho bhikkhave bhikkhuno avijjā-saɱyojanaɱ pahīnaɱ hoti||
ucchinna-mūlaɱ tāl-ā-vatthu-kataɱ||
anabhāva-kataɱ āyati anuppāda-dhammaɱ,|| ||

■

ayaɱ vuccati bhikkhave,||
bhikkhu acchecchi taṇhaɱ,||
vāvattayi saɱyojanaɱ,||
sammā mānābhi samayā antam akāsi dukkhassā ti.|| ||
