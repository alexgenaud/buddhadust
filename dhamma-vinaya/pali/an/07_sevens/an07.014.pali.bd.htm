Aŋguttara Nikāya
Sattaka Nipāta

Sutta 14

Āhuneyya-Puggala Suttaɱ

Adapted from the 1995 edition of the digital version of the Sri Lanka Buddha Jayanti Tripitaka Series.

 

[10]

[1][pts] EVAṂ ME SUTAṂ.|| ||

Ekaɱ samayaɱ Bhagavā Sāvatthiyaɱ viharati.|| ||

Tatra kho Bhagavā bhikkhū āmantesi:|| ||

Bhikkhavo ti.|| ||

Bhadante ti te bhikkhū Bhagavato paccassosuɱ.|| ||

Bhagavā etad avoca:|| ||

Satt'ime bhikkhave puggalā āhuneyyā,||
pāhuneyyā,||
dakkhiṇeyyā,||
añjali-karaṇiyā,||
anuttaraɱ puñña-k-khettaɱ lokassa.|| ||

Katame satta?|| ||

Ubhato bhāga-vimutto,||
paññā-vimutto,||
kāya-sakkhi,||
diṭṭha-p-patto,||
saddhā-vimutto,||
dhamm'ānusārī,||
saddh'ānusārī.|| ||

[11] Ime kho bhikkhave satta puggalā āhuneyyā,||
pāhuneyyā,||
dakkhiṇeyyā,||
añjali-karaṇīyā,||
anuttaraɱ puñña-k-khettaɱ lokassā ti.|| ||
