Aŋguttara Nikāya
Catukka Nipāta
XII: Kesi Vagga

Sutta 118

Saɱvejanīya Suttaɱ

Adapted from the 1995 edition of the digital version of the Sri Lanka Buddha Jayanti Tripitaka Series.

 

[120]

[1][pts] Evaɱ me sutaɱ:|| ||

Ekaɱ samayaɱ Bhagavā Sāvatthiyaɱ viharati.|| ||

Tatra kho Bhagavā bhikkhū āmantesi:|| ||

"Bhikkhavo" ti.|| ||

"Bhadante" ti te bhikkhū Bhagavato paccassosuɱ.|| ||

Bhagavā etad avoca:|| ||

2. "Cattār'imāni bhikkhave saddhassa kula-puttassa dassanīyāni saɱvejanīyāni ṭhānāni.|| ||

Katamāni cattāri?|| ||

Idha Tathāgato jāto ti bhikkhave saddhassa kula-puttassa dassanīyaɱ saɱvejanīyaɱ ṭhānaɱ.|| ||

Idha Tathāgato anuttaraɱ sammā-sambodhiɱ abhisambuddho ti bhikkhave saddhassa kula-puttassa dassanīyaɱ saɱvejanīyaɱ ṭhānaɱ.|| ||

Idha Tathāgato anuttaraɱ dhammacatkaɱ pavattesīti bhikkhave saddhassa kula-puttassa dassanīyaɱ saɱvejanīyaɱ ṭhānaɱ.|| ||

Idha Tathāgato anupādisesāya Nibbānadhātuyā parinibbuto ti bhikkhave saddhassa kula-puttassa dassanīyaɱ saɱvejanīyaɱ ṭhānaɱ.|| ||

[121] Imāni kho bhikkhave cattāri saddhassa kula-puttassa dassanīyāni saɱvejanīyāni ṭhānānī" ti.|| ||
