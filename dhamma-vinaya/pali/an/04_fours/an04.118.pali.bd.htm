Aṅguttara Nikāya
Catukka Nipāta
XII: Kesi Vagga

Sutta 118

Saṃvejanīya Suttaṃ

Adapted from the 1995 edition of the digital version of the Sri Lanka Buddha Jayanti Tripitaka Series.

 

[120]

[1][pts] Evaṃ me sutaṃ:|| ||

Ekaṃ samayaṃ Bhagavā Sāvatthiyaṃ viharati.|| ||

Tatra kho Bhagavā bhikkhū āmantesi:|| ||

"Bhikkhavo" ti.|| ||

"Bhadante" ti te bhikkhū Bhagavato paccassosuṃ.|| ||

Bhagavā etad avoca:|| ||

2. "Cattār'imāni bhikkhave saddhassa kula-puttassa dassanīyāni saṃvejanīyāni ṭhānāni.|| ||

Katamāni cattāri?|| ||

Idha Tathāgato jāto ti bhikkhave saddhassa kula-puttassa dassanīyaṃ saṃvejanīyaṃ ṭhānaṃ.|| ||

Idha Tathāgato anuttaraṃ sammā-sambodhiṃ abhisambuddhoti bhikkhave saddhassa kula-puttassa dassanīyaṃ saṃvejanīyaṃ ṭhānaṃ.|| ||

Idha Tathāgato anuttaraṃ dhammacatkaṃ pavattesīti bhikkhave saddhassa kula-puttassa dassanīyaṃ saṃvejanīyaṃ ṭhānaṃ.|| ||

Idha Tathāgato anupādisesāya Nibbānadhātuyā parinibbuto ti bhikkhave saddhassa kula-puttassa dassanīyaṃ saṃvejanīyaṃ ṭhānaṃ.|| ||

[121] Imāni kho bhikkhave cattāri saddhassa kula-puttassa dassanīyāni saṃvejanīyāni ṭhānānī" ti.|| ||
