Aŋguttara Nikāya
Catukka Nipāta
15. Ābhā Vagga

Sutta 142

Pabhā Suttaɱ

Adapted from the 1995 edition of the digital version of the Sri Lanka Buddha Jayanti Tripitaka Series.

 

[139]

[1][pts] Cattāro'me bhikkhave pabhā.|| ||

Katame cattāro?|| ||

Candappabhā,||
suriyappabhā,||
aggippabhā,||
paññāpabhā.|| ||

Ime kho bhikkhave cattāro pabhā.|| ||

Etad aggaɱ bhikkhave imesaɱ catunnaɱ yad idaɱ paññāpabhā ti.|| ||
