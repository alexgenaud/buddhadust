Aṅguttara Nikāya
4. Catukka Nipāta
VI. Puññābhisanda Vagga

Sutta 56

Dutiya Samajīvī Suttaɱ

Adapted from the 1995 edition of the digital version of the Sri Lanka Buddha Jayanti Tripitaka Series.

 

[62]

[1][pts][bodh] Evaɱ me sutaɱ:|| ||

Ekaɱ samayaɱ Bhagavā bhikkhū āmantesi:|| ||

"Bhikkhavo" ti.|| ||

"Bhadante" ti te bhikkhū Bhagavato paccassosuɱ.|| ||

Bhagavā etad avoca:|| ||

2. Ākankheyyuɱ ce gahapatayo ubho jānipatayo diṭṭhe'va dhamme añña-maññaɱ passituɱ abhisamparāyañ ca añña-maññaɱ passituɱ,||
ubho ca assu sama-saddhā||
sama-sīlā||
sama-cāgā||
sama-paññā.

Te diṭṭhe'va dhamme añña-maññaɱ passanti abhisamparāyañ ca añña-maññaɱ passantī ti.|| ||

 

Ubho saddhā vadaññū ca saññatā dhammajīvino,||
Te honti jānipatayo añña-maññaɱ piyaɱvadā.|| ||

Atthā sampacurā honti vāsatthaɱ upajāyati,||
Amittā dummanā honti ubhinnaɱ samasīlinaɱ.|| ||

Idha dhammaɱ caritvāna sama-sīla-b-batā ubho,||
Nandino deva-lokasmiɱ modanti kāma-kāmino ti.
