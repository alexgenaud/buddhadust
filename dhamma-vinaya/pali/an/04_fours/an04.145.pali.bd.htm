Aŋguttara Nikāya
Catukka Nipāta
15. Ābhā Vagga

Sutta 145

Pajjotā Suttaɱ

Adapted from the 1995 edition of the digital version of the Sri Lanka Buddha Jayanti Tripitaka Series.

 

[140]

[1][pts] Cattāro'me bhikkhave pajjotā.|| ||

Katame cattāro?|| ||

Canda-pajjoto,||
suriya-pajjoto,||
aggi-pajjoto,||
paññā-pajjoto.|| ||

Ime kho bhikkhave cattāro pajjotā.|| ||

Etad aggaɱ bhikkhave imesaɱ catunnaɱ yad idaɱ paññā-pajjoto ti.|| ||
