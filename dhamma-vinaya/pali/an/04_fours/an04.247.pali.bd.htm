Aŋguttara Nikāya
Catukka Nipāta
XXV: Āppatti-bhaya Vagga

Sutta 247

Anariya Vohāra Suttaɱ

Adapted from the 1995 edition of the digital version of the Sri Lanka Buddha Jayanti Tripitaka Series.

 

[248]

[1][pts] Evaɱ me sutaɱ:|| ||

Ekaɱ samayaɱ Bhagavā bhikkhū āmantesi:|| ||

"Bhikkhavo" ti.|| ||

"Bhadante" ti te bhikkhū Bhagavato paccassosuɱ.|| ||

Bhagavā etad avoca:|| ||

Cattāro'me bhikkhave anariya-vohārā.|| ||

Katame cattāro?|| ||

Adiṭṭhe diṭṭha-vāditā,||
asute duta-vāditā,||
amute muta-vāditā,||
aviññāte viññāta-vāditā.|| ||

Ime kho bhikkhave cattāro anariya-vohārāti.|| ||
