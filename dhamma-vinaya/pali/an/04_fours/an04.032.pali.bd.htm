Aŋguttara Nikāya
4. Catukka Nipāta
IV. Cakka Vagga

Sutta 32

Saŋgahavatthu Suttaɱ

Adapted from the 1995 edition of the digital version of the Sri Lanka Buddha Jayanti Tripitaka Series.

 

[32]

[1][pts][than][bodh] Evaɱ me sutaɱ:|| ||

Ekaɱ samayaɱ Bhagavā bhikkhū āmantesi:|| ||

"Bhikkhavo" ti.|| ||

"Bhadante" ti te bhikkhū Bhagavato paccassosuɱ.|| ||

Bhagavā etad avoca:|| ||

2. Cattār'imāni bhikkhave sangaha-vatthūni.|| ||

Katamāni cattāri?|| ||

Dānaɱ,||
peyyavajjaɱ,||
attha-cariyā,||
samān'attatā.|| ||

Imāni kho bhikkhave cattāri saŋgahavatthūnīti.|| ||

 

Dānaɱ ca peyyavajjañ ca attha-cariyā ca yā idha,||
Samānattatā dhammesu tattha tattha yathārahaɱ,||
Ete kho saŋgahā loke rathass'āṇī va yāyato.|| ||

Ete ca saŋgahā nāssu na mātā puttakāraṇā,||
Labhetha mānaɱ pūjaɱ vā pitā vā puttakāraṇā.|| ||

Yasmā ca saŋgahā ete samavekkhanti paṇḍitā,||
Tasmā mahattaɱ papponti pāsaɱsā ca bhavanti te ti.|| ||
