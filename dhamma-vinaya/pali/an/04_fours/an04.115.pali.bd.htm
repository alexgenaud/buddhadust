Aŋguttara Nikāya
Catukka Nipāta
XII: Kesi Vagga

Sutta 115

Ṭhāna Suttaɱ

Adapted from the 1995 edition of the digital version of the Sri Lanka Buddha Jayanti Tripitaka Series.

 

[118]

[1][pts][than] Evaɱ me sutaɱ:|| ||

Ekaɱ samayaɱ Bhagavā Sāvatthiyaɱ viharati.|| ||

Tatra kho Bhagavā bhikkhū āmantesi:|| ||

"Bhikkhavo" ti.|| ||

"Bhadante" ti te bhikkhū Bhagavato paccassosuɱ.|| ||

Bhagavā etad avoca:|| ||

2. Cattār'imāni bhikkhave ṭhānāni.|| ||

Katamāni cattāri?|| ||

Atthi bhikkhave ṭhānaɱ amanāpaɱ kātuɱ,||
tañ ca kayiramānaɱ anatthāya saŋvattati.|| ||

Atthi bhikkhave ṭhānaɱ amanāpaɱ kātuɱ,||
tañ ca kayiramānaɱ atthāya saŋvattati.|| ||

Atthi bhikkhave ṭhānaɱ manāpaɱ kātuɱ,||
tañ ca kayiramānaɱ anatthāya saŋvattati.|| ||

Atthi bhikkhave ṭhānaɱ manāpaɱ kātuɱ,||
tañ ca kayiramānaɱ atthāya saŋvattati.|| ||

Tatra, bhikkhave, yam idaɱ ṭhānaɱ amanāpaɱ kātuɱ,||
tañ ca kayiramānaɱ anatthāya saŋvattati,||
idaɱ bhikkhave ṭhānaɱ ubhayen'eva na kattabbaɱ maññati.|| ||

Yam p'idaɱ ṭhānaɱ amanāpaɱ kātuɱ,||
iminā pi taɱ na kattabbaɱ maññati.|| ||

Yam p'idaɱ ṭhānaɱ kayiramānaɱ anatthāya saŋvattati,||
iminā pi na kattabbaɱ maññati.|| ||

Idaɱ, bhikkhave, ṭhānaɱ ubhayen'eva na kattabbaɱ maññati.|| ||

Tatra, bhikkhave, yam idaɱ ṭhānaɱ amanāpaɱ kātuɱ,||
tañ ca kayiramānaɱ atthāya saŋvattati,||
imasmiɱ bhikkhave ṭhāne bālo ca paṇḍito ca veditabbo puriSatthāme purisa-viriye purisa-parakkame.|| ||

Na bhikkhave bālo iti paṭisacikkhati,||
'kiñ cāpi kho idaɱ ṭhānaɱ amanāpaɱ kātuɱ,||
atha carah'idaɱ ṭhānaɱ kayiramānaɱ atthāya saŋvattatī' ti.|| ||

So taɱ ṭhānaɱ na karoti.|| ||

Tassa taɱ ṭhānaɱ akayiramānaɱ anatthāya saŋvattati.|| ||

Paṇḍito ca kho bhikkhave iti paṭisañcikkhati:||
'kiñ cāpi kho idaɱ ṭhānaɱ amanāpaɱ kātuɱ,||
atha [119] carah'idaɱ ṭhānaɱ kayiramānaɱ atthāya saŋvattatī' ti.|| ||

So taɱ ṭhānaɱ karoti.|| ||

Tassa taɱ ṭhānaɱ kayiramānaɱ atthāya saŋvattatī ti.|| ||

Tatra, bhikkhave, yam idaɱ ṭhānaɱ manāpaɱ kātuɱ,||
tañ ca kayiramānaɱ anatthāya saŋvattati.|| ||

Imasmim pi bhikkhave ṭhāne bālo ca paṇḍito ca veditabbo puriSatthāme purisa-viriye purisa-parakkame.|| ||

Na bhikkhave bālo iti paṭisañcikkhati: 'kiñ cāpi kho idaɱ ṭhānaɱ manāpaɱ kātuɱ,||
atha carah'idaɱ ṭhānaɱ kayiramānaɱ anatthāya saŋvattatī' ti.|| ||

So taɱ ṭhānaɱ karoti.|| ||

Tassa taɱ ṭhānaɱ kayiramānaɱ anatthāya saŋvattati.|| ||

Paṇḍito ca kho bhikkhave iti paṭisaɱcikti:||
'kiñ cāpi kho idaɱ ṭhānaɱ manāpaɱ kātuɱ,||
atha carah'idaɱ ṭhānaɱ kayiramānaɱ anatthāya saŋvattatī' ti.|| ||

So taɱ ṭhānaɱ na karoti.|| ||

Tassa taɱ ṭhānaɱ akayiramānaɱ atthāya saŋvattati.|| ||

Tatra, bhikkhave, yam idaɱ ṭhānaɱ manāpaɱ kātuɱ,||
tañ ca kayiramānaɱ atthāya saŋvattati,||
idaɱ bhikkhave ṭhānaɱ ubhayen'eva kattabbaɱ maññati.|| ||

Yam p'idaɱ ṭhānaɱ manāpaɱ kātuɱ,||
iminā pi taɱ kattabbaɱ maññati.|| ||

Yam p'idaɱ ṭhānaɱ kayiramānaɱ atthāya saŋvattati,||
iminā pi taɱ kattabbaɱ maññati.|| ||

Idaɱ, bhikkhave, ṭhānaɱ ubhayen'eva kattabbaɱ maññati.|| ||

Imāni kho bhikkhave cattāri ṭhānānīti.|| ||
