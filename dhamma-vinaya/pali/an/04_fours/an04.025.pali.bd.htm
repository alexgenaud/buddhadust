Aŋguttara Nikāya
4. Catukka Nipāta
III. Uruvelā Vagga

Sutta 25

Brahma-cariya Suttaɱ

Adapted from the 1995 edition of the digital version of the Sri Lanka Buddha Jayanti Tripitaka Series.

 

[26]

[1][pts][bodh] Evaɱ me sutaɱ:|| ||

Ekaɱ samayaɱ Bhagavā Sāvatthiyaɱ viharati
Jetavane Anāthapiṇḍikassa ārāme.|| ||

Tatra kho Bhagavā bhikkhū āmantesi:|| ||

"Bhikkhavo" ti.|| ||

"Bhadante" ti te bhikkhū Bhagavato paccassosuɱ.|| ||

Bhagavā etad avoca:|| ||

2. Na-y-idaɱ bhikkhave Brahma-cariyaɱ vussati janakuhanatthaɱ,||
na janalapanatthaɱ,||
na lābha-sakkāra-silokā-nisaɱsatthaɱ,||
na iti vā dappamokkhā-nisaɱsatthaɱ,||
na 'iti maɱ jano jānātū' ti.|| ||

Atha kho idaɱ bhikkhave Brahma-cariyaɱ vussati saŋvaratthaɱ,||
pahāṇatthaɱ,||
virāgatthaɱ,||
nirodhatthan" ti.|| ||

 

Saɱvaratthaɱ pahāṇatthaɱ Brahma-cariyaɱ anītihaɱ,||
Adesayi so Bhagavā nibabān'ogadha-gāminaɱ.|| ||

Esa Maggo mahantehi anuyāto mahesihi,||
Ye ca taɱ paṭipajjanti yathā Buddhena desitaɱ,||
Dukkhass'antaɱ karissanti Satthu sāsanakārino ti.|| ||
