Aŋguttara Nikāya
Catukka Nipāta
XXV: Āppatti-bhaya Vagga

Sutta 246[ed1]

Paññāvuddhi Suttaɱ and Bahukāra Suttaɱ

Adapted from the 1995 edition of the digital version of the Sri Lanka Buddha Jayanti Tripitaka Series.

 

[245]

[1][pts] Evaɱ me sutaɱ:|| ||

Ekaɱ samayaɱ Bhagavā bhikkhū āmantesi:|| ||

"Bhikkhavo" ti.|| ||

"Bhadante" ti te bhikkhū Bhagavato paccassosuɱ.|| ||

Bhagavā etad avoca:|| ||

Cattāro'me bhikkhave dhammā paññā-vuddhiyā saŋvattantī.|| ||

Katame cattāro?|| ||

Sappurisa-saɱsevo,||
Sad'Dhamma-savaṇaɱ,||
yoniso mana-sikāro,||
dhamm-ā-nudhamma-paṭipatti.|| ||

Ime kho bhikkhave cattāro dhammā paññā-vuddhiyā saŋvattantī ti.

 

 

Cattāro'me bhikkhave dhammā manussa-bhutassa bahukārā honti.|| ||

Katame cattāro?|| ||

Sappurisa-saɱsevo,||
Sad'Dhamma-savaṇaɱ,||
yoniso mana-sikāro,||
dhamm-ā-nudhamma-paṭipatti.|| ||

[246] Ime kho bhikkhave cattāro dhammā manussa-bhutassa bahukārā hontī ti.|| ||

 

[ed1] The BJT Pali has this as two suttas.
