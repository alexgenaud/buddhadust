Aŋguttara Nikāya
Catukka Nipāta
15. Ābhā Vagga

Sutta 149

Vacī Sucarita Suttaɱ

Adapted from the 1995 edition of the digital version of the Sri Lanka Buddha Jayanti Tripitaka Series.

 

[141]

[1][pts] Evaɱ me sutaɱ:|| ||

Ekaɱ samayaɱ Bhagavā Sāvatthiyaɱ viharati.|| ||

Tatra kho Bhagavā bhikkhū āmantesi:|| ||

"Bhikkhavo" ti.|| ||

"Bhadante" ti te bhikkhū Bhagavato paccassosuɱ.|| ||

Bhagavā etad avoca:|| ||

2. "Cattār'imāni bhikkhave vacī-sucaritāni.|| ||

Katamāni cattāri?|| ||

Sacca-vācā,||
apisuna-vācā,||
saṇha-vācā,||
mantā-bhāsā.|| ||

Imāni kho bhikkhave cattāri vacī-sucaritānī" ti.|| ||
