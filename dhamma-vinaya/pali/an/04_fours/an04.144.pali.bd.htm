Obhāsa Suttaɱ

Adapted from the 1995 edition of the digital version of the Sri Lanka Buddha Jayanti Tripitaka Series.

 

 

[1][pts][than] Cattāro'me bhikkhave obhāsā.|| ||

Katame cattāro?|| ||

Cand'obhāso,||
suriy'obhāso,||
agg'obhāso,||
paññ'obhāso.|| ||

[140] Ime kho bhikkhave cattāro obhāsā.|| ||

Etad aggaɱ bhikkhave imesaɱ catunnaɱ yad idaɱ paññ'obhāso ti.|| ||
