Aŋguttara Nikāyo
IV. Catukka Nipāto
II. Cara Vagga

Sutta 17

Agati Suttaɱ

Adapted from the 1995 edition of the digital version of the Sri Lanka Buddha Jayanti Tripitaka Series.

 

[18]

[1][pts][bodh] Evaɱ me sutaɱ:|| ||

Ekaɱ samayaɱ Bhagavā Sāvatthiyaɱ viharati.|| ||

Tatra kho Bhagavā bhikkhū āmantesi:|| ||

"Bhikkhavo" ti.|| ||

"Bhadante" ti te bhikkhū Bhagavato paccassosuɱ.|| ||

Bhagavā etad avoca:|| ||

2. Cattār'imāni bhikkhave agati-gamanāni.|| ||

katamāni cattāri?|| ||

Chandāgatiɱ gacchati,||
dos-ā-gatiɱ gacchati,||
moh-ā-gatiɱ gacchati,||
bhayāgatiɱ gacchati.|| ||

Imāni kho bhikkhave cattāri agatigamanānī ti.|| ||

 

Chandā dosā bhayā mohā yo dhammaɱ ativattati,||
Nihīyati tassa yaso kāḷapakkheva candimā ti.|| ||
