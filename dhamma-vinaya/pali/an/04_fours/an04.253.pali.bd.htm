Aŋguttara Nikāya
Catukka Nipāta
XXVI: Abhiññā Vagga

Sutta 253

Saŋgaha-Vatthu Suttaɱ

Adapted from the 1995 edition of the digital version of the Sri Lanka Buddha Jayanti Tripitaka Series.

 

[248]

[1][pts] Evaɱ me sutaɱ:|| ||

Ekaɱ samayaɱ Bhagavā Sāvatthiyaɱ viharati.|| ||

Tatra kho Bhagavā bhikkhū āmantesi:|| ||

"Bhikkhavo" ti.|| ||

"Bhadante" ti te bhikkhū Bhagavato paccassosuɱ.|| ||

Bhagavā etad avoca:|| ||

"Cattār'imāni bhikkhave saŋgaha-vatthūni.|| ||

Katamāni cattāri?|| ||

Dānaɱ,||
peyya-vajjaɱ,||
attha-cariyā,||
samān'attatā.|| ||

Imāni kho bhikkhave cattāri saŋgaha-vatthūnī" ti.|| ||
