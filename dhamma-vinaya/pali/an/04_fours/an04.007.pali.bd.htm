Aŋguttara Nikāya
IV. Catukka Nipāta
I. Bhaṇḍagāma Vagga

Sutta 7

Sobhenti Suttaɱ

Adapted from the 1995 edition of the digital version of the Sri Lanka Buddha Jayanti Tripitaka Series.

 

[8]

[1] [pts][bodh] Evaɱ me sutaɱ:|| ||

Ekaɱ samayaɱ Bhagavā Sāvatthiyaɱ viharati.|| ||

Tatra kho Bhagavā bhikkhū āmantesi:|| ||

"Bhikkhavo" ti.|| ||

"Bhadante" ti te bhikkhū Bhagavato paccassosuɱ.|| ||

Bhagavā etad avoca:|| ||

2. Cattāro'me bhikkhave puggalā viyattā vinītā visāradā bahu-s-sutā dhamma-dharā dhamm-ā-nudhamma-paṭipannā Saŋghaɱ sobhenti.|| ||

Katame cattāro?|| ||

Bhikkhu bhikkhave viyatto vinīto visārado bahu-s-suto dhamma-dharo dhamm-ā-nudhamma-paṭipanno Saŋghaɱ sobheti.|| ||

Bhikkhunī bhikkhave viyattā vinītā visāradā bahu-s-sutā dhamma-dharā dhamm-ā-nudhamma-paṭipannā Saŋghaɱ sobheti.|| ||

Upāsako bhikkhave viyatto vinīto visārado bahu-s-suto dhamma-dharo dhamm-ā-nudhamma-paṭipanno Saŋghaɱ sobheti.|| ||

Upāsikā bhikkhave viyattā vinītā visāradā bahu-s-sutā dhamma-dharā dhamm-ā-nudhamma-paṭipannā Saŋghaɱ sobheti.|| ||

Ime kho bhikkhave cattāro viyattā vinītā visāradā bahu-s-sutā dhamama-dharā dhamm-ā-nudhamma-paṭipannā Saŋghaɱ sobhentī ti.|| ||

Yo hoti viyatto ca visārado ca||
Bahu-s-suto dhamma-dharo ca hoti,||
Dhammassa hoti anudhamma-cārī||
Sa tādiso vuccati Saŋgha-sobhano.||
Bhikkhu ca sīla-sampanno bhikkhunī ca bahu-s-sutā,||
Upāsako ca yo saddho yā ca saddhā upāsikā,||
Ete kho Saŋghaɱ sobhenti ete hi Saŋgha-sobhanā ti.|| ||
