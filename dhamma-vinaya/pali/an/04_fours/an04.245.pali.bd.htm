Aŋguttara Nikāya
Catukka Nipāta
XXV: Āppatti-bhaya Vagga

Sutta 245

Thupāraha Suttaɱ

Adapted from the 1995 edition of the digital version of the Sri Lanka Buddha Jayanti Tripitaka Series.

 

[245]

[1][pts] Evaɱ me sutaɱ:|| ||

Ekaɱ samayaɱ Bhagavā bhikkhū āmantesi:|| ||

"Bhikkhavo" ti.|| ||

"Bhadante" ti te bhikkhū Bhagavato paccassosuɱ.|| ||

Bhagavā etad avoca:|| ||

Cattāro'me bhikkhave thūpa-rahā.|| ||

Katame cattāro?|| ||

Tathāgato arahaɱ Sammā-Sam-Buddho thūpa-raho,||
Pacceka-Buddho thūpa-raho,||
Tathāgata-sāvako thūpa-raho,||
rājā cakka-vattī thūpa-raho.|| ||

Ime kho bhikkhave cattāro thūpa-rahāti.|| ||
