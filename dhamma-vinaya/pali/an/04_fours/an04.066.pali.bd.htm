Aŋguttara Nikāya
4. Catukka Nipāta
VII. Pattakamma Vagga

Sutta 66

Sarāga (Puggala) Suttaɱ

Adapted from the 1995 edition of the digital version of the Sri Lanka Buddha Jayanti Tripitaka Series.

 

 

[1][pts] Evaɱ me sutaɱ:|| ||

Ekaɱ samayaɱ Bhagavā bhikkhū āmantesi:|| ||

"Bhikkhavo" ti.|| ||

"Bhadante" ti te bhikkhū Bhagavato paccassosuɱ.|| ||

Bhagavā etad avoca:|| ||

2. Cattāro'me bhikkhave puggalā santo saŋvijj'amānā lokasmiɱ.|| ||

Katame cattāro?|| ||

Sarāgo,||
sadoso,||
samoho,||
samāno.|| ||

Ime kho bhikkhave cattāro puggalā santo saŋvijj'amānā lokasmin ti.|| ||

[72] Sārattā rajanīyesu piya-rūpābhinandino,||
Mohena adhamā sattā baddhā vaḍḍhenti bandhanaɱ.|| ||

Rāgajañ ca dosa-jañ ca moha-jañ cāpi viddasu,||
Karonti akusalaɱ kammaɱ sa-vighātaɱ dukhudayaɱ.|| ||

Avijjānivutā posā andhabhūtā acakkhukā,||
Yathā dhammā tathā santā tass'evan ti na maññare ti.|| ||
