Aŋguttara Nikāya
Catukka Nipāta
XVII: Paṭipadā Vagga

Sutta 167

Moggallāna Paṭipadā Suttaɱ

Adapted from the 1995 edition of the digital version of the Sri Lanka Buddha Jayanti Tripitaka Series.

 

 

[1][pts] EVAṂ ME SUTAṂ.|| ||

Atha kho āyasmā Sāriputto yen'āyasmā Mahā Moggallāno ten'upasankami.|| ||

Upasaŋkamitvā āyasmatā Mahā Moggallānena saddhiɱ sammodi.|| ||

Sammodanīyaɱ kathaɱ sārāṇīyaɱ vīti-sāretvā eka-m-antaɱ nisidi.|| ||

Eka-m-antaɱ nisinno kho āyasmā Sāriputto āyasmantaɱ Mahā Moglagallānaɱ etad avoca:|| ||

"Catasso imā āvuso Moggallāna paṭipadā.|| ||

Katamā catasso?|| ||

Dukkhā-paṭipadā dandhābhiññā,||
dukkhā paṭipadā khippābhiññā,||
sukhā paṭipadā dandhābhiññā,||
sukhā paṭipadā khippābhiññā.|| ||

Imā kho āvuso catasso paṭipadā.|| ||

Imāsaɱ āvuso catassannaɱ paṭipadānaɱ katamaɱ te paṭipadaɱ āgamma āsavehi cittaɱ vimuttan" ti?|| ||

 

§

 

[155] "Catasso imā āvuso Sāriputta paṭipadā.|| ||

Katamā catasso?|| ||

Dukkhā-paṭipadā dandhābhiññā,||
dukkhā paṭipadā khippābhiññā,||
sukhā paṭipadā dandhābhiññā,||
sukhā paṭipadā khippābhiññā.|| ||

Imā kho āvuso catasso paṭipadā.|| ||

Imāsaɱ āvuso catassannaɱ paṭipadānaɱ yā'yaɱ paṭipadā dukkhā khippābhiññā,||
imaɱ me paṭipadaɱ āgamma anupādāya āsavehi cittaɱ vimuttan" ti.|| ||
