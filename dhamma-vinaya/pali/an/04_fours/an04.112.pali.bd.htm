Aŋguttara Nikāya
Catukka Nipāta
XII: Kesi Vagga

Sutta 112

Assājānīya-java Suttaɱ

Adapted from the 1995 edition of the digital version of the Sri Lanka Buddha Jayanti Tripitaka Series.

 

[113]

[1][pts] Evaɱ me sutaɱ:|| ||

Ekaɱ samayaɱ Bhagavā Sāvatthiyaɱ viharati.|| ||

Tatra kho Bhagavā bhikkhū āmantesi:|| ||

"Bhikkhavo" ti.|| ||

"Bhadante" ti te bhikkhū Bhagavato paccassosuɱ.|| ||

Bhagavā etad avoca:|| ||

2. "Catuhi bhikkhave aŋgehi samannāgato rañño bhadro ass-ā-jānīyo rājā-raho hoti rāja bhoggo||
rañño aŋganatv'eva saŋkhaɱ gacchati.|| ||

Katamehi catūhi?|| ||

Ajjavena,||
javena,||
khantiyā,||
soraccena.|| ||

Imehi kho bhikkhave catūhi aŋgehi samannāgato rañño bhadro ass-ā-jānīyo rājā-raho hoti rāja bhoggo||
rañño aŋganatv'eva saŋkhaɱ gacchati.|| ||

2. Evam eva kho bhikkhave catūhi dhammehi samannāgato bhikkhu āhuneyyo hoti pāhuṇeyyo,||
dakkhiṇeyyo,||
añjali-karaṇīyo anuttaraɱ puññakkhetkaɱ lokassa.|| ||

Katamehi catūhi?|| ||

Ajjavena||
javena||
khantiyā||
soraccena.|| ||

Imehi kho bhikkhave catūhi dhammehi samannāgato [114] bhikkhu āhuneyyo hoti pāhuṇeyyo,||
dakkhiṇeyyo,||
añjali-karaṇīyo||
anuttaraɱ puñña-k-khettaɱ lokassā" ti.|| ||
