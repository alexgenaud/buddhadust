Aŋguttara Nikāya
Catukka Nipāta
X: Asura Vagga

Sutta 98

Attahita Suttaɱ

Adapted from the 1995 edition of the digital version of the Sri Lanka Buddha Jayanti Tripitaka Series.

 

[98]

[1][pts] Cattāro'me bhikkhave puggalā santo saŋvijj'amānā lokasmiɱ.|| ||

Katame cattāro?|| ||

Attahitāya paṭipanno no parahitāya.|| ||

Parahitāya paṭipanno no attahitāya.

Attahitāya ca paṭipanno parahitāya ca.|| ||

N'evattahitāya paṭipanno no parahitāya.|| ||

Ime kho bhikkhave cattāro puggalā santo saŋvijj'amānā lokasmin ti.|| ||
