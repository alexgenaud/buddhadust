Aṇguttara Nikāya
Catukka Nipāta
XXV: Āppatti-bhaya Vagga

Sutta 249

Anariya Vohāra Suttaṃ

Adapted from the 1995 edition of the digital version of the Sri Lanka Buddha Jayanti Tripitaka Series.

 

[246]

[1][pts] Evaṃ me sutaṃ:|| ||

Ekaṃ samayaṃ Bhagavā bhikkhū āmantesi:|| ||

"Bhikkhavo" ti.|| ||

"Bhadante" ti te bhikkhū Bhagavato paccassosuṃ.|| ||

Bhagavā etad avoca:|| ||

Cattāro'me bhikkhave anariya-vohārā.|| ||

Katame cattāro?|| ||

Diṭṭhe adiṭṭha-vāditā,||
sute asuta-vāditā,||
mute amuta-vāditā,||
viññāte aviññāta-vāditā.|| ||

Ime kho bhikkhave cattāro anariya-vohārā ti.|| ||
