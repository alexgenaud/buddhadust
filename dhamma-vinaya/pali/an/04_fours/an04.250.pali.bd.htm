Aŋguttara Nikāya
Catukka Nipāta
XXV: Āppatti-bhaya Vagga

Sutta 250

Ariya Vohāra Suttaɱ

Adapted from the 1995 edition of the digital version of the Sri Lanka Buddha Jayanti Tripitaka Series.

 

[246]

[1][pts] Evaɱ me sutaɱ:|| ||

Ekaɱ samayaɱ Bhagavā bhikkhū āmantesi:|| ||

"Bhikkhavo" ti.|| ||

"Bhadante" ti te bhikkhū Bhagavato paccassosuɱ.|| ||

Bhagavā etad avoca:|| ||

Cattāro'me bhikkhave ariya-vohārā.|| ||

Katame cattāro?|| ||

Diṭṭhe diṭṭha-vāditā,||
sute suta-vāditā,||
mute muta-vāditā,||
viññāte viññāta-vāditā.|| ||

Ime kho bhikkhave cattāro ariya-vohārā ti.|| ||
