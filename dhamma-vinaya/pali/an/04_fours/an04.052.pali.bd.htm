Aŋguttara Nikāya
4. Catukka Nipāta
VI. Puññābhisanda Vagga

Sutta 52

Dutiya Puññābhisanda Suttaɱ

Adapted from the 1995 edition of the digital version of the Sri Lanka Buddha Jayanti Tripitaka Series.

 

[56]

[1][pts][bodh] Evaɱ me sutaɱ:|| ||

Ekaɱ samayaɱ Bhagavā Sāvatthiyaɱ viharati
Jetavane Anāthapiṇḍikassa ārāme.|| ||

Tatra kho Bhagavā bhikkhū āmantesi:|| ||

"Bhikkhavo" ti.|| ||

"Bhadante" ti te bhikkhū Bhagavato paccassosuɱ.|| ||

2. Cattāro'me bhikkhave puññ-ā-bhisandā kusal-ā-bhisandā sukhass'āhārā sovaggikā sukha-vipākā sagga-saŋvatta-nikā iṭṭhāya kantāya manāpāya hitāya sukhāya saŋvattanti.|| ||

Katame cattāro?|| ||

Idha, bhikkhave, ariya-sāvako Buddhe avecca-p-pasādena samannāgato hoti:|| ||

Iti pi so Bhagavā arahaɱ Sammā-Sam-Buddho vijjā-caraṇa-sampanno Sugato loka-vidū anuttaro purisa-damma-sārathī Satthā deva-manussānaɱ Buddho Bhagavā ti.|| ||

Ayaɱ bhikkhave paṭhamo puññ-ā-bhisando kusal-ā-bhisando sukhass'āhāro sovaggiko sukha-vipāko sagga-saŋvatta-niko iṭṭhāya kantāya manāpāya hitāya sukhāya saŋvattati.|| ||

Puna ca paraɱ bhikkhave ariya-sāvako dhamme avecca-p-pasādena samantāgato hoti:|| ||

Svākkhāto Bhagavatā dhammo sandiṭṭhiko akāliko ehi passiko opanayiko paccattaɱ veditabbo viññūhīti.|| ||

Ayaɱ bhikkhave dutiyo puññ-ā-bhisando kusal-ā-bhisando sukhass'āhāro sovaggiko sukha-vipāko sagga-saŋvatta-niko iṭṭhāya kantāya manāpāya hitāya sukhāya saŋvattati.|| ||

Puna ca paraɱ bhikkhave ariya-sāvako saŋghe avecca-p-pasādena samannāgato hoti:|| ||

Su-paṭipanno Bhagavato sāvaka-saŋgho,||
uju-paṭipanno Bhagavato sāvaka-saŋgho,||
ñāya-paṭipanno Bhagavato sāvaka-saŋgho,||
sāmīci-paṭipanno Bhagavato sāvaka-saŋgho,||
yad idaɱ cattāri purisa-yugāni,||
aṭṭhapurisa-puggalā.|| ||

Esa Bhagavato sāvaka-saŋgho āhuneyyo pāhuṇeyyo dakkhiṇeyyo añjali-karaṇīyo anuttaraɱ puñña-k-khettaɱ lokassāti.|| ||

Ayaɱ bhikkhave tatiyo puññ-ā-bhisando kusal-ā-bhisando sukhass'āhāro sovaggiko sukha-vipāko sagga-saŋvatta-niko iṭṭhāya kantāya manāpāya hitāya sukhāya saŋvattati.|| ||

Puna ca paraɱ bhikkhave ariya-sāvako ariya-kantehi sīlehi samantāgato hoti akhaṇḍehi acchiddehi asabalehi [57] akammāsehi bhujissehi viññ'uppasatthehi aparām-aṭṭhehi samādhi-saŋvatta-nikehi.|| ||

Ayaɱ bhikkhave catuttho puññ-ā-bhisando kusal-ā-bhisando sukhass'āhāro sovaggiko sukha-vipāko sagga-saŋvatta-niko iṭṭhāya kantāya manāpāya hitāya sukhāya saŋvattati.|| ||

Ime kho bhikkhave cattāro puññ-ā-bhisandā kusal-ā-bhisandā sukhass'āhārā sovaggikā sukha-vipākā sagga-saŋvatta-nikā iṭṭhāya kantāya manāpāya hitāya sukhāya saŋvattantī ti.|| ||

Yassa saddhā Tathāgate acalā suppati-ṭ-ṭhitā,
Sīlañ ca yassa kalyāṇaɱ ariya-kantaɱ pasaɱsitaɱ.|| ||

Saŋghe pasādo yass'atthi uju-bhutañ ca dassanaɱ,
Adaḷiddo ti taɱ āhu amoghaɱ tassa jīvitaɱ.|| ||

Tasmā saddhañ ca sīlañ ca pasādaɱ Dhamma-dassanaɱ,
Anuyuñjetha medhāvī saraɱ Buddhāna sāsanan ti.|| ||
