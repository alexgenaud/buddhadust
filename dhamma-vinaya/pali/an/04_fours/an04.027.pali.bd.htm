Aŋguttara Nikāya
4. Catukka Nipāta
III. Uruvelā Vagga

Sutta 27

Santuṭṭhi Suttaɱ

Adapted from the 1995 edition of the digital version of the Sri Lanka Buddha Jayanti Tripitaka Series.

 

[26]

[1][pts][bodh] Evaɱ me sutaɱ:|| ||

Ekaɱ samayaɱ Bhagavā Sāvatthiyaɱ viharati
Jetavane Anāthapiṇḍikassa ārāme.|| ||

Tatra kho Bhagavā bhikkhū āmantesi:|| ||

"Bhikkhavo" ti.|| ||

"Bhadante" ti te bhikkhū Bhagavato paccassosuɱ.|| ||

Bhagavā etad avoca:|| ||

2. Cattār'imāni bhikkhave appāni ca sulabhāni ca tāni,||
anavajjāni ca.|| ||

Katamāni cattāri?|| ||

Paɱsukūlaɱ bhikkhave cīvarānaɱ||
appañ ca||
sulabhañ [27] ca,||
tañ ca anavajjaɱ.|| ||

Piṇḍiyālopo bhikkhave bhojanānaɱ||
appañ ca||
sulabhañ ca,||
tañ ca anavajjaɱ.|| ||

Rukkha-mūlaɱ bhikkhave sen'āsanānaɱ||
appañ ca||
sulabhañ ca,||
tañ ca anavajjaɱ.|| ||

Pūtimuttaɱ bhikkhave bhesajjānaɱ||
appañ ca||
sulabhañ ca,||
tañ ca anavajjaɱ.|| ||

Imāni kho bhikkhave cattāri||
appāni ca||
sulabhāni ca,||
tāni anavajjāni,||
yato kho bhikkhave bhikkhu||
appena ca||
tuṭṭho hoti||
sulabhena ca||
idam imassāhaɱ aññataraɱ sāmaññaŋgan ti vadāmī ti.|| ||

 

Anavajjena tuṭṭhassa appena sulabhena ca,||
Na sen'āsan amārabbha cīvaram pānabhojanaɱ,||
Vighāto hoti cittassa disā na paṭihañaññati.|| ||

Ye c'assa dhammā akkhātā sāmaññassānulomikā,||
Adhiggahītā tuṭṭhassa appamattassa sikkhato ti.|| ||
