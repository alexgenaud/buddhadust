Aŋguttara Nikāya
4. Catukka Nipāta
III. Uruvelā Vagga

Sutta 26

Kuhaka Suttaɱ

Adapted from the 1995 edition of the digital version of the Sri Lanka Buddha Jayanti Tripitaka Series.

 

[26]

[1][pts][bodh] Evaɱ me sutaɱ:|| ||

Ekaɱ samayaɱ Bhagavā Sāvatthiyaɱ viharati
Jetavane Anāthapiṇḍikassa ārāme.|| ||

Tatra kho Bhagavā bhikkhū āmantesi:|| ||

"Bhikkhavo" ti.|| ||

"Bhadante" ti te bhikkhū Bhagavato paccassosuɱ.|| ||

Bhagavā etad avoca:|| ||

2. Ye te bhikkhave bhikkhū kuhā thaddhā lapā siŋgī uṇṇaḷā asamāhitā,||
na me te bhikkhave bhikkhū māmakā.|| ||

Apagatā ca te bhikkhave bhikkhū imasmā Dhamma-Vinayā.|| ||

Na ca te imasmiɱ Dhamma-Vinaye vuḍḍhiɱ virūḷhiɱ vepullaɱ āpajjanti.|| ||

Ye ca kho te bhikkhave bhikkhū nikkuhā nillapā dhīrā atthaddhā susamāhitā,||
te kho me bhikkhave bhikkhū māmakā.|| ||

Anapagatā ca te bhikkhave bhikkhū imasmā Dhamma-Vinayā.|| ||

Te ca imasmiɱ Dhamma-Vinaye vuḍḍhiɱ virūḷahiɱ vepullaɱ āpajjantī ti.|| ||

 

Kuhā thaddhā lapā siŋgī uṇṇaḷā asamāhitā,||
Na te dhamme virūhanti Sammā-Sam-Buddha desite.|| ||

Nikkuhā nillapā dhīrā atthaddhā susamāhitā,||
Te ve dhamme virūhanti Sammā-Sam-Buddha desite ti.|| ||
