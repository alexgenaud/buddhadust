Aŋguttara Nikāya
4. Catukka Nipāta
VII. Pattakamma Vagga

Sutta 65

Rūpappamāṇa Suttaɱ

Adapted from the 1995 edition of the digital version of the Sri Lanka Buddha Jayanti Tripitaka Series.

 

 

[1][pts] Evaɱ me sutaɱ:|| ||

Ekaɱ samayaɱ Bhagavā bhikkhū āmantesi:|| ||

"Bhikkhavo" ti.|| ||

"Bhadante" ti te bhikkhū Bhagavato paccassosuɱ.|| ||

Bhagavā etad avoca:|| ||

2. Cattāro'me bhikkhave puggalā santo saŋvijj'amānā lokasmiɱ.|| ||

Katame cattāro?|| ||

Rūpappamāṇo rūpappasanno,||
ghosappamāṇo ghosappasanno,||
lūkhappamāṇo lūkhappasanno,||
dhammappamāṇo dhammappasanno.|| ||

Ime kho bhikkhave cattāro puggalā santo saŋvijj'amānā lokasmin ti.|| ||

Ye ca rūpena pāmiɱsu ye ca ghosena anvagū,||
Chandarāgavasūpetā na te jānanti taɱ janaɱ.|| ||

Ajjhattañ ca na jānāti bahiddhā ca na passati,||
Samantāvaraṇo bālo sa ve ghosena vuyhati.|| ||

Ajjhattañ ca na jānāti bahiddhā ca vipassati,||
Bahiddhā phaladassāvī so pi ghosena vuyhati.|| ||

Ajjhattañ ca pajānāti bahiddhā ca vipassati,||
Vinīvaraṇadassāvī na so ghosena vuyhatī ti.|| ||
