Aŋguttara Nikāya
VIII. Aṭṭhaka Nipāta
II: Mahā Vagga

Sutta 12

Sīha Senāpati Suttaɱ

Adapted from the 1995 edition of the digital version of the Sri Lanka Buddha Jayanti Tripitaka Series.

[1][pts][bodh] Evaɱ me sutaɱ:|| ||

Ekaɱ samayaɱ Bhagavā Vesāliyaɱ viharati Mahāvane Kūṭāgāra-sālāyaɱ.|| ||

Tena kho pana samayena sambahulā abhiññātā abhiññātā Licchavī santhāgāre sanni-sinnā sanni-patitā aneka-pariyāyena Buddhassa vaṇṇaɱ bhāsanti,||
bhāsanti,||
Saŋghassa vaṇṇaɱ bhāsanti.|| ||

[180] 2. Tena kho pana samayena Sīho senāpati Nigaṇṭha-sāvako tassaɱ parisāyaɱ nisinno hoti.|| ||

Atha kho Sīhassa senāpatissa etad ahosi:|| ||

'Nissaɱ-sayaɱ kho so Bhagavā arahaɱ Sammā-Sam-Buddho bhavissati.|| ||

Tathā h'ime sambahulā abhiññātā abhiññātā Licchavī santhāgāre sanni-sinnā sanni-patitā aneka-pariyāyena Buddhassa vaṇṇaɱ bhāsanti,||
Dhammassa vaṇṇaɱ bhāsanti,||
Saŋghassa vaṇṇaɱ bhāsanti.|| ||

Yan nūnāhaɱ taɱ Bhagavantaɱ dassanāya upasankameyyaɱ arahantaɱ Sammā-Sam-Buddhan' ti.|| ||

3. Atha kho Sīho senāpati yena Nigaṇṭho Nātaputto ten'upasankami.|| ||

Upasaŋkamitvā Nigaṇṭhaɱ Nātaputtaɱ etad avoca:|| ||

'Icchām ahaɱ bhante,||
samaṇaɱ Gotamaɱ dassanāya upasankamitun' ti.|| ||

'Kiɱ pana tvaɱ Sīha,||
kiriyavādo samāno akiriyavādaɱ samaṇaɱ Gotamaɱ dassanāya upasankamissasi?|| ||

Samaṇo hi Sīha,||
Gotamo akiriyavādo akiriyāya dhammaɱ deseti,||
tena ca sāvake vinetī' ti.|| ||

Atha kho Sīhassa senāpatissa yo ahosi gamiyābhisankhāro Bhagavantaɱ dassanāya,||
so paṭippassambhi.|| ||

ete sambahulā Nigaṇṭhā Vesāliyaɱ rathikāya rathikaɱ siŋghāṭakena siŋghāṭakaɱ bāhā paggayha kandanti.|| ||

Ajja Sīhena senāpatinā thullaɱ pasuɱ vadhitvā samaṇassa Gotamassa bhattaɱ kataɱ,||
taɱ Samaṇo Gotamo jānaɱ uddissa kaṭaɱ maɱsaɱ pari- [188] bhuñjati paṭicca kamman' ti.|| ||

'Alaɱ ayyo,||
dīgha-rattam hi te āyasmanto avaṇṇakāmā Buddhassa,||
avaṇṇakāmā Dhammassa,||
avaṇṇakāmā Saŋghassa||
na ca pana te āyasmanto jīranti taɱ Bhagavantaɱ asatā tucchā musā abhūtena abbh'ācikkhantā,||
na ca mayaɱ jīvitahetū pi sañcicca pāṇaɱ jīvitā voropeyyāmā' ti.|| ||

12. Atha kho Sīho senāpati Buddhapamukhaɱ bhikkhu-Saŋghaɱ paṇītena khādanīyena bhojanīyena sahatthā santappesi sampavāresi.|| ||

Atha kho Sīho senāpati Bhagavantaɱ bhuttāviɱ onītapattapāṇīɱ eka-m-antaɱ nisīdi.|| ||

Eka-m-antaɱ nisinnaɱ kho Sīhaɱ senāpatiɱ Bhagavā dhammiyā kathāya sanda-s-setvā samādapekvā samutte-chetvā sampahaɱ-setvā uṭṭhāy āsanā pakkāmī ti.|| ||
