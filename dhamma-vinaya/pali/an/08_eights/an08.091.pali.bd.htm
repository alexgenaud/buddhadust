Aṅguttara Nikāya
Atthaka Nipāta
Rāgādi Peyyālaɱ

Sutta 91

Aṭṭha-Maggaṅga Suttaɱ

Adapted from the 1995 edition of the digital version of the Sri Lanka Buddha Jayanti Tripitaka Series.

 

[348]

[1][pts][bodh] Evaɱ me sutaɱ:|| ||

Ekaɱ samayaɱ Bhagavā Sāvatthiyaɱ viharati.|| ||

Tatra kho Bhagavā bhikkhū āmantesi:|| ||

"Bhikkhavo" ti.|| ||

"Bhadante" ti te bhikkhū Bhagavato paccassosuɱ.|| ||

Bhagavā etad avoca:|| ||

Rāgassa bhikkhave abhiññāya aṭṭha dhammā bhāvetabbā.|| ||

Katame aṭṭha?|| ||

Sammā-diṭṭhi,||
sammā-saṅkappo,||
sammā-vācā,||
sammā-kammanto,||
sammā-ājīvo,||
sammā-vāyāmo,||
sammā-sati,||
sammā-samādhi.|| ||

Rāgāssa bhikkhave abhiññāya ime aṭṭhadhammā bhāvetabbāti.|| ||
