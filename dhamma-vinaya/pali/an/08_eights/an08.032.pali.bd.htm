Aṅguttara Nikāya
VIII. Aṭṭhaka Nipāta
IV. Dāna Vagga

Sutta 32

Dutiya Dāna Suttaṃ

Adapted from the 1995 edition of the digital version of the Sri Lanka Buddha Jayanti Tripitaka Series.

 

[236]

[1][pts][olds][bodh] Evaṃ me sutaṃ:|| ||

Saddhā hiriyaṃ kusalañ ca dānaṃ||
Dhammā ete sappurisānuyātā,||
Etaṃ hi Maggaṃ diviyaṃ vadantī||
Etena gacchati deva-lokan ti.|| ||
