Aŋguttara Nikāya
VIII. Aṭṭhaka Nipāta
IV. Dāna Vagga

Sutta 32

Dutiya Dāna Suttaɱ

Adapted from the 1995 edition of the digital version of the Sri Lanka Buddha Jayanti Tripitaka Series.

 

[236]

[1][pts][olds][bodh] Evaɱ me sutaɱ:|| ||

Saddhā hiriyaɱ kusalañ ca dānaɱ||
Dhammā ete sappurisānuyātā,||
Etaɱ hi Maggaɱ diviyaɱ vadantī||
Etena gacchati deva-lokan ti.|| ||
