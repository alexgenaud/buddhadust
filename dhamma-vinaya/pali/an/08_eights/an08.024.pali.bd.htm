Aṅguttara Nikāya
VIII. Aṭṭhaka Nipāta
III: Gahapati-Vagga

Sutta 24

Hatthaka-Āḷavaka Saṅgaha-Vatthu Suttaɱ

Adapted from the 1995 edition of the digital version of the Sri Lanka Buddha Jayanti Tripitaka Series.

 

[218]

[1][pts][than][bodh][olds] Ekaɱ samayaɱ Bhagavā Āḷaviyaɱ viharati Aggālave cetiye.|| ||

Atha kho Hatthako Āḷavako pañca-mattehi upā- [219] saka-satehi parivuto yena Bhagavā ten'upasaṅkami.|| ||

Upasaṅkamitvā Bhagavantaɱ abhivādetvā eka-m-antaɱ nisīdi.|| ||

Eka-m-antaɱ nisinnaɱ kho Hatthakaɱ Āḷavakaɱ Bhagavā etad avoca:|| ||

2. "Mahatī kho tyāyaɱ Hatthaka, parisā,||
kathaɱ pana tvaɱ Hatthaka,||
imaɱ mahatiɱ parisaɱ saṅgaṇhāhī" ti?|| ||

 

§

 

"Yān'imāni bhante,||
Bhagavatā desitāni cattāri saṅgaha-vatthūni,||
tenāhaɱ imaɱ parisaɱ saṅgaṇhāmi:|| ||

Ahaɱ bhante, yaɱ jānāmi:|| ||

"Ayaɱ dānena saṅgahetabbo" ti,||
taɱ dānena saṅgaṇhāmi;|| ||

yaɱ jānāmi||
"Ayaɱ peyyavajjena saṅgahetabbo" ti,||
taɱ peyyavajjena saṅgaṇhāmi;|| ||

yaɱ jānāmi||
"Ayaɱ attha-cariyāya saṅgahetabbo" ti||
taɱ attha-cariyāya saṅgaṇhāmi;|| ||

yaɱ jānāmi||
"Ayaɱ samān'attatāya saṅgahetabbo" ti||
taɱ samān'attatāya saṅgaṇhāmi.|| ||

Saṅvijjanti kho pana me bhante, kule bhogā,||
daliddassa kho no tathā sotabbaɱ maññantī" ti.|| ||

■

3. "Sādhu sādhu Hatthaka!|| ||

Yoni kho tyāyaɱ Hatthaka,||
mahatiɱ parisaɱ saṅgahetuɱ.|| ||

Ye hi keci Hatthaka,||
atītam addhānaɱ parisaɱ saṅgahesuɱ,||
sabbe te imeh'eva catūhi saṅgaha-vatthūhi mahatiɱ parisaɱ saṅgahesuɱ.|| ||

Ye pi hi keci Hatthaka,||
anāgatam addhānaɱ mahatiɱ parisaɱ saṅgahessanti,||
sabbe te imeh'eva catūhi saṅgaha-vatthuhi mahatiɱ parisaɱ saṅgahi'ssanti.|| ||

Ye pi hi keci Hatthaka,||
etarahi mahatiɱ parisaɱ saṅgaṇhanti,||
sabbe te imeh'eva catūhi saṅgaha-vatthūhi mahatiɱ parisaɱ saṅgaṇhantī" ti.|| ||

■

4. Atha kho Hatthako Āḷavako Bhagavatā dhammiyā kathāya sanda-s-sito samāda-pito samutte-jito sampahaɱ-sito uṭṭhāy āsanā Bhagavantaɱ abhivādetvā padakkhiṇaɱ katvā [220] pakkāmi.|| ||

 

§

 

Atha kho Bhagavā acira-pakkante Hatthake Āḷavake bhikkhū āmantesi:|| ||

5. "Aṭṭhahi bhikkhave, acchariyehi abbhutehi dhammehi samannāgataɱ Hatthakaɱ Āḷavakaɱ dhāretha.|| ||

Katamehi aṭṭhahi?|| ||

6. Saddho bhikkhave, Hatthako Āḷavako,||
sīlavā bhikkhave, Hatthako Āḷavako,||
hirimā bhikkhave, Hatthako Āḷavako,||
ottapī bhikkhave, Hatthako Āḷavako,||
bahu-s-suto bhikkhave, Hatthako Āḷavako,||
cāgavā bhikkhave, Hatthako Āḷavako,||
paññavā bhikkhave, Hatthako Āḷavako,||
appiccho bhikkhave Hatthako Āḷavako.|| ||

Imehi kho bhikkhave, aṭṭhahi acchariyehi abbhutehi dhammehi samannāgataɱ Hatthakaɱ Āḷavakaɱ dhārethā" ti.|| ||
