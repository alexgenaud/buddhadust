Aŋguttara Nikāya
VIII. Aṭṭhaka Nipāta
VII. Bhūmi-Cāla Vagga

Sutta 64

Adhideva-Ñāṇa-Dassana Suttaɱ

Adapted from the 1995 edition of the digital version of the Sri Lanka Buddha Jayanti Tripitaka Series.

 

[302]

[1][pts] Evaɱ me sutaɱ:|| ||

Ekaɱ samayaɱ Bhagavā Gayāyaɱ viharati Gayāsīse.|| ||

Tatra kho Bhagavā bhikkhū āmantesi:|| ||

'Bhikkhave' ti.|| ||

"Bhadante" ti te bhikkhu Bhagavato paccassosuɱ.|| ||

Bhagavā etad avoca:|| ||

2. Pubbāhaɱ bhikkhave, sambodhā anabhi-sambuddho bodhisatto'va samāno obhāsaɱ hi kho sañjānāmi,||
no ca rūpāni passāmi.|| ||

Tassa mayhaɱ bhikkhave, etad ahosi:|| ||

'Sace kho ahaɱ obhāsañ c'eva sañjāneyyaɱ,||
rūpāni ca passeyyaɱ,||
evaɱ me idaɱ ñāṇa-dassanaɱ parisuddhataraɱ assā' ti.|| ||

So kho ahaɱ bhikkhave, aparena samayena||
appamatto||
ātāpi||
pahit'atto viharanto||
obhāsañ c'eva sañjānāmi,||
rūpāni ca passāmi,||
no ca kho tāhi devatāhi saddhiɱ santiṭṭhāmi,||
sallapāmi,||
sākācchaɱ samāpajjāmi.|| ||

 

 

3. Tassa mayhaɱ bhikkhave, etad ahosi:|| ||

'Sace kho ahaɱ obhāsañ c'eva sañjāneyyaɱ,||
rūpāni ca passeyyaɱ,||

sallapeyyaɱ||

no ca kho tā devatā jānāmi,||
'imā devatā amukambhā vā amukambhā vā devanikāyā' ti.[ed1]|| ||

no ca kho tā devatā jānāmi||
'imā devatā imassa kammassa vipākena ito cutā tattha uppannā' ti,||
tā ca devatā jāneyyaɱ||
'imā devatā imassa kammassa vipākena ito cutā tattha uppannā' ti.|| ||

9. Yāva kīvañ ca me bhikkhave,||
evaɱ aṭṭhaparivaṭṭaɱ adhidevañāṇa-dassanaɱ na suvisuddhaɱ ahosi,||
n'eva tāvāhaɱ bhikkhave,||
sa-devake loke sa-mārake sabrahamke sa-s-samaṇa-brāhmaṇiyā pajāya sadeva-manussāya anuttaraɱ sammā-sambodhiɱ abhisambuddho ti paccaññāsiɱ.|| ||

Yato ca kho me bhikkhave,||
evaɱ aṭṭhaparivattaɱ adhidevañāṇa-dassanaɱ suvisuddhaɱ ahosi.|| ||

Athāhaɱ bhikkhave, [305] sa-devake loke sa-mārake sabrahamke sa-s-samaṇa-brāhmaṇiyā pajāya sadeva-manussāya anuttaraɱ sammā-sambodhiɱ abhisambuddho ti paccaññāsiɱ.|| ||

"Ñāṇañ ca pana me dassanaɱ udapādi:||
"Akuppā me ceto-vimutti.|| ||

Ayamantimā jāti,||
n'atthi dāni puna-b-bhavo" ti.|| ||
