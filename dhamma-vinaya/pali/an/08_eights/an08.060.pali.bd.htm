Aŋguttara Nikāya
VIII. Aṭṭhaka Nipāta
VI. Gotamī Vagga aka Sa-ādhāna-Vagga

Sutta 60

Dutiya Aṭṭha-Puggala Suttaɱ

Adapted from the 1995 edition of the digital version of the Sri Lanka Buddha Jayanti Tripitaka Series.

 

[292]

[1][pts] Evaɱ me sutaɱ:|| ||

Ekaɱ samayaɱ Bhagavā Sāvatthiyaɱ viharati
Jetavane Anāthapiṇḍikassa ārāme.|| ||

Tatra kho Bhagavā bhikkhū āmantesi:|| ||

"Bhikkhavo" ti.|| ||

"Bhadante" ti te bhikkhū Bhagavato paccassosuɱ.|| ||

Bhagavā etad avoca:|| ||

2. "Aṭṭhahi bhikkhave, puggalā āhuneyyā pāhuneyyā dakkhiṇeyyā añjali-karaṇīyā anuttaraɱ puñña-k-khettaɱ lokassa.|| ||

Katamehi aṭṭha?|| ||

[293] 3. Sot'āpanno,|| ||

■

Sot'āpatti-phala-sacchi-kiriyāya paṭipanno,|| ||

■

Sakad-āgāmi,|| ||

■

Sakad-āgāmi-phala-sacchi-kiriyāya paṭipanno,|| ||

■

Anāgāmī,|| ||

■

Anāgāi-phala-sacchi-kiriyāya paṭipanno,|| ||

■

Arahā,|| ||

■

Arahatta-pala-sacchi-kiriyāya paṭipanno.|| ||

Ime kho bhikkhave, aṭṭha puggalā āhuneyyā pāhuneyyā dakkhineyyā añjali-karaṇīyā anuttaraɱ puñña-k-khettaɱ lokassā" ti.

 

 

Cattāro ca paṭipannā cattāro ca phale ṭhitā:||
Esa saŋgho samukkaṭṭho sattānaɱ aṭṭha puggalā

Yajamānānaɱ manussānaɱ puññapekkhānapāṇinaɱ||
Karotaɱ opadhikaɱ puññaɱ saŋghe dinnaɱ maha-p-phalan ti.|| ||
