Aŋguttara Nikāya
VIII. Atthaka Nipāta
IX. Sati-Vagga

Sutta 87

Patta-Nikkujjana Suttaɱ

Adapted from the 1995 edition of the digital version of the Sri Lanka Buddha Jayanti Tripitaka Series.

 

[244]

[1][pts] Evaɱ me sutaɱ:|| ||

Ekaɱ samayaɱ Bhagavā Sāvatthiyaɱ viharati.|| ||

Tatra kho Bhagavā bhikkhū āmantesi:|| ||

"Bhikkhavo" ti.|| ||

"Bhadante" ti te bhikkhū Bhagavato paccassosuɱ.|| ||

Bhagavā etad avoca:|| ||

"Aṭṭhahi bhikkhave aŋgehi samannāgatassa upāsakassa ākaŋkha-māno saŋgho pattaɱ nikkujjayya.|| ||

Katamehi aṭṭhahi?|| ||

[345] 2. Bhikkhunaɱ alābhāya parisakkati,||
bhikkhunaɱ anatthāya parisakkati,||
bhikkhunaɱ anāvāsāya parisakkati,||
bhikkhunaɱ akkosati paribhāsati,||
bhikkhu bhikkhuhi vibhedeti Buddhassa avaṇṇaɱ bhāsati,||
Dhammassa avaṇṇaɱ bhāsati,||
Saŋghassa avaṇṇaɱ bhāsati.|| ||

Imehi kho bhikkhave aṭṭhabaŋgehi samannāgatassa upāsakassa ākaŋkha-māno saŋgho pattaɱ nikkujjeyya.|| ||

 

§

 

Aṭṭhahi bhikkhave aŋgehi samannāgatassa upāsakassa ākaŋkha-māno saŋgho pattaɱ ukkujjeyya.|| ||

Katamehi aṭṭhahi?|| ||

Na bhikkhunaɱ alābhāya parisakkati,||
na bhikkhunaɱ anatthāya parisakkati,||
na bhikkhunaɱ āvāsāya parisakkati,||
na bhikkhunaɱ akkosati paribhāsati,||
na bhikkhu bhikkhuhi vibhedeti Buddhassa vaṇṇaɱ bhāsati,||
Dhammassa vaṇṇaɱ bhāsati,||
Saŋghassa vaṇṇaɱ bhāsati.|| ||

Imehi kho bhikkhave aṭṭhabaŋgehi samannāgatassa upāsakassa ākaŋkha-māno saŋgho pattaɱ ukkujjeyyā" ti.|| ||
