Sutta 121

Aṭṭha-Maggaŋga Suttaɱ

[121.1][pts][bodh] Evaɱ me sutaɱ:|| ||

Ekaɱ samayaɱ Bhagavā Sāvatthiyaɱ viharati.|| ||

Tatra kho Bhagavā bhikkhū āmantesi:|| ||

"Bhikkhavo" ti.|| ||

"Bhadante" ti te bhikkhū Bhagavato paccassosuɱ.|| ||

Bhagavā etad avoca:|| ||

"Dosassa bhikkhave abhiññāya aṭṭha dhammā bhāvetabbā.|| ||

Katame aṭṭha?|| ||

Sammā-diṭṭhi,||
sammā-saŋkappo,||
sammā-vācā,||
sammā-kammanto,||
sammā-ājīvo,||
sammā-vāyāmo,||
sammā-sati,||
sammā-samādhi.|| ||

Dosassa bhikkhave abhiññāya ime aṭṭhadhammā bhāvetabbāti.|| ||

 

 

Sutta 122

Abhibhāyatana Suttaɱ

[122.1][pts] Evaɱ me sutaɱ:|| ||

Ekaɱ samayaɱ Bhagavā Sāvatthiyaɱ viharati.|| ||

Tatra kho Bhagavā bhikkhū āmantesi:|| ||

"Bhikkhavo" ti.|| ||

"Bhadante" ti te bhikkhū Bhagavato paccassosuɱ.|| ||

Bhagavā etad avoca:|| ||

"Dosassa bhikkhave abhiññāya aṭṭha dhamma bhāvetabbā.|| ||

Katamāni aṭṭha?|| ||

2. Ajjhattaɱ rūpa-saññī eko bahiddhā rūpāni passati parittāni su-vaṇṇa-du-b-baṇṇāni.|| ||

'Tāni Abhibhuyya jānāmi passāmī' ti||
evaɱ saññī hoti.|| ||

3. Ajjhattaɱ rūpa-saññī eko bahiddhā rūpāni passati appamāṇāni su-vaṇṇa-du-b-baṇṇāni.|| ||

'Tāni Abhibhuyya jānāmi passāmī' ti evaɱ saññi hoti.|| ||

4. Ajjhattaɱ arūpa-saññī eko bahiddhā rūpāni passati parittāni su-vaṇṇa-du-b-baṇṇāni.|| ||

'Tāni Abhibhuyya jānāmi passāmī' ti evaɱ saññi hoti.|| ||

5. Ajjhattaɱ arūpa-saññī eko bahiddhā rūpāni passati appamāṇāni su-vaṇṇa-du-b-baṇṇāni.|| ||

'Tāni Abhibhuyya jānāmi passāmī' ti evaɱ saññi hoti.|| ||

Idaɱ catutthaɱ abhibh'āyatanaɱ.|| ||

6. Ajjhattaɱ arūpa-saññī eko bahiddhā rūpāni passati nilāni nīla-vaṇṇāni nīlani-dassanāni nīlani-bhāsāni.|| ||

'Tāni Abhibhuyya jānāmi passāmī' ti evaɱ saññi hoti.|| ||

7. Ajjhattaɱ arūpa-saññī eko bahiddhā rūpāni passati pītāni pīnata-vaṇnāni pītani-dassanāni pītani-bhāsāni.|| ||

'Tāni Abhibhuyya jānāmi passāmī' ti evaɱ saññi hoti.|| ||

8. Ajjhattaɱ arūpa-saññī eko bahiddhā rūpāni passati lohita-kāni lohitaka-vaṇṇāni lohitakani-dassanāni lohitakani-bhāsāni.|| ||

'Tāni Abhibhuyya jānāmi passāmī' ti evaɱ saññi hoti.|| ||

9. Ajjhattaɱ arūpa-saññī eko bahiddhā rūpāni passati odātāni oda-vaṇṇātāni odātani-dassanāni odātani-bhāsāni.|| ||

'Tāni Abhibhuyya jānāmi passāmī' ti evaɱ saññi hoti.|| ||

Dosassa bhikkhave abhiññāya imi aṭṭha dhamma bhāvetabbā ti.|| ||

 

 

Sutta 123

Aṭṭhavimokkha Suttaɱ

 

 

[123.1][pts] Evaɱ me sutaɱ:|| ||

Ekaɱ samayaɱ Bhagavā Sāvatthiyaɱ viharati.|| ||

Tatra kho Bhagavā bhikkhū āmantesi:|| ||

"Bhikkhavo" ti.|| ||

"Bhadante" ti te bhikkhū Bhagavato paccassosuɱ.|| ||

Bhagavā etad avoca:|| ||

"Dosassa bhikkhave abhiññāya aṭṭha dhamma bhāvetabbā.|| ||

Katamāni aṭṭha?|| ||

2. Rūpi rūpāni passati.|| ||

2. Ajjhattaɱ arūpa-saññī eko bahiddhā rūpāni passati.|| ||

3. Subhan t'eva adhimutto hoti.|| ||

4. Sabbaso rūpa-saññānaɱ samati-k-kamā paṭigha-saññānaɱ atthaŋ-gamā nānatta-saññānaɱ amana-sikārā|| ||

'Anatto ākāso' ti|| ||

Ākāsanañ-c'āyatanaɱ upasampajja viharati.|| ||

5. Sabbaso Ākāsanañ-c'āyatanaɱ samati-k-kamma|| ||

'Anattaɱ viññāṇan' ti|| ||

Viññāṇañ-c'āyatanaɱ upasampajja viharati.|| ||

6. Sabbaso Viññāṇañ-c'āyatanaɱ samati-k-kamma|| ||

'N'atthi kiñcī' ti|| ||

Ākiñcaññ'āyatanaɱ upasampajja viharati.|| ||

7. Sabbaso Ākiñcaññ'āyatanaɱ samati-k-kamma||
N'eva-saññā-nā-saññ'āyatanaɱ upasampajja viharati.|| ||

8. Sabbosa N'eva-saññā-nā-saññ'āyatanaɱ samati-k-kamma||
saññā-vedayitaɱ nirodhaɱ upasampajja viharati.|| ||

Ime kho bhikkhave aṭṭha vimokkhā ti.|| ||

Dosassa bhikkhave abhiññāya ime aṭṭha dhamma bhāvetabbā.|| ||
