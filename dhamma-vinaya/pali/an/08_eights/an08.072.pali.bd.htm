Aŋguttara Nikāya
VIII. Aṭṭhaka Nipāta
VIII. Yamaka-Vagga

Sutta 72

Dutiya Samanta-Pāsādika Suttaɱ

Adapted from the 1995 edition of the digital version of the Sri Lanka Buddha Jayanti Tripitaka Series.

 

[315]

[1][pts] Evaɱ me sutaɱ:|| ||

Ekaɱ samayaɱ Bhagavā Sāvatthiyaɱ viharati.|| ||

Tatra kho Bhagavā bhikkhū āmantesi:|| ||

"Bhikkhavo" ti.|| ||

"Bhadante" ti te bhikkhū Bhagavato paccassosuɱ.|| ||

Bhagavā etad avoca:|| ||

2. "Saddho ca bhikkhave bhikkhu hoti,||
no ca sīlavā||
evaɱ so ten'aŋgena aparipūro hoti.|| ||

Tena taɱ aŋgaɱ paripūretabbaɱ:|| ||

'Kintāhaɱ saddho ca assaɱ sīlavā vā' ti.|| ||

Yato ca kho bhikkhave bhikkhu saddho ca hoti||
sīlavā ca||
evaɱ so ten'aŋgena paripūro hoti.|| ||

 

§

 

3. Saddho ca bhikkhave bhikkhu hoti,||
sīlavā ca||
no ca bahu-s-suto||
evaɱ so ten'aŋgena aparipūro hoti.|| ||

Tena taɱ aŋgaɱ paripūretabbaɱ:|| ||

'Kintāhaɱ saddho ca||
assaɱ sīlavā ca||
bahu-s-suto cā' ti.|| ||

Yato ca kho bhikkhave bhikkhu saddho ca hoti||
sīlavā ca||
bahu-s-suto ca,||
evaɱ so ten'aŋgena paripūro hoti.|| ||

 

§

 

4. Saddho ca bhikkhave bhikkhu hoti,||
sīlavā ca||
bahu-s-suto ca||
no ca parisā-vacaro||
evaɱ so ten'aŋgena aparipūro hoti.|| ||

Tena taɱ aŋgaɱ paripūretabbaɱ:|| ||

'Kintāhaɱ saddho ca||
assaɱ sīlavā ca||
bahu-s-suto cā||
parisā-vacaro ca' ti.|| ||

Yato ca kho bhikkhave bhikkhu saddho ca hoti||
sīlavā ca||
bahu-s-suto ca,||
parisā-vacaro ca||
evaɱ so ten'aŋgena paripūro hoti.|| ||

 

§

 

5. Saddho ca bhikkhave bhikkhu hoti,||
sīlavā ca||
bahu-s-suto ca||
parisā-vacaro ca||
no ca Dhamma-kathiko parisāya dhammaɱ deseti||
evaɱ so ten'aŋgena aparipūro hoti.|| ||

Tena taɱ aŋgaɱ paripūretabbaɱ:|| ||

'Kintāhaɱ saddho ca||
assaɱ sīlavā ca||
bahu-s-suto cā||
parisā-vacaro ca||
Dhamma-kathiko parisāya dhammaɱ deseti ca' ti.|| ||

Yato ca kho bhikkhave bhikkhu saddho ca hoti||
sīlavā ca||
bahu-s-suto ca,||
parisā-vacaro ca||
Dhamma-kathiko parisāya dhammaɱ deseti ca
evaɱ so ten'aŋgena paripūro hoti.|| ||

 

§

 

6. Saddho ca bhikkhave bhikkhu hoti,||
sīlavā ca||
bahu-s-suto ca||
parisā-vacaro ca||
Dhamma-kathiko parisāya dhammaɱ deseti ca||
no ca visārado ca parisāya dhammaɱ deseti||
[316] evaɱ so ten'aŋgena aparipūro hoti.|| ||

Tena taɱ aŋgaɱ paripūretabbaɱ:|| ||

'Kintāhaɱ saddho ca||
assaɱ sīlavā ca||
bahu-s-suto cā||
parisā-vacaro ca||
Dhamma-kathiko parisāya dhammaɱ deseti ca||
visārado ca parisāya dhammaɱ deseti ca' ti.|| ||

Yato ca kho bhikkhave bhikkhu saddho ca hoti||
sīlavā ca||
bahu-s-suto ca,||
parisā-vacaro ca||
Dhamma-kathiko parisāya dhammaɱ deseti ca||
visārado ca parisāya dhammaɱ deseti ca||
evaɱ so ten'aŋgena paripūro hoti.|| ||

 

§

 

7. Saddho ca bhikkhave bhikkhu hoti,||
sīlavā ca||
bahu-s-suto ca||
parisā-vacaro ca||
Dhamma-kathiko parisāya dhammaɱ deseti ca||
visārado ca parisāya dhammaɱ deseti ca||
no ca ye te santā vimokhā ati-k-kamma uūpe āruppā,||
te kāyena phusitvā viharati;||
evaɱ so ten'aŋgena aparipūro hoti.|| ||

Tena taɱ aŋgaɱ paripūretabbaɱ:|| ||

'Kintāhaɱ saddho ca||
assaɱ sīlavā ca||
bahu-s-suto cā||
parisā-vacaro ca||
Dhamma-kathiko parisāya dhammaɱ deseti ca||
visārado ca parisāya dhammaɱ deseti ca||
ye te santā vimokhā ati-k-kamma uūpe āruppā,||
te kāyena phusitvā viharati ca' ti.|| ||

Yato ca kho bhikkhave bhikkhu saddho ca hoti||
sīlavā ca||
bahu-s-suto ca,||
parisā-vacaro ca||
Dhamma-kathiko parisāya dhammaɱ deseti ca||
visārado ca parisāya dhammaɱ deseti ca||
vimokhā ati-k-kamma uūpe āruppā,||
te kāyena phusitvā viharati ca||
evaɱ so ten'aŋgena paripūro hoti.|| ||

 

§

 

8. Saddho ca bhikkhave bhikkhu hoti,||
sīlavā ca||
bahu-s-suto ca||
parisā-vacaro ca||
Dhamma-kathiko parisāya dhammaɱ deseti ca||
visārado ca parisāya dhammaɱ deseti ca||
vimokhā ati-k-kamma uūpe āruppā,||
te kāyena phusitvā viharati ca||
no ca āsavānaɱ khayā anāsavaɱ ceto-vimuttaɱ paññā-vimuttaɱ diṭṭhe'va dhamme sayaɱ abhiññā sacchi-katvā upasampajja viharati||
evaɱ so ten'aŋgena aparipūro hoti.|| ||

Tena taɱ aŋgaɱ paripūretabbaɱ:|| ||

'Kintāhaɱ saddho ca||
assaɱ sīlavā ca||
bahu-s-suto cā||
parisā-vacaro ca||
Dhamma-kathiko parisāya dhammaɱ deseti ca||
visārado ca parisāya dhammaɱ deseti ca||
vimokhā ati-k-kamma uūpe āruppā,||
te kāyena phusitvā viharati ca||
āsavānaɱ khayā anāsavaɱ ceto-vimuttaɱ paññā-vimuttaɱ diṭṭhe'va dhamme sayaɱ abhiññā sacchi-katvā upasampajja viharati ca' ti.|| ||

Yato ca kho bhikkhave bhikkhu saddho ca hoti||
sīlavā ca||
bahu-s-suto ca,||
Dhamma-kathiko parisāya dhammaɱ deseti ca||
visārado ca parisāya dhammaɱ deseti ca||
vimokhā ati-k-kamma uūpe āruppā,||
te kāyena phusitvā viharati ca||
āsavānaɱ khayā anāsavaɱ ceto-vimuttaɱ paññā-vimuttaɱ diṭṭhe'va dhamme sayaɱ abhiññā sacchi-katvā upasampajja viharati ca||
evaɱ so ten'aŋgena paripūro hoti.|| ||

Imehi kho bhikkhave, aṭṭhehi dhammehi samannāgato bhikkhu samanta-pāsādiko ca hoti sabbā-kāra-paripūro cā ti.|| ||
