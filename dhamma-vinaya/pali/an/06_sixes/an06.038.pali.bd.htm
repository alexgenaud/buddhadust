Aŋguttara Nikāya
Chakka Nipāta
IV. Devatā Vagga

Sutta 38

Atta-Kāra Suttaɱ

Adapted from the 1995 edition of the digital version of the Sri Lanka Buddha Jayanti Tripitaka Series.

 

[337]

[1][pts][olds][niza] Ekaɱ samayaɱ Bhagavā Sāvatthiyaɱ viharati Jetavane Anāthapiṇḍikassa ārāme.|| ||

Atha kho aññataro brāhmaṇo yena Bhagavā ten'upasankami.|| ||

Upasaŋkamitvā Bhagavatā saddhiɱ sammodi.|| ||

Sammodanīyaɱ kathaɱ sārāṇīyaɱ vīti-sāretvā eka-m-antaɱ nisīdi.|| ||

Eka-m-antaɱ nisinno kho so brāhmaṇo Bhagavantaɱ etad avoca:|| ||

2. Ahaɱ hi bho Gotama evaɱ-vādi evaɱ-diṭṭhi:|| ||

N'atthi atta-kāro,||
n'atthi para-kāro ti.|| ||

Māhaɱ brāhmaṇa, evaɱ-vādiɱ evaɱ-diṭṭhiɱ addasaɱ vā assosiɱ vā.|| ||

Kathaɱ hi [338] nāma sayaɱ abhi-k-kamanto sayaɱ paṭi-k-kamanto evaɱ vakkhati:|| ||

'N'atthi atta-kāro,||
natti para-kāro ti'?|| ||

■

Taɱ kiɱ maññasi brāhmaṇa,||
atthi ārambha-dhātū ti?|| ||

Evam bho.|| ||

Ārambha-dhātuyā sati ārambha-vanto sattā paññāyantī ti?|| ||

Evaɱ bho.|| ||

Yaɱ kho brāhmaṇa ārambha-dhātuyā sati ārambha-vanto sattā paññāyantī, ti||
ayaɱ sattānaɱ atta-kāro,||
ayaɱ para-kāro.|| ||

■

Taɱ kiɱ maññasi brāhmaṇa atthi ni-k-khama-dhātu?|| ||

Evaɱ bho.|| ||

Nikkhama-dhātuyā sati ni-k-khama-vanto sattā paññāyantī ti?|| ||

■

Taɱ kiɱ maññasi brāhmaṇa atthi para-k-kama-dhātu?|| ||

■

Taɱ kiɱ maññasi brāhmaṇa atthi thāma-dhātu?|| ||

■

Taɱ kiɱ maññasi brāhmaṇa ṭhiti-dhātu?|| ||

■

Taɱ kiɱ maññasi brāhmaṇa atthi upa-k-kama-dhātu ti?|| ||

Evaɱ bho.|| ||

Upa-k-kama-dhātuyā sati upa-k-kama-vanto sattā paññāyantī ti?|| ||

Evaɱ bho.|| ||

Yaɱ kho brāhmaṇa, upa-k-kama-dhātuyā sati upa-k-kama-vanto sattā paññāyanti,||
ayaɱ sattānaɱ atta-kāro,||
ayaɱ para-kāro.|| ||

■

Māhaɱ brāhmaṇa, evaɱ-vādiɱ evaɱ-diṭṭhiɱ addasaɱ vā assosiɱ vā.|| ||

Kathaɱ hi nāma sayaɱ abhi-k-kamanto sayaɱ paṭi-k-kamanto evaɱ vakkhati:|| ||

'N'atthi atta-kāro,||
n'atthi para-kāro'" ti?|| ||

 

§

 

Abhikkantaɱ bho Gotama,||
abhikkantaɱ bho Gotama!|| ||

Seyyathā pi bho Gotama,||
nikkujjitaɱ vā ukkujjeyya||
paṭicchantaɱ vā vivareyya||
mūḷhassa vā Maggaɱ||
ācikkheyya andha-kāre vā tela-pajjotaɱ dhāreyya||
cakkhūmanto rūpāni dakkhintī ti.|| ||

Evam evaɱ bhotā Gotamena aneka-pariyāyena dhammo pakāsito.|| ||

Es'āhaɱ Bhagavantaɱ Gotamaɱ saraṇaɱ gacchāmi,||
Dhammañ ca||
bhikkhu-Saŋghañ ca.|| ||

Upāsakaɱ maɱ bhavaɱ Gotamo dhāretu ajja-t-agge pāṇupetaɱ saraṇaɱ gatan ti.|| ||
