Sutta 117

Kāy-ā-nupassī Suttaɱ

[p117.1][pts] Evaɱ me sutaɱ:|| ||

Ekaɱ samayaɱ Bhagavā Sāvatthiyaɱ viharati||
Jetavane Anāthapiṇḍikassa ārāme.|| ||

Tatra kho Bhagavā bhikkhu āmantesi:|| ||

"Bhikkhavo" ti.|| ||

"Bhadante" ti te bhikkhū Bhagavato paccassosuɱ.|| ||

Bhagavā etad avoca:|| ||

2. "Cha bhikkhave dhamme a-p-pahāya abhabbo kāye kāy'ānupassī viharituɱ.|| ||

Katame cha?|| ||

Kamm-ā-rāmataɱ,||
bhass-ā-rāmataɱ,||
nidd-ā-rāmataɱ,||
sangaṇ'ik-ā-rāmataɱ,||
indriyesu agutta-dvārataɱ,||
bhojane amatt'aññūtaɱ.|| ||

Ime kho bhikkhave cha dhamme a-p-pahāya abhabbo kāye kāy'ānupassī viharituɱ.|| ||

 

 

Cha bhikkhave dhamme pahāya bhabbo kāye kāy'ānupassī viharituɱ.|| ||

Katame cha?|| ||

Kamm-ā-rāmataɱ,||
bhass-ā-rāmataɱ,||
nidd-ā-rāmataɱ,||
sangaṇ'ik-ā-rāmataɱ,||
indriyesu agutta-dvārataɱ,||
bhojane amatt'aññūtaɱ.|| ||

Ime kho bhikkhave cha dhamme pahāya bhabbo kāye kāy'ānupassī viharitunti." ti.|| ||
