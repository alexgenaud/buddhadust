Aŋguttara Nikāya
Chakka Nipāta
V. Dhammika Vagga

Sutta 45

Iṇa Suttaɱ

Adapted from the 1995 edition of the digital version of the Sri Lanka Buddha Jayanti Tripitaka Series.

 

[351]

[1][pts][than] Evaɱ me sutaɱ:|| ||

Ekaɱ samayaɱ Bhagavā Sāvatthiyaɱ viharati||
Jetavane Anāthapiṇḍikassa ārāme.|| ||

Tatra kho Bhagavā bhikkhu āmantesi:|| ||

"Bhikkhavo" ti.|| ||

"Bhadante" ti te bhikkhū Bhagavato paccassosuɱ.|| ||

Bhagavā etad avoca:|| ||

2. "Dāliddiyaɱ bhikkhave dukkhaɱ lokasmiɱ kāma-bhogino ti?|| ||

Evaɱ bhante.|| ||

2. Yam pi bhikkhave daḷiddo [352] assako anāḷhiyo iṇaɱ ādiyati.|| ||

Itādānam pi bhikkhave dukkhaɱ lokasmiɱ kāma-bhogino ti?|| ||

Evaɱ bhante.|| ||

3. Yam pi bhikkhave daḷiddo assako anāḷhiyo iṇaɱ ādiyitvā vaḍḍhiɱ paṭisuṇāti.|| ||

Vaḍḍhi pi bhikkhave dukkhā lokasmiɱ sāmabhogino ti?|| ||

Evaɱ bhante.|| ||

4. Yam pi bhikkhave daḷiddo assako anāḷhiyo vaḍḍhiɱ paṭisuṇitvā kālābhataɱ vaḍḍhiɱ na deti.|| ||

Codenti pi naɱ codanā pi bhikkhave dukkhā lokasmiɱ kāma-bhogino ti?|| ||

Evaɱ bhante.|| ||

5. Yam pi bhikkhave daḷiddo assako anāḷhiyo codiyamāno na deti.|| ||

Anucaranti pi naɱ.|| ||

Anucariyā pi bhikkhave dukkhā lokasmiɱ kāma-bhogino ti?|| ||

Evaɱ bhante.|| ||

6. Yam pi bhikkhave daḷiddo assako anāḷhiyo anucariyamāno na deti.|| ||

Bandhanti pi naɱ.|| ||

Bandhanam pi bhikkhave dukkhaɱ lokasmiɱ kāma-bhogino ti?|| ||

Evaɱ bhante.|| ||

Iti kho bhikkhave dāḷiddyam pi dukkhaɱ lokasmiɱ kāma-bhogino.|| ||

Iṇādānam pi dukkhaɱ lokasmiɱ kāma-bhogino.|| ||

Vaḍḍhī pi dukkhā lokasmiɱ kāma-bhogino,||
codanā pi dukkhā lokasmiɱ kāma-bhogino.|| ||

Anucariyā pi dukkhā lokasmiɱ kāma-bhogino.|| ||

Bandhanam pi dukkhaɱ lokasmiɱ kāma-bhogino.|| ||

Evam eva kho bhikkhave yassa kassaci saddhā n'atthi kusalesu dhammesu,||
hiri n'atthi kusalesu dhammesu,||
ottappaɱ n'atthi kusalesu dhammesu,||
viriyaɱ n'atthi kusalesu dhammesu,||
paññā n'atthi kusalesu dhammesu,||
ayaɱ vuccati bhikkhave ariyassa vinaye daḷiddo assako anāḷhiyo.|| ||

Sa kho so bhikkhave dasiddo assako anāḷhiyo,||
saddhāya asati kusalesu dhammesu,||
hiriyā asati kusalesu dhammesu,||
ottappe asati kusalesu dhammesu,||
viriye asati kusalesu dhammesu,||
paññāya asati kusalesu dhammesu,||
kāyena du-c-caritaɱ carati.|| ||

Vācāya du-c-caritaɱ carati.|| ||

Manasā du-c-caritaɱ carati.|| ||

Idam assa iṇādānasmiɱ vadāmi.|| ||

So tassa kāya-du-c-caritassa paṭicchā-dana-hetu pāpikaɱ icchaɱ paṇidahati:||
'Mā maɱ jaññā' ti icchati,||
'Mā maɱ jaññā' ti [353] saŋkappeti,||
'Mā maɱ jaññā' ti vācaɱ bhāsati,||
'Mā maɱ jaññā' ti kāyena parakkamati.|| ||

So tassa vacī-du-c-caritassa paṭicchā-dana-hetu pāpikaɱ icchaɱ paṇidahati:||
'mā maɱ chaññā' ti icchati,||
'Mā maɱ jaññā' ti saŋkappeti,||
'Mā maɱ jaññā' ti vācaɱ bhāsati,||
'Mā maɱ jaññā' ti kāyena parakkamati.|| ||

So tassa mano-du-c-caritassa paṭicchā-dana-hetu pāpikaɱ icchaɱ paṇidahati:||
'Mā maɱ jaññā' ti icchati,||
'Mā maɱ jaññā' ti saŋkappeti,||
'Mā maɱ jaññā' ti vācaɱ bhāsati,||
'Mā maɱ jaññā' ti kāyena parakkamati.|| ||

Idam assa vaḍḍhiyā vadāmi.|| ||

Tam enaɱ pesalā sabrahma-cārī evam āhaɱsu:|| ||

"Ayañ ca so āyasmā evaɱ-kārī evaɱ samā-cāro" ti.|| ||

Idam assa codanāya vadāmi.|| ||

Tam enaɱ arañña-gataɱ vā||
rukkha-mūla-gataɱ vā||
sūññā-gāra-gataɱ vā||
vippaṭi-sāra-saha-gatā pāpakā akusala-vitakkā samud'ācaranti.|| ||

Idam assa anucariyāya vadāmi.|| ||

Sa kho so bhikkhave daḷiddo assako anāḷhiyo||
kāyena du-c-caritaɱ caritvā||
vācāya du-c-caritaɱ caritvā||
manasā du-c-caritaɱ caritvā||
kāyassa bhedā param maraṇā Niraya-bandhane vā bajjhati,||
tiracchāna-yoni-bandhane vā.|| ||

Nāhaɱ bhikkhave aññaɱ eka-dhammam pi samanupassāmi evaɱ-dāruṇaɱ emaɱ kaṭukaɱ evaɱ antarāya-karaɱ anuttarassa yoga-k-khemassa adigamāya yatha-yidaɱ bhikkhave Niraya-bandhanaɱ vā tiracchāna-yoni-bandhanaɱ vā, ti.|| ||

 

 

Dāḷiddiyaɱ dukkhaɱ loke iṇādānaɱ ca vuccati,||
daḷiddo iṇam-ādāya bhuñjamāno vihaññati.|| ||

Tato anucarantī naɱ bandhanam pi nigacchati.|| ||

Etaɱ hi bandhanaɱ dukkhaɱ kāma-lābh-ā-bhijappinaɱ.|| ||

Tath'eva ariya-vinaye saddhā yassa na vijjati,||
[354] Ahiriko anottāpi pāpa-kammā-vinicchayo|| ||

Kāya-du-c-caritaɱ katvā vacī-du-c-caritāni ca,||
Mano-du-c-caritaɱ katvā 'Mā maɱ jaññā' ti icchati.|| ||

So saɱsappati kāyena vācāya uda-cetasā,||
Pāpa-kammā pavaḍḍhento tattha tattha puna-p-punaɱ|| ||

So pāpa-kammo dummedho jānaɱ dukkaṭa-mattano,||
daḷiddo iṇam-ādāya bhuñjamāno vihaññati,|| ||

Tato anucarantī naɱ saŋkappā mānasā dukhā,||
Gāme vā yadi vā raññe yassa vippaṭi-sārajā.|| ||

So pāpa-kammo dummedho jānaɱ dukkaṭa-mattano,||
Yonim aññataraɱ gantvā Niraye vā pi bajjhati,||
Etaɱ hi bandhanaɱ dukkhaɱ yamhā dhīro pamuccati.|| ||

Dhamma-laddhehi bhogehi dadaɱ cittaɱ pasādayaɱ||
Ubhayattha kaṭa-g-gāho saddhassa ghara-mesino.|| ||

Diṭṭha-dhamma-hitatthāya samparāya sukhāya ca||
evam etaɱ gaha-ṭ-ṭhānaɱ cāgo puññaɱ pavaḍḍhati.|| ||

Tath'eva ariya-vinaye saddhā yassa patiṭṭhitā||
Hirimano ca ottapī paññavā sīla-saɱvuto.|| ||

Eso kho ariya-vinaye sukhaɱ-jīvīti vuccati||
Nirāmisaɱ sukhaɱ laddhā upekhaɱ adhitiṭṭati.|| ||

Pañca-nīvaraṇe hitvā niccaɱ āraddha-vīriyo||
Jhānāni upasampajja ekodi nipako sato.|| ||

Evaɱ ñatvā yathā-bhūtaɱ sabba-saŋyojana-k-khaye||
Sabbaso anupādāya sammā-cittaɱ vimuccati.|| ||

Tassa sammā vimuttassa ñāṇaɱ ca hoti tādino||
'Akuppā me vimuttī' ti bhava-saŋyojana-k-khaye.|| ||

Etaɱ kho paramaɱ ñāṇaɱ etaɱ sukha-m-anuttaraɱ||
Asokaɱ virajaɱ khemaɱ etam ānaṇyam uttaman ti.|| ||
