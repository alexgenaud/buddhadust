Aŋguttara Nikāya
Chakka-Nipāta
II: Sārāṇīya-Vagga

Sutta 18

Macchika Suttaɱ

Adapted from the 1995 edition of the digital version of the Sri Lanka Buddha Jayanti Tripitaka Series.

 

[301]

[1][pts] Evaɱ me sutaɱ:|| ||

Ekaɱ samayaɱ Bhagavā Kosalesu cārikaɱ carati mahatā bhikkhu saŋghena saddhiɱ.|| ||

Addasā kho Bhagavā addhāna-Magga-paṭipanno aññatarasmiɱ padese macchikaɱ maccha-bandhaɱ macche vadhitvā vadhitvā vikkiṇa-mānaɱ,||
disvā maggā ukkamma aññatarasmiɱ rukkha-mūle paññatte āsane nisīdi.|| ||

Nisajja kho Bhagavā bhikkhū āmantesi:|| ||

2. "Passatha, no tumhe bhikkhave amuɱ macchikaɱ maccha-bandhaɱ macche vadhitvā vadhitvā vikkiṇa-mānan" ti?|| ||

"Evaɱ bhante."|| ||

"Taɱ kiɱ maññatha bhikkhave||
api nu tumhehi||
diṭṭhaɱ vā||
sutaɱ vā:|| ||

'Macchiko macchabandho macche vadhitvā [302] vadhitvā vikkiṇamāno tena kammena tena ājivena||
hatthiyāyī vā||
assayāyī vā||
rathayāyīvā||
yānayāyī vā||
bhoga-bhogī vā||
mahantaɱ vā||
boga-k-khandhaɱ ajjhāvasanto' ti?|| ||

No h'etaɱ bhante.|| ||

Sādhu bhikkhave, mayā pi kho etaɱ bhikkhave||
n'eva diṭṭhaɱ,||
na sutaɱ:|| ||

'Macchiko macchabandho macche vadhitvā vadhitvā vikkiṇamāno tena kammena tena ājivena||
hatthiyāyī vā||
assayāyī vā||
rathayāyī vā||
yānayāyī vā||
bhoga-bhogī vā||
mahantaɱ vā||
bhoga-k-khandhaɱ ajjhāvasanto' ti.|| ||

Taɱ kissa hetu?|| ||

Te hi so bhikkhave macche vajjhe vadhāy'upanīte pāpakena manas-ā-nupekkhati,||
tasmā so n'eva hatthiyāyī hoti,||
na assayāyī,||
na rathayāyī,||
na yānayāyī,||
na bhoga-bhogī,||
na mahantaɱ bhoga-k-khandhaɱ ajjhāvasati.|| ||

■

Taɱ kiɱ maññatha bhikkhave api nu tumhehi||
diṭṭhaɱ vā||
sutaɱ vā:|| ||

'Goghātako gāvo vadhitvā vadhitvā vadhitvā vikkiṇamāno tena kammena tena ājivena||
hatthiyāyī vā||
assayāyī vā||
rathayāyī vā||
yānayāyī vā||
bhoga-bhogī vā||
mahantaɱ vā||
bhoga-k-khandhaɱ ajjhāvasanto' ti|| ||

"No h'etaɱ bhante"|| ||

"Sādhu bhikkhave, mayā pi kho etaɱ bhikkhave||
n'eva diṭṭhaɱ,||
na sutaɱ:|| ||

'Goghātako gāvo vadhitvā vadhitvā vadhitvā vikkiṇamāno tena kammena tena ājivena||
hatthiyāyī vā||
assayāyī vā||
rathayāyī vā||
yānayāyī vā||
bhoga-bhogī vā||
mahantaɱ vā||
bhoga-k-khandhaɱ ajjhāvasanto' ti|| ||

Taɱ kissa hetu?|| ||

Te hi so bhikkhave gāvo vajjhe vadhāy'upanīte pāpakena manas-ā-nupekkhati,||
tasmā so n'eva hatthiyāyī hoti,||
na assayāyī,||
na rathayāyī,||
na yānayāyī,||
na bhoga-bhogī,||
na mahantaɱ bhoga-k-khandhaɱ ajjhāvasati.|| ||

■

Taɱ kiɱ maññatha bhikkhave api nu tumhehi||
diṭṭhaɱ [303] vā||
sutaɱ vā:|| ||

'Orabbhiko orambhe vadhitvā vadhitvā vadhitvā vikkiṇamāno tena kammena tena ājivena||
hatthiyāyī vā||
assayāyī vā||
rathayāyī vā||
yānayāyī vā||
bhoga-bhogī vā||
mahantaɱ vā||
bhoga-k-khandhaɱ ajjhāvasanto' ti?|| ||

"No h'etaɱ bhante"|| ||

Sādhu bhikkhave, mayā pi kho etaɱ bhikkhave n'eva diṭṭhaɱ, na sutaɱ:|| ||

'Orabbhiko orambhe vadhitvā vadhitvā vadhitvā vikkiṇamāno tena kammena tena ājivena||
hatthiyāyī vā||
assayāyī vā||
rathayāyī vā||
yānayāyī vā||
bhoga-bhogī vā||
mahantaɱ vā||
bhoga-k-khandhaɱ ajjhāvasanto' ti.|| ||

Taɱ kissa hetu?|| ||

Te hi so bhikkhave orambhe vajjhe vadhāy'upanīte pāpakena manas-ā-nupekkhati,||
tasmā so n'eva hatthiyāyī hoti,||
na assayāyī,||
na rathayāyī,||
na yānayāyī,||
na bhoga-bhogī,||
na mahantaɱ bhoga-k-khandhaɱ ajjhāvasati.|| ||

Taɱ kiɱ maññatha bhikkhave api nu tumhehi||

mahantaɱ vā||

Sādhu bhikkhave, mayā pi kho etaɱ bhikkhave n'eva diṭṭhaɱ, na sutaɱ:|| ||

'Sūkariko sūkare vadhitvā vadhitvā vadhitvā vikkiṇamāno tena kammena tena ājivena||
hatthiyāyī vā||

'Sākuntiko sākunte vadhitvā vadhitvā vadhitvā vikkiṇamāno tena kammena tena ājivena||

'Sākuntiko sākunte vadhitvā vadhitvā vadhitvā vikkiṇamāno tena kammena tena ājivena||
hatthiyāyī vā||

na yānayāyī,||
na bhoga-bhogī,||

■

Taɱ kiɱ maññatha bhikkhave api nu tumhehi||
diṭṭhaɱ vā||
sutaɱ vā:|| ||

'Māgaviko mage vadhitvā vadhitvā vadhitvā vikkiṇamāno tena kammena tena ājivena||
hatthiyāyī vā||
assayāyī vā||
rathayāyī vā||
yānayāyī vā||
bhoga-bhogī vā||
mahantaɱ vā||
bhoga-k-khandhaɱ ajjhāvasanto' ti?|| ||

"No h'etaɱ bhante"|| ||

Sādhu bhikkhave, mayā pi kho etaɱ bhikkhave||
n'eva diṭṭhaɱ,||
na sutaɱ:|| ||

'Māgaviko mage vadhitvā vadhitvā vadhitvā vikkiṇamāno tena kammena tena ājivena||

Taɱ kissa hetu?|| ||

Te hi so bhikkhave mage vajjhe vadhāy'upanīte pāpakena manas-ā-nupekkhati,||
tasmā so n'eva hatthiyāyī hoti,||
na assayāyī,||
na rathayāyī,||
na yānayāyī,||
na bhoga-bhogī,||
na mahantaɱ bhoga-k-khandhaɱ ajjhāvasati.|| ||

■

Te hi so bhikkhave tiracchānagate pāṇe vajjhe vadhāy'upanīte pāpakena manas-ā-nupekkha-māno||
n'eva hatthiyāyī bhavissati,||
na assayāyī,||
na rathayāyī,||
na yānayāyī,||
na bhoga-bhogī,||
na mahantaɱ bhoga-k-khandhaɱ ajjhāvasissati.|| ||

Ko pana vādo yaɱ manussa-bhutaɱ vajjhaɱ vadhāy'upanītaɱ pāpakena manas-ā-nupekkhatī,||
taɱ hi'ssa bhikkhave hoti dīgha-rattaɱ ahitāya dukkhāya,||
kāyassa bhedā param maraṇā apāyaɱ duggatiɱ vinipātaɱ Nirayaɱ upapajjatī" ti.|| ||
