Aŋguttara Nikāya
Chakka Nipāta
V. Dhammika Vagga

Sutta 48

Dutiya Sandiṭṭhika Suttaɱ

Adapted from the 1995 edition of the digital version of the Sri Lanka Buddha Jayanti Tripitaka Series.

 

[357]

[1][pts][olds] Evaɱ me sutaɱ:|| ||

Ekaɱ samayaɱ Bhagavā Sāvatthiyaɱ viharati||
Jetavane Anāthapiṇḍikassa ārāme.|| ||

Atha kho Moliyasīvako paribbājako yena Bhagavā ten'upasankami.|| ||

Upasaŋkamitvā Bhagavatā saddhiɱ sammodi.|| ||

Sammodanīyaɱ kathaɱ sārāṇīyaɱ vitīsāretvā eka-m-antaɱ nisidi.|| ||

Eka-m-antaɱ nisinno kho Moliyasīvako paribbājako Bhagavantaɱ etad avoca:|| ||

"Sandiṭṭhiko dhammo,||
sandiṭṭhiko dhammo,||
ti bho Gotama vuccati.|| ||

Kittāvatā nu kho bho Gotama [358] sandiṭṭhiko dhammo hoti,||
akāliko ehi passiko opanayiko paccattaɱ veditabbo viñuhi" ti?|| ||

2. "Tena hi brāhmaṇa tañ ñ'ev'ettha paṭipucchissāmi.|| ||

Yathā te khameyya,||
tathā naɱ vyākareyyāsi.|| ||

■

Taɱ kiɱ maññasi brāhmaṇa,||
santaɱ vā ajjhattaɱ rāgaɱ:||
'Atthi me ajjhattaɱ rāgao' ti pajānāsi,||
asantaɱ vā ajjhattaɱ rāgaɱ||
"N'atthi me ajjhattaɱ rāgao' ti pajānāsī" ti?|| ||

"Evaɱ bho Gotama."|| ||

"Yaɱ kho tvaɱ brāhmaṇa santaɱ vā ajjhattaɱ rāgaɱ||
'Atthi me ajjhattaɱ rāgao' ti pajānāsi,||
asantaɱ vā ajjhattaɱ rāgaɱ||
'N'atthi me ajjhattaɱ rāgao' ti pajānāsi:|| ||

Evaɱ kho brāhmaṇa sandiṭṭhiko dhammo hoti,||
akāliko ehi passiko opanayiko paccattaɱ veditabbo viññūhī ti.|| ||

■

Taɱ kiɱ maññasi brāhmaṇa,||
santaɱ vā ajjhattaɱ dosaɱ:||
'Atthi me ajjhattaɱ doso' ti pajānāsi,||
asantaɱ vā ajjhattaɱ dosaɱ||
"N'atthi me ajjhattaɱ doso' ti pajānāsī" ti?|| ||

"Evaɱ bho Gotama."|| ||

"Yaɱ kho tvaɱ brāhmaṇa santaɱ vā ajjhattaɱ dosaɱ||
'Atthi me ajjhattaɱ doso' ti pajānāsi,||
asantaɱ vā ajjhattaɱ dosaɱ||
'N'atthi me ajjhattaɱ doso' ti pajānāsi:|| ||

Evaɱ kho brāhmaṇa sandiṭṭhiko dhammo hoti,||
akāliko ehi passiko opanayiko paccattaɱ veditabbo viññūhī ti.|| ||

■

Taɱ kiɱ maññasi brāhmaṇa,||
santaɱ vā ajjhattaɱ mohaɱ:||
'Atthi me ajjhattaɱ moho' ti pajānāsi,||
asantaɱ vā ajjhattaɱ mohaɱ||
"N'atthi me ajjhattaɱ moho' ti pajānāsī" ti?|| ||

"Evaɱ bho Gotama."|| ||

"Yaɱ kho tvaɱ brāhmaṇa santaɱ vā ajjhattaɱ mohaɱ||
'Atthi me ajjhattaɱ moho' ti pajānāsi,||
asantaɱ vā ajjhattaɱ mohaɱ||
'N'atthi me ajjhattaɱ moho' ti pajānāsi:|| ||

Evaɱ kho brāhmaṇa sandiṭṭhiko dhammo hoti,||
akāliko ehi passiko opanayiko paccattaɱ veditabbo viññūhī ti.|| ||

 

§

Abhikkantaɱ bho Gotama abhikkantaɱ bho Gotama,||
seyyathā pi bho Gotama nikkujjitaɱ vā ukkajjeyya,||
paṭi-c-channaɱ vā vivareyya,||
mūḷhassa vā Maggaɱ ācikkheyya,||
andha-kāre vā tela-pajjotaɱ dhāreyya,||
"cakkhu-manto rūpāni dakkhinti, ti,||
evam'eva'hotā Gotamena aneka-pariyāyena dhammo pakāsito.|| ||

Es'āhaɱ bhavantaɱ Gotamaɱ saraṇaɱ gacchāmi Dhammañ ca bhikkhu Saŋghañ ca.|| ||

Upāsakaɱ maɱ bho Gotama Bhagavā dhāretu ajja-t-agge pāṇupetaɱ saraṇaɱ gatan ti.|| ||
