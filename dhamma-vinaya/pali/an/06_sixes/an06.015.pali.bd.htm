Aŋguttara Nikāya
Chakka-Nipāta
II: Sārāṇīya-Vagga

Sutta 15

Anutappa Suttaɱ

Adapted from the 1995 edition of the digital version of the Sri Lanka Buddha Jayanti Tripitaka Series.

 

[294]

[1][pts] Evaɱ me sutaɱ:|| ||

Ekaɱ samayaɱ Sāriputto Sāvatthiyaɱ viharati.|| ||

Tatra kho Sāriputto bhikkhu āmantesi:|| ||

"Āvuso bhikkhavo" ti.|| ||

"Āvuso" ti te bhikkhū āyasmato Sāriputtassa paccassosuɱ.|| ||

Āyasmā Sāriputto etad avoca:|| ||

2. "Tathā tathā āvuso bhikkhu vihāraɱ kappeti,||
yathā yathā'ssa vihāraɱ kappayato kāla-kiriyā anutappā hoti.|| ||

3. Kathañ c'āvuso bhikkhu tathā tathā vihāraɱ kappeti,||
yathā yathā'ssa vihāraɱ kappayato,||
kāla-kiriyā anutappā hoti?|| ||

Idh'āvuso bhikkhu kamm'ārāmo hoti||
kamma-rato kamm'ārāmataɱ anuyutto,||
bhass'ārāmo hoti||
bhassa-rato bhass-ā-rāmataɱ anuyutto,||
niddā-rāmo hoti||
niddā-rato nidd-ā-rāmataɱ anuyutto,||
sangaṇ'ikā-rāmo hoti||
sangaṇ'ikā-rato sangaṇ'ikā-rāmataɱ anuyutto,||
saŋsagg'ārāmo hoti||
saŋsagga-rato saŋsagg'ārāmataɱ anuyutto,||
papañc'ārāmo hoti||
papañca-rato papañc'ārāmataɱ anuyutetā.|| ||

Evaɱ kho āvuso bhikkhu tathā tathā vihāraɱ kappeti,||
yathā yathā'ssa vihāraɱ kappayato||
kāla-kiriyā anutappā hoti.|| ||

Ayaɱ vuccat'āvuso bhikkhū sakkāy-ā-bhirato,||
na pahāsi sakkāyaɱ sammā dukkhassa antakiriyāya.|| ||

 

 

4. Tathā tathāvuso bhikkhu vihāraɱ kappeti,||
yathā yathā'ssa vihāraɱ kappayato||
kāla-kiriyā ananutappā hoti.|| ||

Kathañ c'āvuso bhikkhu tathā tathā vihāraɱ kappeti,||
yathā yathāssa vihāraɱ kappayato||
bhaddakaɱ maraṇaɱ hoti||
kāla-kiriyā ananutappā hoti?|| ||

Idh'āvuso bhikkhu na kamm'ārāmo hoti||
na kamma-rato||
na kamm'ārāmataɱ anuyutto,||
na bhassāramo hoti||
na bhassa-rato||
na bhass'āramataɱ anuyutto,||
na niddā-rāmo hoti,||
na niddā-rato||
[295] na nidd-ā-rāmataɱ anuyutto,||
na sangaṇ'ikā-rāmo hoti||
na sangaṇ'ikā-rato||
na sangaṇ'ikā-rāmataɱ anuyutto,||
na saŋsagg'ārāmo hoti||
na saŋsagga-rato||
na saŋsagg'ārāmataɱ anuyutto,||
na papañc'ārāmo hoti||
na papañca-rato||
na papañc'ārāmataɱ anuyutto.|| ||

Evaɱ kho āvuso bhikkhu tathā tathāssa vihāraɱ kappeti yathā yathā'ssa vihāraɱ kappayato||
kāla-kiriyā ananutappā hoti.|| ||

Ayaɱ vuccat'āvuso bhikkhu nibbān-ā-bhirato pahāsi sakkāyaɱ sammā-dukkhassa antakiriyāyā" ti.|| ||

Yo papañcaɱ anuyutto papañc-ā-bhirato mago||
Virādhayī so Nibbānaɱ yoga-k-khemaɱ anuttaraɱ.|| ||

Yo ca papañcaɱ hitvāna ni-p-papañca-pade rato||
Ārādhayī so Nibbānaɱ yoga-k-khemaɱ anuttaran ti.|| ||
