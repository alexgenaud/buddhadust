Sutta 119

Tapussa suttaɱ

[1][pts][olds] EVAṂ ME SUTAṂ|| ||

Sāvatthi nidānaɱ|| ||

Chahi bhikkhave dhammehi samannāgato Tapusso.|| ||

Gahapati Tathāgate niṭṭhaɱ-gato amata-d-daso amataɱ sacchi-katvā irīyati.|| ||

Katamehi chahi?|| ||

[451] 2. Buddhe avecca-p-pasādena,||
dhamme avecca-p-pasādena,||
saŋghe avecca-p-pasādena,||
ariyena sīlena,||
ariyena ñāṇena,||
ariyāya vimuttiyā.|| ||

Imehi kho bhikkhave chahi dhammehi samannāgato Tapusso gahapati Tathāgate niṭṭhaɱ gato amata-d-daso amataɱ sacchi-katvā irīyatī ti.|| ||
