Aŋguttara Nikāya
Chakka-Nipāta
I. Āhuneyya-Vagga

Sutta 3

Indriya Suttaɱ

Adapted from the 1995 edition of the digital version of the Sri Lanka Buddha Jayanti Tripitaka Series.

 

[281]

[1][pts] Evaɱ me sutaɱ:|| ||

Ekaɱ samayaɱ Bhagavā Sāvatthiyaɱ viharati||
Jetavane Anāthapiṇḍikassa ārāme.|| ||

Tatra kho Bhagavā bhikkhu āmantesi:|| ||

"Bhikkhavo" ti.|| ||

"Bhadante" ti te bhikkhū Bhagavato paccassosuɱ.|| ||

Bhagavā etad avoca:|| ||

2. "Chahi bhikkhave dhammehi samannāgato bhikkhu āhuneyyo hoti||
pāhuneyyo||
dakkhiṇeyyo||
añjali-karaṇīyo||
anuttaraɱ puñña-k-khettaɱ lokassa.|| ||

Katamehi chahi?|| ||

[282] 3. Saddh'indriyena;||
viriy'indriyena;||
sat'indriyena;||
samādh'indriyena;||
paññ'indriyena;||
āsavānaɱ khayā,||
anāsavaɱ ceto vimuttiɱ,||
paññā-vimuttiɱ,||
diṭṭhe'va dhamme||
sayaɱ abhiññā sacchi-katvā,||
upasampajja viharati.|| ||

Imehi kho bhikkhave chahi dhammehi samannāgato bhikkhu āhuneyyo hoti||
pāhuneyyo||
dakkhiṇeyyo||
añjali-karaṇīyo||
anuttaraɱ puñña-k-khettaɱ lokassa" ti.|| ||
