Aŋguttara Nikāya
Chakka Nipāta
VII. Devatā Vagga

Sutta 65

Anāgāmi-Phala Suttaɱ

Adapted from the 1995 edition of the digital version of the Sri Lanka Buddha Jayanti Tripitaka Series.

 

[421]

[1][pts][olds] Evaɱ me sutaɱ:|| ||

Ekaɱ samayaɱ Bhagavā Sāvatthiyaɱ viharati||
Jetavane Anāthapiṇḍikassa ārāme.|| ||

Tatra kho Bhagavā bhikkhu āmantesi:|| ||

"Bhikkhavo" ti.|| ||

"Bhadante" ti te bhikkhū Bhagavato paccassosuɱ.|| ||

Bhagavā etad avoca:|| ||

2. "Cha bhikkhave dhamme a-p-pahāya abhabbo Anāgāmi-phalaɱ sacchi-kātuɱ.|| ||

Katame cha?|| ||

A-s-saddhiyaɱ,||
ahirikaɱ,||
anottappaɱ,||
kosajjaɱ,||
muṭṭha-saccaɱ,||
duppaññataɱ.|| ||

Ime kho bhikkhave cha dhamme a-p-pahāya abhabbo Anāgāmi-phalaɱ sacchi-kātuɱ.|| ||

 

 

Cha bhikkhave dhamme pahāya bhabbo Anāgāmi-phalaɱ sacchi-kātuɱ.|| ||

Katame cha?|| ||

A-s-saddhiyaɱ,||
ahirikaɱ,||
anottappaɱ,||
kosajjaɱ,||
muṭṭha-saccaɱ,||
duppaññataɱ.|| ||

Ime kho bhikkhave cha dhamme pahāya bhabbo Anāgāmi-phalaɱ sacchi-kātun" ti.|| ||
