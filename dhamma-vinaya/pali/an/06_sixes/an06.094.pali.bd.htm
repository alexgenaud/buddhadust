Aŋguttara Nikāya
Chakka Nipāta
IX. Sīti Vagga

Sutta 94

Tatiya Abhabba-ṭ-Ṭhāna Suttaɱ

Adapted from the 1995 edition of the digital version of the Sri Lanka Buddha Jayanti Tripitaka Series.

 

[439]

[1][pts] Evaɱ me sutaɱ:|| ||

Ekaɱ samayaɱ Bhagavā Sāvatthiyaɱ viharati||
Jetavane Anāthapiṇḍikassa ārāme.|| ||

Tatra kho Bhagavā bhikkhu āmantesi:|| ||

"Bhikkhavo" ti.|| ||

"Bhadante" ti te bhikkhū Bhagavato paccassosuɱ.|| ||

Bhagavā etad avoca:|| ||

2. "Cha yimānī bhikkhave abhabba-ṭ-ṭhānāni.|| ||

Katamāni cha?|| ||

Abhabbo diṭṭhi-sampanno puggalo mātaraɱ jīvitā voropetuɱ,||
abhabbo diṭṭhi-sampanno puggalo pītaraɱ jīvitā voropetuɱ,||
abhabbo diṭṭhi-sampanno puggalo arahantaɱ jivitā voropetuɱ,||
abhabbo diṭṭhi-sampanno puggalo Tathāgatassa duṭṭhena cittena lohitaɱ uppādetuɱ,||
abhabbo diṭṭhi-sampanno puggalo Saŋghaɱ bhindituɱ,||
abhabbo diṭṭhi-sampanno puggalo aññaɱ Satthāraɱ uddisituɱ.|| ||

Imānī kho bhikkhave cha abhabba-ṭ-ṭhānānī" ti.|| ||
