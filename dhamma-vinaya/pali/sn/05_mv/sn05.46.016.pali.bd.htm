Saɱyutta Nikāya,
V: Mahā-Vagga
46. Bojjhanga Saɱyutta
II. Gilāna-Vaggo

Sutta 16

Tatiya Gilāna Suttaɱ

Adapted from the 1995 edition of the digital version of the Sri Lanka Buddha Jayanti Tripitaka Series.

 

 

[1][pts][bodh][piya] Evam me sutaɱ:|| ||

[81] Ekaɱ samayam Bhagavā Rājagahe viharati veluvane Kalandakanivāpe.|| ||

Tena kho pana samayena Bhagavā ābādhiko hoti dukkhito bāḷha-gilāno.|| ||

Atha kho āyasmā Mahā Cundo yena Bhagavā ten'upasankami,||
upasankamitvā Bhagavantaɱ abhivādetvā eka-m-antaɱ nisīdi.|| ||

Eka-m-antaɱ nisinnaɱ kho āyasmantaɱ Mahā Cundaɱ Bhagavā etad avoca:|| ||

"Paṭibhantu taɱ Cunda, bojjhaŋgā" ti.|| ||

"Satt'ime bhante bojjhaŋgā Bhagavatā sammadakkhātā bhāvitā bahulī-katā abhiññāya sambodhāya nibbānāya saŋvattanti.|| ||

Katame satta?|| ||

Sati-sambojjhaŋgo kho bhante, Bhagavatā sammadakkhāto bhāvito bahulī-kato.|| ||

Abhiññāya sambodhāya nibbānāya saŋvattati.|| ||

Dhamma-vicaya-sambojjhaŋgo kho bhante, Bhagavatā sammadakkhāto bhāvito bahulī-kato.|| ||

Abhiññāya sambodhāya nibbānāya saŋvattati.|| ||

Viriya-sambojjhaŋgo kho bhante, Bhagavatā sammadakkhāto bhāvito bahulī-kato.|| ||

Abhiññāya sambodhāya nibbānāya saŋvattati.|| ||

Pīti-sambojjhaŋgo kho bhante, Bhagavatā sammadakkhāto bhāvito bahulī-kato.|| ||

Abhiññāya sambodhāya nibbānāya saŋvattati.|| ||

Passaddhi-sambojjhaŋgo kho bhante, Bhagavatā sammadakkhāto bhāvito bahulī-kato.|| ||

Abhiññāya sambodhāya nibbānāya saŋvattati.|| ||

Samādhi-sambojjhaŋgo kho bhante, Bhagavatā sammadakkhāto bhāvito bahulī-kato.|| ||

Abhiññāya sambodhāya nibbānāya saŋvattati.|| ||

Upekhā-sambojjhaŋgo kho bhante, Bhagavatā sammadakkhāto bhāvito bahulī-kato.|| ||

Abhiññāya sambodhāya nibbānāya saŋvattati.|| ||

Ime kho bhante, satta bojjhaŋgā Bhagavatā sammadakkhānā bhāvitā bahulī-katā.|| ||

Abhiññāya sambodhāya nibbānāya saŋvattantī" ti.|| ||

"Taggha Cunda, bojjhaŋgā!|| ||

Taggha Cunda, bojjhaŋgā" ti!|| ||

Idam avoc'āyasmā Mahā Cundo||
samanuñño Satthā ahosi.|| ||

Vuṭṭhāhi ca Bhagavā tamhā ābādhā.|| ||

Tathā pahīno ca Bhagavato so ābādho ahosīti.|| ||
