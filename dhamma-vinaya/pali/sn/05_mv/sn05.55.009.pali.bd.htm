55. Sot'āpatti Saɱyutta
I. Veḷudvāra Vagga

Sutta 9

Dutiya Giñjakāvasatha Suttaɱ

Adapted from the 1995 edition of the digital version of the Sri Lanka Buddha Jayanti Tripitaka Series.

 

 

[1][pts] Evam me sutaɱ:|| ||

Ekaɱ samayaɱ Bhagavā Ñātike viharati Giñjakāvasathe.|| ||

Atha kho āyasmā Ānando yena Bhagavā ten'upasankami.|| ||

Upasaŋkamitvā Bhagavantaɱ abhivādetvā eka-m-antaɱ nisīdi.|| ||

Eka-m-antaɱ nisinno kho āyasmā Ānando Bhagavantaɱ etad avoca:|| ||

"Asoko nāma bhante, bhikkhu kāla-kato.|| ||

Tassa kā gati,||
ko abhisamparāyo?|| ||

■

Asokī nāma bhante, bhikkhunī kāla-katā.|| ||

Tassā kā gati,||
ko abhisamparāyo?|| ||

 

 

Asoko Ānanda, bhikkhu kāla-kato,||
āsavānaɱ khayā||
anāsavaɱ||
ceto-vimuttiɱ||
paññā-vimuttiɱ||
diṭṭhe'va dhamme sayaɱ abhiññā sacci-katvā||
upasampajja vihāsi.|| ||

■

Asokī Ānanda, bhikkhunī kāla-katā||
pañcannaɱ ora-m-bhāgiyānaɱ saŋyojanānaɱ parikkhayā||
opapātikā tattha parinibbāyinī||
anāvatti-dhammā tasmā-lokā.|| ||

Anacchariyaɱ kho pan'etaɱ Ānanda,||
yaɱ manussabhūto kālaɱ kareyya.|| ||

Tasmiɱ tasmiɱ ce maɱ kāla-kate upasankamitvā||
etam atthaɱ paṭipucjissatha.|| ||

Vihesā p'esā Ānanda, assa Tathāgatassa.|| ||

Tasmā ti h'Ānanda, dhammādāsaŋ nāma dhamma-pariyāyaɱ desissāmi.|| ||

Yena samannāgato ariya-sāvako ākaŋkha-māno attanā va attānaɱ vyākareyya:|| ||

'Khīṇa-Nirayo'mhi||
khīṇa-tiracchāna-yoniko||
khīṇa-petti-visayo||
khīṇā-pāya-duggati-vipāto.|| ||

Sot'āpanno'ham asmi||
avinipāta-dhammo||
niyato||
sambodhi-parāyano'|| ||

Katamo ca so Ānanda, dhammādāso dhamma-pariyāyo yena samannāgato ariya-sāvako ākaŋkha-māno attanāva attānaɱ vyākareyya:|| ||

'Khīṇa-Nirayo'mhi||
khīṇa-tiracchāna-yoniko||
khīṇa-petti-visayo||
khīṇā-pāya-duggati-vipāto.|| ||

Sot'āpanno'ham asmi||
avinipāta-dhammo||
niyato||
sambodhi-parāyano'?|| ||

idh'Ānanda, ariya-sāvako buddhe avecca pasādena samannāgato hoti:|| ||

"Iti pi so Bhagavā||
arahaɱ||
Sammā-Sam-Buddho||

loka-vidu||

Buddho||
Bhagavā" ti.|| ||

'Svākkhāto Bhagavatā dhammo||
sandiṭṭhiko||
akāliko||
ehi passiko||
opanayiko||
paccattaɱ veditabbo viññūhī" ti.|| ||

"Su-paṭipanno Bhagavato sāvaka-saŋgho,||
uju-paṭipanno Bhagavato sāvaka-saŋgho,||
ñāya-paṭipanno Bhagavato sāvaka-saŋgho,||
sāmīci-paṭipanno Bhagavato sāvaka-saŋgho,||
yad idaɱ cattāri purisa-yugāni||

esa Bhagavato sāvaka-saŋgho.|| ||

Āhuneyyo||

Ayaɱ kho so Ānanda, dhammādāso dhamma-pariyāyo yena samannāgato ariya-sāvako ākaŋkha-māno attanāva attānaɱ vyākareyya:|| ||
