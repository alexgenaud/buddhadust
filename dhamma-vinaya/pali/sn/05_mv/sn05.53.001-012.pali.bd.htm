Saɱyutta Nikāya,
V: Mahā-Vagga
53.Jhāna Saɱyutta

Chapter I
Suttas 1-12

Gaŋgāpeyyāla

Adapted from the 1995 edition of the digital version of the Sri Lanka Buddha Jayanti Tripitaka Series.

 

 

Sutta 1

Gangā Nadi Pācīnaninnā Suttaɱ

[1][pts] Evam me sutaɱ:|| ||

Cattāro'me bhikkhave, jhānā.|| ||

Katame cattāro?|| ||

Idha bhikkhave, bhikkhu||
vivicc'eva kāmehi||
vivicca akusalehi dhammehi||
sa-vitakkaɱ sa-vicāraɱ||
viveka-jaɱ pīti-sukhaɱ||
paṭhamaɱ jhānaɱ upasampajja viharati.|| ||

Vitakka-vicārānaɱ vūpasamā||
ajjhattaɱ sampasādanaɱ||
cetaso ekodi-bhāvaɱ||
avitakkaɱ avicāraɱ||
samādhi-jaɱ pīti-sukhaɱ||
dutiyaɱ jhānaɱ upasampajja viharati.|| ||

Pītiyā ca virāgā||
upekhako ca viharati,||
sato ca sampajāno||
sukhañ ca kāyena paṭisaŋvedeti.|| ||

Yaɱ taɱ ariyā ācikkhanti||
'upekhako satimā sukha-vihārī' ti||
tatiyaɱ jhānaɱ upasampajja viharati.|| ||

Sukhassa ca pahānā||
dukkhassa ca pahānā||
pubbe va somanassa-domanassānaɱ attha-gamā||
adukkhaɱ asukhaɱ upekhā-sati-pārisuddhiɱ||
catutthaɱ jhānaɱ upasampajja viharati.|| ||

Ime kho bhikkhave, cattāro jhānā.|| ||

Seyyathā pi bhikkhave Gaŋgā nadī pācīna-ninnā||
pācīna-poṇā||
pācīna-pabbhārā,||
evam eva kho bhikkhave bhikkhu cattāro jhānā bhāvento||
cattāro jhānā bahulī-karonto||
Nibbāna-ninno hoti||
Nibbāna-poṇo||
Nibbānapabbhāro.|| ||

Kathañ ca bhikkhave bhikkhu cattāro jhānā bhāvento cattāro jhānā bahulī-karonto||
Nibbāna-ninno hoti||
Nibbāna-poṇo||
Nibbānapabbhāro?|| ||

Idha bhikkhave, bhikkhu||
vivicc'eva kāmehi||
vivicca akusalehi dhammehi||
sa-vitakkaɱ sa-vicāraɱ||
viveka-jaɱ pīti-sukhaɱ||
paṭhamaɱ jhānaɱ upasampajja viharati.|| ||

Vitakka-vicārānaɱ vūpasamā||
ajjhattaɱ sampasādanaɱ||
cetaso ekodi-bhāvaɱ||
avitakkaɱ avicāraɱ||
samādhi-jaɱ pīti-sukhaɱ||
dutiyaɱ jhānaɱ upasampajja viharati.|| ||

Pītiyā ca virāgā||
upekhako ca viharati,||
sato ca sampajāno||
sukhañ ca kāyena paṭisaŋvedeti.|| ||

Yaɱ taɱ ariyā ācikkhanti||
'upekhako satimā sukha-vihārī' ti||
tatiyaɱ jhānaɱ upasampajja viharati.|| ||

Sukhassa ca pahānā||
dukkhassa ca pahānā||
pubbe va somanassa-domanassānaɱ attha-gamā||
adukkhaɱ asukhaɱ upekhā-sati-pārisuddhiɱ||
catutthaɱ jhānaɱ upasampajja viharati. || ||

Evaɱ kho bhikkhave bhikkhu cattāro jhānā bhāvento||
cattāro jhānā bahulī-karonto||
Nibbāna-ninno hoti||
Nibbāna-poṇo||
Nibbānapabbhāro ti.|| ||
