Adapted from the 1995 edition of the digital version of the Sri Lanka Buddha Jayanti Tripitaka Series.

 

 

[1][pts][bodh] Evam me sutaɱ:|| ||

Ekaɱ samayam Bhagavā Sāvatthiyaɱ viharati Jetavane Anāthapindikassa ārāme.|| ||

Cattāro me bhikkhave, sati-paṭṭhānā bhāvitā bahulī-katā ariyā niyyānikā niyyanti takkarassa sammā dukkha-k-khayāya.|| ||

Katame cattāro?

Idha bhikkhave, bhikkhu kāye kāy'ānupassī viharati||
ātāpī||
sampajāno||
satimā||
vineyya loke abhijjhā-domanassaɱ.|| ||

Vedanāsu vedan'ānupassī viharati||
ātāpī||
sampajāno||
satimā||
vineyya loke abhijjhā-domanassaɱ.|| ||

Citte citt'ānupassī viharati||
ātāpī||
sampajāno||
satimā||
vineyya loke abhijjhā-domanassaɱ.|| ||

Dhammesu Dhamm'ānupassī viharati||
ātāpī||
sampajāno||
satimā||
vineyya loke abhijjhā-domanassaɱ.|| ||

[167] Ime kho bhikkhave, cattāro sati-paṭṭhānā bhāvitā bahulī-katā ariyā niyyānikā niyyanti takkarassa dukkha-k-khayāyā" ti.|| ||
