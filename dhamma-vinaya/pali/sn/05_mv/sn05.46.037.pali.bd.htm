Saɱyutta Nikāya,
V: Mahā-Vagga
46. Bojjhanga Saɱyutta
IV. Nīvaraṇa-Vaggo

Sutta 37

Vuddhi (or Aparihāni?)

Adapted from the 1995 edition of the digital version of the Sri Lanka Buddha Jayanti Tripitaka Series.

 

 

[1][pts][bodh] Evam me sutaɱ:|| ||

Ekaɱ samayam Bhagavā bhikkhū āmantesi:|| ||

"Bhikkhavo" ti.|| ||

"Bhadante" ti te bhikkhū Bhagavato paccassosuɱ.|| ||

Bhagavā etad avoca:|| ||

"Satt'ime bhikkhave, bojjhaŋgā bhāvitā||
bahulī-katā||
buddhiyā aparihānāya saŋvattanti.|| ||

Katame satta?|| ||

Sati-sambojjhaŋgo||
dhamma-vicaya-sambojjhaŋgo||
viriya-sambojjhaŋgo||
pīti-sambojjhaŋgo||
passaddhi-sambojjhaŋgo||
samādhi-sambojjhaŋgo||
upekhā-sambojjhaŋgo.

Ime kho bhikkhave, satta bojjhaŋgā bhāvitā||
bahulī-katā||
buddhiyā aparihānāya saŋvattantī" ti.|| ||
