Saɱyutta Nikāya,
V: Mahā-Vagga
54 Ānāpāna Saɱyutta

1. Eka-dhammavaggo

Sutta 5

Dutiya Phala Suttaɱ

Adapted from the 1995 edition of the digital version of the Sri Lanka Buddha Jayanti Tripitaka Series.

 

 

[1-2][pts][bodh][olds] Ānāpāna-sati bhikkhave bhāvitā bahulī-katā maha-p-phalā hoti mahā-nisaɱsā.||
Kathaɱ bhāvitā ca bhikkhave ānāpāna-sati kathaɱ bahulī-katā maha-p-phalā hoti mahā-nisaɱsā.|| ||

[4] Idha, bhikkhave, bhikkhū araññagato vā rukkha-mūla-gato vā suññāragato vā nisīdati pallaŋkaɱ ābhujitvā ujuɱ kāyaɱ paṇidhāya parimukhaɱ satiɱ upaṭṭha-petvā so sato va assa-sati sato va passa-sati.|| ||

[5] Dīghaɱ vā assa-santo dīghaɱ assasāmīti pajānāti.||
Dīghaɱ vā passa-santo dīghaɱ passasāmīti pajānāti.|| ||

Rassaɱ vā assa-santo rassaɱ assasāmīti pajānāti.||
Rassaɱ vā passa-santo rassaɱ passasāmīti pajānāti.|| ||

[6] Sabba-kāyapaṭisaŋvedī assasi-s-sāmīti sikkhati.||
Sabba-kāyapaṭisaŋvedī passissāmīti sikkhati.|| ||

Passam-bhayaɱ kāya-sankhāraɱ assasi-s-sāmīti sikkhati.||
Passam-bhayaɱ kāya-sankhāraɱ passasi-s-sāmīti sikkhati.|| ||

[7] Pīti-paṭisaŋvedī assasi-s-sāmīti sikkhati.||
Pīti-paṭisaŋvedī passasi-s-sāmīti sikkhati.|| ||

Sukha-paṭisaŋvedī assasi-s-sāmīti sikkhati.||
Sukha-paṭisaŋvedī passasi-s-sāmīti sikkhati.|| ||

Citta-sankhāra-paṭisaŋvedī assasi-s-sāmīti sikkhati.||
Citta-sankhāra-paṭisaŋvedī passasi-s-sāmīti sikkhati.|| ||

Passam-bhayaɱ citta-sankhāraɱ assasi-s-sāmīti sikkhati.||
Passam-bhayaɱ citta-sankhāraɱ passasi-s-sāmīti sikkhati.|| ||

[8] Citta-sankhāra-paṭisaŋvedī assasi-s-sāmīti sikkhati.||
Citta-sankhāra-paṭisaŋvedī passasi-s-sāmīti sikkhati.|| ||

Passam-bhayaɱ citta-sankhāraɱ assasi-s-sāmīti sikkhati.||
Passam-bhayaɱ citta-sankhāraɱ passasi-s-sāmīti sikkhati.|| ||

Citta-paṭisaŋvedī assasi-s-sāmīti sikkhati.||
Citta-paṭisaŋvedī passasi-s-sāmīti sikkhati.|| ||

[9] Abhi-p-pamodayaɱ cittaɱ assasi-s-sāmīti sikkhati.||
Abhi-p-pamodayaɱ cittaɱ passasi-s-sāmīti sikkhati.|| ||

Samādahaɱ cittaɱ assasi-s-sāmīti sikkhati.||
Samādahaɱ cittaɱ passasi-s-sāmīti sikkhati.|| ||

Vimocayaɱ cittaɱ assasi-s-sāmīti sikkhati.||
Vimocayaɱ cittaɱ passasi-s-sāmīti sikkhati.|| ||

[10] Anicc-ā-nupassī assasi-s-sāmīti sikkhati.||
Anicc-ā-nupassī passasi-s-sāmīti sikkhati.|| ||

Virāg-ā-nupassī assasi-s-sāmīti sikkhati.||
Virāg-ā-nupassī passasi-s-sāmīti sikkhati.|| ||

Nirodh-ā-nupassī assasi-s-sāmīti sikkhati.||
Nirodh-ā-nupassī passasi-s-sāmīti sikkhati.|| ||

Paṭinissagg-ā-nupassī passasi-s-sāmīti sikkhati.||
Paṭinissagg-ā-nupassī passasi-s-sāmīti sikkhati.|| ||

[11] Evaɱ bhāvitā kho bhikkhave, ānāpāna-sati evaɱ bahu'īkatā maha-p-phalā hoti mahā-nisaɱsā.|| ||

[12] Evam bhāvitāya kho bhikkhave ānāpāna-satiyā evam bahu'īkatāya satta phalā sattānisaɱsā paṭikaṇkhās katame satta phalā sattānisaɱsā:|| ||

[13] Diṭṭheva dhamme paṭigacca aññaɱ ārādheti.|| ||

No ce diṭṭhe'va dhamme paṭigacca aññaɱ ārādheti,||
atha maraṇa-kāle aññaɱ ārādheti.|| ||

No ce diṭṭhe'va dhamme paṭigacca aññaɱ ārādheti, no ce maraṇa-kāle aññaɱ ārādheti||
atha pañcantaɱ ora-m-bhāgiyānaɱ saŋyojanānaɱ parikkhayā antarā parinibbāyī hoti.|| ||

Upahacca-parinibbāyī hoti.|| ||

Asankhāra-parinibbāyī hoti.|| ||

Sasankhāra-parinibbāyī hoti.|| ||

Uddhaɱsoto hoti Akaniṭṭha-gāmī.|| ||

[14] Evaɱ bhāvitāya kho bhikkhave, ānāpāna-satiyā evaɱ bahulī-katāya ime sattaphalā sattānisaɱsā pāṭikaŋkhā ti.|| ||
