Saɱyutta Nikāya
5. Mahā-Vagga
54. Ānāpāna Saɱyutta
1. Eka-Dhamma Vagga

Sutta 5

Dutiya Phalā Suttaɱ

Adapted from the 1995 edition of the digital version of the Sri Lanka Buddha Jayanti Tripitaka Series.

 

[314]

[1-2][pts][bodh][olds] Evam me sutaɱ:|| ||

Ekaɱ samayam Bhagavā Sāvatthiyaɱ viharati||
Jetavane Anāthapindikassa ārāme.|| ||

Tatra kho Bhagavā bhikkhū āmantesi:|| ||

"Bhikkhavo" ti.|| ||

"Bhadante" ti te bhikkhū Bhagavato paccassosuɱ.|| ||

Bhagavā etad avoca:|| ||

"Ānāpāna-sati bhikkhave||
bhāvitā||
bahulī-katā||
maha-p-phalā hoti||
mahā-nisaɱsā.|| ||

Kathaɱ bhāvitā ca bhikkhave||
ānāpāna-sati||
kathaɱ bahulī-katā||
maha-p-phalā hoti||
mahā-nisaɱsā?|| ||

[4] Idha, bhikkhave, bhikkhū araññagato vā||
rukkha-mūla-gato vā||
suññāta-gato vā||
nisīdati pallaŋkaɱ ābhujitvā ujuɱ kāyaɱ paṇidhāya||
parimukhaɱ satiɱ upaṭṭha-petvā||
so sato va assa-sati||
sato va passa-sati.|| ||

[5] Dīghaɱ vā assa-santo dīghaɱ assasāmī' ti pajānāti.|| ||

Dīghaɱ vā passa-santo 'dīghaɱ passasāmī' ti pajānāti.|| ||

Rassaɱ vā assa-santo 'rassaɱ assasāmī' ti pajānāti.|| ||

Rassaɱ vā passa-santo 'rassaɱ passasāmī' ti pajānāti.|| ||

[6] 'Sabba-kāya-paṭisaŋvedī assasi-s-sāmī' ti sikkhati.|| ||

'Sabba-kāya-paṭisaŋvedī passissāmī' ti sikkhati.|| ||

'Passam-bhayaɱ kāya-sankhāraɱ assasi-s-sāmī' ti sikkhati.|| ||

'Passam-bhayaɱ kāya-sankhāraɱ passasi-s-sāmī' ti sikkhati.|| ||

[7] 'Pīti-paṭisaŋvedī assasi-s-sāmī' ti sikkhati.|| ||

'Pīti-paṭisaŋvedī passasi-s-sāmī' ti sikkhati.|| ||

'Sukha-paṭisaŋvedī assasi-s-sāmī' ti sikkhati.|| ||

'Sukha-paṭisaŋvedī passasi-s-sāmī' ti sikkhati.|| ||

'Citta-sankhāra-paṭisaŋvedī assasi-s-sāmī' ti sikkhati.|| ||

'Citta-sankhāra-paṭisaŋvedī passasi-s-sāmī' ti sikkhati.|| ||

'Passam-bhayaɱ citta-sankhāraɱ assasi-s-sāmī' ti sikkhati.|| ||

'Passam-bhayaɱ citta-sankhāraɱ passasi-s-sāmī' ti sikkhati.|| ||

[8] 'Citta-sankhāra-paṭisaŋvedī assasi-s-sāmī' ti sikkhati.|| ||

'Citta-sankhāra-paṭisaŋvedī passasi-s-sāmī' ti sikkhati.|| ||

'Passam-bhayaɱ citta-sankhāraɱ assasi-s-sāmī' ti sikkhati.|| ||

'Passam-bhayaɱ citta-sankhāraɱ passasi-s-sāmī' ti sikkhati.|| ||

'Citta-paṭisaŋvedī assasi-s-sāmī' ti sikkhati.|| ||

'Citta-paṭisaŋvedī passasi-s-sāmī' ti sikkhati.|| ||

[9] 'Abhi-p-pamodayaɱ cittaɱ assasi-s-sāmī' ti sikkhati.|| ||

'Abhi-p-pamodayaɱ cittaɱ passasi-s-sāmī' ti sikkhati.|| ||

'Samādahaɱ cittaɱ assasi-s-sāmī' ti sikkhati.|| ||

'Samādahaɱ cittaɱ passasi-s-sāmī' ti sikkhati.|| ||

'Vimocayaɱ cittaɱ assasi-s-sāmī' ti sikkhati.|| ||

'Vimocayaɱ cittaɱ passasi-s-sāmī' ti sikkhati.|| ||

[10] 'Anicc-ā-nupassī assasi-s-sāmī' ti sikkhati.|| ||

'Anicc-ā-nupassī passasi-s-sāmī' ti sikkhati.|| ||

Virāg-ā-nupassī assasi-s-sāmī' ti sikkhati.|| ||

Virāg-ā-nupassī passasi-s-sāmī' ti sikkhati.|| ||

Nirodh-ā-nupassī assasi-s-sāmī' ti sikkhati.|| ||

Nirodh-ā-nupassī passasi-s-sāmī' ti sikkhati.|| ||

Paṭinissagg-ā-nupassī passasi-s-sāmī' ti sikkhati.|| ||

Paṭinissagg-ā-nupassī passasi-s-sāmī' ti sikkhati.|| ||

[11] Evaɱ bhāvitā kho bhikkhave,||
ānāpāna-sati||
evaɱ bahuḷī-katā||
maha-p-phalā hoti||
mahā-nisaɱsā.|| ||

[12] Evam bhāvitāya kho bhikkhave||
ānāpāna-satiyā||
evam bahuḷī-katāya||
satta phalā||
satt-ā-nisaɱsā||
paṭikaṇkhās.|| ||

Katame satta phalā satt-ā-nisaɱsā?|| ||

[13] Diṭṭheva dhamme paṭigacca aññaɱ ārādheti.|| ||

No ce diṭṭhe'va dhamme paṭigacca aññaɱ ārādheti,||
atha maraṇa-kāle aññaɱ ārādheti.|| ||

No ce diṭṭhe'va dhamme paṭigacca aññaɱ ārādheti,||
no ce maraṇa-kāle aññaɱ ārādheti||
atha pañcantaɱ ora-m-bhāgiyānaɱ saŋyojanānaɱ pari-k-khayā antarā parinibbāyī hoti.|| ||

No ce diṭṭhe'va dhamme paṭigacca aññaɱ ārādheti,||
no ce maraṇa-kāle aññaɱ ārādheti,||
no atha pañcantaɱ ora-m-bhāgiyānaɱ saŋyojanānaɱ pari-k-khayā antarā parinibbāyī hoti,||
upahacca-parinibbāyī hoti.|| ||

No ce diṭṭhe'va dhamme paṭigacca aññaɱ ārādheti,||
no ce maraṇa-kāle aññaɱ ārādheti,||
no atha pañcantaɱ ora-m-bhāgiyānaɱ saŋyojanānaɱ pari-k-khayā,||
antarā parinibbāyī hoti,||
no upahacca-parinibbāyī hoti,||
asankhāra-parinibbāyī hoti.|| ||

No ce diṭṭhe'va dhamme paṭigacca aññaɱ ārādheti,||
no ce maraṇa-kāle aññaɱ ārādheti,||
no atha pañcantaɱ ora-m-bhāgiyānaɱ saŋyojanānaɱ pari-k-khayā,||
antarā parinibbāyī hoti,||
no upahacca-parinibbāyī hoti,||
no asankhāra-parinibbāyī hoti,||
sa-sankhāra-parinibbāyī hoti.|| ||

No ce diṭṭhe'va dhamme paṭigacca aññaɱ ārādheti,||
no ce maraṇa-kāle aññaɱ ārādheti,||
no atha pañcantaɱ ora-m-bhāgiyānaɱ saŋyojanānaɱ pari-k-khayā,||
antarā parinibbāyī hoti,||
no upahacca-parinibbāyī hoti,||
no asankhāra-parinibbāyī hoti,||
no sa-sankhāra-parinibbāyī hoti,||
uddhaɱ-soto hoti Akaniṭṭha-gāmī.|| ||

[14] Evaɱ bhāvitāya kho bhikkhave,||
ānāpāna-satiyā||
evaɱ bahulī-katāya||
ime satta-phalā||
satt-ā-nisaɱsā pāṭikaŋkhā" ti.|| ||
