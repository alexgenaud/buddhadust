Saɱyutta Nikāya
V. Mahā Vagga
55. Sot'āpatti Saɱyutta
I. Veḷudvāra Vagga

Sutta 5

Dutiya Sāriputta Suttaɱ

Adapted from the 1995 edition of the digital version of the Sri Lanka Buddha Jayanti Tripitaka Series.

 

 

[1][pts] Evam me sutaɱ:|| ||

Ekaɱ samayam Bhagavā Sāvatthiyaɱ viharati Jetavane Anāthapindikassa ārāme.|| ||

Atha kho āyasmā Sāriputto yena Bhagavā ten'upasankami.|| ||

Upasaŋkamitvā Bhagavantaɱ abhivādetvā eka-m-antaɱ nisīdi.|| ||

Eka-m-antaɱ nisinnaɱ kho āyasmantaɱ Sāriputtaɱ Bhagavā etad avoca:|| ||

"'Sot'āpattiyaŋgaɱ! sot'āpattiyaŋgan! ti'|| ||

h'idaɱ Sāriputta, vuccati.|| ||

Katamannukho Sāriputta, sot'āpattiyaŋgan" ti?|| ||

"Sappurisasaŋsevohi bhante, sot'āpattiyaŋgaɱ,||
sad'Dhamma-savanaɱ sot'āpattiyaŋgaɱ,||
yoniso-mana-sikāro sot'āpattiyaŋgaɱ,||
dhamm-ā-nudhamma-paṭipatti sot'āpattiyaŋgan" ti.|| ||

Sādhu sādhu Sāriputta,||
sappurisasaŋsevo hi Sāriputta, sot'āpattiyaŋgaɱ,||
sad'Dhamma-savanaɱ sot'āpattiyaŋgaɱ,||
yoniso-mana-sikāro sot'āpattiyaŋgaɱ,||
dhamm-ā-nudhamma-paṭipatti sot'āpattiyaŋgaɱ.|| ||

'Soto soto' ti|| ||

h'idaɱ Sāriputta, vuccati.|| ||

Katamo nukho Sāriputta, soto" ti?|| ||

"Ayam eva hi bhante, Ariyo Aṭṭhaŋgiko Maggo soto.|| ||

Seyyath'īdaɱ:|| ||

Sammā-diṭṭhi||
sammā-saŋkappo||
sammā-vācā||
sammā-kammanto||
sammā-ājīvo||
sammā-vāyāmo||
sammā-sati||
sammā-samādhī" ti.|| ||

"Sādhu sādhu Sāriputta,||
ayam eva hi Sāriputta Ariyo Aṭṭhaŋgiko Maggo soto.|| ||

Seyyath'īdaɱ:|| ||

Sammā-diṭṭhi||
sammā-saŋkappo||
sammā-vācā||
sammā-kammanto||
sammā-ājīvo||
sammā-vāyāmo||
sammā-sati||
sammā-samādhī ti.|| ||

[348] 'Sot'āpanno sotāponno' ti|| ||

h'idaɱ Sāriputta, vuccati.|| ||

Katamo nukho Sāriputta, Sot'āpanno" ti?|| ||

"Yo hi bhante, iminā ariyena aṭṭhaŋgikena maggena samannāgato,||
ayaɱ vuccati Sot'āpanno||
yo'yaɱ āyasmā evaɱ nāmo||
evaɱ gotto" ti.|| ||

"Sādhu sādhu Sāriputta,||
yo hi Sāriputta,||
iminā ariyena aṭṭhaŋgikena maggena samannāgato||
ayaɱ vuccati Sot'āpanno||
yo'yaɱ āyasmā evaɱ-nāmo||
evaɱ-gotto" ti.|| ||
