Saɱyutta Nikāya
5. Mahā-Vagga
55. Sot'Āpatti Saɱyutta
1. Veḷu-Dvāra Vagga

Sutta 5

Dutiya Sāriputta Suttaɱ

Adapted from the 1995 edition of the digital version of the Sri Lanka Buddha Jayanti Tripitaka Series.

 

[347]

[1][pts] Evam me sutaɱ:|| ||

Ekaɱ samayam Bhagavā Sāvatthiyaɱ viharati Jetavane Anāthapindikassa ārāme.|| ||

Atha kho āyasmā Sāriputto yena Bhagavā ten'upasankami.|| ||

Upasaŋkamitvā Bhagavantaɱ abhivādetvā eka-m-antaɱ nisīdi.|| ||

Eka-m-antaɱ nisinnaɱ kho āyasmantaɱ Sāriputtaɱ Bhagavā etad avoca:|| ||

"'Sot'āpattiyaŋgaɱ! sot'āpatti-y-aŋgan! ti'||
h'idaɱ Sāriputta, vuccati.|| ||

Katamannukho Sāriputta, sot'āpatti-y-aŋgan" ti?|| ||

"Sappurisa-saɱsevo hi bhante, sot'āpatti-y-aŋgaɱ,||
sad'Dhamma-savanaɱ sot'āpatti-y-aŋgaɱ,||
yoniso-mana-sikāro sot'āpatti-y-aŋgaɱ,||
Dhamm-ā-nu-Dhamma-paṭipatti sot'āpatti-y-aŋgan" ti.|| ||

Sādhu sādhu Sāriputta,||
sappurisa-saŋsevo hi Sāriputta, sot'āpatti-y-aŋgaɱ,||
sad'Dhamma-savanaɱ sot'āpatti-y-aŋgaɱ,||
yoniso-mana-sikāro sot'āpatti-y-aŋgaɱ,||
Dhamm-ā-nu-Dhamma-paṭipatti sot'āpatti-y-aŋgaɱ.|| ||

'Soto soto' ti||
h'idaɱ Sāriputta, vuccati.|| ||

Katamo nukho Sāriputta, soto" ti?|| ||

"Ayam eva hi bhante, Ariyo Aṭṭhaŋgiko Maggo soto.|| ||

Seyyath'īdaɱ:|| ||

Sammā-diṭṭhi||
sammā-saŋkappo||
sammā-vācā||
sammā-kammanto||
sammā-ājīvo||
sammā-vāyāmo||
sammā-sati||
sammā-samādhī" ti.|| ||

"Sādhu sādhu Sāriputta,||
ayam eva hi Sāriputta Ariyo Aṭṭhaŋgiko Maggo soto.|| ||

Seyyath'īdaɱ:|| ||

Sammā-diṭṭhi||
sammā-saŋkappo||
sammā-vācā||
sammā-kammanto||
sammā-ājīvo||
sammā-vāyāmo||
sammā-sati||
sammā-samādhī" ti.|| ||

[348] "'Sot'āpanno sot'āpanno' ti||
h'idaɱ Sāriputta, vuccati.|| ||

Katamo nukho Sāriputta, Sot'āpanno" ti?|| ||

"Yo hi bhante, iminā ariyena aṭṭhaŋgikena maggena samannāgato,||
ayaɱ vuccati Sot'āpanno||
yo'yaɱ āyasmā evaɱ nāmo||
evaɱ gotto" ti.|| ||

"Sādhu sādhu Sāriputta,||
yo hi Sāriputta,||
iminā ariyena aṭṭhaŋgikena maggena samannāgato||
ayaɱ vuccati Sot'āpanno||
yo'yaɱ āyasmā evaɱ-nāmo||
evaɱ-gotto" ti.|| ||
