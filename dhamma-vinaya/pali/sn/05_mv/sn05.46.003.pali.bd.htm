Saɱyutta Nikāya,
V: Mahā-Vagga
46. Bojjhanga Saɱyutta
I. Pabbata-Vaggo

Sutta 3

Sīla Suttaɱ

Adapted from the 1995 edition of the digital version of the Sri Lanka Buddha Jayanti Tripitaka Series.

 

[67]

[1][pts][olds][bodh] Evam me sutaɱ:|| ||

Ekaɱ samayam Bhagavā Sāvatthiyaɱ viharati.|| ||

Tatra kho Bhagavā bhikkhū āmantesi Bhikkhavo ti.|| ||

Bhadante ti te bhikkhū Bhagavato paccassosuɱ.|| ||

Bhagavā etad avoca:|| ||

"Ye te bhikkhave bhikkhu sīla-sampannā||
samādhi-sampannā||
paññā-sampannā||
vimutti-sampannā||
vimutti-ñāṇa-dassana-sampannā|| ||

Dassanam pāham bhikkhave,||
tesaɱ bhikkhūnaɱ bahukāraɱ vadāmi.|| ||

3. Savanam pāham bhikkhave,||
tesaɱ bhikkhūnaɱ bahukāraɱ vadāmi.|| ||

Upasaŋkamanam pāham bhikkhave,||
tesaɱ bhikkhūnaɱ bahukāraɱ vadāmi.|| ||

Payirupāsanam pāham bhikkhave,||
tesaɱ bhikkhūnaɱ bahukāraɱ vadāmi.|| ||

Anussatim pāham bhikkhave,||
tesaɱ bhīkkhūnaɱ bahukāraɱ vadāmi.|| ||

Anupabbajjam pāham bhikkhave,||
tesaɱ bhikkhūnaɱ bahukāraɱ vadāmi.|| ||

4. Taɱ kissa hetu?|| ||

Tathārūpānam bhikkhave||
bhikkhūnaɱ dhammaɱ sutvā||
dvayena vūpakāsena vūpakaṭṭho viharati||
kāyavūpakāsena ca||
cittavūpakāsena ca.|| ||

So tathā vūpakaṭṭho viharanto taɱ dhammaɱ anussarati anuvitakketi.|| ||

5. Yasmiɱ samaye bhikkhave,||
bhikkhu tathā vūpakaṭṭho viharanto taɱ dhammaɱ anussarati anuvitakketi [68] sati-sambojjhaŋgo tasmiɱ samaye tassa bhikkhuno āraddho hoti.|| ||

Satisambojjh-a-ŋgaɱ yasmiɱ samaye bhikkhu bhāveti,||
sati-sambojjhaŋgo tasmiɱ samaye bhikkhuno bhāvanā pāripūriɱ gacchati.|| ||

So tathā sato viharanto taɱ dhammaɱ paññāya pavicinati pavicarati parivīmaɱsam āpajjati.|| ||

6. Yasmiɱ samaye bhikkhave,||
bhikkhu tathā sato viharanto taɱ dhammaɱ paññāya pavicinati pavicarati parivīmaɱsam āpajjati,||
dhamma-vicaya-sambojjhaŋgo tasmiɱ samaye tassa bhikkhuno āraddho hoti.|| ||

Dhammavicayasambojjh-a-ŋgaɱ yasmiɱ samaye bhikkhu bhāveti,||
dhamma-vicaya-sambojjhaŋgo tasmiɱ samaye bhikkhuno bhāvanā pāripūriɱ gacchati.|| ||

Tassa taɱ dhammaɱ paññāya pavicinato pavicarato parivīmaɱsam āpajjato āraddhaɱ hoti viriyaɱ asallīnaɱ.|| ||

7. Yasmiɱ samaye bhikkhave,||
bhikkhuno taɱ dhammaɱ paññāya pavicinato pavicarato parivīmaɱsam āpajjato āraddhaɱ hoti viriyaɱ asallīṇaɱ,||
viriya-sambojjhaŋgo tasmiɱ samaye bhikkhuno āraddho hoti.|| ||

Viriyasambojjh-a-ŋgaɱ yasmiɱ samaye bhikkhu bhāveti,||
viriya-sambojjhaŋgo tasmiɱ samaye bhikkhuno bhāvanā pāripūriɱ gacchati.|| ||

Āraddha-viriyassa uppajjati pīti nirāmisā.|| ||

8. Yasmiɱ samaye bhikkhave,||
bhikkhuno āraddha-viriyassa uppajjati pīti nirāmisā,||
pīti-sambojjhaŋgo tasmiɱ samaye bhikkhuno āraddho hoti.|| ||

Pītisambojjh-a-ŋgaɱ yasmiɱ samaye bhikkhu bhāveti,||
pīti-sambojjhaŋgo tasmiɱ samaye bhikkhuno bhāvanā pāripūriɱ gacchati.|| ||

Pītimanassa kāyo pi passambhati cittam pi passambhati.|| ||

9. Yasmiɱ samaye bhikkhave,||
bhikkhuno pītimanassa kāyo pi passambhati cittam pi passambhati,||
passaddhi-sambojjhaŋgo tasmiɱ samaye bhikkhuno āraddho hoti.|| ||

Passaddhisambojjh-a-ŋgaɱ yasmiɱ samaye bhikkhu bhāveti,||
passaddhi-sambojjhaŋgo tasmiɱ samaye bhikkhuno bhāvanā [69] pāripūriɱ gacchati.|| ||

Passaddhakāyassa sukhaɱ hoti.|| ||

Sukhino cittaɱ samādhiyati.|| ||

10. Yasmiɱ samaye bhikkhave,||
bhikkhuno passaddha-kāyassa sukhino cittaɱ samādhiyati,||
samādhi-sambojjhaŋgo tasmiɱ samaye bhikkhuno āraddho hoti.|| ||

Samādhisambojjh-a-ŋgaɱ yasmiɱ samaye bhikkhu bhāveti,||
samādhi-sambojjhaŋgo tasmiɱ samaye bhikkhuno bhāvanā pāripūriɱ gacchati.|| ||

So tathā samāhitaɱ cittaɱ sādhukaɱ ajjh'upekkhitā hoti.|| ||

11. Yasmiɱ samaye bhikkhave,||
bhikkhu tathā samāhitaɱ cittaɱ sādhukaɱ ajjh'upekkhitā hoti||
upekhāsambojjhaŋgo tasmiɱ samaye bhikkhuno āraddho hoti.|| ||

Upekhāsambojjh-a-ŋgaɱ yasmiɱ samaye bhikkhu bhāveti,||
upekhāsambojjhaŋgo tasmiɱ samaye bhikkhuno bhāvanā pāripūriɱ gacchati.|| ||

12. Evaɱ bhāvitesu kho bhikkhave sattasu bojjhaŋgesu evaɱ bahulī-katesu satta phalā satt'ānisaɱsā pāṭikaŋkhā.|| ||

Katame satta phalā satt'ānisaɱsā:|| ||

13. Diṭṭhe va dhamme paṭihacca aññaɱ ārādheti.|| ||

14. No ce diṭṭhe'va dhamme paṭihacca aññaɱ ārādheti,||
atha maraṇa-kāle aññaɱ ārādheti.|| ||

15. No ce diṭṭhe'va dhamme paṭihacca aññaɱ ārādheti,||
no ce maraṇa-kāle aññaɱ ārādheti,||
atha pañcannaɱ ora-m-bhāgiyānaɱ saŋyojanānaɱ parikkhayā antarā-parinibbāyī hoti.|| ||

16. No ce diṭṭhe'va dhamme paṭihacca aññaɱ ārādheti,||
no ce maraṇa-kāle aññaɱ ārādheti,||
no ce pañcannaɱ ora-m-bhāgiyānaɱ saŋyojanānaɱ parikkhayā antarā-parinibbāyī hoti,||
atha pañcannaɱ ora-m-bhāgiyānaɱ saŋyojanānaɱ parikkhayā upahacca parinibbāyī hoti.|| ||

17. No ce diṭṭhe'va dhamme paṭihacca aññaɱ ārādheti,||
no ce maraṇa-kāle aññaɱ ārādheti,||
no ce pañcannaɱ [70] ora-m-bhāgiyānaɱ saŋyojanānaɱ parikkhayā antarā-parinibbāyī hoti,||
no ce pañcannaɱ ora-m-bhāgiyānaɱ saŋyojanānaɱ parikkhayā upahacca parinibbāyī hoti,||
atha pañcannaɱ ora-m-bhāgiyānaɱ saŋyojanānaɱ parikkhayā asankhāra-parinibbāyī hoti.|| ||

18. No ce diṭṭhe'va dhamme paṭihacca aññaɱ ārādheti,||
no ce maraṇa-kāle aññaɱ ārādheti,||
no ce pañcannaɱ ora-m-bhāgiyānaɱ saŋyojanānaɱ parikkhayā antarā parinibbāyī hoti,||
no ce pañcannaɱ ora-m-bhāgiyānaɱ saŋyojanānaɱ parikkhayā upahacca parinibbāyī hoti,||
no ce pañcannaɱ ora-m-bhāgiyānaɱ saŋyojanānaɱ parikkhayā asankhāra-parinibbāyī hoti,||
atha pañcannaɱ ora-m-bhāgiyānaɱ saŋyojanānaɱ parikkhayā sa-sankhāra-parinibbāyī hoti.|| ||

19. No ce diṭṭhe'va dhamme paṭihacca aññaɱ ārādheti,||
no ce maraṇa-kāle aññaɱ ārādheti,||
no ce pañcannaɱ ora-m-bhāgiyānaɱ saŋyojanānaɱ parikkhayā antarā parinibbāyī hoti,||
no ce pañcannaɱ ora-m-bhāgiyānaɱ saŋyojanānaɱ parikkhayā upahacca parinibbāyī hoti,||
no ce pañcannaɱ ora-m-bhāgiyānaɱ saŋyojanānaɱ parikkhayā asankhāra parinibbāyī hoti,||
no ce pañcannaɱ ora-m-bhāgiyānaɱ saŋyojanānaɱ parikkhayā sa-sankhāra parinibbāyī hoti,||
atha pañcannaɱ ora-m-bhāgiyānaɱ saŋyojanānaɱ parikkhayā uddhaɱ-soto hoti Akaniṭṭha-gāmī.|| ||

20. Evaɱ bhāvitesu kho bhikkhave,||
sattasu bojjhaŋgesu evaɱ bahulī-katesu ime satta phalā sattānisaɱsā pāṭikaŋkhā ti.|| ||
