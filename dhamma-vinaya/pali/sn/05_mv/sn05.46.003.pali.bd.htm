Saɱyutta Nikāya
5. Mahā-Vagga
46. Bojjhanga Saɱyutta
1. Pabbata Vagga

Sutta 3

Sīla Suttaɱ

Adapted from the 1995 edition of the digital version of the Sri Lanka Buddha Jayanti Tripitaka Series.

 

[67]

[1][pts][olds][bodh] Evam me sutaɱ:|| ||

Ekaɱ samayam Bhagavā Sāvatthiyaɱ viharati.|| ||

Tatra kho Bhagavā bhikkhū āmantesi:|| ||

"Bhikkhavo" ti.|| ||

"Bhadante" ti te bhikkhū Bhagavato paccassosuɱ.|| ||

Bhagavā etad avoca:|| ||

"Ye te bhikkhave bhikkhu sīla-sampannā||
samādhi-sampannā||
paññā-sampannā||
vimutti-sampannā||
vimutti-ñāṇa-dassana-sampannā|| ||

Dassanam pāham bhikkhave,||
tesaɱ bhikkhūnaɱ bahukāraɱ vadāmi.|| ||

3. Savanam pāham bhikkhave,||
tesaɱ bhikkhūnaɱ bahukāraɱ vadāmi.|| ||

Upasaŋkamanam pāham bhikkhave,||
tesaɱ bhikkhūnaɱ bahukāraɱ vadāmi.|| ||

Payirupāsanam pāham bhikkhave,||
tesaɱ bhikkhūnaɱ bahukāraɱ vadāmi.|| ||

Anussatim pāham bhikkhave,||
tesaɱ bhīkkhūnaɱ bahukāraɱ vadāmi.|| ||

Anupabbajjam pāham bhikkhave,||
tesaɱ bhikkhūnaɱ bahukāraɱ vadāmi.|| ||

4. Taɱ kissa hetu?|| ||

Tathārūpānam bhikkhave||
bhikkhūnaɱ dhammaɱ sutvā||
dvayena vūpakāsena vūpakaṭṭho viharati||
kāya-vūpakāsena ca||
citta-vūpakāsena ca.|| ||

So tathā vūpakaṭṭho viharanto taɱ dhammaɱ anussarati anuvitakketi.|| ||

5. Yasmiɱ samaye bhikkhave,||
bhikkhu tathā vūpakaṭṭho viharanto taɱ dhammaɱ anussarati anuvitakketi [68] sati-sambojjh'aŋgo tasmiɱ samaye tassa bhikkhuno āraddho hoti.|| ||

Sati-sambojjh'aŋgaɱ yasmiɱ samaye bhikkhu bhāveti,||
sati-sambojjh'aŋgo tasmiɱ samaye bhikkhuno bhāvanā pāripūriɱ gacchati.|| ||

So tathā sato viharanto taɱ dhammaɱ paññāya pavicinati pavicarati parivīmaɱsam āpajjati.|| ||

6. Yasmiɱ samaye bhikkhave,||
bhikkhu tathā sato viharanto taɱ dhammaɱ paññāya pavicinati pavicarati parivīmaɱsam āpajjati,||
dhamma-vicaya-sambojjh'aŋgo tasmiɱ samaye tassa bhikkhuno āraddho hoti.|| ||

Dhamma-vicaya-sambojjh'aŋgaɱ yasmiɱ samaye bhikkhu bhāveti,||
dhamma-vicaya-sambojjh'aŋgo tasmiɱ samaye bhikkhuno bhāvanā pāripūriɱ gacchati.|| ||

Tassa taɱ dhammaɱ paññāya pavicinato pavicarato parivīmaɱsam āpajjato āraddhaɱ hoti viriyaɱ asallīnaɱ.|| ||

7. Yasmiɱ samaye bhikkhave,||
bhikkhuno taɱ dhammaɱ paññāya pavicinato pavicarato parivīmaɱsam āpajjato āraddhaɱ hoti viriyaɱ asallīṇaɱ,||
viriya-sambojjh'aŋgo tasmiɱ samaye bhikkhuno āraddho hoti.|| ||

Viriya-sambojjh'aŋgaɱ yasmiɱ samaye bhikkhu bhāveti,||
viriya-sambojjh'aŋgo tasmiɱ samaye bhikkhuno bhāvanā pāripūriɱ gacchati.|| ||

Āraddha-viriyassa uppajjati pīti nirāmisā.|| ||

8. Yasmiɱ samaye bhikkhave,||
bhikkhuno āraddha-viriyassa uppajjati pīti nirāmisā,||
pīti-sambojjh'aŋgo tasmiɱ samaye bhikkhuno āraddho hoti.|| ||

Pīti-sambojjh'aŋgaɱ yasmiɱ samaye bhikkhu bhāveti,||
pīti-sambojjh'aŋgo tasmiɱ samaye bhikkhuno bhāvanā pāripūriɱ gacchati.|| ||

Pīti-manassa kāyo pi passambhati cittam pi passambhati.|| ||

9. Yasmiɱ samaye bhikkhave,||
bhikkhuno pīti-manassa kāyo pi passambhati cittam pi passambhati,||
passaddhi-sambojjh'aŋgo tasmiɱ samaye bhikkhuno āraddho hoti.|| ||

Passaddhi-sambojjh'aŋgaɱ yasmiɱ samaye bhikkhu bhāveti,||
passaddhi-sambojjh'aŋgo tasmiɱ samaye bhikkhuno bhāvanā [69] pāripūriɱ gacchati.|| ||

Passaddha-kāyassa sukhaɱ hoti.|| ||

Sukhino cittaɱ samādhiyati.|| ||

10. Yasmiɱ samaye bhikkhave,||
bhikkhuno passaddha-kāyassa sukhino cittaɱ samādhiyati,||
samādhi-sambojjh'aŋgo tasmiɱ samaye bhikkhuno āraddho hoti.|| ||

Samādhi-sambojjh'aŋgaɱ yasmiɱ samaye bhikkhu bhāveti,||
samādhi-sambojjh'aŋgo tasmiɱ samaye bhikkhuno bhāvanā pāripūriɱ gacchati.|| ||

So tathā samāhitaɱ cittaɱ sādhukaɱ ajjh'upekkhitā hoti.|| ||

11. Yasmiɱ samaye bhikkhave,||
bhikkhu tathā samāhitaɱ cittaɱ sādhukaɱ ajjh'upekkhitā hoti||
upekhā-sambojjh'aŋgo tasmiɱ samaye bhikkhuno āraddho hoti.|| ||

Upekhā-sambojjh'aŋgaɱ yasmiɱ samaye bhikkhu bhāveti,||
upekhā-sambojjh'aŋgo tasmiɱ samaye bhikkhuno bhāvanā pāripūriɱ gacchati.|| ||

12. Evaɱ bhāvitesu kho bhikkhave sattasu bojjh'aŋgesu||
evaɱ bahulī-katesu||
satta phalā satt'ānisaɱsā pāṭikaŋkhā.|| ||

Katame satta phalā satt'ānisaɱsā?|| ||

13. Diṭṭhe'va dhamme paṭihacca aññaɱ ārādheti.|| ||

14. No ce diṭṭhe'va dhamme paṭihacca aññaɱ ārādheti,||
atha maraṇa-kāle aññaɱ ārādheti.|| ||

15. No ce diṭṭhe'va dhamme paṭihacca aññaɱ ārādheti,||
no ce maraṇa-kāle aññaɱ ārādheti,||
atha pañcannaɱ ora-m-bhāgiyānaɱ saŋyojanānaɱ pari-k-khayā antarā-parinibbāyī hoti.|| ||

16. No ce diṭṭhe'va dhamme paṭihacca aññaɱ ārādheti,||
no ce maraṇa-kāle aññaɱ ārādheti,||
no ce pañcannaɱ ora-m-bhāgiyānaɱ saŋyojanānaɱ pari-k-khayā antarā-parinibbāyī hoti,||
atha pañcannaɱ ora-m-bhāgiyānaɱ saŋyojanānaɱ pari-k-khayā upahacca parinibbāyī hoti.|| ||

17. No ce diṭṭhe'va dhamme paṭihacca aññaɱ ārādheti,||
no ce maraṇa-kāle aññaɱ ārādheti,||
no ce pañcannaɱ [70] ora-m-bhāgiyānaɱ saŋyojanānaɱ pari-k-khayā antarā-parinibbāyī hoti,||
no ce pañcannaɱ ora-m-bhāgiyānaɱ saŋyojanānaɱ pari-k-khayā upahacca parinibbāyī hoti,||
atha pañcannaɱ ora-m-bhāgiyānaɱ saŋyojanānaɱ pari-k-khayā a-sankhāra-parinibbāyī hoti.|| ||

18. No ce diṭṭhe'va dhamme paṭihacca aññaɱ ārādheti,||
no ce maraṇa-kāle aññaɱ ārādheti,||
no ce pañcannaɱ ora-m-bhāgiyānaɱ saŋyojanānaɱ pari-k-khayā antarā parinibbāyī hoti,||
no ce pañcannaɱ ora-m-bhāgiyānaɱ saŋyojanānaɱ pari-k-khayā upahacca parinibbāyī hoti,||
no ce pañcannaɱ ora-m-bhāgiyānaɱ saŋyojanānaɱ pari-k-khayā a-sankhāra-parinibbāyī hoti,||
atha pañcannaɱ ora-m-bhāgiyānaɱ saŋyojanānaɱ pari-k-khayā sa-sankhāra-parinibbāyī hoti.|| ||

19. No ce diṭṭhe'va dhamme paṭihacca aññaɱ ārādheti,||
no ce maraṇa-kāle aññaɱ ārādheti,||
no ce pañcannaɱ ora-m-bhāgiyānaɱ saŋyojanānaɱ pari-k-khayā antarā parinibbāyī hoti,||
no ce pañcannaɱ ora-m-bhāgiyānaɱ saŋyojanānaɱ pari-k-khayā upahacca parinibbāyī hoti,||
no ce pañcannaɱ ora-m-bhāgiyānaɱ saŋyojanānaɱ pari-k-khayā a-sankhāra parinibbāyī hoti,||
no ce pañcannaɱ ora-m-bhāgiyānaɱ saŋyojanānaɱ pari-k-khayā sa-sankhāra parinibbāyī hoti,||
atha pañcannaɱ ora-m-bhāgiyānaɱ saŋyojanānaɱ pari-k-khayā uddhaɱ-soto hoti Akaniṭṭha-gāmī.|| ||

20. Evaɱ bhāvitesu kho bhikkhave,||
sattasu bojjh'aŋgesu evaɱ bahulī-katesu ime satta phalā satt-ā-nisaɱsā pāṭikaŋkhā" ti.|| ||
