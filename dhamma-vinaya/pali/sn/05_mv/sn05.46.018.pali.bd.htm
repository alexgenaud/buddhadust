Saɱyutta Nikāya,
V: Mahā-Vagga
46. Bojjhanga Saɱyutta
II. Gilāna-Vaggo

Sutta 18

Viraddha or Āraddha Suttaɱ

Adapted from the 1995 edition of the digital version of the Sri Lanka Buddha Jayanti Tripitaka Series.

 

 

[1][pts][bodh] Evam me sutaɱ:|| ||

Ekaɱ samayam Bhagavā Sāvatthiyaɱ viharati Jetavane Anāthapindikassa ārāme.|| ||

Tatra kho Bhagavā bhikkhū āmantesi Bhikkhavo ti.|| ||

Bhadante ti te bhikkhū Bhagavato paccassosuɱ.|| ||

Bhagavā etad avoca:|| ||

"Yesaŋ kesañci bhikkhave, satta bojjhaŋgā viraddhā||
viraddho tesaŋ ariyo Maggo sammā dukkha-k-khayagāmī.|| ||

Yesaŋ kesañci bhikkhave, satta bojjhaŋgā āraddhā||
āraddho tesaŋ ariyo Maggo sammā dukkha-k-khayagāmī.|| ||

Katame satta?|| ||

Sati-sambojjhaŋgo,||
dhamma-vicaya-sambojjhaŋgo,||
viriya-sambojjhaŋgo,||
pīti-sambojjhaŋgo,||
passaddhi-sambojjhaŋgo,||
samādhi-sambojjhaŋgo,||
upekhā-sambojjhaŋgo.|| ||

Yesaŋ kesañci bhikkhave, satta bojjhaŋgā viraddhā||
viraddho tesaŋ ariyo Maggo sammā dukkha-k-khayagāmī.|| ||

Yesaŋ kesañci bhikkhave, satta bojjhaŋgā āraddhā||
āraddho tesaŋ ariyo Maggo sammā dukkha-k-khayagāmī" ti.|| ||
