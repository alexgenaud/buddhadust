Saɱyutta Nikāya,
V: Mahā-Vagga
56. Sacca Saɱyutta
II Dhamma-Cakka-Pavattana Vagga

Sutta 19

Sankāsanā Suttaɱ

Adapted from the 1995 edition of the digital version of the Sri Lanka Buddha Jayanti Tripitaka Series.

 

 

[1][pts][bodh] Evam me sutaɱ:|| ||

Ekaɱ samayam Bhagavā Sāvatthiyaɱ viharati Jetavane Anāthapiṇḍikassa ārāme.|| ||

Tatra kho Bhagavā bhikkhū āmantesi:|| ||

"Bhikkhavo" ti.|| ||

"Bhadante" ti te bhikkhū Bhagavato paccassosuɱ.|| ||

Bhagavā etad avoca:|| ||

"'Idaɱ dukkhaɱ ariya-saccan' ti bhikkhave,||
mayā paññattaɱ.|| ||

Tattha aparimāṇā vaṇṇā,||
aparimāṇā vyañjanā,||
aparimāṇā sankāsanā,||
iti pi 'idaɱ dukkhaɱ ariya-saccan' ti.|| ||

■

'Idaɱ dukkha-samudayo ariya-saccan' ti bhikkhave,||
mayā paññattaɱ.|| ||

Tattha aparimāṇā vaṇṇā,||
aparimāṇā vyañjanā,||
aparimāṇā sankāsanā,||
iti pi 'idaɱ dukkha-samudayo ariya-saccan' ti.|| ||

■

'Idaɱ dukkha-nirodho ariya-saccan' ti bhikkhave,||
mayā paññattaɱ.|| ||

Tattha aparimāṇā vaṇṇā,||
aparimāṇā vyañjanā,||
aparimāṇā sankāsanā,||
iti pi 'idaɱ dukkha-nirodho ariya-saccan' ti.|| ||

'Idaɱ dukkha-nirodha-gāminī paṭipadā ariya-saccan' ti bhikkhave,||
mayā paññattaɱ.|| ||

Tattha aparimāṇā vaṇṇā,||
aparimāṇā vyañjanā,||
aparimāṇā sankāsanā||
iti pi 'idaɱ dukkha-nirodha-gāminī paṭipadā ariya-saccan' ti.|| ||

Tasmā ti ha bhikkhu,||
'Idaɱ dukkhan' ti yogo karaṇīyo,||
'Ayaɱ dukkha-samudayo' ti yogo karaṇīyo,||
'Ayaɱ dukkha-nirodho' ti yogo karaṇīyo,||
'Ayaɱ dukkha-nirodha-gāminī paṭipadā' ti yogo karaṇīyo" ti.|| ||
