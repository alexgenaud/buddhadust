Saɱyutta Nikāya,
V: Mahā-Vagga

10. (54) ĀnāpānaSaɱyutta

2. Ānanda
or
Dutiyo vaggo

Sutta 16

Dutiya Bhikkhū Suttaɱ

Adapted from the 1995 edition of the digital version of the Sri Lanka Buddha Jayanti Tripitaka Series and proofed against, and mostly resolved to the 1994 Pali Text Society Saɱyutta-Nikaya Part V, edited by M. Leon Feer

 

 

[1-2][pts][bodh] Sāvatthi|| ||

Atha kho sambahulā bhikkhū yena Bhagavā ten'upasankamiɱsu.||
Upasaŋkamitvā Bhagavtaɱ abhivādetvā eka-m-antaɱ nisīdiɱsu.|| ||

Bhikkhū saddhiɱ sammodi.|| ||

Eka-m-antaɱ nisinnā kho te bhikkhū Bhagavāntaɱ etad avocuɱ:|| ||

"Atthi nu kho bhikkhave eko dhammā bhāvito bahulī-kato cattāro dhamme paripūreti,||
cattāro dhammā bhāvitā bahulī-katā satta dhamme paripūrenti,||
satta dhammā bhāvitā bahulī-katā dve dhamme paripūrentī" ti?|| ||

Bhagava mūlakā no bhante dhammā,||
dhammā Bhagavānnettikā,||
Bhagavāmpaṭisaraṇā.|| ||

Sādhu vata bhante,||
Bhagavāntaɱ yeva paṭibhātu etassa bhāsitassa attho,||
Bhagavāto sutvā bhikkhū dhāre-s-santī ti.|| ||

"Atthi bhikkhave eko dhammā bhāvito bahulī-kato cattāro dhamme paripūreti,||
cattāro dhammā bhāvitā bahulī-katā satta [335] dhamme paripūrenti,||
satta dhammā bhāvitā bahulī-katā dve dhamme paripūrentī" ti.|| ||

Katamo pana bhikkhave, eko dhammo bhāvito bahulī-kato cattāro dhamme paripūrenti,||
cattāro dhammā bhāvitā bahulī-katā satta dhamme paripūrenti,||
satta dhammā bhāvitā bahulī-katā dve dhamme paripūrenti:|| ||

Ānāpāna-satisamādhi kho bhikkhave eko dhammo bhāvito bahulī-kato cattāro sati-paṭṭhāne paripūreti,||
cattāro sati-paṭṭhānā bhāvitā bahulī-katā satta bojjhaŋge paripūrenti,||
satta bojjhaŋgā bhāvitā bahulī-katā vijajāvimuttiɱ paripūrenti.|| ||

 

i

 

Kathaɱ bhāvito ca bhikkhave, ānāpāna-sati-samādhi kathaɱ bahulī-kato cattāro sati-paṭṭhāne paripūreti:|| ||

Idha, bhikkhave, bhikkhū araññagato vā rukkha-mūla-gato vā suññāragato vā nisīdati pallaŋkaɱ ābhujitvā ujuɱ kāyaɱ paṇidhāya parimukhaɱ satiɱ upaṭṭha-petvā so sato va assa-sati sato va passa-sati.|| ||

Dīghaɱ vā assa-santo dīghaɱ assasāmīti pajānāti.||
Dīghaɱ vā passa-santo dīghaɱ passasāmīti pajānāti.|| ||

Rassaɱ vā assa-santo rassaɱ assasāmīti pajānāti.||
Rassaɱ vā passa-santo rassaɱ passasāmīti pajānāti.|| ||

Sabba-kāyapaṭisaŋvedī assasi-s-sāmīti sikkhati.||
Sabba-kāyapaṭisaŋvedī passissāmīti sikkhati.|| ||

Passam-bhayaɱ kāya-sankhāraɱ assasi-s-sāmīti sikkhati.||
Passam-bhayaɱ kāya-sankhāraɱ passasi-s-sāmīti sikkhati.|| ||

Pīti-paṭisaŋvedī assasi-s-sāmīti sikkhati.||
Pīti-paṭisaŋvedī passasi-s-sāmīti sikkhati.|| ||

Sukha-paṭisaŋvedī assasi-s-sāmīti sikkhati.||
Sukha-paṭisaŋvedī passasi-s-sāmīti sikkhati.|| ||

Citta-sankhāra-paṭisaŋvedī assasi-s-sāmīti sikkhati.||
Citta-sankhāra-paṭisaŋvedī passasi-s-sāmīti sikkhati.|| ||

Passam-bhayaɱ citta-sankhāraɱ assasi-s-sāmīti sikkhati.||
Passam-bhayaɱ citta-sankhāraɱ passasi-s-sāmīti sikkhati.|| ||

Citta-paṭisaŋvedī assasi-s-sāmīti sikkhati.||
Citta-paṭisaŋvedī passasi-s-sāmīti sikkhati.|| ||

Abhi-p-pamodayaɱ cittaɱ assasi-s-sāmīti sikkhati.||
Abhi-p-pamodayaɱ cittaɱ passasi-s-sāmīti sikkhati.|| ||

Samādahaɱ cittaɱ assasi-s-sāmīti sikkhati.||
Samādahaɱ cittaɱ passasi-s-sāmīti sikkhati.|| ||

Vimocayaɱ cittaɱ assasi-s-sāmīti sikkhati.||
Vimocayaɱ cittaɱ passasi-s-sāmīti sikkhati.|| ||

Anicc-ā-nupassī assasi-s-sāmīti sikkhati.||
Anicc-ā-nupassī passasi-s-sāmīti sikkhati.|| ||

Virāg-ā-nupassī assasi-s-sāmīti sikkhati.||
Virāg-ā-nupassī passasi-s-sāmīti sikkhati.|| ||

Nirodh-ā-nupassī assasi-s-sāmīti sikkhati.||
Nirodh-ā-nupassī passasi-s-sāmīti sikkhati.|| ||

Paṭinissagg-ā-nupassī passasi-s-sāmīti sikkhati.||
Paṭinissagg-ā-nupassī passasi-s-sāmīti sikkhati.|| ||

Yasmiɱ samaye bhikkhave bhikkhū dīghaɱ vā assa-santo dīghaɱ assasāmīti pajānāti,||
dīghaɱ vā passa-santo dīghaɱ passasāmī ti pajānāti.||
Rassaɱ vā assa-santo rassaɱ assasāmīti pajānāti,||
rassaɱ vā passa-santo rassaɱ passasāmīti pajānāti.||
Sabba-kāyapaṭisaŋvedī assasi-s-sāmīti sikkhati,||
sabba-kāyapaṭisaŋvedī passasi-s-sāmīti sikkhati.||
Passam-bhayaɱ kāya-sankhāraɱ assasi-s-sāmīti sikkhati,||
passam-bhayaɱ kāya-sankhāraɱ passasi-s-sāmīti sikkhati.||
Kāye kāy'ānupassī bhikkhave, bhikkhū tasmiɱ samaye viharati ātāpī sampajāno satimā vineyya loke abhijjhā-domanassaɱ.||
Taɱ kissa hetu?|| ||

Kāyaññatarāhaɱ bhikkhave etaɱ vadāmi, yad idaɱ assāsapassāsaɱ.||
Tasmā ti h'bhikkhave, kāye kāy'ānupassī bhikkhū tasmiɱ samaye viharati ātāpī sampajāno satimā vineyya loke abhijjhā-domanassaɱ.|| ||

Yasmiɱ samaye bhikkhave, bhikkhū pīti-paṭisaŋvedī assasi-s-sāmīti sikkhati,||
pīti-paṭisaŋvedī passasissāmiti sikkhati.||
Sukha-paṭisaŋvedī assasi-s-sāmīti sikkhati,||
sukha-paṭiɱvedī passasissāmiti sikkhati.||
Citta-sankhāra-paṭisaŋvedī assasi-s-sāmīti sikkhati,||
citta-sankhāra-paṭisaŋvedī passasi-s-sāmīti sikkhati.||
Passam-bhayaɱ citta-sankhāraɱ assasi-s-sāmīti sikkhati,||
passam-bhayaɱ citta-sankhāraɱ passasi-s-sāmīti sikkhati.||
Vedanāsu vedan'ānupassī bhikkhave, bhikkhū tasmiɱ samaye viharati ātāpī sampajāno satimā vineyaya loke abhijjhā-domanassaɱ.||
Taɱ kissa hetu?|| ||

Vedanaññatarāhaɱ bhikkhave, etaɱ vadāmi yad idaɱ assāsa-passāsānaɱ sādhukaɱ mana-sikāraɱ.||
Tasmā ti ha bhikkhave, vedanāsu vedan'ānupassī bhikkhū tasmiɱ samaye viharati ātāpī sampajāno satimā vineyya loke abhijjhā-domanassaɱ.|| ||

Yasmiɱ samaye bhikkhave, bhikkhū citta-paṭisaŋvedī assasi-s-sāmīti sikkhati,||
citta-paṭisaŋvedī passasi-s-sāmīti sikkhati.||
Abhi-p-pamodayaɱ cittaɱ assasi-s-sāmīti sikkhati,||
abhippamodayaɱ cittaɱ passasi-s-sāmīti sikkhati.||
Samādahaɱ cittaɱ assasi-s-sāmīti sikkhati,||
samādahaɱ cittaɱ passasi-s-sāmīti sikkhati.||
Vimocayaɱ cittaɱ assasi-s-sāmīti sikkhati,||
vimocayaɱ cittaɱ passasi-s-sāmīti sikkhati.||
Citte citt'ānupassī bhikkhave, bhikkhū tasmiɱ samaye viharati ātāpī sampajāno satimā vineyya loke abhijjhā-domanassaɱ.||
Taɱ kissa hetu?|| ||

Nāhaɱ bhikkhave, muṭṭha-s-satissa asampajānassa ānāpāna-sati-samādhibhāvanaɱ vadāmi.||
Tasmā ti ha bhikkhave, citte citt'ānupassī bhikkhū tasmiɱ samaye viharati ātāpī sampajāno satimā vineyya loke abhijjhā-domanassaɱ.|| ||

Yasmiɱ samaye bhikkhave, bhikkhū anicc'ānupassī||
assasi-s-sāmīti sikkhati,||
anicc'ānupassī passasi-s-sāmīti sikkhati.||
Virāg-ā-nupassī assasi-s-sāmīti sikkhati,||
virāgānupassi passasi-s-sāmīti sikkhati.||
Nirodh-ā-nupassī assasi-s-sāmīti sikkhati,||
nirodh-ā-nupassī passasi-s-sāmīti sikkhati.||
Paṭinissagg-ā-nupassī assasi-s-sāmīti sikkhati,||
paṭinissagg-ā-nupassī passasi-s-sāmīti sikkhati.||
Dhammesu Dhamm'ānupassī bhikkhave, bhikkhū tasmiɱ samaye viharati ātāpī sampajāno satimā vineyya loke abhijjhā-domanassaɱ.|| ||

So yaɱ taɱ hoti abhijjhā-domanassānaɱ pahānaɱ taɱ paññāya disvā sādhukaɱ ajjh'upekkhitā hoti.||
Tasmā ti h'bhikkhave, dhammesu Dhamm'ānupassī bhikkhū tasmiɱ samaye viharati ātāpī sampajāno satimā vineyya loke abhijjhā-domanassaɱ.|| ||

Evaɱ bhāvito kho bhikkhave ānāpāna-sati-samādhi evaɱ bahulī-kato cattāro sati-paṭṭhāne paripūreti.|| ||

 

ii

 

Kathaɱ bhāvitā ca bhikkhave cattāro sati-paṭṭhānā kathaɱ bahulī-katā satta sambojjhaŋge paripūrenti.|| ||

Yasmiɱ samaye bhikkhave bhikkhū kāye kāy'ānupassī viharati upaṭṭhitāsati tasmiɱ samaye bhikkhave bhikkhuno sati hoti asammuṭṭhā.||
Yasmiɱ samaye bhikkhave bhikkhūno upaṭṭhitā sati asammuṭṭhā sati-sambojjhaŋgo tasmiɱ samaye bhikkhūno āraddho hoti.||
Satisambojjh-a-ŋgaɱ tasmiɱ samaye bhikkhave bhikkhū bhāveti.||
Sati-sambojjhaŋgo tasmiɱ samaye bhikkhūno bhāvanā-pāripūriɱ gacchati.||
So tathā sato viharanto taɱ dhammaɱ paññāya pavicinati pavicarati parivīmaɱsam āpajjati.|| ||

Yasmiɱ samaye bhikkhave, bhikkhū tathā sato viharanto taɱ dhammaɱ paññāya pavicināti parivīmaɱsam āpajjati.||
Dhammavicayasambojjhaŋgo tasmiɱ samaye bhikkhūno āraddho hoti.||
Dhammavicayasambojjh-a-ŋgaɱ tasmiɱ samaye bhikkhū bhāveti.||
Dhammavicayasambojjhaŋgo tasmiɱ samaye bhikkhūno bhāvanāpāripūriɱ gacchati.||
Tassa taɱ dhammaɱ paññāya pavicanato pavicarato parivīmaɱsam āpajjato āraddhaɱ hoti viriyaɱ asallīnaɱ.|| ||

Yasmiɱ samaye bhikkhave, bhikkhūno taɱ dhammaɱ paññāya pavicinato pavicarato parivīmaɱsam āpajjato āraddhaɱ hoti viriyaɱ asallīnaɱ,||
viriya-sambojjhaŋgo tasmiɱ samaye bhikkhūno āraddho hoti.||
Viriyasambojjh-a-ŋgaɱ tasmiɱ samaye bhikkhū bhāveti.||
Viriyasambojjhaŋgo tasmiɱ samaye bhikkhūno bhāvanāpāripūriɱ gacchati.||
Āraddha-viriyassa uppajjati pīti nirāmisā.|| ||

Yasmiɱ samaye bhikkhave, bhikkhūno āraddha-viriyassa uppajjati pīti nirāmisā,||
pīti-sambojjhaŋgo tasmiɱ samaye bhikkhūno āraddho hoti.||
Pītisambojjh-a-ŋgaɱ tasmiɱ samaye bhikkhū bhāveti.||
Pītisambojjhaŋgo tasmiɱ samaye bhikkhūno bhāvanāpāripūriɱ gacchati.||
Pītimanassa kāyopi passambhati. Cittampi passambhati.|| ||

Yasmiɱ samaye bhikkhave bhikkhūno pītimanassa kāyo pi passambhati,||
cittam pi passambhati,||
passaddhi-sambojjhaŋgo tasmiɱ samaye bhikkhūno āraddho hoti.||
Passaddhisambojjh-a-ŋgaɱ tasmiɱ samaye bhikkhū bhāveti.||
Passaddhisambojjhaŋgo tasmiɱ samaye bhikkhūno bhāvanāpāripūriɱ gacchati.||
Passaddhakāyassa sukhino cittaɱ samādhiyati.|| ||

Yasmiɱ samaye bhikkhave bhikkhūno passaddha-kāyassa sukhino cittaɱ samādhiyati,||
samādhi-sambojjhaŋgo tasmiɱ samaye bhikkhūno āraddho hoti.||
Samādhisambojjh-a-ŋgaɱ tasmiɱ samayo bhikkhū bhāveti.||
Samādhisambojjhaŋgo tasmiɱ samaye bhikkhūno bhāvanāpāripūriɱ gacchati.||
So tathā samāhitaɱ cittaɱ sādhukaɱ ajjh'upekkhitā hoti.|| ||

Yasmiɱ samaye bhikkhave bhikkhū tathā samāhitaɱ cittaɱ sādhukaɱ ajjh'upekkhitā hoti,||
upekhāsambojjhaŋgo tasmiɱ samaye bhikkhūno āraddho hoti.||
Upekhāsambojjh-a-ŋgaɱ tasmiɱ samaye bhikkhū bhāveti.||
Upekhāsambojjhaŋgo tasmiɱ samaye bhikkhūno bhāvanāpāripūriɱ gacchati.|| ||

Yasmiɱ samaye bhikkhave bhikkhū vedanāsu vedan'ānupassī...||
Citte citt'ānupassī...||
Dhammesu Dhamm'ānupassī viharati upaṭṭhitāsati tasmiɱ samaye bhikkhave bhikkhuno sati hoti asammuṭṭhā.|| ||

Yasmiɱ samaye bhikkhave bhikkhūno upaṭṭhitā sati hoti asammuṭṭhā,||
sati-sambojjhaŋgo tasmiɱ samaye bhikkhūno āraddho hoti.||
Satisbojjh-a-ŋgaɱ tasmiɱ samaye bhikkhū bhāveti.||
Sati-sambojjhaŋgo tasmiɱ samaye bhikkhūno bhāvanā-pāripūriɱ gacchati.|| ||

Yathā pathamaɱ sati-paṭṭhānam evaɱ vitthāretabbaɱ|| ||

So tathā sato viharanto taɱ dhammaɱ paññāya pavicanati pavicarati parivīmaɱsamāpajjati.|| ||

Yasmiɱ samaye bhikkhave bhikkhū tathā sato viharanto taɱ dhammaɱ paññāya pavicanāti.|| ||

Pavicarati parivīmaɱsamāpajjati.|| ||

Dhammavicayasambojjhaŋgo tasmiɱ samaye bhikkhūno āraddho hoti.|| ||

Dhammavicayasambojjh-a-ŋgaɱ tasmiɱ samaye bhikkhūno bhāveti.|| ||

Dhammavicayasambojjhaŋgo tasmiɱ samaye bhikkhūno bhāvanāpāripūriɱ gacchati.|| ||

Tassa taɱ dhammaɱ paññāya pavicanato pavicarato parivīmaɱsamāpajjato āraddhaɱ hoti viriyaɱ asallīnaɱ.|| ||

Yasmiɱ samaye bhikkhave bhikkhūno taɱ dhammaɱ paññāya pavicanato pavicarato parivīmaɱsamāpajjato āraddhaɱ hoti viriyaɱ asallīnaɱ,||
viriya-sambojjhaŋgo tasmiɱ samaye bhikkhūno āraddho hoti.|| ||

Viriyasambojjh-a-ŋgaɱ tasmiɱ samaye bhikkhū bhāveti.|| ||

Viriyasambojjhaŋgo tasmiɱ samaye bhikkhūno bhāvanāpāripūriɱ gacchati.|| ||

Āraddha-viriyassa uppajjati pīti nirāmisā.|| ||

Yasmiɱ samaye bhikkhave bhikkhūno āraddha-viriyassa uppajjati pīti nirāmisā,||
pīti-sambojjhaŋgo tasmiɱ samaye bhikkhūno āraddho hoti.|| ||

Pitisambojjhaŋgo tasmiɱ samaye bhikkhūno bhāvanāpāripūriɱ gacchati.|| ||

Pītimanassa kāyopi passambhati.|| ||

Cittampi passambhati.|| ||

Yasmiɱ samaye bhikkhave bhikkhūno pītimanassa kāyo pi passambhati,||
cittampi passambhati,||
passaddhi-sambojjhaŋgo tasmiɱ samaye bhikkhūno āraddho hoti.|| ||

Passaddhisambojjh-a-ŋgaɱ tasmiɱ samaye bhikkhū bhāveti.|| ||

Passaddhisambijjheŋgo tasmiɱ samaye bhikkhūno bhāvanāpāripūriɱ gacchati.|| ||

Passaddhakāyassa sukhino cittaɱ samādhiyati.|| ||

Yasmiɱ samaye bhikkhave, bhikkhūno passaddha-kāyassa sukhino cittaɱ samādhiyati,||
samādhi-sambojjhaŋgo tasmiɱ samaye bhikkhūno āraddho hoti.|| ||

Samādhisambojjh-a-ŋgaɱ tasmiɱ samaye bhikkhū bhāveti.|| ||

Samādhisambojjhaŋgo tasmiɱ samaye bhikkhūno bhāvanāpāripūriɱ gacchati.|| ||

So tathā samāhitaɱ cittaɱ sādhukaɱ ajjh'upekkhitā hoti.|| ||

Yasmiɱ samaye bhikkhave, bhikkhū tathā samāhitaɱ cittaɱ sādhukaɱ ajjh'upekkhitā hoti,||
upekhāsambojjhaŋgo tasmiɱ samaye bhikkhūno āraddho hoti.|| ||

Upekhāsambojjh-a-ŋgaɱ tasmiɱ samaye bhikkhū bhāveti.|| ||

Upekhāsambojjhaŋgo tasmiɱ samaye bhikkhūno bhāvanāpāripūriɱ gacchati.|| ||

Evaɱ bhāvitā kho bhikkhave cattāro sati-paṭṭhānā evaɱ bahulī-katā satta bojjhaŋge paripūrenti.|| ||

 

iii

 

Kathaɱ bhāvitā ca bhikkhave satta bojjhaŋgā kathaɱ bahulī-katā vijjā-vimuttiɱ paripūrenti:|| ||

Idh'bhikkhave, bhikkhū sati-sambojjh-a-ŋgaɱ bhāveti viveka-nissitaɱ||
virāga-nissitaɱ nirodha-nissitaɱ vossagga-pariṇāmi.|| ||

Dhammavicayasambojjh-a-ŋgaɱ bhāveti vivekanisissitaɱ virāga-nissitaɱ nirodha-nissitaɱ vossagga-pariṇāmiɱ.|| ||

Viriyasambojjh-a-ŋgaɱ bhāveti viveka-nissitaɱ virāga-nissitaɱ nirodha-nissitaɱ vossagga-pariṇāmiɱ.|| ||

Pītisambojjhagaɱ bhāveti viveka-nissitaɱ virāga-nissitaɱ nirodha-nissitaɱ vossagga-pariṇāmi.|| ||

Passaddhisambojjh-a-ŋgaɱ bhāveti viveka-nissitaɱ virāga-nissitaɱ nirodha-nissitaɱ vossagga-pariṇāmiɱ.|| ||

Samādhisambojjh-a-ŋgaɱ bhāveti viveka-nissitaɱ virāga-nissitaɱ nirodha-nissitaɱ vossagga-pariṇāmiɱ.|| ||

Upekkhāsambojjh-a-ŋgaɱ bhāveti viveka-nissitaɱ virāga-nissitaɱ nirodha-nissitaɱ vossagga-pariṇāmiɱ.|| ||

Evaɱ bhāvitā kho bhikkhave, satta bojjhaŋgā evaɱ bahulī-katā vijjā-vimuttiɱ paripūrentī ti.|| ||

Yathā purimasuttanto evam vitthāretabbaɱ|| ||
