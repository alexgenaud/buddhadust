[1][pts][olds] Evam me sutaɱ:|| ||

3. Katamā ca bhikkhave, iddhi?|| ||

Idha, bhikkhave, bhikkhu aneka-vihitaɱ iddhi-vidhaɱ pacc'anubhoti:|| ||

Eko pi hutvā bahudhā ahesuɱ.|| ||

Bahudhā hutvā eko ahesuɱ.|| ||

Āvībhāvaɱ tiro-bhāvaɱ tiro-kuḍḍaɱ tiro-pākāraɱ tiro-pabbataɱ asajja-mānā agamaɱsu seyyathā pi ākāse.|| ||

Paṭhaviyaɱ pi ummujjani-mujjaɱ akaɱsu||
seyyathā pi udake.|| ||

Udake pi abhijjamānā agamaɱsu||
seyyathā pi paṭhaviyaɱ.|| ||

Ākāse pi pallaṅkena agamiɱsu||
seyyathā pi pakkhī sakuṇo.|| ||

Ime pi candima-suriye evaɱ mahiddhike evaɱ mah-ā-nubhāve pāṇinā parāmasiɱsu parimajjiɱsu.|| ||

Yāva Brahma-lokā pi kāyena vasaṅ vatteti.|| ||

Ayaɱ vuccati bhikkhave iddhi.|| ||
