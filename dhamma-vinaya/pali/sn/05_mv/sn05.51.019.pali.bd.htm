Saɱyutta Nikāya,
V: Mahā-Vagga
51. Iddhipāda Saɱyutta

Sutta 19

Desanā or Bhāvanā Suttaɱ

Adapted from the 1995 edition of the digital version of the Sri Lanka Buddha Jayanti Tripitaka Series.

 

 

[1][pts][olds] Evam me sutaɱ:|| ||

2. Iddhiñ vo bhikkhave, desessāmi iddhi-pādañ ca iddhi-pādabhāvanañ ca iddhi-pādabhāvAnāgāminiñ ca paṭipadaɱ.|| ||

Taɱ suṇātha.|| ||

3. Katamā ca bhikkhave, iddhi?|| ||

Idha, bhikkhave, bhikkhu aneka-vihitaɱ iddhi-vidhaɱ paccanubhoti:|| ||

Eko pi hutvā bahudhā ahesuɱ.|| ||

Bahudhā hutvā eko ahesuɱ.|| ||

Āvībhāvaɱ tiro-bhāvaɱ tiro-kuḍḍaɱ tiro-pākāraɱ tiro-pabbataɱ asajja-mānā agamaɱsu seyyathā pi ākāse.|| ||

Paṭhaviyaɱ pi ummujjani-mujjaɱ akaɱsu seyyathā pi udake.|| ||

Udake pi abhijjamānā agamaɱsu seyyathā pi paṭhaviyaɱ.|| ||

Ākāse pi pallaŋkena agamiɱsu seyyathā pi pakkhī sakuṇo.|| ||

Ime pi candima-suriye evaɱ mahiddhike evaɱ mah-ā-nubhāve pāṇinā parāmasiɱsu parimajjiɱsu.|| ||

Yāva Brahma-lokā pi kāyena vasaŋ vatteti.|| ||

Ayaɱ vuccati bhikkhave iddhi.|| ||

4. Katamo ca bhikkhave, iddhi-pādo?|| ||

Yo bhikkhave, Maggo yā paṭipadā iddhi-lābhāya iddhi-paṭi-lābhāya saŋvattati.|| ||

Ayaɱ vuccati bhikkhave, iddhi-pādo.|| ||

5. Katamā ca bhikkhave, iddhi-pādabhāvanā?|| ||

Idha bhikkhave, bhikkhu||
chanda-samādhi-padhāna-sankhāra-samannāgataɱ iddhi-pādaɱ bhāveti,||
viriya-samādhi-padhāna-sankhāra-samannāgataɱ iddhi-pādaɱ bhāveti,||
citta-samādhi-padhāna-sankhāra-samannāgataɱ iddhi-pādaɱ bhāveti,||
vīmaɱsā-samādhi-padhāna-sankhāra-samannāgataɱ iddhi-pādaɱ bhāveti,||
ayaɱ vuccati bhikkhave, iddhi-pādabhāvanā.|| ||

6. Katamā ca bhikkhave, iddhi-pādabhāvAnāgāminī paṭipadā?|| ||

Ayam eva Ariyo Aṭṭhaŋgiko Maggo.|| ||

Seyyath'īdaɱ:||
sammā-diṭṭhi||
sammā-saŋkappo||
sammā-vācā||
sammā-kammanto||
sammā ājīvo||
sammā-vāyāmo||
sammā-sati||
sammā-samādhi.|| ||

Ayaɱ vuccati bhikkhave, iddhi-pādabhāvAnāgāminī paṭipadā ti.|| ||
