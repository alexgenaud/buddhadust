Adapted from the 1995 edition of the digital version of the Sri Lanka Buddha Jayanti Tripitaka Series.

[1][pts][olds] Evam me sutaɱ:|| ||

Ekaɱ samayam Bhagavā Sāvatthiyaɱ viharati Jetavane Anāthapindikassa ārāme.|| ||

2. "Cattāro me bhikkhave, iddhi-pādā.|| ||

Katame cattāro?|| ||

Idha, bhikkhave, bhikkhu chanda-samādhi-padhāna-sankhāra-samannāgataɱ||
iddhi-pādaɱ bhāveti.|| ||

Viriya-samādhi-padhāna-sankhāra-samannāgataɱ||
iddhi-pādaɱ bhāveti.|| ||

Citta-samādhi-padhāna-sankhāra-samannāgataɱ||
iddhi-pādaɱ bhāveti.|| ||

Vīmaɱsā-samādhi-padhāna-sankhāra-samannāgataɱ||
iddhi-pādaɱ bhāveti.|| ||

Ime kho bhikkhave, cattāro iddhi-pādā.|| ||
