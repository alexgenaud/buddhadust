Adapted from the 1995 edition of the digital version of the Sri Lanka Buddha Jayanti Tripitaka Series.

 

 

[1][pts][bodh] Evam me sutaɱ:|| ||

Ekaɱ samayam Bhagavā Sāvatthiyaɱ viharati.|| ||

"Akusale ca vo bhikkhave, dhamme desissāmi||

Idaɱ vuccati bhikkhave, akusalā dhammā.|| ||

Katame ca bhikkhave, kusalā dhammā?|| ||

Seyyath'īdaɱ:|| ||

Sammā-diṭṭhi,||
sammā-saŋkappo,||
sammā-vācā,||
