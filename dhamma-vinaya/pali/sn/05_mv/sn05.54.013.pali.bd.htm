54 Ānāpāna Saɱyutta

2. Ānanda
or
Dutiyo vaggo

Sutta 13

Ānanda Suttaɱ

Adapted from the 1995 edition of the digital version of the Sri Lanka Buddha Jayanti Tripitaka Series and proofed against, and mostly resolved to the 1994 Pali Text Society Saɱyutta-Nikaya Part V, edited by M. Leon Feer

 

 

[1-2][pts][than][bodh] Sāvatthi|| ||

Atha kho āyasmā Ānando yena Bhagavā ten'upasankami.||
Upasaŋkamitvā Bhagavāntaɱ abhivādetvā eka-m-antaɱ nisīdi.||
Eka-m-antaɱ nisinno kho āyasmā Ānando Bhagavāntaɱ etad avoca:|| ||

[329] 3. "Atthi nu kho bhante, eko dhammo bhāvito bahulī-kato cattāro dhamme paripūreti;||
cattāro dhammā bhāvitā bahulī-katā satta dhamme paripūrenti;||
satta dhammā bhāvitā bahulī-katā dve dhamme paripūrentī" ti?|| ||

"Atthi kho Ānanda eko dhammo bhāvito bahulī-kato cattāro dhamme paripūreti.;||
cattāro dhammā bhāvitā bahulī-katā satta-dhamme paripūrenti;||
satta dhammā bhāvitā bahulī-katā dve dhamme paripūrenti" ti.|| ||

4. "Katamo pana bhante, eko dhammo bhāvito bahulī-kato cattāro dhamme paripūreti,||
cattāro dhammā bhāvitā bahulī-katā satta dhamme paripūrenti,||
satta dhammā bhāvitā bahulī-katā dve dhamme paripūrentī" ti?|| ||

Ānāpāna-satisamādhi kho Ānanda eko dhammo bhāvito bahulī-kato cattāro sati-paṭṭhāne paripūreti,||
cattāro sati-paṭṭhānā bhāvitā bahulī-katā satta bojjhaŋge paripūrenti,||
satta bojjh'aŋgā bhāvitā bahulī-katā vijjā-vimuttiɱ paripūrenti.|| ||

 

i

 

5. Kathaɱ bhāvito ca, ānāpāna-sati-samādhi kathaɱ bahulī-kato cattāro sati-paṭṭhāne paripūreti?|| ||

6. Idha, Ānanda, bhikkhū araññagato vā rukkha-mūla-gato vā suññāragato vā nisīdati pallaŋkaɱ ābhujitvā ujuɱ kāyaɱ paṇidhāya parimukhaɱ satiɱ upaṭṭha-petvā so sato va assa-sati sato va passa-sati.|| ||

7. Dīghaɱ vā assa-santo dīghaɱ assasāmīti pajānāti.||
Dīghaɱ vā passa-santo dīghaɱ passasāmīti pajānāti.|| ||

Rassaɱ vā assa-santo rassaɱ assasāmīti pajānāti.||
Rassaɱ vā passa-santo rassaɱ passasāmīti pajānāti.|| ||

[8] Sabba-kāyapaṭisaŋvedī assasi-s-sāmīti sikkhati.||
Sabba-kāyapaṭisaŋvedī passissāmīti sikkhati.|| ||

Passam-bhayaɱ kāya-sankhāraɱ assasi-s-sāmīti sikkhati.||
Passam-bhayaɱ kāya-sankhāraɱ passasi-s-sāmīti sikkhati.|| ||

[9] Pīti-paṭisaŋvedī assasi-s-sāmīti sikkhati.||
Pīti-paṭisaŋvedī passasi-s-sāmīti sikkhati.|| ||

Sukha-paṭisaŋvedī assasi-s-sāmīti sikkhati.||
Sukha-paṭisaŋvedī passasi-s-sāmīti sikkhati.|| ||

Citta-sankhāra-paṭisaŋvedī assasi-s-sāmīti sikkhati.||
Citta-sankhāra-paṭisaŋvedī passasi-s-sāmīti sikkhati.|| ||

Passam-bhayaɱ citta-sankhāraɱ assasi-s-sāmīti sikkhati.||
Passam-bhayaɱ citta-sankhāraɱ passasi-s-sāmīti sikkhati.|| ||

[10] Citta-sankhāra-paṭisaŋvedī assasi-s-sāmīti sikkhati.||
Citta-sankhāra-paṭisaŋvedī passasi-s-sāmīti sikkhati.|| ||

Passam-bhayaɱ citta-sankhāraɱ assasi-s-sāmīti sikkhati.||
Passam-bhayaɱ citta-sankhāraɱ passasi-s-sāmīti sikkhati.|| ||

Citta-paṭisaŋvedī assasi-s-sāmīti sikkhati.||
Citta-paṭisaŋvedī passasi-s-sāmīti sikkhati.|| ||

[11] Abhi-p-pamodayaɱ cittaɱ assasi-s-sāmīti sikkhati.||
Abhi-p-pamodayaɱ cittaɱ passasi-s-sāmīti sikkhati.|| ||

Samādahaɱ cittaɱ assasi-s-sāmīti sikkhati.||
Samādahaɱ cittaɱ passasi-s-sāmīti sikkhati.|| ||

Vimocayaɱ cittaɱ assasi-s-sāmīti sikkhati.||
Vimocayaɱ cittaɱ passasi-s-sāmīti sikkhati.|| ||

[12] Anicc-ā-nupassī assasi-s-sāmīti sikkhati.||
Anicc-ā-nupassī passasi-s-sāmīti sikkhati.|| ||

Virāg-ā-nupassī assasi-s-sāmīti sikkhati.||
Virāg-ā-nupassī passasi-s-sāmīti sikkhati.|| ||

Nirodh-ā-nupassī assasi-s-sāmīti sikkhati.||
Nirodh-ā-nupassī passasi-s-sāmīti sikkhati.|| ||

Paṭinissagg-ā-nupassī passasi-s-sāmīti sikkhati.||
Paṭinissagg-ā-nupassī passasi-s-sāmīti sikkhati.|| ||

13. Yasmiɱ samaye Ānanda bhikkhū dīghaɱ vā assa-santo dīghaɱ assasāmīti pajānāti,||
dīghaɱ vā passa-santo dīghaɱ passasāmī ti pajānāti,||
rassaɱ vā assa-santo rassaɱ assasāmīti pajānāti,||
rassaɱ vā passa-santo rassaɱ passasāmīti pajānāti,||
sabba-kāyapaṭisaŋvedī assasi-s-sāmīti sikkhati,||
sabba-kāyapaṭisaŋvedī passasi-s-sāmīti sikkhati,||
passam-bhayaɱ kāya-sankhāraɱ assasi-s-sāmīti sikkhati,||
passam-bhayaɱ kāya-sankhāraɱ passasi-s-sāmīti sikkhati,||
kāye kāy'ānupassī Ānanda,||
bhikkhū tasmiɱ samaye viharati ātāpī sampajāno satimā vineyya loke abhijjhā-domanassaɱ.|| ||

Taɱ kissa hetu?|| ||

14. Kāyaññatarāhaɱ Ānanda etaɱ vadāmi,||
yad idaɱ [330] assāsapassāsaɱ.|| ||

Tasmā ti ha Ānanda, kāye kāy'ānupassī bhikkhū tasmiɱ samaye viharati ātāpī sampajāno satimā vineyya loke abhijjhā-domanassaɱ.|| ||

15. Yasmiɱ samaye Ānanda, bhikkhū pīti-paṭisaŋvedī assasi-s-sāmīti sikkhati,||
pīti-paṭisaŋvedī passasissāmiti sikkhati,||
sukha-paṭisaŋvedī assasi-s-sāmīti sikkhati,||
sukha-paṭiɱvedī passasissāmiti sikkhati,||
citta-sankhāra-paṭisaŋvedī assasi-s-sāmīti sikkhati,||
citta-sankhāra-paṭisaŋvedī passasi-s-sāmīti sikkhati,||
passam-bhayaɱ citta-sankhāraɱ assasi-s-sāmīti sikkhati,||
passam-bhayaɱ citta-sankhāraɱ passasi-s-sāmīti sikkhati,||
vedanāsu vedan'ānupassī Ānanda,||
bhikkhū tasmiɱ samaye viharati ātāpī sampajāno satimā vineyaya loke abhijjhā-domanassaɱ.|| ||

Taɱ kissa hetu?|| ||

16. Vedanaññatarāhaɱ Ānanda, etaɱ vadāmi,||
yad idaɱ assāsa-passāsānaɱ sādhukaɱ mana-sikāraɱ.|| ||

Tasmā ti ha Ānanda, vedanāsu vedan'ānupassī bhikkhū tasmiɱ samaye viharati ātāpī sampajāno satimā vineyya loke abhijjhā-domanassaɱ.|| ||

17. Yasmiɱ samaye Ānanda, bhikkhū citta-paṭisaŋvedī assasi-s-sāmīti sikkhati,||
citta-paṭisaŋvedī passasi-s-sāmīti sikkhati,||
abhippamodayaɱ cittaɱ assasi-s-sāmīti sikkhati,||
abhippamodayaɱ cittaɱ passasi-s-sāmīti sikkhati,||
samādahaɱ cittaɱ assasi-s-sāmīti sikkhati,||
samādahaɱ cittaɱ passasi-s-sāmīti sikkhati,||
vimocayaɱ cittaɱ assasi-s-sāmīti sikkhati,||
vimocayaɱ cittaɱ passasi-s-sāmīti sikkhati,||
citte citt'ānupassī Ānanda, bhikkhū tasmiɱ samaye viharati ātāpī sampajāno satimā vineyya loke abhijjhā-domanassaɱ.|| ||

Taɱ kissa hetu?|| ||

18. Nāhaɱ Ānanda, muṭṭha-s-satissa asampajānassa ānāpāna-sati-samādhibhāvanaɱ vadāmi.|| ||

Tasmā ti h'Ānanda, citte citt'ānupassī bhikkhū tasmiɱ samaye viharati ātāpī sampajāno satimā vineyya loke abhijjhā-domanassaɱ.|| ||

19. Yasmiɱ samaye Ānanda, bhikkhū anicc'ānupassī||
assasi-s-sāmīti sikkhati,||
anicc'ānupassī passasi-s-sāmīti sikkhati,||
virāg-ā-nupassī assasi-s-sāmīti sikkhati,||
virāgānupassi passasi-s-sāmīti sikkhati,||
nirodh-ā-nupassī assasi-s-sāmīti sikkhati,||
nirodh-ā-nupassī passasi-s-sāmīti sikkhati,||
paṭinissagg-ā-nupassī assasi-s-sāmīti sikkhati,||
paṭinissagg-ā-nupassī passasi-s-sāmīti sikkhati,||
dhammesu Dhamm'ānupassī Ānanda,||
bhikkhū tasmiɱ samaye viharati ātāpī sampajāno satimā vineyya loke abhijjhā-domanassaɱ.|| ||

So yaɱ taɱ hoti abhijjhā-domanassānaɱ pahānaɱ taɱ paññāya disvā sādhukaɱ [331] ajjh'upekkhitā hoti.|| ||

Tasmā ti ha Ānanda, dhammesu Dhamm'ānupassī bhikkhū tasmiɱ samaye viharati ātāpī sampajāno satimā vineyya loke abhijjhā-domanassaɱ.|| ||

20. Evaɱ bhāvito kho Ānanda ānāpāna-sati-samādhi evaɱ bahulī-kato cattāro sati-paṭṭhāne paripūreti.|| ||

21. Kathaɱ bhāvitā ca Ānanda cattāro sati-paṭṭhānā kathaɱ bahulī-katā satta sambojjhaŋge paripūrenti?|| ||

22. Yasmiɱ samaye Ānanda bhikkhū kāye kāy'ānupassī viharati upaṭṭhitāsati tasmiɱ samaye Ānanda bhikkhuno sati hoti asammuṭṭhā.|| ||

Yasmiɱ samaye Ānanda bhikkhūno upaṭṭhitā sati asammuṭṭhā sati-sambojjhaŋgo tasmiɱ samaye bhikkhūno āraddho hoti.|| ||

Satisambojjh-a-ŋgaɱ tasmiɱ samaye Ānanda bhikkhū bhāveti||
sati-sambojjhaŋgo tasmiɱ samaye bhikkhūno bhāvanā-pāripūriɱ gacchati.|| ||

So tathā sato viharanto taɱ dhammaɱ paññāya pavicinati pavicarati parivīmaɱsam āpajjati.|| ||

23. Yasmiɱ samaye Ānanda, bhikkhū tathā sato viharanto taɱ dhammaɱ paññāya pavicināti pavicarati parivīmaɱsam āpajjati,||
dhamma-vicaya-sambojjhaŋgo tasmiɱ samaye bhikkhūno āraddho hoti,||
dhamma-vicaya-sambojjh-a-ŋgaɱ tasmiɱ samaye bhikkhū bhāveti,||
dhamma-vicaya-sambojjhaŋgo tasmiɱ samaye bhikkhūno bhāvanāpāripūriɱ gacchati.|| ||

Tassa taɱ dhammaɱ paññāya pavicanato pavicarato parivīmaɱsam āpajjato āraddhaɱ hoti viriyaɱ asallīnaɱ.|| ||

24. Yasmiɱ samaye Ānanda, bhikkhūno taɱ dhammaɱ paññāya pavicinato pavicarato parivīmaɱsam āpajjato [332] āraddhaɱ hoti viriyaɱ asallīnaɱ,||
viriya-sambojjhaŋgo tasmiɱ samaye bhikkhūno āraddho hoti.|| ||

Viriyasambojjh-a-ŋgaɱ tasmiɱ samaye bhikkhū bhāveti,||
viriya-sambojjhaŋgo tasmiɱ samaye bhikkhūno bhāvanā pāripūriɱ gacchati.|| ||

Āraddha viriyassa uppajjati pīti nirāmisā.|| ||

25. Yasmiɱ samaye Ānanda, bhikkhūno āraddha-viriyassa uppajjati pīti nirāmisā,||
pīti-sambojjhaŋgo tasmiɱ samaye bhikkhūno āraddho hoti.|| ||

Pītisambojjh-a-ŋgaɱ tasmiɱ samaye bhikkhū bhāveti,||
pīti-sambojjhaŋgo tasmiɱ samaye bhikkhūno bhāvanā pāripūriɱ gacchati.|| ||

Pītimanassa kāyo pi passambhati,
cittam pi passambhati.|| ||

26. Yasmiɱ samaye Ānanda bhikkhūno pītimanassa kāyo pi passambhati,||
cittam pi passambhati,||
passaddhi-sambojjhaŋgo tasmiɱ samaye bhikkhūno āraddho hoti.|| ||

Passaddhisambojjh-a-ŋgaɱ tasmiɱ samaye bhikkhū bhāveti,||
passaddhi-sambojjhaŋgo tasmiɱ samaye bhikkhūno bhāvanā pāripūriɱ gacchati.|| ||

Passaddhakāyassa sukhino cittaɱ samādhiyati.|| ||

27. Yasmiɱ samaye Ānanda bhikkhūno passaddha-kāyassa sukhino cittaɱ samādhiyati,||
samādhi-sambojjhaŋgo tasmiɱ samaye bhikkhūno āraddho hoti.|| ||

Samādhisambojjh-a-ŋgaɱ tasmiɱ samayo bhikkhū bhāveti,||
samādhi-sambojjhaŋgo tasmiɱ samaye bhikkhūno bhāvanā pāripūriɱ gacchati.|| ||

So tathā samāhitaɱ cittaɱ sādhukaɱ ajjh'upekkhitā hoti.|| ||

28. Yasmiɱ samaye Ānanda bhikkhū tathā samāhitaɱ cittaɱ sādhukaɱ ajjh'upekkhitā hoti,||
upekhāsambojjhaŋgo tasmiɱ samaye bhikkhūno āraddho hoti.|| ||

Upekhāsambojjh-a-ŋgaɱ tasmiɱ samaye bhikkhū bhāveti,
upekhāsambojjhaŋgo tasmiɱ samaye bhikkhūno bhāvanā pāripūriɱ gacchati.|| ||
