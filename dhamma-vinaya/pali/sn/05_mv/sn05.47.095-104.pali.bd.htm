Sutta 95

I. Abhiññā

[95.1][pts] Evam me sutaṃ:|| ||

Ekaṃ samayam Bhagavā Sāvatthiyaṃ viharati.|| ||

Tatra kho Bhagavā bhikkhū āmantesi:|| ||

"Bhikkhavo" ti.|| ||

"Bhadante" ti||
te bhikkhū Bhagavato paccassosuṃ.|| ||

Bhagavā etad avoca:|| ||

"Cattāro me bhikkhave, oghā.|| ||

Katame cattāro?|| ||

Kām'ogho||
bhav'ogho||
diṭṭh'ogho||
avijj'ogho.|| ||

Ime kho bhikkhave, cattāro oghā.|| ||

Imāsaṇ kho bhikkhave, catunnaṃ oghānaṃ abhiññāya||

Idha bhikkhave, bhikkhu kāye kāy'ānupassī viharati||
viveka-nissitaṃ,||
virāga-nissitaṃ,||
nirodha-nissitaṃ,||
