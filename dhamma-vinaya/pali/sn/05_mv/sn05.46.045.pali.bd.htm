5. Mahā-Vagga
46. Bojjhanga Saɱyutta
5. Cakka-Vatti Vagga

Sutta 45

Paññavā Suttaɱ

Adapted from the 1995 edition of the digital version of the Sri Lanka Buddha Jayanti Tripitaka Series.

 

[100]

[1][pts][bodh] Evam me sutaɱ:|| ||

Ekaɱ samayam Bhagavā Sāvatthiyaɱ viharati Jetavane Anāthapindikassa ārāme.|| ||

Atha kho aññataro bhikkhu yena Bhagavā ten'upasankami.|| ||

Upasaŋkamitvā Bhagavantaɱ abhivādetvā eka-m-antaɱ nisīdi.|| ||

Eka-m-antaɱ nisinno kho so bhikkhu Bhagavantaɱ etad avoca:|| ||

"'Paññavā aneḷa-mūgo!|| ||

Paññavā aneḷa-mūgo!' ti bhante, vuccati.|| ||

Kittāvatā nu kho bhante,||
'Paññavā aneḷa-mūgo' ti vuccatī" ti?|| ||

"Sattannaɱ kho bhikkhu,||
bojjh'aŋgānaɱ bhāvitattā||
bahulī-katattā||
'Paññavā aneḷa-mūgo' ti vuccati.|| ||

Katamesaŋ sattannaɱ?|| ||

Sati-sambojjh'aŋgassa||
dhamma-vicaya-sambojjh'aŋgassa||
viriya-sambojjh'aŋgassa||
pīti-sambojjh'aŋgassa||
passaddhi-sambojjh'aŋgassa||
samādhi-sambojjh'aŋgassa||
upekhā-sambojjh'aŋgassa.|| ||

Imesaɱ kho bhikkhu, sattannaɱ bojjh'aŋgānaɱ||
bhāvitattā||
bahulī-katattā||
'Paññavā aneḷa-mūgo' ti vuccatī" ti.|| ||
