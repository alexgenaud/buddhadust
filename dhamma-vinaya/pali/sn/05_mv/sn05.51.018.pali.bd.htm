Saɱyutta Nikāya,
V: Mahā-Vagga
51. Iddhipāda Saɱyutta

Sutta 18

Bhikkhu Suttaɱ

Adapted from the 1995 edition of the digital version of the Sri Lanka Buddha Jayanti Tripitaka Series.

 

 

[1][pts][olds] Evam me sutaɱ:|| ||

2. Catunnaɱ bhikkhave, iddhi-pādānaɱ bhāvitattā bahulī-katattā bhikkhu āsavānaɱ khayā anāsavaɱ ceto-vimuttiɱ paññā-vimuttiɱ diṭṭhe'va dhamme sayaɱ abhiññā sacchi-katvā upasampajja viharati.|| ||

Katamesaŋ catunnaɱ?|| ||

3. Idha bhikkhave, bhikkhu||
chanda-samādhi-padhāna-sankhāra-samannāgataɱ iddhi-pādaɱ bhāveti,||
viriya-samādhi-padhāna-sankhāra-samannāgataɱ iddhi-pādaɱ bhāveti,||
citta-samādhi-padhāna-sankhāra-samannāgataɱ iddhi-pādaɱ bhāveti,||
vīmaɱsā-samādhi-padhāna-sankhāra-samannāgataɱ iddhi-pādaɱ bhāveti,||
ye hi keci bhikkhave atītam addhānaɱ samaṇā vā brāhmaṇā vā aneka-vihitaɱ iddhi-vidhaɱ paccanubhosuɱ eko pi hutvā bahudhā ahesuɱ,||
bahudhā pi hutvā eko ahesuɱ āvībhāvaɱ tiro-bhāvaɱ tiro-kuḍḍaɱ tiro-pākāraɱ tiro-pabbataɱ asajja-mānā agamaɱsu seyyathā pi ākāse.|| ||

Imesaŋ kho bhikkhave, catunnaɱ iddhi-pādānaɱ bhāvitattā bahulī-katattā bhikkhu āsavānaɱ khayā anāsavaɱ ceto-vimuttiɱ paññā-vimuttiɱ diṭṭhe'va dhamme sayaɱ abhiññā sacchi-katvā upasampajja viharatī ti.|| ||
