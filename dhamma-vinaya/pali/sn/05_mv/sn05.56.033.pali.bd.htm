Saɱyutta Nikāya
5. Mahā-Vagga
56. Sacca Saɱyutta
4. Siɱsapā Vagga

Sutta 33

Daṇḍa Suttaɱ

Adapted from the 1995 edition of the digital version of the Sri Lanka Buddha Jayanti Tripitaka Series.

 

[439]

[1][pts][bodh] Evam me sutaɱ:|| ||

Ekaɱ samayam Bhagavā bhikkhū āmantesi:|| ||

"Bhikkhavo" ti.|| ||

"Bhadante" ti te bhikkhū Bhagavato paccassosuɱ.|| ||

Bhagavā etad avoca:|| ||

"Seyyathā pi bhikkhave, daṇḍo upari vehesaṅ khitto||
sakim pi mulena nipatati,||
sakim pi majjhena nipatati,||
sakim pi aggena nipatati.|| ||

Evam eva kho bhikkhave,||
avijjā-nīvaraṇā sattā taṇhā-saɱyojanā sandhāvannā||
sakim pi asmā lokā paraɱ lokaɱ gacchanti,||
sakim pi parasmā lokā imaɱ lokaɱ āga-c-chanti.|| ||

Taɱ kissa hetu?|| ||

Adiṭṭhattā bhikkhave,||
catunnaɱ ariya-saccānaɱ.|| ||

Katamesaṅ catunnaɱ?|| ||

Dukkhassa ariya-saccassa,||
dukkha-samudayassa ariya-saccassa,||
dukkha-nirodhassa ariya-saccassa,||
dukkha-nirodha-gāminī paṭipadāya ariya-saccassa.|| ||

[440] Tasmā'ti ha bhikkhave,||
'idaɱ dukkhan' ti yogo karaṇīyo,||
'ayaɱ dukkha-samudayo' ti yogo karaṇīyo,||
'ayaɱ dukkha-nirodho' ti yogo karaṇīyo,||
'ayaɱ dukkha-nirodha-gāminī paṭipadā' ti yogo karaṇīyo" ti.|| ||
