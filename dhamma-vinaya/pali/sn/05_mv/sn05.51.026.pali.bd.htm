Saɱyutta Nikāya,
V: Mahā-Vagga
51. Iddhipāda Saɱyutta
III. Ayoguḷa-vaggo

Sutta 26

Dutiya Phala Suttaɱ

Adapted from the 1995 edition of the digital version of the Sri Lanka Buddha Jayanti Tripitaka Series.

 

 

[1][pts][olds] Evam me sutaɱ:|| ||

2. Cattāro me bhikkhave, iddhi-pādā.|| ||

Katame cattāro?|| ||

Idha, bhikkhave, bhikkhu chanda-samādhi-padhāna-sankhāra-samannāgataɱ iddhi-pādaɱ bhāveti.|| ||

Viriya-samādhi-padhāna-sankhāra-samannāgataɱ iddhi-pādaɱ bhāveti:|| ||

Citta-samādhi-padhāna-sankhāra-samannāgataɱ iddhi-pādaɱ bhāveti:|| ||

Vimaɱsā-samādhi-padhāna-sankhāra-samannāgataɱ iddhi-pādaɱ bhāveti:|| ||

Ime kho bhikkhave, cattāro iddhi-pādā.|| ||

3. Imesaŋ kho bhikkhave, catunnaɱ iddhi-pādānaɱ bhāvitattā bahulī-katattā sattaphalā sattānisaɱsā pāṭikaŋkhā.|| ||

Katame sattaphalā satt'ānisaɱsā?|| ||

4. Diṭṭhe va dhamme paṭigacca aññaɱ ārādheti.|| ||

No ce diṭṭhe'va dhamme paṭigacca aññaɱ ārādheti||
atha maraṇa-kāle aññaɱ ārādheti.|| ||

No ce diṭṭhe'va dhamme paṭigacca aññaɱ ārādheti no ce maraṇa-kāle aññaɱ ārādheti,||
atha pañcantaɱ ora-m-bhāgiyānaɱ saŋyojanānaɱ parikkhayā antarā parinibbāyī hoti.|| ||

Upahacca parinibbāyī hoti.|| ||

Asankhāraparinibbāyī hoti.|| ||

Sasankhāra-parinibbāyī hoti.|| ||

Uddhaɱsoto hoti Akaniṭṭha-gāmī.|| ||

5. Imesaŋ kho bhikkhave, catunnaɱ iddhi-pādānaɱ bhāvitattā bahulī-katattā ime sattaphalā satt'ānisaɱsā pāṭikaŋkhā ti.|| ||
