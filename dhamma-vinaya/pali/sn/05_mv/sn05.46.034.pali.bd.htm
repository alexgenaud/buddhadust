IV. Nīvaraṇa-Vaggo

Sutta 34

Dutiya Kilesa Suttaɱ

Adapted from the 1995 edition of the digital version of the Sri Lanka Buddha Jayanti Tripitaka Series.

 

 

[1][pts][bodh] Evam me sutaɱ:|| ||

Ekaɱ samayam Bhagavā bhikkhū āmantesi:|| ||

"Bhikkhavo" ti.|| ||

"Bhadante" ti te bhikkhū Bhagavato paccassosuɱ.|| ||

Bhagavā etad avoca:|| ||

"Satt'ime bhikkhave, bojjh'aŋgā||
anāvaraṇā||
anīvaraṇā||
cetaso anupakkilesā||
bhāvitā||
bahulī-katā||
vijjā-vimutti-phala-sacchi-kiriyāya saŋvattanti.|| ||

Katame satta?|| ||

Sati-sambojjhaŋgo kho bhikkhave,||
anāvaraṇā||
anīvaraṇā||
cetaso anupakkilesā||
bhāvitā||
bahulī-katā||
vijjā-vimutti-phala-sacchi-kiriyāya saŋvattanti.|| ||

Dhamma-vicaya-sambojjhaŋgo kho bhikkhave,||
anāvaraṇā||
anīvaraṇā||
cetaso anupakkilesā||
bhāvitā||
bahulī-katā||
vijjā-vimutti-phala-sacchi-kiriyāya saŋvattanti.|| ||

Viriyasambojjhaŋgo kho bhikkhave,||
anāvaraṇā||
anīvaraṇā||
cetaso anupakkilesā||
bhāvitā||
bahulī-katā||
vijjā-vimutti-phala-sacchi-kiriyāya saŋvattanti.|| ||

Pīti-sambojjhaŋgo kho bhikkhave,||
anāvaraṇā||
anīvaraṇā||
cetaso anupakkilesā||
bhāvitā||
bahulī-katā||
vijjā-vimutti-phala-sacchi-kiriyāya saŋvattanti.|| ||

Passaddhi-sambojjhaŋgo kho bhikkhave,||

Upekhā-sambojjhaŋgo kho bhikkhave,||

cetaso anupakkilesā||
bhāvitā||
bahulī-katā||

Ime kho bhikkhave, satta bojjh'aŋgā||
anāvaraṇā||

bhāvitā||
bahulī-katā||
