Saɱyutta Nikāya,
V: Mahā-Vagga
56. Sacca Saɱyutta
IV. Siŋsapā Vagga

Sutta 36

Pāṇā Suttaɱ

Adapted from the 1995 edition of the digital version of the Sri Lanka Buddha Jayanti Tripitaka Series.

 

 

[1][pts][bodh][than] Evam me sutaɱ:|| ||

Ekaɱ samayam Bhagavā bhikkhū āmantesi:|| ||

"Bhikkhavo" ti.|| ||

"Bhadante" ti te bhikkhū Bhagavato paccassosuɱ.|| ||

Bhagavā etad avoca:|| ||

"Seyyathā pi bhikkhave, puriso yaɱ imasmiɱ Jambudīpe tiṇakaṭṭhasākhāpalāsaɱ tam chetvā ekajjhaɱ saɱhareyya.|| ||

Ekajjhaɱ saɱharitvā sūlaɱ kareyya.|| ||

Sūlaɱ karitvā ye mahā-samudde mahantakā pāṇā te mahantakesu sūlesu āvuṇeyya.|| ||

Ye mahā-samudde majjhimakā pāṇā te majjhimakesu sūlesu āvuṇeyya.|| ||

Ye mahā-samudde sukhumakā pāṇā te subumakesu sūlesu āvuṇeyya.|| ||

Apariyādinnā ca bhikkhave, mahā-samudde oḷārikā pāṇā assu.|| ||

Atha te imasmiɱ Jambudīpe tiṇakaṭṭhasākhāpalāsaɱ parikkhayaɱ pariyādānaɱ gaccheyya.|| ||

Ito bahutarā kho bhikkhave, mahā-samudde sukhumakā pāṇā,||
ye na sukarā sūlesu āvuṇituɱ.|| ||

Taɱ kissa hetu?|| ||

[442] Sukhumattā bhikkhave, atta-bhāvassa.|| ||

Evaɱ mahā kho bhikkhave, apāyo.|| ||

Evaɱ mahantasmā kho bhikkhave,||
apāyasmā parimutto diṭṭhi-sampanno puggalo yo "idaɱ dukkhanti" ti yathā-bhūtaɱ pajānāti.||
"Ayaɱ dukkha-samudayo" ti yathā-bhūtaɱ pajānāti.||
"Ayaɱ dukkha-nirodho" ti yathā-bhūtaɱ pajānāti.||
"Ayaɱ dukkha-nirodha-gāminī paṭipadā" ti yathā-bhūtaɱ pajānāti.|| ||

4. Tasmā ti ha bhikkhave, "idaɱ dukkhan" ti yogo karaṇīyo,||
"ayaɱ dukkha-samudayo" ti yogo karaṇīyo,||
"ayaɱ dukkha-nirodha-gāminī paṭipadā" ti yogo karaṇīyo ti.|| ||
