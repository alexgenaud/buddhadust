Saɱyutta Nikāya,
V: Mahā-Vagga
46. Bojjhanga Saɱyutta
IV. Nīvaraṇa-Vaggo

Sutta 41

Vidhā Suttaɱ

Adapted from the 1995 edition of the digital version of the Sri Lanka Buddha Jayanti Tripitaka Series.

 

 

[1][pts][bodh] Evam me sutaɱ:|| ||

Ekaɱ samayam Bhagavā Sāvatthiyaɱ viharati Jetavane Anāthapindikassa ārāme.|| ||

Tatra kho Bhagavā bhikkhū āmantesi Bhikkhavo ti.|| ||

Bhadante ti te bhikkhū Bhagavato paccassosuɱ.|| ||

Bhagavā etad avoca:|| ||

"Ye hi keci bhikkhave, atītam addhānaɱ samaṇā vā brāhmaṇā vā tisso vidhā pajahiɱsu||
sabbe te sattannaɱ bojjhaŋgānaɱ bhāvittatā bahulī-kattatā.|| ||

Ye hi keci bhikkhave, anāgatam addhānaɱ samaṇā vā brāhmaṇā vā tisso vidhā pajahissanti||
sabbe te sattannaɱ bojjhaŋgānaɱ bhāvittatā bahulī-katattā.|| ||

Ye hi keci bhikkhave, etarahi samaṇā vā brāhmaṇā vā tisso vidhā pajahanti,||
sabbe te sattannaɱ bojjhaŋgānaɱ bhāvittatā bahulī-kattatā.|| ||

Katamesaŋ sattannaɱ?|| ||

Sati-sambojjh-a-ŋgassa||
dhamma-vicaya-sambojjh-a-ŋgassa||
viriya-sambojjh-a-ŋgassa||
pīti-sambojjh-a-ŋgassa||
passaddhi-sambojjh-a-ŋgassa||
samādhi-sambojjh-a-ŋgassa||
upekhā-sambojjh-a-ŋgassa.|| ||

Ye hi keci bhikkhave, atītam addhānaɱ samaṇā vā brāhmaṇā vā tisso vidhā pajahiɱsu,||
sabbe te imesaŋ sattannaɱ bojjhaŋgānaɱ bhāvittatā bahulī-katattā.|| ||

Ye hi keci bhikkhave, anāgatam addhānaɱ samaṇā vā brāhmaṇā vā tisso vidhā pajahissanti,||
sabbe te imesaŋ sattannaɱ bojjhaŋgānaɱ bhāvittatā bahulī-katattā.|| ||

Ye hi keci bhikkhave, etarahi samaṇā vā brāhmaṇā vā tisso vidhā pajahanti,||
sabbe te imesaŋ ca sattannaɱ bojjhaŋgānaɱ bhāvittatā bahulī-katattā" ti.|| ||
