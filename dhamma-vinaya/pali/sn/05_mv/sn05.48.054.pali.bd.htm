Adapted from the 1995 edition of the digital version of the Sri Lanka Buddha Jayanti Tripitaka Series.

 

 

[1][pts] Evam me sutaɱ:|| ||

Ekaɱ samayaɱ Bhagavā Kosambīyaɱ viharati Ghositārāme.|| ||

Tatra kho Bhagavā bhikkhū āmantesi:|| ||

"Seyyathā pi bhikkhave, yāni kānici jaŋgamānaɱ pāṇānaɱ pada-jātāni,||
sabbāni tāni hatthi-pade samodhānaɱ gacchanti,||
hatthi-padaɱ tesaŋ aggamakkhāyati||
yad idaɱ mahantattena.|| ||

Evam eva kho bhikkhave,||
yāni kānici padāni bodhāya saŋvattanti,||
paññ'indriyaɱ padaɱ tesaŋ aggamakkhāyati||
yad idaɱ bodhāya.|| ||

Katamāni ca bhikkhave, padāni bodhāya saŋvattanti?|| ||

Saddh'indriyaɱ bhikkhave padaɱ,||
taɱ bodhāya saŋvattati.|| ||

Viriy'indriyaɱ padaɱ,||
taɱ bodhāya saŋvattati.|| ||

Sat'indriyaɱ padaɱ,||
taɱ bodhāya saŋvattati.|| ||

Samādh'indriyaɱ padaɱ,||
taɱ bodhāya saŋvattati.|| ||

Paññ'indriyaɱ padaɱ,||
taɱ bodhāya saŋvattati.|| ||

"Seyyathā pi bhikkhave, yāni kānici jaŋgamānaɱ pāṇānaɱ pada-jātāni,||
sabbāni tāni hatthi-pade samodhānaɱ gacchanti,||
hatthi-padaɱ tesaŋ aggamakkhāyati||
yad idaɱ mahantattena.|| ||

Evam eva kho bhikkhave,||
yāni kānici padāni bodhāya saŋvattanti,||
paññ'indriyaɱ padaɱ tesaŋ aggamakkhāyati||
