Saṃyutta Nikāya
5. Mahā-Vagga
55. Sot'Āpatti Saṃyutta
7. Mahā-Pañña Vagga

Sutta 65

Gambhīra-Paññā Suttaṃ

Adapted from the 1995 edition of the digital version of the Sri Lanka Buddha Jayanti Tripitaka Series.

 

[412]

[1][pts] Evam me sutaṃ:|| ||

Ekaṃ samayam Bhagavā Sāvatthiyaṃ viharati Jetavane Anāthapiṇḍikassa ārāme.|| ||

Tatra kho Bhagavā bhikkhū āmantesi:|| ||

"Bhikkhavo" ti.|| ||

"Bhadante" ti te bhikkhū Bhagavato paccassosuṃ.|| ||

Bhagavā etad avoca:|| ||

"Cattāro me bhikkhave, dhammā bhāvitā||
bahulī-katā||
gambhīra-paññāattāya saṃvaṭṭanti.|| ||

Katame cattāro?|| ||

Sappurisa-saṇsevo||
Sad'Dhamma-savanaṃ||
yoniso-mana-sikāro||
Dhamm-ā-nu-Dhamma-paṭipatti.|| ||

Ime kho bhikkhave, cattāro dhammā bhāvitā||
bahulī-katā||
gambhīra-paññāattāya saṃvaṭṭantī" ti.|| ||
