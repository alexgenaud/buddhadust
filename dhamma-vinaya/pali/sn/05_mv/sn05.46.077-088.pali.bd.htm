Sutta 77

Paṭhama Pācīna Suttaṃ

[1][pts] Evam me sutaṃ:|| ||

Ekaṃ samayam Bhagavā Sāvatthiyaṃ viharati.|| ||

Tatra kho Bhagavā bhikkhū āmantesi:|| ||

"Bhikkhavo" ti.|| ||

"Bhadante" ti||
te bhikkhū Bhagavato paccassosuṃ.|| ||

Bhagavā etad avoca:|| ||

Seyyathā pi bhikkhave, Gaṅgā-nadī||
pācīna-ninnā,||
pācīna-poṇā||
pācīna-pabbhārā.|| ||

Evam eva kho bhikkhave, bhikkhu satta bojjh'aṅge bhāvento||
satta bojjh'aṅge bahulī-karonto||
Nibbāna-ninno hoti,||
Nibbāna-poṇo||
Nibbāna-pabbhāro.|| ||

Kathañ ca bhikkhave, bhikkhu satta bojjh'aṅge bhāvento||
satta bojjh'aṅge bahulī-karonto||
Nibbāna-ninno hoti||
Nibbāna-poṇo||
Nibbāna-pabbhāro?|| ||

Idha bhikkhave, Bhikkhu||
sati-sambojjh'aṅgaṃ bhāveti,||
viveka-nissitaṃ,||
virāga-nissitaṃ,||
nirodha-nissitaṃ,||
vossagga-pariṇāmiṃ.|| ||

Dhamma-vicaya-sambojjh'aṅgaṃ bhāveti,||
viveka-nissitaṃ,||
virāga-nissitaṃ,||
nirodha-nissitaṃ,||
vossagga-pariṇāmiṃ.|| ||

Viriya-sambojjh'aṅgaṃ bhāveti,||
viveka-nissitaṃ,||
virāga-nissitaṃ,||
nirodha-nissitaṃ,||
vossagga-pariṇāmiṃ.|| ||

Pīti-sambojjh'aṅgaṃ bhāveti,||
viveka-nissitaṃ,||
virāga-nissitaṃ,||
nirodha-nissitaṃ,||
vossagga-pariṇāmiṃ.|| ||

Passaddhi-sambojjh'aṅgaṃ bhāveti,||
viveka-nissitaṃ,||
virāga-nissitaṃ,||
nirodha-nissitaṃ,||
vossagga-pariṇāmiṃ.|| ||

Samādhi-sambojjh'aṅgaṃ bhāveti,||
viveka-nissitaṃ,||
virāga-nissitaṃ,||
nirodha-nissitaṃ,||
vossagga-pariṇāmiṃ.|| ||

Upekkhā-sambojjh'aṅgaṃ bhāveti,||
viveka-nissitaṃ,||
virāga-nissitaṃ,||
nirodha-nissitaṃ,||
vossagga-pariṇāmiṃ.|| ||
