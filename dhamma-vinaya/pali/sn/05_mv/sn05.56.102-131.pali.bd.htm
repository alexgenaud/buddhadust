Sutta 102

Manussa-Cuti Suttaṃ

[1][pts][than][olds] Evam me sutaṃ:|| ||

Ekaṃ samayam Bhagavā Vesāliyaṃ viharati Mahāvane Kūṭāgāra-sālāyaṃ.|| ||

2. Atha kho Bhagavā parittaṃ nakha-sikhāyaṃ paṃsuṃ āropetvā bhikkhū āmantesi:|| ||

3. "Taṃ kiṃ maññatha bhikkhave,||
Katamaṃ nu kho bahutaraṃ,||
yo vāyaṃ mayā paritto nakha-sikhāyaṃ paṃsu āropito,||
ayaṃ vā mahā-paṭhavī" ti.|| ||

"Etad eva bhante, bahutaraṃ yad idaṃ mahā-paṭhavī.||
Appamattako yaṃ Bhagavatā paritto nakha-sikhāyaṃ paṃsu āropito.||
Saṅkham pi na upeti,||
upanidhim pi na upeti,||
kalabhāgam pi na upeti,||
mahā-paṭhaviṃ upanidhāya Bhagavatā paritto nakha-sikhāyaṃ paṃsu āropito" ti.|| ||

4. "Evam eva kho bhikkhave, appakā te sattā ye manussā cutā manussesu paccājāyanti.||
Atha kho ete va bahutarā sattā ye manussā cutā Niraye paccājāyanti.|| ||

Taṃ kissa hetu?|| ||

Adiṭṭhattā bhikkhave, catunnaṃ ariya-saccānaṃ.|| ||

Katamesaṃ catunnaṃ?|| ||

Dukkhassa ariya-saccassa,||
dukkha-samudayassa ariya-saccassa,||
dukkha-nirodhassa ariya-saccassa,||
dukkha-nirodha-gāminī-paṭipadāya ariya-saccassa.|| ||

Tasmātiha bhikkhave, 'idaṃ dukkhan' ti yogo karaṇīyo||
'ayaṃ dukkha-samudayo' ti yogo karaṇīyo||
'ayaṃ dukkha-nirodho' ti yogo karaṇīyo||
'ayaṃ dukkha-nirodha-gāminī paṭipadā' ti yogo karaṇīyo" ti.|| ||

Ekaṃ samayam Bhagavā Vesāliyaṃ viharati Mahāvane Kūṭāgāra-sālāyaṃ.|| ||

2. Atha kho Bhagavā parittaṃ nakha-sikhāyaṃ paṃsuṃ āropetvā bhikkhū āmantesi:|| ||

3. "Taṃ kiṃ maññatha bhikkhave,||
Katamaṃ nu kho bahutaraṃ,||
yo vāyaṃ mayā paritto nakha-sikhāyaṃ paṃsu āropito,||
ayaṃ vā mahā-paṭhavī" ti.|| ||

"Etad eva bhante, bahutaraṃ yad idaṃ mahā-paṭhavī.||
Appamattako yaṃ Bhagavatā paritto nakha-sikhāyaṃ paṃsu āropito.||
Saṅkham pi na upeti,||
upanidhim pi na upeti,||
kalabhāgam pi na upeti,||
mahā-paṭhaviṃ upanidhāya Bhagavatā paritto nakha-sikhāyaṃ paṃsu āropito" ti.|| ||

Atha kho ete va bahutarā sattā ye tiracchāna-yoniyā cutā tiracchāna-yoniyā paccājāyanti.|| ||

Taṃ kissa hetu?|| ||

Adiṭṭhattā bhikkhave, catunnaṃ ariya-saccānaṃ.|| ||

Katamesaṃ catunnaṃ?|| ||
