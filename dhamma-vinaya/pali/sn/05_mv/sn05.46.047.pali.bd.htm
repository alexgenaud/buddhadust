46. Bojjhanga Saɱyutta
IV. Nīvaraṇa-Vaggo

Sutta 47

Adalidda Suttaɱ

Adapted from the 1995 edition of the digital version of the Sri Lanka Buddha Jayanti Tripitaka Series.

 

 

[1][pts][bodh] Evam me sutaɱ:|| ||

Ekaɱ samayam Bhagavā Sāvatthiyaɱ viharati Jetavane Anāthapindikassa ārāme.|| ||

Atha kho aññataro bhikkhu yena Bhagavā ten'upasankami.|| ||

Upasaŋkamitvā Bhagavantaɱ abhivādetvā eka-m-antaɱ nisīdi.|| ||

Eka-m-antaɱ nisinno kho so bhikkhu Bhagavantaɱ etad avoca:|| ||

"'Adalidda!|| ||

Adalidda!' ti bhante, vuccati.|| ||

Kittāvatā nu kho bhante,||
'Adalidda' ti vuccatī" ti?|| ||

Sattannaɱ kho bhikkhu,||
bojjhaŋgānaɱ bhāvitattā||
bahulī-katattā||
'Adalidda' ti vuccati.|| ||

Katamesaŋ sattannaɱ?|| ||

Sati-sambojjh-a-ŋgassa||
dhamma-vicaya-sambojjh-a-ŋgassa||
viriya-sambojjh-a-ŋgassa||
pīti-sambojjh-a-ŋgassa||
passaddhi-sambojjh-a-ŋgassa||
samādhi-sambojjh-a-ŋgassa||
upekhā-sambojjh-a-ŋgassa.|| ||

Imesaɱ kho bhikkhu, sattannaɱ bojjhaŋgānaɱ||
bhāvitattā||
bahulī-katattā||
'Adalidda' ti vuccatī" ti.|| ||
