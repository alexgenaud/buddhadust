5. Mahā-Vagga
46. Bojjhanga Saɱyutta
3. Udāyi Vagga

Sutta 27

Nirodha Suttaɱ

Adapted from the 1995 edition of the digital version of the Sri Lanka Buddha Jayanti Tripitaka Series.

 

[87]

[1][pts][bodh] Evam me sutaɱ:|| ||

Ekaɱ samayam Bhagavā Sāvatthiyaɱ viharati Jetavane Anāthapindikassa ārāme.|| ||

Tatra kho Bhagavā bhikkhū āmantesi:|| ||

"Bhikkhavo" ti.|| ||

"Bhadante" ti te bhikkhū Bhagavato paccassosuɱ.|| ||

Bhagavā etad avoca:|| ||

"Yo bhikkhave, Maggo yā paṭipadā taṇhā-nirodhāya saŋvattati,||
taɱ Maggaɱ taɱ paṭipadaɱ bhāvetha.|| ||

Katamo ca bhikkhave, Maggo katamā ca paṭipadā taṇhā-nirodhāya saŋvattati?|| ||

Yad idaɱ satta-bojjh'aŋgā.|| ||

Katame satta?|| ||

Sati-sambojjh'aŋgo||
dhamma-vicaya-sambojjh'aŋgo||
viriya-sambojjh'aŋgo||
pīti-sambojjh'aŋgo||
passaddhi-sambojjh'aŋgo||
samādhi-sambojjh'aŋgo||
upekhā-sambojjh'aŋgo" ti.

"Kathaɱ bhāvitā ca bhikkhave satta bojjh'aŋgā||
kathaɱ bahulī-katā||
taṇhā-nirodhāya saŋvattanti?|| ||

"Idha Udāyi, Bhikkhu||
sati-sambojjh'aŋgaɱ bhāveti,||
viveka-nissitaɱ,||
virāga-nissitaɱ,||
nirodha-nissitaɱ,||
vossagga-pariṇāmiɱ.|| ||

■

Dhamma-vicaya-sambojjh'aŋgaɱ bhāveti,||
viveka-nissitaɱ,||
virāga-nissitaɱ,||
nirodha-nissitaɱ,||
vossagga-pariṇāmiɱ.|| ||

■

Viriya-sambojjh'aŋgaɱ bhāveti,||
viveka-nissitaɱ,||
virāga-nissitaɱ,||
nirodha-nissitaɱ,||
vossagga-pariṇāmiɱ.|| ||

■

Pīti-sambojjh'aŋgaɱ bhāveti,||
viveka-nissitaɱ,||
virāga-nissitaɱ,||
nirodha-nissitaɱ,||
vossagga-pariṇāmiɱ.|| ||

■

Passaddhi-sambojjh'aŋgaɱ bhāveti,||
viveka-nissitaɱ,||
virāga-nissitaɱ,||
nirodha-nissitaɱ,||
vossagga-pariṇāmiɱ.|| ||

■

Samādhi-sambojjh'aŋgaɱ bhāveti,||
viveka-nissitaɱ,||
virāga-nissitaɱ,||
nirodha-nissitaɱ,||
vossagga-pariṇāmiɱ.|| ||

■

Upekkhā-sambojjh'aŋgaɱ bhāveti,||
viveka-nissitaɱ,||
virāga-nissitaɱ,||
nirodha-nissitaɱ,||
vossagga-pariṇāmiɱ.|| ||

Evaɱ bhāvitā kho bhikkhave, satta bojjh'aŋgā||
evaɱ bahulī-katā||
taṇhā-nirodhāya saŋvattatī" ti.|| ||
