Saɱyutta Nikāya
5. Mahā-Vagga
48. Indriya Saɱyutta
6. Sūkara-Khata Vagga

Sutta 58

Sūkara-Khatā Suttaɱ

Adapted from the 1995 edition of the digital version of the Sri Lanka Buddha Jayanti Tripitaka Series.

 

[233]

[1][pts] Evam me sutaɱ:|| ||

Ekaɱ samayaɱ Bhagavā Rājagahe viharati Gijjhakūṭe pabbate Sūkarakhatāyaɱ.|| ||

Tatra kho Bhagavā āyasmantaɱ Sāriputtaɱ āmantesi:|| ||

"Kiɱ nu kho Sāriputta, attha-vasaŋ||
khīṇ'āsavo bhikkhu||
Tathāgate vā||
Tathāgatasāsane vā||
paramani-paccakāraɱ pavattamāno pavattatī" ti?|| ||

[234] "Anuttaraɱ hi bhante,||
yoga-k-khemaɱ sampassamāno khīṇ'āsavo bhikkhu||
Tathāgate vā||
Tathāgatasāsane vā||
paramani-paccakāraɱ pavattamāno pavattatī" ti.|| ||

"Sādhu, sādhu, Sāriputta,||
anuttaraɱ hi Sāriputta,||
yoga-k-khemaɱ sampassamāno khīṇ'āsavo bhikkhu||
Tathāgate vā||
Tathāgatasāsane vā||
paramani-pacca-kāraɱ pavattamāno pavattati.|| ||

Katamo ca Sāriputta||
anuttaro yoga-k-khemo,||
yaɱ sampassamāno khīṇ'āsavo bhikkhu||
Tathāgate vā||
Tathāgatasāsane vā||
paramani-pacca-kāraɱ pavattamāno pavattatī" ti?|| ||

"Idha bhante, khīṇ'āsavo bhikkhu||
saddh'indriyaɱ bhāveti||
upasama-gāmiɱ||
sambodha-gāmiɱ;|| ||

viriy'indriyaɱ bhāveti||
upasama-gāmiɱ||
sambodha-gāmiɱ;|| ||

sat'indriyaɱ bhāveti||
upasama-gāmiɱ||
sambodha-gāmiɱ;|| ||

samādh'indriyaɱ bhāveti||
upasama-gāmiɱ||
sambodha-gāmiɱ;

paññ'indriyaɱ bhāveti||
upasama-gāmiɱ||
sambodha-gāmiɱ.|| ||

Ayaɱ kho bhante, anuttaro yoga-k-khemo||
yaɱ sampassamāno khīṇ'āsavo bhikkhu||
Tathāgate vā||
Tathāgatasāsane vā||
paramani-pacca-kāraɱ pavattamāno pavattatī" ti.|| ||

"Sādhu, sādhu, Sāriputta,||
eso hi Sāriputta, anuttaro yoga-k-khemo||
yaɱ sampassamāno khīṇ'āsavo bhikkhu||
Tathāgate vā||
Tathāgatasāsane vā||
paramani-pacca-kāraɱ pavattamāno pavattati.|| ||

Katamo ca Sāriputta, paramanipaccakāro||
yaɱ khīṇ'āsavo bhikkhu||
Tathāgate vā||
Tathāgatasāsane vā||
paramani-pacca-kāraɱ pavattamāno pavattatī" ti?|| ||

"Idha bhante, khīṇ'āsavo bhikkhu||
satthari sagāravo viharati sappatisso.||
Dhamme sagāravo viharati sappatisso,||
Saŋghe sagāravo viharati sappatisso,||
sikkhāya sagāravo viharati sappatisso,||
samādhismiɱ sagāravo viharati sappatisso.|| ||

Ayaɱ kho bhante, paramanipaccakāro||
yaɱ khīṇ'āsavo bhikkhu||
Tathāgate vā||
Tathāgatasāsane vā||
paramani-pacca-kāraɱ pavattamāno pavattatī" ti.|| ||

[235] "Sādhu, sādhu, Sāriputta,||
eso hi Sāriputta paramanipaccakāro||
yaɱ khīṇ'āsavo bhikkhu||
Tathāgate vā||
Tathāgatasāsane vā||
paramani-pacca-kāraɱ pavattayamāno pavattatī" ti.|| ||
