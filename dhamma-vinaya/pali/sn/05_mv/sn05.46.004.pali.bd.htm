Saɱyutta Nikāya,
V: Mahā-Vagga
46. Bojjhanga Saɱyutta
I. Pabbata-Vaggo

Sutta 4

Vatta Suttaɱ

Adapted from the 1995 edition of the digital version of the Sri Lanka Buddha Jayanti Tripitaka Series.

 

 

[1][pts][olds][bodh] Evam me sutaɱ:|| ||

Ekaɱ samayaɱ āyasmā Sāriputto Sāvatthiyaɱ viharati Jetavane Anāthapiṇḍikassa ārāme.|| ||

Tatra kho āyasmā Sāriputto bhikkhū āmantesi|| ||

"Āvuso bhikkhavo" ti.|| ||

[71] "Āvuso" ti kho te bhikkhū āyasmato Sāriputtassa paccassosuɱ.|| ||

Āyasmā Sāriputto etad avoca:|| ||

"Satt'ime āvuso, bojjhaŋgā.|| ||

Katame satta?|| ||

Sati-sambojjhaŋgo||
dhamma-vicaya-sambojjhaŋgo||
viriya-sambojjhaŋgo||
pīti-sambojjhaŋgo||
passaddhi-sambojjhaŋgo||
samādhi-sambojjhaŋgo||
upekhā-sambojjhaŋgo.|| ||

Ime kho āvuso satta bojjhaŋgā.|| ||

Imesaɱ khvāham āvuso, sattannaɱ bojjhaŋgānaɱ||
yena yena bojjhaŋgena ākaŋkhāmi pubbaṇha-samayaɱ viharituɱ||
tena tena bojjhaŋgena pubbaṇha-samayaɱ viharāmi.|| ||

Yena yena bojjhaŋgena ākaŋkhāmi majjhantikaɱ-samayaɱ viharituɱ,||
tena tena bojjhaŋgena majjhantikaɱ-samayaɱ viharāmi.|| ||

Yena yena bojjhaŋgena ākaŋkhāmi sāyaṇha-samayaɱ viharituɱ,||
tena tena bojjhaŋgena sāyaṇha-samayaɱ viharāmi.|| ||

Sati-sambojjhaŋgo iti ce me āvuso hoti.|| ||

Appamāṇoti me hoti.|| ||

Susamāraddho ti me hoti.|| ||

Tiṭṭhantaɱ ca naɱ tiṭṭhatī ti pajānāmi.|| ||

Sa ce pi me cavati,||
ida-p-paccayā me cavatī ti pajānāmi.|| ||

Dhamma-vicaya-sambojjhaŋgo iti ce me āvuso hoti.|| ||

Appamāṇoti me hoti.|| ||

Susamāraddho ti me hoti.|| ||

Tiṭṭhantaɱ ca naɱ tiṭṭhatī ti pajānāmi.|| ||

Sa ce pi me cavati,||
ida-p-paccayā me cavatī ti pajānāmi.|| ||

Viriya-sambojjhaŋgo iti ce me āvuso hoti.|| ||

Appamāṇoti me hoti.|| ||

Susamāraddhoti me hoti.|| ||

Tiṭṭhantaɱ ca naɱ tiṭṭhatī ti pajānāmi.|| ||

Sa ce pi me cavati,||
ida-p-paccayā me cavatī ti pajānāmi.|| ||

Pīti-sambojjhaŋgo iti ce me āvuso hoti.|| ||

Appamāṇoti me hoti.|| ||

Susamāraddhoti me hoti.|| ||

Tiṭṭhantaɱ ca naɱ tiṭṭhatī ti pajānāmi.|| ||

Sa ce pi me cavati,||
ida-p-paccayā me cavatī ti pajānāmi.|| ||

Passaddhi-sambojjhaŋgo iti ce me āvuso hoti.|| ||

Appamāṇoti me hoti.|| ||

Susamāraddhoti me hoti.|| ||

Tiṭṭhantaɱ ca naɱ tiṭṭhatī ti pajānāmi.|| ||

Sa ce pi me cavati,||
ida-p-paccayā me cavatī ti pajānāmi.|| ||

Samādhi-sambojjhaŋgo iti ce me āvuso hoti.|| ||

Appamāṇoti me hoti.|| ||

Susamāraddhoti me hoti.|| ||

Tiṭṭhantaɱ ca naɱ tiṭṭhatī ti pajānāmi.|| ||

Sa ce pi me cavati,||
ida-p-paccayā me cavatī ti pajānāmi.|| ||

Upekhāsambojjhaŋgo iti ce me āvuso hoti.|| ||

Appamāṇoti me hoti.|| ||

Susamāraddhoti me hoti.|| ||

Tiṭṭhantaɱ ca naɱ tiṭṭhatī ti pajānāmi.|| ||

Sa ce pi me cavati,||
ida-p-paccayā me cavatī ti pajānāmi.|| ||

Seyyathā pi āvuso, rañño vā||
rāja-mahā-mattassa vā||
nānārattānaɱ dussānaɱ dussakaraṇḍako pūro assa.|| ||

So yaññad eva dussayugaɱ ākaŋkheyya pubbaṇha-samayaɱ pārupituɱ.|| ||

Taɱ tad eva dussayugaɱ pubbaṇha-samayaɱ pārupeyya.|| ||

Yaññad eva dussayugaɱ ākaŋkheyya majjhantikaɱ-samayaɱ pārupituɱ.|| ||

Tan tad eva dussayugaɱ majjhantikaɱ-samayaɱ pārupeyya.|| ||

Yaññad eva dussayugaɱ ākaŋkheyya sāyaṇha-samayaɱ pārupituɱ.|| ||

Tan tad eva dussayugaɱ sāyaṇha-samayaɱ pārupeyya.|| ||

[72] Evam eva khvāhaɱ āvuso, imesaɱ sattannaɱ bojjhaŋgānaɱ yena yena bojjhaŋgena ākaŋkhāmi pubbaṇha-samayaɱ viharituɱ.|| ||

Tena tena bojjhaŋgena pubbaṇha-samayaɱ vimarāmi.|| ||

Yena yena bojjhaŋgena ākaŋkhāmi majjhantikaɱ-samayaɱ viharituɱ.|| ||

Tena tena bojjhaŋgena majjhantikaɱ samayaɱ viharāmi.|| ||
