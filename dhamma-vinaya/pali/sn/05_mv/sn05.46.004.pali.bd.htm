Saɱyutta Nikāya
5. Mahā-Vagga
46. Bojjhanga Saɱyutta
1. Pabbata Vagga

Sutta 4

Vatta Suttaɱ

Adapted from the 1995 edition of the digital version of the Sri Lanka Buddha Jayanti Tripitaka Series.

 

 

[1][pts][olds][bodh][than] Evam me sutaɱ:|| ||

Ekaɱ samayaɱ āyasmā Sāriputto Sāvatthiyaɱ viharati Jetavane Anāthapiṇḍikassa ārāme.|| ||

Tatra kho āyasmā Sāriputto bhikkhū āmantesi|| ||

"Āvuso bhikkhavo" ti.|| ||

[71] "Āvuso" ti kho te bhikkhū āyasmato Sāriputtassa paccassosuɱ.|| ||

Āyasmā Sāriputto etad avoca:|| ||

"Satt'ime āvuso, bojjh'aŋgā.|| ||

Katame satta?|| ||

Sati-sambojjh'aŋgo||
dhamma-vicaya-sambojjh'aŋgo||
viriya-sambojjh'aŋgo||
pīti-sambojjh'aŋgo||
passaddhi-sambojjh'aŋgo||
samādhi-sambojjh'aŋgo||
upekhā-sambojjh'aŋgo.|| ||

Ime kho āvuso satta bojjh'aŋgā.|| ||

Imesaɱ khvāham āvuso, sattannaɱ bojjh'aŋgānaɱ||
yena yena bojjh'aŋgena ākaŋkhāmi pubbaṇha-samayaɱ viharituɱ||
tena tena bojjh'aŋgena pubbaṇha-samayaɱ viharāmi.|| ||

Yena yena bojjh'aŋgena ākaŋkhāmi majjhantikaɱ-samayaɱ viharituɱ,||
tena tena bojjh'aŋgena majjhantikaɱ-samayaɱ viharāmi.|| ||

Yena yena bojjh'aŋgena ākaŋkhāmi sāyaṇha-samayaɱ viharituɱ,||
tena tena bojjh'aŋgena sāyaṇha-samayaɱ viharāmi.|| ||

Sati-sambojjh'aŋgo iti ce me āvuso hoti.|| ||

Appamāṇoti me hoti.|| ||

Susamāraddho ti me hoti.|| ||

Tiṭṭhantaɱ ca naɱ tiṭṭhatī ti pajānāmi.|| ||

Sa ce pi me cavati,||
ida-p-paccayā me cavatī ti pajānāmi.|| ||

Dhamma-vicaya-sambojjh'aŋgo iti ce me āvuso hoti.|| ||

Appamāṇoti me hoti.|| ||

Susamāraddho ti me hoti.|| ||

Tiṭṭhantaɱ ca naɱ tiṭṭhatī ti pajānāmi.|| ||

Sa ce pi me cavati,||
ida-p-paccayā me cavatī ti pajānāmi.|| ||

Viriya-sambojjh'aŋgo iti ce me āvuso hoti.|| ||

Appamāṇoti me hoti.|| ||

Susamāraddhoti me hoti.|| ||

Tiṭṭhantaɱ ca naɱ tiṭṭhatī ti pajānāmi.|| ||

Sa ce pi me cavati,||
ida-p-paccayā me cavatī ti pajānāmi.|| ||

Pīti-sambojjh'aŋgo iti ce me āvuso hoti.|| ||

Appamāṇoti me hoti.|| ||

Susamāraddhoti me hoti.|| ||

Tiṭṭhantaɱ ca naɱ tiṭṭhatī ti pajānāmi.|| ||

Sa ce pi me cavati,||
ida-p-paccayā me cavatī ti pajānāmi.|| ||

Passaddhi-sambojjh'aŋgo iti ce me āvuso hoti.|| ||

Appamāṇoti me hoti.|| ||

Susamāraddhoti me hoti.|| ||

Tiṭṭhantaɱ ca naɱ tiṭṭhatī ti pajānāmi.|| ||

Sa ce pi me cavati,||
ida-p-paccayā me cavatī ti pajānāmi.|| ||

Samādhi-sambojjh'aŋgo iti ce me āvuso hoti.|| ||

Appamāṇoti me hoti.|| ||

Susamāraddhoti me hoti.|| ||

Tiṭṭhantaɱ ca naɱ tiṭṭhatī ti pajānāmi.|| ||

Sa ce pi me cavati,||
ida-p-paccayā me cavatī ti pajānāmi.|| ||

Upekhā-sambojjh'aŋgo iti ce me āvuso hoti.|| ||

Appamāṇoti me hoti.|| ||

Susamāraddhoti me hoti.|| ||

Tiṭṭhantaɱ ca naɱ tiṭṭhatī ti pajānāmi.|| ||

Sa ce pi me cavati,||
ida-p-paccayā me cavatī ti pajānāmi.|| ||

Seyyathā pi āvuso, rañño vā||
rāja-mahā-mattassa vā||
nānārattānaɱ dussānaɱ dussakaraṇḍako pūro assa.|| ||

So yaññad eva dussayugaɱ ākaŋkheyya pubbaṇha-samayaɱ pārupituɱ.|| ||

Taɱ tad eva dussayugaɱ pubbaṇha-samayaɱ pārupeyya.|| ||

Yaññad eva dussayugaɱ ākaŋkheyya majjhantikaɱ-samayaɱ pārupituɱ.|| ||

Tan tad eva dussayugaɱ majjhantikaɱ-samayaɱ pārupeyya.|| ||

Yaññad eva dussayugaɱ ākaŋkheyya sāyaṇha-samayaɱ pārupituɱ.|| ||

Tan tad eva dussayugaɱ sāyaṇha-samayaɱ pārupeyya.|| ||

[72] Evam eva khvāhaɱ āvuso, imesaɱ sattannaɱ bojjh'aŋgānaɱ yena yena bojjh'aŋgena ākaŋkhāmi pubbaṇha-samayaɱ viharituɱ.|| ||

Tena tena bojjh'aŋgena pubbaṇha-samayaɱ vimarāmi.|| ||

Yena yena bojjh'aŋgena ākaŋkhāmi majjhantikaɱ-samayaɱ viharituɱ.|| ||

Tena tena bojjh'aŋgena majjhantikaɱ samayaɱ viharāmi.|| ||

Yena yena bojjh'aŋgena ākaŋkhāmi sāyaṇha-samayaɱ viharituɱ.|| ||

Tena tena bojjh'aŋgena sāyaṇha-samayaɱ viharāmi.|| ||

Susamāraddhoti me hoti.|| ||

Tiṭṭhantaɱ ca naɱ tiṭṭhatī ti pajānāmi.|| ||

Sa ce pi me cavati,||
