Sutta 31

Anussuta Suttaɱ

Adapted from the 1995 edition of the digital version of the Sri Lanka Buddha Jayanti Tripitaka Series.

 

 

[1][pts][bodh] Evam me sutaɱ:|| ||

Ekaɱ samayam Bhagavā Sāvatthiyaɱ viharati Jetavane Anāthapindikassa ārāme.|| ||

Tatra kho Bhagavā bhikkhū āmantesi:|| ||

"Bhikkhavo" ti.|| ||

"Bhadante" ti te bhikkhū Bhagavato paccassosuɱ.|| ||

Bhagavā etad avoca:|| ||

"Ayaɱ 'kāye kāy'ānupassanā' ti me bhikkhave,||
pubbe [179] ananussutesu dhammesu cakkhuɱ udapādi,||
ñāṇaɱ udapādi,||
paññā udapādi,||
vijjā udapādi,||
āloko udapādi.|| ||

paññā udapādi,||
vijjā udapādi,||
āloko udapādi.|| ||

paññā udapādi,||

āloko udapādi.|| ||
