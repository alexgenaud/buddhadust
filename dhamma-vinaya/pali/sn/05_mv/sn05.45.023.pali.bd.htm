45. Magga Saɱyutta||
III. Micchatta Vagga

Sutta 23

Paṭhama Paṭipadā Suttaɱ

Adapted from the 1995 edition of the digital version of the Sri Lanka Buddha Jayanti Tripitaka Series.

 

 

[1][pts][bodh] Evam me sutaɱ:|| ||

Ekaɱ samayam Bhagavā Sāvatthiyaɱ viharati.|| ||

Tatra kho Bhagavā bhikkhū āmantesi Bhikkhavo ti.|| ||

Bhadante ti te bhikkhū Bhagavato paccassosuɱ.|| ||

Bhagavā etad avoca:|| ||

"Micchā-paṭipadañ ca vo bhikkhave, desissāmi||
sammāpaṭipadañ ca.|| ||

Taɱ suṇātha.|| ||

Katamā ca bhikkhave, micchā-paṭipadā?|| ||

Seyyath'īdaɱ:|| ||

Micchā-diṭṭhi,||
micchā-saŋkappo,||
micchā-vācā,||
micchā-kammanto,||
micchā-ājīvo,||
micchā-vāyāmo,||
micchā-sati,||
micchā-samādhi.|| ||

Ayaɱ vuccati bhikkhave, micchā-paṭipadā.|| ||

Katamā ca bhikkhave, sammāpaṭipadā?|| ||

Seyyath'īdaɱ:|| ||

Sammā-diṭṭhi,||
sammā-saŋkappo,||
sammā-vācā,||
sammā-kammanto,||
sammā-ājīvo,||
sammā-vāyāmo,||
sammā-sati,||
sammā-samādhi.|| ||

Ayaɱ vuccati bhikkhave, sammāpaṭipadā" ti.|| ||
