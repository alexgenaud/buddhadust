Saɱyutta Nikāya,
V: Mahā-Vagga
48. Indriya Saɱyutta
V. Jarā-vagga

Sutta 41

Jarā Suttaɱ

Adapted from the 1995 edition of the digital version of the Sri Lanka Buddha Jayanti Tripitaka Series.

 

 

[1][pts][than] Evam me sutaɱ:|| ||

Ekaɱ samayaɱ Bhagavā Sāvatthiyaɱ viharati Pubbārāme Migāra-mātu pāsāde.|| ||

Tena kho pana samayena Bhagavā sāyaṇha-samayaɱ paṭisallāṇā vuṭṭhito pacchātape nisinno hoti piṭṭhiɱ otāpayamāno.|| ||

Atha kho āyasmā Ānando yena Bhagavā ten'upasankami.||
Upasaŋkamitvā Bhagavantaɱ abhivādetvā Bhagavato gattāni pāṇinā anomajjanto Bhagavantaɱ etad avoca:|| ||

"Acchariyaɱ bhante, abbhutaɱ bhante, na c'evadāni bhante, Bhagavato tāva parisuddho hoti chavivaṇṇo pariyodāto.|| ||

Sithilāni ca gattāni sabbāni valijātāni.|| ||

Purato pabbhāro ca kāyo.|| ||

Dissati ca indriyānaɱ aññathattaɱ: cakkhu'ndriyassa sot'indriyassa ghān'indriyassa jivh'indriyassa kāy'indriyassāti.|| ||

[217] Evaɱ h'etaɱ Ānanda, hoti jarā-dhammo yobbaññe vyādhi-dhammo ārogye maraṇa-dhammo jīvite na c'eva tāva parisuddho hoti chavivaṇṇo pariyodāto sathilāni ca honti gattāni sabbāni valijātāni|| ||

Purato pabbhāro ca kāyo|| ||

Dissati ca indriyānaɱ aññathattaɱ: cakkhu'ndriyassa sot'indriyassa ghān'indriyassa jivh'indriyassa kāy'indriyassāti.|| ||

Idam avoca Bhagavā.|| ||

Idaɱ vatvā Sugato athāparaɱ etad avoca Satthā:|| ||

 

Dhitaɱ jammi jare atthu||
dubbaṇṇa-karaṇī jare,||
Tāva manoramaɱ vimmaɱ||
jarāya abhimadditaɱ|| ||

Yo pi vassa-sataɱ jīve||
so pi maccuparāyaṇo||
Na kiñci parivajjeti||
sabbam evā bhimaddatī ti.|| ||
