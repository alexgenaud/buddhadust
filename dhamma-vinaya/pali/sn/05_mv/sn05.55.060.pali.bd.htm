Sutta 60

Paññā-Vuḍḍhi Suttaɱ

Adapted from the 1995 edition of the digital version of the Sri Lanka Buddha Jayanti Tripitaka Series.

 

 

[1][pts] Evam me sutaɱ:|| ||

Ekaɱ samayam Bhagavā Sāvatthiyaɱ viharati Jetavane Anāthapiṇḍikassa ārāme.|| ||

Tatra kho Bhagavā bhikkhū āmantesi:|| ||

"Bhikkhavo" ti.|| ||

"Bhadante" ti te bhikkhū Bhagavato paccassosuɱ.|| ||

Bhagavā etad avoca:|| ||

"Cattāro me bhikkhave, dhammā bhāvitā||
bahulī-katā||
paññā-vuḍḍhiya saŋvattanti.|| ||

Katame cattāro?|| ||

Sappurisa-saŋsevo||
Sad'Dhamma-savanaɱ||
yoniso-mana-sikāro||
dhamm-ā-nudhamma-paṭipatti.|| ||

Ime kho bhikkhave, cattāro dhammā bhāvitā||
bahulī-katā||
paññā-vuḍḍhiya saŋvattantī" ti.|| ||
