3. Udāyi Vagga

Sutta 28

Nibbedha Suttaɱ

Adapted from the 1995 edition of the digital version of the Sri Lanka Buddha Jayanti Tripitaka Series.

 

[87]

[1][pts][bodh] Evam me sutaɱ:|| ||

Ekaɱ samayam Bhagavā Sāvatthiyaɱ viharati Jetavane Anāthapindikassa ārāme.|| ||

Tatra kho Bhagavā bhikkhū āmantesi:|| ||

"Bhikkhavo" ti.|| ||

"Bhadante" ti te bhikkhū Bhagavato paccassosuɱ.|| ||

Bhagavā etad avoca:|| ||

"Nibbedha-bhāgiyaɱ vo bhikkhave, Maggaɱ desissāmi.|| ||

Taɱ suṇātha.|| ||

Katamo ca bhikkhave, nibbedha-bhāgiyo Maggo?|| ||

Yad idaɱ satta bojjh'aŋgā.|| ||

Katame satta?|| ||

Sati-sambojjh'aŋgo||
dhamma-vicaya-sambojjh'aŋgo||
viriya-sambojjh'aŋgo||
pīti-sambojjh'aŋgo||
passaddhi-sambojjh'aŋgo||

upekhā-sambojjh'aŋgo" ti.
