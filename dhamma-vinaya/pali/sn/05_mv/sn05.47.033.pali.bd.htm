Sutta 33

Viraddha Suttaɱ

Adapted from the 1995 edition of the digital version of the Sri Lanka Buddha Jayanti Tripitaka Series.

 

 

[1][pts][bodh] Evam me sutaɱ:|| ||

Ekaɱ samayam Bhagavā Sāvatthiyaɱ viharati Jetavane Anāthapindikassa ārāme.|| ||

Tatra kho Bhagavā bhikkhū āmantesi:|| ||

"Bhikkhavo" ti.|| ||

"Bhadante" ti te bhikkhū Bhagavato paccassosuɱ.|| ||

Bhagavā etad avoca:|| ||

"Yesaŋ kesañci bhikkhave, cattāro sati-paṭṭhānā viraddhā||
viraddho tesaŋ ariyo1 Maggo sammā dukkha-k-khayāgāmī.|| ||

[180] Yesaŋ kesañci bhikkhave, cattāro sati-paṭṭhānā āraddhā,||
āraddho tesaŋ ariyo Maggo sammā dukkha-k-khayagāmī.|| ||

Katame cattāro?|| ||

Idha bhikkhave, bhikkhu kāye kāy'ānupassī viharati||
ātāpī||
sampajāno||
satimā||
vineyya loke abhijjhā-domanassaɱ.|| ||

Vedanāsu vedan'ānupassīviharati||
ātāpī||
sampajāno||
satimā||
vineyya loke abhijjhā-domanassaɱ.|| ||

Citte citt'ānupassīviharati||
ātāpī||
sampajāno||
satimā||
vineyya loke abhijjhā-domanassaɱ.|| ||

Dhammesu Dhamm'ānupassīviharati||
ātāpī||
sampajāno||
satimā||
vineyya loke abhijjhā-domanassaɱ.|| ||

"Yesaŋ kesañci bhikkhave, cattāro sati-paṭṭhānā viraddhā||
viraddho tesaŋ ariyo1 Maggo sammā dukkha-k-khayāgāmī.|| ||

Yesaŋ kesañci bhikkhave, cattāro sati-paṭṭhānā āraddhā,||
āraddho tesaŋ ariyo Maggo sammā dukkha-k-khayagāmī" ti.|| ||
