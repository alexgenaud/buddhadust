Adapted from the 1995 edition of the digital version of the Sri Lanka Buddha Jayanti Tripitaka Series.

[1][pts][bodh][than] Evam me sutaɱ:|| ||

Ekaɱ samayam Bhagavā Sāvatthiyaɱ viharati Jetavane Anāthapindikassa ārāme.|| ||

Tatra kho Bhagavā bhikkhū āmantesi:|| ||

"Bhikkhavo" ti.|| ||

"Bhadante" ti te bhikkhū Bhagavato paccassosuɱ.|| ||

Bhagavā etad avoca:|| ||

"Yesaŋ kesañci bhikkhave, cattāro sati-paṭṭhānā viraddhā||
viraddho tesaŋ ariyo Maggo sammā dukkha-k-khayāgāmī.|| ||

cattāro sati-paṭṭhānā āraddhā,||
āraddho tesaŋ ariyo Maggo sammā dukkha-k-khaya-gāmī.|| ||

"Yesaŋ kesañci bhikkhave, cattāro sati-paṭṭhānā viraddhā||
