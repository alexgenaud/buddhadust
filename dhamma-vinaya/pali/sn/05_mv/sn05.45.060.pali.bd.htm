Saɱyutta Nikāya,
V: Mahā-Vagga
45. Magga Saɱyutta
Suriyassa-Peyyālo
Rāgavinaya-nissitaɱ

Sutta 60

Diṭṭhi Suttaɱ

Adapted from the 1995 edition of the digital version of the Sri Lanka Buddha Jayanti Tripitaka Series.

 

 

[1][pts][olds][bodh] Sāvatthi nidānaɱ.|| ||

Suriyassa bhikkhave, udayato etaɱ pubbaŋ-gamaɱ etaɱ pubba-nimittaɱ yad idaɱ aruṇaggaɱ;||
evam eva kho, bhikkhave,||
bhikkhuno ariyassa aṭṭhaŋgikassa Maggassa uppādāya etaɱ pubbaŋ-gamaɱ etaɱ pubba-nimittaɱ yad idaɱ diṭṭhi-sampadā.|| ||

Diṭṭhisampannassetaɱ bhikkhave bhikkhuno pāṭikaŋkhaɱ ariyaɱ aṭṭhaŋgikaɱ Maggaɱ bhāvessati ariyaɱ aṭṭhaŋgikaɱ Maggaɱ bahulī-karissatī ti.|| ||

Kathañ ca bhikkhave, bhikkhu diṭṭhi-sampanno ariyaɱ aṭṭhaŋgikaɱ Maggaɱ bhāveti ariyaɱ aṭṭhaŋgikaɱ Maggaɱ bahulī-karoti:||
idha bhikkhave, bhikkhu sammā-diṭṭhiɱ bhāveti rāga-vinaya-pariyosānaɱ dosa-vinayapariyosānaɱ moha-vinayapariyosānaɱ,||
sammā-saŋkappaɱ bhāveti rāga-vinaya-pariyosānaɱ dosa-vinayapariyosānaɱ moha-vinayapariyosānaɱ,||
sammā-vācaɱ bhāveti rāga-vinaya-pariyosānaɱ dosa-vinayapariyosānaɱ moha-vinayapariyosānaɱ,||
sammā-kammantaɱ bhāveti rāga-vinaya-pariyosānaɱ dosa-vinayapariyosānaɱ moha-vinayapariyosānaɱ,||
sammā ājīvaɱ bhāveti rāga-vinaya-pariyosānaɱ dosa-vinayapariyosānaɱ moha-vinayapariyosānaɱ,||
sammā-vāyāmaɱ bhāveti rāga-vinaya-pariyosānaɱ dosa-vinayapariyosānaɱ moha-vinayapariyosānaɱ,||
sammā-satiɱ bhāveti rāga-vinaya-pariyosānaɱ dosa-vinayapariyosānaɱ moha-vinayapariyosānaɱ,||
sammā-samādhiɱ bhāveti rāga-vinaya-pariyosānaɱ dosa-vinayapariyosānaɱ moha-vinayapariyosānaɱ,.|| ||

Evaɱ kho bhikkhave, bhikkhu diṭṭhi-sampanno ariyaɱ aṭṭhaŋgikaɱ Maggaɱ bhāveti, ariyaɱ aṭṭhaŋgikaɱ Maggaɱ bahulī-karotī ti.|| ||
