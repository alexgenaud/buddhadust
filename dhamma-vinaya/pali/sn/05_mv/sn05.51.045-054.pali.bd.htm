Saɱyutta Nikāya,
V: Mahā-Vagga
51. Iddhipāda Saɱyutta

Chapter V
Suttas 45-54

Appamāda Vaggo

Adapted from the 1995 edition of the digital version of the Sri Lanka Buddha Jayanti Tripitaka Series.

 

 

Sutta 45

Tathāgata Suttaɱ

[1][pts][olds] Evam me sutaɱ:|| ||

Ekaɱ samayam Bhagavā Sāvatthiyaɱ viharati.|| ||

2. Yāvatā bhikkhave, sattā apadā vā dvipadā vā catu-p-padā vā bahu-p-padā vā rūpino vā arūpino vā saññino vā asaññino vā n'eva-saññi-nā-saññino vā nāsaññino vā||
Tathāgato tesaɱ aggam akkhāyati arahaɱ Sammā-Sam-Buddho.|| ||

Evam eva kho bhikkhave, ye keci kusalā dhammā,||
sabbe te appamāda-mūlakā appamāda-samo-saraṇā appamādo tesaɱ dhammānaɱ aggam akkhāyati.|| ||

Appamattass etaɱ bhikkhave, bhikkhuno pāṭikaŋkhaɱ cattāro iddhi-pāde bhāvessati,||
cattāro iddhi-pāde bahulī-karissatī ti.|| ||

Kathañ ca bhikkhave, bhikkhu appamatto cattāro iddhi-pāde bhāveti,||
cattāro iddhi-pāde bahulī-karoti?|| ||

3. Idha bhikkhave bhikkhu chanda-samādhi-padhāna-sankhāra-samannāgataɱ iddhi-pādaɱ bhāveti,|| ||

viriya-samādhi-padhāna-sankhāra-samannāgataɱ iddhi-pādaɱ bhāveti,|| ||

citta-samādhi-padhāna-sankhāra-samannāgataɱ iddhi-pādaɱ bhāveti,|| ||

vīmaɱsā-samādhi-padhāna-sankhāra-samannāgataɱ iddhi-pādaɱ bhāveti.|| ||

Evaɱ eva kho bhikkhave bhikkhu appamatto cattāro iddhi-pāde bhāveti,||
cattāro iddhi-pāde bahulī-karoti ti.|| ||
