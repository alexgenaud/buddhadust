Saɱyutta Nikāya,
V: Mahā-Vagga
46. Bojjhanga Saɱyutta
IV. Nīvaraṇa-Vaggo

Sutta 50

Dutiya Aŋga Suttaɱ

Adapted from the 1995 edition of the digital version of the Sri Lanka Buddha Jayanti Tripitaka Series.

 

 

[1][pts][bodh] Evam me sutaɱ:|| ||

Ekaɱ samayam Bhagavā Sāvatthiyaɱ viharati Jetavane Anāthapindikassa ārāme.|| ||

Tatra kho Bhagavā bhikkhū āmantesi:|| ||

"Bhikkhavo" ti.|| ||

"Bhadante" ti te bhikkhū Bhagavato paccassosuɱ.|| ||

Bhagavā etad avoca:|| ||

"Bāhiraɱ bhikkhave, aŋganti karitvā nāññaɱ ekaŋgam pi samanupassāmi||
sattannaɱ bojjhaŋgānaɱ uppādāya||
yatha-y-idaɱ bhikkhave, kalyāṇa-mittatā.

Kalyāṇa-mittass'etaɱ bhikkhave, bhikkhuno pāṭikaŋkhaɱ||
satta bojjhaŋge bhāvessati||
satta bojjhaŋge bahulī-karissati.

Kathañ ca bhikkhave, bhikkhu kalyāṇa-mitto||
satta bojjhaŋge bhāveti,||
satta bojjhaŋge bahulī-karoti?|| ||

Idha bhikkhave, Bhikkhu||
sati-sambojjh-a-ŋgaɱ bhāveti,||
viveka-nissitaɱ,||
virāga-nissitaɱ,||
nirodha-nissitaɱ,||
vossagga-pariṇāmiɱ.|| ||

Dhamma-vicaya-sambojjh-a-ŋgaɱ bhāveti,||
viveka-nissitaɱ,||
virāga-nissitaɱ,||
nirodha-nissitaɱ,||
vossagga-pariṇāmiɱ.|| ||

Viriya-sambojjh-a-ŋgaɱ bhāveti,||
viveka-nissitaɱ,||
virāga-nissitaɱ,||
nirodha-nissitaɱ,||
vossagga-pariṇāmiɱ.|| ||

Pīti-sambojjh-a-ŋgaɱ bhāveti,||
viveka-nissitaɱ,||
virāga-nissitaɱ,||
nirodha-nissitaɱ,||
vossagga-pariṇāmiɱ.|| ||

Passaddhi-sambojjh-a-ŋgaɱ bhāveti,||
viveka-nissitaɱ,||
virāga-nissitaɱ,||
nirodha-nissitaɱ,||
vossagga-pariṇāmiɱ.|| ||

Samādhi-sambojjh-a-ŋgaɱ bhāveti,||
viveka-nissitaɱ,||
virāga-nissitaɱ,||
nirodha-nissitaɱ,||
vossagga-pariṇāmiɱ.|| ||

Upekkhā-sambojjh-a-ŋgaɱ bhāveti,||
viveka-nissitaɱ,||
virāga-nissitaɱ,||
nirodha-nissitaɱ,||
vossagga-pariṇāmiɱ.|| ||

Evaɱ kho bhikkhave, bhikkhu kalyāṇa-mitto||
satta bojjhaŋge bhāveti,||
satta bojjhaŋge bahulī-karotī" ti.|| ||
