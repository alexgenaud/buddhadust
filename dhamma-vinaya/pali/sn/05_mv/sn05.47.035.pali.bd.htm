Saɱyutta Nikāya,
V: Mahā-Vagga
47. Satipaṭṭhana Saɱyutta
4. Anussuta-Vagga

Sutta 35

Sata Suttaɱ

Adapted from the 1995 edition of the digital version of the Sri Lanka Buddha Jayanti Tripitaka Series.

 

 

[1][pts][bodh][than][olds] Evam me sutaɱ:|| ||

2. Ekaɱ samayaɱ Bhagavā Sāvatthiyaɱ viharati Jetavane Anāthapiṇḍikassa ārāme.|| ||

Tatra kho Bhagavā bhikkhū āmantesi:|| ||

"Bhikkhavo" ti.|| ||

"Bhadante" ti te bhikkhū Bhagavato paccassosuɱ.|| ||

Bhagavā etad avoca:|| ||

3. Sato bhikkhave, bhikkhu vihareyya sampajāno,||
ayaɱ vo amhākaɱ anusāsanī.|| ||

4. Kathañ ca bhikkhave, bhikkhu sato hoti?|| ||

Idha bhikkhave, bhikkhu kāye kāy'ānupassī viharati ātāpī sampajāno satimā vineyya loke abhijjhā-domanassaɱ.|| ||

Vedanāsu vedan'ānupassī viharati ātāpī sampajāno satimā vineyya loke abhijjhā-domanassaɱ.|| ||

Citte citt'ānupassī viharati ātāpī sampajāno satimā vineyya loke abhijjhā-domanassaɱ.|| ||

Dhammesu Dhamm'ānupassī viharati ātāpī sampajāno satimā vineyya loke abhijjhā-domanassaɱ.|| ||

Evaɱ kho bhikkhave, bhikkhu sato hoti.|| ||

5. Kathañ ca bhikkhave, bhikkhu sampajāno hoti?|| ||

Idha bhikkhave, bhikkhuno viditā vedanā uppajjanti,||
viditā [181] upaṭṭhahanti,||
viditā abbhatthaɱ gacchanti.|| ||

Viditā vitakkā uppajjanti,||
viditā upaṭṭhahanti,||
viditā abbhatthaɱ gacchanti.|| ||

Viditā saññā uppajjanti,||
viditā upaṭṭhahanti,||
viditā abbhatthaɱ gacchanti.|| ||

Evaɱ kho bhikkhave, bhikkhu sampajāno hoti.|| ||

6. Sato bhikkhave bhikkhu vihareyya sampajāno,||
ayaɱ vo amhākaɱ anusāsanī ti.|| ||
