Sutta 18

Vijjā Suttaɱ

Adapted from the 1995 edition of the digital version of the Sri Lanka Buddha Jayanti Tripitaka Series.

 

[429]

[1][pts][bodh] Evam me sutaɱ:|| ||

Atha kho aññataro bhikkhu yena Bhagavā ten'upasaṅkami.|| ||

Upasaṅkamitvā Bhagavantaɱ abhivādetvā eka-m-antaɱ nisīdi.|| ||

Eka-m-antaɱ nisinno kho so bhikkhu Bhagavantaɱ etad avoca:|| ||

"'Vijjā vijjā' ti bhante, vuccati.|| ||

Katamā nu kho bhante, vijjā,||
kittāvatā ca vijjā-gato hotī" ti?|| ||

[430] "Yaɱ kho bhikkhu, dukkhe ñāṇaɱ,||
dukkha-samudaye ñāṇaɱ,||
dukkha-nirodhe ñāṇaɱ,||
dukkha-nirodha-gāminiyā paṭipadāya ñāṇaɱ||
ayaɱ vuccati bhikkhu, vijjā,||
ettāvatā ca vijjā-gato hoti.|| ||

Tasmā'ti ha bhikkhu, 'idaɱ dukkhan' ti yogo karaṇīyo,||
'ayaɱ dukkha-samudayo' ti yogo karaṇīyo,||
'ayaɱ dukkha-nirodho' ti yogo karaṇīyo,||
'ayaɱ dukkha-nirodha-gāminī paṭipadā' ti yogo karaṇīyo" ti.|| ||
