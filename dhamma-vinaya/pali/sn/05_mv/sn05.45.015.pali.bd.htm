Adapted from the 1995 edition of the digital version of the Sri Lanka Buddha Jayanti Tripitaka Series.

 

 

[1][pts][bodh] Evam me sutaɱ:|| ||

"Aṭṭh'ime bhikkhave, dhammā bhāvitā bahulī-katā anuppannā uppajjanti nāññātra Sugata-Vinayā.|| ||

Sammā-diṭṭhi,||

Ime kho bhikkhave, aṭṭha dhammā bhāvitā bahulī-katā anuppannā uppajjanti nāññatra Sugata-Vinayā" ti.|| ||
