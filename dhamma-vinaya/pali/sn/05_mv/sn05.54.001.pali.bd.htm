Saɱyutta Nikāya,
V: Mahā-Vagga
54. Ānāpāna Saɱyutta

Sutta 1

Adapted from the 1995 edition of the digital version of the Sri Lanka Buddha Jayanti Tripitaka Series.

 

 

[311]

Eka-dhammasuttaɱ

[1][pts][bodh][than][olds] Evam me sutaɱ ekaɱ samayam Bhagavā Sāvatthiyaɱ viharati Jetavane Anāthapindikassa ārāme:[1]|| ||

[2][pts][bodh][than][olds] Tatra kho Bhagavā bhikkhū āmantesi Bhikkhavo ti|| ||

Bhadante ti te bhikkhū Bhagavato paccassosuɱ|| ||

Bhagavā etad avoca:|| ||

[3][pts][bodh][than][olds] Eka-dhammo bhikkhave, bhāvito bahulī-kato maha-p-phalo hoti mahā-nisamso.||
Katamo eka-dhammo?||
Ānāpāna-sati.|| ||

Kathaɱ bhāvitā ca bhikkhave ānāpāna-sati?||
Kathaɱ bahulī-katā maha-p-phalā hoti, mahā-nisaɱsā?|| ||

[4][pts][bodh][than][olds] Idha, bhikkhave, bhikkhū araññagato vā rukkha-mūla-gato vā suññ-ā-gāra-gato vā nisīdati pallaŋkaɱ ābhujitvā ujuɱ kāyaɱ paṇidhāya parimukhaɱ satiɱ upaṭṭha-petvā so sato va assa-sati sato va passa-sati.|| ||

[5][pts][bodh][than][olds] Dīghaɱ vā assa-santo dīghaɱ assasāmī ti pajānātī.||
Dīghaɱ vā passa-santo dīghaɱ passasāmī ti pajānāti.|| ||

Rassaɱ vā assa-santo rassaɱ assasāmīti pajānāti.||
Rassaɱ vā passa-santo rassaɱ passasāmīti pajānāti.|| ||

[6][pts][bodh][than][olds] Sabba-kāya-paṭisaŋvedi assasi-s-sāmīti sikkhati.||
Sabba-kāya-paṭisaŋvedi passasi-s-sāmīti sikkhati.|| ||

Passam-bhayaɱ kāya-sankhāraɱ assasi-s-sāmīti sikkhati.||
Passam-bhayaɱ kāya-sankhāraɱ passasi-s-sāmīti sikkhati.|| ||

[312] [7][pts][bodh][than][olds] Pīti-paṭisaŋvedī assasi-s-sāmīti sikkhati.||
Pīti-paṭisaŋvedī passasi-s-sāmīti sikkhati.|| ||

Sukha-paṭisaŋvedī assasi-s-sāmīti sikkhati.||
Sukha-paṭisaŋvedī passasi-s-sāmīti sikkhati.|| ||

[8][pts][bodh][than][olds] Citta-sankhāra-paṭisaŋvedī assasi-s-sāmīti sikkhati.||
Citta-sankhāra-paṭisaŋvedī passasi-s-sāmīti sikkhati.|| ||

Passam-bhayaɱ citta-sankhāraɱ assasi-s-sāmīti sikkhati.||
Passam-bhayaɱ citta-sankhāraɱ passasi-s-sāmīti sikkhati.|| ||

Citta-paṭisaŋvedī assasi-s-sāmīti sikkhati.||
Citta-paṭisaŋvedī passasi-s-sāmīti sikkhati.|| ||

[9][pts][bodh][than][olds] Abhi-p-pamodayaɱ cittaɱ assasi-s-sāmīti sikkhati.||
Abhi-p-pamodayaɱ cittaɱ passasi-s-sāmīti sikkhati.|| ||

Samādahaɱ cittaɱ assasi-s-sāmīti sikkhati.||
Samādahaɱ cittaɱ passasi-s-sāmīti sikkhati.|| ||

Vimocayaɱ cittaɱ assasi-s-sāmīti sikkhati.||
Vimocayaɱ cittaɱ passasi-s-sāmīti sikkhati.|| ||

[10][pts][bodh][than][olds] Anicc-ā-nupassī assasi-s-sāmīti sikkhati.||
Anicc-ā-nupassī passasi-s-sāmīti sikkhati.|| ||

Virāg-ā-nupassī assasi-s-sāmīti sikkhati.||
Virāg-ā-nupassī passasi-s-sāmīti sikkhati.|| ||

Nirodh-ā-nupassī assasi-s-sāmīti sikkhati.||
Nirodh-ā-nupassī passasi-s-sāmīti sikkhati.|| ||

Paṭinissagg-ā-nupassī assasi-s-sāmīti sikkhati.||
Paṭinissagg-ā-nupassī passasi-s-sāmīti sikkhati.|| ||

[11][pts][bodh][than][olds] Evaɱ bhāvitā kho bhikkhave, ānāpāna-sati evaɱ bahulī-katā maha-p-phalā hoti mahā-nisaɱsāti.|| ||
