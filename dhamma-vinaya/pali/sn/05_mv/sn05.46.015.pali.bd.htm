Saɱyutta Nikāya
5. Mahā-Vagga
46. Bojjhanga Saɱyutta
2. Gilāna Vagga

Sutta 15

Dutiya Gilāna Suttaɱ

Adapted from the 1995 edition of the digital version of the Sri Lanka Buddha Jayanti Tripitaka Series.

 

[80]

[1][pts][bodh] Evam me sutaɱ:|| ||

Ekaɱ samayam Bhagavā Rājagahe viharati veluvane Kalandakanivāpe.|| ||

Tena kho pana samayen'āyasmā Mahā Moggallāno pipphaliguhāyaɱ viharati||
ābādhiko dukkhito bāḷha-gilāno.|| ||

Atha kho Bhagavā sāyaṇha-samayaɱ paṭisallanā vuṭṭhito||
yen'āyasmā Mahā Moggallāno ten'upasankami.|| ||

Upasaŋkamitvā paññatena āsane nisīdi.|| ||

Nisajja kho Bhagavā āyasmantaɱ Mahā Moggallāno etad avoca:|| ||

"Kacci te Moggallāno, khamanīyaɱ?|| ||

Kacci yāpanīyaɱ?|| ||

Kacci dukkhā vedanā?|| ||

Paṭikkamanti||
no abhi-k-kaman ti?|| ||

Paṭikkamosānaɱ paññāyati||
no abhi-k-kamo" ti?|| ||

"Na me bhante, khamanīyaɱ.|| ||

Na yāpanīyaɱ bāḷhā me dukkhā vedanā abhi-k-kamanti,||
no paṭi-k-kamanti.|| ||

Abhi-k-kam'osānaɱ paññāyati,||
no paṭikkamo" ti.|| ||

"Satt'ime Moggallāno bojjh'aŋgā mayā sammadakkhātā bhāvitā||
bahulī-katā||
abhiññāya||
sambodhāya||
nibbāṇaya saŋvattanti.|| ||

Katame satta?|| ||

Sati-sambojjh'aŋgo kho Moggallāna, mayā sammadakkhāto bhāvito||
bahulī-katā||
abhiññāya||
sambodhāya||
nibbāṇaya saŋvattanti.|| ||

Dhamma-vicaya-sambojjh'aŋgo kho Moggallāna, mayā sammadakkhāto bhāvito||
bahulī-katā||
abhiññāya||
sambodhāya||
nibbāṇaya saŋvattanti.|| ||

Viriya-sambojjh'aŋgo kho Moggallāna, mayā sammadakkhāto bhāvito||
bahulī-katā||
abhiññāya||
sambodhāya||
nibbāṇaya saŋvattanti.|| ||

Pīti-sambojjh'aŋgo kho Moggallāna, mayā sammadakkhāto bhāvito||
bahulī-katā||
abhiññāya||
sambodhāya||
nibbāṇaya saŋvattanti.|| ||

Passaddhi-sambojjh'aŋgo kho Moggallāna, mayā sammadakkhāto bhāvito||
bahulī-katā||
abhiññāya||
sambodhāya||
nibbāṇaya saŋvattanti.|| ||

Samādhi-sambojjh'aŋgo kho Moggallāna, mayā sammadakkhāto bhāvito||
bahulī-katā||
abhiññāya||
sambodhāya||
nibbāṇaya saŋvattanti.|| ||

Upekhā-sambojjh'aŋgo kho Moggallāno, mayā sammadakkhāto bhāvito||
bahulī-katā||
abhiññāya||
sambodhāya||
nibbāṇaya saŋvattanti.|| ||

Ime kho Moggallāno, satta bojjh'aŋgā mayā sammadakkhānā bhāvitā||
bahulī-katā||
abhiññāya||
sambodhāya||
nibbāṇaya saŋvattanti" ti.|| ||

"Taggha Bhagava, bojjh'aŋgā!|| ||

Taggha Sugata, bojjh'aŋgā" ti!|| ||

Idam avoca Bhagavā.|| ||

Attamano āyasmā Mahā Moggallāno Bhagavato bhāsitaɱ abhinandi.|| ||

Vuṭṭhāhi cāyasmā Mahā Moggallāno tamhā ābādhā.|| ||

Tathā pahīno ca āyasmato Mahā Moggallāno so ābādho ahosī.|| ||
