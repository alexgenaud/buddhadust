Saɱyutta Nikāya,
V: Mahā-Vagga
46. Bojjhanga Saɱyutta
II. Gilāna-Vaggo

Sutta 15

Dutiya Gilāna Suttaɱ

Adapted from the 1995 edition of the digital version of the Sri Lanka Buddha Jayanti Tripitaka Series.

 

 

[1][pts][bodh] Evam me sutaɱ:|| ||

Ekaɱ samayam Bhagavā Rājagahe viharati veluvane Kalandakanivāpe.|| ||

Tena kho pana samayen'āyasmā Mahā Moggallāno pipphaliguhāyaɱ viharati ābādhiko dukkhito bāḷha-gilāno.|| ||

Atha kho Bhagavā sāyaṇha-samayaɱ paṭisallanā vuṭṭhito yen'āyasmā Mahā Moggallāno ten'upasankami.||
Upasaŋkamitvā paññatena āsane nisīdi.|| ||

Nisajja kho Bhagavā āyasmantaɱ Mahā Moggallāno etad avoca:|| ||

"Kacci te Moggallāno, khamanīyaɱ?|| ||

Kacci yāpanīyaɱ?|| ||

Kacci dukkhā vedanā?|| ||

Paṭikkamanti no abhi-k-kaman ti?|| ||

Paṭikkamosānaɱ paññāyati no abhi-k-kamo" ti?|| ||

"Na me bhante, khamanīyaɱ.|| ||

Na yāpanīyaɱ bāḷhā me dukkhā vedanā abhi-k-kamanti, no paṭi-k-kamanti.|| ||

Abhi-k-kam'osānaɱ paññāyati, no paṭikkamo" ti.|| ||

"Satt'ime Moggallāno bojjhaŋgā mayā sammadakkhātā bhāvitā bahulī-katā abhiññāya sambodhāya nibbāṇaya saŋvattanti.|| ||

Katame satta?|| ||

Sati-sambojjhaŋgo kho Moggallāna, mayā sammadakkhāto bhāvito bahulī-kato.|| ||

Abhiññāya sambodhāya nibbānāya saŋvattati.|| ||

Dhamma-vicaya-sambojjhaŋgo kho Moggallāna, mayā sammadakkhāto bhāvito bahulī-kato.|| ||

Abhiññāya sambodhāya nibbānāya saŋvattati.|| ||

Viriya-sambojjhaŋgo kho Moggallāna, mayā sammadakkhāto bhāvito bahulī-kato.|| ||

Abhiññāya sambodhāya nibbānāya saŋvattati.|| ||

Pīti-sambojjhaŋgo kho Moggallāna, mayā sammadakkhāto bhāvito bahulī-kato.|| ||

Abhiññāya sambodhāya nibbānāya saŋvattati.|| ||

Passaddhi-sambojjhaŋgo kho Moggallāna, mayā sammadakkhāto bhāvito bahulī-kato.|| ||

Abhiññāya sambodhāya nibbānāya saŋvattati.|| ||

Samādhi-sambojjhaŋgo kho Moggallāna, mayā sammadakkhāto bhāvito bahulī-kato.|| ||

Abhiññāya sambodhāya nibbānāya saŋvattati.|| ||

Upekhāsambojjhaŋgo kho Moggallāno, mayā sammadakkhāto bhāvito bahulī-kato.|| ||

Abhiññāya sambodhāya nibbānāya saŋvattati.|| ||

Ime kho Moggallāno, satta bojjhaŋgā mayā sammadakkhānā bhāvitā bahulī-katā abhiññāya sambodhāya nibbānāya saŋvattantī" ti.|| ||

"Taggha Bhagava, bojjhaŋgā!|| ||

Taggha Sugata, bojjhaŋgā" ti!|| ||

Idam avoca Bhagavā.|| ||

Attamano āyasmā Mahā Moggallāno Bhagavato bhāsitaɱ abhinandi.|| ||

Vuṭṭhāhi cāyasmā Mahā Moggallāno tamhā ābādhā.|| ||

Tathā pahīno ca āyasmato Mahā Moggallāno so ābādho ahosī ti.|| ||
