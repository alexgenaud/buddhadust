[1][pts][olds] Evam me sutaɱ:|| ||

Ekaɱ samayam Bhagavā Sāvatthiyaɱ viharati Jetavane Anāthapindikassa ārāme.|| ||

2. "Cattāro'me bhikkhave, iddhi-pādā||
bhāvitā||
bahulī-katā||
ariyā niyyānikā niyyanti takkarassa||
sammā-dukkha-k-khayāya.|| ||

Katame cattāro?|| ||

3. Idha, bhikkhave, bhikkhu chanda-samādhi-padhāna-sankhāra-samannāgataɱ||
iddhi-pādaɱ bhāveti.|| ||

Viriya-samādhi-padhāna-sankhāra-samannāgataɱ||
iddhi-pādaɱ bhāveti.|| ||

Citta-samādhi-padhāna-sankhāra-samannāgataɱ||
iddhi-pādaɱ bhāveti.|| ||

Vīmaɱsā-samādhi-padhāna-sankhāra-samannāgataɱ||
iddhi-pādaɱ bhāveti.|| ||

4. Ime kho bhikkhave cattāro iddhi-pādā||
bhāvitā||
bahulī-katā||
ariyā niyyānikā niyyanti takkarassa||
sammā-dukkha-k-khayāyā" ti.|| ||
