Saɱyutta Nikāya,
V: Mahā-Vagga
54 Ānāpāna Saɱyutta

1. Eka-dhamma Vaggo

Sutta 8

Dīpa Suttaɱ

Adapted from the 1995 edition of the digital version of the Sri Lanka Buddha Jayanti Tripitaka Series.

 

[316]

[1-2][pts][bodh][olds][than] Sāvatthi||
voca|| ||

[3] Ānāpāna-satisamādhi bhikkhave, bhāvito bahulī-kato maha-p-phalo hoti mahā-nisamso.|| ||

Kathaɱ bhāvito ca bhikkhave, ānāpāna-sati-samādhi kathaɱ bahulī-kato maha-p-phalo hoti mahā-nisaɱsā:|| ||

[317] [4] Idha, bhikkhave, bhikkhū araññagato vā rukkha-mūla-gato vā suññāragato vā nisīdati pallaŋkaɱ ābhujitvā ujuɱ kāyaɱ paṇidhāya parimukhaɱ satiɱ upaṭṭha-petvā so sato va assa-sati sato va passa-sati.|| ||

[5] Dīghaɱ vā assa-santo dīghaɱ assasāmīti pajānāti.||
Dīghaɱ vā passa-santo dīghaɱ passasāmīti pajānāti.|| ||

Rassaɱ vā assa-santo rassaɱ assasāmīti pajānāti.||
Rassaɱ vā passa-santo rassaɱ passasāmīti pajānāti.|| ||

[6] Sabba-kāyapaṭisaŋvedī assasi-s-sāmīti sikkhati.||
Sabba-kāyapaṭisaŋvedī passissāmīti sikkhati.|| ||

Passam-bhayaɱ kāya-sankhāraɱ assasi-s-sāmīti sikkhati.||
Passam-bhayaɱ kāya-sankhāraɱ passasi-s-sāmīti sikkhati.|| ||

[7] Pīti-paṭisaŋvedī assasi-s-sāmīti sikkhati.||
Pīti-paṭisaŋvedī passasi-s-sāmīti sikkhati.|| ||

Sukha-paṭisaŋvedī assasi-s-sāmīti sikkhati.||
Sukha-paṭisaŋvedī passasi-s-sāmīti sikkhati.|| ||

Citta-sankhāra-paṭisaŋvedī assasi-s-sāmīti sikkhati.||
Citta-sankhāra-paṭisaŋvedī passasi-s-sāmīti sikkhati.|| ||

Passam-bhayaɱ citta-sankhāraɱ assasi-s-sāmīti sikkhati.||
Passam-bhayaɱ citta-sankhāraɱ passasi-s-sāmīti sikkhati.|| ||

[8] Citta-sankhāra-paṭisaŋvedī assasi-s-sāmīti sikkhati.||
Citta-sankhāra-paṭisaŋvedī passasi-s-sāmīti sikkhati.|| ||

Passam-bhayaɱ citta-sankhāraɱ assasi-s-sāmīti sikkhati.||
Passam-bhayaɱ citta-sankhāraɱ passasi-s-sāmīti sikkhati.|| ||

Citta-paṭisaŋvedī assasi-s-sāmīti sikkhati.||
Citta-paṭisaŋvedī passasi-s-sāmīti sikkhati.|| ||

[9] Abhi-p-pamodayaɱ cittaɱ assasi-s-sāmīti sikkhati.||
Abhi-p-pamodayaɱ cittaɱ passasi-s-sāmīti sikkhati.|| ||

Samādahaɱ cittaɱ assasi-s-sāmīti sikkhati.||
Samādahaɱ cittaɱ passasi-s-sāmīti sikkhati.|| ||

Vimocayaɱ cittaɱ assasi-s-sāmīti sikkhati.||
Vimocayaɱ cittaɱ passasi-s-sāmīti sikkhati.|| ||

[10] Anicc-ā-nupassī assasi-s-sāmīti sikkhati.||
Anicc-ā-nupassī passasi-s-sāmīti sikkhati.|| ||

Virāg-ā-nupassī assasi-s-sāmīti sikkhati.||
Virāg-ā-nupassī passasi-s-sāmīti sikkhati.|| ||

Nirodh-ā-nupassī assasi-s-sāmīti sikkhati.||
Nirodh-ā-nupassī passasi-s-sāmīti sikkhati.|| ||

Paṭinissagg-ā-nupassī passasi-s-sāmīti sikkhati.||
Paṭinissagg-ā-nupassī passasi-s-sāmīti sikkhati.|| ||

[11] Evaɱ bhāvitā kho bhikkhave, ānāpāna-satisamādimhi evaɱ bahu'īkatā maha-p-phalo hoti mahā-nisamso.|| ||

[12] Aham pi sudaɱ bhikkhave pubb'eva sambodhā anabhi-sambuddho bodhisattova samāno iminā vihārena bahulaɱ vihārāmi.||
Tassa mahyaɱ bhikkhave iminā vihārena bahulaɱ viharato n'eva kāyo kilamati na cakkhūni||
anupādāya ca me āsavehi cittaɱ vīmuccati.|| ||

[13] Tasmā ti ha bhikkhave, bhikkhū ce pi ākankheyya:||
"N'eva kāyo kilameyya na ca cakkhūni||
anupādāya ca me āsavehi cittaɱ vimucceyyā" ti||
ayam eva ānāpāna-sati-samādhi sādhukaɱ mana-sikātabbo.|| ||

[14] Tasmā ti ha bhikkhave, bhikkhū ce pi ākankheyya:||
"Ye me gehasitā sarasaŋkappā te pahīyeyyunti".||
ayam eva ānāpāna-sati-samādhi sādhukaɱ mana-sikātabbo.|| ||

[15] Tasmā ti ha bhikkhave, bhikkhū ce pi ākankheyya||
"Appaṭikkūle paṭi-k-kūla-saññī vihareyyanti"||
ayam eva ānāpāna-sati-samādhi sādhukaɱ mana-sikātabbo.|| ||

[16] Tasmā ti ha bhikkhave, bhikkhū ce pi ākankheyya||
"Paṭikkūle a-p-paṭi-k-kūla-saññī vihareyyanti"||
ayam eva ānāpāna-sati-samādhi sādhukaɱ mana-sikātabbo.|| ||

[17] Tasmā ti ha bhikkhave, bhikkhū ce pi ākankheyya||
"Appaṭikkūle ca paṭikkūle ca paṭi-k-kūla-saññī vihareyyanti"||
ayam eva ānāpāna-sati-samādhi sādhukaɱ mana-sikātabbo.|| ||

[18] Tasmā ti ha bhikkhave, bhikkhū ce pi ākankheyya||
[318] "Paṭikkūle ca a-p-paṭikkūle ca a-p-paṭi-k-kūla-saññī vihareyyanti"||
ayam eva ānāpāna-sati-samādhi sādhukaɱ mana-sikātabbo.|| ||

[19] Tasmā ti ha bhikkhave, bhikkhū ce pi ākankheyya||
"Appaṭikkūlañca paṭikkūlañca tad ubhayaɱ abhini-vajchetvā upekhako vihareyyaɱ sato sampajāno ti"||
ayam eva ānāpāna-sati-samādhi sādhukaɱ mana-sikātabbo.|| ||

[20] Tasmā ti ha bhikkhave, bhikkhū ce pi ākankheyya||
"Vivicc'eva kāmehi vivicca akusalehi dhammehi sa-vitakkaɱ sa-vicāraɱ viveka-jaɱ pīti-sukhaɱ paṭhamaɱ-jhānaɱ upasampajja vihareyyanti"||
ayam eva ānāpāna-sati-samādhi sādhukaɱ mana-sikātabbo.|| ||

[21] Tasmā ti ha bhikkhave, bhikkhū ce pi ākankheyya:||
"Vitakka-vicārānaɱ vūpasamā ajjhattaɱ sampasādanaɱ cetaso ekodi-bhāvaɱ avitakkaɱ avicāraɱ samādhi-jaɱ pīti-sukhaɱ dutiyaɱ-jhānaɱ upasampajja vihareyyakti"||
ayam eva ānāpāna-sati-samādhi sādhukaɱ mana-sikātabbo.|| ||

[22] Tasmā ti ha bhikkhave, bhikkhū ce pi ākankheyya||
"Pītiyā ca virāgā upekhako ca vihareyyaɱ sato ca sampajāno sukhañca kāyena paṭisaŋvedeyyaɱ yaɱ taɱ ariyā ācikkhanti upekhako satimā sukha-vihārīti tatiyaɱ-jhānaɱ upasampajja vihareyyanti"||
ayam eva ānāpāna-sati-samādhi sādhukaɱ mana-sikātabbo.|| ||

[23] Tasmā ti ha bhikkhave, bhikkhū ce pi ākankheyya||
"Sukhassa ca pahānā dukkhassa ca pahānā pubb'eva somanassa-domanassānaɱ attha-gamā adukkha-ɱ-asukhaɱ upekhāsati-pārisuddhiɱ catutthaɱ-jhānaɱ upasampajja vihareyyanti"||
ayam eva ānāpāna-sati-samādhi sādhukhaɱ mana-sikātabbo.|| ||

[24] Tasmā ti ha bhikkhave, bhikkhū ce pi ākankheyya||
"Sabbaso rūpa-saññānaɱ samati-k-kamā paṭigha-saññānaɱ attha-gamā nānatta-saññānaɱ amanasi-kārā ananto ākāsoti ākāsanañvāyatanaɱ upasampajja vihareyyanti"||
ayam eva ānāpāna-sati-samādhi sādhukaɱ mana-sikātabbo.|| ||

[25] Tasmā ti ha bhikkhave, bhikkhū ce pi ākankheyya||
"Sabbaso Ākāsanañ-c'āyatanaɱ samati-k-kamā anantaɱ [319] viññāṇanti Viññāṇañ-c'āyatanaɱ upasampajja vihareyyanti"||
ayam eva ānāpāna-sati-samādhi sādhukaɱ mana-sikātabbo.|| ||

[26] Tasmā ti ha bhikkhave, bhikkhū ce pi ākankheyya||
"Sabbaso Viññāṇañ-c'āyatanaɱ samati-k-kamā 'N'atthi kiñcī' ti Ākiñcaññ'āyatanaɱ upasampajja vihareyyanti"||
ayam eva ānāpāna-sati-samādhi sādhukaɱ mana-sikātabbo.|| ||

[27] Tasmā ti ha bhikkhave, bhikkhū ce pi ākankheyya||
"Sabbaso Ākiñcaññ'āyatanaɱ samati-k-kamā N'eva-saññā-nā-saññ'āyatanaɱ upasampajja vihareyyanti"||
ayam eva ānāpāna-sati-samādhi sādhukaɱ mana-sikātabbo.|| ||

[28] Tasmā ti ha bhikkhave, bhikkhū ce pi ākankheyya:||
"Sabbaso N'eva-saññā-nā-saññ'āyatanaɱ samati-k-kamā saññā-vedayita-nirodhaɱ upasampajja vihareyyanti"||
ayam eva ānāpāna-sati-samādhi sādhukaɱ mana-sikātabbo.|| ||

[29] Evaɱ bhāvite kho bhikkhave, ānāpāna-sati-samādhimhi evaɱ bahulī-kate sukhañca vedanaɱ vedeti sā aniccāti pajānāti,||
anajjhositāti pajānāti,||
anabhinanditāti pajānāti,||
Dukkhaɱ ce vedanaɱ vedeti sā aniccāti pajānāti,||
anajjhositāti pajānāti,||
anabhinanditāti pajānāti,||
Adukkha-m-asukhañ ce vedanaɱ vedeti sā aniccāti pajānāti,||
anajjhositāti pajānāti,||
anabhinanditāti pajānāti.|| ||

[30] So sukhañ ce vedanaɱ vediyati visaŋyutto naɱ vediyati.||
Dukkhañ ce vedanaɱ vediyati visaŋyutto naɱ vediyati.||
Adukkhamasukhañ ce vedanaɱ vediyati visaŋyutto naɱ vediyati.|| ||

So kāya-pariyantikaɱ vedanaɱ vediya-māno||
kāya-pariyantikaɱ vedanaɱ vediyāmīti pajānāti||
jīvita-pariyantikaɱ vedanaɱ vediya-māno||
jīvita-pariyantikaɱ vedanaɱ vediyāmīti pajānāti.|| ||
Kāyassa bhedā uddhaɱ jīvita-pariyādānā idh'eva sabba-vedayitāni anabhinanditāni sītibhavissantīti pajānāti.|| ||

[31] Seyyathā pi, bhikkhave, telañca paṭicca vaṭṭiñca paṭicca telappadīpo jhāyeyya,||
tass'eva telassa ca vaṭṭiyā ca pariyādānā an-āhāro nibbāyeyya,||
evam eva kho, bhikkhave, bhikkhū [320] kāya-pariyantikaɱ vedanaɱ vediya-māno kāya-pariyantikaɱ vedanaɱ vediyāmī ti pajānāti.||
Jīvita-pariyantikaɱ vedanaɱ vediya-māno jīvita-pariyantikaɱ vedanaɱ vediyāmīti pajānāti.|| ||
Kāyassa bhedā uddhaɱ jīvita-pariyādānā idh'eva sabba-vedayitāni sītibhavissantī ti pajānātī ti.|| ||
