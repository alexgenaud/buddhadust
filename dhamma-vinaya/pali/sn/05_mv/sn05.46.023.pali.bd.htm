Saɱyutta Nikāya,
V: Mahā-Vagga
46. Bojjhanga Saɱyutta
III. Udāyi-Vaggo

Sutta 23

Ṭhānā Suttaɱ

Adapted from the 1995 edition of the digital version of the Sri Lanka Buddha Jayanti Tripitaka Series.

 

 

[1][pts][bodh] Evam me sutaɱ:|| ||

Ekaɱ samayam Bhagavā Sāvatthiyaɱ viharati Jetavane Anāthapindikassa ārāme.|| ||

Tatra kho Bhagavā bhikkhū āmantesi:|| ||

"Bhikkhavo" ti.|| ||

"Bhadante" ti te bhikkhū Bhagavato paccassosuɱ.|| ||

Bhagavā etad avoca:|| ||

"Kāma-rāga-ṭ-ṭhāniyānaɱ bhikkhave,||
dhammānaɱ mana-sikāra-bahulī-kārā anuppanno c'eva kāma-c-chando uppajjati,||
uppanno ca kāma-c-chando bhiyyobhāvāya vepullāya saŋvattati.|| ||

■

Vyāpādaṭṭhāniyānaɱ bhikkhave,||
dhammānaɱ mana-sikāra-bahulī-kārā anuppanno c'eva vyāpādo uppajjati,||
uppanno ca vyāpādo bhiyyobhāvāya vepullāya saŋvattati.|| ||

■

Thīna-middhaṭṭhāniyānaɱ bhikkhave,||
dhammānaɱ mana-sikāra-bahulī-kārā anuppanno c'eva thīna-middhaɱ uppajjati,||
uppannañ ca thīna-middhaɱ bhiyyobhāvāya vepullāya saŋvattati.|| ||

■

Uddhacca-kukkuccaṭṭhāniyānaɱ bhikkhave,||
dhammānaɱ mana-sikāra-bahulī-kārā anuppanno c'eva uddhacca-kukkuccaɱ uppajjati,||
uppannañ ca uddhacca-kukkuccaɱ bhiyyobhāvāya vepullāya saŋvattati.|| ||

■

Vicikicacchāṭhāniyānaɱ bhikkhave,||
dhammānaɱ mana-sikāra-bahulī-kārā anuppanno c'eva vicikicchā uppajjati,||
uppannā ca vicikicchā bhiyyobhāvāya vepullāya saŋvattati.|| ||

 

 

Sati-sambojjh-a-ŋgaṭṭhāniyānaɱ bhikkhave,||
dhammānaɱ mana-sikāra bahulī-kārā anuppanno c'eva sati-sambojjhaŋgo uppajjati,||
uppanno ca sati-sambojjhaŋgo bhāvanāpāripūriɱ gacchati.|| ||

■

Dhamma-vicaya-sambojjh-a-ŋgaṭṭhāniyānaɱ bhikkhave,||
dhammānaɱ mana-sikāra bahulī-kārā anuppanno c'eva dhamma-vicaya-sambojjhaŋgo uppajjati,||
uppanno ca dhamma-vicaya-sambojjhaŋgo bhāvanāpāripūriɱ gacchati.|| ||

■

Viriya-sambojjh-a-ŋgaṭṭhāniyānaɱ bhikkhave,||
dhammānaɱ mana-sikāra bahulī-kārā anuppanno c'eva viriya-sambojjhaŋgo uppajjati,||
uppanno ca viriya-sambojjhaŋgo bhāvanāpāripūriɱ gacchati.|| ||

■

Pīti-sambojjh-a-ŋgaṭṭhāniyānaɱ bhikkhave,||
dhammānaɱ mana-sikāra bahulī-kārā anuppanno c'eva pīti-sambojjhaŋgo uppajjati,||
uppanno ca pīti-sambojjhaŋgo bhāvanāpāripūriɱ gacchati.|| ||

■

Passaddhi-sambojjh-a-ŋgaṭṭhāniyānaɱ bhikkhave,||
dhammānaɱ mana-sikāra bahulī-kārā anuppanno c'eva passaddhi-sambojjhaŋgo uppajjati,||
uppanno ca passaddhi-sambojjhaŋgo bhāvanāpāripūriɱ gacchati.|| ||

■

Samādhi-sambojjh-a-ŋgaṭṭhāniyānaɱ bhikkhave,||
dhammānaɱ mana-sikāra bahulī-kārā anuppanno c'eva samādhi-sambojjhaŋgo uppajjati,||
uppanno ca samādhi-sambojjhaŋgo bhāvanāpāripūriɱ gacchati.|| ||

■

Upekhā-sambojjh-a-ŋgaṭṭhānīyānaɱ bhikkhave,||
dhammānaɱ mana-sikāra bahulī-kārā anuppanno c'eva upekhā-sambojjhaŋgo uppajjati,||
-uppanno ca upekhā-sambojjhaŋgo bhāvanāpāripūriɱ gacchatī" ti.|| ||
