Saɱyutta Nikāya
5. Mahā-Vagga
45. Magga Saɱyutta
1. Avijjā Vagga

Sutta 9

Sūka Suttaɱ

Adapted from the 1995 edition of the digital version of the Sri Lanka Buddha Jayanti Tripitaka Series.

 

 

[1][pts][bodh] Evam me sutaɱ:|| ||

Ekaɱ samayam Bhagavā Sāvatthiyaɱ viharati Jetavane Anāthapindikassa ārāme.|| ||

Tatra kho Bhagavā bhikkhū āmantesi:|| ||

"Bhikkhavo" ti.|| ||

"Bhadante" ti te bhikkhū Bhagavato paccassosuɱ.|| ||

Bhagavā etad avoca:|| ||

"Seyyathā pi bhikkhave, sālisūkaɱ vā||
yava-sūkaɱ vā micchā-paṇihitaɱ||
hatthena vā||
pādena vā akkantaɱ||
hatthaɱ vā||
pādaɱ vā bhindissati||
lohitaɱ vā uppādessati||
iti n'etaɱ ṭhānaɱ vijjati.|| ||

Taɱ kissa hetu?|| ||

Micchā-paṇihitattā bhikkhave, sūkassa.|| ||

■

Evam eva kho bhikkhave, so vata bhikkhu micchā-paṇihitāya diṭṭhiyā||
micchā-paṇihitāya Magga-bhāvanāya||
avijjaɱ bhecchati||
vijjaɱ uppādessati||
Nibbānaɱ sacchi-karissati||
iti n'etaɱ ṭhānaɱ vijjati.|| ||

Taɱ kissa hetu?|| ||

Micchā-paṇihitattā bhikkhave, diṭṭhiyā.|| ||

■

Seyyathā pi bhikkhave, sālisūkaɱ vā yavasūkaɱ vā sammā-paṇihitaɱ,||
hatthena vā pādena vā akkantaɱ hatthaɱ vā pādaɱ vā bheindissati lohitaɱ vā uppādessati||
iti ṭhānam etaɱ vijjati.|| ||

Taɱ kissa hetu?|| ||

Sammā-paṇihitattā bhikkhave, [11] sūkassa.|| ||

■

Evam eva kho bhikkhave, so vata bhikkhu sammā-paṇihitāya diṭṭhiyā||
sammā-paṇihitāya Magga-bhāvanāya||
avijjaɱ bhindissati||
vijjaɱ uppādessati||
Nibbānaɱ sacchi-karissati||
iti ṭhāname taɱ vijjati.|| ||

Taɱ kissa hetu?|| ||

Sammā-paṇihitattā bhikkhave, diṭṭhiyā.|| ||

 

§

 

Kathañ ca bhikkhave, bhikkhu sammā-paṇihitāya diṭṭhiyā||
sammā-paṇihitāya Magga-bhāvanāya||
avijjaɱ bhindati||
vijjaɱ uppādeti||
Nibbānaɱ sacchi-karoti?|| ||

Idha bhikkhave, bhikkhu sammā-diṭṭhiɱ bhāveti||
viveka-nissataɱ||
virāga-nissitaɱ||
nirodha-nissitaɱ||
vossagga-pariṇāmiɱ;|| ||

sammā-saŋkappaɱ bhāveti||
viveka-nissataɱ||
virāga-nissitaɱ||
nirodha-nissitaɱ||
vossagga-pariṇāmiɱ;|| ||

sammā-vācaɱ bhāveti||
viveka-nissataɱ||
virāga-nissitaɱ||
nirodha-nissitaɱ||
vossagga-pariṇāmiɱ;|| ||

sammā-kammantaɱ bhāveti||
viveka-nissataɱ||
virāga-nissitaɱ||
nirodha-nissitaɱ||
vossagga-pariṇāmiɱ;|| ||

sammā-ājīvaɱ bhāveti||
viveka-nissataɱ||
virāga-nissitaɱ||
nirodha-nissitaɱ||
vossagga-pariṇāmiɱ;|| ||

sammā-vāyāmaɱ bhāveti||
viveka-nissataɱ||
virāga-nissitaɱ||
nirodha-nissitaɱ||
vossagga-pariṇāmiɱ;|| ||

sammā-satiɱ bhāveti||
viveka-nissataɱ||
virāga-nissitaɱ||
nirodha-nissitaɱ||
vossagga-pariṇāmiɱ;|| ||

sammā-samādhiɱ bhāveti||
viveka-nissataɱ||
virāga-nissitaɱ||
nirodha-nissitaɱ||
vossagga-pariṇāmiɱ.|| ||

Evaɱ ce so bhikkhave, bhikkhu sammā-paṇihitāya diṭṭhiyā||
sammā-paṇihitāya Magga-bhāvanāya||
avijjaɱ bhindati||
vijjaɱ uppādeti||
Nibbānaɱ sacchi-karotī" ti.|| ||
