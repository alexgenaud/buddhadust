Saɱyutta Nikāya,
V: Mahā-Vagga
46. Bojjhanga Saɱyutta
IV. Nīvaraṇa-Vaggo

Sutta 48

Ādicca Suttaɱ

Adapted from the 1995 edition of the digital version of the Sri Lanka Buddha Jayanti Tripitaka Series.

 

 

[1][pts][bodh] Evam me sutaɱ:|| ||

Ekaɱ samayam Bhagavā Sāvatthiyaɱ viharati Jetavane Anāthapindikassa ārāme.|| ||

Tatra kho Bhagavā bhikkhū āmantesi:|| ||

"Bhikkhavo" ti.|| ||

"Bhadante" ti te bhikkhū Bhagavato paccassosuɱ.|| ||

Bhagavā etad avoca:|| ||

"Ādiccassa bhikkhave, udayato etaɱ pubbaŋ-gamaɱ||
etaɱ pubba-nimittaɱ||
yad idaɱ aruṇugaɱ.|| ||

Evam eva kho bhikkhave, bhikkhuno sattannaɱ bojjhaŋgānaɱ uppādāya||
etaɱ pubbaŋ-gamaɱ||
etaɱ pubba-nimittaɱ||
yad idaɱ kalyāṇa-mittatā.|| ||

Kalyāṇa-mittass'etaɱ bhikkhave, bhikkhuno pāṭikaŋkhaɱ||
satta bojjhaŋge bhāvessati||
satta bojjhaŋge bahulī-karissati.|| ||

Kathañ ca bhikkhave, bhikkhu kalyāṇa-mitto||
satta bojjhaŋge bhāveti||
satta bojjhaŋge bahulī-karoti?|| ||

Idha bhikkhave, Bhikkhu||
sati-sambojjh-a-ŋgaɱ bhāveti,||
viveka-nissitaɱ,||
virāga-nissitaɱ,||
nirodha-nissitaɱ,||
vossagga-pariṇāmiɱ.|| ||

[64] Dhamma-vicaya-sambojjh-a-ŋgaɱ bhāveti,||
viveka-nissitaɱ,||
virāga-nissitaɱ,||
nirodha-nissitaɱ,||
vossagga-pariṇāmiɱ.|| ||

Viriya-sambojjh-a-ŋgaɱ bhāveti,||
viveka-nissitaɱ,||
virāga-nissitaɱ,||
nirodha-nissitaɱ,||
vossagga-pariṇāmiɱ.|| ||

Pīti-sambojjh-a-ŋgaɱ bhāveti,||
viveka-nissitaɱ,||
virāga-nissitaɱ,||
nirodha-nissitaɱ,||
vossagga-pariṇāmiɱ.|| ||

Passaddhi-sambojjh-a-ŋgaɱ bhāveti,||
viveka-nissitaɱ,||
virāga-nissitaɱ,||
nirodha-nissitaɱ,||
vossagga-pariṇāmiɱ.|| ||

Samādhi-sambojjh-a-ŋgaɱ bhāveti,||
viveka-nissitaɱ,||
virāga-nissitaɱ,||
nirodha-nissitaɱ,||
vossagga-pariṇāmiɱ.|| ||

Upekkhā-sambojjh-a-ŋgaɱ bhāveti,||
viveka-nissitaɱ,||
virāga-nissitaɱ,||
nirodha-nissitaɱ,||
vossagga-pariṇāmiɱ.|| ||

Evaɱ kho bhikkhave, bhikkhu kalyāṇa-mitto||
satta bojjhaŋge bhāveti||
satta bojjhaŋge bahulī-karotī" ti.|| ||
