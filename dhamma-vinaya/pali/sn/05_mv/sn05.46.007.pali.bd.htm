I. Pabbata-Vaggo

Sutta 7

Kūṭāgāra Suttaɱ

Adapted from the 1995 edition of the digital version of the Sri Lanka Buddha Jayanti Tripitaka Series.

 

 

[1][pts][bodh] Evam me sutaɱ:|| ||

Seyyathā pi bhikkhave, kūṭā-gārassa yā kāci gopānasiyo sabbā tā kūṭa-ninnā||
kūṭa-poṇā||
kūṭa-pabbhārā,||
evam eva kho, bhikkhave, bhikkhu satta bojjhaŋge bhāvento||
satta bojjhaŋge bahulī-karonto||
Nibbāna-ninno hoti||
Nibbāna-poṇo||
Nibbāna-pabbhāro.|| ||

[76] Kathañ ca bhikkhave bhikkhu||
satta bojjhaŋge bhāvento||
satta bojjhaŋge bahulī-karonto||
Nibbāna-ninno hoti||
Nibbāna-poṇo||
Nibbāna-pabbhāro?|| ||

Idha, bhikkhave, bhikkhu,||
sati-sambojjh-a-ŋgaɱ bhāveti,||
viveka-nissitaɱ,||
virāga-nissitaɱ,||
nirodha-nissitaɱ,||
vossagga-pariṇāmiɱ.|| ||

Dhamma-vicayasambojjh-a-ŋgaɱ bhāveti,||
viveka-nissitaɱ,||
virāga-nissitaɱ,||
nirodha-nissitaɱ,||
vossagga-pariṇāmiɱ.|| ||

Viriyasambojjh-a-ŋgaɱ bhāveti,||
viveka-nissitaɱ,||
virāga-nissitaɱ,||
nirodha-nissitaɱ,||
vossagga-pariṇāmiɱ.|| ||

Pītisambojjh-a-ŋgaɱ bhāveti,||
viveka-nissitaɱ,||
virāga-nissitaɱ,||
nirodha-nissitaɱ,||
vossagga-pariṇāmiɱ.|| ||

Passaddhisambojjh-a-ŋgaɱ bhāveti,||
viveka-nissitaɱ,||
virāga-nissitaɱ,||
nirodha-nissitaɱ,||
vossagga-pariṇāmiɱ.|| ||

Samādhisambojjh-a-ŋgaɱ bhāveti,||
viveka-nissitaɱ,||
virāga-nissitaɱ,||
nirodha-nissitaɱ,||
vossagga-pariṇāmiɱ.|| ||

Upekkhāsambojjh-a-ŋgaɱ bhāveti,||
viveka-nissitaɱ,||
virāga-nissitaɱ,||
nirodha-nissitaɱ,||
vossagga-pariṇāmiɱ.|| ||

Evaɱ kho bhikkhave bhikkhu||
satta bojjhaŋge bhāvento||
satta bojjhaŋge bahulī-karonto||
Nibbāna-ninno hoti||
Nibbāna-poṇo||
Nibbāna-pabbhāro.|| ||
