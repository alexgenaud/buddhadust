Saṃyutta Nikāya
5. Mahā-Vagga
46. Bojjhaṇga Saṃyutta
1. Pabbata Vagga

Sutta 7

Kūṭ-Ā-gāra Suttaṃ

Adapted from the 1995 edition of the digital version of the Sri Lanka Buddha Jayanti Tripitaka Series.

 

[75]

[1][pts][bodh] Evam me sutaṃ:|| ||

"Seyyathā pi bhikkhave, kūṭā-gārassa yā kāci gopānasiyo sabbā tā kūṭa-ninnā||
kūṭa-poṇā||
kūṭa-pabbhārā,||
evam eva kho, bhikkhave, bhikkhu satta bojjh'aṇge bhāvento||
satta bojjh'aṇge bahulī-karonto||
Nibbāna-ninno hoti||
Nibbāna-poṇo||
Nibbāna-pabbhāro.|| ||

[76] Kathañ ca bhikkhave bhikkhu||
satta bojjh'aṇge bhāvento||
satta bojjh'aṇge bahulī-karonto||
Nibbāna-ninno hoti||
Nibbāna-poṇo||
Nibbāna-pabbhāro?|| ||

Idha, bhikkhave, bhikkhu,||
sati-sambojjh'aṇgaṃ bhāveti,||
viveka-nissitaṃ,||
virāga-nissitaṃ,||
nirodha-nissitaṃ,||
vossagga-pariṇāmiṃ.|| ||

Dhamma-vicaya-sambojjh'aṇgaṃ bhāveti,||
viveka-nissitaṃ,||
virāga-nissitaṃ,||
nirodha-nissitaṃ,||
vossagga-pariṇāmiṃ.|| ||

Viriya-sambojjh'aṇgaṃ bhāveti,||
viveka-nissitaṃ,||
virāga-nissitaṃ,||
nirodha-nissitaṃ,||
vossagga-pariṇāmiṃ.|| ||

Pīti-sambojjh'aṇgaṃ bhāveti,||
viveka-nissitaṃ,||
virāga-nissitaṃ,||
nirodha-nissitaṃ,||
vossagga-pariṇāmiṃ.|| ||

Passaddhi-sambojjh'aṇgaṃ bhāveti,||
viveka-nissitaṃ,||
virāga-nissitaṃ,||
nirodha-nissitaṃ,||
vossagga-pariṇāmiṃ.|| ||

Samādhi-sambojjh'aṇgaṃ bhāveti,||
viveka-nissitaṃ,||
virāga-nissitaṃ,||
nirodha-nissitaṃ,||
vossagga-pariṇāmiṃ.|| ||

Upekkhā-sambojjh'aṇgaṃ bhāveti,||
viveka-nissitaṃ,||
virāga-nissitaṃ,||
nirodha-nissitaṃ,||
vossagga-pariṇāmiṃ.|| ||

Evaṃ kho bhikkhave bhikkhu||
satta bojjh'aṇge bhāvento||
satta bojjh'aṇge bahulī-karonto||
Nibbāna-ninno hoti||
Nibbāna-poṇo||
Nibbāna-pabbhāro" ti.|| ||
