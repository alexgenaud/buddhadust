Sutta 83

Tathāgata Suttaɱ

i. Viveka

[83.1][pts] Evam me sutaɱ:|| ||

Ekaɱ samayam Bhagavā Sāvatthiyaɱ viharati.|| ||

Tatra kho Bhagavā bhikkhū āmantesi:|| ||

"Bhikkhavo" ti.|| ||

"Bhadante" ti||
te bhikkhū Bhagavato paccassosuɱ.|| ||

Bhagavā etad avoca:|| ||

[83.2][pts] Yāvatā bhikkhave, sattā apadā vā dvipadā vā catu-p-padā vā bahu-p-padā vā rūpino vā arūpino vā saññino vā asaññino vā n'eva-saññi-nā-saññino vā nāsaññino vā||
Tathāgato tesaɱ aggam akkhāyati arahaɱ Sammā-Sam-Buddho.|| ||

Evam eva kho bhikkhave, ye keci kusalā dhammā,||
sabbe te appamāda-mūlakā appamāda-samo-saraṇā appamādo tesaɱ dhammānaɱ aggam akkhāyati.|| ||

Appamattass'etaɱ bhikkhave, bhikkhuno pāṭikaŋkhaɱ pañc'indriyāni bhāvessati,||
pañc'indriyāni bahulī-karissatī ti.|| ||

Kathañ ca bhikkhave, bhikkhu appamatto pañc'indriyāni bhāveti,||
pañc'indriyāni bahulī-karoti?|| ||

Idha bhikkhave, bhikkhu saddh'indriyam bhāveti||
viveka-nissitaɱ,||
virāga-nissitaɱ,||
nirodha-nissitaɱ,||
vossagga-pariṇāmiɱ;|| ||

viriy'indriyam bhāveti||
viveka-nissitaɱ,||
virāga-nissitaɱ,||
nirodha-nissitaɱ,||
vossagga-pariṇāmiɱ;|| ||

sat'indriyam bhāveti||
viveka-nissitaɱ,||
virāga-nissitaɱ,||
nirodha-nissitaɱ,||
vossagga-pariṇāmiɱ;|| ||

samādh'indriyam bhāveti||
viveka-nissitaɱ,||
virāga-nissitaɱ,||
nirodha-nissitaɱ,||
vossagga-pariṇāmiɱ.|| ||

paññ'indriyam bhāveti||
viveka-nissitaɱ,||
virāga-nissitaɱ,||
nirodha-nissitaɱ,||
vossagga-pariṇāmiɱ.|| ||

Evaɱ kho bhikkhave, bhikkhu appamatto pañc'indriyāni bhāveti||
Ariyaɱ aṭṭhaŋgikaɱ Maggaɱ bahulītarotī ti.|| ||
