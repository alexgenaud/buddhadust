Saɱyutta Nikāya,
V: Mahā-Vagga
50. Bala Saɱyutta
I. Gŋgā-Peyyālo

Suttas 1-12

Adapted from the 1995 edition of the digital version of the Sri Lanka Buddha Jayanti Tripitaka Series.

 

 

Sutta 1

Paṭhama Pācīna Suttaɱ

[1][pts] Evam me sutaɱ:|| ||

Ekaɱ samayam Bhagavā Sāvatthiyaɱ viharati.|| ||

Tatra kho Bhagavā bhikkhū āmantesi:|| ||

"Bhikkhavo" ti.|| ||

"Bhadante" ti||
te bhikkhū Bhagavato paccassosuɱ.|| ||

Bhagavā etad avoca:|| ||

Pañc'imāni bhikkhave, balāni.|| ||

Katamāni pañca?|| ||

Saddhā-balaɱ||
viriya-balraɱ||
sati-balaɱ||
samādhi-balaɱ||
paññā-balaɱ.|| ||

Imāni kho bhikkhave, pañca balāni.|| ||

Seyyathā pi bhikkhave, Gaŋgā-nadī||
pācīna-ninnā,||
pācīna-poṇā||
pācīna-pabbhārā.|| ||

Evam eva kho bhikkhave, bhikkhu pañca balāni bhāvento||
pañca balāni bahulī-karonto||
Nibbāna-ninno hoti,||
Nibbāna-poṇo||
Nibbāna-pabbhāro.|| ||

Kathañ ca bhikkhave, bhikkhu pañca balāni bhāvento||
pañca balāni bahulī-karonto||
Nibbāna-ninno hoti||
Nibbāna-poṇo||
Nibbāna-pabbhāro?|| ||

Idha bhikkhave, bhikkhu saddhā-balaɱ bhāveti||
viveka-nissitaɱ,||
virāga-nissitaɱ,||
nirodha-nissitaɱ,||
vossagga-pariṇāmiɱ;|| ||

Idha bhikkhave, bhikkhu viriya-balaɱ bhāveti||
viveka-nissitaɱ,||
virāga-nissitaɱ,||
nirodha-nissitaɱ,||
vossagga-pariṇāmiɱ;|| ||

Idha bhikkhave, bhikkhu sati-balaɱ bhāveti||
viveka-nissitaɱ,||
virāga-nissitaɱ,||
nirodha-nissitaɱ,||
vossagga-pariṇāmiɱ;|| ||

Idha bhikkhave, bhikkhu samādhi-balaɱ bhāveti||
viveka-nissitaɱ,||
virāga-nissitaɱ,||
nirodha-nissitaɱ,||
vossagga-pariṇāmiɱ;|| ||

Idha bhikkhave, bhikkhu pañca balāni bhāveti||
viveka-nissitaɱ,||
virāga-nissitaɱ,||
nirodha-nissitaɱ,||
vossagga-pariṇāmiɱ.|| ||
