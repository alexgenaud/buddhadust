Sutta 127

Paṭhama Pācīna Suttaɱ

[1][pts][bodh] Evam me sutaɱ:|| ||

Ekaɱ samayam Bhagavā Sāvatthiyaɱ viharati.|| ||

Tatra kho Bhagavā bhikkhū āmantesi:|| ||

"Bhikkhavo" ti.|| ||

"Bhadante" ti||
te bhikkhū Bhagavato paccassosuɱ.|| ||

Bhagavā etad avoca:|| ||

Seyyathā pi bhikkhave, Gaŋgā-nadī||
pācīna-ninnā,||
pācīna-poṇā||
pācīna-pabbhārā.|| ||

Evam eva kho bhikkhave, bhikkhu ariyaɱ aṭṭhaŋgikaɱ Maggaɱ bhāvento||
ariyaɱ aṭṭhaŋgikaɱ Maggaɱ bahulī-karonto||
Nibbāna-ninno hoti,||
Nibbāna-poṇo||
Nibbāna-pabbhāro.|| ||

Kathañ ca bhikkhave, bhikkhu ariyaɱ aṭṭhaŋgikaɱ Maggaɱ bhāvento||
ariyaɱ aṭṭhaŋgikaɱ Maggaɱ bahulī-karonto||
Nibbāna-ninno hoti||
Nibbāna-poṇo||
Nibbāna-pabbhāro?|| ||

Idha bhikkhave, bhikkhu||
sammā-diṭṭhiɱ bhāveti||
Nibbāna-ninnaɱ||
Nibbāna-poṇaɱ||
Nibbānapabbhāraɱ;|| ||

sammā-saŋkappaɱ bhāveti||
Nibbāna-ninnaɱ||
Nibbāna-poṇaɱ||
Nibbānapabbhāraɱ;|| ||

sammā-vācaɱ bhāveti||
Nibbāna-ninnaɱ||
Nibbāna-poṇaɱ||
Nibbānapabbhāraɱ;|| ||

sammā-kammantaɱ bhāveti||
Nibbāna-ninnaɱ||
Nibbāna-poṇaɱ||
Nibbānapabbhāraɱ;|| ||

sammā-ājīvaɱ bhāveti||
Nibbāna-ninnaɱ||
Nibbāna-poṇaɱ||
Nibbānapabbhāraɱ;|| ||

sammā-vāyāmaɱ bhāveti||
Nibbāna-ninnaɱ||
Nibbāna-poṇaɱ||
Nibbānapabbhāraɱ;|| ||

sammā-satiɱ bhāveti||
Nibbāna-ninnaɱ||
Nibbāna-poṇaɱ||
Nibbānapabbhāraɱ;|| ||

sammā-samādhiɱ bhāveti||
Nibbāna-ninnaɱ||

Nibbānapabbhāraɱ.|| ||
