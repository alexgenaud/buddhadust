Sutta 127

Paṭhama Pācīna Suttaṃ

[1][pts][bodh] Evam me sutaṃ:|| ||

Ekaṃ samayam Bhagavā Sāvatthiyaṃ viharati.|| ||

Tatra kho Bhagavā bhikkhū āmantesi:|| ||

"Bhikkhavo" ti.|| ||

"Bhadante" ti||
te bhikkhū Bhagavato paccassosuṃ.|| ||

Bhagavā etad avoca:|| ||

Seyyathā pi bhikkhave, Gaṅgā-nadī||
pācīna-ninnā,||
pācīna-poṇā||
pācīna-pabbhārā.|| ||

Evam eva kho bhikkhave, bhikkhu||
ariyaṃ aṭṭhaṅgikaṃ Maggaṃ bhāvento||
ariyaṃ aṭṭhaṅgikaṃ Maggaṃ bahulī-karonto||
Nibbāna-ninno hoti,||
Nibbāna-poṇo||
Nibbāna-pabbhāro.|| ||

Kathañ ca bhikkhave, bhikkhu||
ariyaṃ aṭṭhaṅgikaṃ Maggaṃ bhāvento||
ariyaṃ aṭṭhaṅgikaṃ Maggaṃ bahulī-karonto||
Nibbāna-ninno hoti||
Nibbāna-poṇo||
Nibbāna-pabbhāro?|| ||

Idha bhikkhave, bhikkhu||
sammā-diṭṭhiṃ bhāveti||
Nibbāna-ninnaṃ||
Nibbāna-poṇaṃ||
Nibbāna pabbhāraṃ.|| ||

Sammā-saṅkappaṃ bhāveti||
Nibbāna-ninnaṃ||
Nibbāna-poṇaṃ||
Nibbāna pabbhāraṃ.|| ||

Sammā-vācaṃ bhāveti||
Nibbāna-ninnaṃ||
Nibbāna-poṇaṃ||
Nibbāna pabbhāraṃ.|| ||

Sammā-kammantaṃ bhāveti||
Nibbāna-ninnaṃ||
Nibbāna-poṇaṃ||
Nibbāna pabbhāraṃ.|| ||

Sammā-ājīvaṃ bhāveti||
Nibbāna-ninnaṃ||
Nibbāna-poṇaṃ||
Nibbāna pabbhāraṃ.|| ||

Sammā-vāyāmaṃ bhāveti||
Nibbāna-ninnaṃ||
Nibbāna-poṇaṃ||
Nibbāna pabbhāraṃ.|| ||

Sammā-satiṃ bhāveti||

Sammā-samādhiṃ bhāveti||
