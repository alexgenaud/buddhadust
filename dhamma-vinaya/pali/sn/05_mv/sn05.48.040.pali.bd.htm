Saɱyutta Nikāya,
V: Mahā-Vagga
48. Indriya Saɱyutta
IV. Sukh'indriya (or Uppaṭi) -vagga

Sutta 40

Uppatika Suttaɱ

Adapted from the 1995 edition of the digital version of the Sri Lanka Buddha Jayanti Tripitaka Series.

 

 

[213] [1][wood][olds] Evam me sutaɱ:|| ||

Ekaɱ samayam Bhagavā Sāvatthiyaɱ viharati.|| ||

Tatra kho Bhagavā bhikkhū āmantesi:|| ||

"Bhikkhavo" ti.|| ||

"Bhadante" ti||
te bhikkhū Bhagavato paccassosuɱ.|| ||

Bhagavā etad avoca:|| ||

"Pañc'imāni, bhikkhave, indriyāni.|| ||

Katamāni pañca?|| ||

Dukkh'indriyaɱ||
domanass'indriyaɱ||
sukh'indriyaɱ||
somanass'indriyaɱ||
upekkh'indriyaɱ.|| ||

Imāni kho bhikkhave, pañc'indriyānī|| ||

■

Idha, bhikkhave, bhikkhuno appamattassa||
ātāpino||
pahitattassa viharato||
uppajjati dukkh'indriyaɱ.|| ||

So evaɱ pajānāti:|| ||

'Uppannaɱ kho me idaɱ dukkh'indriyaɱ.|| ||

Tañ ca kho sanimittaɱ||
sa-nidānaɱ||
sa-sankhāraɱ||
sappaccayaɱ.|| ||

Taɱ ca animittaɱ||
anidānaɱ||
asankhāraɱ||
appaccayaɱ||
dukkh'indriyaɱ uppajjissati,||
n'etaɱ ṭhānaɱ vijjatī' ti.|| ||

So dukkh'indriyañ ca pajānāti||
dukkh'indriya-samudayañ ca pajānati||
dukkh'indriya-nirodhañ ca pajānāti.|| ||

Yattha c'uppannaɱ dukkh'indriyaɱ aparisesaŋ nirujjhati.|| ||

Tañ ca pajānāti.|| ||

Kattha c'uppannaɱ dukkh'indriyaɱ aparisesaŋ nirujjhati?|| ||

Idha bhikkhave, bhikkhu||
vivicc'eva kāmehi||
vivicca akusalehi dhammehi||
sa-vitakkaɱ||
sa-vicāraɱ||
viveka-jaɱ pīti-sukhaɱ||
paṭhamaɱ jhānam upasampajja viharati.|| ||

Ettha c'uppannaɱ dukkh'indriyaɱ aparisesaŋ nirujjhati.|| ||

Ayaɱ vuccati bhikkhave:||
'Bhikkhu aññāsi dukkh'indriyassa nirodhaɱ||
tathattāya cittaɱ upasaŋhāsi.|| ||

■

Idha, bhikkhave, bhikkhuno appamattassa||
ātāpino||
pahitattassa viharato||
uppajjati domanass'indriyaɱ.|| ||

[214] So evaɱ pajānāti:|| ||

'Uppannaɱ kho me idaɱ domanass'indriyaɱ.|| ||

Tañ ca kho sanimittaɱ||
sa-nidānaɱ||
sa-sankhāraɱ||
sappaccayaɱ.|| ||

Taɱ ca animittaɱ||
anidānaɱ||
asankhāraɱ||
appaccayaɱ||
domanass'indriyaɱ uppajjissati,||
n'etaɱ ṭhānaɱ vijjatī' ti.|| ||

So domanass'indriyañ ca pajānāti||
domanass'indriya-nirodhañ ca pajānāti.|| ||

Yattha c'uppannaɱ domanass'indriyaɱ aparisesaŋ nirujjhati.|| ||

Tañ ca pajānāti.|| ||

Kattha c'uppannaɱ domanass'indriyaɱ aparisesaŋ nirujjhati?|| ||

Idha bhikkhave, bhikkhu||
vitakka-vicārānaɱ vūpasamā||
ajjhattaɱ sampasādanaɱ||
cetaso ekodi-bhāvaɱ||
avitakkaɱ||
avicāraɱ||
samādhi-jaɱ pīti-sukhaɱ||
dutiyaɱ jhānam upasampajja viharati.|| ||

Ettha c'uppannaɱ domanass'indriyaɱ aparisesaŋ nirujjhati.|| ||

Ayaɱ vuccati bhikkhave:||
'Bhikkhu aññāsi domanass'indriyassa nirodhaɱ||
tathattāya cittaɱ upasaŋhāsi.|| ||

■

Idha, bhikkhave, bhikkhuno appamattassa||
ātāpino||
pahitattassa viharato||
uppajjati sukh'indriyaɱ.|| ||

So evaɱ pajānāti:|| ||

'Uppannaɱ kho me idaɱ sukh'indriyaɱ.|| ||

Tañ ca kho sanimittaɱ||
sa-nidānaɱ||
sa-sankhāraɱ||
sappaccayaɱ.|| ||

Taɱ ca animittaɱ||
anidānaɱ||
asankhāraɱ||
appaccayaɱ||
sukh'indriyaɱ uppajjissati,||
n'etaɱ ṭhānaɱ vijjatī' ti.|| ||

So sukh'indriyañ ca pajānāti||
sukh'indriya-nirodhañ ca pajānāti.|| ||

Yattha c'uppannaɱ sukh'indriyaɱ aparisesaŋ nirujjhati.|| ||

Tañ ca pajānāti.|| ||

Kattha c'uppannaɱ sukh'indriyaɱ aparisesaŋ nirujjhati?|| ||

Idha bhikkhave, bhikkhu||
pītiyā ca virāgā||
upekkhako ca viharati||
sato ca sampajāno||
sukhañ ca kāyena paṭisaŋvedeti.|| ||

Yaɱ taɱ ariyā ācikkhanti|| ||

'Upekkhako satimā sukha-vihārī' ti||
tatiyaɱ jhānam upasampajja viharati.|| ||

Ettha c'uppannaɱ sukh'indriyaɱ aparisesaŋ nirujjhati.|| ||

Ayaɱ vuccati bhikkhave:||
'Bhikkhu aññāsi sukh'indriyassa nirodhaɱ||
tathattāya cittaɱ upasaŋhāsi.|| ||

■

[215] Idha, bhikkhave, bhikkhuno appamattassa||
ātāpino||
pahitattassa viharato||
uppajjati somanass'indriyaɱ.|| ||

So evaɱ pajānāti:|| ||

'Uppannaɱ kho me idaɱ somanass'indriyaɱ.|| ||

Tañ ca kho sanimittaɱ||
sa-nidānaɱ||
sa-sankhāraɱ||
sappaccayaɱ.|| ||

Taɱ ca animittaɱ||
anidānaɱ||
asankhāraɱ||
appaccayaɱ||
somanass'indriyaɱ uppajjissati,||
n'etaɱ ṭhānaɱ vijjatī' ti.|| ||

So somanass'indriyañ ca pajānāti||
somanass'indriya-nirodhañ ca pajānāti.|| ||

Yattha c'uppannaɱ somanass'indriyaɱ aparisesaŋ nirujjhati.|| ||

Tañ ca pajānāti.|| ||

Kattha c'uppannaɱ somanass'indriyaɱ aparisesaŋ nirujjhati?|| ||

Idha bhikkhave, bhikkhu||
sukhassa ca pahānā||
dukkhassa ca pahānā||
pubb'eva somanassa domanassānaɱ attha-gamā||
adukkhaɱ||
asukhaɱ||
upekkhā-sati-pārisuddhiɱ||
catutthaɱ jhānam upasampajja viharati.|| ||

Ettha c'uppannaɱ somanass'indriyaɱ aparisesaŋ nirujjhati.|| ||

Ayaɱ vuccati bhikkhave:||
'Bhikkhu aññāsi somanass'indriyassa nirodhaɱ||
tathattāya cittaɱ upasaŋhāsi.|| ||

■

Idha, bhikkhave, bhikkhuno appamattassa||
ātāpino||
pahitattassa viharato||
uppajjati upekkh'indriyaɱ.|| ||

So evaɱ pajānāti:|| ||

'Uppannaɱ kho me idaɱ upekkh'indriyaɱ.|| ||

Tañ ca kho sanimittaɱ||
sa-nidānaɱ||
sa-sankhāraɱ||
sappaccayaɱ.|| ||

Taɱ ca animittaɱ||
anidānaɱ||
asankhāraɱ||
appaccayaɱ||
upekkh'indriyaɱ uppajjissati,||
n'etaɱ ṭhānaɱ vijjatī' ti.|| ||

So upekkh'indriyañ ca pajānāti||
upekkh'indriya-nirodhañ ca pajānāti.|| ||

Yattha c'uppannaɱ upekkh'indriyaɱ aparisesaŋ nirujjhati.|| ||

Tañ ca pajānāti.|| ||

Kattha c'uppannaɱ upekkh'indriyaɱ aparisesaŋ nirujjhati?|| ||

Idha bhikkhave, bhikkhu||
sabbaso N'eva-saññā-nā-saññ'āyatanaɱ samati-k-kama||
saññā-vedayita-nirodhaɱ upasampajja viharati.|| ||

Ettha c'uppannaɱ upekkh'indriyaɱ aparisesaŋ nirujjhati.|| ||

Ayaɱ vuccati bhikkhave:||
'Bhikkhu aññāsi upekkh'indriyassa nirodhaɱ||
tathattāya cittaɱ upasaŋhāsi.|| ||

Sukh'indriya-vaggo catuttho.
