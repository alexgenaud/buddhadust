Saɱyutta Nikāya,
V: Mahā-Vagga
48. Indriya Saɱyutta
2. Jara Vagga

Sutta 50

Saddha or Āpaṇa Suttaɱ

Adapted from the 1995 edition of the digital version of the Sri Lanka Buddha Jayanti Tripitaka Series.

 

 

[1][pts] Evam me sutaɱ:|| ||

Ekaɱ samayaɱ Bhagavā Aŋgesu viharati āpaṇaɱ nāma aŋgānaɱ nigamo.|| ||

2. Tatra kho Bhagavā āyasmantaɱ Sāriputtaɱ āmantesi:|| ||

"Yo so Sāriputta, ariya-sāvako Tathāgate ekanta-gato abhi-p-pasanno,||
api nu so Tathāgate vā Tathāgatasāsane vā kaŋkheyya vā vici-kiccheyya vā" ti?|| ||

3. Yo so bhante, ariya-sāvako Tathāgate ekanta-gato abhi-p-pasanno,||
na so Tathāgate vā Tathāgatasāsane vā kaŋkheyya vā vici-kiccheyya vā.|| ||

4. Saddhassa hi bhante, ariya-sāvakassa etaɱ pāṭikaŋkhaɱ:|| ||

Yaɱ āraddha-viriyo viharissati akusalānaɱ dhammānaɱ pahānāya kusalānaɱ dhammānaɱ upasampadāya thāmavā daḷha-parakkamo anikkhitta-dhuro kusalesu dhammesu.|| ||

Yaɱ hissa bhante, viriyaɱ tad assa viriy'indriyaɱ.|| ||

5. Saddhassa hi bhante, ariya-sāvakassa āraddha-viriyassa etaɱ pāṭikaŋkhaɱ:|| ||

Yaɱ satimā bhavissati paramena sati-nepakkena samannāgato cira-katam pi cira-bhāsitam pi saritā anussaritā.|| ||

Yā hi'ssa bhante, sati, tad assa sat'indriyaɱ.|| ||

6. Saddhassa hi bhante, ariya-sāvakassa||
āraddha-viriyassa||
upaṭṭhita-satino etaɱ pāṭikaŋkhaɱ:|| ||

Yaɱ vossagg'ārammaṇaɱ karitvā labhissati samādhiɱ labhissati cittassa ek'aggataɱ.|| ||

Yo hissa bhante, samādhi tadassa samādh'indriyaɱ.|| ||

7. Saddhassa hi bhante, ariya-sāvakassa||
āraddha-viriyassa||
upaṭṭhita-satino||
[226] samāhitacittassa etaɱ pāṭikaŋkhaɱ:|| ||

Yaɱ evaɱ pajānissati anamataggo kho saŋsāro pubbā koṭi na paññāyati.|| ||

Avijjānīvaraṇānaɱ sattānaɱ taṇhā-saŋyojanānaɱ sandhāvataɱ saŋsarataɱ||
avijjāya tv'eva tamokāyassa asesavirāga-nirodho,||
santam etaɱ padaɱ,||
paṇītam etaɱ padaɱ,||
yad idaɱ sabba-sankhāra-samatho||
sabb'ūpadhi-paṭinissaggo||
taṇha-k-khayo||
virāgo||
nirodho||
Nibbānaɱ.|| ||

Yā hi'ssa bhante, paññā tad assa paññ'indriyaɱ.|| ||

8. Saddho so bhante, ariya-sāvako
evaɱ padahitvā padahitvā||
evaɱ saritvā saritvā||
evaɱ samādahitvā samādahitvā||
evam pajānitvā pajānitvā||
evam abhisadda-hati:|| ||

"Ime kho te dhammā ye me pubbe sutāva ahesuɱ||
te'dān'āhaɱ etarahi kāyena ca phusitvā viharāmi||
paññāya ca ativijjha passāmī" ti.|| ||

Yā hi'ssa bhante, saddhā tad assa saddh'indriyan ti.|| ||

9. Sādhu sādhu Sāriputta.|| ||

Yo so Sāriputta, ariya-sāvako Tathāgate ekanta-gato abhi-p-pasanno,||
na so Tathāgate vā Tathāgatasāsane vā kaŋkheyya vā vici-kiccheyya vā.|| ||

Saddhassa hi Sāriputta, ariya-sāvakassa etaɱ pāṭikaŋkhaɱ:|| ||

Yaɱ āraddha-viriyo viharissati akusalānaɱ dhammānaɱ pahānāya kusalānaɱ dhammānaɱ upasampadāya thāmavā daḷha-parakkamo anikkhitta-dhuro kusalesu dhammesu.|| ||

Yaɱ hissa Sāriputta, viriyaɱ tad assa viriy'indriyaɱ.|| ||

10. Saddhassa hi Sāriputta, ariya-sāvakassa āraddha-viriyassa etaɱ pāṭikaŋkhaɱ:|| ||

Yaɱ satimā bhavissati paramena sati-nepakkena samannāgato cira-katam pi cira-bhāsitam pi saritā anussaritā.|| ||

Yā hi'ssa Sāriputta, sati, tad assa sat'indriyaɱ.|| ||

11. Saddhassa hi Sāriputta, ariya-sāvakassa||
āraddha-viriyassa||
upaṭṭhita-satino etaɱ pāṭikaŋkhaɱ:|| ||

Yaɱ vossagg'ārammaṇaɱ karitvā labhissati samādhiɱ labhissati cittassa ek'aggataɱ.|| ||

Yo hissa Sāriputta, samādhi tadassa samādh'indriyaɱ.|| ||

12. Saddhassa hi Sāriputta, ariya-sāvakassa||
āraddha-viriyassa||
upaṭṭhita-satino||
samāhitacittassa etaɱ pāṭikaŋkhaɱ:|| ||

Yaɱ evaɱ pajānissati anamataggo kho saŋsāro pubbā koṭi na paññāyati.|| ||

Avijjānīvaraṇānaɱ sattānaɱ taṇhā-saŋyojanānaɱ sandhāvataɱ saŋsarataɱ||
avijjāya tv'eva tamokāyassa asesavirāga-nirodho,||
santam etaɱ padaɱ,||
paṇītam etaɱ padaɱ,||
yad idaɱ sabba-sankhāra-samatho||
sabb'ūpadhi-paṭinissaggo||
taṇha-k-khayo||
virāgo||
nirodho||
Nibbānaɱ.|| ||

Yā hi'ssa Sāriputta, paññā tad assa paññ'indriyaɱ.|| ||

13. Saddho so Sāriputta, ariya-sāvako
evaɱ padahitvā padahitvā||
evaɱ saritvā saritvā||
evaɱ samādahitvā samādahitvā||
evam pajānitvā pajānitvā||
evam abhisadda-hati:|| ||

"Ime kho te dhammā ye me pubbe sutāva ahesuɱ||
te'dān'āhaɱ [227] etarahi kāyena ca phusitvā viharāmi||
paññāya ca ativijjha passāmī" ti.|| ||

Yā hi'ssa Sāriputta, saddhā tad assa saddh'indriyan ti.|| ||
