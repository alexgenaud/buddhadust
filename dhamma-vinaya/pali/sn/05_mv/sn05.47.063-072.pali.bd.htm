Sutta 63

Tathāgata Suttaɱ

i. Viveka

[63.1][pts] Evam me sutaɱ:|| ||

Ekaɱ samayam Bhagavā Sāvatthiyaɱ viharati.|| ||

Tatra kho Bhagavā bhikkhū āmantesi:|| ||

"Bhikkhavo" ti.|| ||

"Bhadante" ti||
te bhikkhū Bhagavato paccassosuɱ.|| ||

Bhagavā etad avoca:|| ||

[63.2][pts] "Yāvatā bhikkhave, sattā apadā vā||
dvipadā vā||
catu-p-padā vā||
bahu-p-padā vā||
rūpino vā||
arūpino vā||
saññino vā||
asaññino vā||
n'eva-saññi-nā-saññino vā||
nāsaññino vā||
Tathāgato tesaɱ aggam akkhāyati arahaɱ Sammā Sambuddho.|| ||

Evam eva kho bhikkhave, ye keci kusalā dhammā,||
sabbe te appamāda-mūlakā||
appamāda-samo-saraṇā||
appamādo tesaɱ dhammānaɱ aggam akkhāyati.|| ||

Appamattass'etaɱ bhikkhave, bhikkhuno pāṭikaṅkhaɱ||
cattaro sati-paṭṭhāne bhāvessati,||
cattaro sati-paṭṭhāne bahulī-karissatī.|| ||

Kathañ ca bhikkhave, bhikkhu appamatto||
cattaro sati-paṭṭhāne bhāveti,||
cattaro sati-paṭṭhāne bahulī-karoti?|| ||

Idha bhikkhave, bhikkhu kāye kāy'ānupassī viharati[ed1]||
viveka-nissitaɱ,||
virāga-nissitaɱ,||
nirodha-nissitaɱ,||
vossagga-pariṇāmiɱ;|| ||

Vedanāsu vedan'ānupassī viharati||
viveka-nissitaɱ,||
virāga-nissitaɱ,||
nirodha-nissitaɱ,||
vossagga-pariṇāmiɱ;|| ||

Citte citt'ānupassī viharati||
viveka-nissitaɱ,||
virāga-nissitaɱ,||
nirodha-nissitaɱ,||
vossagga-pariṇāmiɱ;|| ||

Dhammesu Dhamm'ānupassī viharati||
viveka-nissitaɱ,||
virāga-nissitaɱ,||
nirodha-nissitaɱ,||
vossagga-pariṇāmiɱ.|| ||

Evaɱ kho bhikkhave, bhikkhu appamatto||
cattaro sati-paṭṭhāne bhāveti||
cattaro sati-paṭṭhāne bahulī-karotī" ti.|| ||
