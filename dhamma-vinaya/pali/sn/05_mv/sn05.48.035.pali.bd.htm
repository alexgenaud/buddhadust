Saɱyutta Nikāya,
V: Mahā-Vagga
48. Indriya Saɱyutta
IV. Sukhindriya (or Uppaṭi) -vagga

Sutta 35

Dutiya Sammaṇabrāhmaṇa Suttaɱ

Adapted from the 1995 edition of the digital version of the Sri Lanka Buddha Jayanti Tripitaka Series.

 

 

[1][pts] Evam me sutaɱ:|| ||

Ekaɱ samayam Bhagavā Sāvatthiyaɱ viharati.|| ||

Tatra kho Bhagavā bhikkhū āmantesi:|| ||

"Bhikkhavo" ti.|| ||

"Bhadante" ti||
te bhikkhū Bhagavato paccassosuɱ.|| ||

Bhagavā etad avoca:|| ||

"Pañc'imāni, bhikkhave, indriyāni.|| ||

Katamāni pañca?|| ||

Sukh'indriyaɱ||
dukkh'indriyaɱ||
somanass'indriyaɱ||
domanass'indriyaɱ||
upekkh'indriyaɱ.|| ||

Imāni kho bhikkhave, pañc'indriyānī|| ||

"Ye hi keci, bhikkhave, samaṇā vā brāhmaṇā vā||
sukh'indriyaɱ na pajānanti,||
sukh'indriyaɱ-samudayaɱ na pajānanti,||
sukh'indriyaɱ-nirodhaɱ na pajānanti,||
sukh'indriyaɱ-nirodha-gāminī-paṭipadaɱ na pajānanti;|| ||

Dukkh'indriyaɱ na pajānanti,||
dukkh'indriyaɱ-samudayaɱ na pajānanti,||
dukkh'indriyaɱ-nirodhaɱ na pajānanti,||
dukkh'indriyaɱ-nirodha-gāminī-paṭipadaɱ na pajānanti;|| ||

Somanass'indriyaɱ na pajānanti,||
somanass'indriyaɱ-samudayaɱ na pajānanti,||
somanass'indriyaɱ-nirodhaɱ na pajānanti,||
v-nirodha-gāminī-paṭipadaɱ na pajānanti;|| ||

Domanass'indriyaɱ na pajānanti,||
domanass'indriyaɱ-samudayaɱ na pajānanti,||
domanass'indriyaɱ-nirodhaɱ na pajānanti,||
domanass'indriyaɱ-nirodha-gāminī-paṭipadaɱ na pajānanti;|| ||

Upekkh'indriyaɱ na pajānanti,||
upekkh'indriyaɱ-samudayaɱ na pajānanti,||
upekkh'indriyaɱ-nirodhaɱ na pajānanti,||
upekkh'indriyaɱ-nirodha-gāminī-paṭipadaɱ na pajānanti;|| ||

na me te bhikkhave samaṇā vā brāhmaṇā vā||
samaṇesu vā samaṇa-sammatā||
brāhmaṇesu vā brāhmaṇa-sammatā||
na ca pana te āyasmanto sāmaññ'atthaɱ vā brahmaññ'atthaɱ vā||
diṭṭhe'va dhamme sayaɱ abhiññā sacchi-katvā upasampajja viharanti.|| ||

 

 

"Ye ca kho keci, bhikkhave, samaṇā vā brāhmaṇā vā||
sukh'indriyaɱ pajānanti,||
sukh'indriyaɱ-samudayaɱ pajānanti,||
sukh'indriyaɱ-nirodhaɱ pajānanti,||
sukh'indriyaɱ-nirodha-gāminī-paṭipadaɱ pajānanti;|| ||

Dukkh'indriyaɱ pajānanti,||
dukkh'indriyaɱ-samudayaɱ pajānanti,||
dukkh'indriyaɱ-nirodhaɱ pajānanti,||
dukkh'indriyaɱ-nirodha-gāminī-paṭipadaɱ pajānanti;|| ||

Somanass'indriyaɱ pajānanti,||
somanass'indriyaɱ-samudayaɱ pajānanti,||
somanass'indriyaɱ-nirodhaɱ pajānanti,||
somanass'indriyaɱ-nirodha-gāminī-paṭipadaɱ pajānanti;|| ||

Domanass'indriyaɱ pajānanti,||
domanass'indriyaɱ-samudayaɱ pajānanti,||
domanass'indriyaɱ-nirodhaɱ pajānanti,||
domanass'indriyaɱ-nirodha-gāminī-paṭipadaɱ pajānanti;|| ||

Upekkh'indriyaɱ pajānanti,||
upekkh'indriyaɱ-samudayaɱ pajānanti,||
upekkh'indriyaɱ-nirodhaɱ pajānanti,||
upekkh'indriyaɱ-nirodha-gāminī-paṭipadaɱ pajānanti;|| ||

Ye ca kho kici bhikkhave, samaṇā vā brāhmaṇā vā||
samaṇesu vā samaṇa-sammatā||
brāhmaṇesu vā brāhmaṇa-sammatā||
te ca panāyasmanto sāmaññ'atthaɱ vā brahmaññ'atthaɱ vā||
diṭṭhe'va dhamme sayaɱ abhiññā-sacchi-katvā upasampajja viharantī" ti.|| ||
