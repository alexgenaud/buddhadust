Saɱyutta Nikāya:
V. Mahā-Vagga
46. Bojjhaŋga Saɱyuttaa
Chapter VI: Bojjangasakaccam

Sutta 53

Aggi Suttaɱ

Adapted from the 1995 edition of the digital version of the Sri Lanka Buddha Jayanti Tripitaka Series.

 

 

[1][pts][bodh][than] Evam me sutaɱ:|| ||

2. Atha kho sambahulā bhikkhū pubbaṇha-samayaɱ nivāsetvā patta-cīvaraɱ ādāya Sāvatthiɱ piṇḍāya pavisiɱsu.|| ||

3. Atha kho tesaɱ bhikkhūnaɱ etad ahosi:|| ||

"Atippago kho tāva Sāvatthiyaɱ piṇḍāya carituɱ.|| ||

Yaɱ nūna mayaɱ yena añña-titthiyānaɱ paribabājakānaɱ ārāmo ten'upasankameyyāmā" ti.|| ||

4. Atha kho te bhikkhū yena aññatitthayānaɱ paribbājakānaɱ ārāmo ten'upasankamiɱsu.|| ||

Upasaŋkamitvā tehi añña-titthiyehi paribbājakehi saddhiɱ sammodiɱsu.|| ||

Sammodanīyaɱ kathaɱ sārāṇīyaɱ vīti-sāretvā eka-m-antaɱ nisīdiɱsu.|| ||

Eka-m-antaɱ nisinne kho te bhikkhū te añña-titthiyā paribbājakā etad avocuɱ:|| ||

5. "Samaṇo āvuso Gotamo sāvakānaɱ evaɱ dhammaɱ deseti.|| ||

"Etha tumhe bhikkhave, pañca nīvaraṇe pahāya cetaso upakkilese paññāya dubbalī-karaṇe satta bojjhaŋge yathā-bhūtaɱ bhāvethā" ti.|| ||

Mayam pi kho āvuso, sāvakānaɱ evaɱ dhammaɱ desema.|| ||

"Etha tumhe āvuso, pañca nīvaraṇe pahāya cetaso upakkilese paññāya dubbalī-karaṇe satta bojjhaŋge yathā-bhūtaɱ bhāvethā" ti.|| ||

Idha no āvuso ko viseso, ko adhippāyo.|| ||

Kiɱ nānā-karaṇaɱ, samaṇassa vā Gotamassa amhākaɱ vā yad idaɱ Dhamma-desanāya vā Dhamma-desanaɱ anusāsaniyā vā anusāsanin" ti.|| ||

6. Atha kho te bhikkhū tesaɱ añña-titthiyānaɱ paribbājakānaɱ bhāsitaɱ n'eva abhinandiɱsu, na paṭikkosiɱsu.|| ||

Anabhinan'ditvā a-p-paṭikkositvā uṭṭhāy'āsanā pakkamiɱsu,|| ||

"Bhagavato santike etassa bhāsitassa atthaɱ ājānissāmā" ti.|| ||

7. Atha kho te bhikkhū Sāvatthiyaɱ piṇḍāya caritvā pacchābhantaɱ piṇḍa-pāta-paṭikkantā yena Bhagavā ten'upasankamiɱsu.|| ||

Upasaŋkamitvā Bhagavantaɱ abhivādetvā eka-m-antaɱ nisīdiɱsu.|| ||

8. Eka-m-antaɱ nisinnā kho te bhikkhū Bhagavantaɱ etad avocuɱ:|| ||

"Idha mayaɱ bhante pubbaṇha-samayaɱ nivāsetvā patta-cīvaram ādāya Sāvatthiɱ piṇḍāya pavisimha.|| ||

Tesaɱ no bhante, amhākaɱ etad ahosi:|| ||

"Atippago kho tāva Sāvatthiyaɱ piṇḍāya carituɱ yaɱ nūna mayaɱ yena añña-titthiyānaɱ paribbājakānaɱ ārāmo ten'upasankameyyāmā ti.|| ||

Atha kho mayaɱ bhante, yena añña-titthiyānaɱ paribbājakānaɱ ārāmo ten'upasankamimha.|| ||

Upasaŋkamitvā tehi añña-titthiyehi paribbājakehi saddhiɱ sammodimha.|| ||

Sammodanīyaɱ kathaɱ sārāṇīyaɱ vīti-sāretvā eka-m-antaɱ nisīdimha.|| ||

Eka-m-antaɱ nisinne kho amhe bhante, te añña-titthiyā paribbājakā etad avocuɱ:|| ||

9. "Samaṇo āvuso Gotamo sāvakānaɱ evaɱ dhammaɱ deseti.|| ||

"Etha tumhe bhikkhave, pañca nīvaraṇe pahāya cetaso upakkilese paññāya dubbalī-karaṇe satta bojjhaŋge yathā-bhūtaɱ bhāvethā" ti.|| ||

Mayam pi kho āvuso, sāvakānaɱ evaɱ dhammaɱ desema:|| ||

"Etha tumhe āvuso, pañca nīvaraṇe pahāya cetaso upakkilese paññāya dubbalī-karaṇe, satta bojjhaŋge yathā-bhūtaɱ bhāvethā" ti.|| ||

Idha no āvuso, ko viseso ko adhippāyo.|| ||

Kiɱ nānā-karaṇaɱ samaṇassa vā Gotamassa amhākaɱ vā yad idaɱ Dhamma-desanāya vā Dhamma-desanaɱ anusāsaniyā vā anusāsanin ti.|| ||

10. Atha kho mayaɱ bhante, tesaɱ añña-titthiyānaɱ paribbājakānaɱ bhāsitaɱ n'eva abhinandimha, na paṭikkosimha.|| ||

Anabhinan'ditvā a-p-paṭikkositvā uṭṭhāy āsanā pakkamimha.|| ||

"Bhagavato santike etassa bhāsitassa atthaɱ ājānissāmā" ti.|| ||

11. Evaɱ vādino bhikkhave, añña-titthiyā paribbājakā evam assu vacanīyā:|| ||

"Yasmiɱ āvuso, samaye līnaɱ cittaɱ hoti.|| ||

Katamesaɱ tasmiɱ samaye bojjh'aŋgānaɱ akālo bhāvanāya. Katamesaɱ tasmiɱ samaye bojjh'aŋgānaɱ kālo bhāvanāya:|| ||

Yasmiɱ pan'āvuso samaye uddhataɱ cittaɱ hoti. Katamesaɱ tasmiɱ samaye bojjh'aŋgānaɱ akālo bhāvanāya.|| ||

Katamesaɱ tasmiɱ samaye bojjh'aŋgānaɱ kālo bhāvanāyā" ti.|| ||

Evaɱ puṭṭhā bhikkhave, añña-titthiyā paribbājakā na c'eva sampāyissanti,||
uttariñ ca vighātaɱ āpajji-s-santi.|| ||

12. Taɱ kissa hetu?|| ||

Yathā taɱ bhikkhave avisayasmiɱ.|| ||

Nāhaɱ taɱ bhikkhave, passāmi sa-devake loke sa-Mārake sa-brahmake sa-s-samaṇa-brāhmaṇiyā pajāya sadeva-manussāya yo imesaɱ pañhānaɱ veyyā-karaṇena cittaɱ ārādheyya aññatra Tathāgatena vā Tathāgata-sāvakena vā ito vā pana sutvā.|| ||

I. Akālo

13. Yasmiɱ bhikkhave, samaye līnaɱ cittaɱ hoti.|| ||

Akālo tasmiɱ samaye passaddhi-sambojjhaŋssa bhāvanāya.|| ||

Akālo samādhisambojjh-a-ŋgassa bhāvanāya.|| ||

Akālo upekhā-sambojjh-a-ŋgassa bhāvanāya.|| ||

Taɱ kissa hetu?|| ||

Līnaɱ bhikkhave cittaɱ.|| ||

Taɱ etehi dhammehi dussamuṭṭhāpayaɱ hoti.|| ||

14. Seyyathā pi bhikkhave, puriso parittaɱ aggiɱ ujjāletukāmo assa.|| ||

So tattha allāni c'eva tiṇāni pakkhipeyya, allāni ca gomayāni pakkhipeyya,||
allāni c'eva kaṭṭhani pakkhipeyya,||
udakavātañ ca dadeyya,||
paɱsukena ca okireyya,||
bhabbo nu kho so puriso parittaɱ aggiɱ ujjāletuɱ?|| ||

No h'etaɱ bhante.|| ||

Evam eva kho bhikkhave, yasmiɱ samaye līnaɱ cittaɱ hoti.|| ||

Akālo tasmiɱ samaye passaddhi-sambojjh-a-ŋgassa bhāvanāya.|| ||

Akālo samādhisambojjh-a-ŋgassa bhāvanāya.|| ||

Akālo upekhā-sambojjh-a-ŋgassa bhāvanāya.|| ||

Taɱ kissa hetu?|| ||

Līnaɱ bhikkhave cittaɱ.|| ||

Taɱ etehi dhammehi dussamuṭṭhāpayaɱ hoti.|| ||

II. Kālo

15. Yasmiɱ ca kho bhikkhave, samaye līnaɱ cittaɱ hoti.

Kālo tasmiɱ samaye dhamma-vicaya-sambojjh-a-ŋgassa bhāvanāya.|| ||

Kālo viriya-sambojjh-a-ŋgassa bhāvanāya.|| ||

Kālo pīti-sambojjh-a-ŋgassa bhāvanāya.|| ||

Taɱ kissa hetu?|| ||

Līnaɱ bhikkhave cittaɱ.|| ||

Taɱ etehi dhammehi susamuṭṭhāpayaɱ hoti.|| ||

16. Seyyathā pi bhikkhave, puriso parittaɱ aggiɱ ujjāletukāmo assa.|| ||

So tattha sukkhāni c'eva tiṇāni pakkhipeyya,||
sukkhāni gomayāni pakkhipeyya,||
sukkhāni kaṭṭhāni pakkhipeyya.|| ||

Mukhavātaɱ ca dadeyya,||
na ca paɱsukena okireyya.|| ||

Bhabbo nu kho so puriso parittaɱ aggiɱ ujjāletun ti.|| ||

"Evaɱ bhante" ti.|| ||

Evam eva kho bhikkhave, yasmiɱ samaye līnaɱ cittaɱ hoti.|| ||

Kālo tasmiɱ samaye dhamma-vicaya-sambojjh-a-ŋgassa bhāvanāya.|| ||

Kālo viriya-sambojjh-a-ŋgassa bhāvanāya.|| ||

Kālo pīti-sambojjh-a-ŋgassa bhāvanāya.|| ||

Taɱ kissa hetu?|| ||

Līnaɱ bhikkhave cittaɱ.|| ||

Taɱ etehi dhammehi susamuṭṭhāpayaɱ hoti.|| ||

III. Akālo

Yasmiɱ bhikkhave, samaye uddhataɱ cittaɱ hoti.|| ||

Akālo tasmiɱ samaye dhamma-vicaya-sambojjh-a-ŋgassa bhāvanāya.|| ||

Akālo viriya-sambojjh-a-ŋgassa bhāvanāya.|| ||

Akālo pīti-sambojjh-a-ŋgassa bhāvanāya.|| ||

Taɱ kissa hetu?|| ||

Uddhataɱ bhikkhave cittaɱ.|| ||

Taɱ etehi dhammehi duvūpasamaɱ hoti.|| ||

18. Seyyathā pi bhikkhave, puriso mahantaɱ aggi-k-khandhaɱ nibbāpetukāmo assa.|| ||

So tattha sukkhāni c'eva tiṇāni pakkhipeyya, sukkhāni gomayāni pakkhipeyya,||
sukkhāni kaṭṭhāni pakkhipeyya,||
mukhavātañ ca dadeyya,||
na ca paɱsukena okireyya.|| ||

Bhabbo nu kho so puriso mahantaɱ aggi-k-khandhaɱ nibbāpetun ti?|| ||

No h'etaɱ bhante.|| ||

Evam eva kho bhikkhave, yasmiɱ samaye uddhataɱ cittaɱ hoti.|| ||

Akālo tasmiɱ samaye dhamma-vicaya-sambojjh-a-ŋgassa bhāvanāya.|| ||

Akālo viriya-sambojjh-a-ŋgassa bhāvanāya.|| ||

Akālo pīti-sambojjh-a-ŋgassa bhāvanāya.|| ||

Taɱ kissa hetu?|| ||

Uddhataɱ bhikkhave cittaɱ.|| ||

Taɱ etehi dhammehi duvūpasamaɱ hoti.|| ||

IV. Kālo

19. Yasmiɱ ca kho bhikkhave, samaye uddhataɱ cittaɱ hoti.|| ||

Kālo tasmiɱ samaye passaddhi-sambojjhaŋssa bhāvanāya.|| ||

Kālo samādhisambojjh-a-ŋgassa bhāvanāya.|| ||

Kālo upekhā-sambojjh-a-ŋgassa bhāvanāya.|| ||

Taɱ kissa hetu?|| ||

Uddhataɱ bhikkhave cittaɱ.|| ||

Taɱ etehi dhammehi suvūpasamaɱ hoti.|| ||

20. Seyyathā pi bhikkhave, puriso mahantaɱ aggi-k-khandhaɱ nibbāpetukāmo assa.|| ||

So tattha allāni c'eva tiṇāni pakkhipeyya,||
allāni c'eva gomayāni pakkhipeyya,||
allāni ca kaṭṭhāni pakkhipeyya,||
udakavātañ ca dadeyya.|| ||

Paɱsukena ca okireyya.|| ||

Bhabbo nu kho so puriso parittaɱ aggi-k-khandhaɱ nibbāpetun ti?|| ||

Evaɱ bhante.

Evam eva kho bhikkhave, yasmiɱ samaye uddhakaɱ cittaɱ hoti.|| ||

Kālo tasmiɱ samaye passaddhi-sambojjh-a-ŋgassa bhāvanāya.|| ||

Kālo samādhisambojjh-a-ŋgassa bhāvanāya.|| ||

Kālo upekhā-sambojjh-a-ŋgassa bhāvanāya.|| ||

Taɱ kissa hetu?|| ||

Uddhakaɱ bhikkhave cittaɱ.|| ||

Taɱ etehi dhammehi suvūpasamaɱ hoti.|| ||

21. Satiɱ khvāhaɱ bhikkhave sabbatthikaɱ vadāmī ti.|| ||
