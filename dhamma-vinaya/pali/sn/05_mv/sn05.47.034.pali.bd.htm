Saɱyutta Nikāya,
V: Mahā-Vagga
47. Satipaṭṭhana Saɱyutta
4. Anussuta-Vagga

Sutta 34

Bhāvanā Suttaɱ

Adapted from the 1995 edition of the digital version of the Sri Lanka Buddha Jayanti Tripitaka Series.

 

 

[1][pts][bodh] Evam me sutaɱ:|| ||

Ekaɱ samayam Bhagavā Sāvatthiyaɱ viharati Jetavane Anāthapindikassa ārāme.|| ||

Tatra kho Bhagavā bhikkhū āmantesi:|| ||

"Bhikkhavo" ti.|| ||

"Bhadante" ti te bhikkhū Bhagavato paccassosuɱ.|| ||

Bhagavā etad avoca:|| ||

"Cattāro me bhikkhave, sati-paṭṭhānā bhāvitā||
bahulī-katā||
apārā pāraɱ gamanāya saŋvattanti.|| ||

Katame cattāro?|| ||

Idha bhikkhave, bhikkhu kāye kāy'ānupassī viharati||
ātāpī||
sampajāno||
satimā||
vineyya loke abhijjhā-domanassaɱ.|| ||

Vedanāsu vedan'ānupassīviharati||
ātāpī||
sampajāno||
satimā||
vineyya loke abhijjhā-domanassaɱ.|| ||

Citte citt'ānupassīviharati||
ātāpī||
sampajāno||
satimā||
vineyya loke abhijjhā-domanassaɱ.|| ||

Dhammesu Dhamm'ānupassīviharati||
ātāpī||
sampajāno||
satimā||
vineyya loke abhijjhā-domanassaɱ.|| ||

"Cattāro me bhikkhave, sati-paṭṭhānā bhāvitā||
bahulī-katā||
apārā pāraɱ gamanāya saŋvattantī" ti.|| ||
