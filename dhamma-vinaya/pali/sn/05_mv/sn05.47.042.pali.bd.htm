Saɱyutta Nikāya,
V: Mahā-Vagga
47. Satipaṭṭhana Saɱyutta
5. Amata-Vagga

Sutta 42

Samudaya Suttaɱ

Adapted from the 1995 edition of the digital version of the Sri Lanka Buddha Jayanti Tripitaka Series.

 

 

[1][pts][bodh][than][olds] Evam me sutaɱ:|| ||

2. Ekaɱ samayaɱ Bhagavā Sāvatthiyaɱ viharati Jetavane Anāthapiṇḍikassa ārāme.|| ||

Tatra kho Bhagavā bhikkhū āmantesi:|| ||

"Bhikkhavo" ti.|| ||

"Bhadante" ti te bhikkhū Bhagavato paccassosuɱ.|| ||

Bhagavā etad avoca:|| ||

"Catunnaɱ bhikkhave, sati-paṭṭhānānaɱ samudayañ ca atthagamañ ca desissāmi.|| ||

Taɱ suṇātha.|| ||

Ko ca bhikkhave, kāyassa samudayo?|| ||

Āhāra-samudayā kāyassa samudayo.|| ||

Āhāra-nirodhā kāyassa atthagamo.|| ||

Ko ca bhikkhave, vedanānaɱ samudayo?|| ||

Phassa-samudayā vedanānaɱ samudayo.|| ||

Phassa-nirodhā vedanānaɱ atthagamo.|| ||

Ko ca bhikkhave, cittassa samudayo?|| ||

Nāma-rūpa-samudayā cittassa samudayo.|| ||

Nāma-rūpa-nirodhā cittassa atthagamo.|| ||

Ko ca bhikkhave, dhammānaɱ samudayo?|| ||

Manasikāra-samudayā dhammānaɱ samudayo.|| ||

Manasikāra-nirodhā dhammānaɱ atthagamo" ti.|| ||
