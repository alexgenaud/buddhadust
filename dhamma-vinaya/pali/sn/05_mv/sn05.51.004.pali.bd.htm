Saɱyutta Nikāya,
V: Mahā-Vagga
51. Iddhipāda Saɱyutta

Sutta 4

Nibbidā Suttaɱ

Adapted from the 1995 edition of the digital version of the Sri Lanka Buddha Jayanti Tripitaka Series.

 

 

[1][pts][olds] Evam me sutaɱ:|| ||

Evam me sutaɱ ekaɱ samayam Bhagavā Sāvatthiyaɱ viharati Jetavane Anāthapindikassa ārāme.|| ||

2. Cattāro'me bhikkhave, iddhi-pādā bhāvitā bahulī-katā ekanta-nibbidāya virāgāya nirodhāya upasamāya abhiññāya sambodhāya nibbānāya saŋvattanti.|| ||

Katame cattāro?|| ||

3. Idha, bhikkhave, bhikkhu chanda-samādhi-padhāna-sankhāra-samannāgataɱ iddhi-pādaɱ bhāveti,|| ||

viriya-samādhi-padhāna-sankhāra-samannāgataɱ iddhi-pādaɱ bhāveti,|| ||

citta-samādhi-padhāna-sankhāra-samannāgataɱ iddhi-pādaɱ bhāveti,|| ||

vīmaɱsā-samādhi-padhāna-sankhāra-samannāgataɱ iddhi-pādaɱ bhāveti.|| ||

4. Ime kho bhikkhave cattāro iddhi-pādā bhāvitā bahulī-katā ekanta-nibbidāya virāgāya nirodhāya upasamāya abhiññāya sambodhāya nibbānāya saŋvattantī ti.|| ||
