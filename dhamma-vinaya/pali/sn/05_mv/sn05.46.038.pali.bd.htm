Saɱyutta Nikāya,
V: Mahā-Vagga
46. Bojjhanga Saɱyutta
IV. Nīvaraṇa-Vaggo

Sutta 38

Āvaraṇa-Nīvaraṇa (or Nīvaraṇāvaraṇa) Suttaɱ

Adapted from the 1995 edition of the digital version of the Sri Lanka Buddha Jayanti Tripitaka Series.

 

 

[1][pts][bodh] Evam me sutaɱ:|| ||

Ekaɱ samayam Bhagavā bhikkhū āmantesi:|| ||

"Bhikkhavo" ti.|| ||

"Bhadante" ti te bhikkhū Bhagavato paccassosuɱ.|| ||

Bhagavā etad avoca:|| ||

"Pañc'ime bhikkhave, āvaraṇā nīvaraṇā cetaso upakkilesā paññāya dubbalī-karaṇā.|| ||

Katame pañca?|| ||

Kāma-c-chando bhikkhave, āvaraṇo nīvaraṇo cetaso upakkileso paññāya dubbalī-karaṇo.|| ||

Vyāpādo bhikkhave, āvaraṇo nīvaraṇo cetaso upakkileso paññāya dubbalī-karaṇo.|| ||

Thīna-middhaɱ bhikkhave, āvaraṇaɱ nīvaraṇaɱ cetaso upakkilesaɱ paññāya dubbalī-karaṇaɱ.|| ||

Uddhacca-kukkuccaɱ [95] bhikkhave, āvaraṇaɱ nīvaraṇaɱ cetaso upakkilesaɱ paññāya dubbalī-karaṇaɱ.|| ||

Vicikicchā bhikkhave, āvaraṇā nīvaraṇā cetaso upakkilesā paññāya dubbalī-karaṇā.|| ||

Ime kho bhikkhave, pañca āvaraṇā nīvaraṇā cetaso upakkilesā paññāya dubbalī-karaṇā.

Satt'ime bhikkhave bojjhaŋgā anāvaraṇā anīvaraṇā cetaso anupakkilesā bhavitā bahulī-katā vijjā-vimutti-phala-sacchi-kiriyāya saɱvittanti.|| ||

Katame satta?|| ||

Sati-sambojjhaŋgo bhikkhave anāvaraṇo anīvaraṇo cetaso anupakkileso bhavito bahulī-kato vijjā-vimutti-phala-sacchi-kiriyāya saɱvittanti.|| ||

Dhamma-vicaya-sambojjhaŋgo bhikkhave anāvaraṇo anīvaraṇo cetaso anupakkileso bhavito bahulī-kato vijjā-vimutti-phala-sacchi-kiriyāya saɱvittanti.|| ||

 

Viriya-sambojjhaŋgo bhikkhave anāvaraṇo anīvaraṇo cetaso anupakkileso bhavito bahulī-kato vijjā-vimutti-phala-sacchi-kiriyāya saɱvittanti.|| ||

Pīti-sambojjhaŋgo bhikkhave anāvaraṇo anīvaraṇo cetaso anupakkileso bhavito bahulī-kato vijjā-vimutti-phala-sacchi-kiriyāya saɱvittanti.|| ||

Passaddhi-sambojjhaŋgo bhikkhave anāvaraṇo anīvaraṇo cetaso anupakkileso bhavito bahulī-kato vijjā-vimutti-phala-sacchi-kiriyāya saɱvittanti.|| ||

Samādhi-sambojjhaŋgo bhikkhave anāvaraṇo anīvaraṇo cetaso anupakkileso bhavito bahulī-kato vijjā-vimutti-phala-sacchi-kiriyāya saɱvittanti.|| ||

Upekkhā-sambojjhaŋgo bhikkhave anāvaraṇo anīvaraṇo cetaso anupakkileso bhavito bahulī-kato vijjā-vimutti-phala-sacchi-kiriyāya saɱvittanti.|| ||

Ime kho bhikkhave satta bojjhaŋgā anāvaraṇā anīvaraṇā cetaso anupakkilesā bhavitā bahulī-katā vijjā-vimutti-phala-sacchi-kiriyāya saɱvittanti.|| ||

 

[2][pts][bodh] Yasmiɱ bhikkhave, samaye ariya-sāvako aṭṭhiɱ katvā manasi-katvā sabba-cetaso samannā-haritvā ohita-soto dhammaɱ suṇāti.|| ||

Imassa pañca nīvaraṇā tasmiɱ samaye na honti.|| ||

Satta bojjhaŋgā tasmiɱ samaye bhāvanā pāripūriɱ gacchanti.|| ||

Katame pañca nīvaraṇā tasmiɱ samaye na honti?|| ||

Kāma-c-chanda-nīvaraṇaɱ tasmiɱ samaye na hoti.|| ||

Vyāpāda-nīvaraṇaɱ tasmiɱ samaye na hoti.|| ||

Thīna-middha-nīvaraṇaɱ tasmiɱ samaye na hoti.|| ||

Uddhacca-kukkucca-nīvaraṇaɱ tasmiɱ samaye na hoti.|| ||

Vicikicchā-nīvaraṇaɱ tasmiɱ samaye na hoti.|| ||

Imassa pañca-nīvaraṇā tasmiɱ samaye na honti.|| ||

■

Katame satta bojjhaŋgā tasmiɱ samaye bhāvanā pāripūriɱ gacchanti?|| ||

Sati-sambojjhaŋgo tasmiɱ samaye bhāvanā pāripūriɱ gacchati.|| ||

Dhamma-vicaya-sambojjhaŋgo tasmiɱ samaye bhāvanā pāripūriɱ gacchati.|| ||

Viriya-sambojjhaŋgo tasmiɱ samaye bhāvanā pāripūriɱ gacchati.|| ||

Pīti-sambojjhaŋgo tasmiɱ samaye bhāvanā pāripūriɱ gacchati.|| ||

Passaddhi-sambojjhaŋgo tasmiɱ samaye bhāvanā pāripūriɱ gacchati.|| ||

Samādhi-sambojjhaŋgo tasmiɱ samaye bhāvanā pāripūriɱ gacchati.|| ||

Upekhā-sambojjhaŋgo tasmiɱ samaye bhāvanā pāripūriɱ gacchati.|| ||

[96] Yasmiɱ bhikkhave, samaye ariya-sāvako aṭṭhiɱ katvā manasi katvā sabba-cetaso samannā-haritvā ohita-soto dhammaɱ suṇāti.|| ||

Imassa pañca nīvaraṇā tasmiɱ samaye na honti.|| ||

Ime satta bojjhaŋgā tasmiɱ samaye bhāvan āpāripūriɱ gacchantī" ti.|| ||
