3. "Idaɱ bhante, Kapilavatthu iddhañ c'eva phītañ ca bāhu-jaññaɱ ākiṇṇa-manussaɱ sambādha-vyuhaɱ.|| ||

bhantena pi sakaṭena samāgacchāmi,||
bhantena pi purisena samāgacchāmi.|| ||

Tassa mayhaɱ bhante, tasmiɱ samaye||
mussat'eva Bhagavantaɱ ārabbha sati||
mussati dhammaɱ ārabbha sati||
mussati Saŋghaɱ ārabbha sati.|| ||

Tassa mayhaɱ bhante, evaɱ hoti:|| ||

'Imehi c'āhaɱ samaye kālaɱ kareyyaɱ kā mayhaɱ gati,||
ko abhisamparāyo'" ti?|| ||
