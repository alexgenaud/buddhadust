Saɱyutta Nikāya,
V: Mahā-Vagga
46. Bojjhanga Saɱyutta
IV. Nīvaraṇa-Vaggo

Sutta 42

Cakka-vatti Suttaɱ

Adapted from the 1995 edition of the digital version of the Sri Lanka Buddha Jayanti Tripitaka Series.

 

 

[1][pts][bodh] Evam me sutaɱ:|| ||

Ekaɱ samayam Bhagavā Sāvatthiyaɱ viharati Jetavane Anāthapindikassa ārāme.|| ||

Tatra kho Bhagavā bhikkhū āmantesi:|| ||

"Bhikkhavo" ti.|| ||

"Bhadante" ti te bhikkhū Bhagavato paccassosuɱ.|| ||

Bhagavā etad avoca:|| ||

"Rañño bhikkhave, cakka-vattissa pātu-bhāvā sattannaɱ ratanānaɱ pātu-bhāvo hoti.|| ||

Katamesaŋ sattannaɱ?|| ||

Cakka-ratanassa pātu-bhāvo hoti,||
hatthi-ratanassa pātu-bhāvo hoti,||
assa-ratanassa pātu-bhāvo hoti,||
maṇi-ratanassa pātu-bhāvo hoti,||
itthi-ratanassa pātu-bhāvo hoti,||
gahapati-ratanassa pātu-bhāvo hoti,||
parināyaka-ratanassa pātu-bhāvo hoti.|| ||

Rañño bhikkhave, cakka-vattissa pātu-bhāvā imesaŋ sattannaɱ ratanānaɱ pātu-bhāvo hoti.|| ||

Tathāgatassa bhikkhave, pātu-bhāvā arahato Sammā-Sam-Buddhassa sattannaɱ bojjh-a-ŋga-ratanānaɱ pātu-bhāvo hoti.|| ||

Katamesaŋ sattannaɱ?|| ||

Sati-sambojjh-a-ŋga-ratanassa pātu-bhāvo hoti||
dhamma-vicaya-sambojjh-a-ŋga-ratanassa pātu-bhāvo hoti||
viriya-sambojjh-a-ŋga-ratanassa pātu-bhāvo hoti||
pīti-sambojjh-a-ŋga-ratanassa pātu-bhāvo hoti||
passaddhi-sambojjh-a-ŋga-ratanassa pātu-bhāvo hoti||
samādhi-sambojjh-a-ŋga-ratanassa pātu-bhāvo hoti||
upekhā-sambojjh-a-ŋga-ratanassa pātu-bhāvo hoti.|| ||

Tathāgatassa bhikkhave, pātu-bhāvā arahato Sammā-Sam-Buddhassa imesaŋ sattannaɱ bojjh-a-ŋga-ratanānaɱ pātu-bhāvo hotī" ti.|| ||
