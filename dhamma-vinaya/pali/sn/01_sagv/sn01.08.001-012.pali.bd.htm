Saɱyutta Nikāya
I. Sagātha Vagga
8. Vaŋgīsa-Thera-saŋyutta

Suttas 1-12

Adapted from the 1995 edition of the digital version of the Sri Lanka Buddha Jayanti Tripitaka Series.

Sutta 1

Nikkhanta Suttaɱ

[1.1][pts] Evaɱ me sutaɱ.|| ||

Ekaɱ samayaɱ āyasmā Vaŋgīso Āḷaviyaɱ viharati Aggāḷave cetiye āyasmatā Nigrodha-Kappena upajjhāyena saddhiɱ.|| ||

2. Tena kho pana samayen'āyasmā Vaŋgīso navako hoti acira-pabba-jito ohiyyako vihārapālo.|| ||

3. Atha kho sambahulā itthiyo samalaŋkaritvā yen'Aggāḷavako ārāmo ten'upasankamiɱsu vihārapekkhikāyo.|| ||

4. Atha kho āyasmato Vaŋgīsassa tā itthiyo disvā anabhirati uppajjati,||
rāgo cittaɱ anuddhaɱseti.|| ||

5. Atha kho āyasmato Vaŋgīsassa etad ahosi:|| ||

"Alābhā vata me,||
na vata me lābhā.|| ||

Dulladdhaɱ vata me,||
na vata me su-laddhaɱ.|| ||

Yassa me anabhirati uppannā,||
rāgo cittaɱ anuddhaɱseti.|| ||

Taɱ kut'ettha labbhā yam me paro anabhiratiɱ vinodatvā abhiratiɱ uppādeyya.|| ||

Yaɱ nūnāhaɱ attanā va attano anabhiratiɱ vinodetvā abhiratiɱ uppādeyyan" ti.|| ||

6. Atha kho āyasmā Vaŋgīso attanā va attano anabhiratiɱ vinodetvā abhiratiɱ uppādetvā tāyaɱ yelāyaɱ imā gāthāyo abhāsi.|| ||

"Nikkhantaɱ vata maɱ santaɱ agārasmā nagāriyaɱ,||
Vitakkā upadhāvanti pagabbhā kaṇhato ime.||
Uggaputtā mahissāsā sikkhitā daḷha-dhammino,||
Samantā parikireyyuɱ sahassaɱ apalāyinaɱ.||
Sace pi ettato bhiyyo āgamissanti itthiyo,||
N'eva maɱ vyādhayissanti dhamme s'amhi patiṭṭhito.||
[186]Sakkhī hi me sutaɱ etaɱ Buddhass'ādiccabandhuno,||
Nibbāṇagamanaɱ Maggaɱ tattha me nirato mano.||
Evañ ce maɱ viharantaɱ pāpimaɱ upagacchasi,||
Tathā maccu karissāmi na me Maggam pi dakkhisī" ti.|| ||

Sutta 2

Arati Suttaɱ

[2.1][pts] Evaɱ me sutaɱ.|| ||

2. Ekaɱ samayaɱ āyasmā Vaŋgīso Āḷaviyaɱ viharati Aggāḷave cetiye āyasmatā Nigrodha-Kappena upajjhāyena saddhiɱ.|| ||

3. Tena kho pana samayen'āyasmā Nigrodha-Kappo pacchā-bhattaɱ piṇḍa-pāta-paṭikkanto vihāraɱ pavisati.|| ||

Sāyaɱ vā ni-k-khamati aparajju vā kāle.|| ||

4. Tena kho pana samayena āyasmato Vaŋgīsassa anabhirati uppannā hoti,||
rāgo cittaɱ anuddhaɱseti.|| ||

5. Atha kho āyasmato Vaŋgīsassa etad ahosi:|| ||

"Alābhā vata me,||
na vata me lābhā.|| ||

Dulladdhaɱ vata me,||
na vata me su-laddhaɱ.|| ||

Yassa me anabharati uppannā,||
rāgo cittaɱ anuddhaɱseti.|| ||

Taɱ kut'ettha labbhā yaɱ me paro anabhiratiɱ vinodetvā abhiratiɱ uppādeyyan.|| ||

Yaɱ nūnāhaɱ attanā va attano anabhiratiɱ vinodetvā abhiratiɱ uppādeyyan" ti.|| ||

6. Atha kho āyasmā Vaŋgīso attanā va attano anabhiratiɱ vinodetvā abhiratiɱ uppādetvā tāyaɱ velāyaɱ imā gāthāyo abhāsi:|| ||

"Aratiñ ca ratiñ ca pahāya||
sabbaso gehasitañ ca vitakkaɱ,||
Vanathaɱ na kareyya kuhiñci||
nibbanatho anato sa hi bhikkhu.||
Yam idha puthuviñ ca vehāsaɱ||
rūpa-gatañ ca jagatogadhaɱ,||
Kiñci parijīyati sabbam aniccaɱ||
evaɱ samecca caranti mutattā.||
Upadhīsu janā gadhitā||
diṭṭhasute paṭighe ca mute ca,||
Ettha vinodaya chandam anejo||
yo tattha na limpati taɱ munim āhu.||
[187]Atha saṭṭhitasitā vitakkā||
puthujanatāya adhammā niviṭṭhā,||
Na ca vaggagat'assa kuhiñci||
no pana duṭṭhullabhāṇī sa bhikkhu.||
Dabbo cirarattasamāhito||
akuhako nipako apihālu,||
Santapadaɱ ajjhagamā muni||
paṭicca parinibbuto kankhati kālan" ti.|| ||

Sutta 3

Pesalā-Atimaññanā Suttaɱ

[3.1][pts] Evaɱ me sutaɱ.|| ||

Ekaɱ samayaɱ āyasmā Vaŋgīso Āḷaviyaɱ viharati Aggāḷave cetiye āyasmatā Nigrodha-Kappena upajjhāyena saddhiɱ.|| ||

2. Tena kho pana samayen'āyasmā Vaŋgīso attano paṭibhāṇena aññe pesale bhikkhū atimaññati.|| ||

3. Atha kho āyasmato Vaŋgīsassa etad ahosi:|| ||

"Alābhā vata me,||
na vata me lābhā.|| ||

Dulladdhaɱ vata me,||
na vata me su-laddhaɱ.|| ||

Yvāhaɱ attano paṭibhāṇena aññe pesale bhikkhū atimaññāmī" ti.|| ||

4. Atha kho āyasmā Vaŋgīso attanā va attano vippaṭisāraɱ uppādetvā tāyaɱ velāyaɱ imā gāthāyo abhāsi:|| ||

"Mānaɱ pajahassu Gotama,||
mānapathañ ca jahassu asesaɱ||
mānapathasmiɱ samucchito||
vippaṭisār'ahuvā cirarattaɱ.||
Makkhena makkhitā pajā||
mānahatā Nirayaɱ papatanti,||
Socanti janā cirarattaɱ||
mānagatā Nirayaɱ upapannā||
Na hi socati bhikkhu kadāci||
Maggajino sammāpaṭipanno,||
Kittiñ ca sukhañ c'anubhoti||
[188]dhammaarato ti tam āhu tathattaɱ.||
Tasmā akhilo'dha padhānavā||
nīvaraṇāni pahāya visuddho,||
Mānañ ca pahāya asesaɱ||
vijjāy'antakaro samitāvī" ti.|| ||

Sutta 4

Ānanda Suttaɱ

[4.1][pts][than] Evaɱ me sutaɱ.|| ||

Ekaɱ samayaɱ āyasmā Ānando Sāvatthīyaɱ viharati Jetavane Anāthapiṇḍikassa ārāme.|| ||

2. Atha kho āyasmā Ānando pubbaṇha-samayaɱ nivāsetvā patta-cīvaram ādāya Sāvatthīɱ piṇḍāya pāvisi āyasmatā Vaŋgīsena pacchā-samaṇena.|| ||

3. Tena kho pana samayena āyasmato Vaŋgīsassa anabhirati uppannā hoti,||
rāgo cittaɱ anuddhaɱseti.|| ||

4. Atha kho āyasmā Vaŋgīso āyasmantaɱ Ānandaɱ gāthāya ajjhabhāsi:|| ||

"Kāma-rāgena ḍayhāmi cittaɱ me pariḍayhati,||
Sādhu nibbāpanaɱ brūhi anukampāya Gotamā" ti.|| ||

[Ānando:]|| ||

"Saññāya vipariyesā cittaɱ te pariḍayhati,||
Nimittaɱ parivajjehi subhaɱ rāgūpasaɱhitaɱ.||
Sankhāre parato passa dukkhato mā ca attato,||
Nibbāpehi mahārāgaɱ mā ḍayhittho puna-p-punaɱ.||
Asubhāya cittaɱ bhāvehi ek'aggaɱ susamāhitaɱ,||
Sati kāyagatā ty atthu nibbidābahulo bhava.||
Animittaɱ ca bhāvehi mān-ā-nusayam ujjaha,||
Tato mānābhi-samayā upasanto carissasī" ti.|| ||

Sutta 5

Subhāsita Suttaɱ

[5.1][pts] Evaɱ me sutaɱ.|| ||

Ekaɱ samayaɱ Bhagavā Sāvatthīyaɱ viharati Jetavane Anāthapiṇḍikassa ārāme.|| ||

2. Tatra kho Bhagavā bhikkhū āmantesi:|| ||

"Bhikkhavo" ti!|| ||

3. "Bhadante" ti te bhikkhū Bhagavato paccassosuɱ.|| ||

4. Bhagavā etad avoca:|| ||

"Catuhi bhikkhave aŋgehi samannāgatā vācā subhā-sitā hoti na dubbhā-sitā anavajjā ca,||
ananuvajjā ca viññūnaɱ.|| ||

Katamehi catūhi?|| ||

5. Idha, bhikkhave, bhikkhū su-bhāsitaɱ yeva bhāsati no dubbhāsitaɱ.|| ||

Dhammaɱ yeva bhāsati no adhammaɱ.|| ||

[189] Piyaɱ yeva bhāsati no appiyaɱ.|| ||

Saccaɱ yeva bhāsati no alikaɱ.|| ||

Imehi kho bhikkhave catūhi aŋgehi samannāgatā vācā subhā-sitā hoti no dubbhā-sitā anavajjā ca ananuvajjā ca viññūnan" ti.|| ||

6. Idam avoca Bhagavā.|| ||

Idaɱ vatvāna Sugato athāparaɱ etad avoca Satthā:|| ||

"Subhāsitaɱ uttamam āhu santo||
dhammaɱ bhaṇe nādhammaɱ taɱ dutiyaɱ,||
Piyaɱ bhaṇe nāppiyaɱ taɱ tatiyaɱ||
sacchaɱ bhaṇe nālikaɱ taɱ catutthan" ti.|| ||

7. Atha kho āyasmā Vaŋgīso uṭṭhāy'āsanā ekaɱsaɱ uttarā-saŋgaɱ karitvā yena Bhagavā ten'añjaliɱ paṇāmetvā Bhagavantaɱ etad avoca:|| ||

"Paṭibhāti maɱ Bhagavā paṭibhāti maɱ Sugatā" ti.|| ||

8. "Paṭibhātu taɱ Vaŋgīsā" ti Bhagavā avoca.|| ||

9. Atha kho āyasmā Vaŋgīso Bhagavantaɱ sammukhā sarūpāhi gāthāhi abhitthavi:|| ||

"Tam eva vācaɱ bhāseyya yāy'attānaɱ na tāpaye,||
Pare ca na vihiɱseyya sā ve vācā subhā-sitā||
Piyavācaɱ va bhāseyya yā vācā paṭinanditā,||
Yaɱ anādāya pāpāni paresaɱ bhāsate piyaɱ.||
Saccaɱ ve amatā vācā esa dhammo sanantano,||
Sacce atthe ca dhamme ca āhu santo patiṭṭhitā.||
Yaɱ Buddho bhāsate vācaɱ khemaɱ nibbāṇapattiyā,||
Dukkhass'antakiriyāya sā ve vācānam uttamā" ti.|| ||

Sutta 6

Sāriputta Suttaɱ

[6.1][pts] Evaɱ me sutaɱ.|| ||

Ekaɱ samayaɱ āyasmā Sāriputto Sāvatthīyaɱ viharati Jetavane Anāthapiṇḍikassa ārāme.|| ||

2. Tena kho pana samayen'āyasmā Sāriputto bhikkhū dhammiyā kathāya sandasseti sam-ā-dapeti samuttejeti sampahaɱseti poriyā vācāya vissaṭṭhāya aneḷa-gaḷāya atthassa viññāpaniyā.|| ||

Te ca bhikkhū aṭṭhikatvā manasi-katvā sabba-cetaso samannā-haritvā ohita-sotā dhammaɱ suṇanti.|| ||

3. Atha kho ayasmato Vaŋgīsassa etad ahosi:|| ||

"Ayaɱ [190] kho āyasmā Sāriputto bhikkhū dhammiyā kathāya sandasseti sam-ā-dapeti samuttejeti sampahaɱseti poriyā vācāya visaṭṭhāya aneḷa-gaḷāya atthassa viññāpaniyā.|| ||

Te ca bhikkhū aṭṭhikatvā manasi-katvā sabba-cetaso samannā-haritvā ohita-sotā dhammaɱ suṇanti.|| ||

Yaɱ nūnāhaɱ āyasmantaɱ Sāriputtaɱ sammukhā sarūpāhi gāthāhi abhitthaveyyan" ti.|| ||

4. Atha kho āyasmā Vaŋgīso uṭṭhāy'āsanā ekaɱsaɱ uttarā-saŋgaɱ karitvā yen'āyasmā Sāriputto ten'añjaliɱ paṇāmetvā āyasmantaɱ Sāriputtaɱ etad avoca:|| ||

"Paṭibhāti maɱ āvuso Sāriputta,||
paṭibhāti maɱ āvuso Sāriputtā" ti.|| ||

5. "Paṭibhātu taɱ āvuso Vaŋgīsā" ti.|| ||

6. Atha kho āyasmā Vaŋgīso āyasmantaɱ Sāriputtaɱ sammukhā sarūpāhi gāthāhi abhitthavi:|| ||

"Gambhirapañño medhāvī Maggā-Maggassa kovido,||
Sāriputto mahā-pañño dhammaɱ deseti bhikkhunaɱ.||
Saŋkhittena pi deseti vitthārena pi bhāsati,||
Sālikāy'iva nigghoso paṭibhānaɱ udīrayi.||
Tassa taɱ desayantassa suṇanti madhuraɱ giraɱ,||
Sarena rajanīyena savaṇīyena vaggunā,||
Udaggacittā muditā sotaɱ odhenti Bhikkhavo" ti.|| ||

Sutta 7

Pavāraṇa Suttaɱ

[7.1][pts] Evaɱ me sutaɱ.|| ||

Ekaɱ samayaɱ Bhagavā Sāvatthīyaɱ viharati Pubbārāme Migāra-mātu pāsāde mahatā bhikkhu-saŋghena saddhiɱ pañca-mattehi bhikkhu-satehi sabbeh'eva arahantehi.|| ||

2. Tena kho pana samayena Bhagavā tadah'uposathe paṇṇarase pavāraṇāya bhikkhu-Saŋgharivuto ajjhokāse nisinno hoti.|| ||

3. Atha kho Bhagavā tuṇhī-bhūtaɱ bhikkhu-Saŋghaɱ anuviloketvā bhikkhū āmantesi:|| ||

4. "Handa dāni bhikkhave, pavārayāmi vo na ca me kiñci garahatha kāyikaɱ vā vācasikaɱ vā" ti?|| ||

5. Evaɱ vutte āyasmā Sāriputto uṭṭhāy'āsanā ekaɱsaɱ uttarā-saŋgaɱ karitvā yena Bhagavā ten'añjaliɱ paṇāmetvā Bhagavantaɱ etad avoca:|| ||

"Na kho mayaɱ bhante,||
Bhagavato kiñci garahāma kāyikaɱ vā vācasikaɱ vā.|| ||

Bhagavā [191] hi bhante,||
anuppannassa Maggassa uppādetā||
asañjātassa Maggassa sañjanetā,||
anakkhātassa Maggassa akkhātā||
Maggaññū||
Maggavidū||
Maggakovido||
maggānugā ca bhante,||
etarahi sāvakā viharanti pacchā samannāgatā.|| ||

Ahaɱ ca kho bhante,||
Bhagavantaɱ pavāremi||
na ca me Bhagavā kiñci garahati kāyikaɱ vā vācasikaɱ vā" ti.|| ||

6. "Na khvāhaɱ te Sāriputta kiñci garahāmi kāyikaɱ vā vācasikaɱ vā.|| ||

Paṇḍito tvaɱ Sāriputta,||
mahā-pañño tvaɱ Sāriputta,||
puthupañño tvaɱ Sāriputta,||
hāsapañño tvaɱ Sāriputta,||
javanapañño tvaɱ Sāriputta,||
tikkhapañño tvaɱ Sāriputta,||
nibbedhika-pañño tvaɱ Sāriputta.|| ||

Seyyathā pi Sāriputta,||
rañño cakka-vattissa jeṭṭhaputto pitarā pavattitaɱ cakkaɱ samma-d-eva anuppavatteti.|| ||

Evam eva kho tvaɱ Sāriputta mayā anuttaraɱ Dhamma-cakkaɱ pavattitaɱ samma-d-eva anuppavattesī" ti.|| ||

7. "No ce kira me bhante Bhagavā kiñci garahati kāyikaɱ vā vācasikaɱ vā.|| ||

Imesam pana bhante Bhagavā pañcannaɱ bhikkhu-satānaɱ na kiñci garahati kāyikaɱ vā vācasikaɱ vā" ti.|| ||

8. "Imesam pi khvāhaɱ Sāriputta pañcannaɱ bhikkhu-satānaɱ na kiñci garahāmi kāyikaɱ vā vācasikaɱ vā.|| ||

Imesaɱ pi Sāriputta pañcannaɱ bhikkhu-satānaɱ saṭṭhi bhikkhū tevijjā,||
saṭṭhi bhikkhū chaḷabhiññā,||
saṭṭhi bhikkhū ubhato bhāga-vimuttā||
atha itare paññā-vimuttā" ti.|| ||

9. Atha kho āyasmā Vaŋgīso uṭṭhāy'āsanā ekaɱsaɱ uttarā-saŋgaɱ karitvā yena Bhagavā ten'añjaliɱ paṇāmetvā Bhagavantaɱ etad avoca:|| ||

10. "Paṭibhāti maɱ Bhagavā
paṭibhāti maɱ Sugatā" ti.|| ||

11. "Paṭibhātu taɱ Vaŋgīsā" ti Bhagavā avoca.|| ||

12. Atha kho āyasmā Vaŋgīso Bhagavantaɱ sammukhā sarūpāhi gāthāhi abhitthavi:|| ||

"Ajja paṇṇarase visuddhiyā||
bhikkhū-pañca-satā samāgatā,||
Saŋyojana-bandhanacchidā||
anīghā khīṇa-puna-b-bhavā isī.||
[192] Cakka-vattī yathā rājā amaccaparivārito,||
Samantā anupariyeti sāgarantaɱ mahiɱ imaɱ.||
Evaɱ vijita-saŋgāmaɱ satthavāhaɱ anuttaraɱ,||
Sāvakā payirupāsanti tevijjā maccubhāyino.||
Sabbe Bhagavato puttā palāp'ettha na vijjati,||
Taṇhāsallassa hantāraɱ vande ādiccabandhunan" ti.|| ||

Sutta 8

Parosahassa Suttaɱ

[8.1][pts] Evaɱ me sutaɱ.|| ||

Ekaɱ samayaɱ Bhagavā Sāvatthīyaɱ viharati Jetavane Anāthapiṇḍikassa ārāme mahatā bhikkhu-saŋghena saddhiɱ aḍḍhatelasehi bhikkhu-satehi.|| ||

2. Tena kho pana samayena Bhagavā bhikkhū nibbāṇa-paṭisaŋyuttāya dhammiyā kathāya sandasseti sam-ā-dapeti samuttejeti sampahaɱseti.|| ||

Te ca bhikkhū aṭṭhikatvā manasi-katvā sabba-cetaso samannā-haritvā ohita-sotā dhammaɱ suṇanti.|| ||

3. Atha kho āyasmato Vaŋgīsassa etad ahosi:

"Ayaɱ kho Bhagavā bhikkhū nibbāṇa-paṭisaŋyuttāya dhammiyā kathāya sandasseti sam-ā-dapeti samuttejeti sampahaɱseti.|| ||

Te ca bhikkhū aṭṭhikatvā manasi-katvā sabba-cetaso samannā-haritvā ohita-sotā dhammaɱ suṇanti.|| ||

Yaɱ nūnāhaɱ Bhagavantaɱ sammukhā sarūpāhi gāthāhi abhitthaveyyan" ti.|| ||

4. Atha kho āyasmā Vaŋgīso uṭṭhāy'āsanā ekaɱsaɱ uttarā-saŋgaɱ karitvā yena Bhagavā ten'añjalim paṇāmetvā Bhagavantaɱ etad avoca:|| ||

"Paṭibhāti maɱ Bhagavā,||
paṭibhāti maɱ Sugatā" ti.|| ||

5. "Paṭibhātu taɱ Vaŋgīsā" ti Bhagavā avoca.|| ||

6. Atha kho āyasmā Vaŋgīso Bhagavantaɱ sammukhā sarūpāhi gāthāhi abhitthavi:|| ||

"Parosahassaɱ bhikkhūnaɱ Sugataɱ payirupāsati,||
Desentaɱ virajaɱ dhammaɱ nibbāṇaɱ akutobhayaɱ.||
Suṇanti dhammaɱ vimalaɱ Sammā-Sambuddhadesitaɱ,||
Sobhati vata sambuddho bhikkhu-Saŋghapurakkhato.||
Nāganāmo'si Bhagavā isīnaɱ isisattamo,||
Mahāmegho va hutvāna sāvake abhivassati,||
[193] Divāvihārā ni-k-khamma Satthudassanakamyatā,||
Sāvako te mahāvīra pāde vandati Vaŋgīso" ti.|| ||

7. "Kin nu te Vaŋgīsa imā gāthāyo pubbe parivitakkitā udāhu ṭhānaso va taɱ paṭibhantī" ti?|| ||

8. "Na kho me bhante imā gāthāyo pubbe parivitakkitā atha kho ṭhānaso va maɱ paṭibhantī" ti.|| ||

9. "Tena hi taɱ Vaŋgīsa bhīyosomattāya pubbe aparivitakkitā gāthāyo paṭibhantū" ti.|| ||

10. "Evaɱ bhante" ti kho āyasmā Vaŋgīso Bhagavato paṭissutvā bhīyosomattāya Bhagavantaɱ pubbe aparivitakkāhi gāthāhi abhitthavi:|| ||

"Ummaggapathaɱ Mārassa Abhibhuyya||
carasi pabhijja khilāni,||
Taɱ passatha bandhapamuñcakaraɱ||
asitaɱ bhāgaso pavibhajjaɱ.||
Oghassa hi nittharaṇ'-atthaɱ||
aneka-vihitaɱ Maggaɱ akkhāsi,||
Tasmiɱ te amate akkhāte||
dhammaddasā ṭhitā asaɱhīrā.||
Pajjotakaro ativijjha||
sabbaṭṭhitīnaɱ ati-k-kamam addasa,||
ñatvā ca sacchi-katvā ca||
aggaɱ so desayi dasaṭṭhānaɱ||
Evaɱ sudesite dhamme||
ko pamādo vijānataɱ dhammaɱ,||
Tasmā hi tassa Bhagavato sāsane||
appamatto sadā namassam anusikkhe" ti.|| ||

Sutta 9

Koṇḍañña Suttaɱ

[9.1][pts] Evaɱ me sutaɱ.|| ||

Ekaɱ samayaɱ Bhagavā Rājagahe viharati Veḷuvane kalaṇḍaka nivāpe.|| ||

2. Atha kho āyasmā Aññāsi-Koṇḍañño sucirass'eva yena Bhagavā ten'upasankami.|| ||

Upasaŋkamitvā Bhagavato pādesu sirasā nipatitvā Bhagavato pādāni mukhena ca paricumbati.|| ||

[194] Pāṇīhi ca parisambāhati.|| ||

Nāmañ ca sāveti:|| ||

"Koṇḍañño'haɱ Bhagavā||
Koṇḍañño'haɱ Sugatā" ti.|| ||

3. Atha kho āyasmato Vaŋgīsassa etad ahosi:|| ||

"Ayaɱ kho āyasmā Aññāsi-Koṇḍañño sucirass'eva yena Bhagavā ten'upasankami.|| ||

Upasaŋkamitvā Bhagavato pādesu sirasā nipatitvā Bhagavato pādāni mukhena ca paricumbati.|| ||

Pāṇīhi ca parisambāhati.|| ||

Nāmañ ca sāveti:|| ||

'Koṇḍañño'haɱ Bhagavā||
koṇḍañño'haɱ sugatā' ti.|| ||

Yaɱ nūnāhaɱ āyasmantaɱ Aññāsi-Koṇḍaññaɱ Bhagavato sammukhā sarūpāhi gāthāhi abhitthaveyyan" ti.|| ||

4. Atha kho āyasmā Vaŋgīso uṭṭhāy'āsanā ekaɱsaɱ uttarā-saŋgaɱ karitvā yena Bhagavā ten'añjaliɱ paṇāmetvā Bhagavantaɱ etad avoca:|| ||

"Paṭibhāti maɱ Bhagavā
paṭibhāti maɱ Sugatā" ti.|| ||

5. "Paṭibhātu taɱ Vaŋgīsā" ti Bhagavā āvoca.|| ||

6. Atha kho āyasmā Vaŋgīso āyasmantaɱ Aññāsi-Koṇḍaññaɱ Bhagavato sammukhā sarūpāhi gāthāhi abhitthavi:|| ||

"BuddhānuBuddho so thero Koṇḍaññā tibbanikkamo,||
Lābhī sukha-vihārānaɱ vivekānaɱ abhiṇhaso.||
Yaɱ sāvakena pattabbaɱ Satth-usāsanakārinā,||
Sabb'assa taɱ anuppattaɱ appamattassa sikkhato.||
Mahānubhāvo tevijjo ceto-pariyāyakovido,||
Koṇḍañño Buddhadāsāvako pāde vandati Satthuno" ti.|| ||

Sutta 10

Moggallāna Suttaɱ

[10.1][pts] Evaɱ me sutaɱ.|| ||

Ekaɱ samayaɱ Bhagavā Rājagahe viharati Isigilipasse Kāḷasilāyaɱ mahatā bhikkhu-saŋghena saddhiɱ pañca-mattehi bhikkhu-satehi sabbeh'eva arahantehi.|| ||

Tesaɱ sudaɱ āyasmā Mahā-Moggallāno cetasā cittaɱ samannesati vimuttaɱ nirupadhiɱ.|| ||

2. Atha kho āyasmato Vaŋgīsassa etad ahosi:|| ||

"Ayaɱ kho Bhagavā Rājagahe viharati Isigilipasse Kāḷasilāyaɱ mahatā bhikkhu-saŋghena saddhiɱ pañca-mattehi bhikkhu-satehi sabbeh'eva arahantehi.|| ||

Tesaɱ sudaɱ āyasmā Mahā-Moggallāno cetasā cittaɱ samannesati vimuttaɱ nirupadhiɱ.|| ||

Yaɱ nūnāhaɱ āyasmantaɱ Mahā-Moggallānaɱ Bhagavato sammukhā sarūpāhi gāthāhi abhitthaveyyan" ti.|| ||

[195] 3. Atha kho āyasmā Vaŋgīso uṭṭhāy'āsanā ekaɱsaɱ uttarā-saŋgaɱ karitvā yena Bhagavā ten'añjaliɱ paṇāmetvā Bhagavantaɱ etad avoca:|| ||

"Paṭibhāti maɱ Bhagavā
paṭibhāti maɱ Sugatā" ti.|| ||

4. "Paṭibhātu taɱ Vaŋgīsā" ti Bhagavā avoca.|| ||

5. Atha kho āyasmā Vaŋgīso āyasmantaɱ Mahā-Moggallānaɱ Bhagavato sammukhā sarūpāhi gāthāhi abhitthavi:|| ||

"Nagassa passe āsīnaɱ muniɱ dukkhassa pāraguɱ,||
Sāvakā payirupāsanti tevijjā maccuhāyino.||
Te cetasā anupariyeti Moggallāno mahiddhiko,||
Cittaɱ n'esaɱ samann'esaɱ vimuttaɱ nirūpadhiɱ.||
Evaɱ sabbaŋgasamnnaɱ muniɱ dukkhassa pāraguɱ,||
Anekākārasampannaɱ payirupāsanti Gotaman" ti.|| ||

Sutta 11

Gaggarā Suttaɱ

[11.1][pts] Evaɱ me sutaɱ.|| ||

Ekaɱ samayaɱ Bhagavā Campāyaɱ viharati Gaggarāya pokkharaṇiyā tīre mahatā bhikkhu-saŋghena saddhiɱ pañca-mattehi bhikkhu-satehi sattahi ca upāsaka-satehi sattahi ca upāsikāsatehi anekehi ca devatāsahassehi.|| ||

Tyāssudaɱ Bhagavā atirocati vaṇṇena c'eva yasasā ca.|| ||

2. Atha kho āyasmato Vaŋgīsassa etad ahosi:|| ||

"Ayaɱ kho Bhagavā Campāyaɱ viharati Gaggarāya pokkharaṇiyā tīre mahatā bhikkhu-saŋghena saddhiɱ pañca-mattehi bhikkhu-satehi sattahi ca upāsaka-satehi sattahi ca upāsikāsatehi anekehi devatāsahassehi.|| ||

Tyāssudaɱ Bhagavā atirocati vaṇṇena c'eva yasasā ca.|| ||

Yaɱ nūnāhaɱ Bhagavantaɱ sammukhā sarūpāya gāthāya abhitthaveyyan" ti.|| ||

3. Atha kho āyasmā Vaŋgīso uṭṭhāy āsanā ekaɱsaɱ uttarā-saŋgaɱ karitvā yena Bhagavā ten'añjaliɱ paṇāmetvā Bhagavantaɱ etad avoca:|| ||

paṭibhāti maɱ sugatā" ti.|| ||

4. "Paṭibhātu taɱ Vaŋgīsā" ti Bhagavā avoca.|| ||

5. Atha kho āyasmā Vaŋgīso Bhagavato sammukhā sarūpāya gāthāya abhitthavi:|| ||

[196] "Cando yathā vigata-valāhake nabhe||

Atirocasi yasasā sabba-lokan" ti.|| ||

Sutta 12

Vaŋgīsa Suttaɱ

[12.1][pts] Evaɱ me sutaɱ.|| ||

Ekaɱ samayaɱ Bhagavā Sāvatthīyaɱ viharati Jetavane Anāthapiṇḍikassa ārāme.|| ||

2. Tena kho pana samayen'āyasmā Vaŋgīso acira-Arahattappatto hoti vimutti-sukha-paṭisaŋvedī.|| ||

Tāyaɱ velāyaɱ imā gāthāyo abhāsi:|| ||

"Kāveyyamattā vicarimha pubbe gāmā gāmaɱ purā puraɱ,||
Ath'addasāma sambuddhaɱ saddhā no udapajjatha||
So me dhammam adesesi khandhe āyatāni dhātuyo ca,||
Tassāhaɱ dhamamaɱ sutvāna pabbajiɱ anagāriyaɱ.||

Bhikkhūnaɱ bhikkhunīnañ ca ye niyāmagataddasā.||
Sā-gataɱ vata me āsi mama Buddhassa santike,||
Tisso vijjā anuppattā kataɱ Buddhassa sāsanan ti.||
Pubbe-nivāsaɱ jānāmi dibba-cakkhuɱ visodhitaɱ||
Tevijjo iddhippatto'mhi ceto-pariyāya kovido" ti.|| ||
