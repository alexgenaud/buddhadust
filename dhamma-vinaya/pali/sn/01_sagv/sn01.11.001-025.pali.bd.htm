Sutta 1

Suvīra Suttaɱ

[1.1][rhyc][bodh] Evaɱ me sutaɱ:|| ||

Ekaɱ samayaɱ Bhagavā Sāvatthiyaɱ viharati Jetavane Anāthapiṇḍikassa ārāme.|| ||

Tatra kho Bhagavā bhikkhū āmantesi:|| ||

"Bhikkhavo" ti.|| ||

"Bhadante" ti te bhikkhū Bhagavato paccassosuɱ. Bhagavā etad avoca:|| ||

Bhuta-pubbaɱ bhikkhave Asurā deve abhiyaɱsu.|| ||

Atha kho bhikkhave Sakko devānaɱ Indo suvīraɱ deva-puttaɱ āmantesi.|| ||

"Ete tāta suvīra, Asurā deve abhiyan" ti.|| ||

Gaccha, tāta suvīra,||
Asure paccuyyāhīti.|| ||

Evaɱ bhaddaɱ tavāti kho bhikkhave suvīro deva-putto Sakkassa devānam indassa paṭissutvā pamādaɱ āpādesi.|| ||

Dutiyam pi kho bhikkhave Sakko devānaɱ Indo suvīraɱ deva-puttaɱ āmantesi:|| ||

Ete tāta suvīra, Asurā deve abhiyanti.|| ||

Gaccha tāta suvīra,||
Asure paccuyyāhīti.|| ||

Evaɱ bhaddaɱ tavāti kho bhikkhave suvīro deva-putto Sakkassa devānam indassa paṭissutvā dutiyam pi pamādaɱ āpādesi.|| ||

Tatiyam pi kho bhikkhave Sakko devānaɱ Indo suvīraɱ deva-puttaɱ āmantesi:|| ||

Ete tāta suvīra,||
Asurā deve abhiyanti.|| ||

Gaccha tāta suvīra,||
Asure paccuyyāhīti.|| ||

Evaɱ bhaddaɱ tavāti kho bhikkhave suvīro deva-putto Sakkassa devānam indassa paṭissutvā tatiyam pi pamādaɱ āpādesi.|| ||

[217] Atha kho bhikkhave Sakko devānaɱ Indo suvīraɱ deva-puttaɱ gāthāya ajjhabhāsi:|| ||

Anuṭṭhahaɱ avāyamaɱ sukhaɱ yatrādhigacchati,||
Suvīra tattha gacchāhi mañca tatth'eva pāpayāti.|| ||

(Suvīro:)|| ||

Alasassa anuṭṭhātā na ca kiccāni kāraye,||
Sabbakāmasamiddhassa tamme sakka varaɱ disāti|| ||

(Sakko:)|| ||

Yatthālaso anuṭṭhātā accantaɱ sukhamedhati,||
Suvīra tattha gacchāhi mañca tatth'eva pāpayāti.|| ||

(Suvīro:)|| ||

Akammunā devaseṭṭha sakka vindemu yaɱ sukhaɱ,||
Asokaɱ anupāyāsaɱ tamme sakka varaɱ disāti.|| ||

(Sakko:)|| ||

Sace atthi akammena koci kvaci na jīvati,||
Nibbāṇassa hi so Maggo suvīra tattha gacchāhi mañca tatth'eva pāpayāti.|| ||

So hi nāma bhikkhave,||
Sakko devānaɱ Indo sakaɱ puññaphalaɱ upajīvamāno devānaɱ Tāvatiɱsānaɱ issariyādhipaccaɱ rajjaɱ kārento uṭṭhānaviriyassa vaṇṇa-vādī bhavissati.|| ||

Idha kho taɱ bhikkhave sobhetha,||
yaɱ tumhe evaɱ svākkhāte Dhamma-Vinaye pabba-jitā samānā uṭṭhaheyyātha ghaṭeyyātha vāyameyyātha appattassa pattiyā anadigatassa adhigamāya asacchi-katassa sacchi-kiriyāyāti.|| ||

Sutta 3

Dhajagga Suttaɱ

[3.1][rhyc][bodh][than][piya] Evaɱ me sutaɱ:|| ||

Ekaɱ samayaɱ Bhagavā Sāvatthiyaɱ viharati Jetavane Anāthapiṇḍikassa ārāme.|| ||

Tatra kho Bhagavā bhikkhū āmantesi:|| ||

"Bhikkhavo"ti.|| ||

"Bhadante" ti te bhikkhū Bhagavato paccassosuɱ.|| ||

Bhūta-pubbaɱ bhikkhave dev-ā-sura-saŋgāmo samupabbūḷho ahosi.|| ||

lomahaɱso vā||

so pahiyyissati.|| ||

No ce me dhajaggaɱ ullokeyyātha,||
atha pajāpatissa devarājassa dhajaggaɱ ullokeyyātha.|| ||

chambhitattaɱ vā||
lomahaɱso vā,||
so pahiyyissati.|| ||

No ce pajāpatissa devarājassa dhajaggaɱ ullokeyyātha,||

Varuṇassa hi vo devarājassa dhajaggaɱ ullokayataɱ yaɱ bhavissati bhayaɱ vā||
chambhitattaɱ vā,||
lomahaɱso vā,||
so pahiyyissati.|| ||

Īsānassa hi vo devarājassa dhajaggaɱ ullokayataɱ yaɱ bhavissati bhayaɱ vā||
chambhitattaɱ vā||

yaɱ bhavissati bhayaɱ vā||
chambhitattaɱ vā||
lomahaɱso vā,||
so pahīyethāpi no pi pahīyetha.|| ||

Taɱ kissa hetu?|| ||

Sakko hi bhikkhave devānaɱ Indo avīta-rāgo avītadoso avītamoho bhīrucchamhī utrāsi palāyīti.|| ||

Ahaɱ ca kho bhikkhave evaɱ vadāmi:|| ||

Sace tumhākaɱ bhikkhave araññagatānaɱ vā||
rukkha-mūla-gatānaɱ vā||
suññ-ā-gāra-gatānaɱ vā||
uppajjeyya bhayaɱ vā||
chambhitattaɱ vā||
lomahaɱso vā,||
mameva tasmiɱ samaye anussareyyātha."|| ||

Iti pi so Bhagavā arahaɱ Sammā-Sam-Buddho vijjā-caraṇa-sampanno Sugato loka-vidū anuttaro purisa-damma-sārathi Satthā deva-manussānaɱ Buddho Bhagavā" ti.|| ||

chambhitattaɱ vā||
lomahaɱso vā,||
so pahiyyissati.|| ||

"Svākkhāto Bhagavatā dhammo sandiṭṭhiko akāliko ehi passiko opanayiko paccattaɱ veditabbo viññūhī" ti.|| ||

chambhitattaɱ vā||
lomahaɱso vā,||
so pahiyyissati.|| ||

aṭṭha purisa-puggalā,||
esa Bhagavato sāvaka-saŋgho āhuneyyo pāhuneyyo dakkhiṇeyyo añjali-karaṇīyo anuttaraɱ puñña-k-khettaɱ lokassā' ti.|| ||

chambhitattaɱ vā||

Vepacitti Suttaɱ

[4.1][rhyc][bodh][than] Evaɱ me sutaɱ:|| ||

"bhikkhavo' ti.|| ||

"Bhadante" ti te bhikkhū Bhagavato paccassosuɱ.|| ||

Bhagavā etad avoca:|| ||

[221] "Bhūta-pubbaɱ bhikkhave dev-ā-sura-saŋgāmo samupabbūḷho ahosi.|| ||

Atha kho bhikkhave Vepacitti asurindo Asure āmantesi:|| ||

Sace mārisā dev-ā-sura-saŋgāme samūpabbūḷhe Asurā jineyyuɱ,||
devā parājeyyuɱ,||
yena taɱ sakkaɱ devānam indaɱ kaṇṭhapañcamehi bandhanehi bandhitvā mama santike āneyyātha Asurapuranti.|| ||

Sakko pi kho bhikkhave devānaɱ Indo deve Tāvatiɱse āmantesi.|| ||

Sace mārisā dev-ā-sura-saŋgāme samūpabbūḷhe devā jineyyuɱ,||
Asurā parājeyyuɱ,||
yena taɱ Vepacittiɱ asurindaɱ kaṇṭhapañcamehi bandhanehi bandhitvā mama santike āneyyātha sudhammaɱ sabhanti.|| ||

Tasmiɱ kho pana bhikkhave saŋgāme devā jiniɱsu.|| ||

Asurā parājiɱsu.|| ||

Atha kho bhikkhave devā Tāvatiɱsā Vepacittiɱ asurindaɱ kaṇṭhapañcamehi bandhanehi bandhitvā Sakkassa devānam indassa santike ānesuɱ sudhammaɱ sabhaɱ.|| ||

Tatra sudaɱ bhikkhave Vepacitti asurindo kaṇṭhapañcamehi bandhanehi baddho sakkaɱ devānam indaɱ sudhammaɱ sabhaɱ pavisantañ ca||
ni-k-khamantañ ca||
asabbhāhi pharusāhi vācāhi akkosati,||
paribhāsati.|| ||

Atha kho bhikkhave mātalī saŋgāhako sakkaɱ devānam indaɱ gāthāya ajjhabhāsi:|| ||

Bhayā nu Maghavā Sakka dubbalyā no titikkhasi,||
Suṇanto pharusaɱ vācaɱ sammukhā Vepacittinoti.|| ||

(Sakko:)|| ||

Nāhaɱ bhayā na dubbalyā khamāmi Vepacittino,||
Kathañhi mādiso viññū bālena paṭisaɱyujeti.|| ||

Bhiyyo bālā pabhijjeyyuɱ no c'assa paṭisedhako Tasmā bhūsena daṇḍena dhīro bālaɱ nisedhayeti.|| ||

Etad eva ahaɱ maññe bālassa paṭisedhanaɱ,||
Paraɱ saŋkupitaɱ ñatvā yo sato upasammatī ti.|| ||

(Mātali:)|| ||

Etad eva titikkhāya vajjaɱ passāmi vāsava,||
Yadā naɱ maññati bālo bhayā myāyaɱ titikkhati,||
Ajjhārūhati dummedho goḷva bhiyyo palāyinanti.|| ||

(Sakko:)|| ||

[222] Kāmaɱ maññatu vā mā vā bhayā myāyaɱ titikkhati,||
Sadatthaparamā atthā khantyā bhiyyo na vijjati.|| ||

Yo have balavā santo dubbalassa titikkhati,||
Tamāhu paramaɱ khantiɱ niccaɱ khamati dubbalo.|| ||

Abalantaɱ balaɱ āhu yassa bālabalaɱ balaɱ,||
Balassa dhammaguttassa paṭivattā na vijjati.|| ||

Tass'eva tena pāpiyo yo kuddhaɱ paṭikujjhati,||
Kuddhaɱ appaṭikujjhanto saŋgāmaɱ jeti dujjayaɱ.|| ||

Ubhinnamatthaɱ carati attano ca parassa ca,||
Paraɱ saɱkupitaɱ ñatvā yo sato upasammati.|| ||

Ubhinnaɱ tikicchantānaɱ1 attano ca parassa ca,||
Janā maññanti bāloti ye Dhammassa akovidāti|| ||

So hi nāma bhikkhave Sakko devānaɱ Indo sakaɱ puññaphalaɱ upajīvamāno devānaɱ Tāvatiɱsānaɱ issariyādhipaccaɱ rajjaɱ kārento khanti-soraccassa vaṇṇa-vādī bhavissati.|| ||

"Idha kho taɱ bhikkhave sobhetha,||
yaɱ tumhe evaɱ svākkhāte Dhamma-Vinaye pabba-jitā samānā khamā ca bhaveyyātha soratā cā" ti.|| ||
