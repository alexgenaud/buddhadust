2. Anāthapiṇḍika Vagga

Sutta 11

Candimasa Suttaɱ

[11.1][rhyc] Evam me sutaɱ ekaɱ samayaɱ Bhagavā Sāvatthiyaɱ viharati Jetavane Anāthapiṇḍikassa ārāme.|| ||

Atha kho Candimaso deva-putto abhikkantāya rattiyā abhikkanta-vaṇṇo kevala-kappaɱ Jetavanaɱ obhāsetvā yena [52] Bhagavā ten'upasankami.|| ||

Upasaŋkamitvā Bhagavantaɱ abhivādetvā eka-m-antaɱ aṭṭhāsi.|| ||

Eka-m-antaɱ ṭhito kho Candimaso deva-putto Bhagavato santike imaɱ gāthaɱ abhāsi:|| ||

"Te hi sotthiɱ gamissanti kacche vāmakase magā,||
Jhānāni upasampajja ekodīnipakā satā" ti.|| ||

(Bhagavā:)|| ||

"Te hi pāraɱ gamissanti chetvā jālaɱ va ambujo,||
Jhānāni upasampajja appamattā raṇañjahā" ti.|| ||

Sutta 12

Vendu Suttaɱ

[12.1][rhyc] Evam me sutaɱ ekaɱ samayaɱ Bhagavā Sāvatthiyaɱ viharati Jetavane Anāthapiṇḍikassa ārāme.|| ||

Eka-m-antaɱ ṭhito kho veṇhu1 deva-putto Bhagavato santike imaɱ gāthaɱ abhāsi:|| ||

"Sukhitā vata te manujā Sugataɱ payirupāsiya,||
Yuñjaɱ Gotama sāsane appamattānusikkhare" ti.|| ||

"Ye me pavutte satthipade (veṇhoti Bhagavā) anusikkhanti jhāyino,||
Kāle te appamajjantā na maccuvasagā siyun" ti.|| ||

Sutta 13

Dīghalaṭṭhi Suttaɱ

[13.1][rhyc] Evaɱ me sutaɱ.|| ||

Ekaɱ samayaɱ Bhagavā Rājagahe viharati Veḷuvane Kalandakanivāpe.|| ||

Atha kho Dīghalaṭṭhi deva-putto abhikkantāya rattiyā abhikkanta-vaṇṇo kevala-kappaɱ Veḷuvanaɱ obhāsetvā yena Bhagavā ten'upasankami. Upasaŋkamitvā Bhagavantaɱ abhivādetvā eka-m-antaɱ aṭṭhāsi. Eka-m-antaɱ ṭhito kho Dīghalaṭṭhi deva-putto Bhagavato santike imaɱ gāthām abhāsi:|| ||

"Bhikkhu siyā jhāyī vimutta-citto||
Ākaŋkhe ce hadayassānupattiɱ,||
Lokassa ñatvā udayabbayañ ca||
Sucetaso asito tadānisaɱso" ti.|| ||

Sutta 14

Nandana Suttaɱ

[14.1][rhyc] Evam me sutaɱ ekaɱ samayaɱ Bhagavā Sāvatthiyaɱ viharati Jetavane Anāthapiṇḍikassa ārāme.|| ||

Eka-m-antaɱ ṭhito kho Nandano deva-putto Bhagavantaɱ gāthāya ajjhabhāsi:|| ||

"Pucchāmi taɱ Gotama bhūripaññaɱ||
Anāvaṭaɱ Bhagavato ñāṇa-dassanaɱ||
[53] Kathaɱvidhaɱ sīlavantaɱ vadanti||
Kathaɱvidhaɱ paññavantaɱ vadanti||
Kathaɱvidho dukkhamaticca irīyati.||
Kathaɱvidhaɱ devatā pūjayantī" ti.|| ||

(Bhagavā:)|| ||

"Yo sīlavā paññavā bhāvitatto||
Samāhito jhānarato satīmā,||
Sabb'assa sokā vigatā pahīṇā||
Khīṇ'āsavo antimadehadhārī.|| ||

Tathāvidhaɱ sīlavantaɱ vadanti||
Tathāvidhaɱ paññavantaɱ vadanti.||
Tathāvidho dukkham aticca irīyati||
Tathāvidhaɱ devatā pūjayantī" ti.|| ||

Sutta 15

Candana Suttaɱ

[15.1][rhyc] Evam me sutaɱ ekaɱ samayaɱ Bhagavā Sāvatthiyaɱ viharati Jetavane Anāthapiṇḍikassa ārāme.|| ||

Eka-m-antaɱ ṭhito kho Candano deva-putto Bhagavantaɱ gāthāya ajjhabhāsi:|| ||

"Kathaɱ sū taratī oghaɱ rattin-divam atandito||
Appatiṭṭhe anālambe ko gambhīre na sīdatī" ti?|| ||

(Bhagavā)|| ||

"Sabbadā sīla-sampanno paññavā susamāhito,||
Āraddha-viriyo pahit'atto oghaɱ tarati duttaraɱ|| ||

Virato kāma-saññāya rūpasaŋyojanātigo,||
Nandibhavāparikkhīṇo so gambhīre na sīdatī" ti.|| ||

Sutta 16

Sudatta Suttaɱ

[16.1][rhyc] Evam me sutaɱ ekaɱ samayaɱ Bhagavā Sāvatthiyaɱ viharati Jetavane Anāthapiṇḍikassa ārāme.|| ||

Eka-m-antaɱ ṭhito kho Sudatto deva-putto Bhagavato santike imaɱ gāthaɱ abhāsi:|| ||

"Sattiyā viya omaṭṭho ḍayha-māno va matthake,||
Kāmarāga-p-pahāṇāya sato bhikkhu paribbaje" ti.|| ||

(Bhagavā:)|| ||

"Sattiyā viya omaṭṭho ḍayha-māno va matthake,||
Sakkāya-diṭṭhippahāṇāya sato bhikkhu paribbaje" ti.|| ||

Sutta 17

Subrahma Suttaɱ

[17.1][rhyc] Evam me sutaɱ ekaɱ samayaɱ Bhagavā Sāvatthiyaɱ viharati Jetavane Anāthapiṇḍikassa ārāme.|| ||

Eka-m-antaɱ ṭhito kho Subrahmā deva-putto Bhagavantaɱ gāthāya ajjhabhāsi:|| ||

"Niccaɱ utrastam idaɱ cittaɱ niccaɱ ubbiggam idaɱ mano||
[54] Anuppannesu kicchesu atho uppatitesu ca||
Sace atthi anutrastaɱ taɱ me akkhāhi pucchito" ti.|| ||

(Bhagavā:)|| ||

"Na aññatra bojjhaŋgātapasā nāññatra indriya-saɱvarā,||
Na aññatra sabba-nissaggā sotthiɱ passāmi pāṇinan" ti.|| ||

Tatth'ev'antara-dhāyī.|| ||

Sutta 18

Kakudha Suttaɱ

[18.1][rhyc] Evaɱ me sutaɱ ekaɱ samayaɱ Bhagavā Sākete viharati Añjanavane Migadāye.|| ||

Atha kho Kakudho deva-putto abhikkantāya rattiyā abhikkanta-vaṇṇo kevala-kappaɱ Añjanavanaɱ obhāsetvā yena Bhagavā ten'upasankami.|| ||

Upasaŋkamitvā Bhagavantaɱ abhivādetvā eka-m-antaɱ aṭṭhāsi.|| ||

Eka-m-antaɱ ṭhito kho Kakudho deva-putto Bhagavantaɱ etad avoca:|| ||

"Nandasi samaṇā" ti?|| ||

"Kiɱ laddhā āvuso" ti?|| ||

"Tena hi samaṇa socasī" ti?|| ||

"Kiɱ jīyittha āvuso" ti?|| ||

"Tena hi samaṇa n'eva nandasi na ca socasī" ti?|| ||

"Evam āvuso" ti.|| ||

"Kacci tvaɱ anigho bhikkhu atho nandī na vijjati,||
Kacci taɱ ekam āsīnaɱ arati nābhikīratī" ti?|| ||

(Bhagavā:)|| ||

"Anīgho ve ahaɱ yakkha atho nandi na vijjati,||
Atho maɱ ekam āsīnaɱ arati nābhikīratī" ti.|| ||

(Devaputto:)|| ||

"Kathaɱ tvaɱ anigho bhikkhu kathaɱ nandī na vijjati,||
Kathaɱ taɱ ekam āsīnaɱ aratī nābhikīratī" ti?|| ||

(Bhagavā:)|| ||

"Aghajātassa ve nandī nandījātassa ve aghaɱ,||
Anandī anagho bhikkhu evaɱ jānāhi āvuso" ti.|| ||

(Devaputto:)|| ||

"Cirassaɱ vata passāmi brāhmaṇaɱ parinibbutaɱ,||
Anandiɱ anaghaɱ bhikkhuɱ tiṇṇaɱ loke visattikan" ti.|| ||

Sutta 19

Uttara Suttaɱ

[19.1][rhyc][than] Evam me sutaɱ ekaɱ samayaɱ Bhagavā Rājagahe viharati.|| ||

Eka-m-antaɱ ṭhito kho Uttaro deva-putto Bhagavato santike imaɱ gāthaɱ abhāsi:|| ||

[55] "Upanīyati jīvitam appam āyu||
jarūpanītassa na santi tāṇā,||
Etaɱ bhayaɱ maraṇe pekkhamāno||
puññāni kayirātha sukhāvahānī" ti.|| ||

(Bhagavā:)|| ||

"Upanīyati jīvitam appam āyu||
jarūpanītassa na santi tāṇā,||
Etaɱ bhayaɱ maraṇe pekkhamāno||
lokāmisaɱ pajahe santipekkho" ti.|| ||

Sutta 20

Anāthapiṇḍika Suttaɱ

[20.1][rhyc] Evam me sutaɱ ekaɱ samayaɱ Bhagavā Sāvatthiyaɱ viharati Jetavane Anāthapiṇḍikassa ārāme.|| ||

Eka-m-antaɱ ṭhito kho Anāthapiṇḍiko deva-putto Bhagavato santike imā gāthāyo abhāsi:|| ||

"Idaɱ hi taɱ Jetavanaɱ isiSaŋghanisevitaɱ,||
Āvutthaɱ dhammarājena pītisañjananaɱ mama.|| ||

Kammaɱ vijjā ca dhammo ca sīlaɱ jīvitam uttamaɱ,||
Etena maccā sujjhanti na gottena dhanena vā.|| ||

Tasmā hi paṇḍito poso sampassaɱ attham attano,||
Yoniso vicine dhammaɱ evaɱ tattha visujjhati.|| ||

Sāriputto va paññāya sīlen'upasamena ca,||
Yo pi pāragato bhikkhu etāva paramo siyā" ti.|| ||

2. Idam avoca Anāthapiṇḍiko deva-putto.|| ||

Idaɱ vatvā Bhagavantaɱ abhivādetvā padakkhiṇaɱ katvā tatth'eva antara-dhāyī.|| ||

3. Atha kho Bhagavā tassā rattiyā accayena bhikkhū āmantesi.|| ||

4. Imaɱ bhikkhave rattiɱ aññataro deva-putto abhikkantāya rattiyā abhikkanta-vaṇṇo kevala-kappaɱ Jetavanaɱ obhāsetvā yen'āhaɱ ten'upasankami.|| ||

Upasaŋkamitvā maɱ abhivādetvā eka-m-antaɱ aṭṭhāsi.|| ||

Eka-m-antaɱ ṭhito kho bhikkhave so deva-putto mama santike imā gāthāyo abhāsi:|| ||

5. "'Idaɱ hi taɱ Jetavanaɱ isisaŋghanisevitaɱ,||
Āvutthaɱ dhammarājena pītisañjananaɱ mama.|| ||

Kammaɱ vijjā ca dhammo ca sīlaɱ jīvitam uttamaɱ,||
Etena maccā sujjhanti na gottena dhanena vā.|| ||

Tasmā hi paṇḍito poso sampassaɱ attham attano,||
[56] Yoniso vicine dhammaɱ evaɱ tattha visujjhati.|| ||

Sāriputto va paññāya sīlen'upasamena ca,||
Yo pi pāragato bhikkhu etāva paramo siyā' ti.|| ||

6. Idam avoca bhikkhave so deva-putto.|| ||

Idaɱ vatvā maɱ||
abhivādetvā padakkhiṇaɱ katvā tatth'eva antara-dhāyī" ti.|| ||

7. Evaɱ vutte āyasmā Ānando Bhagavantaɱ etad avoca:|| ||

"So hi nūna bhante Anāthapiṇḍiko deva-putto bhavissati.|| ||

Anāthapiṇḍiko gahapati āyasmante Sāriputte abhi-p-pasanno ahosī" ti.|| ||

8. "Sādhu sādhu Ānanda.|| ||

Yāvatakaɱ kho Ānanda takkāya pattabbaɱ anuppattaɱ tayā.|| ||

Anāthapiṇḍiko hi so Ānanda deva-putto" ti.|| ||
