I. Pathamo-vagga

 

Sutta 1

Tapo Kamma Suttaɱ

[1.1][pts][bodh][olds][upal] Evaɱ me sutaɱ.|| ||

Ekaɱ saɱayaɱ Bhagavā Uruvelāyaɱ viharati najjā Nerañjarāya tīre Ajapāla-nigrodha-mūle pathamābhisambuddho.|| ||

[1.2][pts][bodh][olds] Atha kho Bhagavato raho-gatassa paṭisallīnassa evam cetaso parivitakko udapādi.|| ||

"Mutto vat'amhi tāya dukkara-kārikāya||
sādhu mutto vat'amhi tāya anattha-saɱhitāya dukkara-kārikāya||
sādhu ṭhito sato bodhiɱ samajjhagan" ti.|| ||

[1.3][pts][bodh][olds] Atha kho Māro pāpimā Bhagavato cetasā ceto-parivitakkam aññāya yena Bhagavā ten-upasaŋkami.|| ||

Upasaŋkamitvā Bhagavantaɱ gāthāya ajjhabhāsi.|| ||

"Tapo kammā apakkamma||
yena sujjhanti mānavā||
asuddho maññati suddho||
suddhiMaggam aparaddho" ti.|| ||

[1.4][pts][bodh][olds] Atha kho Bhagavā Māro ayam pāpimā iti viditvā Māram pāpimantam gāthāhi paccabhāsi:|| ||

"Anattha-sañhitaɱ ñatvā||
yaɱ kiñci aparaɱ tapaɱ||
sabbānatthāvahaɱ hoti||
piyārittam va dhammaniɱ.|| ||

Sīlaɱ samādhi-paññañca||
Maggam bodhāya bhāvayaɱ||
patto-smi paramaɱ suddhiɱ||
nihato tvaɱ asi antakā" ti.|| ||

[1.5][pts][bodh][olds] Atha kho Māro pāpimā jānāti||
"Maɱ Bhagavā jānāti maɱ Sugato" ti||
dukkhī dummano tatth'ev'antara-dhāyī.|| ||

Sutta 2

Nāgo or Hatthi-Rāja-Vaṇṇa Suttaɱ

[2.1][pts][bodh][olds][upal] Evaɱ me sutaɱ.|| ||

Ekaɱ saɱayaɱ Bhagavā Uruvelāyaɱ viharati najjā Nerañjarāya tīre Ajapāla-nigrodha pathamābhi- [104] sambuddho.|| ||

Tena kho pana samayena Bhagavā ratt-andhakāra-timisāyam ajjhokāse nisinno hoti||
devo ca ekam ekam phusāyati.|| ||

[2.2][pts][bodh][olds] Atha kho Māro pāpimā Bhagavato bhayaɱ chambhitattaɱ lomahaɱsam uppādetu-kāmo mahantaɱ hatthirāja-vaṇṇam abhinimminitvā yena Bhagavā ten-upasankami.|| ||

[2.3][pts][bodh][olds] Seyyathā pi nāma mahā ariṭṭhako maṇi evam assa sīsaɱ hoti||
seyyathā pi nāma suddhaɱ rūpiyam evam assa dantā honti||
seyyathā pi nāma mahatī nangalasīsā evam assa soṇḍo hoti.|| ||

[2.4][pts][bodh][olds] Atha kho Bhagavā Māro ayam pāpimā iti viditvā Māram pāpimantam gāthāya ajjhabhāsi.|| ||

Saɱsāraɱ dīgham addhānam||
vaṇṇaɱ katvā subhāsubham||
alan-te tena pāpima||
nihato tvam asi antakā ti.|| ||

[2.5][pts][bodh][olds] Atha kho Māro pāpimā jānāti||
"Maɱ Bhagavā jānāti maɱ Sugato" ti||
dukkhī dummano tatth'ev'antara-dhāyī.|| ||

Sutta 3

Subha Suttaɱ

[3.1][pts][bodh][olds][upal] Uruvelāyaɱ viharati.|| ||

[3.2][pts][bodh][olds] Tena kho pana samayena Bhagavā ratt-andhakāra-timisāyaɱ ajjhokāse nisinno hoti devo ca ekam ekaɱ phusāyati.|| ||

[3.3][pts][bodh][olds] Atha kho Māro pāpimā Bhagavato bhayaɱ chambhitattaɱ loma-haɱsam uppādetu-kāmo yena Bhagavā ten-upasankami.|| ||

[3.4][pts][bodh][olds] Upassaŋkamitvā Bhagavato avidūre uccāvacā vaṇṇanibhā upadaɱ-seti subhā c'eva asubhā ca.|| ||

[3.5][pts][bodh][olds] Atha kho Bhagavā Māro ayam pāpimā iti viditvā Māraɱ pāpimantam gāthāhi ajjhabhāsi:|| ||

vaṇṇaɱ katvā subhā-subhaɱ||
alan-te tena pāpima||

Ye ca kāyena vācāya||
manasā ca susaɱvutā||
na te Māra vasānugā||
na te Mārassa paccagū ti.|| ||

[3.6][pts][bodh][olds] Atha kho Māro pāpimā jānāti||

Sutta 4

Paṭhama Māra-Pāsa Suttaɱ

[4.1][pts][bodh][upal] Evaɱ me sutaɱ.|| ||

Ekaɱ samayaɱ Bhagavā Bārāṇasiyaɱ viharati Isipatane Migadāye.|| ||

Tatra kho Bhagavā bhikkh āmantesi.|| ||

Bhikkhavo-ti.|| ||

Bhadante ti te bhikkh Bhagavato paccassosuɱ.|| ||

[4.2][pts][bodh] Bhagavā etad avoca:.|| ||

Mayhaɱ kho bhikkhave yoniso mana-sikārā||
yoniso samma-p-padhānā||
anuttarā vimutti anuppattā||
anuttarā vimutti sacchi-katā.|| ||

Tumhe pi bhikkhave yoniso mana-sikārā||
yoniso sammapadhānā||
anuttaraɱ vimuttim anupāpuṇātha||
anuttaraɱ vimuttiɱ sacchikarothā ti.|| ||

[4.3][pts][bodh] Atha kho Māro pāpimā yena Bhagavā ten-upasankami||
upasankamitvā Bhagavantaɱ gāthāya ajjhabhāsi.|| ||

Baddho-si māra-pāsena||
ye dibbā ye ca mānusā||
māra-bandhanna-baddhosi||
na me samaṇa mokkhasīti.|| ||

Atha kho Bhagavā Māro ayam pāpimā iti viditvā Māram pāpimantam gāthāya ajjhabhāsi.|| ||

[4.4][pts][bodh] Mutto-ham māra-pāsena||
ye dibbā ye ca mānusā||
mārabandhana-mutto mhi||
nihato tvam asi antakāti.|| ||

[4.5][pts][bodh] Atha kho Māro pāpimā jānāti||
"Maɱ Bhagavā jānāti maɱ Sugato" ti||
dukkhī dummano tatth'ev'antara-dhāyī.|| ||

Sutta 5

Dutiya Māra-Pāsa Suttam

[5.1][pts][bodh][upal] Evaɱ me sutaɱ.|| ||

Ekaɱ samayaɱ Bhagavā Bārāṇasiyaɱ viharati Isipatane Migadāye.|| ||

Tatra kho Bhagavā bhikkhū āmantesi:|| ||

Bhikkhavo ti!|| ||

Bhadante ti te bhikkhū Bhagavato paccassosuɱ.|| ||

[5.2][pts][bodh] Bhagavā etad avoca:

Mutto'haɱ bhikkhave sabbapāsehi ye dibbā ye ca mānusā,||
Tumhe pi bhikkhave muttā sabbapāsehi ye dibbā ye ca mānusā,||
caratha bhikkhave cārikaɱ bahu-jana-hitāya bahu-jana-sukhāya lokānukampakāya atthāya hitāya sukhāya deva-manussānaɱ.|| ||

Mā ekena dve agamettha,||
desetha bhikkhave dhammaɱ ādi-kalyāṇaɱ majjhe kalyāṇaɱ pariyosāna-kalyāṇaɱ||
sātthaɱ savyañ janaɱ kevala-paripuṇṇaɱ parisuddhaɱ Brahma-cariyaɱ pakāsetha.|| ||

Santi sattā apparajakkha-jātikā||
assavaṇatā [106] dhammassa parihāyanti,||
bhavissanti dhammassa aññātāro.|| ||

Aham pi bhikkhave yena Uruvelā Senāninigamo ten'upasankamissāmi dhamma-desanāyā ti.|| ||

[5.3][pts][bodh] Atha kho Māro pāpimā yena Bhagavā ten'upasankami,||
upasankamitvā Bhagavantaɱ gāthāya ajjhabhāsi:|| ||

Baddho-si sabba-pāsehi||
ye dibbā ye ca mānusā,||
Mahā-bandhana-baddho si||
na me samaṇa mokkhasīti.|| ||

[5.4][pts][bodh] Mutto-haɱ sabbapāsehi||
ye dibbā ye ca mānusā,||
Mahā-bandhana-mutto mhi||
nihato tvam asi antakā ti.|| ||

[5.5][pts][bodh] Atha kho Māro pāpimā jānāti||
"Maɱ Bhagavā jānāti maɱ Sugato" ti||
dukkhī dummano tatth'ev'antara-dhāyī.|| ||

Sutta 6

Sappa Suttaɱ

[6.1][pts][bodh][upal] Evaɱ me sutaɱ.|| ||

Ekaɱ samayaɱ Bhagavā Rājagahe viharati Veḷuvane kalandaka-nivāpe.|| ||

[6.2][pts][bodh] Tena kho pana samayena Bhagavā rattandhakāra-timisāyaɱ ajjhokāse nisinno hoti,||
devo ca ekam ekaɱ phusāyati.|| ||

[6.3][pts][bodh] Atha kho Māro pāpimā Bhagavato bhayaɱ chambhitattaɱ loma-haɱsaɱ uppādetu-kāmo mahantaɱ sappa-rājavaṇṇaɱ abhinimminitvā yena Bhagavā ten'upasankami.|| ||

[6.4][pts][bodh] seyyathā pi nāma mahatī eka-rukkhikā nāvā,||
evam assa kāyo hoti||
seyyathā pi nāma soṇḍikā kilañjaɱ,||
evam assa phaṇo hoti,||
seyyathā pi nāma kosalikā kaɱsapātī,||
evam assa akkhīni bhavanti,||
seyyathā pi nāma deve ga'aga'āyante vijjullatā niccharanti,||
evam assa mukhato jivhā niccharati,||
seyyathā pi nāma kammāra-gaggariyā dhamamānāya saddo hoti,||
evam assa assāsa-passāsānaɱ saddo hoti.|| ||

[6.5][pts][bodh] Atha kho Bhagavā Māro ayaɱ pāpimā iti viditvā Māraɱ pāpimantaɱ gāthāhi ajjhabhāsi:

Yo suñña-gehāni sevati||
seyyā so muni atta-saññato,||
vossajja careyya tattha so||
paṭirūpaɱ hi tathāvidhassa taɱ.|| ||

Carakā bahū bheravā bahū||
atho ḍaɱsā siriɱsapā bahū,||
[107] lomam pi na tattha iñjaye||
suññāgāra-gato mahā muni.|| ||

Nabhaɱ phaleyya paṭhavīɱ caleyya||
sabbe pi pāṇā uda santaseyyuɱ,||
sallam pi ce urasi pakappayeyyuɱ||
upadhīsu tāṇaɱ na karonti buddhā ti.|| ||

[6.6][pts][bodh] Atha kho Māro pāpimā jānāti||
"Maɱ Bhagavā jānāti maɱ Sugato" ti||
dukkhī dummano tatth'ev'antara-dhāyī.|| ||

Sutta 9

Paṭhama Āyu Suttaɱ

Ekaɱ samayaɱ Bhagavā Rājagahe viharati Veḷuvane kalandaka-nivāpe.|| ||

Bhikkhavo ti.|| ||

Bhadante ti te bhikkhū Bhagavato paccassosuɱ.|| ||

[9.3][pts][bodh] Bhagavā etad avoca:|| ||

Appam idaɱ bhikkhave manussānaɱ āyu,||
gamanīyo samparāyo,||
kattabbaɱ kusalaɱ,||
caritabbaɱ Brahma-cariyaɱ,||
n'atthi jātassa amaraṇaɱ,||
yo bhikkhave ciraɱ jīvati,||
so vassa-sataɱ appaɱ vā bhiyo ti.|| ||

Dīgham āyu manussānaɱ||
na naɱ hī'e suporiso,||
careyya khīramatto va||
n'atthi maccussa āgamo ti.|| ||

[9.5][pts][bodh] Appam āyu manussānaɱ||
hī'eyya naɱ suporiso,||
careyyāditta-sīso va||
n'atthi maccussa nāgamo ti.|| ||

[9.6][pts][bodh] Atha kho Māro pāpimā jānāti||

Sutta 13

Sakalika Suttaɱ

[13.1][pts][than][bodh][upal] Evaɱ me sutaɱ.|| ||

Ekaɱ samayaɱ Bhagavā Rājagahe viharati Maddakucchismiɱ Migadāye.|| ||

[13.2][pts][bodh] Tena kho pana samayena Bhagavato pādo sakalikāya khato hoti.|| ||

Bhusā sudaɱ Bhagavato vedanā vattanti sārīrikā dukkhā tibbā kharā kaṭukā asātā amanāpā.|| ||

Tāsudaɱ Bhagavā sato sampajāno adhivāseti avihañña-māno.|| ||

Atha kho Bhagavā catugguṇaɱ sanghāṭiɱ paññapetvā dakkhiṇena passena sīhaseyyaɱ kappesi pāde pādaɱ accādhāya sato sampajāno.|| ||]

[13.3][pts][bodh] Atha kho Māro pāpimā yena Bhagavā ten'upasankami.|| ||

Upasaŋkamitvā Bhagavantaɱ gāthāya ajjhabhāsi:|| ||

Mandiyā nu sesi udāhu kāveyya-matto||
Atthā nu te sampacurā na santi,||
Eko vivitte sayanāsanamhi||
Niddāmukho kim idaɱ soppasevā ti.|| ||

[13.4][pts][bodh] Na mandiyā sayāmi nāpi kāveyya-matto||
Atthaɱ sameccāham apetasoko,||
Eko vivitte sayanāsanamhi||
Sayām-ahaɱ sabba-bhūtānukampī.|| ||

Yesam pi sallaɱ urasī paviṭṭhaɱ||
Muhuɱ muhuɱ hadayaɱ vedhamānaɱ,||
Te cāpi soppaɱ labhare sasallā||
[111] kasmā ahaɱ na supe vītasallo.|| ||

Jaggaɱ na saŋke na pi bhemi sottuɱ||
Rattindivā nānutapanti māmaɱ,||
Hāniɱ na passāmi kuhiñci loke||
Tasmā supe sabba-bhūtānukampīti.|| ||

[13.5][pts][bodh] Atha kho Māro pāpimā jānāti||
"Maɱ Bhagavā jānāti maɱ Sugato" ti||
dukkhī dummano tatth'ev'antara-dhāyī.|| ||

Sutta 14

[14.1][pts][bodh][upal] Ekaɱ samayaɱ Bhagavā Kosalesu viharati Ekasālāyaɱ brāhmaṇa-gāme.|| ||

Tena kho pana samayena Bhagavā mahatiyā gihiparisāya parivuto dhammaɱ deseti.|| ||

[14.2][pts][bodh] Atha kho Mārassa pāpimato etad ahosi:|| ||

Ayaɱ kho Samaṇo Gotamo mahatiyā gihiparisāya parivuto dhammaɱ deseti.|| ||
