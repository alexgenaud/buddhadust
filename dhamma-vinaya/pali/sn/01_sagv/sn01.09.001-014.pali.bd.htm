Saɱyutta Nikāya
Sagatha Vagga

Vana Saɱyutta

Suttas 1-14

Adapted from the 1995 edition of the digital version of the Sri Lanka Buddha Jayanti Tripitaka Series.

 

[197]

Sutta 1

Viveka Suttaɱ

[1.1][pts][than] Evaɱ me sutaɱ.|| ||

Ekaɱ samayaɱ aññataro bhikkhu Kosalesu viharati aññatarasmiɱ vana-saṇḍe.|| ||

2. Tena kho pana samayena so bhikkhu divā-vihāragato pāpake akusale vitakke vitakketi gehanissite.|| ||

3. Atha kho yā tasmiɱ vana-saṇḍe adhivatthā devatā tassa bhikkhuno anukampikā attha-kāmā taɱ bhikkhuɱ saɱvejetukāmā yena so bhikkhu ten'upasankami.|| ||

4. Upasaŋkamitvā taɱ bhikkhuɱ gāthāhi ajjhabhāsi:|| ||

"Viveka-kāmosi vanaɱ paviṭṭho atha te mano niccharatī bahiddhā,||
Jano janasmiɱ vinayassu chandaɱ tato sukhī hohisi vīta-rāgo.||
Aratiɱ pajahāsi sato bhavāsi sataɱ taɱ sādayāmase,||
Pātālarajo hi duttaro mā taɱ kāmarajo avāhari.||
Sakuṇo yathā paɱsukuṇḍito vidhunaɱ pātayati sitaɱ rajaɱ,||
Evaɱ bhikkhu padhānavā satimā vidhunaɱ pātayati sitaɱ rajan" ti.|| ||

Atha kho so bhikkhu tāya devatāya saɱvejito saɱvegamāpādī.|| ||

Sutta 2

Upaṭṭhāna Suttaɱ

[2.1][pts] Evaɱ me sutaɱ.|| ||

Ekaɱ samayaɱ aññataro bhikkhu Kosalesu viharati aññatarasmiɱ vana-saṇḍe.|| ||

[198]Tena kho pana samayena so bhikkhu divā-vihāragato supati.|| ||

3. Atha kho yā tasmiɱ vanasaṇaḍe adhivatthā devatā tassa bhikkhuno anukampikā attha-kāmā taɱ bhikkhuɱ saɱvejetukāmā yena so bhikkhu ten'upasankami.|| ||

4. Upasaŋkamitvā taɱ bhikkhuɱ gāthāhi ajjhabhāsi:|| ||

"Uṭṭhehi bhikkhu kiɱ sesi ko attho supinena te,||
Āturassa hi kā niddā sallaviddhassa ruppato.||
Yāya saddhāya pabba-jito agārasmā'nagāriyaɱ,||
Tam eva saddhaɱ brūhehi mā niddāya vasaɱ gamī" ti.|| ||

5. "Aniccā addhuvā kāmā yesu mando samucchito,||
Bandhesu muttaɱ asitaɱ kasmā pabba-jitaɱ tape.||
Chandarāgassa vinayā avijjāsamati-k-kamā,||
Taɱ ñāṇaɱ pariyodātaɱ kasmā pabba-jitaɱ tape.||
Bhetvā avijjaɱ vijjāya āsavānaɱ parikkhayā,||
Asokaɱ anupāyāsaɱ kasmā pabba-jitaɱ tape.||
Āraddha-viriyaɱ pahit'attaɱ niccaɱ daḷha-parakkamaɱ,||
Nibbāṇaɱ abhikaŋkhantaɱ kasmā pabba-jitaɱ tape" ti.|| ||

Sutta 3

Kassapagotta Suttaɱ

[3.1][pts] Evaɱ me sutaɱ.|| ||

Ekaɱ samayaɱ āyasmā Kassapa-gotto Kosalesu viharati aññatarasmiɱ vana-saṇḍe.|| ||

2. Tena kho pana samayen'āyasmā Kassapa-gotto divā-vihāra-gato aññataraɱ ch'etaɱ ovadati.|| ||

3. Atha kho yā tasmiɱ vana-saṇḍe adhivatvā devatā āyasmato Kassapa-gottassa anukampikā attha-kāmā āyasmantaɱ Kassapagottaɱ saɱvejetukāmā yen'āyasmā Kassapa-gotto ten'upasankami.|| ||

4. Upasaŋkamitvā āyasmantaɱ Kassapa-gottaɱ gāthāhi ajjhabhāsi:|| ||

"Giriduggacaraɱ ch'etaɱ appapaññaɱ acetasaɱ,||
Akāle ovadaɱ bhikkhu mando va paṭibhāti maɱ.||
Suṇāti na vijānāti āloketi na passati,||
Dhammasmiɱ bhaññamānasmiɱ atthaɱ bālo na bujjhati.||
[199]sace pi dasa pajjote dhārayissasi Kassapa,||
N'eva dakkhiti rūpāni cakkhu hi'ssa na vijjatī" ti.|| ||

Atha kho āyasmā Kassapagotto tāya devatāya saɱvejito saɱvegamāpādī.|| ||

Sutta 4

Sambahulā (or Cārika) Suttaɱ

[4.1][pts] Evaɱ me sutaɱ.|| ||

Ekaɱ samayaɱ sambahulā bhikakhū Kosalesu viharanti aññatarasmiɱ vana-saṇḍe.|| ||

2. Atha kho te bhikkhū vassaɱ vutthā temāsaccayena cārikaɱ pakkamiɱsu.|| ||

3. Atha kho yā tasmiɱ vana-saṇḍe adhivatthā devatā te bhikkhū apassantī paridevamānā tāyaɱ velāyaɱ imaɱ gāthaɱ abhāsi:|| ||

"Arati viya me'jja khāyati||
bahuke disvāna vivitte āsane,||
Te cittakathā bahu-s-sutā||
ko'me Gotama-sāvakā gatā" ti.|| ||

4. Evaɱ vutte aññatarā devatā taɱ devataɱ gāthāya ajjhabhāsi:|| ||

"Magadhaɱ gatā Kosalaɱ gatā||
ekacciyā pana Vajjibhūmiyā,||
Migā viya asaŋgacārino||
aniketā viharanti bhikkhavo" ti.|| ||

Sutta 5

Ānanda Suttaɱ

[5.1][pts] Evaɱ me sutaɱ.

Ekaɱ samayaɱ āyasmā Ānando Kosalesu viharati aññatarasmiɱ vana-saṇḍe.|| ||

2. Tena kho pana samayen'āyasmā Ānando ati-velaɱ gihīsaññattibahulo viharati.|| ||

3. Atha kho yā tasmiɱ vana-saṇḍe adhivatthā devatā āyasmato Ānandassa anukampikā attha-kāmā āyasmantaɱ Ānandaɱ saɱvejetukāmā yen'āyasmā Ānando ten'upasankami.|| ||

Upasaŋkamitvā āyasmantaɱ Ānandaɱ gāthāya ajjhabhāsi:|| ||

"Rukkha-mūlagahaṇaɱ pasakkiya||
nibbāṇaɱ hadayasmiɱ opiya,||
[200]jhāya Gotama mā ca pamādo||
kiɱ te biḷibiḷikā karissatī" ti.|| ||

4. Atha kho āyasmā Ānando tāya devatāya saɱvejito saɱvegam āpādī.|| ||

Sutta 6

Anuruddha Suttaɱ

[6.1][pts][than] Evaɱ me sutaɱ.|| ||

Ekaɱ samayaɱ āyasmā Anuruddho Kosalesu viharati aññatarasmiɱ vana-saṇḍe.|| ||

2. Atha kho aññatarā Tāvatiɱsakāyikā devatā Jālinī nāma āyasmato Anuruddhassa purāṇa-dutiyikā yen'āyasmā Anuruddho ten'upasankami.|| ||

3. Upasaŋkamitvā āyasmantaɱ Anuruddhaɱ gāthāhi ajjhabhāsi:|| ||

"Tattha cittaɱ paṇidhehi yattha te vusitaɱ pure,||
Tāvatiɱsesu devesu sabba-kāmasamiddhisu||
Purakkhato parivuto devakaññāhi sobhasī" ti.|| ||

4.[Anuruddho:]|| ||

"Duggatā devakaññāyo sakkāyasmiɱ patiṭṭhitā,||
Te cāpi duggatā sattā devakaññāhi patthitātika" ti.|| ||

5. [Jālinī:]|| ||

"Na te sukhaɱ pajānanti ye na passanti Nandanaɱ,||
Āvāsaɱ naradevānaɱ tidasānaɱ yasassinan" ti.|| ||

6. [Anuruddho:]|| ||

"Na tvaɱ bāle vijānāsi yathā arahataɱ vaco,||
Aniccā sabbe sankhārā uppādavayadhammino,||
Uppajjitvā nirujjhanti tesaɱ vūpasamo sukho.||
N'atthi dāni punāvāso deva-kāyasmiɱ Jālinī,||
Vikkhīṇo jāti-saɱsāro n'atthi dāni puna-b-bhavo" ti.|| ||

Sutta 7

Nāgadatta Suttaɱ

[7.1][pts] Evaɱ me sutaɱ.|| ||

Ekaɱ samayaɱ āyasmā Nāgadatto Kosalesu viharati aññatarasmiɱ vana-saṇḍe.|| ||

2. Tena kho pana samayen'āyasmā Nāgadatto atikālena gāmaɱ pavisati,||
atidivā paṭikkamati.|| ||

3. Atha kho yā tasmiɱ vana-saṇḍe adhivatthā devatā āyasmato Nāgadattassa anukampikā attha-kāmā āyasmantaɱ Nāgadattaɱ saɱvejetukāmā yen'āyasmā Nāgadatto ten'upasankami.|| ||

[201] 4. Upasaŋkamitvā āyasmantaɱ Nāgadattaɱ gāthāhi ajjhabhāsi:|| ||

"Kāle pavissa Nāgadatta||
divā ca āgantvā ati-velacārī,||
Saɱsaṭṭho gahaṭṭhehi||
samānasukha-dukkho.||
Bhāyāmi Nāgadattaɱ suppagabbhaɱ||
kulesu vinibaddhaɱ,||
Mā h'eva maccurañño balavato||
antakassa vasam eyyā" ti.|| ||

5. Atha kho āyasmā Nāgadatto tāya devatāya saɱvejito saɱvegam āpādī.|| ||

Sutta 8

Kulagharaṇī Suttaɱ

[8.1][pts] Evaɱ me sutaɱ.|| ||

Ekaɱ samayaɱ aññataro bhikkhu Kosalesu viharati aññatarasmiɱ vana-saṇḍe.|| ||

2. Tena kho pana samayena so bhikkhu aññatarasmiɱ kule ati-velaɱ ajjhogāḷhappatto viharati.|| ||

3. Atha kho yā tasmiɱ vana-saṇḍe adhivatthā devatā tassa bhikkhuno anukamipikā attha-kāmā taɱ bhikkhuɱ saɱvejetukāmā yā tasmiɱ kule kulagharaṇī tassā vaṇṇaɱ abhinimminitvā yena so bhikkhu ten'upasankami.

4. Upasaŋkamitvā taɱ bhikkhuɱ gāthāhi ajjhabhāsi:|| ||

"Nadītīresu saṇṭhāne sabhāsu rathiyāsu ca,||
Janā saŋgamma mantenti mañ ca tañ ca kim antaran" ti.|| ||

5. "Bahū hi saddā paccūhā khamitabbā tapassinā,||
Na tena maŋku hotabbo na hi tena kilissati.||
Yo ca saddaparittāsī vane vātamigo yathā,||
Lahucitto ti taɱ āhu nāssa sampajjate vatan" ti.|| ||

Sutta 9

Vajjiputta Suttaɱ

[9.1][pts][than] Evaɱ me sutaɱ.|| ||

Ekaɱ samayaɱ aññataro Vajjiputtako bhikkhu Vesāliyaɱ viharati aññatarasmiɱ vana-saṇḍe.|| ||

2. Tena kho pana samayena Vesāliyaɱ sabbaratticāro hoti.|| ||

[202] 3. Atha kho so bhikkhū Vesāliyaɱ turiyatāḷitavāditanigghosasaddaɱ sutvā paridevamāno tāyaɱ velāyaɱ imaɱ gāthaɱ abhāsi:

"Ekakā mayaɱ araññe viharāma||
apaviddhaɱ va vanasmiɱ dārukaɱ,||
Etādisikāya rattiyā||
ko su nāma amhehi pāpiyo" ti.|| ||

4. Atha kho yā tasmiɱ vana-saṇḍe adhivatthā devatā tassa bhikkhuno anukampikā attakāmā taɱ bhikkhuɱ saɱvejetukāmā yena so bhikkhu ten'upasaŋaɱkami.|| ||

5. Upasaŋkamitvā taɱ bhikkhuɱ gāthāya ajjhabhāsi:|| ||

"Ekako tvaɱ araññe viharasi||
apaviddhaɱ va vanasmiɱ dārukaɱ,||
Tassa te bahukā pihayanti||
nerayikā viya saggagāminan" ti.|| ||

6. Atha kho so bhikkhu tāya devatāya saɱvajito saɱvegam āpādī.|| ||

Sutta 10

Sajjhāya Suttaɱ

[10.1][pts] Evaɱ me sutaɱ.|| ||

Ekaɱ samayaɱ aññataro bhikkhu Kosalesu viharati aññatarasmiɱ vana-saṇḍe.|| ||

2. Tena kho pana samayena so bhikkhu yaɱ sudaɱ pubbe ati-velaɱ sajjhāya bahulo viharati.|| ||

So aparena samayena appossukko tuṇhī-bhūto saɱkasāyati.|| ||

3. Atha kho yā tasmiɱ vana-saṇḍe adhivatthā devatā tassa bhikkhuno dhammaɱ asuṇantī yena so bhikkhu ten'upasankami.|| ||

Upasaŋkamitvā taɱ bhikkhuɱ gāthāhi ajjhabhāsi:|| ||

"Kasmā tuvaɱ dhamma-padāni bhikkhū||
nādhīyasi bhikkhūhi saɱvasanto,||
Sutvāna dhammaɱ labhati ppasādaɱ||
diṭṭhe'va dhamme labhati ppasaɱsan" ti?

"Ahu pure dhamma-padesu chando||
yāva virāgena samāgamimha,||
[203]yato virāgena samāgamimha,||
yaɱ kiñci diṭṭhaɱ va sutaɱ vā mutaɱ,||
Aññāya nikkhepanam āhu santo" ti.|| ||

Sutta 11

Ayoniso Suttaɱ

[11.1][pts][than] Evaɱ me sutaɱ.|| ||

Ekaɱ samayaɱ aññataro bhikkhu Kosalesu viharati aññatarasmiɱ vana-saṇḍe.|| ||

2. Tena kho pana samayena so bhikkhu divā-vihāragato pāpake akusale vitakke vitakketi.|| ||

Seyyath'īdaɱ:||
kāma-vitakkaɱ||
vyāpāda-vitakkaɱ||
vihiɱsā-vitakkaɱ.|| ||

3. Atha kho yā tasmiɱ vana-saṇḍe adhivatthā devatā tassa bhikkhuno anukampikā attha-kāmā taɱ bhikkhuɱ saɱvejetukāmā yena so bhikkhu ten'upasankami.|| ||

4. Upasaŋkamitvā taɱ bhikkhuɱ gāthāhi ajjhabhāsi:|| ||

"Ayoniso-mana-sikārā bho vitakkehi majjasi,||
Ayoniɱ paṭinissajja yoniso anuvicintaya.||
Satthāraɱ dhammam ārabbha Saŋghaɱ sīlāni-vattano,||
Adhigacchasi pāmojjaɱ pītisukham asaɱsayaɱ,||
Tato pāmojjabahulo dukkhass'antaɱ karissasī" ti.|| ||

5. Atha kho so bhikkhu tāya devatāya saɱvejito saɱvegam āpādī.|| ||

Sutta 12

Majjhathika (or Saṇika) Suttaɱ

[12.1][pts] Evaɱ me sutaɱ.|| ||

Ekaɱ samayaɱ aññataro bhikkhu Kosalesu viharati aññatarasmiɱ vana-saṇḍe.|| ||

2. Atha kho tasmiɱ vana-saṇḍe adhivatthā devatā yena so bhikkhu ten'upasankami.|| ||

3. Upasaŋkamitvā tassa bhikkhuno santike imaɱ gāthaɱ abhāsi:|| ||

"Ṭhite majjhantike kāle sanni-sinnesu pakkhisu,||
Saṇate va mahāraññaɱ taɱ bhayaɱ paṭibhāti maɱ" ti.|| ||

[Bhikkhu: —]|| ||

"Ṭhite majjhantike kāle sanni-sinnesu pakkhisu,||
Saṇate va mahāraññaɱ sā rati paṭibhāti man" ti.|| ||

Sutta 13

Pākat-Indriya Suttaɱ aka Sambahulā-Bhikkhū Suttaɱ

[13.1][pts] Evaɱ me sutaɱ.|| ||

Ekaɱ samayaɱ sambahulā bhikkhū Kosalesu viharanti aññatarasmiɱ vana-saṇḍe,||
uddhatā uṇṇaḷā capalā mukharā [204] vikiṇṇa-vācā muṭṭha-s-satino asampajānā asamāhitā vibbhanta-cittā pākat'indriyā.|| ||

2. Atha kho yā tasmiɱ vana-saṇḍe adhivatthā devatā tesaɱ bhikkhūnaɱ anukampikā attha-kāmā te bhikkhū saɱvejetukāmā yena te bhikkhū ten'upasankami.

3. Upasaŋkamitvā te bhikkhū gāthāhi ajjhabhāsi.|| ||

"Sukha-jīvino pure āsuɱ bhikkhū Gotama-sāvakā,||
Anicchā piṇḍam esanā anicchā sayanāsanaɱ,||
Loke aniccataɱ ñatvā dukkhass'antaɱ akaɱsu te.||
Dupposaɱ katvā attānaɱ gāme gāmaṇikā viya,||
Bhūtvā bhutvā nipajjanti parāgāresu mucchitā.||
Saŋghassa añjaliɱ katvā idh'ekacce vadām'ahaɱ,||
Appaviddhā anāthā te yathā petā tath'eva te.||
Ye kho pamattā viharanti te me sandhāya bhāsitaɱ,||
Ye appamattā viharanti namo tesaɱ karom'ahan" ti.|| ||

4. Atha kho te bhikkhū tāya devatāya saɱvejitā saɱvegam āpādun.|| ||

Sutta 14

Gandhatthena, or Puṇḍarīka, or Upaṭṭhāna Suttaɱ

[14.1][pts][than][olen] Evaɱ me sutaɱ.|| ||

Ekaɱ samayaɱ aññataro bhikkhu Kosalesu viharati aññatarasmiɱ vana-saṇḍe.|| ||

2. Tena kho pana samayena so bhikkhu pacchā-bhattaɱ piṇḍa-pāta-paṭikkanto pokkharaṇiɱ ogāhetvā padumaɱ upasiŋghati.|| ||

3. Atha kho yā tasmiɱ vana-saṇḍe adhivatthā devatā tassa bhikkhuno anukampikā attha-kāmā taɱ bhikkhuɱ saɱvejetukāmā yena so bhikkhu ten'upasankami.|| ||

4. Upasaŋkamitvā taɱ bhikkhuɱ gāthāya ajjhabhāsi:|| ||

"Yam etaɱ vārijaɱ pupphaɱ adinnaɱ upasiŋghasi,||
Ekaŋgam etaɱ theyyānaɱ gandhattheno'si mārisā" ti.|| ||

5. [Bhikkhu:]|| ||

"Na harāmi na bhañjāmi ārā siŋghāmi vārijaɱ,||
Atha kena nu vaṇṇena gandhattheno'ti vuccati.|| ||

Yavāyaɱ bhisāni khaṇati puṇḍarīkāni bhañjati,||
Evaɱ ākiṇṇakammanto kasmā eso na vuccatī" ti.|| ||

[205] 6. [Devatā:]|| ||

"Ākiṇṇaluddo puriso dhāti celaɱ va makkhito,||
Tasmiɱ me vacanaɱ n'atthi tañ ca arahāmi vattave.||
Anaŋgaṇassa posassa niccaɱ sucigavesino,||
Vāḷaggamattaɱ pāpassa abbhāmattaɱ va khāyatī" ti.|| ||

7. [Bhikkhu:]|| ||

"Addhā maɱ yakkha jānāsi atho maɱ anukampasi,||
Puna pi yakkha vajjāsi yadā passasi edisaɱ" ti.|| ||

8. [Devatā:]|| ||

"N'eva taɱ upajīvāmi na pi te bhatak'āmhase,||
Tvam eva bhikkhu jāneyya yena gaccheyya suggatin" ti.|| ||

9. Atha kho so bhikkhu tāya devatāya saɱvejito saɱvegam āpādī ti.|| ||
