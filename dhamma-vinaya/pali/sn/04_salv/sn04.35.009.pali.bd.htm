Saɱyutta Nikāya
4. Saḷāyatana Vagga
35. Saḷāyatana Saɱyutta
§ I: Mūla-Paññāsa
1. Anicca Vagga Paṭhama

Sutta 9

Dutiya Ajjhatta Anatta Suttaɱ (Anattām 3; Ajjhattam)

Adapted from the 1995 edition of the digital version of the Sri Lanka Buddha Jayanti Tripitaka Series.

 

[4]

[1][pts][bodh] Evaɱ me sutaɱ:|| ||

Ekaɱ samayaɱ Bhagavā Sāvatthiyaɱ viharati Jetavane Anāthapiṇḍikassa ārāme.|| ||

2. Tatra kho bhagavā bhikkhū āmantesi|| ||

"Bhikkhavo" ti.|| ||

"Bhadante" ti||
te bhikkhū bhagavato paccassosuɱ.|| ||

3. Bhagavā etad avoca:|| ||

"Cakkhuɱ bhikkhave anattaɱ atīt-ā-nāgataɱ,||
ko pana vādo pacc'uppannassa.|| ||

Evaɱ passaɱ bhikkhave sutavā [5] ariya-sāvako atītasmiɱ pi cakkhusmiɱ anapekho hoti,||
anāgataɱ cakkhuɱ n'ābhinandati,||
pacc'uppannassa cakkhussa||
nibbidāya||
virāgāya||
nirodhāya paṭipanno hoti.|| ||

■

Sotaɱ anattaɱ atīt-ā-nāgataɱ,||
ko pana vādo pacc'uppannassa.|| ||

Evaɱ passaɱ bhikkhave sutavā ariya-sāvako atītasmiɱ pi sotasmiɱ anapekho hoti,||
anāgataɱ sotaɱ n'ābhinandati,||
pacc'uppannassa sotassa||
nibbidāya||
virāgāya||
nirodhāya paṭipanno hoti.|| ||

■

Ghānaɱ anattaɱ atīt-ā-nāgataɱ,||
ko pana vādo pacc'uppannassa.|| ||

Evaɱ passaɱ bhikkhave sutavā ariya-sāvako atītasmiɱ pi ghānasmiɱ anapekho hoti,||
anāgataɱ ghānaɱ n'ābhinandati,||
pacc'uppannassa ghānassa||
nibbidāya||
virāgāya||
nirodhāya paṭipanno hoti.|| ||

■

Jivhā anattaɱ atīt-ā-nāgatā,||
ko pana vādo pacc'uppannāya.|| ||

Evaɱ passaɱ bhikkhave sutavā ariya-sāvako atītāya jivhāya anapekho hoti,||
anāgataɱ jivhaɱ n'ābhinandati,||
pacc'uppannāya jivhāya||
nibbidāya||
virāgāya||
nirodhāya paṭipanno hoti.|| ||

■

Kāyo anattaɱ atīt-ā-nāgato,||
ko pana vādo pacc'uppannassa.|| ||

Evaɱ passaɱ bhikkhave sutavā ariya-sāvako atītasmiɱ pi kāyasmiɱ anapekho hoti,||
anāgataɱ kāyaɱ n'ābhinandati,||
pacc'uppannassa kāyassa||
nibbidāya||
virāgāya||
nirodhāya paṭipanno hoti.|| ||

■

Mano anattaɱ atīt-ā-nāgato,||
ko pana vādo pacc'uppannassa.|| ||

Evaɱ passaɱ bhikkhave sutavā ariya-sāvako atītasmiɱ manasmiɱ anapekho hoti,||
anāgataɱ manaɱ n'ābhinandati,||
pacc'uppannassa manassa||
nibbidāya||
virāgāya||
nirodhāya paṭipanno hoti" ti.|| ||
