Saɱyutta Nikāya:
IV. Saḷāyatana Vagga
35: Saḷāyatana Saɱyutta
Mūlapaññāsa
1. Anicca Vagga

Sutta 5

Bāhira Dukkha Suttaɱ (Dukkham 2; Bāhiram)

Adapted from the 1995 edition of the digital version of the Sri Lanka Buddha Jayanti Tripitaka Series.

 

[3]

[1][pts][bodh] Evaɱ me sutaɱ:|| ||

Ekaɱ samayaɱ Bhagavā Sāvatthiyaɱ viharati Jetavane Anāthapiṇḍikassa ārāme.|| ||

2. Tatra kho bhagavā bhikkhū āmantesi|| ||

"Bhikkhavo" ti.|| ||

"Bhadante" ti||
te bhikkhū bhagavato paccassosuɱ.|| ||

3. Bhagavā etad avoca:|| ||

Rūpaɱ bhikkhave dukkha,||
yaɱ dukkhaɱ tad anattā,||
yad anattā taɱ:|| ||

'N'etaɱ mama,||
n'eso ham asmi,||
na me'so attā' ti.|| ||

Evam etaɱ yathā-bhūtaɱ samma-p-paññāya daṭṭhabbaɱ. || ||

■

4. Saddā dukkha,||
yaɱ dukkhaɱ tad anattā,||
yad anattā taɱ:|| ||

'N'etaɱ mama,||
n'eso ham asmi,||
na me'so attā' ti.|| ||

Evam etaɱ yathā-bhūtaɱ samma-p-paññāya daṭṭhabbaɱ. || ||

■

5. Gandhā dukkha,||
yaɱ dukkhaɱ tad anattā,||
yad anattā taɱ:|| ||

'N'etaɱ mama,||
n'eso ham asmi,||
na me'so attā' ti.|| ||

Evam etaɱ yathā-bhūtaɱ samma-p-paññāya daṭṭhabbaɱ. || ||

■

6. Rasā dukkha,||
yaɱ dukkhaɱ tad anattā,||
yad anattā taɱ:|| ||

'N'etaɱ mama,||
n'eso ham asmi,||
na me'so attā' ti.|| ||

Evam etaɱ yathā-bhūtaɱ samma-p-paññāya daṭṭhabbaɱ. || ||

■

7. Poṭṭhabbā dukkha,||
yaɱ dukkhaɱ tad anattā,||
yad anattā taɱ:|| ||

'N'etaɱ mama,||
n'eso ham asmi,||
na me'so attā' ti.|| ||

Evam etaɱ yathā-bhūtaɱ samma-p-paññāya daṭṭhabbaɱ. || ||

■

8. Dhammā dukkha,||
yaɱ dukkhaɱ tad anattā,||
yad anattā taɱ:|| ||

'N'etaɱ mama,||
n'eso ham asmi,||
na me'so attā' ti.|| ||

Evam etaɱ yathā-bhūtaɱ samma-p-paññāya daṭṭhabbaɱ. || ||

9. Evaɱ passaɱ bhikkhave sutavā ariya-sāvako rūpesu pi nibbindati,||
saddesu pi nibbindati,||
gandhesu pi nibbindati,||
rasesu pi nibbindati,||
phoṭṭhabbesu pi nibbindati,||
dhammesu pi nibbindati,||
nibbindaɱ virajjati,||
virāgā vimuccati,||
vimuttasmiɱ vimuttamiti ñāṇaɱ hoti:|| ||

'Khīṇā jāti,||
vusitaɱ Brahma-cariyaɱ,||
kataɱ karaṇīyaɱ,||
nāparaɱ itthattāyā' ti||
pajānātī" ti.|| ||
