Saɱyutta Nikāya
4. Saḷāyatana Vagga
40. Moggallāna Saɱyutta

Sutta 10

Sakka Suttaɱ

Adapted from the 1995 edition of the digital version of the Sri Lanka Buddha Jayanti Tripitaka Series.

 

[269]

[1][pts] Evaɱ me sutaɱ:|| ||

Ekaɱ samayaɱ āyasmā Mahā Moggallāno Sāvatthiyaɱ viharati Jetavane Anāthapiṇḍikassa ārāme.|| ||

Atha kho āyasmā Mahā Moggallāno seyyathā pi nāma balavā puriso sammiñjitaɱ vā bāhaɱ pasāreyya pasāritaɱ vā bāhaɱ sammiñjeyya,||
evam eva Jetavane antara-hito devesu Tāvatiɱsesu pātur ahosi.|| ||

Atha kho Sakko devānam Indo pañcahi devatāsatehi saddhiɱ yen'āyasmā Mahā Moggallāno ten'upasankami,||
[270] upasankamitvā āyasmantaɱ Mahā Moggallānaɱ abhivādetvā eka-m-antaɱ aṭṭhāsi.|| ||

Eka-m-antaɱ ṭhitaɱ kho Sakkaɱ devānam Indaɱ āyasmā Mahā Moggallāno etad avoca:|| ||

"Sādhu kho devānam Inda Buddhaɱ saraṇa-gamanaɱ hoti,||
Buddha saraṇa-gamana-hetu kho devānam Inda||
evam idh'ekacce sattā||
kāyassa bhedā param māraṇā||
sugatiɱ saggaɱ lokaɱ upapajjanti.|| ||

-◦-

Sādhu kho devānam Inda Dhammaɱ saraṇa-gamanaɱ hoti,||
Dhammaɱ saraṇa-gamana-hetu kho devānam Inda||
evam idh'ekacce sattā||
kāyassa bhedā param māraṇā||
sugatiɱ saggaɱ lokaɱ upapajjanti.|| ||

-◦-

Sādhu kho devānam Inda Sanghaɱ saraṇa-gamanaɱ hoti,||
Sanghaɱ saraṇa-gamana-hetu kho devānam Inda||

kāyassa bhedā param māraṇā||
sugatiɱ saggaɱ lokaɱ upapajjantī" ti.|| ||
