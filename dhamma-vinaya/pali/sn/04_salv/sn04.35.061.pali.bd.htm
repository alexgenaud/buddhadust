Saɱyutta Nikāya:
IV. Saḷāyatana Vagga
35: Saḷāyatana Saɱyutta
Paññāsaka Dutiya
1. Avijjā Vagga

Sutta 61

Sabb'ūpādāna-Pariyādinna Suttaɱ

Adapted from the 1995 edition of the digital version of the Sri Lanka Buddha Jayanti Tripitaka Series.

 

 

[1][pts][bodh] Evaɱ me sutaɱ:|| ||

Ekaɱ samayaɱ Bhagavā Sāvatthiyaɱ viharati Jetavane Anāthapiṇḍikassa ārāme.|| ||

2. Tatra kho bhagavā bhikkhū āmantesi|| ||

"Bhikkhavo" ti.|| ||

"Bhadante" ti||
te bhikkhū bhagavato paccassosuɱ.|| ||

3. Bhagavā etad avoca:|| ||

Sabb'ūpādāna-pariyādinna vo bhikkhave dhammaɱ desissāmi.|| ||

Taɱ suṇātha.|| ||

Katamo ca bhikkhave sabb'ūpādāna-pariyādinna dhammo?|| ||

Cakkhuñ ca paṭicca rūpe ca uppajjati,||
cakkhu-viññāṇaɱ||
tiṇṇaɱ saŋgati phasso,||
phassa-paccayā vedanā.|| ||

Evaɱ [33] passaɱ bhikkhave sutavā ariya-sāvako||
cakkhusmim pi nibbindati,||
rūpesu pi nibbindati,||
cakkhu-viññāṇe pi nibbindati,||
cakkhu-samphasse pi nibbindati,||
vedanāya pi nibbindati.|| ||

Nibbindaɱ virajjati,||
virāgā vimuccati||
vimokkhā, 'Pariññātaɱ me upādānan,' ti pajānāti.|| ||

■

Sotañ ca paṭicca sadde ca uppajjati,||
sota-viññāṇaɱ||
tiṇṇaɱ saŋgati phasso,||
phassa-paccayā vedanā.|| ||

Evaɱ passaɱ bhikkhave sutavā ariya-sāvako||
sotasmim pi nibbindati,||
saddesu pi nibbindati,||
sota viññāṇe pi nibbindati,||
sota-samphasse pi nibbindati,||
vedanāya pi nibbindati.|| ||

Nibbindaɱ virajjati,||
virāgā vimuccati||
vimokkhā, 'Pariññātaɱ me upādānan,' ti pajānāti.|| ||

■

Ghānañ ca paṭicca gandhe ca uppajjati,||
ghāna-viññāṇaɱ||
tiṇṇaɱ saŋgati phasso,||
phassa-paccayā vedanā.|| ||

Evaɱ passaɱ bhikkhave sutavā ariya-sāvako||
ghānasmim pi nibbindati,||
gandhesu pi nibbindati,||
ghāna-viññāṇe pi nibbindati,||
ghāna-samphasse pi nibbindati,||
vedanāya pi nibbindati.|| ||

Nibbindaɱ virajjati,||
virāgā vimuccati||
vimokkhā, 'Pariññātaɱ me upādānan,' ti pajānāti.|| ||

■

Jivhañ ca paṭicca rase ca uppajjati,||
jivhā-viññāṇaɱ||
tiṇṇaɱ saŋgati phasso,||
phassa-paccayā vedanā.|| ||

Evaɱ passaɱ bhikkhave sutavā ariya-sāvako||
jivhāya pi nibbindati,||
rasesu pi nibbindati,||
jivhā-viññāṇe pi nibbindati,||
jivhā-samphasse pi nibbindati,||
vedanāya pi nibbindati.|| ||

Nibbindaɱ virajjati,||
virāgā vimuccati||
vimokkhā, 'Pariññātaɱ me upādānan,' ti pajānāti.|| ||

■

Kāyañ ca paṭicca phoṭṭhabbe ca uppajjati,||
kāya-viññāṇaɱ||
tiṇṇaɱ saŋgati phasso,||
phassa-paccayā vedanā.|| ||

Evaɱ passaɱ bhikkhave sutavā ariya-sāvako||
kāye pi nibbindati,||
phoṭṭhabbesu pi nibbindati,||
kāya-viññāṇe pi nibbindati,||
kāya-samphasse pi nibbindati,||
vedanāya pi nibbindati.|| ||

Nibbindaɱ virajjati,||
virāgā vimuccati||
vimokkhā, 'Pariññātaɱ me upādānan,' ti pajānāti.|| ||

■

Manañ ca paṭicca dhamme ca uppajjati,||
mano-viññāṇaɱ||
tiṇṇaɱ saŋgati phasso,||
phassa-paccayā vedanā.|| ||

Evaɱ passaɱ bhikkhave sutavā ariya-sāvako||
manasmim pi nibbindati,||
dhammesu pi nibbindati,||
mano-viññāṇe pi nibbindati,||
Mano-samphasse pi nibbindati,||
vedanāya pi nibbindati.|| ||

Nibbindaɱ virajjati,||
virāgā vimuccati||
vimokkhā, 'Pariññātaɱ me upādānan,' ti pajānāti.|| ||

■|| ||

Ayaɱ kho bhikkhave sabb'ūpādāna-pariyādinna dhammo" ti.|| ||
