Saɱyutta Nikāya:
IV. Saḷāyatana Vagga
35: Saḷāyatana Saɱyutta
Mūlapaññāsa
3. Sabba Vagga

Sutta 23

Sabba Suttaɱ

Adapted from the 1995 edition of the digital version of the Sri Lanka Buddha Jayanti Tripitaka Series.

 

[15]

[1][pts][than][olds][bodh] Evaɱ me sutaɱ:|| ||

Ekaɱ samayaɱ Bhagavā Sāvatthiyaɱ viharati Jetavane Anāthapiṇḍikassa ārāme.|| ||

Tatra kho Bhagavā bhikkhū āmantesi Bhikkhaveti.|| ||

Sabbaɱ vo bhikkhave desissāmi||
taɱ suṇātha.|| ||

Kiñca bhikkhave sabbaɱ?|| ||

Cakkhuñ c'eva rūpā ca||
sotañ ca saddā ca||
ghānañca gandhā ca||
jivhā ca rasā ca||
kāyo ca phoṭṭhabbā ca||
mano ca dhammā ca.|| ||

Idaɱ vuccati bhikkhave sabbaɱ.|| ||

Yo bhikkhave evaɱ vadeyya:|| ||

Aham etaɱ sabbaɱ pacca-k-khāya aññaɱ sabbaɱ paññāpessāmīti,||
tassā vācāvatthur ev'assa||
puṭṭho ca na sampāyeyya,||
uttariñ ca vighātaɱ āpajjeyya.|| ||

Taɱ kissa hetu?|| ||

Yathā taɱ bhikkhave avisayasminti.|| ||
