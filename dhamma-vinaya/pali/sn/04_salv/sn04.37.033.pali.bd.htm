IV. Saḷāyatana Vagga
37: Mātu-gāma Saɱyutta

Sutta 33

Visāradavāda Suttaɱ

Adapted from the 1995 edition of the digital version of the Sri Lanka Buddha Jayanti Tripitaka Series.

 

 

[1][pts] Evaɱ me sutaɱ:|| ||

Ekaɱ samayaɱ Bhagavā bhikkhū āmantesi|| ||

"Bhikkhavo" ti.|| ||

"Bhadante" ti||
te bhikkhū bhagavato paccassosuɱ.|| ||

3. Bhagavā etad avoca:|| ||

"Pañcahi bhikkhave dhammehi samannāgato mātu-gāmo visārado agāraɱ ajjhāvasati.|| ||

Katamehi pañcanahi?|| ||

Pāṇāti-pātā paṭivirato ca hoti,||
adinn'ādānā paṭivirato ca hoti,||
kāmesu micchā-cārā paṭivirato ca hoti,||
musā-vādā paṭivirato ca hoti,||
surā-mera-yamajja-pamā-daṭṭhānā paṭivirato ca hoti.|| ||

Imehi kho bhikkhave pañcahi dhammehi samannāgato mātu-gāmo visārado agāraɱ ajjhāvasatī" ti.|| ||
