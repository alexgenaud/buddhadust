Dutiya Mārapāsa Suttaɱ

Adapted from the 1995 edition of the digital version of the Sri Lanka Buddha Jayanti Tripitaka Series.

[1][pts][than][bodh] Evaɱ me sutaɱ:|| ||

Ekaɱ samayaɱ Bhagavā Sāvatthiyaɱ viharati Jetavane Anāthapiṇḍikassa ārāme.|| ||

iṭṭhā||
kantā||
manāpā||

kām'ūpasaɱ-hitā||
rajanīyā.|| ||

abhivadati||
ajjhosāya||
tiṭṭhati,||
ayaɱ vuccati bhikkhave bhikkhu||
baddho cakkhu-viññeyyesu rūpesu,
āvāsagato Mārassa,||
Mārassa vasaŋgato.|| ||

Paṭimukkassa Māra-pāso,||
baddho so Māra-bandhanena||
yathākāmakaraṇīyo pāpimato.|| ||
