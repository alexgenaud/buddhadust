Saɱyutta Nikāya
4. Saḷāyatana Vagga
39. Sāmaṇḍaka Saɱyutta

Sutta 16

Dukkaraɱ Suttaɱ

Adapted from the 1995 edition of the digital version of the Sri Lanka Buddha Jayanti Tripitaka Series.

 

[262]

[1][pts] Evaɱ me sutaɱ:|| ||

Ekaɱ samayaɱ āyasmā Sāriputto vajjīsu Viharati||
Ukkavelāyaɱ Gaŋgāya nadiyā tīre.|| ||

Atha kho Sāmaṇḍakāni paribbājako yen'āyasmā Sāriputto ten'upasankami.|| ||

Upasaŋkamitvā āyasmatā Sāriputtena saddhiɱ sammodi.|| ||

Sammodanīyaɱ kathaɱ sārāṇīyaɱ vīti-sāretvā eka-m-antaɱ nisīdi.|| ||

Eka-m-antaɱ nisinno kho Sāmaṇḍakāni paribbājako āyasmantaɱ Sāriputtaɱ etad avoca:|| ||

"Kin nu kho āvuso Sāriputta imasmiɱ Dhamma-Vinaye dukkaran" ti?|| ||

"Pabbajjā kho āvuso imasmiɱ Dhamma-Vinaye dukkarā" ti.|| ||

"Pabbajitena pan'āvuso kiɱ dukkaran" ti?|| ||

"Pabbajitena kho āvuso abhirati dukkarā" ti.|| ||

"Abhiratena pana āvuso kiɱ dukkaran" ti?|| ||

"Abhiratena kho āvuso Dhamm-ā-nu-Dhamma-paṭipatti dukkarā" ti.|| ||

"Kiɱ va ciraɱ pan'āvuso Dhamm-ā-nu-Dhamma-paṭipanno bhikkhu arahaɱ assā" ti?|| ||

"Na ciraɱ āvuso" ti.|| ||
