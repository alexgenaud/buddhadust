Saɱyutta Nikāya
IV. Saḷāyatana Vagga
35: Saḷāyatana Saɱyutta
Paññāsaka Dutiya
5. Saḷa Vagga

Sutta 96

Parihāna-dhamma Suttaɱ

Adapted from the 1995 edition of the digital version of the Sri Lanka Buddha Jayanti Tripitaka Series.

 

[76]

[1][pts][bodh][olds] Ekaɱ samayaɱ Bhagavā Rājagahe viharati Veḷuvane Kalandakanivāpe|| ||

[2] Parihānadhammañ ca vo bhikkhave, desissāmi aparihānadhammañ ca cha ca abhibhāyatanāni.|| ||

[3] Kathañ ca bhikkhave parihāna-dhammo hoti.|| ||

[4] Idha, bhikkhave,||
bhikkhuno cakkhunā rūpaɱ disvā uppajjanti pāpakā akusalā dhammā sarasaŋkappā saŋyojanīyā.|| ||

Tañ ce bhikkhu adhivāseti na pajahati na vinodeti na vyantī-karoti na anabhāvaɱ gameti.||
Veditabbam etaɱ bhikkhave bhikkhunā,||
parihāyāmi kusalehi dhammehi||
parihānaɱ h'etaɱ vuttaɱ Bhagavatā ti.|| ||

[5] Puna ca paraɱ bhikkhave,||
bhikkhuno sotena saddaɱ sutvā uppajjanti pāpakā akusalā dhammā sarasaŋkappā saŋyojanīyā.|| ||

Tañ ce bhikkhū adhivāseti na pajahati na vinodeti na vyantī-karoti na anabhāvaɱ gameti.||
Veditabbam etaɱ bhikkhave bhikkhunā,||
parihāyāmi kusalehi dhammehi,||
parihānaɱ h'etaɱ vuttaɱ Bhagavatā ti.|| ||

[6] Puna ca paraɱ bhikkhave,||
bhikkhuno ghānena gandhaɱ ghāyitvā uppajjanti pāpakā akusalā dhammā sarasaŋkappā saŋyojanīyā.|| ||

Tañ ce bhikkhū adhivāseti na pajahati na vinodeti na vyantī-karoti na anabhāvaɱ gameti.||
Veditabbam etaɱ bhikkhave bhikkhunā,||
parihāyāmi kusalehi dhammehi,||
parihānaɱ h'etaɱ vuttaɱ Bhagavatā ti.|| ||

[7] Puna ca paraɱ bhikkhave,||
bhikkhuno jivhāya rasaɱ sāyitvā uppajjanti pāpakā akusalā dhammā sarasaŋkappā saŋyojanīyā.|| ||

Tañ ce bhikkhū adhivāseti na pajahati na vinodeti na vyantī-karoti na anabhāvaɱ gameti.||
Veditabbam etaɱ bhikkhave bhikkhunā,||
parihāyāmi kusalehi dhammehi,||
parihānaɱ h'etaɱ vuttaɱ Bhagavatā ti.|| ||

[8] Puna ca paraɱ bhikkhave,||
bhikkhuno kāyena phoṭṭhabbaɱ sutvā uppajjanti pāpakā akusalā dhammā sarasaŋkappā saŋyojanīyā.|| ||

Tañ ce bhikkhū adhivāseti na pajahati na vinodeti na vyantī-karoti na anabhāvaɱ gameti.||
Veditabbam etaɱ bhikkhave bhikkhunā,||
parihāyāmi kusalehi dhammehi,||
parihānaɱ h'etaɱ vuttaɱ Bhagavatā ti.|| ||

[9] Puna ca paraɱ bhikkhave, bhikkhuno manasā dhammaɱ [77] viññāya uppajjanti pāpakā akusalā dhammā sarasaŋkappā saŋyojanīyā.|| ||

Tañ ce bhikkhū adhivāseti na pajahati na vinodeti na vyantī-karoti na anabhāvaɱ gameti.||
Veditabbam etaɱ bhikkhave bhikkhunā,||
parihāyāmi kusalehi dhammehi,||
parihānaɱ h'etaɱ vuttaɱ Bhagavatā ti.|| ||

[10] Evaɱ kho bhikkhave parihāna dhammo hoti.|| ||

[11] Kathañ ca bhikkhave aparihāna-dhammo hoti?|| ||

[12] Idha, bhikkhave, bhikkhuno cakkhunā rūpaɱ disvā ūppajjanti pāpakā akusalā dhammā sarasaŋkappā saŋyojanīyā.|| ||

Tañ ce bhikkhave bhikkhū nādhi-vāseti na pajahati na vinodeti na vyantī-karoti na anabhāvaɱ gameti.|| Veditabbam etaɱ bhikkhave bhikkhunā
na parihāyāmi kusalehi dhammehi
aparihānaɱ h'etaɱ vuttam Bhagavatā ti|| ||

[13] Puna ca paraɱ bhikkhave,||
bhikkhuno sotena saddaɱ sutvā uppajjanti pāpakā akusalā dhammā sarasaŋkappā saŋyojanīyā.

Tañ ce bhikkhave bhikkhū nādhi-vāseti na pajahati na vinodeti na vyantī-karoti na anabhāvaɱ gameti.|| Veditabbam etaɱ bhikkhave bhikkhunā
na parihāyāmi kusalehi dhammehi
aparihānaɱ h'etaɱ vuttam Bhagavatā ti|| ||

[14] Puna ca paraɱ bhikkhave,||
bhikkhuno ghānena gandhaɱ ghāyitvā uppajjanti pāpakā akusalā dhammā sarasaŋkappā saŋyojanīyā.|| ||

Tañ ce bhikkhave bhikkhū nādhi-vāseti na pajahati na vinodeti na vyantī-karoti na anabhāvaɱ gameti.|| Veditabbam etaɱ bhikkhave bhikkhunā
na parihāyāmi kusalehi dhammehi
aparihānaɱ h'etaɱ vuttam Bhagavatā ti|| ||

[15] Puna ca paraɱ bhikkhave,||
bhikkhuno jivhāya rasaɱ sāyitvā uppajjanti pāpakā akusalā dhammā sarasaŋkappā saŋyojanīyā.|| ||

Tañ ce bhikkhave bhikkhū nādhi-vāseti na pajahati na vinodeti na vyantī-karoti na anabhāvaɱ gameti.|| Veditabbam etaɱ bhikkhave bhikkhunā
na parihāyāmi kusalehi dhammehi
aparihānaɱ h'etaɱ vuttam Bhagavatā ti|| ||

[16] Puna ca paraɱ bhikkhave,||
bhikkhuno kāyena phoṭṭhabbaɱ sutvā uppajjanti pāpakā akusalā dhammā sarasaŋkappā saŋyojanīyā.|| ||

Tañ ce bhikkhave bhikkhū nādhi-vāseti na pajahati na vinodeti na vyantī-karoti na anabhāvaɱ gameti.|| Veditabbam etaɱ bhikkhave bhikkhunā
na parihāyāmi kusalehi dhammehi
aparihānaɱ h'etaɱ vuttam Bhagavatā ti|| ||

[17] Puna ca paraɱ bhikkhave, bhikkhuno manasā dhammaɱ viññāya uppajjanti pāpakā akusalā dhammā sarasaŋkappā saŋyojanīyā.|| ||

Tañ ce bhikkhave bhikkhū nādhi-vāseti na pajahati na vinodeti na vyantī-karoti na anabhāvaɱ gameti.|| Veditabbam etaɱ bhikkhave bhikkhunā
na parihāyāmi kusalehi dhammehi
aparihānaɱ h'etaɱ vuttam Bhagavatā ti|| ||

[18] Evam kho bhikkhave aparihāna-dhammo hoti|| ||

[19] Katamāni ca bhikkhave cha abhibhāyatanāni.|| ||

[20] Idha, bhikkhave, bhikkhuno cakkhunā rūpaɱ disvā nuppajjanti pāpakā akusalā dhammā sarasaŋkappā saŋyojaniyā|| ||

Veditabbam etam bhikkhave bhikkhunā abhibhūtam etam āyatanaɱ||
abhibhāyatanaɱ h'etaɱ vuttam Bhagavatā ti

[21] Puna ca paraɱ bhikkhave bhikkhuno sotena saddaɱ sutvā nūppajjanti pāpakā akusalā dhammā sarasaŋkappā saŋyojanīyā.|| ||

Veditabbam etaɱ bhikkhave bhikkhunā abhibhūtam etaɱ āyatanaɱ,||
abhibhāyatanaɱ h'etaɱ vuttaɱ Bhagavatā ti.|| ||

[22] Puna ca paraɱ bhikkhave bhikkhuno ghānena gandhaɱ gāyitvā nūppajjanti pāpakā akusalā dhammā sarasaŋkappā saŋyojanīyā.|| ||

Veditabbam etaɱ bhikkhave bhikkhunā abhibhūtam etaɱ āyatanaɱ, abhibhāyatanaɱ h'etaɱ vuttaɱ Bhagavatā ti.|| ||

[23] Puna ca paraɱ bhikkhave bhikkhuno jivhāya rasaɱ sāyitvā nūpajjanti pāpakā akusalā dhammā sarasaŋkappā saŋyojanīyā.|| ||

Veditabbam etaɱ bhikkhave bhikkhunā abhibhūtam etaɱ āyatanaɱ,||
abhibhāyatanaɱ h'etaɱ vuttaɱ Bhagavatā ti.|| ||

[24] Puna ca paraɱ bhikkhave bhikkhuno kāyena phoṭṭhabbaɱ phusitvā nūpajjanti pāpakā akusalā dhammā sarasaŋkappā saŋyojanīyā.|| ||

Veditabbam etaɱ bhikkhave bhikkhunā abhibhūtam etaɱ āyatanaɱ,||
abhibhāyatanaɱ h'etaɱ vuttaɱ Bhagavatā ti.|| ||

[25] Puna ca paraɱ bhikkhave bhikkhuno manasā dhammaɱ viññāya nūpajjanti pāpakā akusalā dhammā sarasaŋkappā saŋyojanīyā.|| ||

Veditabbam etaɱ bhikkhave bhikkhunā abhibhūtam etaɱ āyatanaɱ,||
abhibhāyatanaɱ h'etaɱ vuttaɱ Bhagavatā ti.|| ||

[26] Imāni vuccanti bhikkhave cha abhibhāyatanānī ti.|| ||
