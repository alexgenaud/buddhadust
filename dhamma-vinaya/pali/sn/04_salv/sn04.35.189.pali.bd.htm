Sutta 189

Bālisik'Opama Suttaṃ

Adapted from the 1995 edition of the digital version of the Sri Lanka Buddha Jayanti Tripitaka Series.

[1][pts][than][bodh] Evaṃ me sutaṃ:|| ||

Ekaṃ samayaṃ Bhagavā bhikkhū āmantesi|| ||

3. Bhagavā etad avoca:[ed1]|| ||

"Seyyathā pi, bhikkhave,||
bālisiko āmisa-gataṃ||
bālisaṃ gambhīre udaka-rahade pakkhipeyya,||
tam enaṃ añña-[159] taro āmisacakkhu maccho gileyya;||
evaṃ hi so bhikkhave maccho gilitabaḷiso||
bālisikassa anayaṃ āpanno||
vyasanaṃ āpanno||
yathā-kāma-karaṇīyo bālisikassa.|| ||

Evam eva kho bhikkhave cha yime baḷisā lokasmiṃ anayāya sattāṇaṃ,||
vyābādhāya pāṇinaṃ.|| ||

Katame cha?|| ||
