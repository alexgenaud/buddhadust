Saɱyutta Nikāya
4. Saḷāyatana Vagga
35. Saḷāyatana Saɱyutta
§ IV: Paññāsaka Catuttha
3. Samudda Vagga

Sutta 192

Kāmabhū Suttaɱ

Adapted from the 1995 edition of the digital version of the Sri Lanka Buddha Jayanti Tripitaka Series.

 

[165]

[1][pts][bodh] Evaɱ me sutaɱ:|| ||

Ekaɱ samayaɱ āyasmā ca Ānando āyasmā ca Kāmabhū Bārāṇasiyaɱ viharanti||
Isipatane Migadāye.|| ||

[2] Atha kho āyasmā Kāmabhū sāyaṇha-samayaɱ paṭisallāṇā vuṭṭhito yen āyasmā Ānando ten'upasaṅkami.|| ||

Upasaṅkamitvā āyasmatā Ānando saddhiɱ sammodi,||
sammodanīyaɱ kathaɱ sārāṇīyaɱ vīti-sāretvā eka-m-antaɱ nisīdi.|| ||

[3] Eka-m-antaɱ nisinno kho āyasmā Kāmabhū āyasmantaɱ Ānando etad avoca:|| ||

"Kin nu kho āvuso Ānanda||
cakkhu rūpānaɱ saɱyojanaɱ rūpā cakkhussa saɱyojanaɱ?

Sotaɱ saddānaɱ saɱyojanaɱ||
saddā sotassa saɱyojanaɱ?

Ghānaɱ gandhānaɱ saɱyojanaɱ||
gandhā ghānassa saɱyojanaɱ?

Jivhā rasānaɱ saɱyojanaɱ||
rasā jivhāya saɱyojanaɱ?

Kāyo phoṭṭhabbānaɱ saɱyojanaɱ||
phoṭṭhabbā kāyassa saɱyojanaɱ?

Mano dhammānaɱ saɱyojanaɱ||
dhammā manassa saɱyojanan" ti?|| ||

■

[4] "Na kho āvuso Kāmabhū,||
cakkhu rūpānaɱ saɱyojanaɱ,||
na rūpā cakkhussa saɱyojanaɱ.|| ||

Yaṇ ca tattha tad ubhayaɱ paṭicca uppajjati chanda-rāgo||
taɱ tattha saɱyojanaɱ.|| ||

Sota saddānaɱ saɱyojanaɱ,||
na saddā sotassa saɱyojanaɱ.|| ||

Yaṇ ca tattha tad ubhayaɱ paṭicca uppajjati chanda-rāgo||
taɱ tattha saɱyojanaɱ.|| ||

Ghāna ghandhānaɱ saɱyojanaɱ,||
na gandhā ghānassa saɱyojanaɱ.|| ||

Yaṇ ca tattha tad ubhayaɱ paṭicca uppajjati chanda-rāgo||
taɱ tattha saɱyojanaɱ.|| ||

Jivhā rasānaɱ saɱyojanaɱ,||
na rasā jivhāya saɱyojanaɱ.|| ||

Yaṇ ca tattha tad ubhayaɱ paṭicca uppajjati chanda-rāgo||
taɱ tattha saɱyojanaɱ.|| ||

Kāyo phoṭṭhabbānaɱ saɱyojanaɱ,||
na phoṭṭhabbā kāyassa saɱyojanaɱ.|| ||

Yaṇ ca tattha tad ubhayaɱ paṭicca uppajjati chanda-rāgo||
taɱ tattha saɱyojanaɱ.|| ||

Mano dhammānaɱ saɱyojanaɱ,||
na dhammā manassa saɱyojanaɱ.|| ||

Yaṇ ca tattha tad ubhayaɱ paṭicca uppajjati chanda-rāgo||
taɱ tattha saɱyojanaɱ.|| ||

 

§

 

[166] [5] Seyyathā pi āvuso kāḷo ca balivaddo||
odāto ca balivaddo||
ekena dāmena vā||
yottena vā saɱyuttāassu.|| ||

Yo nu kho evaɱ vadeyya:||
kāḷo balivaddo odātassa balivaddassa saɱyojanaɱ,||
odāto balivaddo kāḷassa balivaddassa saɱyojanan ti||
sammā nu kho so vadamāno vadeyyā" ti?|| ||

"No h'etaɱ āvuso."|| ||

"Na kho āvuso kāḷo balivaddo||
odātassa balivaddassa saɱyojanaɱ,||
na pi odāto balivaddo kāḷassa||
balivaddassa saɱyojanaɱ,||
yena ca kho te ekena dāmena vā||
yottena vā saɱyuttā,||
taɱ tattha saɱyojanaɱ.|| ||

Evam eva kho āvuso na cakkhu rūpānaɱ saɱyojanaɱ,||
na rūpā cakkhussa saɱyojanaɱ.|| ||

Yaṇ ca tattha tad ubhayaɱ paṭicca uppajjati chanda-rāgo||
taɱ tattha saɱyojanaɱ.|| ||

Na sotaɱ saddānaɱ saɱyojanaɱ,||
na saddā sotassa saɱyojanaɱ.|| ||

Yaṇ ca tattha tad ubhayaɱ paṭicca uppajjati chanda-rāgo||
taɱ tattha saɱyojanaɱ.|| ||

Na ghānaɱ gandhānaɱ saɱyojanaɱ,||
na gandhā ghānassa saɱyojanaɱ.|| ||

Yaṇ ca tattha tad ubhayaɱ paṭicca uppajjati chanda-rāgo||
taɱ tattha saɱyojanaɱ.|| ||

Na jivhā rasānaɱ saɱyojanaɱ,||
na rasā jivhāya saɱyojanaɱ.|| ||

Yaṇ ca tattha tad ubhayaɱ paṭicca uppajjati chanda-rāgo||
taɱ tattha saɱyojanaɱ.|| ||

Na kayo phoṭṭhabbānaɱ saɱyojanaɱ,||
na phoṭṭhabbā kāyassa saɱyojanaɱ.|| ||

Yañ ca tattha tad ubhayaɱ paṭicca uppajjati chanda-rāgo||
taɱ tattha saɱyojanaɱ.|| ||

Na mano dhammānaɱ saɱyojanaɱ,||
na dhammā manassa saɱyojanaɱ.|| ||

Yaṇ ca tattha tad ubhayaɱ paṭicca uppajjati chanda-rāgo||
taɱ tattha saɱyojanaɱ.|| ||

 

§

 

[6] Cakkhuɱ vā āvuso rūpānaɱ saɱyojanaɱ abhavissa,||
rūpā vā cakkhussa saɱyojanaɱ,||
na idaɱ brahma-cariy-a-vāso paññāyetha sammā dukkha-k-khayāya.|| ||

Yasmā ca kho āvuso||
na cakkhu rūpānaɱ saɱyojanaɱ||
na rūpā cakkhussa saɱyojanaɱ,||
yañ ca tattha tad ubhayaɱ paṭicca uppajjati chanda-rāgo||
taɱ tattha saɱyojanaɱ,||
tasmā brahma-cariy-a-vāso paññāyati sammā dukkhayāya.|| ||

■

Sotaɱ vā āvuso saddānaɱ saɱyojanaɱ abhavissa,||
saddā vā sotassa saɱyojanaɱ,||
na idaɱ brahma-cariy-a-vāso paññāyetha sammā dukkha-k-khayāya.|| ||

Yasmā ca kho āvuso||
na sota saddānaɱ saɱyojanaɱ||
na saddā sotassa saɱyojanaɱ,||
yañ ca tattha tad ubhayaɱ paṭicca uppajjati chanda-rāgo||
taɱ tattha saɱyojanaɱ,||
tasmā brahma-cariy-a-vāso paññāyati sammā dukkhayāya.|| ||

■

Ghānaɱ vā āvuso gandhānaɱ saɱyojanaɱ abhavissa,||
gandhā vā ghānassa saɱyojanaɱ,||
na idaɱ brahma-cariy-a-vāso paññāyetha sammā dukkha-k-khayāya.|| ||

Yasmā ca kho āvuso||
na ghānaɱ gandhānaɱ saɱyojanaɱ||
na gandhā ghānassa saɱyojanaɱ,||
yañ ca tattha tad ubhayaɱ paṭicca uppajjati chanda-rāgo||
taɱ tattha saɱyojanaɱ,||
tasmā brahma-cariy-a-vāso paññāyati sammā dukkhayāya.|| ||

■

Jivhā vā āvuso rasānaɱ saɱyojanaɱ abhavissa,||
rasā vā jivhāya saɱyojanaɱ,||
na idaɱ brahma-cariy-a-vāso paññāyetha sammā dukkha-k-khayāya.|| ||

Yasmā ca kho āvuso||
na jivhā rasānaɱ saɱyojanaɱ||
na rasā jivhāya saɱyojanaɱ,||
yañ ca tattha tad ubhayaɱ paṭicca uppajjati chanda-rāgo||
taɱ tattha saɱyojanaɱ,||
tasmā brahma-cariy-a-vāso paññāyati sammā dukkhayāya.|| ||

■

Kāyo vā āvuso phoṭṭhabbānaɱ saɱyojanaɱ abhavissa,||
phoṭṭhabbā vā kāyassa saɱyojanaɱ,||
na idaɱ brahma-cariy-a-vāso paññāyetha sammā dukkha-k-khayāya.|| ||

Yasmā ca kho āvuso||
na kāyo phoṭṭhabbānaɱ saɱyojanaɱ||
na phoṭṭhabbā kāyassa saɱyojanaɱ,||
yañ ca tattha tad ubhayaɱ paṭicca uppajjati chanda-rāgo||
taɱ tattha saɱyojanaɱ,||
tasmā brahma-cariy-a-vāso paññāyati sammā dukkhayāya.|| ||

■

Mano vā āvuso dhammānaɱ saɱyojanaɱ abhavissa,||
dhammā vā manassa saɱyojanaɱ,||
na idaɱ brahma-cariy-a-vāso paññāyetha sammā dukkha-k-khayāya.|| ||

Yasmā ca kho āvuso||
na mano dhammānaɱ saɱyojanaɱ||
na dhammā manassa saɱyojanaɱ,||
yañ ca tattha tad ubhayaɱ paṭicca uppajjati chanda-rāgo||
taɱ tattha saɱyojanaɱ,||
tasmā brahma-cariy-a-vāso paññāyati sammā dukkhayāya.|| ||

[8] Saṅvijjati kho āvuso Bhagavato cakkhu,||
passati Bhagavā cakkhunā rūpaɱ,||
chanda-rāgo Bhagavato n'atthi,||
su-vimutta-citto Bhagavā.|| ||

Saṅvijjati kho āvuso Bhagavato sota,||
passati Bhagavā sotena saddaɱ,||
chanda-rāgo Bhagavato n'atthi,||
su-vimutta-citto Bhagavā.|| ||

Saṅvijjati kho āvuso Bhagavato ghāna,||
passati Bhagavā ghānena gandhaɱ,||
chanda-rāgo Bhagavato n'atthi,||
su-vimutta-citto Bhagavā.|| ||

Saṅvijjati kho āvuso Bhagavato jivhā,||
sāyati Bhagavā jivhāya rasaɱ,||
chanda-rāgo Bhagavato n'atthi,||
su-vimutta-citto Bhagavā.|| ||

Saṅvijjati kho āvuso Bhagavato kāyo,||
sāyati Bhagavā kāyo phoṭṭhabbānaɱ,||
chanda-rāgo Bhagavato n'atthi,||
su-vimutta-citto Bhagavā.|| ||

Saṅvijjati kho āvuso Bhagavato mano,||
jānāti Bhagavā manasā dhammaɱ,||
chanda-rāgo Bhagavato n'atthi,||
su-vimutta-citto Bhagavā.|| ||
