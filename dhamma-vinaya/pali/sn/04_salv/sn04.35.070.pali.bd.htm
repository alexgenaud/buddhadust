Saɱyutta Nikāya:
IV. Saḷāyatana Vagga
35: Saḷāyatana Saɱyutta
Paññāsako Dutiyo
2. Migajāla Vagga

Sutta 70

Upavāṇa Suttaɱ

Adapted from the 1995 edition of the digital version of the Sri Lanka Buddha Jayanti Tripitaka Series.

 

[41]

[1][pts][bodh] Atha kho āyasmā Upavāṇo yena bhagavā tenupasaŋkami.|| ||

Upasan-kamitvā bhagavantaɱ abhivādetvā eka-m-antaɱ nisīdi.|| ||

Eka-m-antaɱ nisinno kho āyasmā Upavāṇo bhagavantaɱ etad avoca:|| ||

Sandiṭṭhiko dhammo!|| ||

Sandiṭṭhiko dhammo' ti||
bhante vuccati.|| ||

Kittāvatā nu kho bhante sandiṭṭhiko dhammo hoti,||
akāliko||
ehi passiko||
opanayiko||
paccattaɱ veditabbo viññūhī" ti?|| ||

Idha pana Upavāṇa bhikkhu cakkhunā rūpaɱ disvā rūpa-paṭisaɱvedī ca hoti||
rūpa-rāga-paṭisaɱvedī ca.|| ||

Santañ ca ajjhattaɱ rūpesu rāgaɱ:|| ||

'Atthi me ajjhattaɱ rūpesu rāgo' ti pajānāti.|| ||

Yantaɱ Upavāṇa bhikkhu cakkhunā rūpaɱ disvā rūpa-paṭisaɱvedī ca hoti,||
rūpa-rāga-paṭisaɱvedīca||
santañ ca ajjhattaɱ rūpesu rāgaɱ||
'Atthi me ajjhattaɱ rūpesu rāgo' ti pajānāti,||
evam pi kho Upavāṇa sandiṭṭhiko dhammo hoti||
akāliko||
ehi passiko||
opanayiko||
paccattaɱ veditabbo viññūhi.|| ||
