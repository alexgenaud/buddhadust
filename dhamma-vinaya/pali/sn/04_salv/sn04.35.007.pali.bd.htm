Saɱyutta Nikāya:
IV. Saḷāyatana Vagga
35: Saḷāyatana Saɱyutta
Mūlapaññāsa
1. Anicca Vagga

Sutta 7

Dutiya Ajjhatta Anicca Suttaɱ (Aniccam 3; Ajjhattam)

Adapted from the 1995 edition of the digital version of the Sri Lanka Buddha Jayanti Tripitaka Series.

 

[4]

[1][pts][bodh] Evaɱ me sutaɱ:|| ||

Ekaɱ samayaɱ Bhagavā Sāvatthiyaɱ viharati Jetavane Anāthapiṇḍikassa ārāme.|| ||

2. Tatra kho bhagavā bhikkhū āmantesi|| ||

"Bhikkhavo" ti.|| ||

"Bhadante" ti||
te bhikkhū bhagavato paccassosuɱ.|| ||

3. Bhagavā etad avoca:|| ||

Cakkhuɱ bhikkhave aniccaɱ atīt-ā-nāgataɱ,||
ko pana vādo paccuppannassa.|| ||

Evaɱ passaɱ bhikkhave sutavā ariya-sāvako atītasmiɱ pi cakkhusmiɱ anapekho hoti,||
anāgataɱ cakkhuɱ nābhinandati,||
paccappannassa cakkhussa||
nibbidāya||
virāgāya||
nirodhāya paṭipanno hoti.|| ||

Sotaɱ aniccaɱ atīt-ā-nāgataɱ,||
ko pana vādo paccuppannassa.|| ||

Evaɱ passaɱ bhikkhave sutavā ariya-sāvako atītasmiɱ pi sotasmiɱ anapekho hoti,||
anāgataɱ sotaɱ nābhinandati,||
paccappannassa sotassa||
nibbidāya||
virāgāya||
nirodhāya paṭipanno hoti.|| ||

Ghānaɱ aniccaɱ atīt-ā-nāgataɱ,||
ko pana vādo paccuppannassa.|| ||

Evaɱ passaɱ bhikkhave sutavā ariya-sāvako atītasmiɱ pi ghānasmiɱ anapekho hoti,||
anāgataɱ ghānaɱ nābhinandati,||
paccappannassa ghānassa||
nibbidāya||
virāgāya||
nirodhāya paṭipanno hoti.|| ||

Jivhā aniccā atītānāgatā,||
ko pana vādo paccuppannāya.|| ||

Evaɱ passaɱ bhikkhave sutavā ariya-sāvako atītāya jivhāya anapekho hoti,||
anāgataɱ jivhaɱ nābhinandati,||
paccuppannāya jivhāya||
nibbidāya||
virāgāya||
nirodhāya paṭipanno hoti.|| ||

Kāyo anicco atītānāgato,||
ko pana vādo paccuppannassa.|| ||

Evaɱ passaɱ bhikkhave sutavā ariya-sāvako atītasmiɱ pi kāyasmiɱ anapekho hoti,||
anāgataɱ kāyaɱ nābhinandati,||
paccappannassa kāyassa||
nibbidāya||
virāgāya||
nirodhāya paṭipanno hoti.|| ||

Mano anicco atītānāgato,||
ko pana vādo paccuppannassa.|| ||

Evaɱ passaɱ bhikkhave sutavā ariya-sāvako atītasmiɱ manasmiɱ anapekho hoti,||
anāgataɱ manaɱ nābhinandati,||
paccappannassa manassa||
nibbidāya||
virāgāya||
nirodhāya paṭipanno hoti.|| ||
