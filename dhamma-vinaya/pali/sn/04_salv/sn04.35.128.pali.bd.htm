Saṃyutta Nikāya
4. Saḷāyatana Vagga
35. Saḷāyatana Saṃyutta
§ III: Paññāsaka Tatiya
3. Gahapati Vagga

Sutta 128

Soṇa Suttaṃ

Adapted from the 1995 edition of the digital version of the Sri Lanka Buddha Jayanti Tripitaka Series.

 

[113]

[1][pts] Evaṃ me sutaṃ:|| ||

Ekaṃ samayaṃ Bhagavā Rājagahe viharati Vephavane Kalandakanivāpe.|| ||

Atha kho Soṇo gahapati-putto yena Bhagavā ten'upasaṅkami.|| ||

Upasan-kamitvā Bhagavantaṃ abhivādetvā eka-m-antaṃ aṭṭhāsi.|| ||

Eka-m-antaṃ nisinno kho Soṇo gahapati-putto Bhagavantaṃ etad avoca: || ||

Ko nu kho bhante hetu ko paccayo||
yena-m-idh'ekacce sattā diṭṭhe'va dhamme no parinibbāyanti?|| ||

Ko pana bhante hetu ko paccayo||
yena-m-idh'ekacce sattā diṭṭhe'va dhamme parinibbāyantī" ti?|| ||
