4. Saḷāyatana Vagga
35. Saḷāyatana Saɱyutta
§ I: Mūla-Paññāsa
2. Yamaka Vagga

Sutta 22

Dutiya Uppāda Suttaɱ

Adapted from the 1995 edition of the digital version of the Sri Lanka Buddha Jayanti Tripitaka Series.

 

 

[1][pts] Evaɱ me sutaɱ:|| ||

Ekaɱ samayaɱ Bhagavā Sāvatthiyaɱ viharati Jetavane Anāthapiṇḍikassa ārāme.|| ||

2. Tatra kho bhagavā bhikkhū āmantesi|| ||

"Bhikkhavo" ti.|| ||

"Bhadante" ti||
te bhikkhū bhagavato paccassosuɱ.|| ||

3. Bhagavā etad avoca:|| ||

"Yo bhikkhave rūpānaɱ uppādo||
ṭhiti||
abhinibbatti||
pātu-bhāvo,||
dukkhasseso uppādo||
rogānaɱ ṭhiti||
jarā-maraṇassa pātu-bhāvo.|| ||

Yo saddānaɱ uppādo||
ṭhiti||
abhinibbatti||
pātu-bhāvo,||
dukkhasseso uppādo||
rogānaɱ ṭhiti||
jarā-maraṇassa pātu-bhāvo.|| ||

Yo gandhānaɱ uppādo||
ṭhiti||
abhinibbatti||
pātu-bhāvo,||
dukkhasseso uppādo||
rogānaɱ ṭhiti||
jarā-maraṇassa pātu-bhāvo.|| ||

Yo rasānaɱ uppādo||
ṭhiti||
abhinibbatti||
pātu-bhāvo,||
dukkhasseso uppādo||
rogānaɱ ṭhiti||
jarā-maraṇassa pātu-bhāvo.|| ||

Yo phoṭṭhabbānaɱ uppādo||
ṭhiti||
abhinibbatti||
pātu-bhāvo,||
dukkhasseso uppādo||
rogānaɱ ṭhiti||
jarā-maraṇassa pātu-bhāvo.|| ||

Yo dhammānaɱ uppādo||
ṭhiti||
abhinibbatti||
pātu-bhāvo,||
dukkhasseso uppādo||
rogānaɱ ṭhiti||
jarā-maraṇassa pātu-bhāvo.|| ||
