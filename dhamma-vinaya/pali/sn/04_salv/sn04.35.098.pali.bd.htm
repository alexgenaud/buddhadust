Paññāsaka Dutiya
5. Saḷa Vagga

Sutta 98

Saɱvara Suttaɱ

Adapted from the 1995 edition of the digital version of the Sri Lanka Buddha Jayanti Tripitaka Series.

[1][pts][bodh] Evaɱ me sutaɱ:|| ||

Ekaɱ samayaɱ Bhagavā Sāvatthiyaɱ viharati.|| ||

2. Tatra kho bhagavā bhikkhū āmantesi|| ||

"Bhikkhavo" ti.|| ||

"Bhadante" ti||
te bhikkhū bhagavato paccassosuɱ.|| ||

3. Bhagavā etad avoca:|| ||

Saɱvarañ ca vo bhikkhave desissāmi asaŋvarañ ca.|| ||

Taɱ suṇātha.|| ||

Kathañ ca bhikkhave asaɱvaro hoti?|| ||

rūpā iṭṭhā||
kantā||
manāpā||

rajanīyā.|| ||

abhivadati||

'Parihāyāmi kusalehi dhammehi,||
parihānaɱ h'etaɱ vuttaɱ Bhagavatā' ti.|| ||
