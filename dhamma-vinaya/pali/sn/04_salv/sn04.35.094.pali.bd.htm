Sutta 94

Cha-Phassāyatana (Paṭhama Saŋgayya) Suttaɱ

Adapted from the 1995 edition of the digital version of the Sri Lanka Buddha Jayanti Tripitaka Series.

 

[70]

[1][pts][olds][bodh] Evaɱ me sutaɱ:|| ||

Ekaɱ samayaɱ Bhagavā Sāvatthiyaɱ viharati.|| ||

2. Tatra kho bhagavā bhikkhū āmantesi|| ||

"Bhikkhavo" ti.|| ||

"Bhadante" ti||
te bhikkhū bhagavato paccassosuɱ.|| ||

aguttā||
arakkhitā||
asaɱvutā||
dukkhā-dhivāhā honti.|| ||

adantaɱ||

adantaɱ||

sukhādhivāhā honti" ti.|| ||

Idam avoca bhagavā.|| ||

Idaɱ vatvā Sugato athāparaɱ etad avoca Satthā:|| ||

"Chaḷeva phass'āyatanāni bhikkhavo||
Asaɱvuto yattha dukkhaɱ nigacchati,||
Tesañ ca ye saŋvaraṇaɱ avediɱsu||
Saddhādutiyā viharantānavassutā.|| ||

Disvāna rūpāni manoramāni||
Atho pi disvā amanoramāni||
Manorame rāgapathaɱ vinodaye||
Na cappiyaɱ me ti manaɱ padosaye|| ||

[71] Saddañ ca sutavā dutiyaɱ piy-ā-p-piyaɱ||
Piyam hi sadde na samucchito siyā||
Athappiye dosagataɱ vinodaye||
Na cappiyaɱ me ti manaɱ padosaye|| ||

Gandhañ ca ghātvā surabhiɱ manoramaɱ||
Atho pi ghātvā asuciɱ akantiyaɱ||
Akantiyasmiɱ paṭighaɱ vinodaye||
Chandānunīto na ca kantiye siyā|| ||

Rasañ ca bhotvā sāditañ ca sāduɱ ca||
Atho pi bhotvāna asādum ekadā||
Sāduɱ rasaɱ nājjhosāya bhuñjati||
Virodhaɱ āsādusu no padaɱ saye|| ||

Phassena phuṭṭho na sukhena majje||
Dukkhena phuṭṭho pi na sampavedhe||
Phassadvayaɱ sukha-dukkhe upekkho||
Anānuruddho aviruddha kenaci|| ||

Papañca-saññā itarītarā narā||
Papañca-yantā upayanti saññino||
Mano-mayaɱ gehasitañ ca sabbaɱ||
Panujja nekkhammasitaɱ irīyati|| ||

Evaɱ mano chassu yadā subhāvito||
Phuṭṭhassa cittaɱ na vikampate kvaci||
Te rāgadose Abhibhuyya bhikkhavo||
Bhavātha jāti-maraṇassa pāragā" ti.|| ||
