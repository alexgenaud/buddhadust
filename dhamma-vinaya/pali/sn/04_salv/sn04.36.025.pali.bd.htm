Saṃyutta Nikāya
4. Saḷāyatana Vagga
36. Vedanā Saṃyutta
3. Aṭṭha-Sata-Pariyāya Vagga

Sutta 25

Bhikkhunā Suttaṃ

Adapted from the 1995 edition of the digital version of the Sri Lanka Buddha Jayanti Tripitaka Series.

 

[234]

[1][pts][bodh] Evaṃ me sutaṃ:|| ||

Atha kho sambahulā bhikkhū yena Bhagavā ten'upasaṅkami. Upasaṅkamitvā Bhagavantaṃ abhivādetvā eka-m-antaṃ nisīdi.|| ||

Eka-m-antaṃ nisinno kho so bhikkhū Bhagavantaṃ etad avoca:|| ||

"Katamānu kho bhante vedanā?|| ||

Katamo vedanā-samudayo?|| ||

Tatamā vedanā-samudaya-gāminī paṭipadā?|| ||

Katamo vedanā-nirodho?|| ||

Katamā vedanā-nirodha-gāminī paṭipadā?|| ||

Ko vedanāya assādo?|| ||

Ko ādīnavo?|| ||

Kīṃ nissaraṇan" ti?|| ||

■

"Tisso imā bhikkhave vedanā.|| ||

Sukhā vedanā||
dukkhā vedanā||
adukkha-m-asukhā vedanā.|| ||

Imā vuccanti bhikkhave vedanā.|| ||

Phassa-samudayā vedanā-samudayo.|| ||

Taṇhā vedanā-samudaya-gāminī paṭipadā.|| ||

Phassa-nirodhā vedanā-nirodho.|| ||

Ayam-eva Ariyo Aṭṭhaṅgiko Maggo vedanā-nirodha-gāminī paṭipadā.|| ||

Seyyath'idaṃ:||
sammā-diṭṭhi||
sammā-saṃkappo||
sammā-vācā||
sammā-kammantā||
sammā-ājīvo||
sammā-vāyāmo||
sammā-sati||
sammā-samādhi.|| ||

Yaṃ vedanā paṭicca uppajjati sukhaṃ somanassaṃ ayaṃ vedanāya assādo.|| ||

Yā vedanā aniccā dukkhā vipariṇāma-dhammā ayaṃ vedanāya ādīnavo.|| ||

Yo vedanāya chanda-rāga-vinayo||
chanda-rāga-p-pahānaṃ,||
idaṃ vedanāya nissaraṇan" ti.|| ||
