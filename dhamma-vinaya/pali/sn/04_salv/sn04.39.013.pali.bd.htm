Saɱyutta Nikāya:
IV. Saḷāyatana Vagga:
39: Sāmaṇḍaka Saɱyuttaɱ

Sutta 13

Bhava Suttaɱ

Adapted from the 1995 edition of the digital version of the Sri Lanka Buddha Jayanti Tripitaka Series.

 

 

[1][pts] Evaɱ me sutaɱ:|| ||

Ekaɱ samayaɱ āyasmā Sāriputto vajjīsu Viharati||
Ukkavelāyaɱ Gaŋgāya nadiyā tīre.|| ||

Atha kho Sāmaṇḍakāni paribbājako yen'āyasmā Sāriputto ten'upasankami.|| ||

Upasaŋkamitvā āyasmatā Sāriputtena saddhiɱ sammodi.|| ||

Sammodanīyaɱ kathaɱ sārāṇīyaɱ vīti-sāretvā eka-m-antaɱ nisīdi.|| ||

Eka-m-antaɱ nisinno kho Sāmaṇḍakāni paribbājako āyasmantaɱ Sāriputtaɱ etad avoca:|| ||

"'Bhavo bhavo' ti||
āvuso Sāriputta vuccati.|| ||

Katamā nu kho āvuso bhavo" ti?|| ||

"Tayome āvuso bhavā:||
kāma-bhavo||
rūpa-bhavo||
arūpa-bhavo.|| ||

Ime kho āvuso tayo bhavā" ti.|| ||

"Atthi pan'āvuso Maggo||
atthi paṭipadā||
etāsaɱ bhavānaɱ pariññāyā" ti?|| ||

Atthi kho āvuso Maggo||
atthi paṭipadā||
etāsaɱ bhavānaɱ pariññāyā" ti.|| ||

"Katamo pan'āvuso Maggo||
katamā paṭipadā||
etāsaɱ bhavānaɱ pariññāyā" ti?|| ||

"Ayam eva kho āvuso ariyo aṭṭhaŋgiko Maggo||
etāsaɱ bhavānaɱ pariññāyā,||
seyyath'īdaɱ:|| ||

Sammā-diṭṭhi||
sammā-saŋkappo||
sammā-vācā||
sammā-kammanto||
sammā-ājīvo||
sammā-vāyāmo||
sammā-sati||
sammā-samādhi.|| ||

Ayaɱ kho āvuso Maggo||
ayaɱ paṭipadā||
etāsaɱ bhavānaɱ pariññāyā" ti.|| ||

"Bhaddako āvuso Maggo||
bhaddikā paṭipadā||
etāsaɱ bhavānaɱ pariññāyā,||
alañ ca pan'āvuso Sāriputta appamādāyā" ti.|| ||
