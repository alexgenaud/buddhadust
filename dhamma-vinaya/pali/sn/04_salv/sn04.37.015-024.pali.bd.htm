Saɱyutta Nikāya:
IV. Saḷāyatana Vagga
37: Mātu-gāma Saɱyutta

Suttas 15-24

Anuruddho II: Sukkapakkho

Adapted from the 1995 edition of the digital version of the Sri Lanka Buddha Jayanti Tripitaka Series.

 

 

Evaɱ me sutaɱ:|| ||

Atha kho āyasmā anuruddho yena Bhagavā ten'upasankami.|| ||

Upasaŋkamitvā Bhagavantaɱ abhivādetvā eka-m-antaɱ nisīdi.|| ||

Eka-m-antaɱ nisinno kho āyasmā anuruddho Bhagavantaɱ etad avoca:|| ||

"Idh'āhaɱ bhante mātu-gāmaɱ passāmi dibbena cakkhunā visuddhena atikkanta-mānusa-kena kāyassa bhedā param maraṇā sugatiɱ saggaɱ lokaɱ upapajjantaɱ.|| ||

Katīhi nu kho bhante dhammehi samannāgato mātu-gāmo kāyassa bhedā param maraṇā sugatiɱ saggaɱ lokaɱ upapajjatī" ti?|| ||

 

 

Sutta 15
Anuruddho II: Sukkapakkho: 1

Akodhano

[15.1][pts] Pañcahi kho Anuruddha dhammehi samannāgato mātu-gāmo kāyassa bhedā param maraṇā sugatiɱ saggaɱ lokaɱ upapajjantaɱ.|| ||

Katamehi pañcahi?|| ||

Saddho ca hoti,||
hirimā ca hoti,||
ottāpī ca hoti,||
akodhano ca hoti,||
paññavā ca hoti.|| ||

Imehi kho Anuruddha pañcahi dhammehi samannāgato mātu-gāmo kāyassa bhedā param maraṇā sugatiɱ saggaɱ lokaɱ upapajjantaɱ" ti.|| ||
