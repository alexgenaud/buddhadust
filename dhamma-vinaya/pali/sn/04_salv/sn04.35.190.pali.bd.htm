Saɱyutta Nikāya
IV. Saḷāyatana Vagga
35: Saḷāyatana Saɱyutta
Paññāsa Catutthaɱ
3. Samudda Vagga

Sutta 190

Khīra-Rukkhopama Suttaɱ

Adapted from the 1995 edition of the digital version of the Sri Lanka Buddha Jayanti Tripitaka Series.

 

[159]

[1][pts][bodh] Evaɱ me sutaɱ:|| ||

Ekaɱ samayaɱ Bhagavā bhikkhū āmantesi|| ||

"Bhikkhavo" ti.|| ||

"Bhadante" ti||
te bhikkhū bhagavato paccassosuɱ.|| ||

3. Bhagavā etad avoca:|| ||

"Yassa kassaci bhikkhave bhikkhussa vā bhikkhuniyā [160]||
vā cakkhu-viññeyyesu rūpesu||
yo rāgo so atthī,||
yo doso so atthi,||
yo moho so atthi,||
yo rāgo so appahīno,||
yo doso so appahīno,||
yo moho so appahīno;||
tassa parittā ce pi cakkhu-viññeyyā rūpā||
cakkhussa āpāthaɱ āga-c-chanti,||
pariyādiyantevāssa cittaɱ,||
ko pana vādo adhimattānaɱ.|| ||

Taɱ kissa hetu?|| ||

Yo bhikkhave rāgo so atthi,||
yo doso so atthi,||
yo moho so atthi;||
yo rāgo so appahīno,||
yo doso so appahīno,||
yo moho so appahīno.|| ||
