Saɱyutta Nikāya
4. Saḷāyatana Vagga
38. Jambukhādaka Saɱyutta

Sutta 9

Avijjā Suttaɱ

Adapted from the 1995 edition of the digital version of the Sri Lanka Buddha Jayanti Tripitaka Series.

 

[256]

[1][pts] Evaɱ me sutaɱ:|| ||

Ekaɱ samayaɱ āyasmā Sāriputto Magadhesu viharati Nālagāmake.|| ||

Atha kho Jambukhādako paribbājako yen'āyasmā Sāriputto ten'upasankami.|| ||

Upasaŋkamitvā āyasmatā Sāriputtena saddhiɱ sammodi.|| ||

Sammodanīyaɱ kathaɱ sārāṇīyaɱ vīti-sāretvā eka-m-antaɱ nisīdi.|| ||

Eka-m-antaɱ nisinno kho Jambukhādako paribbājako āyasmantaɱ Sāriputtaɱ etad avoca:|| ||

"'Avijjā avijjā' ti||
āvuso Sāriputta vuccati.|| ||

Katamā nu kho āvuso avijjā" ti?|| ||

"Yaɱ kho āvuso dukkhe aññāṇaɱ||
dukkha-samudaye aññāṇaɱ||
dukkha-nirodhe aññāṇaɱ||
dukkha-nirodha-gāminiyā paṭipadāya aññāṇaɱ.|| ||

Ayaɱ vuccat'āvuso avijjā" ti.|| ||

"Atthi pan'āvuso Maggo||
atthi paṭipadā||
etissā avijjāya pahānāyā" ti?|| ||

"Atthi kho āvuso Maggo||
atthi paṭipadā||
etissā avijjāya pahānāyā" ti.|| ||

"Katamo pan'āvuso Maggo||
katamā paṭipadā||
etissā avijjāya pahānāyā" ti?|| ||

"Ayam eva kho āvuso Ariyo Aṭṭh'aŋgiko Maggo||
etissā avijjāya pahānāyā,||
seyyath'īdaɱ:|| ||

Sammā-diṭṭhi||
sammā-saŋkappo||
sammā-vācā||
sammā-kammanto||
sammā-ājīvo||
sammā-vāyāmo||
sammā-sati||
sammā-samādhi.|| ||

Ayaɱ kho āvuso Maggo||
ayaɱ paṭipadā||
etissā avijjāya pahānāyā" ti.|| ||

"Bhaddako āvuso Maggo||
bhaddikā paṭipadā||
etissā [257] avijjāya pahānāyā,||
alañ ca pan'āvuso Sāriputta appamādāyā" ti.|| ||
