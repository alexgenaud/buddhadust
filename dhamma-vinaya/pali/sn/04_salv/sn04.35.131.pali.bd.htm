Saɱyutta Nikāya
4. Saḷāyatana Vagga
35. Saḷāyatana Saɱyutta
§ III: Paññāsaka Tatiya
3. Gahapati Vagga

Sutta 131

Nakula-Pitā Suttaɱ

Adapted from the 1995 edition of the digital version of the Sri Lanka Buddha Jayanti Tripitaka Series.

 

[116]

[1][pts] Evaɱ me sutaɱ:|| ||

Ekaɱ samayaɱ Bhagavā bhaggesu viharati Suɱsumāragire Bhesakalāvane Migadāye.|| ||

Atha kho Nakulapitā gahapati yena Bhagavā ten'upasaɱkami.|| ||

Upasan-kamitvā Bhagavantaɱ abhivādetvā eka-m-antaɱ nisīdi.|| ||

Eka-m-antaɱ nisinno kho Nakulapitā gahapati Bhagavantaɱ etad avoca:|| ||

"Ko nu kho bhante hetu||
ko paccayo||
yena-m-idh'ekacce sattā diṭṭhe'va dhamme||
no parinibbāyanti?|| ||

Ko pana bhante hetu||
ko paccayo||
yena-m-idh'ekacce sattā diṭṭhe'va dhamme||
parinibbāyantī" ti?|| ||
