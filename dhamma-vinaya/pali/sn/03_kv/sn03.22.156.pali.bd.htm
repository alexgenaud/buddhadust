Saɱyutta Nikāya
3. Khandha Vagga
22. Khandha Saɱyutta
15. Diṭṭhi Vagga

Sutta 156

Paṭhama Abhinivesa Suttaɱ

Adapted from the 1995 edition of the digital version of the Sri Lanka Buddha Jayanti Tripitaka Series.

 

[186]

[1][pts] Evaɱ me sutaɱ:|| ||

Ekaɱ samayaɱ Bhagavā Sāvatthiyaɱ viharati Jetavane Anāthapiṇḍikassa ārāme.|| ||

Tatra kho Bhagavā bhikkhu āmantesi:|| ||

"Bhikkhavo" ti.|| ||

"Bhadante" ti||
te bhikkh Bhagavato paccassosuɱ|| ||

Bhagavā etad avoca:|| ||

"Kismiɱ nu kho bhikkhave,||
sati kiɱ upādāya||
kiɱ abhinivissa uppajanti saŋyojan-ā-bhinivesa-vinibandhāti" ti?|| ||

"Bhagavaɱ-mulakā no bhante, dhammā.|| ||

Bhagavaɱ-nettikā Bhagavam-paṭisaraṇā.|| ||

Sādhu vata bhaṇte,||
Bhagavantaɱ yeva paṭibhātu etassa bhāsitassa attho,||
Bhagavato sutvā bhikkhu dhāressan" ti.|| ||

"Rūpe kho bhikkhave, sati||
rūpaɱ upādāya||
rūpaɱ abhinivissa||
uppajanti saŋyojan-ā-bhinivesa-vinibandhāti.|| ||

Vedanāya sati||
vedanaɱ upādāya||
vedanā abhinivissa||
uppajanti saŋyojan-ā-bhinivesa-vinibandhāti.|| ||

Saññāya sati||
saññaɱ upādāya||
saññā abhinivissa||
uppajanti saŋyojan-ā-bhinivesa-vinibandhāti.|| ||

Sankhāresu sati||
sankhāre upādāya||
sankhāre abhinivissa||
uppajanti saŋyojan-ā-bhinivesa-vinibandhāti.|| ||

Viññāṇe sati||
viññāṇaɱ upādāya||
viññāṇaɱ abhinivissa||
uppajanti saŋyojan-ā-bhinivesa-vinibandhāti.|| ||

 

§

 

Taɱ kiɱ maññasi bhikkhave?|| ||

Rūpaɱ niccaɱ vā aniccaɱ vā" ti?|| ||

"Aniccaɱ bhante."|| ||

"Yaɱ pan-ā-niccaɱ dukkhaɱ vā taɱ sukhaɱ vā" ti?|| ||

"Dukkhaɱ bhante."|| ||

"Yaɱ pan-ā-niccaɱ dukkhaɱ vipari-ṇāma-dhammaɱ, api nu taɱ anupādāya||
uppajanti saŋyojan-ā-bhinivesa-vinibandhāti" ti?|| ||

"No h'etaɱ bhante."|| ||

■

"Vedanā niccaɱ vā aniccaɱ vā" ti?|| ||

"Aniccaɱ bhante."|| ||

"Yaɱ pan-ā-niccaɱ dukkhaɱ vā taɱ sukhaɱ vā" ti?|| ||

"Dukkhaɱ bhante."|| ||

"Yaɱ pan-ā-niccaɱ dukkhaɱ vipari-ṇāma-dhammaɱ, api nu taɱ anupādāya||
uppajanti saŋyojan-ā-bhinivesa-vinibandhāti" ti?|| ||

"No h'etaɱ bhante."|| ||

■

"Saññā niccaɱ vā aniccaɱ vā" ti?|| ||

"Aniccaɱ bhante."|| ||

"Yaɱ pan-ā-niccaɱ dukkhaɱ vā taɱ sukhaɱ vā" ti?|| ||

"Dukkhaɱ bhante."|| ||

"Yaɱ pan-ā-niccaɱ dukkhaɱ vipari-ṇāma-dhammaɱ, api nu taɱ anupādāya||
uppajanti saŋyojan-ā-bhinivesa-vinibandhāti" ti?|| ||

"No h'etaɱ bhante."|| ||

■

"Sankhārā niccaɱ vā aniccaɱ vā" ti?|| ||

"Aniccaɱ bhante."|| ||

"Yaɱ pan-ā-niccaɱ dukkhaɱ vā taɱ sukhaɱ vā" ti?|| ||

"Dukkhaɱ bhante."|| ||

"Yaɱ pan-ā-niccaɱ dukkhaɱ vipari-ṇāma-dhammaɱ, api nu taɱ anupādāya||
uppajanti saŋyojan-ā-bhinivesa-vinibandhāti" ti?|| ||

"No h'etaɱ bhante."|| ||

■

"Viññāṇaɱ niccaɱ vā aniccaɱ vā" ti?|| ||

"Aniccaɱ bhante."|| ||

"Yaɱ pan-ā-niccaɱ dukkhaɱ vā taɱ sukhaɱ vā" ti?|| ||

"Dukkhaɱ bhante."|| ||

"Yaɱ pan-ā-niccaɱ dukkhaɱ vipari-ṇāma-dhammaɱ, api nu taɱ anupādāya||
uppajanti saŋyojan-ā-bhinivesa-vinibandhāti" ti?|| ||

"No h'etaɱ bhante."|| ||

 

§

 

"Evaɱ passaɱ bhikkhave, sutavā ariya-sāvako||
rūpasmim pi nibbandati,||
vedanāya pi nibbandati,||
saññāya pi nibbindati,||
sankhāresu pi nibbindati,||
viññāṇasmim pi nibbindati.|| ||

Nibbidaɱ virajjati.|| ||

Virāgā vimuccati.|| ||

Vimuttasmiɱ 'vimuttami' ti||
ñāṇaɱ hoti:|| ||

'Khīṇā jāti,||
vusitaɱ Brahma-cariyaɱ,||
kataɱ karaṇīyaɱ,||
nāparaɱ itthattāyā' ti pajānātī" ti.|| ||
