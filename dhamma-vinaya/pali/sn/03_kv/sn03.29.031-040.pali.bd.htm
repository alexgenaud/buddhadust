Saṃyutta Nikāya
3. Khandha Vagga
29. Nāga Saṃyutta

Suttas 31-40

Tatiya Dān'Upakārā Sutta Dasakaṃ
or
Saṇseda-Ja-Dān'Ūpakāra Sutta Dasakaṃ

Adapted from the 1995 edition of the digital version of the Sri Lanka Buddha Jayanti Tripitaka Series.

 

 

Sutta 31

Annadāyaka Saṇsedaja Suttaṃ

[31.1][pts] Evaṃ me sutaṃ:|| ||

Ekaṃ samayaṃ Bhagavā Sāvatthiyaṃ viharati Jetavane Anāthapiṇḍikassa ārāme.|| ||

Atha kho aññataro bhikkhu yena Bhagavā ten'upasaṇkami.|| ||

Upasaṃkamitvā Bhagavantaṃ abhivādetvā eka-m-antaṃ nisīdi.|| ||

Eka-m-antaṃ nisinno kho so bhikkhu Bhagavantaṃ etad avoca:|| ||

"Ko nu kho bhante, hetu,||
ko paccayo||
yena-m-idh'ekacco kāyassa bhedā param maraṇā saṃseda-janaṃ nāgānaṃ saha-vyataṃ upapajjatī" ti?|| ||

"Idha bhikkhu ekacco kāyena dvaya-kārī hoti||
vācāya dvaya-kāri||
manasā dvaya-kāri,||
tassa sutaṃ hoti:|| ||

'Saṇsedajā nāgā dīghā-yukā vaṇṇa-vanto sukha-bahulā.' ti.|| ||

Tassa evaṃ hoti:|| ||

'Aho vatāhaṃ kāyassa bhedā param maraṇā saṃseda-janaṃ nāgānaṃ saha-vyataṃ upapa-j-jeyyan' ti.|| ||

So annaṃ deti.|| ||

So kāyassa bhedā param maraṇā saṃseda-janaṃ nāgānaṃ saha-vyataṃ uppajjati.|| ||

Ayaṃ kho bhikkhu hetu||
ayaṃ paccayo||
yena-m-idh'ekacco kāyassa bhedā param maraṇā saṃseda-janaṃ nāgānaṃ saha-vyataṃ upapajjatī" ti.|| ||
