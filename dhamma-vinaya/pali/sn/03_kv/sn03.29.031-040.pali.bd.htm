Saɱyutta Nikāya:
III. Khandhā Vagga:
29: Nāga Saɱutta

Suttas 31-40

Tatiya Dān'Upakārā Sutta Dasakaɱ
or
Saŋsedajadānūpakāra Sutta Dasakaɱ

Adapted from the 1995 edition of the digital version of the Sri Lanka Buddha Jayanti Tripitaka Series.

 

 

Sutta 31

Annadāyaka Saŋsedaja Suttaɱ

[31.1][pts] Evaɱ me sutaɱ:|| ||

Ekaɱ samayaɱ Bhagavā Sāvatthiyaɱ viharati Jetavane Anāthapiṇḍikassa ārāme.|| ||

Atha kho aññataro bhikkhu yena Bhagavā ten'upasankami.|| ||

Upasaɱkamitvā Bhagavantaɱ abhivādetvā eka-m-antaɱ nisīdi.|| ||

Eka-m-antaɱ nisinno kho so bhikkhu Bhagavantaɱ etad avoca:|| ||

"Ko nu kho bhante, hetu,||
ko paccayo||
yena-m-idh'ekacco kāyassa bhedā param maraṇā saŋsedajanaɱ nāgānaɱ saha-vyataɱ upapajjatī" ti?|| ||

Idha bhikkhu ekacco kāyena dvayakārī hoti||
vācāya dvayakāri||
manasā dvayakāri,||
tassa sutaɱ hoti:|| ||

'Saŋsedajā nāgā dīghā-yukā vaṇṇavanto sukha-bahulā.' ti.|| ||

Tassa evaɱ hoti:|| ||

'Aho vatāhaɱ kāyassa bhedā param maraṇā saŋsedajanaɱ nāgānaɱ saha-vyataɱ upapa-j-jeyyan' ti.|| ||

So annaɱ deti.|| ||

So kāyassa bhedā param maraṇā saŋsedajanaɱ nāgānaɱ saha-vyataɱ uppajjati.|| ||

Ayaɱ kho bhikkhu hetu||
ayaɱ paccayo||
yena-m-idh'ekacco kāyassa bhedā param maraṇā saŋsedajanaɱ nāgānaɱ saha-vyataɱ upapajjatī" ti.|| ||
