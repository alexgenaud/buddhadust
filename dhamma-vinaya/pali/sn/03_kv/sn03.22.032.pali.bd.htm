22. Khandha Saɱyutta
3. Bhāra Vagga

Sutta 32

Pabhangu Suttaɱ

Adapted from the 1995 edition of the digital version of the Sri Lanka Buddha Jayanti Tripitaka Series.

 

[32]

[1][pts][bodh] Evaɱ me sutaɱ.|| ||

Ekaɱ samayaɱ Bhagavā Sāvatthiyaɱ viharati.|| ||

Tatra kho Bhagavā bhikkhu āmantesi:|| ||

"Bhikkhavo" ti.|| ||

"Bhadante" ti te bhikkh Bhagavato paccassosuɱ|| ||

Bhagavā etad avoca:|| ||

"Pabhanguñ ca vo kho bhikkhave, desissāmi||
a-p-pabhanguñ ca.|| ||

Taɱ suṇātha||
sādhukaɱ manasi-karotha||
bhāsissamī" ti.|| ||

"Evaɱ bhante" ti kho te bhikkhū Bhagavato paccassosuɱ.|| ||

Bhagavā etad avoca:|| ||

"Kiñ ca bhikkhave, pabhangu,||
kiɱ a-p-pabhangu?|| ||

[33] Rūpaɱ bhikkhave, pabhangu,||
yo tassa nirodho||
vūpasamo||
atthagamo||
idaɱ a-p-pabhangu.|| ||

Vedanā pabhangu,||
yo tassā nirodho||
vūpasamo||
atthagamo||
idaɱ a-p-pabhangu.|| ||

Saññā pabhangu,||
yo tassā nirodho||
vūpasamo||
atthagamo,||
idaɱ a-p-pabhangu.|| ||

Sankhārā pabhangu,||
yo tesaɱ nirodho||
vūpasamo||
atthagamo||
idaɱ a-p-pabhangu.|| ||

Viññāṇaɱ pabhangu,||
yo tassa nirodho||
vūpasamo||
atthagamo||
idaɱ a-p-pabhangu" ti.|| ||
