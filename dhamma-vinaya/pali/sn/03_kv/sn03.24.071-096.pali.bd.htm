Saɱyutta Nikāya:
III. Khandhā Vagga:
24: Diṭṭhi Saɱyutta

4. Catutthagamanam

Suttas 71-96

Adapted from the 1995 edition of the digital version of the Sri Lanka Buddha Jayanti Tripitaka Series.

[Note: As the suttas of this Chapter are identical to the previous Suttas 1-18 and 37-44 it has been abridged here by the inclusion of the first and last suttas (45 and 70) in their complete form and Suttas 46-69 are given by title only but are linked to their corresponding sutta in the previous Chapters.]

 

 

Sutta 71

Vātā Suttaɱ

[71.1][pts] Evaɱ me sutaɱ:|| ||

Ekaɱ samayaɱ Bhagavā Sāvatthiyaɱ viharati Jetavane Anāthapiṇḍikassa ārāme.|| ||

|| ||

Tatra kho Bhagavā bhikkhu āmantesi:|| ||

"Bhikkhavo" ti.|| ||

"Bhadante" ti||
te bhikkh Bhagavato paccassosuɱ|| ||

Bhagavā etad avoca:|| ||

"Kismiɱ nu kho bhikkhave, sati kiɱ upādāya kiɱ abhinivissa evaɱ diṭṭhi uppajjati:|| ||

'Na vātā vāyanti,||
na najjo sandanti,||
na gbbhniyo vijāyanti,||
na candima-suriyā udenti vā||
apenti vā||
esikaṭṭhāyiṭṭhitā'" ti?|| ||

"Bhagavaɱmulakā no bhante, dhammā.|| ||

Bhagavaɱ-nettikā Bhagavam-paṭisaraṇā.|| ||

Sādhu vata bhaṇte,||
Bhagavantaɱ yeva paṭibhātu etassa bhāsitassa attho,||
Bhagavato sutvā bhikkhu dhāressan" ti.|| ||

 

 

"Rūpe kho bhikkhave, sati rūpaɱ upādāya||
rūpaɱ abhinivissa evem diṭṭhi uppajjati:|| ||

'Na vātā vāyanti,||
na najjo sandanti,||
na gbbhniyo vijāyanti,||
na candima-suriyā udenti vā||
apenti vā||
esikaṭṭhāyiṭṭhitā' ti.|| ||

Vedanāya sati vedanaɱ upādāya||
vedanā abhinivissa evem diṭṭhi uppajjati:|| ||

'Na vātā vāyanti,||
na najjo sandanti,||
na gbbhniyo vijāyanti,||
na candima-suriyā udenti vā||
apenti vā||
esikaṭṭhāyiṭṭhitā' ti.|| ||

Saññāya sati saññaɱ upādāya||
saññā abhinivissa evem diṭṭhi uppajjati:|| ||

'Na vātā vāyanti,||
na najjo sandanti,||
na gbbhniyo vijāyanti,||
na candima-suriyā udenti vā||
apenti vā||
esikaṭṭhāyiṭṭhitā' ti.|| ||

Saɱkhāresu sati||
saɱkhāre upādāya saɱkhāre abhinivissa evem diṭṭhi uppajjati:|| ||

'Na vātā vāyanti,||
na najjo sandanti,||
na gbbhniyo vijāyanti,||
na candima-suriyā udenti vā||
apenti vā||
esikaṭṭhāyiṭṭhitā' ti.|| ||

Viññāṇe sati||
viññāṇaɱ upādāya viññāṇaɱ abhinivissa evem diṭṭhi uppajjati:|| ||

'Na vātā vāyanti,||
na najjo sandanti,||
na gbbhniyo vijāyanti,||
na candima-suriyā udenti vā||
apenti vā||
esikaṭṭhāyiṭṭhitā' ti.|| ||

 

 

Taɱ kiɱ maññasi bhikkhave?|| ||

Rūpaɱ niccaɱ vā aniccaɱ vā" ti?|| ||

"Aniccaɱ bhante."|| ||

"Yaɱ panāniccaɱ dukkhaɱ vā taɱ sukhaɱ vā" ti?|| ||

"Dukkhaɱ bhante."|| ||

"Yaɱ panāniccaɱ dukkhaɱ vipari-ṇāma-dhammaɱ,||
api nu taɱ anupādāya evaɱ diṭṭhi uppajjeyya:|| ||

'Na vātā vāyanti,||
na najjo sandanti,||
na gbbhniyo vijāyanti,||
na candima-suriyā udenti vā||
apenti vā||
esikaṭṭhāyiṭṭhitā' ti?|| ||

"No h'etaɱ bhante."|| ||

■

Vedanā niccaɱ vā aniccaɱ vā" ti?|| ||

"Aniccaɱ bhante."|| ||

"Yaɱ panāniccaɱ dukkhaɱ vā taɱ sukhaɱ vā" ti?|| ||

"Dukkhaɱ bhante."|| ||

'Na vātā vāyanti,||

"Aniccaɱ bhante."|| ||

"Yaɱ panāniccaɱ dukkhaɱ vā taɱ sukhaɱ vā" ti?|| ||

"Dukkhaɱ bhante."|| ||

"Yaɱ panāniccaɱ dukkhaɱ vipari-ṇāma-dhammaɱ,||

'Na vātā vāyanti,||

"No h'etaɱ bhante."|| ||

■

"Aniccaɱ bhante."|| ||

"Yaɱ panāniccaɱ dukkhaɱ vā taɱ sukhaɱ vā" ti?|| ||

"Dukkhaɱ bhante."|| ||

"Yaɱ panāniccaɱ dukkhaɱ vipari-ṇāma-dhammaɱ,||

'Na vātā vāyanti,||

Yam pidaɱ diṭṭhaɱ,||
sutaɱ,||

"Aniccaɱ bhante."|| ||

"Yaɱ panāniccaɱ dukkhaɱ vā taɱ sukhaɱ vā" ti?|| ||

api nu taɱ anupādāya evaɱ diṭṭhi uppajjeyya:|| ||

esikaṭṭhāyiṭṭhitā' ti?|| ||

Yato kho bhikkhave, ariya-sāvakassa imesu chasu ṭhānesu kaŋkhā pahīnā hoti,||
dukkhe pissa kaŋkhā pahīnā hoti,||
dukkha-samudaye pissa kaŋkhā pahīnā hoti,||
dukkha-nirodhe pissa kaŋkhā pahīnā hoti,||
dukkha-nirodha-gāminiyāpaṭipadāya pissa kaŋkhā pahīnā hoti.|| ||

Ayaɱ vuccati bhikkhave, ariya-sāvako||
Sot'āpanno||
avinipāta-dhammo||
niyato||
sambodhi-parāyano" ti.|| ||

72. Etaɱ Mama Suttaɱ,

This is Mine,

73. So Attā Suttaɱ

That is the Self,

74. No ca Me Siyā Suttaɱ,

It May Not Be Mine,

75. N'atthi Suttaɱ,

There is Not,

76. Karoto Suttaɱ,

PTS: For Him Who Acts,

77. Hetu Suttaɱ,

Condition,

78. Mahā Diṭṭhi Suttaɱ

By the (Great) Heresy,

79. Sassata Loka Suttaɱ,

The World is Eternal,

80. Asassata Loka Suttaɱ,

The World is Not Eternal,

81. Antavā Suttaɱ

Limited (is the World),

82. Anantavā Suttaɱ

Unlimited (is the world),

83. Taŋ Jīvan, Taŋ Sarīra Suttaɱ

What the Life Is, That Is the Body,

84. Aññaŋ Jīvan, Aññaŋ Sarīra Suttaɱ

PTS: The Life is One Thing, the Body is Another,

85. Hoti Tathāgata Suttaɱ

The Tathāgata Exists,

86. Na Hoti Tathāgata Suttaɱ

The Tathāgata Exists Not,

87. Hoti ca na ca Hoti Tathāgata Suttaɱ

The Tathāgata both Exists and Exists Not,

88. N'eva Hoti na na Hoti Tathāgata Suttaɱ

The Tathāgata Neither Exists nor Exists Not,

89. Rūpī Attā Suttaɱ

PTS: The Self has Form,

90. Arūpī Attā Suttaɱ

PTS: The Self is Formless,

91. Rūpī ca Arūpī Attā Suttaɱ

PTS: The Self Both has Form and Is Formless,

92. N'eva Rūpī N'ārūpī Attā Suttaɱ

PTS: The Self Neither has Form Nor Is Formless,

93. Ekanta-sukhī Suttaɱ

PTS: The Self Is Sheer Bliss,

94. Ekanta-dukkhī Suttaɱ

PTS: The Self Is Sheer Suffering,

95. Sukha-dukkhī Suttaɱ

PTS: The Self Is Bliss and Suffering,
