Saɱyutta Nikāya:
III. Khandhā Vagga:
22: Khandhāsaɱyutta

Sutta 105

Sakkāya Suttaɱ

Adapted from the 1995 edition of the digital version of the Sri Lanka Buddha Jayanti Tripitaka Series.

 

 

[1][pts] Evaɱ me sutaɱ:|| ||

Ekaɱ samayaɱ Bhagavā Sāvatthiyaɱ viharati Jetavane Anāthapiṇḍikassa ārāme.|| ||

Tatra kho Bhagavā bhikkhu āmantesi:|| ||

"Bhikkhavo" ti.|| ||

"Bhadante" ti||
te bhikkh Bhagavato paccassosuɱ|| ||

Bhagavā etad avoca:|| ||

"Sakkāyña ca vo bhikkhave, desissāmi||
sakkāya-samudayañ ca||
sakkāya-nirodhañ ca||
sakkāya-nirodha-gāminiñ ca paṭipadaɱ.|| ||

Taɱ suṇātha!|| ||

Katamo ca bhikkhave, sakkāyo?|| ||

Pañc'upādāna-k-khandhā tissa vacanīyaɱ.|| ||

Katame pañca?|| ||

Seyyath'īdaɱ:|| ||

Rūp'ūpādāna-k-khandho,||
vedan'ūpādāna-k-khandho,||
saññ'ūpādānakkhanadho,||
saɱkhārūpādāna-k-khandho,||
viññāṇ'ūpādāna-k-khandho.|| ||

Ayaɱ vuccati bhikkhave, sakkāyo.|| ||

Katamo ca bhikkhave, sakkāya-samudayo?|| ||

Yāyaɱ taṇhā pono-bhavikā nandi rāgasahagatā tatra tatrābhinandinī,||
seyyath'īdaɱ:|| ||

kāma-taṇhā,||
bhava-taṇhā,||
vibhava-taṇhā.|| ||

Ayaɱ vuccati bhikkhave, sakkāya-samudayo.|| ||

Katamo ca bhikkhave, sakkāya-nirodho?|| ||

Yo tassā yeva taṇhāya asesa-virāga-nirodho cāgo paṭinissaggo mutti anālayo.|| ||

Ayaɱ vuccati bhikkhave, sakkāya-nirodho.|| ||

Katamo ca bhikkhave, sakkāya-nirodha-gāmini paṭipada?|| ||

Ayam-eva Ariyo Aṭṭhaŋgiko Maggo.|| ||

Seyyath'īdaɱ:|| ||

sammā-diṭṭhi,||
sammā-saɱkappo,||
sammā-vācā,||
sammā-kammanto,||
sammā-ājīvo,||
sammā-vāyāmo,||
sammā-sati,||
sammā-samādhi.|| ||

Ayaɱ vuccati bhikkhave, sakkāya-nirodha-gāmini paṭipadanta.|| ||
