3. Khandha Vagga
27. Kilesa Saɱyutta

Sutta 6

Saññā Suttaɱ

Adapted from the 1995 edition of the digital version of the Sri Lanka Buddha Jayanti Tripitaka Series.

 

[233]

[1][pts][than] Evaɱ me sutaɱ:|| ||

Ekaɱ samayaɱ Bhagavā Sāvatthiyaɱ viharati Jetavane Anāthapiṇḍikassa ārāme.|| ||

Tatra kho Bhagavā bhikkhu āmantesi:|| ||

"Bhikkhavo" ti.|| ||

"Bhadante" ti||
te bhikkh Bhagavato paccassosuɱ|| ||

Bhagavā etad avoca:|| ||

Yo bhikkhave, rūpa-saññāya chanda-rāgo,||
cittassso upakkileso.|| ||

Yo sadda-saññāya chanda-rāgo,||
cittasse'so upakkileso.|| ||

Yo gandha-saññāya chanda-rāgo,||
cittasse'so upakkileso.|| ||

Yo rasa-saññāya vedanāya chanda-rāgo,||
cittasse'so upakkileso.|| ||

Yo phoṭṭhabba-saññāya chanda-rāgo,||
cittasse'so upakkileso.|| ||

Yo dhamma-saññāya chanda-rāgo,||
cittasse'so upakkileso.|| ||

Yato kho bhikkhave,||
bhikkhuno imesu chasu ṭhānesu cetaso upakkileso pahīno hoti,||
nekkhammaninnaɱ c'assa cittaɱ hoti,||
nekkhamma-paribhāvitaɱ cittaɱ kammanīyaɱ khāyati abhiññā sacchi-karaṇiyesu dhammesū" ti.|| ||
