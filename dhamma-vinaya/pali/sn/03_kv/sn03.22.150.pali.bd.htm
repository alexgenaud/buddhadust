Saɱyutta Nikāya:
III. Khandhā Vagga:
22: Khandhā Saɱyutta

Sutta 150

Etaɱ Mama Suttaɱ

Adapted from the 1995 edition of the digital version of the Sri Lanka Buddha Jayanti Tripitaka Series.

 

[181]

[1][pts] Evaɱ me sutaɱ:|| ||

Ekaɱ samayaɱ Bhagavā Sāvatthiyaɱ viharati Jetavane Anāthapiṇḍikassa ārāme.|| ||

Tatra kho Bhagavā bhikkhu āmantesi:|| ||

"Bhikkhavo" ti.|| ||

"Bhadante" ti||
te bhikkh Bhagavato paccassosuɱ|| ||

Bhagavā etad avoca:|| ||

"Kismiɱ nu kho bhikkhave,||
sati kiɱ upādāya||
kiɱ abhinivissa||
'Etaɱ mama,||
eso ham asmi,||
eso me attā ti samanupassa' ti" ti?|| ||

"Bhagavaɱ-mulakā no bhante, dhammā.|| ||

Bhagavaɱ-nettikā Bhagavam-paṭisaraṇā.|| ||

Sādhu vata bhaṇte,||
Bhagavantaɱ yeva paṭibhātu etassa bhāsitassa attho,||
Bhagavato sutvā bhikkhu dhāressan" ti.|| ||

"Rūpe kho bhikkhave, sati rūpaɱ upādāya||
rūpaɱ [182] abhinivissa||
'etaɱ mama,||
eso ham asmi,||
eso me atta ti samanupassati.|| ||

Vedanāya sati vedanaɱ upādāya||
vedanā abhinivissa||
'etaɱ mama,||
eso ham asmi,||
eso me atta' ti samanupassati.|| ||

Saññāya sati saññaɱ upādāya||
saññā abhinivissa||
'etaɱ mama,||
eso ham asmi,||
eso me atta' ti samanupassati.|| ||

Saṅkhāresu sati||
saṅkhāre upādāya saṅkhāre abhinivissa||
'etaɱ mama,||
eso ham asmi,||
eso me atta' ti samanupassati.|| ||

Viññāṇe sati||
viññāṇaɱ upādāya viññāṇaɱ abhinivissa||
'etaɱ mama,||
eso ham asmi,||
eso me atta' ti samanupassati.|| ||

 

§

 

Taɱ kiɱ maññasi bhikkhave?|| ||

Rūpaɱ niccaɱ vā aniccaɱ vā" ti?|| ||

"Aniccaɱ bhante."|| ||

"Yaɱ pan-ā-niccaɱ dukkhaɱ vā taɱ sukhaɱ vā" ti?|| ||

"Dukkhaɱ bhante."|| ||

"Yaɱ pan-ā-niccaɱ dukkhaɱ vipariṇāma-dhammaɱ, api nu taɱ anupādāya:|| ||

'Etaɱ mama,||
eso ham asmi,||
eso me attā' ti samanupassati"?|| ||

"No h'etaɱ bhante."|| ||

■

"Vedanā niccaɱ vā aniccaɱ vā" ti?|| ||

"Aniccaɱ bhante."|| ||

"Dukkhaɱ bhante."|| ||

"Yaɱ pan-ā-niccaɱ dukkhaɱ vipariṇāma-dhammaɱ, api nu taɱ anupādāya:|| ||

'Etaɱ mama,||
eso ham asmi,||
eso me attā' ti samanupassati"?|| ||

"No h'etaɱ bhante."|| ||

■

"Saññā niccaɱ vā aniccaɱ vā" ti?|| ||

"Aniccaɱ bhante."|| ||

"Yaɱ pan-ā-niccaɱ dukkhaɱ vā taɱ sukhaɱ vā" ti?|| ||

"Dukkhaɱ bhante."|| ||

"Yaɱ pan-ā-niccaɱ dukkhaɱ vipariṇāma-dhammaɱ, api nu taɱ anupādāya:|| ||

'Etaɱ mama,||
eso ham asmi,||
eso me attā' ti samanupassati"?|| ||

"No h'etaɱ bhante."|| ||

■

"Saṅkhārā niccaɱ vā aniccaɱ vā" ti?|| ||

"Yaɱ pan-ā-niccaɱ dukkhaɱ vā taɱ sukhaɱ vā" ti?|| ||

"Dukkhaɱ bhante."|| ||

"Yaɱ pan-ā-niccaɱ dukkhaɱ vipariṇāma-dhammaɱ, api nu taɱ anupādāya:|| ||

'Etaɱ mama,||

"No h'etaɱ bhante."|| ||

"Dukkhaɱ bhante."|| ||

"Yaɱ pan-ā-niccaɱ dukkhaɱ vipariṇāma-dhammaɱ, api nu taɱ anupādāya:|| ||

§

 

"Evaɱ passaɱ bhikkhave, sutavā ariya-sāvako||
rūpasmim pi nibbandati,||
vedanāya pi nibbandati,||
saññāya pi nibbindati,||
saṅkhāresu pi nibbindati,||
viññāṇasmim pi nibbindati.|| ||

Nibbidaɱ virajjati.|| ||

Virāgā vimuccati.|| ||

Vimuttasmiɱ 'vimuttami' ti||
ñāṇaɱ hoti:|| ||

'Khīṇā jāti,||
vusitaɱ Brahma-cariyaɱ,||
kataɱ karaṇīyaɱ,||
nāparaɱ itthattāyā' ti pajānātī" ti.|| ||
