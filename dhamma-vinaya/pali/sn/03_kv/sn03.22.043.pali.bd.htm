Saɱyutta Nikāya
3. Khandha Vagga
22. Khandha Saɱyutta
5. Atta-Dīpa Vagga

Sutta 43

Atta-Dīpa Suttaɱ

Adapted from the 1995 edition of the digital version of the Sri Lanka Buddha Jayanti Tripitaka Series.

 

[42]

[1-2][pts][wlsh][bodh][olds] Evaɱ me sutaɱ.|| ||

Ekaɱ samayaɱ Bhagavā Sāvatthiyaɱ viharati.|| ||

Tatra kho Bhagavā bhikkhu āmantesi:|| ||

"Bhikkhavo" ti.|| ||

"Bhadante" ti te bhikkh Bhagavato paccassosuɱ|| ||

Bhagavā etad avoca:|| ||

[3][pts][bodh][olds] "Atta-dīpā bhikkhave, viharatha||
atta-saraṇā||
anañña-saraṇā||
Dhamma-dīpā||
Dhamma-saraṇā||
anañña-saraṇā.|| ||

[4][pts][bodh][olds] Atta-dīpānaɱ bhikkhave,||
viharataɱ atta-saraṇānaɱ||
anañña-saraṇānaɱ||
Dhamma-dīpānaɱ||
Dhamma-saraṇānaɱ||
anañña-saraṇānaɱ,||
yoni yeva upapari-k-khitabbo.|| ||

"Kiñjātikā soka-parideva-dukkha-domanass'upāyāsā,||
kiɱ pahotikā" ti.|| ||

[5][pts][bodh][olds] Kiñjātikā ca bhikkhave,||
soka-parideva-dukkha-domanass'upāyāsā||
kiɱ pahotikā?|| ||

[6][pts][bodh][olds] Idha, bhikkhave, a-s-sutavā puthujjano||
ariyānaɱ adassāvī||
ariya-Dhammassa akovido||
ariya-Dhamme avinīto,||
sappurisānaɱ adassāvī||
sappurisa-Dhammassa akovido||
sappurisa-Dhamme avinīto,||
rūpaɱ attato samanupassati,||
rūpa-vantaɱ vā attānaɱ,||
attani vā rūpaɱ,||
rūpsamiɱ vā attānaɱ.|| ||

Tassa taɱ rūpaɱ vipariṇamati,||
aññathā hoti.|| ||

Tassa rūpa-vipariṇām-aññathā-bhāvā||
uppajjanti soka-parideva-dukkha-domanass'upāyāsā.|| ||

[7][pts][bodh][olds] Vedanaɱ attato samanupassati,||
vedanā-vantaɱ vā attānaɱ||
attani vā vedanaɱ,||
vedanāya vā attānaɱ.|| ||

Tassa sā vedanā vipariṇamati,||
aññathā hoti.|| ||

Tassa vedan-ā-vipariṇām-aññathā-bhāvā||
uppajjanti soka-parideva-dukkha-domanass'upāyāsā.|| ||

[8][pts][bodh][olds] Saññaɱ attato samanupassati,||
saññā-vantaɱ vā attānaɱ,||
attani vā saññaɱ,||
saññāya vā attānaɱ.|| ||

Tassa sā saññā vipariṇamati,||
aññathā hoti.|| ||

Tassa saññā-vipariṇām-aññathā-bhāvā||
uppajjanti soka-parideva-dukkha-domanass'upāyāsā.|| ||

[9][pts][bodh][olds] Sankhāre attato samanupassati,||
sankhāra-vantaɱ vā attānaɱ,||
attani vā sankhāre,||
sankhāresu vā attānaɱ.|| ||

Tassa te sankhārā vipariṇamanti,||
aññathā honti.|| ||

Tassa sankhāra-vipariṇām-aññathā-bhāvā||
uppajjanti soka-parideva-dukkha-domanass'upāyāsā.|| ||

[10][pts][bodh][olds] Viññāṇaɱ attato samanupassati,||
viññāṇa-vantaɱ vā attānaɱ,||
attani vā viññāṇaɱ,||
viññāṇasmiɱ vā attānaɱ.|| ||

[43] Tassa taɱ viññāṇaɱ vipariṇamati,||
aññathā hoti.

Tassa viññāṇa-vipariṇām-aññathā-bhāvā||
uppajjanti soka-parideva-dukkha-domanass'upāyāsā.|| ||

[11][pts][bodh][olds] Rūpassa tv'eva bhikkhave,||
aniccataɱ viditvā vipariṇāmaɱ virāgaɱ nirodhaɱ pubbe c'eva rūpaɱ etarahi ca||
sabbaɱ rūpaɱ aniccaɱ dukkhaɱ vipari-ṇāma-dhammanti||
evam etaɱ yathā-bhūtaɱ samma-p-paññāya passato ye soka-parideva-dukkha-domanass'upāyāsā te pahīyanti,||
tesaɱ pahānā na paritassati,||
aparitassaɱ sukhaɱ viharati.|| ||

Sukhaɱ viharaɱ bhikkhu 'tad-aŋgani-b-buto' ti vuccati.|| ||

[12][pts][bodh][olds] Vedanāya tv'eva bhikkhave,||
aniccataɱ viditvā vipariṇāmaɱ virāgaɱ nirodhaɱ pubbe c'eva vedanā etarahi ca sabbā vedanā aniccā dukkhā vipariṇāma-dhammāti evam etaɱ yathā-bhūtaɱ samma-p-paññāya passato ye soka-parideva-dukkha-domanass'upāyāsā te pahīyanti,||
tesaɱ pahānā na paritassati,||
aparitassaɱ sukhaɱ viharati.|| ||

Sukhaɱ viharaɱ bhikkhu 'tad-aŋgani-b-buto' ti vuccati.|| ||

[13][pts][bodh][olds] Saññāya tv'eva bhikkhave,||
aniccataɱ viditvā vipariṇāmaɱ virāgaɱ nirodhaɱ pubbe c'eva saññāya etarahi ca sabbā saññāya aniccā dukkhā vipariṇāma-dhammāti evam etaɱ yathā-bhūtaɱ samma-p-paññāya passato ye soka-parideva-dukkha-domanass'upāyāsā te pahīyanti,||
tesaɱ pahānā na paritassati,||
aparitassaɱ sukhaɱ viharati.|| ||

Sukhaɱ viharaɱ bhikkhu 'tad-aŋgani-b-buto' ti vuccati.|| ||

[14][pts][bodh][olds] Sankhārānaɱ tv'eva bhikkhave,||
aniccataɱ viditvā vipariṇāmaɱ virāgaɱ nirodhaɱ pubbe ce sankhārā etarahi ca sabbe sankhārā aniccā dukkhā vipariṇāma-dhammāti evam etaɱ yathā-bhūtaɱ samma-p-paññāya passato ye soka-parideva-dukkha-domanass'upāyāsā te pahīyanti,||
tesaɱ pahānā na paritassati,||
aparitassaɱ sukhaɱ viharati.|| ||

Sukhaɱ viharaɱ bhikkhu 'tad-aŋgani-b-buto' ti vuccati.|| ||

[15][pts][bodh][olds] Viññāṇassa tv'eva bhikkhave,||
aniccataɱ viditvā vipariṇāmaɱ virāgaɱ nirodhaɱ pubbe ce viññāṇaɱ etarahi ca sabbaɱ viññāṇaɱ aniccaɱ dukkhaɱ vipari-ṇāma-dhammanti evam etaɱ yathā-bhūtaɱ samma-p-paññāya passato ye soka-parideva-dukkha-domanass'upāyāsā te pahīyanti,||
tesaɱ pahānā na paritassati,||
aparitassaɱ sukhaɱ viharati.|| ||

Sukhaɱ viharaɱ bhikkhu 'tad-aŋgani-b-buto' ti vuccati" ti.
