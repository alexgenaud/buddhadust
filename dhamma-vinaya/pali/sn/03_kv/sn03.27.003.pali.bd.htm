Saɱyutta Nikāya
3. Khandha Vagga
27. Kilesa Saɱyutta

Sutta 3

Viññāṇa Suttaɱ

Adapted from the 1995 edition of the digital version of the Sri Lanka Buddha Jayanti Tripitaka Series.

 

[232]

[1][pts][than] Evaɱ me sutaɱ:|| ||

Ekaɱ samayaɱ Bhagavā Sāvatthiyaɱ viharati Jetavane Anāthapiṇḍikassa ārāme.|| ||

Tatra kho Bhagavā bhikkhu āmantesi:|| ||

"Bhikkhavo" ti.|| ||

"Bhadante" ti||
te bhikkh Bhagavato paccassosuɱ|| ||

Bhagavā etad avoca:|| ||

Yo bhikkhave, cakkhu-viññāṇasmiɱ chanda-rāgo,||
cittassso upakkileso.|| ||

Yo sota-viññāṇasmiɱ chanda-rāgo,||
cittasse'so upakkileso.|| ||

Yo ghāna-viññāṇasmiɱ chanda-rāgo,||
cittasse'so upakkileso.|| ||

Yo jivhā-viññāṇasmiɱ chanda-rāgo,||
cittasse'so upakkileso.|| ||

Yo kāya-viññāṇasmiɱ chanda-rāgo,||
cittasse'so upakkileso.|| ||

Yo mano-viññāṇasmiɱ chanda-rāgo,||
cittasse'so upakkileso.|| ||

Yato kho bhikkhave,||
bhikkhuno imesu chasu ṭhānesu cetaso upakkileso pahīno hoti,||
nekkhammaninnaɱ c'assa [233] cittaɱ hoti,||
nekkhammaparibhāvitaɱ cittaɱ kammanīyaɱ khāyati abhiññā sacchi-karaṇiyesu dhammesū" ti.|| ||
