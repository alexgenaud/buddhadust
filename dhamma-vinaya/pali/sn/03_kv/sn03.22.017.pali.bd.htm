Sutta 17

Tatiya Yad Anicca Suttaɱ

Adapted from the 1995 edition of the digital version of the Sri Lanka Buddha Jayanti Tripitaka Series.

 

[22]

[1][pts][bodh] Evaɱ me sutaɱ.|| ||

Ekaɱ samayaɱ Bhagavā Sāvatthiyaɱ viharati.|| ||

Tatra kho Bhagavā bhikkhu āmantesi:|| ||

"Bhikkhavo" ti.|| ||

"Bhadante" ti te bhikkh Bhagavato paccassosuɱ|| ||

Bhagavā etad avoca:|| ||

"Rūpaɱ bhikkhave, anantā,||
yad antatā taɱ||
'n'etaɱ mama||
neso'ham asmi,||
na me so attā' ti.|| ||

Evam etaɱ yathā-bhūtaɱ samma-p-paññāya daṭṭhabbaɱ.|| ||

■

Vedanā, anantā,||
yad antatā taɱ||
'n'etaɱ mama||
neso'ham asmi,||
na me so attā' ti.|| ||

Evam etaɱ yathā-bhūtaɱ samma-p-paññāya daṭṭhabbaɱ.|| ||

■

Saññā, anantā,||
yad antatā taɱ||
'n'etaɱ mama||
neso'ham asmi,||
na me so attā' ti.|| ||

Evam etaɱ yathā-bhūtaɱ samma-p-paññāya daṭṭhabbaɱ.|| ||

■

Saɱkhārā, anantā,||
yad antatā taɱ||
'n'etaɱ mama||
neso'ham asmi,||
na me so attā' ti.|| ||

Evam etaɱ yathā-bhūtaɱ samma-p-paññāya daṭṭhabbaɱ.|| ||

■

Viññāṇaɱ, anantā,||
yad antatā taɱ||
'n'etaɱ mama||
neso'ham asmi,||
na me so attā' ti.|| ||

Evam etaɱ yathā-bhūtaɱ samma-p-paññāya daṭṭhabbaɱ" ti.|| ||

Evaɱ passaɱ bhikkhave, sutavā ariya-sāvako||
rūpasmim pi nibbindati,||
vedanāya pi nibbindati,||
saññāya pi nibbindati||
saɱkhāresu pi nibbindati,||
viññāṇasmim pi nibbindati.|| ||

Nibbindaɱ virajjati,||
virāgā vimuccati,||
vimuttasmiɱ vimuttamiti||
ñāṇaɱ hoti:|| ||

'Khīṇā jāti||
vusitaɱ Brahma-cariyaɱ||
kataɱ karaṇīyaɱ||
nāparaɱ itthattāyā' ti pajānātī" ti.|| ||
