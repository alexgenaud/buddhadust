Saɱyutta Nikāya
3. Khandha Vagga
22. Khandha Saɱyutta
10. Puppha Vagga

Sutta 97

Nakha-Sikh'Opama Suttaɱ

Adapted from the 1995 edition of the digital version of the Sri Lanka Buddha Jayanti Tripitaka Series.

 

[147]

[1][pts][than][bodh] Evaɱ me sutaɱ:|| ||

Ekaɱ samayaɱ Bhagavā Sāvatthiyaɱ viharati||
Jetavane Anāthapiṇḍikassa ārāme.|| ||

Eka-m-antaɱ nisinno kho so bhikkhu,||
Bhagavantaɱ etad avoca:|| ||

"Atthi nu kho bhante, kiñci rūpaɱ yaɱ rūpaɱ||
niccaɱ||
dhuvaɱ||
sassataɱ,||
avipariṇāma-dhammaɱ||
sassatisamaɱ tath'eva ṭhassati?|| ||

Atthi nu kho bhante, kāci vedanā yā vedanā||
niccaɱ||
dhuvaɱ||
sassataɱ,||
avipariṇāma-dhammaɱ||
sassatisamaɱ tath'eva ṭhassati?|| ||

Atthi nu kho bhante, kāci saññā yā saññā||
niccaɱ||
dhuvaɱ||
sassataɱ,||
avipariṇāma-dhammaɱ||
sassatisamaɱ tath'eva ṭhassati?|| ||

Atthi nu kho bhante, keci sankhārā ye sankhārā||
niccaɱ||
dhuvaɱ||
sassataɱ,||
avipariṇāma-dhammaɱ||
sassatisamaɱ tath'eva ṭhassati?|| ||

Atthi nu kho bhante, kiñci viññāṇaɱ yaɱ viññāṇaɱ||
niccaɱ||
dhuvaɱ||
sassataɱ,||
avipariṇāma-dhammaɱ||
sassatisamaɱ tath'eva ṭhassati" ti?|| ||

■

"N'atthi kho bhikkhu, kiñci rūpaɱ yaɱ rūpaɱ||
niccaɱ||
dhuvaɱ||
sassataɱ,||
avipariṇāma-dhammaɱ||
sassatisamaɱ tath'eva ṭhassati.|| ||

N'atthi kho bhikkhu, kāci vedanā yā vedanā||
niccaɱ||
dhuvaɱ||
sassataɱ,||
avipariṇāma-dhammaɱ||
sassatisamaɱ tath'eva ṭhassati.|| ||

N'atthi kho bhikkhu, kāci saññā yā saññā||
niccaɱ||
dhuvaɱ||
sassataɱ,||
avipariṇāma-dhammaɱ||
sassatisamaɱ tath'eva ṭhassati.|| ||

N'atthi kho bhikkhu, keci sankhārā ye sankhārā||
niccaɱ||
dhuvaɱ||
sassataɱ,||
avipariṇāma-dhammaɱ||
sassatisamaɱ tath'eva ṭhassati.|| ||

N'atthi kho bhikkhu, kiñci viññāṇaɱ, yaɱ viññāṇaɱ||
niccaɱ||
dhuvaɱ||
sassataɱ,||
avipariṇāma-dhammaɱ||
sassatisamaɱ tath'eva ṭhassati" ti.|| ||

 

§

 

Atha kho Bhagavā parittaɱ nakha-sikhāyaɱ paɱsuɱ āropetvā taɱ bhikkhuɱ etad avoca:|| ||

"Ettakam pi kho bhikkhu,||
rūpaɱ n'atthī||
niccaɱ||
dhuvaɱ||
sassataɱ,||
avipariṇāma-dhammaɱ||
sassatisamaɱ tath'eva ṭhassati.|| ||

Ettakam ce pi bhikkhu,||
rūpaɱ abhavissa||
niccaɱ||
dhuvaɱ||
sassataɱ,||
avipariṇāma-dhammaɱ||
sassatisamaɱ tath'eva ṭhassati||
na yidaɱ brahma-cariy-a-vāso paññāyetha sammā-dukkha-k-khayāya.|| ||

Yasmā ca kho bhikkhu,||
ettakam pi rūpaɱ n'atthi||
niccaɱ||
dhuvaɱ||
sassataɱ,||
avipariṇāma-dhammaɱ||
sassatisamaɱ tath'eva ṭhassati||
tasmā brahma-cariy-a-vāso paññāyati sammā-dukkha-k-khayāya.|| ||

■

niccaɱ||
dhuvaɱ||
sassataɱ,||
avipariṇāma-dhammaɱ||
sassatisamaɱ tath'eva ṭhassati.|| ||

Ettakam ce pi bhikkhu,||
vedanā abhavissa||
niccaɱ||
dhuvaɱ||
sassataɱ,||
avipariṇāma-dhammaɱ||
sassatisamaɱ tath'eva ṭhassati||
na yidaɱ brahma-cariy-a-vāso paññāyetha sammā-dukkha-k-khayāya.|| ||

Yasmā ca kho bhikkhu,||
ettakam pi vedanā n'atthi||
niccaɱ||
dhuvaɱ||
sassataɱ,||
avipariṇāma-dhammaɱ||
sassatisamaɱ tath'eva ṭhassati||
tasmā brahma-cariy-a-vāso paññāyati sammā-dukkha-k-khayāya.|| ||

■

Ettikā pi kho bhikkhu, saññā n'atthī||
niccaɱ||
dhuvaɱ||
sassataɱ,||
avipariṇāma-dhammaɱ||
sassatisamaɱ tath'eva ṭhassati.|| ||

Ettakam ce pi bhikkhu,||
saññā abhavissa||
niccaɱ||
dhuvaɱ||
sassataɱ,||
avipariṇāma-dhammaɱ||
sassatisamaɱ tath'eva ṭhassati||
na yidaɱ brahma-cariy-a-vāso paññāyetha sammā-dukkha-k-khayāya.|| ||

Yasmā ca kho bhikkhu,||
ettakam pi saññā n'atthi||
niccaɱ||
dhuvaɱ||
sassataɱ,||
avipariṇāma-dhammaɱ||
sassatisamaɱ tath'eva ṭhassati||
tasmā brahma-cariy-a-vāso paññāyati sammā-dukkha-k-khayāya.|| ||

■

Ettikā pi kho bhikkhu, sankhārā n'atthī||
niccaɱ||
dhuvaɱ||
sassataɱ,||
avipariṇāma-dhammaɱ||
sassatisamaɱ tath'eva ṭhassati.|| ||

Ettakam ce pi bhikkhu,||
sankhārā abhavissa||
niccaɱ||
dhuvaɱ||
sassataɱ,||
avipariṇāma-dhammaɱ||
sassatisamaɱ tath'eva ṭhassati||
na yidaɱ brahma-cariy-a-vāso paññāyetha sammā-dukkha-k-khayāya.|| ||

Yasmā ca kho bhikkhu,||
ettakam pi sankhārā n'atthi||
niccaɱ||
dhuvaɱ||
sassataɱ,||
avipariṇāma-dhammaɱ||
sassatisamaɱ tath'eva ṭhassati||
tasmā brahma-cariy-a-vāso paññāyati sammā-dukkha-k-khayāya.|| ||

■

Ettikā pi kho bhikkhu, viññāṇaɱ n'atthī||
niccaɱ||
dhuvaɱ||
sassataɱ,||
avipariṇāma-dhammaɱ||
sassatisamaɱ tath'eva ṭhassati.|| ||

Ettakam ce pi bhikkhu,||
viññāṇaɱ abhavissa||
niccaɱ||
dhuvaɱ||
sassataɱ,||
avipariṇāma-dhammaɱ||
sassatisamaɱ tath'eva ṭhassati||
na yidaɱ brahma-cariy-a-vāso paññāyetha sammā-dukkha-k-khayāya.|| ||

Yasmā ca kho bhikkhu,||
ettakam pi viññāṇaɱ n'atthi||
niccaɱ||
dhuvaɱ||
sassataɱ,||
avipariṇāma-dhammaɱ||
sassatisamaɱ tath'eva ṭhassati||
tasmā brahma-cariy-a-vāso paññāyati sammā-dukkha-k-khayāya.|| ||

 

§

 

Taɱ kiɱ maññasi bhikkhu?|| ||

Rūpaɱ niccaɱ vā aniccaɱ vā" ti?|| ||

"Aniccaɱ bhante."|| ||

"Yaɱ pan-ā-niccaɱ dukkhaɱ vā taɱ sukhaɱ vā" ti?|| ||

"Dukkhaɱ bhante."|| ||

"Yaɱ pan-ā-niccaɱ dukkhaɱ vipari-ṇāma-dhammaɱ kallaɱ nu taɱ samanupassituɱ:|| ||

'Etaɱ mama,||
eso ham asmi,||
eso me attā' ti"?|| ||

"No h'etaɱ bhante."|| ||

■

"Vedanā niccaɱ vā aniccaɱ vā" ti?|| ||

"Aniccaɱ bhante."|| ||

"Yaɱ pan-ā-niccaɱ dukkhaɱ vā taɱ sukhaɱ vā" ti?|| ||

"Dukkhaɱ bhante."|| ||

"Yaɱ pan-ā-niccaɱ dukkhaɱ vipari-ṇāma-dhammaɱ kallaɱ nu taɱ samanupassituɱ:|| ||

'Etaɱ mama,||
eso ham asmi,||
eso me attā' ti"?|| ||

"No h'etaɱ bhante."|| ||

■

"Saññā niccaɱ vā aniccaɱ vā" ti?|| ||

"Aniccaɱ bhante."|| ||

"Yaɱ pan-ā-niccaɱ dukkhaɱ vā taɱ sukhaɱ vā" ti?|| ||

"Dukkhaɱ bhante."|| ||

"Yaɱ pan-ā-niccaɱ dukkhaɱ vipari-ṇāma-dhammaɱ kallaɱ nu taɱ samanupassituɱ:|| ||

'Etaɱ mama,||
eso ham asmi,||
eso me attā' ti"?|| ||

"No h'etaɱ bhante."|| ||

■

"Sankhārā niccaɱ vā aniccaɱ vā" ti?|| ||

"Aniccaɱ bhante."|| ||

"Yaɱ pan-ā-niccaɱ dukkhaɱ vā taɱ sukhaɱ vā" ti?|| ||

"Dukkhaɱ bhante."|| ||

"Yaɱ pan-ā-niccaɱ dukkhaɱ vipari-ṇāma-dhammaɱ kallaɱ nu taɱ samanupassituɱ:|| ||

'Etaɱ mama,||
eso ham asmi,||
eso me attā' ti"?|| ||

"No h'etaɱ bhante."|| ||

■

"Viññāṇaɱ niccaɱ vā aniccaɱ vā" ti?|| ||

"Aniccaɱ bhante."|| ||

"Yaɱ pan-ā-niccaɱ dukkhaɱ vā taɱ sukhaɱ vā" ti?|| ||

"Dukkhaɱ bhante."|| ||

"Yaɱ pan-ā-niccaɱ dukkhaɱ vipari-ṇāma-dhammaɱ kallaɱ nu taɱ samanupassituɱ:|| ||

'Etaɱ mama,||
eso ham asmi,||
eso me attā' ti"?|| ||

"No h'etaɱ bhante."|| ||

 

§

 

"Tasmā ti ha bhikkhu,||
yaɱ kiñci rūpaɱ atīt-ā-nāgata-pacc'uppannaɱ||
ajjhattaɱ vā bahiddhā||
vā oḷārikaɱ vā sukhumaɱ vā||
hīnaɱ vā paṇītaɱ vā||
yaɱ dūre santike vā sabbaɱ rūpaɱ:|| ||

'N'etaɱ mama||
neso ham asmi||
na me'so attā' ti.|| ||

Evam etaɱ yathā-bhūtaɱ samma-p-paññāya daṭṭhabbaɱ.|| ||

■

Yā kāci vedanā atīt-ā-nāgata-pacc'uppannaɱ||
ajjhattaɱ vā bahiddhā||
vā oḷārikaɱ vā sukhumaɱ vā||
hīnaɱ vā paṇītaɱ vā||
yaɱ dūre santike vā sabbaɱ vedanaɱ:|| ||

'N'etaɱ mama||
neso ham asmi||
na me'so attā' ti.|| ||

Evam etaɱ yathā-bhūtaɱ samma-p-paññāya daṭṭhabbaɱ.|| ||

■

Yā kāci saññā atīt-ā-nāgata-pacc'uppannaɱ||
ajjhattaɱ vā bahiddhā||
vā oḷārikaɱ vā sukhumaɱ vā||
hīnaɱ vā paṇītaɱ vā||
yaɱ dūre santike vā sabbaɱ saññaɱ:|| ||

'N'etaɱ mama||
neso ham asmi||
na me'so attā' ti.|| ||

Evam etaɱ yathā-bhūtaɱ samma-p-paññāya daṭṭhabbaɱ.|| ||

■

Ye keci sankhārā atīt-ā-nāgata-pacc'uppannaɱ||
ajjhattaɱ vā bahiddhā||
vā oḷārikaɱ vā sukhumaɱ vā||
hīnaɱ vā paṇītaɱ vā||
yaɱ dūre santike vā sabbaɱ sankhāraɱ:|| ||

'N'etaɱ mama||
neso ham asmi||
na me'so attā' ti.|| ||

Evam etaɱ yathā-bhūtaɱ samma-p-paññāya daṭṭhabbaɱ.|| ||

■

Yaɱ kiñci viññāṇaɱ atīt-ā-nāgata-pacc'uppannaɱ||
ajjhattaɱ vā bahiddhā||
vā oḷārikaɱ vā sukhumaɱ vā||
hīnaɱ vā paṇītaɱ vā||
yaɱ dūre santike vā sabbaɱ viññāṇaɱ:|| ||

'N'etaɱ mama||
neso ham asmi||
na me'so attā' ti.|| ||

Evam etaɱ yathā-bhūtaɱ samma-p-paññāya daṭṭhabbaɱ.|| ||

■

Evaɱ passaɱ bhikkhu, sutavā ariya-sāvako||
rūpasmim pi nibbandati,||
vedanāya pi nibbandati,||
saññāya pi nibbindati,||
sankhāresu pi nibbindati,||
viññāṇasmim pi nibbindati.|| ||

Nibbidaɱ virajjati.|| ||

Virāgā vimuccati.|| ||

Vimuttasmiɱ 'vimuttami' ti||
ñāṇaɱ hoti:|| ||

'Khīṇā jāti,||
vusitaɱ Brahma-cariyaɱ,||
kataɱ karaṇīyaɱ,||
nāparaɱ itthattāyā' ti pajānātī" ti.|| ||
