Saɱyutta Nikāya:
III. Khandhā Vagga:
22: Khandhāsaɱyutta

Sutta 123

Sutavatā Suttaɱ

Adapted from the 1995 edition of the digital version of the Sri Lanka Buddha Jayanti Tripitaka Series.

 

 

[1][pts] Evaɱ me sutaɱ:|| ||

Ekaɱ samayaɱ āyasmā Sāriputto||
āysamā ca Mahā-Koṭṭhito,||
Bārāṇasiyaɱ viharanti||
Isipatane Migadāye.|| ||

Atha kho āyasmā Mahā-Kotthito sāyaṇha-samayaɱ paṭisallāṇā vuṭṭhito yen'āyasmā Sāriputto ten'upasankami.|| ||

Upasaɱkamitvā āyasmatā Sāriputtena saddhiɱ sammodi.|| ||

Sammodanīyaɱ kathaɱ sāraṇīyaɱ vīti-sāretvā eka-m-antaɱ nisīdi.|| ||

Eka-m-antaɱ nisinno kho āyasmā Mahā-Kotthito||
āyasmantaɱ Sāriputtaɱ etad avoca:|| ||

"Sutavatā āvuso Sāriputta, bhikkhunā||
katame dhammā yoniso mana-sikātabbā" ti?|| ||

"Sutavatāvuso Koṭṭhita, bhikkhunā||
pañc'upādāna-k-khandhā||
aniccato,||
dukkhato,||
rogato,||
gaṇḍato,||
sallato,||
aghato,||
ābādhato,||
parato,||
palokato,||
suññato,||
anattato||
yoniso mana-sikātabbā.|| ||

Katame pañca?|| ||

Seyyath'īdaɱ:|| ||

Rūp'upādāna-k-khandho||
vedanupādāna-k-khandho||
saññupādāna-k-khandho||
saɱkhārūpādāna-k-khandho||
viññāṇapādāna-k-khandho.|| ||

Sutavatāvuso Koṭṭhika, bhikkhunā||
ime pañc'upādāna-k-khandhā||
aniccato,||
dukkhato,||
rogato,||
gaṇḍato,||
slalato,||
aghato,||
ābādhato,||
parato,||
palokato,||
suññato,||
anattato||
yoniso mana-sikātabbā.|| ||

Ṭhānaɱ kho pan'etaɱ āvuso, vijjati||

dukkhato,||
rogato,||
gaṇḍato,||
slalato,||
aghato,||
ābādhato,||
parato,||
palokato,||
suññato,||
anattato||
yoniso mananasi karonto||
sotāptiphalaɱ sacchikareyyā" ti.|| ||
