III. Khandhā Vagga:
24: Diṭṭhi Saɱutta
1. Sot'āpatti Vagga

Sutta 9

Sassata Loka Suttaɱ

Adapted from the 1995 edition of the digital version of the Sri Lanka Buddha Jayanti Tripitaka Series.

 

 

[1][pts] Evaɱ me sutaɱ:|| ||

Ekaɱ samayaɱ Bhagavā Sāvatthiyaɱ viharati Jetavane Anāthapiṇḍikassa ārāme.|| ||

|| ||

Tatra kho Bhagavā bhikkhu āmantesi:|| ||

"Bhikkhavo" ti.|| ||

"Bhadante" ti||
te bhikkh Bhagavato paccassosuɱ|| ||

Bhagavā etad avoca:|| ||

"Kismiɱ nu kho bhikkhave, sati kiɱ upādāya kiɱ abhinivissa evaɱ diṭṭhi uppajjati:|| ||

'Sassato loko' ti"?|| ||

"Bhagavaɱmulakā no bhante, dhammā.|| ||

Bhagavaɱ-nettikā Bhagavam-paṭisaraṇā.|| ||

Sādhu vata bhaṇte,||
Bhagavantaɱ yeva paṭibhātu etassa bhāsitassa attho,||
Bhagavato sutvā bhikkhu dhāressan" ti.|| ||

 

 

"Rūpe kho bhikkhave, sati rūpaɱ upādāya||
rūpaɱ abhinivissa evem diṭṭhi uppajjati:|| ||

'Sassato loko' ti.|| ||

Vedanāya sati vedanaɱ upādāya||
vedanā abhinivissa evem diṭṭhi uppajjati:|| ||

'Sassato loko' ti.|| ||

Saññāya sati saññaɱ upādāya||
saññā abhinivissa evem diṭṭhi uppajjati:|| ||

'Sassato loko' ti.|| ||

Saɱkhāresu sati||
saɱkhāre upādāya saɱkhāre abhinivissa evem diṭṭhi uppajjati:|| ||

'Sassato loko' ti.|| ||

Viññāṇe sati||
viññāṇaɱ upādāya viññāṇaɱ abhinivissa evem diṭṭhi uppajjati:|| ||

'Sassato loko' ti.|| ||

 

 

Taɱ kiɱ maññasi bhikkhave?|| ||

Rūpaɱ niccaɱ vā aniccaɱ vā" ti?|| ||

"Aniccaɱ bhante."|| ||

"Yaɱ panāniccaɱ dukkhaɱ vā taɱ sukhaɱ vā" ti?|| ||

"Dukkhaɱ bhante."|| ||

"Yaɱ panāniccaɱ dukkhaɱ vipari-ṇāma-dhammaɱ,||
api nu taɱ anupādāya evaɱ diṭṭhi uppajjeyya:|| ||

'Sassato loko' ti.|| ||

"No h'etaɱ bhante."|| ||

"Dukkhaɱ bhante."|| ||

"Yaɱ panāniccaɱ dukkhaɱ vipari-ṇāma-dhammaɱ,||
