Saɱyutta Nikāya
3. Khandha Vagga
22. Khandha Saɱyutta
2. Dutiya Anicca Vagga

Sutta 18

Paṭhama Hetu Suttaɱ

Adapted from the 1995 edition of the digital version of the Sri Lanka Buddha Jayanti Tripitaka Series.

 

[23]

[1][pts][bodh] Evaɱ me sutaɱ.|| ||

Ekaɱ samayaɱ Bhagavā Sāvatthiyaɱ viharati.|| ||

Tatra kho Bhagavā bhikkhu āmantesi:|| ||

"Bhikkhavo" ti.|| ||

"Bhadante" ti te bhikkh Bhagavato paccassosuɱ|| ||

Bhagavā etad avoca:|| ||

"Rūpaɱ bhikkhave aniccaɱ.|| ||

Yo pi hetu||
yo pi paccayo||
rūpassa uppādāya,||
so pi anicco.|| ||

Anicca-sambhūtaɱ bhikkhave,||
rūpaɱ kuto niccaɱ bhavissati?|| ||

■

"Vedanā bhikkhave aniccaɱ.|| ||

Yo pi hetu||
yo pi paccayo||
vedanāya uppādāya,||
so pi anicco.|| ||

Anicca-sambhūtaɱ bhikkhave,||
vedanā kuto niccaɱ bhavissati?|| ||

■

"Saññā bhikkhave aniccaɱ.|| ||

Yo pi hetu||
yo pi paccayo||
saññāāya uppādāya,||
so pi anicco.|| ||

Anicca-sambhūtaɱ bhikkhave,||
saññā kuto niccaɱ bhavissati?|| ||

■

"Saṅkhārā bhikkhave aniccaɱ.|| ||

Yo pi hetu||
yo pi paccayo||
saṅkhāresu uppādāya,||
so pi anicco.|| ||

Anicca-sambhūtaɱ bhikkhave,||
saṅkhārā kuto niccaɱ bhavissati?|| ||

■

"Viññāṇam bhikkhave aniccaɱ.|| ||

Yo pi hetu||
yo pi paccayo||
viññāṇassa uppādāya,||
so pi anicco.|| ||

Anicca-sambhūtaɱ bhikkhave,||
viññāṇaɱ kuto niccaɱ bhavissati?|| ||

■

Evaɱ passaɱ bhikkhave, sutavā ariya-sāvako||
rūpasmim pi nibbindati,||
vedanāya pi nibbindati,||
saññāya pi nibbindati||
saṅkhāresu pi nibbindati,||
viññāṇasmim pi nibbindati.|| ||

Nibbindaɱ virajjati,||
virāgā vimuccati,||
vimuttasmiɱ vimuttamiti||
ñāṇaɱ hoti:|| ||

'Khīṇā jāti||
vusitaɱ Brahma-cariyaɱ||
kataɱ karaṇīyaɱ||
nāparaɱ itthattāyā' ti pajānātī" ti.|| ||
