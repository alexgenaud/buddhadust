Saɱyutta Nikāya
3. Khandha Vagga
22. Khandha Saɱyutta
3. Bhāra Vagga

Sutta 30

Uppāda Suttaɱ

Adapted from the 1995 edition of the digital version of the Sri Lanka Buddha Jayanti Tripitaka Series.

 

[31]

[1][pts][bodh] Evaɱ me sutaɱ.|| ||

Ekaɱ samayaɱ Bhagavā Sāvatthiyaɱ viharati.|| ||

Tatra kho Bhagavā bhikkhu āmantesi:|| ||

"Bhikkhavo" ti.|| ||

"Bhadante" ti te bhikkh Bhagavato paccassosuɱ|| ||

Bhagavā etad avoca:|| ||

"Yo bhikkhave, rūpassa uppādo ṭhīti abhinibbatti pātu-bhāvo,||
[32] dukkhasseso uppādo rogānaɱ ṭhīti jarā-maraṇassa pātu-bhāvo.|| ||

Yo vedanāya uppādo ṭhīti abhinibbatti pātu-bhāvo,||
dukkhasseso uppādo rogānaɱ ṭhīti jarā-maraṇassa pātu-bhāvo.|| ||

Yo bhikkhave, saññāya uppādo ṭhīti abhinibbatti pātu-bhāvo,||
dukkhasseso uppādo rogānaɱ ṭhīti jarā-maraṇassa pātu-bhāvo.|| ||

Yo bhikkhave, sankhārānaɱ uppādo ṭhīti abhinibbatti pātu-bhāvo,||
dukkhasseso uppādo rogānaɱ ṭhīti jarā-maraṇassa pātu-bhāvo pātu-bhāvo.|| ||

Yo bhikkhave, viññāṇassa uppādo ṭhīti abhinibbatti pātu-bhāvo,||
dukkhasseso uppādo rogānaɱ ṭhīti jarā-maraṇassa pātu-bhāvo.|| ||

 

 

Yo ca kho bhikkhave, rūpassa nirodho vūpasamo atthaŋ-gamo,||
dukkhasseso nirodho rogānaɱ vupasamo jarā-maraṇassa atthaŋ-gamo.|| ||

Yo vedanāya nirodho vūpasamo atthaŋ-gamo,||
dukkhasseso nirodho rogānaɱ vūpasamo jarā-maraṇassa atthaŋ-gamo.|| ||

Yo saññāya nirodho vūpasamo atthaŋ-gamo,||
dukkhasseso nirodho rogānaɱ vūpasamo jarā-maraṇassa atthaŋ-gamo.|| ||

Yo saɱkhārānaɱ nirodho vūpasamo atthaŋ-gamo,||
dukkhasseso nirodho rogānaɱ vūpasamo jarā-maraṇassa atthaŋ-gamo.|| ||

Yo viññāṇassa nirodho vūpasamo atthaŋ-gamo,||
dukkhasseso nirodho rogānaɱ vūpasamo jarā-maraṇassa atthaŋ-gamo" ti.|| ||
