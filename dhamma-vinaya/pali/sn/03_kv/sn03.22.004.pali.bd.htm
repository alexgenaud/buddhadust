Sutta 4

Dutiya Hāliddikāni Suttaɱ

Adapted from the 1995 edition of the digital version of the Sri Lanka Buddha Jayanti Tripitaka Series.

 

[12]

[1][pts][bodh] Evaɱ me sutaɱ:|| ||

Ekaɱ samayaɱ āyasmā Mahā-Kaccāno avantīsu viharati kuriraghare papāte pabbate.|| ||

[13] Atha kho hāliddikāni gahapati yen'āyasmā Mahā-Kaccāno ten'upasaṅkami.|| ||

Upasaṅkamitvā āyasmantaɱ Mahā-Kaccānaɱ abhivādetvā eka-m-antaɱ nisīdi.|| ||

Eka-m-antaɱ nisinno kho hāliddikāni gahapati āyasmantaɱ Mahā-Kaccānaɱ etad avoca:|| ||

"Vuttam idaɱ bhante,||
Bhagavatā Sakka-Pañhe:

'Ye te samaṇa-brāhmaṇā taṇhā-saṅkhaya-vimuttā,||
te accanta-niṭṭhā accanta-yoga-k-khemino accanta-brahma-cārino Accantapariyosānā seṭṭhā deva-manussānan' ti.|| ||

Imassa nu kho bhante,||
Bhagavatā saṅkhittena bhāsitassa kathaɱ vitthārena attho daṭṭhabbo" ti?|| ||
