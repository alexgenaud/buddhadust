Saɱyutta Nikāya
II. Nidāna Vagga
20. Opammasaɱyutta

Sutta 3

Kula Suttaɱ

Adapted from the 1995 edition of the digital version of the Sri Lanka Buddha Jayanti Tripitaka Series.

 

 

[1][rhyc] EVAṂ ME SUTAṂ:|| ||

Ekaɱ samayaɱ Bhagavā Sāvatthiyaɱ viharati.||
Jetavane Anāthapiṇḍikassa ārāme.|| ||

[2][rhyc] Sayyathāpi bhikkhave, yāni kānici kulāni bahutthikāni appapurisāni,||
tāni suppadhaɱsiyāni honti corehi kumbhatthenakehi.|| ||

[3] Evam eva kho bhikkhave,||
yassa kassaci bhikkhuno mettā ceto-vimutti abhāvitā abahulī-katā,||
so suppadhaɱsiyo hoti amanussehi.|| ||

[4][rhyc] Seyyathā pi bhikkhave,||
yāni kānici kulāni appitthikāni bahupurisāni,||
tāni duppadhaɱsiyāni honti corehi kumbhatthenakehi.|| ||

[5] Evam eva kho bhikkhave,||
yassa kassaci bhikkhuno mettā ceto-vimutti bhāvitā bahulī-katā,||
so duppadhaɱsiyo hoti amanussehi.|| ||

[6][rhyc] Tasmā ti ha bhikkhave,||
evaɱ sikkhitabbaɱ:|| ||

Mettā no ceto-vimutti bhāvitā bhavissati||
bahulī-katā yānī-katā vatthu-katā anuṭṭhitā paricitā susamāraddhā' ti.|| ||

Evaɱ hi vo bhikkhave, sikkhitabbanti.|| ||
