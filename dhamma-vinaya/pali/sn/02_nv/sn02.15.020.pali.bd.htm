Saɱyutta-Nikāya,
Nidāna-vaggo
15. Anamatagga Saɱyuttam

Sutta 20

Vepullapabbatam Suttaɱ

Adapted from the 1995 edition of the digital version of the Sri Lanka Buddha Jayanti Tripitaka Series.

 

 

[1][pts] Evaɱ me sutaɱ:|| ||

Ekaɱ samayaɱ Bhagavā Rājagahe viharati Gijjhakūṭe pabbate.|| ||

Tatra kho Bhagavā bhikkhu āmantesi:|| ||

"Bhikkhavo" ti.|| ||

"Bhadante" ti te bhikkh Bhagavato paccassosuɱ.|| ||

Bhagavā etad avoca:|| ||

"Anamataggoyaɱ bhikkhave, saɱsāro.|| ||

Pubbā koṭi na paññāyati avijjā-nīvaraṇānaɱ sattānaɱ taṇhā-saɱyojānānaɱ sandhāvataɱ saɱsarataɱ.|| ||

Pubbā koṭi na paññāyati avijjā-nīvaraṇānaɱ sattānaɱ taṇhā-saɱyojānānaɱ sandhāvataɱ saɱsarataɱ.|| ||

■

Bhūta-pubbaɱ bhikkhave,||
imassa Vepullassa pabbatassa Pācīnavaɱso tv'eva samaññā udapādi.|| ||

Tena kho pana [191] bhikkhave,||
samayena manussānaɱ Tivarā tv'eva samaññā udapādi.|| ||

Tivarānaɱ bhikkhave,||
manussānaɱ cattārisaɱ vassa-sahassāni āyu-p-pamāṇaɱ ahosi.|| ||

Tivarā bhikkhave,||
manussā Pācīnavaɱsaŋ pabbataɱ catuhena ārohanti,||
catuhena orohanti.|| ||

Tena kho pana samayena Kakusandho Bhagavā arahaɱ Sammā-Sam-Buddho loke uppanno hoti.|| ||

Kakusandhassa bhikkhave,||
Bhagavato arahato Sammā-Sam-Buddhassa||
Vidhura-Sañjīvaɱ nāma sāvakayugaɱ ahosi||
aggaɱ bhaddayugaɱ.|| ||

Passatha bhikkhave,||
sā cevimassa pabbatassa samaññā antara-hitā.|| ||

Te ca manussā kālaɱkatā.|| ||

So ca Bhagavā parinibbuto.|| ||

Evaɱ aniccā bhikkhave, sankhārā,||
evaɱ addhuvā bhikkhave, sankhārā,||
evaɱ anassāsikā bhikkhave, sankhārā.|| ||

Yāvañ c'idaɱ bhikkhave,||
alam eva sabba-sankhāresu nibbindituɱ,||
alaɱ virajjituɱ,||
alaɱ vimuccituɱ.|| ||

■

Bhūta-pubbaɱ bhikkhave,||
imassa Vepullassa pabbatassa Vaŋkako tv'eva samaññā udapādi.|| ||

Tena kho pana bhikkhave,||
samayena manussānaɱ Rohitassā tv'eva samaññā udapādi.|| ||

Rohitassānaɱ bhikkhave,||
manussānaɱ tiɱsavassa-sahassāni āyu-p-pamāṇaɱ ahosi.|| ||

Rohitassā bhikkhave,||
manussā Vaŋkakaɱ pabbataɱ tīhena ārohanti,||
tīhena orohanti.|| ||

Tena kho pana bhikkhave,||
samayena Konāgamano||
Bhagavā arahaɱ Sammā-Sam-Buddho||
loke uppanno hoti.|| ||

Konāgamanassa bhikkhave,||
Bhagavato arahato Sammā-Sam-Buddhassa||
Bhiyyo-Suttaraɱ nāma sāvakayugaɱ ahosi||
aggaɱ bhaddayugaɱ.|| ||

Passatha bhikkhave,||
sā cevimassa pabbatassa samaññā antara-hitā.|| ||

Te ca manussā kālaɱkatā.|| ||

So ca Bhagavā parinibbuto.|| ||

[192] Evaɱ aniccā bhikkhave, sankhārā,||
evaɱ addhuvā bhikkhave, sankhārā,||
evaɱ anassāsikā bhikkhave, sankhārā.|| ||

Yāvañ c'idaɱ bhikkhave,||
alam eva sabba-sankhāresu nibbindituɱ,||
alaɱ virajjituɱ,||
alaɱ vimuccituɱ.|| ||

■

Bhūta-pubbaɱ bhikkhave,||
imassa Vepullassa pabbatassa Suphasso tv'eva samaññā udapādi.|| ||

Tena kho pana bhikkhave,||
samayena manussānaɱ Suppiya tv'eva samaññā udapādi.|| ||

Suppiyānaɱ bhikkhave,||
manussānaɱ vīsati vassa-sahassāni āyu-p-pamāṇaɱ ahosi.|| ||

Suppiyā bhikkhave,||
manussā Suphassaŋ pabbataɱ dvīhena ārohanti,||
dvīhena orohanti.|| ||

Tena kho pana bhikkhave,||
samayena Kassapo||
Bhagavā arahaɱ Sammā-Sam-Buddho||
loke uppanno hoti.|| ||

Kassapassa bhikkhave,||
Bhagavato arahato Sammā-Sam-Buddhassa||
Tissa-Bhāradvājaɱ nāma sāvakayugaɱ ahosi||
aggaɱ bhaddayugaɱ.|| ||

Passatha bhikkhave,||
sā cevimassa pabbatassa samaññā antara-hitā.|| ||

Te ca manussā kālaɱkatā.|| ||

So ca Bhagavā parinibbuto.|| ||

Evaɱ aniccā bhikkhave, sankhārā,||
evaɱ addhuvā bhikkhave, sankhārā,||
evaɱ anassāsikā bhikkhave, sankhārā.|| ||

Yāvañ c'idaɱ bhikkhave,||
alam eva sabba-sankhāresu nibbindituɱ,||
alaɱ virajjituɱ,||
alaɱ vimuccituɱ.|| ||

■

Etarahi kho pana bhikkhave,||
imassa Vepullassa pabbatassa Vepullo tv'eva samaññā udapādi.|| ||

Etarahi kho pana bhikkhave,||
imesaŋ manussānaɱ Māgadhakā tv'eva samaññā udapādi.|| ||

Māgadhakānaɱ bhikkhave,||
manussānaɱ appakaɱ āyu-p-pamāṇaɱ parittaɱ lahukaɱ.|| ||

Yo ciraɱ jīvati,||
so vassa-sataɱ,||
appaɱ vā bhiyyo.|| ||

Māgadhakā bhikkhave,||
manussā Vepullaɱ pabbataɱ muhuttena ārohanti,||
muhuttena orohanti.|| ||

Etarahi kho pan'āhaɱ bhikkhave,||
arahaɱ Sammā-Sam-Buddho||
loke uppanno.|| ||

Mayhaɱ kho pana bhikkhave,||
Sāriputta-Moggallānaɱ nāma sāvakayugaɱ||
aggaɱ bhaddayugaɱ.|| ||

Bhavissati bhikkhave, so samayo,||
yā ayañ cevimassa [193] pabbatassa samaññā antara-dhāyissati.|| ||

Ime ce manussā kālaɱ karissanti.|| ||

Ahañ ca parinibbāyissāmi.|| ||

Evaɱ aniccā bhikkhave, sankhārā,||
evaɱ addhuvā bhikkhave, sankhārā,||
evaɱ anassāsikā bhikkhave, sankhārā.|| ||

Yāvañ c'idaɱ bhikkhave,||
alam eva sabba-sankhāresu nibbindituɱ,||
alaɱ virajjituɱ,||
alaɱ vimuccituɱ" ti.|| ||

Idam avoca Bhagavā.|| ||

Idaɱ vatvā Sugato athāparaɱ etad avoca Satthā:|| ||

Pācīnavaɱso Tivarānaɱ||
Rohitassāna Vaŋkako,||
Suppiyānaɱ Suphassā ti||
Māgadhānañca Vepullo.|| ||

Aniccā vata sankhārā||
uppāda-vayadhammino,||
Uppajjitvā nirujjhanti||
tesaŋ vūpasamo sukho ti,|| ||

Duggatavaggo dutiyo.
