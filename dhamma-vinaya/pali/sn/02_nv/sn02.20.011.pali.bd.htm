20. Opammasamyutta
Sutta 11

Sigāla Suttaɱ

Adapted from the 1995 edition of the digital version of the Sri Lanka Buddha Jayanti Tripitaka Series.

 

 

[1][rhyc] EVAṂ ME SUTAṂ:|| ||

Ekaɱ samayaɱ Bhagavā Sāvatthiyaɱ viharati.||
Jetavane Anāthapiṇḍikassa ārāme.|| ||

[2] Assuttha no tumhe bhikkhave,||
rattiyā paccūsa-samayaɱ sigālassa vassamānassā ti?|| ||

Evaɱ bhante.|| ||

[3] Eso kho bhikkhave,||
jarasingālo ukkaṇṇakena nāma rogajātena phuṭṭho.|| ||

So yena yena icchati,||
tena tena gacchati.|| ||

Yattha yattha icchati,||
tattha tattha tiṭṭhati.|| ||

Yattha yattha icchati,||
tattha tattha nisīdati.|| ||

Yattha yattha icchati,||
tattha tattha nipajjati.|| ||

Sītako pi naɱ vāto upavāyati.|| ||

[4] Sādhu khvassa bhikkhave,||
yaɱ idh'ekacco Sakya-puttiya-paṭiñño eva-rūpam pi atta-bhāva-paṭilābhaɱ paṭisaŋvedayetha.|| ||

[5] Tasmā ti ha bhikkhave,||
evaɱ sikkhitabbaɱ:|| ||

"Appamattā viharissāmā" ti.|| ||

[6] Evaɱ hi vo bhikkhave, sikkhitabbanti.|| ||
