[2][pts][bodh][than][olds] Cattāro me bhikkhave, āhārā bhūtānaɱ vā sattānaɱ ṭhitiyā sambhavesīnaɱ vā anuggahāya.|| ||

[3][pts][bodh][than][olds] Katame cattāro?|| ||

Kabalinkāro āhāro||

sattānaɱ ṭhitiyā sambhavesīnaɱ vā anuggahāya.|| ||
