Saɱyutta Nikāya
II. Nidāna Vagga
14. Dhātu-Saɱyuttaɱ
IV. Catuttha Vagga

Sutta 31

Pubba Suttaɱ

Adapted from the 1995 edition of the digital version of the Sri Lanka Buddha Jayanti Tripitaka Series.

 

[169]

[1][pts] Evaɱ me sutaɱ:|| ||

Ekaɱ samayaɱ Bhagavā Sāvatthiyaɱ viharati Jetavane Anāthapiṇḍikassa ārāme.|| ||

Tatra kho Bhagavā bhikkhū āmantesi:|| ||

"Bhikkhavo" ti.|| ||

"Bhadante" ti te bhikkhū Bhagavato paccassosuɱ.|| ||

Bhagavā etad avoca:|| ||

2. [170] "Pubbe me bhikkhave, sambodhā anabhi-sambuddhassa bodhisattass'eva sato etad ahosi:|| ||

3. 'Ko nu kho paṭhavi-dhātuyā assādo ko ādīnavo kiɱ nissaraṇaɱ?|| ||

Ko āpo-dhātuyā assādo ko ādīnavo kiɱ nissaraṇaɱ?|| ||

Ko tejo-dhātuyā assādo ko ādīnavo kiɱ nissaraṇaɱ?|| ||

Ko vāyo-dhātuyā assādo ko ādīnavo kiɱ nissaraṇan' ti?|| ||

4. Tassa mayhaɱ bhikkhave, etad ahosi:|| ||

5. Yaɱ kho paṭhavi-dhātuɱ paṭicca uppajjati sukhaɱ somanassaɱ,||
ayaɱ paṭhavi-dhātuyā assādo.|| ||

Yaɱ paṭhavi-dhātuyā aniccā dukkhā vipariṇāma-dhammā,||
ayaɱ paṭhavi-dhātuyā ādīnavo.|| ||

Yaɱ paṭhavi-dhātuyā chanda-rāga-vinayo chanda-rāga-p-pahāṇaɱ,||
idaɱ paṭhavi-dhātuyā nissaraṇaɱ.|| ||

6. Yaɱ āpo-dhātuɱ paṭicca uppajjati sukhaɱ somanassaɱ,||
ayaɱ āpo-dhātuyā assādo.|| ||

Yaɱ āpo-dhātu aniccā dukkhā vipariṇāma-dhammā,||
ayaɱ āpo-dhātuyā ādīnavo.|| ||

Yaɱ āpo-dhātuyā chanda-rāga-vinayo chanda-rāga-p-pahāṇaɱ,||
idaɱ āpo-dhātuyā nissaraṇaɱ.|| ||

7. Yaɱ tejo-dhātuɱ paṭicca uppajjati sukhaɱ somanassaɱ,||
ayaɱ tejo-dhātuyā assādo.|| ||

Yaɱ tejo-dhātu aniccā dukkhā vipariṇāma-dhammā,||
ayaɱ tejo-dhātuyā ādīnavo.|| ||

Yaɱ tejo-dhātuyā chanda-rāga-vinayo chanda-rāga-p-pahāṇaɱ,||
idaɱ tejo-dhātuyā nissaraṇaɱ.|| ||

8. Yaɱ vāyo-dhātuɱ paṭicca uppajjati sukhaɱ somanassaɱ,||
ayaɱ vāyo-dhātuyā assādo.|| ||

Yaɱ vāyo-dhātu aniccā dukkhā vipariṇāma-dhammā,||
ayaɱ vāyo-dhātuyā ādīnavo.|| ||

Yo vāyo-dhātuyā chanda-rāga-vinayo chanda-rāga-p-pahāṇaɱ,||
idaɱ vāyo-dhātuyā nissaraṇaɱ.|| ||

9. Yāva kīvañc'āhaɱ bhikkhave,||
imāsaɱ catunnaɱ dhātūnaɱ evaɱ assādañ ca||
assādato ādīnavañ ca||
ādīnavato nissaraṇañ ca||
nissaraṇato yathā-bhūtaɱ nābbhaññāsiɱ,||
n'eva tāvāhaɱ bhikkhave,||
sa-devake loke sa-mārake sa-brahmake,||
sa-s-samaṇa-brāhmaṇiyā pajāya sadeva-manussāya anuttaraɱ sammā-sambodhiɱ abhisambuddho ti paccaññāsiɱ.|| ||

10. Yato cakkhvāhaɱ bhikkhave,||
imāsaɱ catunnaɱ dhātūnaɱ evaɱ assādañ ca||
assādato ādīnavañ ca||
ādīnavato nissaraṇañ ca||
nissaraṇato yathā-bhūtaɱ abbhaññāsiɱ,||
ath'āhaɱ bhikkhave,||
sa-devake loke sa-mārake sa-brahmake,||
sa-s-samaṇa-brāhmaṇiyā pajāya sadeva-manussāya anuttaraɱ sammā-sambodhiɱ abhisambuddhoti paccaññāsiɱ.|| ||

11. [171] Ñāṇañ ca pana me dassanaɱ udapādi:|| ||

'Akuppā me ceto-vimutti||
ayam antimā jāti||
n'atthi-dāni puna-b-bhavo'" ti.|| ||
