Saɱyutta Nikāya
II. Nidāna Vagga
18. Rāhula Saɱyuttam
1. Pathama Vagga

Sutta 4

Samphassa Suttaɱ

Adapted from the 1995 edition of the digital version of the Sri Lanka Buddha Jayanti Tripitaka Series.

 

[246]

[1][pts] Evaɱ me sutaɱ:|| ||

Evaɱ me sutaɱ, ekaɱ samayaɱ Bhagavā Sāvatthiyaɱ viharati Jetavane Anāthapiṇḍikassa ārāme.|| ||

Atha kho āyasmā Rāhulo yena Bhagavā ten'upasankami.|| ||

Upasaŋkamitvā Bhagavantaɱ abhivādetvā eka-m-antaɱ nisīdi.|| ||

Eka-m-antaɱ nisinno kho āyasmā Rāhulo Bhagavantaɱ etad avoca:|| ||

"Sādhu me bhante,||
Bhagavā saŋkkhittena dhammaɱ desetu yam ahaɱ Bhagavato dhammaɱ sutvā eko vūpakaṭṭho appamatto ātāpī pahit'atto vihareyyan" ti.|| ||

"Taɱ kiɱ maññasi Rāhula,||
cakkhu-samphasso nicco vā anicco vā" ti?|| ||

"Aniccaɱ bhante."|| ||

"Yam pan-ā-niccaɱ dukkhaɱ vā taɱ sukhaɱ vā" ti?|| ||

"Dukkhaɱ bhante."|| ||

"Yam pan-ā-niccaɱ dukkhaɱ vipari-ṇāma-dhammaɱ,||
kallaɱ nu taɱ samanupassituɱ:|| ||

'Etaɱ mama,||
eso ham asmi,||
eso me attā' ti?"|| ||

"No h'etaɱ bhante."|| ||
