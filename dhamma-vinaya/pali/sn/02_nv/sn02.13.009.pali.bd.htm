Saɱyutta Nikāya
II. Nidāna Vagga
13. Abhisamaya Saɱyutta

Sutta 9

Paṭhama Pabbat'Ūpama Suttaɱ

Adapted from the 1995 edition of the digital version of the Sri Lanka Buddha Jayanti Tripitaka Series.

 

[137]

[1][pts] Evaɱ me sutaɱ:|| ||

Ekaɱ samayaɱ Bhagavā Sāvatthiyaɱ viharati Jetavane Anāthapiṇḍikassa ārāme.|| ||

Tatra kho Bhagavā bhikkhu āmantesi:|| ||

"Bhikkhavo" ti.|| ||

"Bhadante" ti te bhikkh Bhagavato paccassosuɱ|| ||

Bhagavā etad avoca:|| ||

"Seyyathā pi bhikkhave, puriso Himavato pabba-tarājassa satta sāsapa-mattiyo pāsāṇa-sakkharā upanikkhipeyya.|| ||

Taɱ kiɱ maññatha bhikkhave?|| ||

Katamannukho bahutaraɱ yā vā satta sāsapa-mattiyo pāsāṇa-sakkharā upanikkhittā,||
yo vā himavā pabba-tarājā" ti.|| ||

"Etad eva bhante, bahutaraɱ yad idaɱ himavā pabba-tarājā.|| ||

Appamattikā satta sāsapa-mattiyo pāsāṇa-sakkharā [138] upanikkhittā.|| ||

N'eva satimaɱ kalaɱ upenti,||
na sahassimaɱ kalaɱ upenti,||
na satasahassimaɱ kalaɱ upenti,||
himavantaɱ pabba-tarājaɱ upanidhāya satta sāsapa-mattiyo pāsāṇa-sakkharā upanikkhittā" ti.|| ||

"Evam eva kho bhikkhave,||
ariya-sāvakassa diṭṭhi-sampannassa puggalassa abhisametāvino etad eva bahutaraɱ dukkhaɱ||
yad idaɱ parikkhīṇaɱ pariyādinnaɱ,||
appamattakaɱ avasiṭṭhaɱ.|| ||

N'eva satimaɱ kalaɱ upeti,||
na sahassimaɱ kalaɱ upeti,||
na satasahassimaɱ kalaɱ upeti,||
purimaɱ dukkha-k-khandhaɱ parikkhīṇaɱ pariyādinnaɱ upanidhāya||
yad idaɱ satta-k-khattūɱ paramatā.|| ||

Evaɱ mahatthiyo kho bhikkhave dhammābhisamayo,||
evaɱ mahatthiyo Dhamma-cakkhu paṭilābho" ti.|| ||
