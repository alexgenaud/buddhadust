Saṃyutta Nikāya
II. Nidāna Vagga
18. Rāhula Saṃyuttam
2. Dutiya Vagga

Sutta 14

Samphasso Suttaṃ

Adapted from the 1995 edition of the digital version of the Sri Lanka Buddha Jayanti Tripitaka Series.

 

[251]

[1][pts] Evaṃ me sutaṃ:|| ||

Evaṃ me sutaṃ, ekaṃ samayaṃ Bhagavā Sāvatthiyaṃ viharati Jetavane Anāthapiṇḍikassa ārāme.|| ||

Atha kho āyasmā Rāhulo yena Bhagavā ten'upasaṇkami.|| ||

Upasaṇkamitvā Bhagavantaṃ abhivādetvā eka-m-antaṃ nisīdi.|| ||

Eka-m-antaṃ nisinnaṃ kho āyasmantaṃ Rāhulaṃ Bhagavā etad avoca:|| ||

"Taṃ kiṃ maññasi Rāhula,||
cakkhu-samphasso nicco vā anicco vā" ti?|| ||

"Aniccaṃ bhante."|| ||

"Yam panāniccaṃ dukkhaṃ vā taṃ sukhaṃ vā" ti?|| ||

"Dukkhaṃ bhante."|| ||

"Yam panāniccaṃ dukkhaṃ vipariṇāma-dhammaṃ,||
kallaṃ nu taṃ samanupassituṃ:|| ||

'Etaṃ mama,||
eso ham asmi,||
eso me attā' ti?"|| ||

"No h'etaṃ bhante."|| ||
