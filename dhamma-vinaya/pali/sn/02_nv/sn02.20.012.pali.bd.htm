20. Opammasamyutta
Sutta 12

Dutiya Sigāla Suttaɱ

Adapted from the 1995 edition of the digital version of the Sri Lanka Buddha Jayanti Tripitaka Series.

 

 

[1][rhyc] EVAṂ ME SUTAṂ:|| ||

Ekaɱ samayaɱ Bhagavā Sāvatthiyaɱ viharati.||
Jetavane Anāthapiṇḍikassa ārāme.|| ||

[2] Assuttha no tumhe bhikkhave,||
rattiyā paccūsa-samayaɱ sigālassa vassamānassā' ti?|| ||

Evaɱ bhante.|| ||

[3] Siyā kho bhikkhave,||
tasmiɱ jarasigāle yā kāci kataññutā kata-veditā,||
na tv'eva idh'ekacce Sakya-puttiya-paṭiññe pi yā kāci kataññutā kata-veditā.|| ||

[4] Tasmāt ti ha bhikkhave,||
evaɱ sikkhitabbaɱ:|| ||

"Kataññuno bhavissāma kata-vedino,||
amhesu appakam pi kataɱ mā nassissatī" ti.|| ||

Evaɱ hi vo bhikkhave, sikkhitabban ti.|| ||

Opammasaŋyuttaɱ samattaɱ.|| ||
