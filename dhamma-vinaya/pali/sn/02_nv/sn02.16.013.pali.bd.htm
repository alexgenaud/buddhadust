Saɱyutta-Nikāya,
Nidāna-vaggo
16. Kassapa Saɱyutta

Sutta 13

Sad'Dhamma-Patirūpaka Suttaɱ

Adapted from the 1995 edition of the digital version of the Sri Lanka Buddha Jayanti Tripitaka Series.

 

[223]

[1][pts][than][upal][bodh] Evaɱ me sutaɱ:|| ||

Ekaɱ samayaɱ Bhagavā Sāvatthiyaɱ viharati Jetavane Anāthapiṇḍikassa ārāme.|| ||

2. Atha kho āyasmā Mahā-Kassapo yena Bhagavā ten'upasankami.|| ||

Upasaŋkamitvā Bhagavantaɱ abhivādetvā eka-m-antaɱ nisīdi.|| ||

Eka-m-antaɱ nisinno kho āyasmā Mahā-Kassapo Bhagavantaɱ etad avoca:|| ||

Ko nu kho bhante, hetu ko paccayo yena pubbe appatarāni c'eva sikkhā-padāni ahesuɱ,||
bahutarā ca bhikkhu aññāya saṇṭhahiɱsu?|| ||

Ko pana bhante hetu ko paccayo yen'etarahi bahutarāni c'eva sikkhā-padāni appatarā ca bhikkhū aññāya saṇṭhahantī' ti?|| ||

4. Evaɱ h'etaɱ Kassapa, hoti||
sattesu hāyamānesu,||
Sad'Dhamme antara-dhāyamāne,||
bahutarāni c'eva sikkhā-padāni honti||
appatarā ca bhikkhū aññāya saṇṭhahanti.|| ||

5. Na tāva Kassapa, Sad'Dhammassa antara-dhānaɱ hoti,||
yāva na Sad'Dhammapaṭirūpakaɱ loke uppajjati.|| ||

Yato ca kho Kassapa,||
Sad'Dhammapaṭirūpakaɱ loke uppajjati,||
atha Sad'Dhammassa antara-dhānaɱ hoti.|| ||

6. Seyyathā pi Kassapa,||
na tāva jāta-rūpassa antara-dhānaɱ hoti,||
yāva na jāta-rūpapaṭirūpakaɱ loke uppajjati||
yato ca kho Kassapa, jāta-rūpapaṭirūpakaɱ loke uppajjati,||
atha jāta-rūpassa antara-dhānaɱ hoti.|| ||

7. Evam eva kho Kassapa,||
na tāva Sad'Dhammassa antara-dhānaɱ hoti,||
yāva na Sad'Dhammapaṭirūpakaɱ loke uppajjati,||
yato ca kho Kassapa Sad'Dhammapaṭirūpakaɱ loke uppajjati,||
atha Sad'Dhammassa antara-dhānaɱ hoti.|| ||

8. Na kho Kassapa,||
paṭhavīdhātu Sad'Dhammaɱ antaradhāpeti.|| ||

9. Na āpo-dhātu Sad'Dhammaɱ antaradhāpeti.|| ||

10. Na tejo-dhātu Sad'Dhammaɱ antaradhāpeti.|| ||

11. Na vāyo-dhātu Sad'Dhammaɱ antaradhāpeti.|| ||

12. Atha kho idh'eva te uppajjanti mogha-purisā ye imaɱ Sad'Dhammaɱ antaradhāpenti.|| ||

13. Seyyathā pi Kassapa, nāvā ādiken'eva opilavati,||
na kho Kassapa, evaɱ Sad'Dhammassa antara-dhānaɱ hoti.|| ||

14. Pañca kho me Kassapa, okkamaṇiyā dhammā Sad'Dhammassa sammosāya antara-dhānāya saŋvattanti.|| ||

Katame pañca?|| ||

15. Idha Kassapa, bhikkhū,||
bhikkhuniyo,||
upāsakā,||
upāsikāyo||
satthari agāravā viharanti appatissā,||
dhamme agāravā viharanti appatissā,||
Saŋgha agāravā viharanti appatissā,||
sikkhāya agāravā viharanti appatissā,||
samādhismiɱ agāravā viharanti appatissā.|| ||

Ime kho Kassapa, pañca okkamaṇiyā dhammā Sad'Dhammassa sammosāya antara-dhānāya saŋvattanti.|| ||

16. Pañca kho me Kassapa, dhammā Sad'Dhammassa ṭhitiyā asammosāya antara-dhānāya saŋvattanti.|| ||

Katame pañca?|| ||

17. Idha Kassapa, bhikkhu,||
bhikkhuniyo,||
upāsakā,||
upāsikāyo||
satthari sagāravā viharanti sappatissā,||
dhamme sagāravā viharanti sappatissā,||
saŋghe sagāravā viharanti sappatissā,||
sikkhāya sagāravā viharanti sappatissā,||
samādhismiɱ sagāravā viharanti sappatissā.|| ||

18. Ime kho Kassapa, pañca dhammā Sad'Dhammassa ṭhitiyā asammosāya antara-dhānāya saŋvattantī' ti.|| ||
