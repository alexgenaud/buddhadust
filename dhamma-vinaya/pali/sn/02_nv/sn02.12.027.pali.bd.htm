3. Dasa-Balā Vagga

Sutta 27

Paccaya Suttaɱ

Adapted from the 1995 edition of the digital version of the Sri Lanka Buddha Jayanti Tripitaka Series.

 

[42]

[1][pts][olds][bodh] Evaɱ me sutaɱ:|| ||

Ekaɱ samayaɱ Sāvatthiyaɱ-|| ||

2. "Avijjā-paccayā bhikkhave sankhārā,||
sankhāra-paccayā viññāṇaɱ,||
viññāṇa-paccayā nāma-rūpaɱ,||

saḷāyatana-paccayā phasso,||
phassa-paccayā vedanā,||
vedanā-paccayā taṇhā,||

upādāna-paccayā bhavo,||
bhava-paccayā jāti,||
jāti-paccayā jarā-māraṇaɱ,||
soka-parideva-dukkha-domanass-upāyāsā sambhavanti.|| ||

Evam etassa kevalassa dukkha-k-khandhassa samudayo hoti.|| ||

 

§

 

Katamañ ca bhikkhave jarā-māraṇaɱ?|| ||

Yā tesaŋ tesaŋ sattānaɱ tamhi tamhi satta-nikāye jarā jīraṇatā khaṇḍiccaɱ pāliccaɱ valittacatā āyuno saŋhāni indriyānaɱ paripāko,||
ayaɱ vuccati jarā.|| ||

Yā tesaŋ tesaŋ sattānaɱ tamhā tamhā satta-nikāyā cuti cavanatā bhedo antara-dhānaɱ maccu-māraṇaɱ kāla-kiriyā khandhānaɱ bhedo kaḷebarassa nikkhepo jīvit'indriyassa upacchedo,||
idaɱ vuccati māraṇaɱ.|| ||

Iti ayañ ca jarā idaɱ ca māraṇaɱ idaɱ vuccati bhikkhave jarā-māraṇaɱ.|| ||

Jāti-samudayā jarā-māraṇa-samudayo,||
jāti-nirodhā jarā-māraṇa-nirodho.|| ||

Ayam eva Ariyo Aṭṭhaŋgiko Maggo jarā-māraṇa-nirodha-gāminī paṭipadā.|| ||

Seyyath'īdaɱ:|| ||

Sammā-diṭṭhi,||
sammā-saŋkappo,||
sammā-vācā,||
sammā-kammanto,||
sammā-ājīvo,||
sammā-vāyāmo,||
sammā-sati,||
sammā-samādhi.|| ||

khandhānaɱ pātu-bhāvo āyatanānaɱ paṭilābho,||
ayaɱ vuccati bhikkhave jāti.|| ||

Bhava-samudayā jāti-samudayo.|| ||

Bhava-nirodhā jāti-nirodho.|| ||
