Saɱyutta Nikāya
II. Nidāna Vagga
12. Nidāna Saɱyutta
3. Dasa-Balā Vagga

Sutta 25

Bhūmija Suttaɱ

Adapted from the 1995 edition of the digital version of the Sri Lanka Buddha Jayanti Tripitaka Series.

 

 

[1][pts][than][bodh] Evaɱ me sutaɱ:|| ||

Ekaɱ samayaɱ Sāvatthiyaɱ-|| ||

2. Atha kho āyasmā Bhumijo sāyaṇha-samayaɱ patisallānā vuṭṭhito yen'āyasmā Sāriputto ten'upasankami.|| ||

[38] Upasaŋkamitvā āyasmatā Sāriputtena saddhiɱ sammodi.|| ||

Sammodanīyaɱ kathaɱ sārāṇīyaɱ vīti-sāretvā eka-m-antaɱ nisīdi.|| ||

Eka-m-antaɱ nisinno kho āyasmā Bhumijo āyasmantaɱ Sāriputtaɱ etad avoca:|| ||

3. Santāvuso Sāriputta,||
eke samaṇa-brāhmaṇā kamma-vādā sayaŋ-kataɱ sukha-dukkhaɱ paññā-penti.|| ||

Santi pan'āvuso Sāriputta,||
eke samaṇa-brāhmaṇā kamma-vādā paraɱ-kataɱ sukha-dukkhaɱ paññā-penti.|| ||

Santi pan'āvuso Sāriputta,||
eke samaṇa-brāhmaṇā kamma-vādā sayaɱ-katañ ca paraɱ-katañ ca sukha-dukkhaɱ paññā-penti.|| ||

Santi pan'āvuso Sāriputta,||
eke samaṇa-brāhmaṇā kamma-vādā asayaɱ-kāraɱ aparaɱ-kāraɱ adhicca-samuppannaɱ sukha-dukkhaɱ paññā-penti.|| ||

4. Idha pan'āvuso Sāriputta, Samaṇo Gotamo kiɱ vādī,||
kim akkhāyī?|| ||

Kathaɱ vyākaramānā ca mayaɱ vutta-vādino c'eva Samaṇassa Gotamassa assāma||
na ca Samaṇaɱ Gotamaɱ abhūtena abbh'ācikkheyyāma?|| ||

Dhammassa c'ānudhammaɱ vyākareyyāma||
na ca koci saha-dhammiko vād-ā-nupāto gārayhaɱ ṭhānaɱ āgaccheyyā" ti?|| ||

Paṭicca-samuppannaɱ kho āvuso dukkhaɱ vuttaɱ Bhagavatā.

Kim paṭicca?|| ||

Phassaŋ paṭicca.|| ||

Iti vadaɱ vuttavādī c'eva Bhagavato assa||
na ca Bhagavantaɱ abhūtena abbh'ācikkheyya||
dhammassa c'ānudhammaɱ vyākareyya||
na ca koci saha-dhammiko vād-ā-nupāto gārayhaɱ ṭhānaɱ āgaccheyya.|| ||

8. Tatra āvuso ye te samaṇa-brāhmaṇā kamma-vādā sayaɱ-kataɱ dukkhaɱ paññā-penti,||
tad api phassapacacayā.|| ||

Ye pi te samaṇa-brāhmaṇā kamma-vādā paraɱ-kataɱ dukkhaɱ paññā-penti||
tad api phassa-paccayā.|| ||

Ye pi te samaṇa-brāhmaṇā kamma-vādā sayaɱ-katañ ca paraɱ-katañ ca dukkhaɱ paññā-penti,||
tad api phassa-paccayā.|| ||

Ye pi te samaṇa-brāhmaṇā kamma-vādā asayaɱ-kāraɱ aparaɱ-kāraɱ adhicca-samuppannaɱ dukkhaɱ paññā-penti,||
tad api phassa-paccayā.|| ||

9. Tatra āvuso ye te samaṇa-brāhmaṇā kamma-vādā sayaɱ-kataɱ dukkhaɱ paññā-penti,||
te vata aññatra phassā paṭisaŋvedissantī' ti||
n'etaɱ ṭhānaɱ vijjati.|| ||

Ye pi te samaṇa-brāhmaṇā kamma-vādā paraɱ-kataɱ dukkhaɱ paññā-penti,||
te vata aññatra phassā paṭisaŋvedissantī' ti||
n'etaɱ ṭhānaɱ vijjati.|| ||

Ye pi te [39] samaṇa-brāhmaṇā kamma-vādā sayaɱ-katañ ca paraɱ-katañ ca dukkhaɱ paññā-penti,||
te vata aññatra phassā paṭisaŋvedissantī' ti||
n'etaɱ ṭhānaɱ vijjati.|| ||

Ye pi te samaṇa-brāhmaṇā kamma-vādā asayaɱ-kāraɱ aparaɱ-kāraɱ adhicca-samuppannaɱ dukkhaɱ paññā-penti,||
te vata aññatra phassā paṭisaŋvedissantī' ti||
n'etaɱ ṭhānaɱ vijjatī' ti.|| ||

 

 

Assosi kho āyasmā Ānando āyasmato Sāriputtassa āyasmatā bhūmijena saddhiɱ imaɱ kathā-sallāpaɱ.|| ||

Atha kho āyasmā Ānando yena Bhagavā ten'upasankami.|| ||

Upasaŋkamitvā Bhagavantaɱ abhivādetvā emantaɱ nisīdi.|| ||

Eka-m-antaɱ nisinno kho āyasmā Ānando yāvatako āyasmato Sāriputtassa āyasmatā bhūmijena saddhiɱ ahosi kathā-sallāpo,||
taɱ sabbaɱ Bhagavato ārocesi.|| ||

 

 

Sādhu sādhu Ānanda,||
yathā taɱ Sāriputto ca sammā vyākaramāno vyākareyya,||
paṭicca-samuppannaɱ kho Ānanda dukkhaɱ vuttaɱ mayā.|| ||

Kiɱ paṭicca?|| ||

Phassaŋ paṭicca.|| ||

Iti vadaɱ vuttavādī c'eva me assa||
na ca maɱ abhūtena abbh'ācikkheyya,||
dhammassa c'ānudhammaɱ vyākareyya,||
na ca ko ci saha-dhammiko vād-ā-nupāto gārayhaɱ ṭhānaɱ āgaccheyya.|| ||

13. Tatr'Ānanda, ye te samaṇa-brāhmaṇā kamma-vādā sayaɱ-kataɱ dukkhaɱ paññā-penti,||
tad api phassa-paccayā.|| ||

Ye pi te samaṇa-brāhmaṇā kamma-vādā paraɱ-kataɱ dukkhaɱ paññā-penti,||
tad api phassa-paccayā.|| ||

Ye pi te samaṇa-brāhmaṇā kamma-vādā sayaɱ-katañ ca paraɱ-katañ ca dukkhaɱ paññā-penti,||
tad api phassa-paccayā.|| ||

Ye pi te samaṇa-brāhmaṇā kamma-vādā asayaɱ-kāraɱ aparaɱ-kāraɱ adhicca-samuppannaɱ dukkhaɱ paññā-penti,||
tad api phassa-paccayā.|| ||

14. Tatr'Ānanda ye pi te samaṇa-brāhmaṇā kamma-vādā sayaɱ-kataɱ dukkhaɱ paññā-penti,||
te vata aññatra phassā paṭisaŋvedissantī' ti||
n'etaɱ ṭhānaɱ vijjati.|| ||

Ye pi te samaṇa-brāhmaṇā kamma-vādā paraɱ-kataɱ dukkhaɱ paññā-penti, te vata aññatra phassā paṭisaŋvedissantī' ti||
n'etaɱ ṭhānaɱ vijjati.|| ||

Ye pi te samaṇa-brāhmaṇā kamma-vādā sayaɱ-katañ ca paraɱ-katañ ca dukkhaɱ paññā-penti, te vata aññatra phassā paṭisaŋvedissantī' ti||
n'etaɱ ṭhānaɱ vijjati.|| ||

Ye pi te samaṇa-brāhmaṇā kamma-vādā asayaɱ-kāraɱ aparaɱ-kāraɱ adhicca-samuppannaɱ dukkhaɱ paññā-penti, te vata aññatra phassā paṭisaŋvedissantī' ti||
n'etaɱ ṭhānaɱ vijjati.|| ||

 

 

13. Kāye vā h'Ānanda sati kāya-sañcetanā-hetu uppajjati [40] ajjhattaɱ sukha-dukkhaɱ,||
vācāya vā h'Ānanda sati vacī-sañcetanā-hetu uppajjati ajjhattaɱ sukha-dukkhaɱ,||
mane vā h'Ānanda sati mano-sañcetanā-hetu uppajjati ajjhattaɱ sukha-dukkhaɱ.|| ||

■

Avijjā-paccayā va sāmaɱ vā taɱ Ānanda kāya-sankhāraɱ abhisankharoti||
yam paccayāssa taɱ uppajjati ajjhattaɱ sukha-dukkhaɱ,||
pare vāssa taɱ Ānanda kāya-sankhāraɱ abhisankharonti||
yam paccayāssa taɱ uppajjati ajjhattaɱ sukha-dukkhaɱ.|| ||

Sampajāno vā taɱ Ānanda kāya-sankhāraɱ abhisankharoti||
yaɱ paccayāssa taɱ uppajjati ajjhattaɱ sukha-dukkhaɱ,||
asampajāno vā taɱ Ānanda kāya-sankhāraɱ abhisankharoti||
yaɱ paccayāssa taɱ uppajjati ajjhattaɱ sukha-dukkhaɱ.|| ||

Avijjā-paccayā va sāmaɱ vā taɱ Ānanda vacī-sankhāraɱ abhisankharoti||
yam paccayāssa taɱ uppajjati ajjhattaɱ sukha-dukkhaɱ,||
pare vāssa taɱ Ānanda vacī-sankhāraɱ abhisankharonti||
yam paccayāssa taɱ uppajjati ajjhattaɱ sukha-dukkhaɱ.|| ||

Sampajāno vā taɱ Ānanda vacī-sankhāraɱ abhisankharoti||
yaɱ paccayāssa taɱ uppajjati ajjhattaɱ sukha-dukkhaɱ,||
asampajāno vā taɱ Ānanda vacī-sankhāraɱ abhisankharoti||
yaɱ paccayāssa taɱ uppajjati ajjhattaɱ sukha-dukkhaɱ.|| ||

Avijjā-paccayā va sāmaɱ vā taɱ Ānanda mano-sankhāraɱ abhisankharoti||
yam paccayāssa taɱ uppajjati ajjhattaɱ sukha-dukkhaɱ,||
pare vāssa taɱ Ānanda mano-sankhāraɱ abhisankharonti||
yam paccayāssa taɱ uppajjati ajjhattaɱ sukha-dukkhaɱ.|| ||

Sampajāno vā taɱ Ānanda mano-sankhāraɱ abhisankharoti||
yaɱ paccayāssa taɱ uppajjati ajjhattaɱ sukha-dukkhaɱ,||
asampajāno vā taɱ Ānanda mano-sankhāraɱ abhisankharoti||
yaɱ paccayāssa taɱ uppajjati ajjhattaɱ sukha-dukkhaɱ.|| ||

■

Imesu Ānanda dhammesu avijjā anupatitā.|| ||

Avijjāya tv'eva Ānanda asesa-virāga-nirodhā||
so kāyo na hoti||
yaɱ paccayāssa taɱ uppajjati ajjhattaɱ sukha-dukkhaɱ.|| ||

Sā vācā na hoti||
yaɱ paccayāssa taɱ uppajjati ajjhattaɱ sukha-dukkhaɱ.|| ||

So mano na hoti||
yaɱ paccayāssa taɱ [41] uppajjati ajjhattaɱ sukha-dukkhaɱ.|| ||

Khettaɱ taɱ na hoti||
vatthu taɱ na hoti||
āyatanaɱ taɱ na hoti||
adhikaraṇaɱ taɱ na hoti||
yaɱ paccayāssa taɱ uppajjati ajjhattaɱ sukha-dukkhan" ti.|| ||
