II. Nidāna Vagga
12. Nidāna Saɱyutta
6. Rukkha Vagga

Sutta 54

Dutiya Saŋyojana Suttaɱ

Adapted from the 1995 edition of the digital version of the Sri Lanka Buddha Jayanti Tripitaka Series.

 

[87]

[1][pts][bodh] Evaɱ me sutaɱ:|| ||

Ekaɱ samayaɱ Bhagavā Sāvatthiyaɱ viharati Jetavane Anāthapiṇḍikassa ārāme.|| ||

2. Tatra kho Bhagavā bhikkhu āmantesi:|| ||

"Bhikkhavo" ti.|| ||

"Bhadante" ti te bhikkh Bhagavato paccassosuɱ|| ||

3. Bhagavā etad avoca:|| ||

"Seyyathā pi bhikkhave, telaɱ ca paṭicca vaṭṭiɱ ca paṭicca telappadīpo jhāyeyya,||
tatra puriso kālena kālaɱ telaɱ āsiñceyya,||
vaṭṭiɱ upasaŋhareyya.|| ||

Evaɱ hi so bhikkhave telappadīpo tadāhāro tadūpādāno ciraɱ dīgham addhānaɱ jaleyya.|| ||

Evam eva kho bhikkhave,||
saŋyojanīyesu dhammesu assādānupassino viharato taṇhā pavaḍḍhati.|| ||

Taṇhā-paccayā upādānaɱ.|| ||

Upādāna-paccayā bhavo.|| ||

Bhava-paccayā jāti.|| ||

Jāti-paccayā jarā-maraṇaɱ,||
soka-parideva-dukkha-domanass-upāyāsā sambhavanti.|| ||

Evam etassa kevalassa dukkha-k-khandhassa samudayo hoti.|| ||

 

§

 

Seyyathā pi bhikkhave, telaɱ ca paṭicca vaṭṭiɱ ca paṭicca telappadīpo jhāyeyya,||
tatra puriso na kālena kālaɱ telaɱ āsiñceyya,||
na vaṭṭiɱ ca upasaŋhareyya.|| ||

Evaɱ hi so bhikkhave telappadīpo purimassa ca upādānassa pariyādānā aññassa ca anupahārā an-āhāro nibbāyeyya.|| ||

Evam eva kho bhikkhave,||
saŋyojanīyesu dhammesu ādīnavānupassino viharato taṇhā nirujjhati.|| ||

Taṇhā-nirodhā upādāna-nirodho.|| ||

Upādāna-nirodhā bhava-nirodho.|| ||

Bhava-nirodhā jāti-nirodho.|| ||

Jāti-nirodhā jarā-maraṇaɱ,||
soka-parideva-dukkha-domanass-upāyāsā nirujjhanti.|| ||

Evam etassa kevalassa dukkha-k-khandhassa nirodho hotī" ti.|| ||
