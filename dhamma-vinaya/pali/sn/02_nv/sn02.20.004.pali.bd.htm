Saɱyutta Nikāya
II. Nidāna Vagga
20. Opammasaɱyutta

Sutta 4

Ukkhāsata Suttaɱ

Adapted from the 1995 edition of the digital version of the Sri Lanka Buddha Jayanti Tripitaka Series.

 

 

[1][rhyc][than][olds] EVAṂ ME SUTAṂ:|| ||

Ekaɱ samayaɱ Bhagavā Sāvatthiyaɱ viharati.||
Jetavane Anāthapiṇḍikassa ārāme.|| ||

[2] Yo bhikkhave, pubbaṇha-samayaɱ ukkhāsataɱ dānaɱ dadeyya,||
yo majjhantikaɱ-samayaɱ ukkhāsataɱ dānaɱ dadeyya,||
yo vā sāyaṇha-samayaɱ ukkhāsataɱ dānaɱ dadeyya,||
yo vā pubbaṇha-samayaɱ antamaso gaddūhana-matta pi mettaɱ cittaɱ bhāveyya,||
yo vā majjhantikaɱ-samayaɱ antamaso gaddūhana-matta pi mettaɱ cittaɱ bhāveyya,||
yo vā sāyaṇha-samayaɱ antamaso gaddūhana-matta pi mettaɱ cittaɱ bhāveyya,||
idaɱ tato maha-p-phalataraɱ.|| ||

[3] Tasmā ti ha bhikkhave,||
evaɱ sikkhitabbaɱ:|| ||

Mettā no ceto-vimutti bhāvitā bhavissati||
bahulī-katā yānī-katā vatthu-katā anuṭṭhitā paricitā susamāraddhā' ti.|| ||

Evaɱ hi vo bhikkhave sikkhitabbanti.|| ||
