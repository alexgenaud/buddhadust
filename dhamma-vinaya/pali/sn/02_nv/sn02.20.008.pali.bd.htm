Saɱyutta-Nikāya
Nidāna-vaggo

20. Opammasamyutta
Sutta 8

Kaliŋgaro Suttaɱ

Adapted from the 1995 edition of the digital version of the Sri Lanka Buddha Jayanti Tripitaka Series.

 

 

[1][rhyc] EVAṂ ME SUTAṂ:|| ||

Ekaɱ samayaɱ Bhagavā Vesāliyaɱ viharati Mahāvane Kūṭāgāra-sālāyaɱ.|| ||

[2] Tatra kho Bhagavā bhikkhū āmantesi:|| ||

"Bhikkhavo" ti.|| ||

"Bhadante" ti te bhikkhu Bhagavato paccassosuɱ.|| ||

Bhagavā etad avoca:|| ||

[3] Kaliŋgarūpadhānā bhikkhave,||
etarahi Licchavī viharanti appamattā ātāpino upāsanasmiɱ.|| ||

Tesaŋ rājā Māgadho Ajātasattu Vedehi-putto na labhati otāraɱ,||
na labhati ārammaṇaɱ.|| ||

[4] Bhavissanti bhikkhave,||
anāgatam addhānaɱ Licchavī sukhumālā mudutaluṇahatthapādā||
te mudukā suseyyā sutūlabimbohanāsu yāva suriyuggamanā seyyaɱ kappessanti.|| ||

Tesaŋ rājā Māgadho Ajātasattu Vedehi-putto lacchati otāraɱ,||
lacchati ārammaṇaɱ.|| ||

[5] Kaliŋgarūpadhānā bhikkhave,||
etarahi bhikkhū viharanti appamattā ātāpino padhānasmiɱ.|| ||

Tesaŋ Māro pāpimā na labhati otāraɱ,||
na labhati ārammaṇaɱ.|| ||

[6] Bhavissanti bhikkhave,||
anāgatam addhānaɱ bhikkhū sukhumālā mudutaluṇahatthapādā||
te mudukā suseyyā sutūlabimbohanāsu yāva sūriyuggamanā seyyaɱ kappessanti.|| ||

Tesaŋ Māro pāpimā lacchati otāraɱ,||
lacchati ārammaṇaɱ.|| ||

[7] Tasmā ti ha bhikkhave,||
evaɱ sikkhitabbaɱ:|| ||

"Kaliŋgarūpadhānā viharissāma appamattā ātāpino padhānasmin" ti.|| ||

Evaɱ hi vo bhikkhave, sikkhitabbanti.|| ||
