Saɱyutta Nikāya
II. Nidāna Vagga
15. Anamat'agga-Saɱyuttaɱ
II. Dutiya Vagga

Sutta 12

Sukhita Suttaɱ

Adapted from the 1995 edition of the digital version of the Sri Lanka Buddha Jayanti Tripitaka Series.

 

[186]

[1][pts][bodh][than][olds] Ekaɱ samayaɱ Bhagavā Sāvatthiyaɱ viharati.|| ||

[2][pts][bodh][than][olds] Tatra kho Bhagavā bhikkhū āmantesi:|| ||

"Bhikkhavo" ti.|| ||

"Bhadante" ti te bhikkhū Bhagavato paccassosuɱ.|| ||

[3][pts][bodh][than][olds] Bhagavā etad avoca:|| ||

"Anamat'aggāyam bhikkhave saɱsāro pubbākoṭi na paññāyati avijjā-nīvaraṇānaɱ sattānaɱ taṇhā-saŋyojanānaɱ sandhāvataɱ saɱsarataɱ.|| ||

[4][pts][bodh][than][olds] Yam bhikkhave passeyyātha sukhitaɱ sajjitaɱ [187] niṭṭham ettha gantabbaɱ:|| ||

'Amhehi pi eva-rūpam paccanubhūtaɱ dīghena addhunā' ti.|| ||

[5][pts][bodh][than][olds] Taɱ kissa hetu?|| ||

Anamat'aggāyaɱ bhikkhave saɱsāro pubbakoṭi na paññāyati avijjā-nīvaraṇānaɱ sattānaɱ taṇhā-saŋyojanānaɱ sandhāvataɱ saɱsarataɱ.|| ||

[6][pts][bodh][than][olds] Evaɱ dīgha-rattam kho bhikkhave dukkham paccanubhtaɱ tibbam paccanubhtaɱ vyasanaɱ paccanubhtaɱ kaṭasi vaḍḍhitā.|| ||

[7][pts][bodh][than][olds] Yāvañ cidam bhikkhave alam eva sabba-sankhāresu nibbindituɱ alaɱ virajjituɱ alaɱ vimuccitun" ti.|| ||
