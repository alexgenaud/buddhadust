Saɱyutta-Nikāya
Nidāna-vaggo

15. Anamatagga-saɱyuttam

Sutta 12

Sukhita Suttaɱ

Adapted from the 1995 edition of the digital version of the Sri Lanka Buddha Jayanti Tripitaka Series.

 

 

[1][pts][bodh][than][olds] Ekaɱ samayaɱ Bhagavā Sāvatthiyaɱ viharati|| ||

[2][pts][bodh][than][olds] Tatra kho Bhagavā bhikkhū āmantesi Bhikkhavo ti|| ||

Bhadante ti te bhikkhū Bhagavato paccassosuɱ|| ||

[3][pts][bodh][than][olds] Bhagavā etad avoca:|| ||

Anamataggāyam bhikkhave saɱsāro pubbākoṭi na paññāyati avijjānīvaraṇānaɱ sattānaɱ taṇhāsaŋyojanānaɱ sandhāvataɱ saɱsarataɱ.|| ||

[4][pts][bodh][than][olds] Yam bhikkhave passeyyātha sukhitaɱ sajjitaɱ [187] niṭṭham ettha gantabbaɱ Amhehi pi eva-rūpam paccanubhūtaɱ dīghena addhunāti|| ||

[5][pts][bodh][than][olds] Taɱ kissa hetu||
Anamataggāyaɱ bhikkhave saɱsāro pubbakoṭi na paññāyati avijjānīvaraṇānaɱ sattānaɱ taṇhāsaŋyojanānaɱ sandhāvataɱ saɱsarataɱ|| ||

[6][pts][bodh][than][olds] Evaɱ dīgha-rattam kho bhikkhave dukkham paccanubhtaɱ tibbam paccanubhtaɱ vyasanaɱ paccanubhtaɱ kaṭasi vaḍḍhitā|| ||

[7][pts][bodh][than][olds] Yāvañcidam bhikkhave alam eva sabba-sankhāresu nibbindituɱ alaɱ virajjituɱ alaɱ vimuccitunti|| ||
