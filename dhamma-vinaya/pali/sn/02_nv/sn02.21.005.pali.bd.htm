Saɱyutta Nikāya
II. Nidāna Vagga
XXI. Bhikkhu Saɱyutta

Sutta 5

Sujāta Suttaɱ

Adapted from the 1995 edition of the digital version of the Sri Lanka Buddha Jayanti Tripitaka Series.

 

[278]

[1][rhyc][bodh] EVAṂ ME SUTAṂ:|| ||

Ekaɱ samayaɱ Bhagavā Sāvatthiyaɱ viharati.||
Jetavane Anāthapiṇḍikassa ārāme.|| ||

[2][rhyc] Atha kho āyasmā Sujāto yena Bhagavā ten'upasankami.|| ||

[3][rhyc] Addasā kho Bhagavā āyasmantaɱ Sujātaɱ dūrato va āga-c-chantaɱ.|| ||

Disvāna bhikkhū āmantesi:|| ||

[4][rhyc] Ubhayenevāyaɱ bhikkhave, kula-putto sobhati vata:|| ||

Yañ ca abhirūpo dassanīyo pāsādiko paramāya vaṇṇa-pokkha-ratāya samannāgato|| ||

Yassa catthāya kula-puttā samma-d-eva agārasmā anagāriyaɱ pabbajanti,||
tad anuttaraɱ Brahma-cariya-pariyosānaɱ diṭṭhe'va dhamme sayaɱ abhiññā sacchi-katvā upasampajja viharatī' ti.|| ||

[5][rhyc] Idam avoca Bhagavā.|| ||

Idaɱ vatvā Sugato athāparaɱ etad avoca Satthā:|| ||

Sobhati vatāyaɱ bhikkhu||
ujubhūtena cetasā,||
Vippamyutto visaññutto||
anupādāya nibbuto,||
dhāreti antimaɱ dehaɱ||
chetvā Māraɱ savāhananti.|| ||
