Saɱyutta Nikaya
Nidāna Vagga
14. Dhatu Saɱyutta

Sutta 17

Assaddha Suttaɱ

Adapted from the 1995 edition of the digital version of the Sri Lanka Buddha Jayanti Tripitaka Series.

 

 

[1][pts] Evaɱ me sutaɱ:|| ||

Ekaɱ samayaɱ Bhagavā Sāvatthiyaɱ viharati.|| ||

Tatra kho Bhagavā bhikkhū āmantesi:|| ||

"Bhikkhavo" ti.|| ||

"Bhadante" ti te bhikkhū Bhagavato paccassosuɱ.|| ||

Bhagavā etad avoca:|| ||

2. Dhātuso va bhikkhave, sattā saɱsandanti samenti:|| ||

3. Assaddhā asaddhehi saddhiɱ saɱsandanti samenti,||
ahirikā ahirikehi saddhiɱ saɱsandanti samenti,||
anottāpino anottāpīhi saddhiɱ saɱsandanti samenti,||
appassutā appassutehi saddhiɱ saɱsandanti samenti,||
kusītā kusītehi saddhiɱ saɱsandanti samenti,||
muṭṭha-s-satino muṭṭhassatīhi saddhiɱ saɱsandanti samenti,||
duppaññā duppaññehi saddhiɱ saɱsandanti samenti.|| ||

4. Atītam pi bhikkhave, addhānaɱ dhātuso va sattā saɱsandiɱsu samiɱsu:|| ||

5. Assaddhā assaddhehi saddhiɱ saɱsandiɱsu samiɱsu,||
ahirikā ahirikehi saddhiɱ saɱsandiɱsu samiɱsu,||
anottāpino anottāpīhi saddhiɱ saɱsandiɱsu samiɱsu,||
appassutā appassutehi saddhiɱ saɱsandiɱsu samiɱsu,||
kusītā kusītehi saddhiɱ saɱsandiɱsu samiɱsu,||
muṭṭhasatino muṭṭhasatīhi saddhiɱ saɱsandiɱsu samiɱsu,||
duppaññā duppaññehi saddhiɱ saɱsandiɱsu samiɱsu.|| ||

6. Anāgatam pi bhikkhave, addhānaɱ dhātuso va sattā saɱsandissanti samessanti:|| ||

7. Assaddhā assaddhehi saddhiɱ saɱsandissanti samessanti,||
ahirikā ahirikehi saddhiɱ saɱsandissanti samessanti,||
anottapino anottāpīhi saddhiɱ [160] saɱsandissanti samessanti,||
appassutā appassutehi saddhiɱ saɱsandissanti samessanti,||
kusītā kusītehi saddhiɱ saɱsandissanti samessanti,||
muṭṭhasatino muṭṭhasatīhi saddhiɱ saɱsandiɱsu samiɱsu,||
duppaññā duppaññehi saddhiɱ saɱsandissanti samessanti.|| ||

8. Etarahi pi bhikkhave, paccuppannaɱ addhānaɱ dhātuso va sattā saɱsandanti samenti:|| ||

9. Assaddhā asaddhehi saddhiɱ saɱsandanti samenti,||
ahirikā ahirikehi saddhiɱ saɱsandanti samenti,||
anottāpino anottāpīhi saddhiɱ saɱsandanti samenti,||
appassutā appassutehi saddhiɱ saɱsandanti samenti,||
kusītā kusītehi saddhiɱ saɱsandanti samenti,||
muṭṭhasatino muṭṭhasatīhi saddhiɱ saɱsandanti samenti,||
duppaññā duppaññehi saddhiɱ saɱsandanti samenti.|| ||

 

§

 

10. Dhātuso va bhikkhave, sattā saɱsandanti samenti:|| ||

11. Saddhā saddhehi saddhiɱ saɱsandanti samenti,||
hirimanā hirimanehi saddhiɱ saɱsandanti samenti,||
ottāpino ottāpīhi saddhiɱ saɱsandanti samenti,||
bahu-s-sutā bahu-s-sutehi saddhiɱ saɱsandanti samenti,||
āraddha-viriyā āraddha-viriyehi saddhiɱ saɱsandanti samenti,||
upaṭṭhita-satino upaṭṭhitasatīhi saddhiɱ saɱsandanti samenti,||
paññavanto paññavantehi saddhiɱ saɱsandanti samenti.|| ||

12. Atītam pi bhikkhave, addhānaɱ dhātuso va sattā saɱsandiɱsu samiɱsu:|| ||

13. Saddhā saddhehi saddhiɱ saɱsandiɱsu samiɱsu,||
hirimanā hirimanehi saddhiɱ saɱsandiɱsu samiɱsu,||
ottāpino ottāpīhi saddhiɱ saɱsandiɱsu samiɱsu,||
bahu-s-sutā bahu-s-sutehi saddhiɱ saɱsandiɱsu samiɱsu,||
āraddha-viriyā āraddha-viriyehi saddhiɱ saɱsandiɱsu samiɱsu,||
upaṭṭhita-satino upaṭṭhitasatīhi saddhiɱ saɱsandiɱsu samiɱsu,||
paññavanto paññavantehi saddhiɱ saɱsandiɱsu samiɱsu.|| ||

14. Anāgatam pi bhikkhave, addhānaɱ dhātusova sattā saɱsandissanti samessanti:|| ||

15. Saddhā saddhehi saddhiɱ saɱsandissanti samessanti,||
hirimanā hirimanehi saddhiɱ saɱsandissanti samessanti,||
ottāpino ottāpīhi saddhiɱ saɱsandissanti samessanti,||
bahu-s-sutā bahu-s-sutehi saddhiɱ saɱsandissanti samessanti,||
āraddha-viriyā āraddha-viriyehi saddhiɱ saɱsandissanti samessanti,||
upaṭṭhita-satino upaṭṭhitasatīhi saddhiɱ saɱsandissanti samessanti,||
paññavanto paññavantehi saddhiɱ saɱsandissanti samessanti.|| ||

16. Etarahi pi bhikkhave, paccuppannaɱ addhānaɱ dhātuso va sattā saɱsandanti samenti:|| ||

17. Saddhā saddhehi saddhiɱ saɱsandanti samenti,||
hirimanā hirimanehi saddhiɱ saɱsandanti samenti,||
ottāpino ottāpīhi saddhiɱ saɱsandanti samenti,||
bahu-s-sutā bahu-s-sutehi saddhiɱ saɱsandanti samenti,||
āraddha-viriyā āraddha-viriyehi saddhiɱ saɱsandanti samenti,||
upaṭṭhita-satino upaṭṭhitasatīhi saddhiɱ saɱsandanti samenti,||
paññavanto paññavantehi saddhiɱ saɱsandanti samentī ti.|| ||
