Adapted from the 1995 edition of the digital version of the Sri Lanka Buddha Jayanti Tripitaka Series.

[1][pts][than][bodh] Evaɱ me sutaɱ.|| ||

anaññathatā||
idappaccayatā.|| ||

Ayaɱ vuccati bhikkhave, paṭicca-samuppādo.)|| ||

18. Yato kho bhikkhave,||
ariya-sāvakassa ayañ ca paṭicca-samuppādo||
ime ca paṭicca-samuppannā dhammā||
yathā-bhūtaɱ samma-p-paññāya su-diṭṭhā honti,||
so vata pubbantaɱ vā paridhāvissati:|| ||

'Ahosiɱ nu khvāhaɱ atītam addhānaɱ?|| ||

Na nu kho ahosiɱ atītam addhānaɱ?|| ||

Kiɱ nu kho ahosiɱ atītam addhānaɱ?|| ||

Kathaɱ nu kho ahosiɱ atītam addhānaɱ?|| ||

Kiɱ hutvā kiɱ ahosiɱ||
nu khvāhaɱ atītam addhānan' ti?|| ||

■

19. Aparantaɱ vā upadhāvissati:|| ||

'Bhavissāmi nu khvāhaɱ anāgatam addhānaɱ?|| ||

Na nu kho bhavissāmi [27] anāgatam addhānaɱ?|| ||

Kiɱ nu kho bhavissāmi anāgatam addhānaɱ?|| ||

Kathaɱ nu kho bhavissāmi anāgatam addhānaɱ?|| ||

Kiɱ hutvā kiɱ bhavissāmi nu khvāhaɱ anāgatam addhānan' ti?|| ||

■

20. Etarahi vā paccuppannaɱ addhānaɱ ajjhattaɱ kathaŋkathī bhavissati:|| ||

'Ahaɱ nu khosmi?|| ||

No nu khosmi?|| ||

Kiɱ nu khosmi?|| ||

Kathaɱ nu khosmi?|| ||

Ayaɱ nu kho satto kuto āgato,||
so kuhiɱ gāmī bhavissatī' ti?|| ||

N'etaɱ ṭhānaɱ vijjati.|| ||

21. Taɱ kissa hetu?|| ||

Tathā hi bhikkhave,||
ariya-sāvakassa ayañ ca paṭicca-samuppādo,||
ime ca paṭicca-samuppannā dhammā yathā-bhūtaɱ samma-p-paññāya su-diṭṭhāti.|| ||

Dasamaɱ.|| ||
