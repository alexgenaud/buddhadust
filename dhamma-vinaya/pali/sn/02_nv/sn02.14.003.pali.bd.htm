Saɱyutta Nikāya
II. Nidāna Vagga
14. Dhātu-Saɱyuttaɱ
I. Nānatta Vagga Paṭhama
1. Ajjhatta-Pañcakaɱ

Sutta 3

No ce tam [No Phassa-Nānatta] Suttaɱ

Adapted from the 1995 edition of the digital version of the Sri Lanka Buddha Jayanti Tripitaka Series.

 

 

[pts][bodh][olds] Evaɱ me sutaɱ:|| ||

Ekaɱ samayaɱ Sāvatthiyaɱ viharati Jetavane Anāthapiṇḍikassa ārāme.|| ||

2. "Dhātu-nānattaɱ bhikkhave, paṭicca uppajjati phassa-nānattaɱ,||
no phassa-nānattaɱ paṭicca uppajjati dhātu-nānattaɱ.|| ||

3. Katamañ ca bhikkhave dhātu-nānattaɱ?|| ||

4. Cakkhu-dhātu||
sota-dhātu||
ghāṇa-dhātu||
jivhā-dhātu||
kāya-dhātu||
mano-dhātu.|| ||

Idaɱ vuccati bhikkhave dhātu-nānattaɱ.|| ||

5. Kathañ ca bhikkhave dhātu-nānattaɱ paṭicca uppajjati phassa-nānattaɱ,||
no phassa-nānattaɱ paṭicca uppajjati dhātu-nānattaɱ?|| ||

6. Cakkhu-dhātu bhikkhave paṭicca uppajjati cakkhu-samphasso,||
no cakkhu-samphassaɱ paṭicca uppajjati cakkhu-dhātu.|| ||

Sota-dhātu bhikkhave paṭicca uppajjati sota-samphasso,||
no sota-samphassaɱ paṭicca uppajjati sota-dhātu.|| ||

Ghāṇa-dhātu bhikkhave paṭicca uppajjati ghāṇdhā-samphasso,||
no ghāṇdhā-samphassaɱ paṭicca uppajjati ghāṇdhā-dhātu.|| ||

Jivhā-dhātu bhikkhave paṭicca uppajjati jivhā-samphasso,||
no jivhā-samphassaɱ paṭicca uppajjati jivhā-dhātu.|| ||

Kāya-dhātu bhikkhave paṭicca uppajjati kāya-samphasso,||
no kāya-samphassaɱ paṭicca uppajjati kāya-dhātu.|| ||

Mano-dhātuɱ paṭicca uppajjati mano-samphasso,||
no mano-samphassaɱ paṭicca uppajjati mano-dhātu.|| ||

7. Evaɱ kho bhikkhave, dhātu-nānattaɱ paṭicca uppajjati phassa-nānattaɱ.|| ||

No phassa-nānattaɱ paṭicca uppajjati dhātu-nānattan" ti.|| ||
