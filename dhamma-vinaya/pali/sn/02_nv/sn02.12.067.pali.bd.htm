Saɱyutta Nikāya
II. Nidāna Vagga
12. Nidāna Saɱyutta
7. Mahā Vagga

Sutta 67

Nala-Kalapiyaɱ Suttaɱ

Adapted from the 1995 edition of the digital version of the Sri Lanka Buddha Jayanti Tripitaka Series.

 

[112]

[1][pts][than][olds][bodh] Evaɱ me sutaɱ.|| ||

Ekaɱ samayaɱ āyasmā ca Sāriputto āyasmā ca Mahā Koṭṭhito Bārāṇasiyaɱ viharanti Isipatane Migadāye.|| ||

Atha kho āyasmā Mahā Koṭṭhito sāyaṇha-samayaɱ patisallāṇā vuṭṭhito yen'āyasmā Sāriputto ten'upasankami.|| ||

Upasaɱkamitvā āyasmatā Sāriputtena saddhiɱ sammodi.|| ||

Sammodanīyaɱ kathaɱ sārāṇīyaɱ vīti-sāretvā eka-m-antaɱ nisīdi.|| ||

Eka-m-antaɱ nisinno kho āyasmā Mahā Koṭṭhito āyasmantaɱ Sāriputtaɱ etad avoca:|| ||

"Kin nu kho āvuso Sāriputta,||
sayaŋ-kataɱ jarā-maraṇaɱ,||
para-kataɱ jarā-maraṇaɱ,||
[113] sayaŋ-katañ ca para-katañ ca jarā-maraṇaɱ,||
udāhu asayaɱ-kāraɱ aparaŋ-kāraɱ adhicca -||
samuppannaɱ jarā-maraṇan" ti?|| ||

"Na kho āvuso Koṭṭhika,||
sayaŋ-kataɱ jarā-maraṇaɱ,||
na para-kataɱ jarā-maraṇaɱ,||
na sayaŋ-katañ ca para-katañ ca jarā-maraṇaɱ,||
nāpi asayaɱ-kāraɱ,||
aparaŋ-kāraɱ adhicca samuppannaɱ jarā-maraṇaɱ.|| ||

Api ca jāti-paccayā jarā-maraṇan" ti.|| ||

"Tena hāvuso upamaɱ te karissāmi.|| ||

dve nalakalāpiyo añña-maññaɱ nissāya tiṭṭheyyuɱ,||

Evam etassa kevalassa dukkha-k-khandhassa samudayo hoti.|| ||

phassa-nirodhā vedanā-nirodho,||
vedanā-nirodhā taṇhā-nirodho,||
taṇhā-nirodhā upādāna-nirodho,||

jāti-nirodhā jarā-maraṇaɱ,||
soka-parideva-dukkha-domanass'-upāyāsā nirujjhanti.|| ||

Evam etassa kevalassa dukkha-k-khandhassa nirodho hotī" ti.|| ||
