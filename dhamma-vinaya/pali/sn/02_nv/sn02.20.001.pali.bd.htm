Saɱyutta Nikaya
2. Nidāna Vagga

20. Opamma-Samyuttam

Sutta 1

Kūṭa

Adapted from the 1995 edition of the digital version of the Sri Lanka Buddha Jayanti Tripitaka Series.

 

 

[1][rhyc][olds]EVAṂ ME SUTAṂ:|| ||

Ekaɱ samayaɱ Bhagavā Sāvatthiyaɱ viharati.||
Jetavane Anāthapiṇḍikassa ārāme.|| ||

[2][rhyc][olds] Tatra kho Bhagavā bhikkhu āmantesi bhikkhavo' ti.|| ||

Bhadante' ti kho te bhikkhū Bhagavato paccassosuɱ.|| ||

Bhagavā etad avoca:|| ||

[3][rhyc][olds] seyyathā pi bhikkhave,||
kūṭā-gārassa yā kāci gopānasiyo,||
sabbā tā kūṭa-ŋ-gamā||
kūṭa-samo-saraṇā,||
kūṭasamugghātā||
sabbā tā samugghātaɱ gacchanti.|| ||

[4][rhyc][olds] Evam eva kho bhikkhave,||
ye keci akusala dhammā||
sabbe te avijjaŋgamā[1]||
avijjāsam-osaraṇā,||
avijjāsamugghātā||
sabbe te samugghātaɱ gacchanti.|| ||

[5][rhyc][olds] Tasmā ti ha bhikkhave, evaɱ sikkhitabbaɱ:|| ||

"Appamattā viharissāmā" ti.|| ||

Evaɱ hi vo bhikkhave, sikkhitabbanti.|| ||

 

[1] Avijjaŋgamā. PTS switches to avijjāmūlakā here
