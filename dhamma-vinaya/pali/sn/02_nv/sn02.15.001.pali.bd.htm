Saɱyutta-Nikāya,
Nidāna-vaggo

15. Anamatagga-saɱyuttam

Sutta 1

Tiṇakaṭṭham Suttaɱ

Adapted from the 1995 edition of the digital version of the Sri Lanka Buddha Jayanti Tripitaka Series.

 

 

[1][pts][bodh][olds] Evam me sutaɱ|| ||

Ekaɱ samayaɱ Bhagavā Sāvatthiyaɱ viharati||
Jetavane Anāthapiṇḍikassa ārāme|| ||

[2][pts][bodh][olds] Tatra kho Bhagavā bhikkh āmantesi||
Bhikkhavo ti||
Bhadante ti te bhikkh Bhagavato paccassosuɱ|| ||

[3][pts][bodh][olds] Bhagavā etad avoca|| ||

Anamataggāyaɱ bhikkhave saɱsāro pubbākoṭi na paññāyati avijjānīvaraṇānaɱ sattānaɱ taṇhāsaŋyojanānaɱ sandhāvataɱ saɱsarataɱ|| ||

[4][pts][bodh][olds] seyyathā pi, bhikkhave, purisoyaɱ imasmiɱ Jambudīpe tiṇakaṭṭhasākhā palāsaɱ tacchetvā ekajjhaɱ saɱharitvā caturaŋgulaɱ caturaŋgulaɱ ghaṭikaɱ karitvā nikkhippeyya||
Ayaɱ me mātā tassā me mātu ayam mātā ti|| ||

Apariyādinnā ca bhikkhave tassa purisassa mātu mātaro assu||
atha imasmiɱ Jambudīpe tiṇakaṭṭhasākhāpalāsaɱ parikkhayam pariyādānaɱ gaccheyya|| ||

[5][pts][bodh][olds] Taɱ kissa hetu|| ||

Anamataggāyaɱ bhikkhave saɱsāro pubbakoṭi na paññāyati avijjānīvaraṇānaɱ sattānaɱ taṇhāsaŋyojanānaɱ sandhāvataɱ saɱsarataɱ|| ||

[6][pts][bodh][olds] Evaɱ dīgha-rattam kho bhikkhave dukkham paccanubhtaɱ tibbam paccanubhtaɱ vyasanaɱ paccanubhtaɱ kaṭasi vaḍḍhitā|| ||

[7][pts][bodh][olds] Yāvañcidam bhikkhave alam eva sabba-sankhāresu nibbindituɱ alaɱ virajjituɱ alaɱ vimuccitunti|| ||
