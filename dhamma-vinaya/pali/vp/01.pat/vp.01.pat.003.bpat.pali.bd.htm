Pāṭidesanīyā
[Index][sbe][than]

Ime kho panāyasmanto cattāro pāṭidesanīyā dhammā uddesaɱ āga-c-chanti.|| ||

1: Paṭhamapāṭidesanīyasikkhāpadaɱ[1]|| ||

Yo pana bhikkhu aññātikāya bhikkhuniyā antaragharaɱ paviṭṭhāya hatthato khādanīyaɱ vā bhojanīyaɱ vā sahatthā paṭiggahetvā khādeyya vā bhuājeyya vā. Paṭidesetabbaɱ tena bhikkhunā: 'Gārayhaɱ āvuso dhammaɱ āpajjiɱ, asappāyaɱ, pāṭidesanīyaɱ, taɱ paṭidesemī'-ti.|| ||

2: Dutiyapāṭidesanīyasikkhāpadaɱ|| ||

Bhikkhū pan'eva kulesu nimantitā bhuājanti. Tatra ce sā bhikkhunī vosāsamānarūpā ṭhitā hoti: 'Idha sūpaɱ detha, idha odanaɱ dethā'-ti. Tehi bhikkhūhi sā bhikkhunī apasādetabbā: 'Apasakka tāva bhagini, yāva bhikkhū bhuājanti.' Ekassa pi ce[2] bhikkhuno nappaṭibhāseyya taɱ bhikkhuniɱ apasādetuɱ: 'Apasakka tāva bhagini, yāva bhikkhū bhuājantī'-ti. Paṭidesetabbaɱ tehi bhikkhūhi: 'Gārayhaɱ āvuso dhammaɱ āpajjimhā, asappāyaɱ, pāṭidesanīyaɱ, taɱ paṭidesemā'-ti.|| ||

3: Tatiyapāṭidesanīyasikkhāpadaɱ|| ||

Yāni kho pana tāni sekhasammatāni kulāni. Yo pana bhikkhu tathā-rūpesu sekhasammatesu kulesu pubbe animantito agilāno khādanīyaɱ vā bhojanīyaɱ vā sahatthā paṭiggahetvā khādeyya vā bhuājeyya vā. Paṭidesetabbaɱ tena bhikkhunā: 'Gārayhaɱ āvuso dhammaɱ āpajjiɱ, asappāyaɱ, pāṭidesanīyaɱ, taɱ paṭidesemī'-ti.|| ||

4: Catutthapāṭidesanīyasikkhāpadaɱ|| ||

Yāni kho pana tāni āraññakāni sen'āsanāni sāsaŋkasammatāni sappaṭi-bhayāni. Yo pana bhikkhu tathā-rūpesu sen'āsanesu viharanto pubbe appaṭisaɱviditaɱ khādanīyaɱ vā bhojanīyaɱ vā ajjhārāme sahatthā paṭiggahetvā agilāno khādeyya vā bhuājeyya vā. Paṭidesetabbaɱ tena bhikkhunā: 'Gārayhaɱ āvuso dhammaɱ āpajjiɱ, asappāyaɱ, pāṭidesanīyaɱ, taɱ paṭidesemī'-ti.|| ||

Uddiṭṭhā kho āyasmanto cattāro pāṭidesanīyā dhammā.||
Tatthāyasmante pucchāmi: kaccittha parisuddhā?||
Dutiyam-pi pucchāmi: kaccittha parisuddhā?||
Tatiyam-pi pucchāmi: kaccittha parisuddhā?||
Parisuddhetthāyasmanto, tasmā tuṇhī, evam-etaɱ dhārayāmi.|| ||

Pāṭidesanīyā niṭṭhitā|| ||

 

 

Sekhiyā
[Index][sbe][than]

Ime kho panāyasmanto sekhiyā dhammā uddesaɱ āga-c-chanti.|| ||

1: Parimaṇḍalasikkhāpadaɱ[3]|| ||

Parimaṇḍalaɱ nivāsessāmī-ti, sikkhā karaṇīyā.|| ||

2:|| ||

Parimaṇḍalaɱ pārupissāmī-ti, sikkhā karaṇīyā.|| ||

Supaṭi-c-channo antaraghare gamissāmī-ti, sikkhā karaṇīyā.|| ||

4:|| ||

Supaṭi-c-channo antaraghare nisīdissāmī-ti, sikkhā karaṇīyā.|| ||

Susaɱvuto antaraghare gamissāmī-ti, sikkhā karaṇīyā.|| ||

6:|| ||

Susaɱvuto antaraghare nisīdissāmī-ti, sikkhā karaṇīyā.|| ||

Okkhitta-cakkhu antaraghare gamissāmī-ti, sikkhā karaṇīyā.|| ||

8:|| ||

Okkhitta-cakkhu antaraghare nisīdissāmī-ti, sikkhā karaṇīyā.|| ||

9: Ukkhittakasikkhāpadaɱ|| ||

Na ukkhittakāya antaraghare gamissāmī-ti, sikkhā karaṇīyā.|| ||

10:|| ||

Na ukkhittakāya antaraghare nisīdissāmī-ti, sikkhā karaṇīyā.|| ||

Parimaṇḍalavaggo paṭhamo|| ||

12:|| ||

Na ujjagghikāya antaraghare nisīdissāmī-ti, sikkhā karaṇīyā.|| ||

13: Uccasaddāsikkhāpadaɱ|| ||

14:|| ||

Appasaddo antaraghare nisīdissāmī-ti, sikkhā karaṇīyā.|| ||

15: Kāyappacālakādisikkhāpadaɱ|| ||

16:|| ||

18:|| ||

Na bāhuppacālakaɱ antaraghare nisīdissāmī-ti, sikkhā karaṇīyā.|| ||

20:|| ||

Na sīsappacālakaɱ antaraghare nisīdissāmī-ti, sikkhā karaṇīyā.|| ||

Ujjagghiakavaggo dutiyo|| ||

21: Khambhakatasikkhāpadaɱ|| ||

Na khambhakato antaraghare gamissāmī-ti, sikkhā karaṇīyā.|| ||

22:|| ||

Na khambhakato antaraghare nisīdissāmī-ti, sikkhā karaṇīyā.|| ||

23: Oguṇṭhitasikkhāpadaɱ|| ||

Na oguṇṭhito antaraghare gamissāmī-ti, sikkhā karaṇīyā.|| ||

24:|| ||

Na oguṇṭhito antaraghare nisīdissāmī-ti, sikkhā karaṇīyā.|| ||

25: Ukkuṭikasikkhāpadaɱ|| ||

Na ukkuṭikāya antaraghare gamissāmī-ti, sikkhā karaṇīyā.|| ||

26: Pallatthikasikkhāpadaɱ|| ||

Na pallatthikāya antaraghare nisīdissāmī-ti, sikkhā karaṇīyā.|| ||

27: Sakkaccapaṭiggahaṇasikkhāpadaɱ|| ||

Sakkaccaɱ piṇḍa-pātaɱ paṭiggahessāmī-ti, sikkhā karaṇīyā.|| ||

28: Pattasaññīpaṭiggahaṇasikkhāpadaɱ|| ||

Pattasaññī piṇḍa-pātaɱ paṭiggahessāmī-ti, sikkhā karaṇīyā.|| ||

29: Samasūpakapaṭiggahaṇasikkhāpadaɱ|| ||

Samasūpakaɱ piṇḍa-pātaɱ paṭiggahessāmī-ti, sikkhā karaṇīyā.|| ||

30: Samatittikasikkhāpadaɱ|| ||

Samatittikaɱ piṇḍa-pātaɱ paṭiggahessāmī-ti, sikkhā karaṇīyā.|| ||

Khambhakavaggo tatiyo|| ||

31: Sakkaccabuājanasikkhāpadaɱ|| ||

Sakkaccaɱ piṇḍa-pātaɱ bhuājissāmī-ti, sikkhā karaṇīyā.|| ||

32: Pattasaññībhuājanasikkhāpadaɱ|| ||

Pattasaññī piṇḍa-pātaɱ bhuājissāmī-ti, sikkhā karaṇīyā.|| ||

33: Sapadānasikkhāpadaɱ|| ||

Sapadānaɱ piṇḍa-pātaɱ bhuājissāmī-ti, sikkhā karaṇīyā.|| ||

34: Samasūpakasikkhāpadaɱ|| ||

Samasūpakaɱ piṇḍa-pātaɱ bhuājissāmī-ti, sikkhā karaṇīyā.|| ||

35: Nathūpakatasikkhāpadaɱ|| ||

Na thūpato[4] omadditvā piṇḍa-pātaɱ bhuājissāmī-ti, sikkhā karaṇīyā.|| ||

36: Odanappaṭicchādanasikkhāpadaɱ|| ||

Na sūpaɱ vā byaājanaɱ vā odanena paṭicchādessāmi bhīyokamyataɱ[5] upādāyāti, sikkhā karaṇīyā.|| ||

37: Sūpodanaviññattisikkhāpadaɱ|| ||

Na sūpaɱ vā odanaɱ vā agilāno attano atthāya viññāpetvā bhuājissāmī-ti, sikkhā karaṇīyā.|| ||

38: Ujjhānasaññīsikkhāpadaɱ|| ||

Na ujjhānasaññī paresaɱ pattaɱ olokessāmī-ti, sikkhā karaṇīyā.|| ||

39: Kaba'asikkhāpadaɱ|| ||

Nātimahantaɱ kaba'aɱ karissāmī-ti, sikkhā karaṇīyā.|| ||

40: Ālopasikkhāpadaɱ|| ||

Parimaṇḍalaɱ ālopaɱ karissāmī-ti, sikkhā karaṇīyā.|| ||

Sakkaccavaggo catuttho|| ||

41: Anāhaṭasikkhāpadaɱ|| ||

Na anāhaṭe kaba'e mukhadvāraɱ vivarissāmī-ti, sikkhā karaṇīyā.|| ||

42: Bhuājamānasikkhāpadaɱ|| ||

Na bhuājamāno sabbaɱ hatthaɱ mukhe pakkhipissāmī-ti, sikkhā karaṇīyā.|| ||

43: Sakaba'asikkhāpadaɱ|| ||

Na sakaba'ena mukhena byāharissāmī-ti, sikkhā karaṇīyā.|| ||

44: Piṇḍukkhepakasikkhāpadaɱ|| ||

Na piṇḍukkhepakaɱ bhuājissāmī-ti, sikkhā karaṇīyā.|| ||

45: Kaba'avacchedakasikkhāpadaɱ|| ||

Na kaba'āvacchedakaɱ bhuājissāmī-ti, sikkhā karaṇīyā.|| ||

46: Avagaṇḍakārakasikkhāpadaɱ|| ||

47: Hatthaniddhunakasikkhāpadaɱ|| ||

Na hatthaniddhunakaɱ bhuājissāmī-ti,[6] sikkhā karaṇīyā.|| ||

48: Sitthāvakārakasikkhāpadaɱ|| ||

Na sitthāvakārakaɱ bhuājissāmī-ti, sikkhā karaṇīyā.|| ||

49: Jivhānicchārakasikkhāpadaɱ|| ||

Na jivhānicchārakaɱ bhuājissāmī-ti, sikkhā karaṇīyā.|| ||

50: Capucapukārakasikkhāpadaɱ|| ||

Na capucapukārakaɱ bhuājissāmī-ti, sikkhā karaṇīyā.|| ||

Kaba'avaggo paācamo|| ||

51: Surusurukārakasikkhāpadaɱ|| ||

Na surusurukārakaɱ bhuājissāmī-ti, sikkhā karaṇīyā.|| ||

52: Hatthanillehakādisikkhāpadaɱ|| ||

Na hatthanillehakaɱ bhuājissāmī-ti, sikkhā karaṇīyā.|| ||

53: Pattanillehakasikkhāpadaɱ|| ||

Na pattanillehakaɱ bhuājissāmī-ti,[7] sikkhā karaṇīyā.|| ||

54: Oṭṭhanillehakasikkhāpadaɱ|| ||

Na oṭṭhanillehakaɱ bhuājissāmī-ti, sikkhā karaṇīyā.|| ||

55: Sāmisasikkhāpadaɱ|| ||

Na sāmisena hatthena pānīyathālakaɱ paṭiggahessāmī-ti, sikkhā karaṇīyā.|| ||

56: Sasitthakasikkhāpadaɱ|| ||

Na sasitthakaɱ pattadhovanaɱ antaraghare chaḍḍessāmī-ti, sikkhā karaṇīyā.|| ||

57: Chattapāṇisikkhāpadaɱ|| ||

Na chattapāṇissa agilānassa dhammaɱ desissāmī-ti,[8] sikkhā karaṇīyā.|| ||

58: Daṇḍapāṇisikkhāpadaɱ|| ||

Na daṇḍapāṇissa agilānassa dhammaɱ desissāmī-ti, sikkhā karaṇīyā.|| ||

59: Satthapāṇisikkhāpadaɱ|| ||

Na satthapāṇissa agilānassa dhammaɱ desissāmī-ti, sikkhā karaṇīyā.|| ||

60: Āyudhapāṇisikkhāpadaɱ|| ||

Na āyudhapāṇissa agilānassa dhammaɱ desissāmī-ti, sikkhā karaṇīyā.|| ||

Surusuruvaggo chaṭṭho|| ||

62: Upāhanasikkhāpadaɱ|| ||

Na upāhanārū'hassa agilānassa dhammaɱ desissāmī-ti, sikkhā karaṇīyā.|| ||

Na yānagatassa agilānassa dhammaɱ desissāmī-ti, sikkhā karaṇīyā.|| ||

65: Pallatthikasikkhāpadaɱ|| ||

Na pallatthikāya nisinnassa agilānassa dhammaɱ desissāmī-ti, sikkhā karaṇīyā.|| ||

66: Veṭhitasikkhāpadaɱ|| ||

Na veṭhitasīsassa agilānassa dhammaɱ desissāmī-ti, sikkhā karaṇīyā.|| ||

67: Oguṇṭhitasikkhāpadaɱ|| ||

69: Nīcāsanasikkhāpadaɱ|| ||

Na nīce āsane nisīditvā ucce āsane nisinnassa agilānassa dhammaɱ desissāmī-ti, sikkhā karaṇīyā.|| ||

Na ṭhito nisinnassa agilānassa dhammaɱ desissāmī-ti, sikkhā karaṇīyā.|| ||

71: Pacchatogamana-sikkhāpadaɱ|| ||

Na pacchato gacchanto purato gacchantassa agilānassa dhammaɱ desissāmī-ti, sikkhā karaṇīyā.|| ||

73: Ṭhito-uccārasikkhāpadaɱ|| ||

Na ṭhito agilāno uccāraɱ vā passāvaɱ vā karissāmī-ti, sikkhā karaṇīyā.|| ||

75: Udake-uccārasikkhāpadaɱ|| ||

Na udake agilāno uccāraɱ vā passāvaɱ vā khe'aɱ vā karissāmī-ti, sikkhā karaṇīyā.|| ||

Adhikaraṇasamathā
[Index][sbe][than]

Ime kho panāyasmanto satta adhikaraṇasamathā dhammā uddesaɱ āga-c-chanti.|| ||

Uppannuppannānaɱ adhikaraṇānaɱ samathāya vūpasamāya:|| ||

1: Sammukhāvinayo dātabbo.|| ||

2: Sativinayo dātabbo.|| ||

3: Amūḷhavinayo dātabbo.|| ||

4: Paṭiññāya kāretabbaɱ.|| ||

5: Yebhuyyasikā.|| ||

6: Tassapāpiyyasikā.|| ||

7: Tiṇavatthārakoti.|| ||

Uddiṭṭhā kho āyasmanto satta adhikaraṇasamathā dhammā.||
Tatthāyasmante pucchāmi: kaccittha parisuddhā?||
Dutiyam-pi pucchāmi: kaccittha parisuddhā?||
Tatiyam-pi pucchāmi: kaccittha parisuddhā?||
Parisuddhetthāyasmanto, tasmā tuṇhī, evam-etaɱ dhārayāmi.|| ||

Adhikaraṇasamathā Niṭṭhitā|| ||

Uddiṭṭhaɱ kho āyasmanto nidānaɱ.||
Uddiṭṭhā cattāro pārājikā dhammā.||
Uddiṭṭhā terasa saŋghā-disesā dhammā.||
Uddiṭṭhā dve aniyatā dhammā.||
Uddiṭṭhā tiɱsa nissaggiyā pācittiyā dhammā||
Uddiṭṭhā dvenavuti pācittiyā dhammā.||
Uddiṭṭhā cattāro pāṭidesanīyā dhammā.||
Uddiṭṭhā sekhiyā dhammā.||
Uddiṭṭhā satta adhikaraṇasamathā dhammā.|| ||

Ettakaɱ tassa Bhagavato suttāgataɱ suttapariyāpannaɱ anvaddhamāsaɱ uddesaɱ āga-c-chati. Tattha sabbeheva samaggehi sammodamānehi avivadamānehi sikkhitabbaɱ.|| ||

BhikkhuPātimokkhaɱ niṭṭhitaɱ|| ||
