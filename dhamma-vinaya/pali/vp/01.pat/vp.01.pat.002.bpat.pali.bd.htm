Bhikkhu-Pātimokkha Pāli

Adapted from the 1995 edition of the digital version of the Sri Lanka Buddha Jayanti Tripitaka Series and proofed against, and mostly resolved to the 1979 Pali Text Society Aṅguttara-Nikaya edited by The Rev. Suddhapācittiyā
[Index][sbe][than]

Ime kho pan'āyasmanto dvenavuti pācittiyā dhammā uddesaɱ āga-c-chanti.|| ||

1: Musā-vādasikkhāpadaɱ|| ||

Sampajānamusā-vāde, pācittiyaɱ.|| ||

2: Omasavādasikkhāpadaɱ|| ||

Omasavāde, pācittiyaɱ.|| ||

3: Pesuññasikkhāpadaɱ|| ||

Bhikkhupesuññe, pācittiyaɱ.|| ||

4: Padasodhammasikkhāpadaɱ|| ||

Yo pana bhikkhu anupasampannaɱ padaso dhammaɱ vāceyya, pācittiyaɱ.|| ||

5: Sahaseyyasikkhāpadaɱ|| ||

Yo pana bhikkhu anupasampannena uttariɱ[1] dirattatirattaɱ sahaseyyaɱ kappeyya, pācittiyaɱ.|| ||

6: Dutiyasahaseyyasikkhāpadaɱ|| ||

Yo pana bhikkhu mātu-gāmena sahaseyyaɱ kappeyya, pācittiyaɱ.|| ||

7: Dhammadesanāsikkhāpadaɱ|| ||

Yo pana bhikkhu mātu-gāmassa uttariɱ chappaācavācāhi dhammaɱ deseyya, aññatra viññunā purisaviggahena, pācittiyaɱ.|| ||

8: Bhūtārocanasikkhāpadaɱ|| ||

Yo pana bhikkhu anupasampannassa uttari-manussa-dhammaɱ āroceyya bhūtasmiɱ, pācittiyaɱ.|| ||

9: Duṭṭhullārocanasikkhāpadaɱ|| ||

Yo pana bhikkhu bhikkhussa duṭṭhullaɱ āpattiɱ anupasampannassa āroceyya, aññatra bhikkhusammutiyā, pācittiyaɱ.|| ||

10: Paṭhavikhaṇanasikkhāpadaɱ|| ||

Yo pana bhikkhu paṭhaviɱ khaṇeyya vā khaṇāpeyya vā, pācittiyaɱ.|| ||

Musā-vādāvaggo paṭhamo|| ||

11: Bhūtagāmasikkhāpadaɱ|| ||

Bhūtagāmapātavyatāya, pācittiyaɱ.|| ||

12: Aññavādakasikkhāpadaɱ|| ||

Aññavādake vihesake, pācittiyaɱ.|| ||

13: Ujjhāyanasikkhāpadaɱ|| ||

Ujjhāpanake khīyanake, pācittiyaɱ.|| ||

14: Paṭhamasen'āsanasikkhāpadaɱ|| ||

Yo pana bhikkhu saṅghikaɱ maācaɱ vā pīṭhaɱ vā bhisiɱ vā kocchaɱ vā ajjhokāse santharitvā vā santharāpetvā vā, taɱ pakkamanto n'eva uddhareyya na uddharāpeyya, anāpucchaɱ vā gaccheyya, pācittiyaɱ.|| ||

15: Dutiyasen'āsanasikkhāpadaɱ|| ||

Yo pana bhikkhu saṅghike vihāre seyyaɱ santharitvā vā santharāpetvā vā, taɱ pakkamanto n'eva uddhareyya na uddharāpeyya, anāpucchaɱ vā gaccheyya, pācittiyaɱ.|| ||

16: Anupakhajjasikkhāpadaɱ|| ||

Yo pana bhikkhu saṅghike vihāre jānaɱ pubbūpagataɱ bhikkhuɱ anupakhajja seyyaɱ kappeyya: 'Yassa sambādho bhavissati, so pakkamissatī'-ti. Etad-eva paccayaɱ karitvā anaññaɱ, pācittiyaɱ.|| ||

17: Nikkaḍḍhanasikkhāpadaɱ|| ||

Yo pana bhikkhu bhikkhuɱ kupito anatta-mano saṅghikā vihārā nikkaḍḍheyya vā nikkaḍḍhāpeyya vā, pācittiyaɱ.|| ||

18: Vehāsakūṭisikkhāpadaɱ|| ||

Yo pana bhikkhu saṅghike vihāre uparivehāsakuṭiyā āhaccapādakaɱ maācaɱ vā pīṭhaɱ vā abhinisīdeyya vā abhinipajjeyya vā, pācittiyaɱ.|| ||

19: Mahallakavihārasikkhāpadaɱ|| ||

Mahallakaɱ pana bhikkhunā vihāraɱ kārayamānena, yāva dvārakosā aggalaṭṭhapanāya ālokasandhiparikammāya dvatticchadanassa pariyāyaɱ, appaharite ṭhitena adhiṭṭhātabbaɱ. Tato ce uttariɱ appaharite pi ṭhito adhiṭṭhaheyya, pācittiyaɱ.|| ||

20: Sappāṇasikkhāpadaɱ|| ||

Yo pana bhikkhu jānaɱ sappāṇakaɱ udakaɱ tiṇaɱ vā mattikaɱ vā siāceyya vā siācāpeyya vā, pācittiyaɱ.|| ||

Bhūtagāmavaggo dutiyo|| ||

21: Ovādasikkhāpadaɱ|| ||

Yo pana bhikkhu asammato bhikkhuniyo ovadeyya, pācittiyaɱ.|| ||

22: Atthaṅgatasikkhāpadaɱ|| ||

Sammato pi ce bhikkhu atthaṅgate suriye bhikkhuniyo ovadeyya, pācittiyaɱ.|| ||

23: Bhikkhunūpassayasikkhāpadaɱ|| ||

Yo pana bhikkhu bhikkhun'ūpassayaɱ upsaṅkamitvā bhikkhuniyo ovadeyya, aññatra samayā, pācittiyaɱ.|| ||

Tatthāyaɱ samayo: gilānā hoti bhikkhunī - ayaɱ tattha samayo.|| ||

24: Āmisasikkhāpadaɱ|| ||

Yo pana bhikkhu evaɱ vadeyya: 'āmisahetu bhikkhū[2] bhikkhuniyo ovadantī'-ti, pācittiyaɱ.|| ||

25: Cīvaradānasikkhāpadaɱ|| ||

Yo pana bhikkhu aññātikāya bhikkhuniyā cīvaraɱ dadeyya, aññatra pārivaṭṭakā, pācittiyaɱ.|| ||

26: Cīvarasibbanasikkhāpadaɱ|| ||

Yo pana bhikkhu aññātikāya bhikkhuniyā cīvaraɱ sibbeyya vā sibbāpeyya vā, pācittiyaɱ.|| ||

27: Saɱvidhānasikkhāpadaɱ|| ||

Yo pana bhikkhu[3] bhikkhuniyā saddhiɱ saɱvidhāya ekaddhāna-maggaɱ paṭipajjeyya antamaso gāmantaram-pi, aññatra samayā, pācittiyaɱ.|| ||

Tatthāyaɱ samayo: sattha-gamanīyo hoti Maggo sāsaṅkasammato sappaṭi-bhayo - ayaɱ tattha samayo.|| ||

28: Nāvābhirūhatasikkhāpadaɱ|| ||

Yo pana bhikkhu bhikkhuniyā saddhiɱ saɱvidhāya ekaɱ nāvaɱ abhirūheyya uddhagāminiɱ[4] vā adhogāminiɱ vā, aññatra tiriyaɱ taraṇāya, pācittiyaɱ.|| ||

29: Paripācitasikkhāpadaɱ|| ||

Yo pana bhikkhu jānaɱ bhikkhunīparipācitaɱ piṇḍa-pātaɱ bhuājeyya, aññatra pubbe gihīsamārambhā, pācittiyaɱ.|| ||

30: Rahonisajjasikkhāpadaɱ|| ||

Yo pana bhikkhu bhikkhuniyā saddhiɱ eko ekāya raho nisajjaɱ kappeyya, pācittiyaɱ.|| ||

Bhikkhunovādavaggo tatiyo|| ||

31: āvasathapiṇḍasikkhāpadaɱ|| ||

Agilānena bhikkhunā eko āvasathapiṇḍo bhuājitabbo. Tato ce uttariɱ bhuājeyya, pācittiyaɱ.|| ||

32: Gaṇabhojanasikkhāpadaɱ|| ||

Gaṇabhojane, aññatra samayā, pācittiyaɱ.|| ||

Tatthāyaɱ samayo: gilānasamayo, cīvaradānasamayo, cīvarakārasamayo, addhānagamanasamayo, nāvābhirūhanasamayo, mahāsamayo, samaṇabhattasamayo - ayaɱ tattha samayo.|| ||

33: Paramparabhojanasikkhāpadaɱ:|| ||

Paramparabhojane, aññatra samayā, pācittiyaɱ.|| ||

Tatthāyaɱ samayo: gilānasamayo, cīvaradānasamayo, cīvarakārasamayo - ayaɱ tattha samayo.|| ||

34: Kāṇamātusikkhāpadaɱ|| ||

Bhikkhuɱ pan'eva kulaɱ upagataɱ pūvehi vā manthehi vā abhihaṭṭhuɱ pavāreyya. ākaṅkha-mānena bhikkhunā dvattipattapūrā paṭiggahetabbā. Tato ce uttariɱ paṭiggaṇheyya, pācittiyaɱ.|| ||

Dvattipattapūre paṭiggahetvā tato nīharitvā bhikkhūhi saddhiɱ saɱvibhajitabbaɱ. Ayaɱ tattha sāmīci.|| ||

35: Paṭhamapavāraṇasikkhāpadaɱ|| ||

Yo pana bhikkhu bhuttāvī pavārito anatirittaɱ, khādanīyaɱ vā bhojanīyaɱ vā khādeyya vā bhuājeyya vā, pācittiyaɱ.|| ||

36: Dutiyapavāraṇasikkhāpadaɱ|| ||

Yo pana bhikkhu bhikkhuɱ bhuttāviɱ pavāritaɱ anatirittena khādanīyena vā bhojanīyena vā abhihaṭṭhuɱ pavāreyya: 'Handa bhikkhu khāda vā bhuāja vā'-ti, jānaɱ āsādanāpekkho, bhuttasmiɱ, pācittiyaɱ.|| ||

37: Vikālabhojanasikkhāpadaɱ|| ||

Yo pana bhikkhu vikāle khādanīyaɱ vā bhojanīyaɱ vā khādeyya vā bhuājeyya vā, pācittiyaɱ.|| ||

38: Sannidhikārasikkhāpadaɱ|| ||

Yo pana bhikkhu[5] sannidhikārakaɱ khādanīyaɱ vā bhojanīyaɱ vā khādeyya vā bhuājeyya vā, pācittiyaɱ.|| ||

39: Paṇītabhojanasikkhāpadaɱ|| ||

Yāni kho pana tāni paṇītabhojanāni, seyyath'īdaɱ: sappi, navanītaɱ, telaɱ, madhu, phāṇitaɱ, maccho, maɱsaɱ, khīraɱ, dadhi. Yo pana bhikkhu eva-rūpāni paṇītabhojanāni agilāno attano atthāya viññāpetvā bhuājeyya, pācittiyaɱ.|| ||

40: Dantaponasikkhāpadaɱ|| ||

Yo pana bhikkhu adinnaɱ mukhadvāraɱ āhāraɱ āhareyya, aññatra udakadantaponā, pācittiyaɱ.|| ||

41: Acelakasikkhāpadaɱ|| ||

Yo pana bhikkhu acelakassa vā paribbājakassa vā paribbājikāya vā sahatthā khādanīyaɱ vā bhojanīyaɱ vā dadeyya, pācittiyaɱ.|| ||

42: Uyyojanasikkhāpadaɱ|| ||

Yo pana bhikkhu bhikkhuɱ: 'Eh'āvuso gāmaɱ vā nigamaɱ vā piṇḍāya pavisissāmā'-ti. Tassa dāpetvā vā adāpetvā vā uyyojeyya: 'Gacch'āvuso na me tayā saddhiɱ kathā vā nisajjā vā phāsu hoti, ekakassa me kathā vā nisajjā vā phāsu hotī'-ti. Etad-eva paccayaɱ karitvā anaññaɱ, pācittiyaɱ.|| ||

43: Sabhojanasikkhāpadaɱ|| ||

Yo pana bhikkhu sabhojane kule anupakhajja nisajjaɱ kappeyya, pācittiyaɱ.|| ||

44: Paṭhamarahonisajjasikkhāpadaɱ|| ||

Yo pana bhikkhu[6] mātu-gāmena saddhiɱ raho paṭi-c-channe āsane nisajjaɱ kappeyya, pācittiyaɱ.|| ||

45: Dutiyarahonisajjasikkhāpadaɱ|| ||

Yo pana bhikkhu mātu-gāmena saddhiɱ eko ekāya raho nisajjaɱ kappeyya, pācittiyaɱ.|| ||

46: Cārittasikkhāpadaɱ|| ||

Yo pana bhikkhu nimantito sabhatto samāno santaɱ bhikkhuɱ anāpucchā purebhattaɱ vā pacchā-bhattaɱ vā kulesu cārittaɱ āpajjeyya, aññatra samayā, pācittiyaɱ.|| ||

Tatthāyaɱ samayo: cīvaradānasamayo, cīvarakārasamayo - ayaɱ tattha samayo.|| ||

47: Mahānāmasikkhāpadaɱ|| ||

Agilānena bhikkhunā cātumāsa-p-paccayapavāraṇā sāditabbā, aññatra punapavāraṇāya, aññatra niccapavāraṇāya. Tato ce uttariɱ sādiyeyya, pācittiyaɱ.|| ||

48: Uyyuttasenāsikkhāpadaɱ|| ||

Yo pana bhikkhu uyyuttaɱ senaɱ dassanāya gaccheyya, aññatra tathā-rūpa-p-paccayā, pācittiyaɱ.|| ||

49: Senāvāsasikkhāpadaɱ|| ||

Siyā ca tassa bhikkhuno kocid-eva paccayo senaɱ gamanāya, dirattatirattaɱ tena bhikkhunā senāya vasitabbaɱ. Tato ce uttariɱ vaseyya, pācittiyaɱ.|| ||

50: Uyyodhikasikkhāpadaɱ|| ||

Dirattatirattaɱ ce bhikkhu senāya vasamāno uyyodhikaɱ vā balaggaɱ vā senābyūhaɱ vā anīkadassanaɱ vā gaccheyya, pācittiyaɱ.|| ||

Acelakavaggo paācamo|| ||

51: Surāpānasikkhāpadaɱ|| ||

Surāmerayapāne, pācittiyaɱ.|| ||

52: Aṅgulipatodakasikkhāpadaɱ|| ||

Aṅgulipatodake, pācittiyaɱ.|| ||

53: Hassadhammasikkhāpadaɱ|| ||

Udake hassadhamme, pācittiyaɱ.|| ||

54: Anādariyasikkhapadaɱ|| ||

Anādariye, pācittiyaɱ.|| ||

55: Bhiɱsāpanakasikkhāpadaɱ|| ||

Yo pana bhikkhu bhikkhuɱ bhiɱsāpeyya, pācittiyaɱ.|| ||

56: Jotisikkhapadaɱ|| ||

Yo pana bhikkhu agilāno visibbanāpekkho jotiɱ samādaheyya vā samādahāpeyya vā, aññatra tathā-rūpa-p-paccayā, pācittiyaɱ.|| ||

57: Nahātasikkhāpadaɱ|| ||

Yo pana bhikkhu orenaddhamāsaɱ nahāyeyya, aññatra samayā, pācittiyaɱ.|| ||

Tatthāyaɱ samayo: diyaḍḍho māso seso gimhānan-ti, vassānassa paṭhamo māso, iccete aḍḍhateyyamāsā, uṇhasamayo, pariḷāhasamayo, gilānasamayo, kammasamayo, addhānagamanasamayo, vāta-vuṭṭhisamayo - ayaɱ tattha samayo.|| ||

58: Du-b-baṇṇakarasikkhāpadaɱ|| ||

Navaɱ pana bhikkhunā cīvaralābhena tiṇṇaɱ du-b-baṇṇa-karaṇānaɱ aññataraɱ du-b-baṇṇa-karaṇaɱ ādātabbaɱ, nīlaɱ vā kaddamaɱ vā kā'asāmaɱ vā. Anādā ce bhikkhu tiṇṇaɱ du-b-baṇṇa-karaṇānaɱ aññataraɱ du-b-baṇṇa-karaṇaɱ navaɱ cīvaraɱ paribhuājeyya, pācittiyaɱ.|| ||

59: Vikappanasikkhāpadaɱ|| ||

Yo pana bhikkhu bhikkhussa vā bhikkhuniyā vā sikkhamānāya vā sāmaṇerassa vā sāmaṇeriyā vā sāmaɱ cīvaraɱ vikappetvā apaccuddhārakaɱ paribhuājeyya, pācittiyaɱ.|| ||

60: Cīvarāpanidhānasikkhāpadaɱ|| ||

Yo pana bhikkhu[7] bhikkhussa pattaɱ vā cīvaraɱ vā nisīdanaɱ vā sūcigharaɱ vā kāya-bandhanaɱ vā apanidheyya vā apanidhāpeyya vā antamaso hassāpekkho pi, pācittiyaɱ.|| ||

Surāpānavaggo chaṭṭho|| ||

61: Saāciccapāṇasikkhāpadaɱ|| ||

Yo pana bhikkhu saācicca pāṇaɱ jīvitā voropeyya, pācittiyaɱ.|| ||

62: Sappāṇakasikkhāpadaɱ|| ||

Yo pana bhikkhu jānaɱ sappāṇakaɱ udakaɱ paribhuājeyya, pācittiyaɱ.|| ||

63: Ukkoṭanasikkhāpadaɱ|| ||

Yo pana bhikkhu jānaɱ yathā-dhammaɱ nihatādhikaraṇaɱ punakammāya ukkoṭeyya, pācittiyaɱ.|| ||

64: Duṭṭhullasikkhāpadaɱ|| ||

Yo pana bhikkhu bhikkhussa jānaɱ duṭṭhullaɱ āpattiɱ paṭicchādeyya, pācittiyaɱ.|| ||

65: Ūnavīsati-vassasikkhāpadaɱ|| ||

Yo pana bhikkhu jānaɱ ūnavīsati-vassaɱ puggalaɱ upasampādeyya, so ca puggalo anupasampanno, te ca bhikkhū gārayhā. Idaɱ tasmiɱ pācittiyaɱ.|| ||

66: Theyyasatthasikkhāpadaɱ|| ||

Yo pana bhikkhu jānaɱ theyyasatthena saddhiɱ saɱvidhāya ekaddhāna-maggaɱ paṭipajjeyya antamaso gāmantaram-pi, pācittiyaɱ.|| ||

67: Saɱvidhānasikkhāpadaɱ|| ||

Yo pana bhikkhu mātu-gāmena saddhiɱ saɱvidhāya ekaddhāna-maggaɱ paṭipajjeyya antamaso gāmantaram-pi, pācittiyaɱ.|| ||

68: Ariṭṭhasikkhāpadaɱ|| ||

Yo pana bhikkhu evaɱ vadeyya: 'Tath'āhaɱ Bhagavatā dhammaɱ desitaɱ ājānāmi. Yathā yeme antarāyikā dhammā vuttā Bhagavatā, te paṭisevato nālaɱ antarāyāyā'-ti. So bhikkhu bhikkhūhi evam-assa vacanīyo: 'Mā āyasmā evaɱ avaca. Mā Bhagavantaɱ abbh'ācikkhi, na hi sādhu Bhagavato abbha-k-khānaɱ, na hi Bhagavā evaɱ vadeyya. An-eka-pariyāyena āvuso antarāyikā dhammā antarāyikā vuttā Bhagavatā, alaā-ca pana te paṭisevato antarāyāyā'-ti. Evaā-ca pana so bhikkhu bhikkhūhi vuccamāno tatheva paggaṇheyya, so bhikkhu bhikkhūhi yāvatatiyaɱ samanubhāsitabbo tassa paṭinissaggāya. Yāvatatiyaɱ ce samanubhāsiyamāno tam-paṭinissajjeyya iccetaɱ kusalaɱ. No ce paṭinissajjeyya, pācittiyaɱ.|| ||

69: Ukkhittasambhogasikkhāpadaɱ|| ||

Yo pana bhikkhu jānaɱ tathāvādinā bhikkhunā akaṭānudhammena taɱ diṭṭhiɱ appaṭinissaṭṭhena saddhiɱ sambhuājeyya vā saɱvaseyya vā saha vā seyyaɱ kappeyya, pācittiyaɱ.|| ||

70: Kaṇṭakasikkhāpadaɱ|| ||

Samaṇuddeso pi ce evaɱ vadeyya: 'Tath'āhaɱ Bhagavatā dhammaɱ desitaɱ ājānāmi. Yathā yeme antarāyikā dhammā vuttā Bhagavatā, te paṭisevato nālaɱ antarāyāyā'-ti. So samaṇ'uddeso bhikkhūhi evam-assa vacanīyo: 'Mā āvuso samaṇ'uddesa evaɱ avaca. Mā Bhagavantaɱ abbh'ācikkhi, na hi sādhu Bhagavato abbha-k-khānaɱ, na hi Bhagavā evaɱ vadeyya. An-eka-pariyāyena āvuso samaṇ'uddesa antarāyikā dhammā antarāyikā vuttā Bhagavatā, alaā-ca pana te paṭisevato antarāyāyā'-ti. Evaā-ca pana so samaṇ'uddeso bhikkhūhi vuccamāno tatheva paggaṇheyya, so samaṇ'uddeso bhikkhūhi evam-assa vacanīyo: 'Ajja-t-agge te āvuso samaṇ'uddesa na c'eva so Bhagavā Satthā apadisitabbo, yam-pi c'aññe samaṇ'uddesā labhanti bhikkhūhi saddhiɱ dirattatirattaɱ sahaseyyaɱ, sā pi te n'atthi, cara pare vinassā'-ti. Yo pana bhikkhu[8] jānaɱ tathānāsitaɱ samaṇ'uddesaɱ upalāpeyya vā upaṭṭhāpeyya vā sambhuājeyya vā saha vā seyyaɱ kappeyya, pācittiyaɱ.|| ||

Sappāṇakavaggo sattamo|| ||

71: Sahadhammikasikkhāpadaɱ|| ||

Yo pana bhikkhu bhikkhūhi saha-dhammikaɱ vuccamāno evaɱ vadeyya: 'Na tāvāhaɱ āvuso etasmiɱ sikkhā-pade sikkhissāmi, yāva na aññaɱ bhikkhuɱ vyattaɱ vinaya-dharaɱ paripucchāmī'-ti, pācittiyaɱ.|| ||

Sikkhamānena bhikkhave bhikkhunā aññātabbaɱ paripucchitabbaɱ paripaāhitabbaɱ. Ayaɱ tattha sāmīci.|| ||

72: Vilekhanasikkhāpadaɱ|| ||

Yo pana bhikkhu Pātimokkhe uddissamāne evaɱ vadeyya: 'Kiɱ pan'imehi khuddānukhuddakehi sikkhā-padehi uddiṭṭhehi, yāva-d-eva kukkuccāya, vihesāya, vilekhāya saɱvaṭṭantī'-ti. Sikkhāpadavivaṇṇake, pācittiyaɱ.|| ||

73: Mohanasikkhāpadaɱ|| ||

Yo pana bhikkhu anvaddhamāsaɱ Pātimokkhe uddissamāne evaɱ vadeyya: 'Idān'eva kho ahaɱ jānāmi, ayam-pi kira dhammo suttāgato suttapariyāpanno anvaddhamāsaɱ uddesaɱ āga-c-chatī'-ti. Taā-ce bhikkhuɱ aññe bhikkhū jāneyyum: 'Nisinnapubbaɱ iminā bhikkhunā dvatti-k-khattuɱ Pātimokkhe uddissamāne. Ko pana vādo bhiyyo na ca tassa bhikkhuno aññāṇakena mutti atthi. Yaā-ca tattha āpattiɱ āpanno, taā-ca yathādhammo kāretabbo, uttariɱ cassa moho āropetabbo: 'Tassa te āvuso alābhā, tassa te dulladdhaɱ. Yaɱ tvaɱ Pātimokkhe uddissamāne, na sādhukaɱ atthikatvā mana-sikarosī'-ti. Idaɱ tasmiɱ mohanake, pācittiyaɱ.|| ||

74: Pahārasikkhāpadaɱ|| ||

Yo pana bhikkhu bhikkhussa kupito anatta-mano pahāraɱ dadeyya, pācittiyaɱ.|| ||

75: Talasattikasikkhāpadaɱ|| ||

Yo pana bhikkhu bhikkhussa kupito anatta-mano talasattikaɱ uggireyya, pācittiyaɱ.|| ||

76: Amūlakasikkhāpadaɱ|| ||

Yo pana bhikkhu bhikkhuɱ amūlakena saṅghā-disesena anuddhaɱseyya, pācittiyaɱ.|| ||

77: Saāciccasikkhāpadaɱ|| ||

Yo pana bhikkhu bhikkhussa saācicca kukkuccaɱ upadaheyya: 'Itissa muhuttam-pi aphāsu bhavissatī'-ti. Etad-eva paccayaɱ karitvā anaññaɱ, pācittiyaɱ.|| ||

78: Upassutisikkhāpadaɱ|| ||

Yo pana bhikkhu bhikkhūnaɱ bhaṇḍanajātānaɱ kalahajātānaɱ vivādāpannānaɱ upassutiɱ tiṭṭheyya: 'Yaɱ ime bhaṇissanti, taɱ sossāmī'-ti. Etad-eva paccayaɱ karitvā anaññaɱ, pācittiyaɱ.|| ||

79: Kammapaṭibāhanasikkhāpadaɱ|| ||

Yo pana bhikkhu dhammikānaɱ kammānaɱ chandaɱ datvā pacchā khīyanadhammaɱ āpajjeyya, pācittiyaɱ.|| ||

80: Chandaɱadatvāgamana-sikkhāpadaɱ|| ||

Yo pana bhikkhu saṅghe vinicchayakathāya vattamānāya chandaɱ adatvā uṭṭhāy āsanā pakkameyya, pācittiyaɱ.|| ||

81: Dabbasikkhāpadaɱ|| ||

Yo pana bhikkhu samaggena saṅghena cīvaraɱ datvā pacchā khīyanadhammaɱ āpajjeyya: 'Yathāsanthutaɱ bhikkhū saṅghikaɱ lābhaɱ pariṇāmentī'-ti, pācittiyaɱ.|| ||

82: Pariṇāmana-sikkhāpadaɱ|| ||

Yo pana bhikkhu jānaɱ saṅghikaɱ lābhaɱ pariṇataɱ puggalassa pariṇāmeyya, pācittiyaɱ.|| ||

Sahadhammikavaggo aṭṭhamo|| ||

83: Rājantarapurasikkhāpadaɱ|| ||

Yo pana bhikkhu[9] rañño khattiyassa muddhāvisittassa anikkhantarājake anīgataratanake pubbe appaṭisaɱvidito indakhīlaɱ atikkāmeyya, pācittiyaɱ.|| ||

84: Ratanasikkhāpadaɱ|| ||

Yo pana bhikkhu ratanaɱ vā ratanasammataɱ vā, aññatra ajjhārāmā vā ajjhāvasathā vā uggaṇheyya vā uggaṇhāpeyya vā, pācittiyaɱ.|| ||

Ratanaɱ vā pana bhikkhunā ratanasammataɱ vā ajjhārāme vā ajjhāvasathe vā uggahetvā vā uggahāpetvā vā nikkhipitabbaɱ: 'Yassa bhavissati, so harissatī'-ti. Ayaɱ tattha sāmīci.|| ||

85: Vikālagāmappavesanasikkhāpadaɱ:[10]|| ||

Yo pana bhikkhu santaɱ bhikkhuɱ anāpucchā vikāle gāmaɱ paviseyya, aññatra tathā-rūpā accāyikā karaṇīyā, pācittiyaɱ.|| ||

86: Sūcigharasikkhāpadaɱ|| ||

Yo pana bhikkhu[11] aṭṭhimayaɱ vā danta-mayaɱ vā visāṇamayaɱ vā sūcigharaɱ kārāpeyya, bhedanakaɱ, pācittiyaɱ.|| ||

87: Maācasikkhāpadaɱ|| ||

Navaɱ pana bhikkhunā maācaɱ vā pīṭhaɱ vā kārayamānena aṭṭhaṅgulapādakaɱ kāretabbaɱ Sugataṅgulena, aññatra heṭṭhimāya aṭaniyā. Taɱ atikkāmayato, chedanakaɱ, pācittiyaɱ.|| ||

88: Tūlonaddhasikkhāpadaɱ|| ||

Yo pana bhikkhu maācaɱ vā pīṭhaɱ vā tūlonaddhaɱ kārāpeyya, uddālanakaɱ, pācittiyaɱ.|| ||

89: Nisīdanasikkhāpadaɱ|| ||

Nisīdanaɱ pana bhikkhunā kārayamānena pamāṇikaɱ kāretabbaɱ. Tatridaɱ pamāṇaɱ: dīghaso dve vidatthiyo Sugatavidatthiyā, tiriyaɱ diyaḍḍhaɱ, dasā vidatthi. Taɱ atikkāmayato, chedanakaɱ, pācittiyaɱ.|| ||

90: Kaṇḍupaṭicchādisikkhāpadaɱ|| ||

Kaṇḍupaṭicchādiɱ pana bhikkhunā kārayamānena pamāṇikā kāretabbā. Tatridaɱ[12] pamāṇaɱ: dīghaso catasso vidatthiyo Sugatavidatthiyā, tiriyaɱ dve vidatthiyo. Taɱ atikkāmayato, chedanakaɱ, pācittiyaɱ.|| ||

91: Vassikasāṭikasikkhāpadaɱ|| ||

Vassikasāṭikaɱ pana bhikkhunā kārayamānena pamāṇikā kāretabbā. Tatridaɱ[13] pamāṇaɱ: dīghaso cha vidatthiyo Sugatavidatthiyā, tiriyaɱ aḍḍhateyyā. Taɱ atikkāmayato, chedanakaɱ, pācittiyaɱ.|| ||

92: Nandattherasikkhāpadaɱ|| ||

Yo pana bhikkhu Sugatacīvarappamāṇaɱ cīvaraɱ kārāpeyya atirekaɱ vā, chedanakaɱ, pācittiyaɱ.|| ||

Tatridaɱ[14] Sugatassa Sugatacīvarappamāṇaɱ: dīghaso nava vidatthiyo Sugatavidatthiyā, tiriyaɱ cha vidatthiyo. Idaɱ Sugatassa Sugatacīvarappamāṇaɱ.|| ||

Rājavaggo navamo|| ||

Uddiṭṭhā kho āyasmanto dvenavuti pācittiyā dhammā.||
Tatthāyasmante pucchāmi: kaccittha parisuddhā?||
Dutiyam-pi pucchāmi: kaccittha parisuddhā?||
Tatiyam-pi pucchāmi: kaccittha parisuddhā?||
Parisuddhetthāyasmanto, tasmā tuṇhī, evam-etaɱ dhārayāmi.|| ||

Pācittiyā niṭṭhitā|| ||
