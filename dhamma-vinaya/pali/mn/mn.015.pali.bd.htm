Majjhima Nikāya
1. Mūla-Paṇṇāsa
2. Sīhanāda Vagga

Sutta 15

Anumāna Suttaɱ

Adapted from the 1995 edition of the digital version of the Sri Lanka Buddha Jayanti Tripitaka Series.

 

[95]

[1][chlm][pts][upal] Evaɱ me sutaɱ:|| ||

Ekaɱ samayaɱ āyasmā Mahā Moggallāno Bhaggesu viharati Suɱsumāragire bhesakalāvane Migadāye.|| ||

Tatra kho āyasmā Mahā Moggallāno bhikkhū āmantesi:|| ||

"Āvuso Bhikkhavo" ti.|| ||

"Āvuso" ti||
kho te bhikkhū āyasmato Mahā Moggallānassa paccassosuɱ.|| ||

Āyasmā Mahā Moggallāno etad avoca:|| ||

[2] "Pavāreti ce pi āvuso bhikkhū:|| ||

'Vadantu maɱ āyasmanto,||
vacanīyo'mhi āyasmantehī' ti,|| ||

so ca hoti dubbaco do-vacassa-karaṇehi dhammehi||
samannāgato akkhamo appada-k-khiṇaggāhī anusāsaniɱ||
atha kho naɱ sabrahma-cārī||
na c'eva vattabbaɱ maññanti||
na ca anusāsitabbaɱ maññanti||
na ca tasmiɱ puggale vissāsaɱ āpajjitabbaɱ maññanti.|| ||
