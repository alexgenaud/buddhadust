[1][chlm][pts][upal] Evaɱ me sutaɱ:|| ||

Ekaɱ samayaɱ Bhagavā sāvattiyaɱ viharati Jetavane Anāthapiṇḍikassa ārāme.|| ||

Tena kho pana samayena subho māṇavo Todeyya-putto Sāvatthīyaɱ paṭivasati aññatarassa gahapatissa nivesane kenacid-eva karaṇīyena.|| ||
