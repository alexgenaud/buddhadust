Majjhima Nikāya
II. Majjhima-Paṇṇāsa
5. Brāhmaṇa Vagga

Sutta 95

Caŋkī Suttaɱ

Adapted from the 1995 edition of the digital version of the Sri Lanka Buddha Jayanti Tripitaka Series.

 

 

[1][chlm][pts][than][ntbb][upal] Evaɱ me sutaɱ:|| ||

Ekaɱ samayaɱ Bhagavā Kosalesu cārikaɱ cara-māno mahatā bhikkhu-saŋghena saddhiɱ yena opasādaɱ1 nāma kosalānaɱ brāhmaṇa-gāmo tad avasari.|| ||

Tatra sudaɱ Bhagavā opasāde viharati uttarena opasādaɱ devavane sālavane.|| ||

Tena kho pana samayena Caŋkībrāhmaṇo opasādaɱ ajjhāvasati sattussadaɱ satiṇakaṭṭhodakaɱ sadhaññaɱ rāja-bhoggaɱ raññā Pasenadinā kosalena dinnaɱ rājadāyaɱ brahmadeyyaɱ.|| ||

Assosuɱ kho opasādakā brāhmaṇa-gahapatikā: 'samaṇo khalu bho Gotamo Sakya-putto Sakya-kulā pabba-jito Kosalesu cārikaɱ cara-māno mahatā bhikkhu-saŋghena saddhiɱ opasādaɱ anuppatto opasāde viharati uttarena opasādaɱ devavane sālavane|| ||

Taɱ kho pana Bhagavantaɱ Gotamaɱ evaɱ kalyāṇo kitti-saddo abbhu-g-gato: 'Iti pi so Bhagavā arahaɱ Sammā-Sam-Buddho vijjā-caraṇa-sampanno Sugato loka-vidū anuttaro purisa-damma-sārathi Satthā deva-manussānaɱ Buddho Bhagavā' ti.|| ||

So imaɱ lokaɱ sa-devakaɱ sa-mārakaɱ sa-brahmakaɱ sa-s-samaṇa-brāhmaṇiɱ pajaɱ sa-deva-manussaɱ sayaɱ abhiññā sacchi-katvā pavedeti,||
so dhammaɱ deseti ādi kalyāṇaɱ majjhe kalyāṇaɱ pariyosāna-kalyāṇaɱ sātthaɱ savyañ janaɱ kevala-paripuṇṇaɱ parisuddhaɱ Brahma-cariyaɱ pakāseti.|| ||

Sādhu kho pana tathā-rūpānaɱ arahataɱ dassanaɱ hotī' ti.|| ||

Atha kho opasādakā2 brāhmaṇa-gahapatikā opasādā ni-k-khamitvā saŋghāsaŋghī gaṇībhūtā uttarena mukhā4 gacchanti yena devavanaɱ sāla-vanaɱ.|| ||

Tena kho pana samayena Caŋkī brāhmaṇo upari pāsāde divāseyyaɱ upagato hoti.|| ||

Addasā kho Caŋkī brahmaṇo opasādake brāhmaṇa-gahapatike,||
opasādā ni-k-khamitvā saŋghāsaŋghī gaṇībhūte uttarena mukhe gacchante yena devavanaɱ sāla-vanaɱ,||
disvāna khattaɱ āmantesi: kin nu kho bho khatte,||
opasādakā brāhmaṇa-gahapatikā opasādā ni-k-khamitvā saŋghāsaŋghī gaṇībhūtā uttarena mukhā gacchanti yena devavanaɱ sāla-vananti.|| ||

Atthi bho Caŋkī,||
samaṇo bho Gotamo Sakya-putto Sakya-kulā pabba-jito Kosalesu cārikaɱ cara-māno mahatā bhikkhu-saŋghena saddhiɱ opasādaɱ anuppatto opasāde viharati uttarena opasādaɱ devavane sālavane.|| ||

Taɱ kho pana Bhagavantaɱ Gotamaɱ evaɱ kalyāṇo kitti-saddo abbhu-g-gato: iti pi so Bhagavā arahaɱ Sammā-Sam-Buddho vijjā-caraṇa-sampanno Sugato loka-vidū anttaro purisa-damma-sārathi Satthā deva-manussānaɱ Buddho Bhagavā' ti.|| ||

Tamete bhavantaɱ Gotamaɱ dassanāya upasankaman' ti.|| ||

Tena hi bho khatte,||
yena opasādakā brāhmaṇa-gahapatikā ten'upasankama,||
upasankamitvā opasādake brāhmaṇa-gahapatike evaɱ vadehi: Caŋkī bho brāhmaṇo evam āha: āgamentu kira bhavanto,||
Caŋkīpi brāhmaṇo samaṇaɱ getamaɱ dassanāya upasankamissa' ti.|| ||

Evaɱ bhoti kho so khatto1 caŋkissa brāhmaṇassa [165] paṭissutvā yena opasādakā brāhmaṇa-gahapatikā ten'upasankami,||
upasankamitvā opasādake brāhmaṇa-gahapatike etad avoca: Caŋkī bho brāhmaṇo evam āha: āgamentu kira bhavanto,||
Caŋkīpi brāhmaṇo samaṇaɱ Gotamaɱ dassanāya upasankamissa' ti.|| ||

Tena kho pana samayena nānā-verajjakānaɱ brāhmaṇānaɱ pañca-mattāni brāhmaṇasatāni opasāde paṭivasanti kenacid-eva karaṇīyena.|| ||

Assosuɱ kho te brāhmaṇā Caŋkī kira brāhmaṇo samaṇaɱ Gotamaɱ dassanāya upasankamissa' ti.|| ||

Atha kho te brāhmaṇā yena Caŋkī brāhmaṇo ten'upasankamiɱsu,||
upasankamitvā caŋkiɱ brāhmaṇaɱ etad avocuɱ: saccaɱ kira bhavaɱ Caŋkī samaṇaɱ Gotamaɱ dassanāya upasankamissatī' ti.|| ||

Evaɱ kho me bho hoti,aham pi samaṇaɱ Gotamaɱ dassanāya upasankamissāmīti.|| ||

Mā bhavaɱ Caŋkī samaṇaɱ Gotamaɱ dassanāya upasankami.|| ||

Na arahati bhavaɱ Caŋkī samaṇaɱ Gotamaɱ dassanāya upasankamituɱ,||
samaṇotv'eva Gotamo arahati bhavantaɱ caŋkiɱ dassanāya upasankamituɱ.|| ||

Bhavaɱ hī Caŋkī ubhato sujāto mātito ca pitito ca,||
saɱsuddhagahaṇiko yāva sattamā pitā-mah'ayugā akkhitto anupakkuṭṭho jāti-vādena,||
yam pi bhavaɱ Caŋkī ubhato sujāto mātito ca pītito ca,||
saɱsuddhagahaṇiko yāva sattamā pitā-mah'ayugā akkhitto anupakkuṭṭho jāti-vādena.|| ||

Imināpaŋgena na arahati bhavaɱ Caŋkī samaṇaɱ Gotamaɱ dassanāya upasankamituɱ,||
samaṇotv'eva Gotamo arahati bhavantaɱ caŋkiɱ dassanāya upasankamituɱ.|| ||

Yam pi bhavaɱ hi Caŋkī aḍḍho maha-d-dhano mahā-bhogo imināpaŋgena na arahati bhavaɱ Caŋkī samaṇaɱ Gotamaɱ dassanāya upasankamituɱ,||
samaṇotv'eva Gotamo arahati bhavantaɱ caŋkiɱ dassanāya upasankamituɱ.|| ||

Yam pi bhavaɱ hi Caŋkī tiṇṇaɱ vedanānaɱ pāragu sanighaṇḍukeṭubhānaɱ sākkhara-p-pabhe-dānaɱ itihāsa-pañca-mānaɱ,padako veyyā-karaṇaṇo lokāyatamahā-purisa-lakkhaṇesu anavayo imināpaŋgena na arahati bhavaɱ ḍaŋki samaṇaɱ Gotamaɱ dassanāya upasankamituɱ,||
yam pi bhavaɱ hi Caŋkī abhirūpo dassanīyo pāsādiko paramāya vaṇṇa-pokkha-ratāya samannāgato brahmavaṇṇī brahmavaccasī akkhuddāvakāso dassanāya imināpaŋgena na arahati bhavaɱ Caŋkī samaṇaɱ Gotamaɱ dasnāya upasankamituɱ,||
samaṇotv'eva Gotamo arahati bhavantaɱ caŋkiɱ dassanāya upasankamituɱ.|| ||

Yam pi bhavaɱ Caŋkī,||
sīlavā vuddhasīlī vuddhasīlena samannāgato imināpaŋgena na arahati bhavaɱ Caŋkī samaṇaɱ Gotamaɱ dassanāya upasankamituɱ.|| ||

Yam pi bhavaɱ hi Caŋkī kalyāṇa-vāco kalyāṇa-vākkaraṇo [166] poriyā vācāya samannāgato vissaṭṭhāya aneḷaga'āya atthassa viññāpaniyā imināpaŋgena na arahati bhavaɱ Caŋkī samaṇaɱ Gotamaɱ dassanāya upasankamituɱ,||
yam pi bhavaɱ Caŋkī,||
bahunnaɱ ācariya-pācariyo tīṇi māṇavakasatāni mante vāceti imināpaŋgena na arahati bhavaɱ Caŋkī samaṇaɱ Gotamaɱ dassanāya upasankamituɱ,||
yam pi bhavaɱ hi Caŋkī rañño Pasenadissa Kosalassa sakkato garukato mānito pūjito apacito imināpaŋgena na arahati bhavaɱ Caŋkī samaṇaɱ Gotamaɱ dassanāya upasankamituɱ,||
yam pi bhavaɱ Caŋkī brāhmaṇassa pokkhara-sātissa sakkato garukato mānito pūjito apacito imināpaŋgena na arahati bhavaɱ Caŋkī samaṇaɱ Gotamaɱ dassanāya upasankamituɱ,||
yam pi bhavaɱ Caŋkī opasādaɱ ajjhāvasati sattussadaɱ satiṇakaṭṭhodakaɱ sadhaññaɱ rāja-bhoggaɱ raññaɱ Pasenadinā kosalena dinnaɱ rājadāyaɱ brahmadeyyaɱ.|| ||

Yam pi bhavaɱ Caŋkī opasādaɱ ajjhāvasati sattussadaɱ satiṇakaṭṭhodakaɱ sadhaññaɱ rāja-bhoggaɱ raññā Pasenadinā kosalena dinnaɱ rājadāyaɱ brahmadeyyaɱ imināpaŋgena na arahati bhavaɱ Caŋkī samaṇaɱ Gotamaɱ dassanāya upasankamituɱ.|| ||

Samaṇo tv'eva Gotamo arahati bhavantaɱ caŋkiɱ dassanāya upasankamitun' ti.|| ||

Evaɱ vutte Caŋkī brāhmaṇo te brāhmaṇe etad avoca: tena hi bho,||
mama pi suṇātha yathā mayam'eva arahāma taɱ bhavantaɱ Gotamaɱ dassanāya upasankamituɱ.|| ||

Na tv'eva arahati so bhavaɱ gātamo amhākaɱ dassanāya upasankamituɱ.|| ||

Samaṇo khalū bho Gotamo ubhato sujāto mātito ca pitito ca,||
saɱsuddhagahaṇiko yāva sattamā pitā-mah'ayugā akkhitto anupakkuṭṭho jāti-vādena.|| ||

Yam pi bho Samaṇo Gotamo ubhato sujāto mātito ca pitato ca,||
saɱsuddhagahaṇiko yāva sattamā pitā-mah'ayugā akkhitto anupakkuṭṭho jāti-vādena,||
imināpaŋgena na arahati so bhavaɱ Gotamo amhākaɱ dassanāya upasankamituɱ,||
atha kho mayam'eva arahāma taɱ bhavantaɱ Gotamaɱ dassanāya upasankamituɱ.|| ||

Samaṇo khalū bho Gotamo pahūtaɱ hirañña-suvaṇṇaɱ pahāya pabba-jito bhumigatañ ca vehāsaṭṭhañca yam pi bho Samaṇo Gotamo pahutaɱ hirañña-suvaṇṇaɱ pahāya pabba-jito bhūmigatañ ca vehāsaṭṭhañca imināpaŋgena na arahati so bhavaɱ Gotamo amhākaɱ dassanāya upasankamituɱ,||
atha kho mayam'eva arahāma taɱ bhavantaɱ dassanāya upasankamituɱ.|| ||

Samaṇo khalu bho Gotamo daharo samāno susukālakeso bhadrena yobbanena samannāgato paṭhamena vayasā agārasmā anagāriyaɱ pabba-jito imināpaŋgena na arahati so bhavaɱ Gotamo amhākaɱ dassanāya upasankamituɱ,||
atha kho mayam'eva arahāma taɱ bhavantaɱ Gotamaɱ dassanāya upasankamituɱ.|| ||

Samaṇo khalu bho Gotamo akāmakānaɱ mātā-pitunnaɱ assumukhānaɱ rudantānaɱ kesa-massuɱ ohāretvā kāsāyāni vatthāni acchādetvā agārasmā anagāriyaɱ pabba-jito imināpaŋgena na arahati so bhavaɱ Gotamo amhākaɱ dassanāya upasankamituɱ,||
atha kho mayam'eva arahāma taɱ bhavantaɱ Gotamaɱ dassanāya upasankamituɱ.|| ||

Samaṇo khalu bho Gotamo abhirūpo dassanīyo pasādiko paramāya vaṇṇa-pokkha-ratāya samannāgato [167] brahmavaṇṇī brahmavaccasī akkhuddāvakāso dassanāya imināpaŋgena na arahati kho bhavaɱ Gotamo amhākaɱ dassanāya upasankamituɱ,||
atha kho mayam'eva arahāma taɱ bhavantaɱ Gotamaɱ dassanāya upasankamituɱ.|| ||

Samaṇo khalu bho Gotamo sīlavā ariyasilī kusalasīlī kusalasīlena samannāgato imināpaŋgena na arahati so bhavaɱ Gotamo amhākaɱ dassanāya upasankamituɱ,||
atha kho mayam'eva arahāma taɱ bhavantaɱ Gotamaɱ dassanāya upasankamituɱ.|| ||

Samaṇo khalu bho Gotamo kalyāṇa-vāco kalyāṇa-vākkaraṇo poriyā vācāya samannāgato vissaṭṭhāya aneḷaga'āya atthassa viññāpaniyā imināpaŋgena na arahati so bhavaɱ Gotamo amhākaɱ dassanāya upasankamituɱ,||
atha kho mayam'eva arahāma taɱ bhavantaɱ Gotamaɱ dassanāya upasankamituɱ.|| ||

Samaṇo khalu bho Gotamo bahunnaɱ ācariya-pācariyo imināpaŋgena na arahati so bhavaɱ Gotamo amhākaɱ dassanāya upasankamituɱ,||
atha kho mayam'eva arahāma taɱ bhavantaɱ Gotamaɱ dassanāya upasankamituɱ.|| ||

Samaṇo khalu bho Gotamo khīṇakāmarāgo vigatacāpallo imināpaŋgena na arahati so bhavaɱ Gotamo amhākaɱ dassanāya upasankamituɱ,||
atha kho mayam'eva arahāma taɱ bhavantaɱ Gotamaɱ dassanāya upasankamituɱ.|| ||

Samaṇo khalu bho Gotamo kammavādī kiriyavādī apāpapurekkhāro brahmaññāya pajāya imināpaŋgena na arahati so bhavaɱ Gotamo amhākaɱ dassanāya upasankamituɱ,||
atha kho mayam'eva arahāma taɱ bhavantaɱ Gotamaɱ dassanāya upasankamituɱ.|| ||

Samaṇo khalu bho Gotamo uccākulā pabba-jito asambhinnā khattiyakulā imināpaŋgena na arahati so bhavaɱ Gotamo amhākaɱ dassanāya upasankamituɱ,||
atha kho mayam'eva arahāma taɱ bhavantaɱ Gotamaɱ dassanāya upasankamituɱ.|| ||

Samaṇo khalu bho Gotamo aḍḍhakulā pabba-jito maha-d-dhanā mahā-bhogā imināpaŋgena na arahati so bhavaɱ Gotamo amhākaɱ dassanāya upasankamituɱ,||
atha kho mayam'eva arahāma taɱ bhavantaɱ Gotamaɱ dassanāya upasankamituɱ.|| ||

Samaṇaɱ khalu bho Gotamaɱ tiroraṭṭhā tirojanapadā sampucchituɱ āga-c-chanti imināpaŋgena na arahati so bhavaɱ Gotamo amhākaɱ dassanāya upasankamituɱ,||
atha kho mayam'eva arahāma taɱ bhavantaɱ Gotamaɱ dassanāya upasankamituɱ.|| ||

Samaṇaɱ khalu bho Gotamaɱ anekāni devatāsahassāni pāṇehi saraṇaɱ gatāni imināpaŋgena na arahati so bhavaɱ Gotamo amhākaɱ dassanāya upasankamituɱ,||
atha kho mayam'eva arahāma taɱ bhavantaɱ Gotamaɱ dassanāya upasankamituɱ.|| ||

Samaṇaɱ khalu bho Gotamaɱ evaɱ kalyāṇo kitti-saddo abbhu-g-gato: iti pi so Bhagavā arahaɱ Sammā-Sam-Buddho vijjācaranasampanno Sugato loka-vidū anuttaro purisa-damma-sārathi Satthā deva-manussānaɱ Buddho Bhagavāti imināpaŋgena na arahati so bhavaɱ Gotamo amhākaɱ dassanāya upasankamituɱ,||
atha kho mayam'eva arahāma taɱ bhavantaɱ Gotamaɱ dassanāya upasankamituɱ.|| ||

Samaṇo khalu bho Gotamo dvattiɱsamahā-purisa-lakkhaṇehi samannāgato imināpaŋgena na arahati so bhavaɱ Gotamo amhākaɱ dassanāya upasankamituɱ,||
atha kho mayam'eva arahāma taɱ bhavantaɱ Gotamaɱ dassanāya upasankamituɱ.|| ||

Samaṇaɱ khalu bho Gotamaɱ rājā Māgadho seniyo Bimbisāro saputta-dāro pāṇehi saraṇaɱ gato imināpaŋgena na arahati so bhavaɱ Gotamo amhākaɱ dassanāya upasankamituɱ,||
atha kho mayam'eva arahāma taɱ bhavantaɱ Gotamaɱ dassanāya upasankamituɱ.|| ||

Samaṇaɱ khalu bho Gotamaɱ rāja Pasenadi kosalo saputta-dāro pāṇehi saraṇaɱ gato imināpaŋgena na arahati so bhavaɱ Gotamo amhākaɱ dassanāya upasankamituɱ,||
atha kho mayam'eva arahāma taɱ bhavantaɱ Gotamaɱ dassanāya upasankamituɱ.|| ||

Samaṇaɱ khalu bho Gotamaɱ brāhmaṇo pokkhara-sāti saputta-dāro pāṇehi saraṇaɱ gato imināpaŋgena na arahati so bhavaɱ Gotamo amhākaɱ dassanāya upasankamituɱ ,||
atha kho mayam'eva arahāma taɱ bhavantaɱ Gotamaɱ dassanāya upasankamituɱ.|| ||

Samaṇo khalu bho Gotamo opasādaɱ anuppatto opasāde viharati uttarena opasādaɱ devavane sālavane.|| ||

Ye pana kho keci samaṇā vā brāhmaṇā vā amhākaɱ gāmakkhettaɱ āgaccanti.|| ||

Atithi no te honti.|| ||

Atithi kho panamhehi sakkātabbā garukātabbā mānetabbā pūjetabbā,||
yam pi bho Samaṇo Gotamo opasādaɱ anuppatto opasāde viharati uttarena opasādaɱ devavane sālavane.|| ||

Atithi asmākaɱ Samaṇo Gotamo.|| ||

Atithi kho panambhehi sakkātabbā garukātabbo mānetabbo pūjetabbo.|| ||

[168] iminā paŋgena na arahati so bhavaɱ Gotamo amhākaɱ dassanāya upasankamituɱ.|| ||

Atha kho mayam'eva arahāma taɱ bhavantaɱ Gotamaɱ dassanāya upasankamituɱ.|| ||

Ettakaɱ kho ahaɱ bho tassa bhoto Gotamassa vaṇṇaɱ pariyāpuṇāmi.|| ||

Na ca so bhavaɱ Gotamo ettakavaṇṇo,||
aparimāṇavaṇṇo hi so bhavaɱGotamo.|| ||

Eka-m-ekena pi bho,||
aŋgena samannāgato na arahati so bhavaɱ Gotamo amhākaɱ dassanāya upasankamituɱ.|| ||

Atha kho mayam'eva arahāma taɱ bhavantaɱ Gotamaɱ dassanāya upasankamituɱ.|| ||

Tena hi bho sabb'eva mayaɱ samaṇaɱ Gotamaɱ dassanāya upasankamissāmā' ti.|| ||

Atha kho Caŋkī brāhmaṇo mahatā brāhmaṇagaṇena saddhiɱ yena Bhagavā ten'upasankami.|| ||

Upasaŋkamitvā Bhagavatā saddhiɱ sammodi,||
sammodanīyaɱ kathaɱ sārāṇīyaɱ vīti-sāretvā eka-m-antaɱ nisīdi.|| ||

Tena kho pana samayena Bhagavā vuddhehi vuddhehi brāhmaṇehi saddhiɱ kiñci kiñci kathaɱ sārāṇīyaɱ vīti-sāretvā nisinno hoti.|| ||

Tena kho pana samayena kāpaṭiko nāma māṇavo daharo vuttasiro soḷāsavassuddesiko jātiyā,||
tiṇṇaɱ vedānaɱ pāragu sanighanḍukeṭubhānaɱ sākkhara-p-pabhe-dānaɱ itihāsa-pañca-mānaɱ padako veyyā-karaṇo lokāyatamahā-purisa-lakkhaṇesu anavayo,||
tassaɱ parisāyaɱ nisinno hoti.|| ||

So vuḍḍhānaɱ brāhmaṇānaɱ Bhagavatā saddhiɱ mantayamānānaɱ antar'antarā kathaɱ opāteti.|| ||

Atha kho Bhagavā kāpaṭikaɱ māṇavaɱ apasādesi: māyasmā Bhāradvājo vuḍḍhānaɱ vuḍḍhānaɱ brāhmaṇānaɱ mantayamānānaɱ antar'antarā kathaɱ opātetu3,||
kathā-pariyosānaɱ āyasmā bharadvājo āgametu' ti.|| ||

Evaɱ vutte Caŋkī brāhmaṇo Bhagavantaɱ etad avoca: mā bhavaɱ Gotamo kāpaṭikaɱ māṇavaɱ apasādesi,||
kula-putto ca kāpaṭiko māṇavo bahu-s-suto ca kāpaṭiko māṇavo kalyāṇa-vākkaraṇo va kāpaṭiko māṇavo paṇḍito ca kāpaṭiko māṇavo,||
pahoti ca kāpaṭiko māṇavo bhotā Gotamena saddhiɱ asmiɱ vacane patimantetun' ti.|| ||

Atha kho Bhagavato etad ahosi: ḷaddhā [169] kho kāpaṭikassa māṇavassa tevijjake pāvacane kataɱ4 bhavissati.|| ||

Tathā hi naɱ brāhmaṇā sampurekkharontī' ti.|| ||

Atha kho kāpaṭikassa māṇavassa etad ahosi: 'yadā me Samaṇo Gotamo cakkhunā cakkhuɱ upasaŋharissati,||
ath'āhaɱ samaṇaɱ Gotamaɱ pañhaɱ pucchissāmī' ti.|| ||

Atha kho Bhagavā kāpaṭikassa māṇavassa cetasā ceto-parivitakkam-aññāya yena kāpaṭiko māṇavo tena cakkhūni upasaɱhāsi.|| ||

Atha kho kāpaṭikassa māṇavassa etad ahosi: 'samannāharati kho maɱ Samaṇo Gotamo,||
