Majjhima Nikāya
II. Majjhima-Paṇṇāsa
5. Brāhmaṇa Vagga

Sutta 92

Sela Suttaɱ

Adapted from the 1995 edition of the digital version of the Sri Lanka Buddha Jayanti Tripitaka Series.

 

[146] [102][ed1]

[1][chlm][pts][upal] Evaɱ me sutaɱ:|| ||

Ekaɱ samayaɱ Bhagavā Aŋguttarāpesu cārikaɱ cara-māno mahatā bhikkhu-saŋghena saddhiɱ aḍḍhateḷa [103] sehi bhikkhu-satehi yena Āpaṇaɱ nāma Aŋguttarāpānaɱ nigamo tad avasari.|| ||

Assosi kho Keṇiyo jaṭilo:|| ||

"Samaṇo khalu bho Gotamo Sakya-putto Sakya-kulā pabba-jito Aŋguttarāpesu cārikaɱ cara-māno mahatā bhikkhu-saŋghena saddhiɱ aḍḍhateḷasehi bhikkhu-satehi Āpaṇaɱ anuppatto,||
taɱ kho pana bhavantaɱ Gotamaɱ evaɱ kalyāṇo kitti-saddo abbhu-g-gato,|| ||

"Iti pi so Bhagavā arahaɱ sammāSamBuddho vijjā-caraṇa-sampanno Sugato loka-vidu anuttaro purisa-damma-sārathi Satthā deva-manussānaɱ Buddho Bhagavā" ti so imaɱ lokaɱ sa-devakaɱ sa-mārakaɱ sabrahamakaɱ sa-s-samaṇa-brāhmaṇiɱ pajaɱ sadevamunassaɱ sayaɱ abhiññā sacchi-katvā pavedeti,||
so dhammaɱ deseti ādi-kalyāṇa majjhe kalyāṇaɱ pariyosāna-kalyāṇaɱ sātthaɱ savyañajanaɱ kevala-paripuṇaṇaɱ pariyuddhaɱ Brahma-cariyaɱ pakāseti,||
sādhu kho pana tathā-rūpānaɱ arahataɱ dasasnaɱ hoti" ti.|| ||

Atha kho Keṇiyo jaṭilo yena Bhagavā ten'upasankami,||
upasankamitvā Bhagavatā saddhiɱ sammodi,||
sammodiniyaɱ kathaɱ sārāṇiyaɱ vitisāretvā eka-m-antaɱ nisīdi.|| ||

Eka-m-antaɱ nisinnaɱ kho Keṇiyaɱ jaṭilaɱ Bhagavā dhammiyā kathāya sandassesi samādapesi samuttejesi samp'ahaɱsesi.|| ||

Atha kho Keṇiyā jaṭilo Bhagavatā dhammiyā kathāya sanda-s-sito samāda-pito samutte-jito samp'ahaɱsito Bhagavantaɱ etad avoca:|| ||

"Adhivāsetu me bhavaɱ Gotamo svātanāya bhattaɱ saddhiɱ bhikkhu-saŋghenā" ti,||
evaɱ vutto Bhagavā Keṇiyaɱ jaṭilaɱ etad avoca:|| ||

"Mahā kho Keṇiya, [104] bhikkhu-saŋgho aḍḍhateḷasāni bhikkhu-satāni,||
tvañ ca kho brāhmaṇesu abhi-p-pasanno" ti.|| ||

Dutiyam pi kho Keṇiyo jaṭilo Bhagavantaɱ etad avoca: "Kiñ cāpi bho Gotama,||
mahā bhikkhasaŋgho aḍḍhateḷasāni bhikkhu-satāni,||
ahañ ca brāhmaṇesu abhi-p-pasanno,||
adhivāsetu me bhavaɱ Gotamo svātanāya bhattaɱ saddhiɱ bhikkhu-saŋghenā" ti.|| ||

Dutiyam pi kho Bhagavā Keṇiyo jaṭilaɱ etad avoca:|| ||

"Mahā Keṇiya,||
bhikkhasaŋgho aḍḍhateḷasāni bhikkhu-satāni,||
tvañ ca kho brāhmaṇesu abhi-p-pasanno"ti.|| ||

Tatiyam pi kho Keṇiyo jaṭilo Bhagavantaɱ etad avoca:|| ||

"Kiñcā pi bho Gotama,||
mahā bhikkhasaŋgho aḍḍhateḷasāni bhikkhu-satāni,||
ahañ ca brāhmaṇesu abhi-p-pasanno,||
adhivāsetv-eva me bhavaɱ Gotamo svātanāya bhattaɱ saddhiɱ bhikkhu-saŋghenā" ti.|| ||

Adhivāsesi Bhagavā tuṇahibhāvena.|| ||

Atha kho Keṇiyo jaṭilo Bhagavantaɱ adhivāsanaɱ viditvā uṭṭhāyasā yena sako assame ten'upasankami,||
upasankamitvā mitt-ā-macce ñātisālohite āmantesi:|| ||

"Suṇantu me bhonto mittāmacacā ñātisā-lohitā samaṇo me Gotamo nimantito svātanāya bhattaɱ sadadhiɱ bhikkhu-saŋghena yena me kāyaveyyāvaṭikaɱ kareyyā" ti.|| ||

"Evaɱ bho" ti kho Keṇiyassa jaṭilassa mitt-ā-maccā ñātisā-lohitā Keṇiyassa jaṭilassa paṭissutvā app-ekacce uddhanānani khaṇanti,||
app-ekacce kaṭṭhāni phālenti,||
app-ekacce bhājanti dhovanti,||
app-ekacce udaka-maṇikaɱ patiṭṭhāpenti,||
app-ekacce āsanāni paññā-penti.|| ||

Keṇiyo pana jaṭilo sāmaɱ yeva maṇḍalamālaɱ paṭiyādeti.|| ||

Tena kho pana samayena Selo brāhmaṇo Āpaṇe paṭivasati [105] tiṇaṇaɱ vedānaɱ pāragu sanighaṇaṭukeṭubhānaɱ sākkhara-p-pabhe-dānaɱ itihāsa-pañca-mānaɱ padako veyyā-karaṇo lokāyatamahā-purisa-lakkhaṇesu anavayo,||
tīṇi māṇavakasatāni matte vāceti.|| ||

Tena kho pana samayena Keṇiye jaṭile Sele brāhmaṇe abhi-p-pasanno hoti|| ||

Atha kho Selo brāhmaṇo tihi māṇavakasatehi parivuto jaŋghā-vihāraɱ anucaŋkammāno anuvicara-māno yena Keṇiyassa jaṭilassa assamo ten'upasankami.|| ||

Addasā kho Selo brāhmaṇo Keṇiyasmiɱ jaṭile app-ekacce uddhanānani khaṇante,||
app-ekacce kaṭṭhāni phālenti,||
app-ekacce bhājanti dhovanti,||
app-ekacce udaka-maṇikaɱ patiṭṭhāpenti,||
app-ekacce āsanāni paññā-penti.|| ||

Keṇiyaɱ pana jaṭilaɱ sāmañ ñeva maṇḍalamālaɱ paṭiyādentaɱ disvā Keṇiyaɱ jaṭilaɱ etad avoca: || ||

"Ki nu bhoto Keṇiyassa āvāho vā bhavissati,||
vihāho vā bhavissati,||
mahāyañño vā pacucapaṭṭhito,||
rājā vā Māgadho Seniyo Bimbisāro nimantino svātanāya saddhiɱ balakāyenā" ti?|| ||

"Na me Sela,||
āvāho bhavissati,||
na pi vihābho bhavissati,||
na pi rājā Māgadho Seniyeba Bimbisāro nimattito svātanāya saddhiɱ balakāyena,||
api ca kho me mahāyañño pacc'upaṭṭhito atithi.|| ||

Samaṇo Gotamo Sakya-putto Sakya-kulā pabba-jito Aŋguttarāpesu cārikaɱ cara-māno mahatā bhikkhu-saŋghena saddhiɱ aḍḍhateḷasehi bhikkhu-satehi Āpaṇaɱ anuppatto.|| ||

Taɱ kho [106] pana bhavantaɱ Gotamaɱ evaɱ kalyāṇo nitti saddo abbhu-g-gato "Iti pi so Bhagavā arahaɱ sammāSamBuddho vijjā-caraṇa-sampanno Sugato loka-vidu anuttaro purisa-damma-sārathi Satthā deva-manussānaɱ Buddho Bhagavā" ti so me nimattito svātanāya saddhiɱ bhikkhu-saŋghenā" ti.|| ||

"Buddho" ti kho bho Keṇiya, vadesi?|| ||

"'Buddho' ti bho Sela, vadāmi."|| ||

"Buddho" ti bho Keṇiya, vadesi?|| ||

"'Buddho' ti bho sela vadāmi" ti.|| ||

Atha bho Selassa brahmaṇassa etad ahosi:|| ||

"Ghoso pi kho eso dullabho lokasmiɱ yadidaɱ 'Buddho' ti.|| ||

Āgatāni kho pana asmākaɱ mantesu dvattiɱsa mahā-purisa-lakkhanāni,||
yehi samannāgatassa mahāpurisassa dve va gatiyo bhavanti anaññā.|| ||

Sace agāraɱ ajjhāvasati,||
rājā hoti cakka-vatti dhammiko Dhamma-rājā cāturanto vijitāvi jana-padatthācariya-p-patto santaratanasamannāgato.|| ||

Tass'imāni satta ratanāni bhavanti,||
seyyath'īdaɱ:||
cakka-ratanaɱ hatthi-ratanaɱ assa-ratanaɱ maṇi-ratanaɱ itthi-ratanaɱ gahapati-ratanaɱ pariṇāyakaratanam eva sattamaɱ,||
parosahassaɱ kho pan'assa puttā bhavatti surā viraŋgarūpā parasena-p-pamaddanā,||
so imaɱ paṭhaviɱ sāgarapariyantaɱ adaṇḍena asatthena dhammena abhivijiya ajjhāvasati.|| ||

Sace kho panāgārasmā anagāriyaɱ pabbajati,||
arahaɱ hoti sammāSamBuddho loka vivatta-c-chaddo' —|| ||

"Kahaɱ pana bho Keṇiya,||
etarahi so bhavaɱ Gotamo viharati arahaɱ sammāsammuddho" ti?|| ||

Evaɱ vutte Keṇiyo jaṭilo dakkhiṇaɱ bāhaɱ paggahetvā Selaɱ brāhmaṇaɱ etad avoca:|| ||

[107] "Yen'esā bho Sela nilavanarāji" ti.|| ||

Atha kho Selo brāhmaṇo tihi māṇavakasatehi saddhiɱ yena Bhagavā tenupasaŋkakami.|| ||

Atha kho Selo brāhmaṇo te māṇavake āmantesi:|| ||

"Appa-saddā honto āga-c-chantu pāde padaɱ nikkhipantā,||
durāsadā hi te Bhagavanto sīhā va ekacarā,||
yadā c'āhaɱ bho samaṇena Gotamena saddhiɱ manteyyaɱ,||
mā me bhonto,||
antar'antarā kathaɱ opātetha,||
kathā-pariyosānaɱ me bhavanto āgamentu" ti.|| ||

Atha kho Selo brāhmaṇo yena Bhagavā ten'upasankami,||
upasankamitvā Bhagavatā saddhiɱ sammodi,||
sammodaniyaɱ kathaɱ sārāṇiyaɱ vitisāretvā eka-m-antaɱ nisīdi.|| ||

Eka-m-antaɱ nisinno kho Selo brāhmaṇo Bhagavato kāye dvattiɱsa mahā-purisa-lakkhanāni samannesi.|| ||

Addasā kho Selo brāhmaṇo Bhagavato kāye dvattiɱsa mahāpuraghalakkhaṇāni yebhuyyena ṭhapetvā dve,||
dvisu mahā-purisa-lakkhaṇesu kankhati vici-kicchati nādhi-muccati na sampasidati,||
kosohite ca vatthaguyhe pahutajivhatāya ca.|| ||

Atha kho Bhagavato etad ahosi:|| ||

"Passati kho me ayaɱ Selo brāhmaṇo dvittiɱsa mahā-purisa-lakkhaṇāni yebhuyyena ṭhapetvā dve,||
dvisu mahā-purisa-lakkhaṇesu kankhati vici-kicchati nādhi-muccati na sampasidati,||
kosohite ca vatthaguyhe pahutajivhatāya cā" ti.|| ||

Atha kho Bhagavā tathā-rūpaɱ iddhābhisankhāraɱ abisaŋkhāsi yathā addasa Selo brāhmaṇo Bhagavato kosohitaɱ [108] vatthaguyhaɱ.|| ||

Atha kho Bhagavā jivhaɱ ninnāmetvā ubho pi kaṇṇasotāni anumasi paṭimasi,||
ubho pi nāsikasotāni anumasi paṭimasi,||
kevalam pi nalāṭamaṇḍalaɱ jivhāya chadesi.|| ||

Atha kho Selassa brahmaṇassa etad ahosi:|| ||

"Samannāgato kho samaṇo Gotamo dvattiɱsamahā-purisa-lakkhaṇehi paripuṇṇehi no aparipuṇṇehi,||
no ca kho naɱ jānāmi 'Buddho vā no vā.'|| ||

Sutaɱ kho pana metaɱ brāhmaṇānaɱ vuddhānaɱ mahallakānaɱ ācariya-pācariyānaɱ bhāsa-mānānaɱ.|| ||

'Ye te bhavanti arahanto sammāSamBuddho te sake vaṇṇe bhaññamāne attānaɱ pātu-karonti' ti,||
yan nunāhaɱ samaṇaɱ Gotamaɱ sammukhā sāruppāhi gāthāhi abhitthaveyyan" ti.|| ||

Atha kho Sele brāhmaṇo Bhagavantaɱ sammukhā sāruppāhi gāthāhi abhitthavi.|| ||

"Paripuṇṇakāyo suruci sujāto cārudasasano,||
Suvaṇṇavaṇṇo si Bhagavā susukkadāṭho si viriyavā.|| ||

Narassa hi sujātassa ye bhavanti viyañajanā||
Sabb'ete tava kāyasmiɱ mahā-purisa-lakkhaṇā.|| ||

Pasannanetto sumukho brahā uju patāpavā,||
Majjhe samaṇaSaŋghassa ādicco va virocasi.|| ||

Kalyāṇadassano bhikkhu kañcanasannibhattaco,||
Kiɱ te samaṇabhāvena evaɱ uttamavaṇṇino.|| ||

Rājā arahasi bhavituɱ cakka-vatti rathesabho,||
Cāturanto vijitāvī Jambusaṇḍassa issaro.|| ||

[109] Khattiyā bhoja-rājāno anuyuttā bhavatti te,||
Rājābhirājā manujindo rajjaɱ kārehi Gotama."|| ||

"Rājāham asmi Sela (iti Bhagavā ) Dhamma-rājā anuttaro,||
Dhammena cakkaɱ vattemi cakkaɱ appati-vattiyaɱ."|| ||

"SamBuddho paṭijānāsi (iti Selo brāhmaṇo) 'Dhamma-rājā anuttaro||
Dhammena cakkaɱ vattemi' ita bhāsasi Gotama.|| ||

Ko nu senāpita bhoto sāvako Satthu-d-anthayo,||
Ko te imaɱ anuvatteti Dhamma-cakkaɱ pavattitaɱ."|| ||

"Mayā pavattitaɱ cakkaɱ (Selā ti Bhagavā) Dhamma-cakkaɱ anuttaraɱ,||
Sāriputto anuvatteti anujāto Tathāgataɱ|| ||

Abhiññeyyaɱ abhiññātaɱ bhāvetabbañ ca bhāvitaɱ,||
Pahātabbaɱ pahinaɱ me, tasmā Buddho'smi brāhmaṇa|| ||

Vinayassu mayi kaŋkhaɱ adhimuccassu brāhmaṇa,||
Dullabhaɱ dassanaɱ hoti sambuddhānaɱ abhiṇhaso.|| ||

[110] Yesaɱ vo dullabho loke pātu-bhāvo abhiṇahaso,||
So'haɱ brāhmaṇa sambudedhā salla-katto anuttaro|| ||

Brahmabhūto atitulo Mārasenappamaddano,||
Sabbāmitte vasikatvā modāmi akutobhayo."|| ||

"Imaɱ bhonto nisāmetha, yathā bhāsati cakkhumā,||
Sallakatto mahāvīro siho va nadati vane|| ||

Brahmabhūtaɱ atitulaɱ Mārasenappamaddanaɱ,||
Ko disvā na-ppasideyya api kaṇh'ābhijātiko.|| ||

Yo maɱ icchati anvetu, yo vā n'icchati gacchatu,||
Idh'āhaɱ pabbajissāmi varapaññassa santike."|| ||

"Etañ ce ruccati bhoto Sammā-Sam-Buddha-sāsanaɱ,||
Mayam pi pabbajissāma varapaññassa santike."|| ||

"Brāhmaṇā tisatā ime yā canti pañajalikatā,||
Buhmacariyaɱ carissāma Bhagavā tava santike."|| ||

"Svākkhātaɱ Brahma-cariyaɱ (Selā ti Bhagavā) sandiṭṭhikam akalikaɱ,||
Yattha amoghā pabbajjā appamattassa sikkhato" ti.|| ||

Alattha kho Selo brāhmaṇo sapariso Bhagavato santike pabbajjaɱ,||
alattha upasampadaɱ.|| ||

Atha kho Keṇiyo jaṭilo tassā rattiyā accayena sake assame paṇitaɱ khādaniyaɱ bhojaniyaɱ paṭiyādāpetvā [111] Bhagavato kālaɱ ārocāpasi:|| ||

"Kālo bho Gotama niṭṭhitaɱ bhattan" ti.|| ||

Atha kho Bhagavā pubbanahasamayaɱ nivāsetvā pattacivaram ādāya yena Keṇiyassa jaṭilassa assamo ten'upasankami.|| ||

Upasaŋkamitvā paññatte āsane nisīdi,||
saddhiɱ bhikkhu-saŋghena.|| ||

Atha kho Keṇiyo jaṭilo Buddha-pamukhaɱ bhikkhu-Saŋghaɱ paṇitena khādaniyena bhojaniyena sahatthā santappesi sampavāresi.|| ||

Atha kho Keṇiyo jaṭilo Bhagavantaɱ bhuttāmiɱ onitapattapāṇiɱ aññataraɱ nicaɱ āsanaɱ gahetvā eka-m-antaɱ nisīdi.|| ||

Eka-m-antaɱ nisinnaɱ kho Kheṇiyaɱ jaṭilaɱ Bhagavā imāhi gāthāhi anumodi.|| ||

"Aggihuttamukhā yaññā, Sāvitti chandaso mukhaɱ,||
Rājā mukhaɱ manussānaɱ, nadinaɱ sāgaro mukhaɱ.|| ||

Nakkhattānaɱ mukhaɱ cando, ādicco tapataɱ mukhaɱ,||
Puññaɱ ākaŋkha-mānānaɱ saŋgho ve yajataɱ mukhat" ti.|| ||

Atha kho Bhagavā Keṇiyaɱ jaṭilaɱ imāhi gāthāhi anumo-ditvā uṭṭhāy āsanā pakkāmi.|| ||

Atha kho āyasmā Selo sapariso eko vupakaṭṭho appamatto ātāpi pahit'atto viharatto na cirass'eva yass'atthāya kula-puttā samma-d-eva agārasmā anagāriyaɱ pabbajanti.|| ||

Tad anuttaraɱ Brahma-cariya-pariyosānaɱ diṭṭhe'va dhamme sayaɱ abhiññā sacchi-katvā upasampajja vihāsi.|| ||

Khiṇā jāti,||
vusitaɱ Brahma-cariyaɱ,||
kataɱ karaṇiyaɱ,||
nāparaɱ itthāntāyā' ti abbhaññāsi.|| ||

Aññataro ca kho pan'āyasmā Selo sapariyo arahataɱ ahosi.|| ||

Atha kho āyasmā Selo sapariso yena Bhagavā ten'upasankami.|| ||

Upasaŋkamitvā ekaɱsaɱ cīvaraɱ katvā yena Bhagavā ten'añajalim paṇāmetvā Bhagavantaɱ gāthāhi ajjhabhāsi.|| ||

"Yaɱ taɱ saraṇam āgamma ito aṭṭhami cakkhuma,||
Sattarattena Bhagavā dant'ambha tava sāsane.|| ||

Tuvaɱ Buddho, tuvaɱ Satthā, tuvaɱ Mārābhābhu muni,||
Tuvaɱ anusaye chetvā tiṇṇo tāres'imaɱ pajaɱ.|| ||

Upadhi te samatikkantā, āsavā te padālitā,||
Siho si anupādāno pahiṇahayabheravo.|| ||

Bhikkhavo tisatā ime tiṭṭhanti pañajalikatā,||
Pāde cira pasārehi nāgā vandantu Satthuno" ti.|| ||

Selasuttaɱ dutiyaɱ
