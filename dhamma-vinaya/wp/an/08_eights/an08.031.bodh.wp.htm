[236] [1165]

[1][pts][olds] "Bhikkhus, there are these eight gifts.

What eight?

(1) Having insulted [the recipient], one gives a gift.

(2) One gives a gift from fear.

(3) One gives a gift, [thinking]:

'He gave to me.'

(4) One gives a gift, [thinking]:

'He will give to me.'

(5) One gives a gift, [thinking]:

'Giving is good.'

(6) One gives a gift, [thinking]:

'I cook; these people do not cook.

It isn't right that I who cook should not give to those who do not cook.'

(7) One gives a gift, [thinking]:

'Because I have given this gift, I will gain a good reputation.'

(8) One gives a gift for the purpose of ornamenting the mind, equipping the mind."
