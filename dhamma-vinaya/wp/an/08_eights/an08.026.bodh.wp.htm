[222] [1155]

[1][pts][than] On one occasion the Blessed One was dwelling at Rājagaha in Jīvaka's mango grove. Then Jīvaka Komarabhacca approached the Blessed One, paid homage to him, sat down to one side, and said to him:

"In what way, bhante, is one a lay follower."

[The rest as in 8:25.]
