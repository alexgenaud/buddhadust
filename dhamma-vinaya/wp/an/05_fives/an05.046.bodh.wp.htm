[1][pts] "Bhikkhus, there are these five accomplishments.

What five?

Accomplishment in faith,
accomplishment in virtuous behavior,
accomplishment in learning,
accomplishment in generosity,
and accomplishment in wisdom.

These are the five accomplishments."
