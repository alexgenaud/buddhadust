[1][pts][than] "Bhikkhus, growing in five ways, a female noble disciple grows by a noble growth, and she absorbs the essence and the best of this life.

What five?

She grows in faith,
virtuous behavior,
learning,
generosity, and
wisdom.

Growing in these five ways, a female noble disciple grows by a noble growth, and she absorbs the essence and the best of this life."

She who grows in faith and virtuous behavior,
in wisdom, generosity, and learning —
such virtuous female lay follower
absorbs for herself the essence of this life.
