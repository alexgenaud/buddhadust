[1][pts] "Bhikkhus, when virtuous renunciants dwell in dependence on a village or a town, the people there generate much merit in three ways.

What three?

By body, speech, and mind.

When virtuous renunciants dwell in dependence on a village or a town, the people there generate much merit in these three ways."
