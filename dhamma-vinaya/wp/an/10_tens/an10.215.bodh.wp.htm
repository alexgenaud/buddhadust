[1][pts] "Bhikkhus, possessing ten qualities, a female lay follower dwells without self-confidence at home.

What ten?

(1) She destroys life ... and (10) holds wrong view.

Possessing these ten qualities, a female lay follower dwells without self-confidence at home.

"Bhikkhus, possessing ten qualities, a female lay follower dwells self-confident at home.

What ten?

(1) She abstains from the destruction of life ... and (10) holds right view.

Possessing these ten qualities, a female lay follower dwells self-confident at home."
