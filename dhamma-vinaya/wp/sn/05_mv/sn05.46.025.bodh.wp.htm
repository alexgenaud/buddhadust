[1][pts] "Bhikkhus, I will teach you seven things that lead to non-decline.

Listen to that....

"And what, bhikkhus, are the seven things that lead to non-decline?

They are: the seven factors of enlightenment.

What seven?

The enlightenment factor of mindfulness ... the enlightenment factor of equanimity.

These are the seven things that lead to non-decline."
