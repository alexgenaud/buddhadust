[1][pts] Thus have I heard.

"At Sāvatthī.

"Bhikkhus, these eight things, purified, cleansed, flawless, free from corruptions, if unarisen do not arise apart from the Discipline of a Fortunate One.

What eight?

Right view ... right concentration.

These eight things...."
