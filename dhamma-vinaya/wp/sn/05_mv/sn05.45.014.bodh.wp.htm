[1][pts] Thus have I heard.

"At Sāvatthī.

"Bhikkhus, these eight things, developed and cultivated, if unarisen do not arise apart from the appearance of aTathāgata, an Arahant, a Perfectly Enlightened One.

What eight?

Right view ... right concentration.

These eight things...."
