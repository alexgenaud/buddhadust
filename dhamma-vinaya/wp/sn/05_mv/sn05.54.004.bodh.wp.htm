Connected Discourses on Breathing

Book 1: One Thing

Sutta 4

Fruits (1)

Translated from the Pali by Bhikkhu Bodhi

 

[1][pts][olds] (All as in preceding sutta, with the following addition:)

[314] "When, bhikkhus, mindfulness of breathing has been developed and cultivated in this way, one of two fruits may be expected: either final knowledge in this very life or, if there is a residue of clinging, the state of non-retuming."
