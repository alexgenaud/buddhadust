[1][pts] "Bhikkhus, those who have neglected the seven factors of enlightenment have neglected the noble path leading to the complete destruction of suffering.

Those who have undertaken the seven factors of enlightenment have undertaken the noble path leading to the complete destruction of suffering.

"What seven?

The enlightenment factor of mindfulness ... the enlightenment factor of equanimity.

"Bhikkhus, those who have neglected ... who have undertaken these seven factors of enlightenment have undertaken the noble path leading to the complete destruction of suffering."
