[1][pts][nypo][olds] "Bhikkhus, these three feelings are impermanent, conditioned, dependently arisen, subject to destruction, subject to vanishing, subject to fading away, subject to cessation.

What three?

Pleasant feeling, painful feeling, neither-painful-nor-pleasant feeling.

These three feelings are impermanent, conditioned, dependently arisen, subject to destruction, subject to vanishing, subject to fading away, subject to cessation."
