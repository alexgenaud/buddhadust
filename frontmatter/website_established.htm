"Visitors" are viewers who have come to the site one or more times;
"Hits" count every call to the web server (for example one for every graphic element, etc), and are not counted;
"Sessions" counts Visitors who have viewed more than one page during one visit;
Crackers, worms, well-known spiders, and visits by the webmaster have been largely eliminated from the count. A few spiders get through but I figure that a spider crawls a site because at the other end somewhere there is someone interested, so counting it as one visitor is probably conservative.
